Symptom

IS-U Invoicing EA19 terminates with error TU-540 "Documents reversed by adjustment are invoiced with older documents".

Environment

  • SAP IS-U/CCS - Industry Solution for Utilities / Customer Care & Service.
  • SAP ERP 6.0 Enhancement Package 4 (IS-UT 604) and higher.

Reproducing the Issue

  • Call transaction EA19 for a certain contract account having a payment plan (budget billing procedure 3).
  • Invoicing terminates with error TU-540.

Cause

  • Incomplete customizing for generating a payment plan concerning the considered maximum number of months in the past (table TE015, field MXMNT).
  • Adjustment reversal documents are involved.

Resolution

  • Maintain the customizing table for payment plans TE015 according your requirements.
  • Set a high number of months as maximum number of month for searching documents in the past (field MXMNT) according to the periods of considered adjustment reversal documents, eg 48 months.
