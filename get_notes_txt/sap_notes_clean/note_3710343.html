Symptom

In SAP Cloud connector you verify that SAC Subaccount fails to connect.

Environment

SAP Cloud Connector release independent.

Reproducing the Issue

In SAP Cloud Connector, select the subaccount and click on "connect" button.

On "tunnel information" status you verify the message "failed to connect" and also message "An error occurred when trying to connect to <subaccount ID>. See 'Log and Trace Files' and in particular scc_core.trc for details. Associated entries were logged on or around <timestamp>"

In the timestamp mentioned, below error message is perceived on scc_core.trc traces: 

#Tunnel account:///<subaccount ID> connect failed
com.sap.core.connectivity.tunnel.core.handshake.TunnelHandshakeException: Invalid status of handshake response: 400 Bad Request

Cause

Subaccount is deleted or does not exist anymore. 

Resolution

Check with the subaccount owner for the status of the subaccount.

More information can also be checked in 3404816 - SAC Subaccount and URL Details in SAP Analytics Cloud.

See Also
