Symptom

The employer is required to submit confirmation of the first mandatory contribution payment to the employee's retirement savings account in PDF format.

Other Terms

HRCZ, HCZCERCC, HR_CZ_ER_CONTR_CERT, PDF form

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant for the Czech Republic only.

Solution

  • A new PDF form, HR_CZ_ER_CONTR_CERT, has been introduced, along with a new report, HCZCERCC, designed to facilitate the preparation of the aforementioned form. To generate the report, users are required to provide the employee's personnel ID, the date of the first payment, and the date of issue of the confirmation.

Important: Apply current version of SAP Note 875986 for NW 7.0x or 1668882 for NW 7.3x and higher. Otherwise an old version of SNOTE can lead to missing coding changes when applying SAP Notes.

You can find more information about the SAP Note Assistant in SAP Service Marketplace, under SAP Note Assistant.
