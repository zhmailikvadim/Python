Symptom

After scheduling production orders in transaction cm25, the corresponding sales order confirmations are wrong: either the confirmation date & quantity for the item corresponds to the one belonging to another item or it is wrongly set to zero.

Other Terms

group check, SD_CO_ORDER_CHANGE_ORDER, SD_CONFIRMATION_SALES_ORDER, SD_SALES_DOCUMENT_SAVE

Reason and Prerequisites

It can happen when multiple production orders corresponding to multiple items of the same sales order and rescheduled together. In addition, there are active integration model active for the ATP check in APO. The symptom only occurs in the ERP releases and not in the S/4hana releases.
