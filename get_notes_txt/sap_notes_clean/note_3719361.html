Symptom

  • The Manage Banner Cards page displays English labels instead of user's preference locale in the latest Home Page settings screen.
  • The problem persists in the 2H 2025 system.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Manage Home Page
  2. Navigate to the "Manage Banner Cards" settings screen.
  3. Observe that labels are displayed in English instead of user's preference locale

Cause

The issue is due to undeployed language packages for non-English locales.

Resolution

  1. The fix for this issue is planned for release in 1H 2026 updates:
  • Preview: April 14th, 2026
  • Product: May 16th, 2026
