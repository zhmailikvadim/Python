Symptom

When adding an item to a service order, you encounter the error message "Too many ship-to parties maintained". Checking the item partners there are indeed two ship to parties maintained. It could occur for a service item, a spare part item or a sales item. The problem could also occur for payer or bill-to party.

Reason and Prerequisites

The problem is due to a wrong access sequence 0005 ('Preceding Document -> Business Partner Relationships: Sold-To Party -> Current Partner: Sold-To Party.') maintained in the item partner procedure. This access sequence will derive the ship-to party again from the sold-to party on item level, although the ship-to party may have changed on header level and the changed partner was inherited to item level.

Solution

Please change the access sequence to 1000 ('Preceding Document') on item level. This will make sure that the header partner will be inherited without error. Still if you copy an item, a deviating item partner in the source document will be copied even if the header partner in the target document is different. (If you remove the access sequence entirely. this will also solve the problem, but a deviating item partner in the source document will be no longer considered.) The standard customizing and delivery content will be changed accordingly.
