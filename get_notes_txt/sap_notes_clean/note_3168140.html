Symptom

1. In HNOCSCKNT_SYNC report, the inactive pernrs are processed.

Other Terms

HRNO, HNOCSCKNT_SYNC, sickness processing

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Norway.
HRSP SAPK-10201INS4HCM, HRSP SAPK-10111INS4HCM, HRSP SAPK-10024INS4HCM, SAPK-608D5INSAPHR, SAPK-604K7INSAPHR, SAPK-600O1INSAPHR (or newer)

Solution

1. HNOCSCKNT_SYNC logic was adjusted and the inactive pernrs are skipped.
