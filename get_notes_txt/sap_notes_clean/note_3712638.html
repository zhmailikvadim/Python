Symptom

  • My contractor's SOW, Work Order, Expense Sheet or Time Sheet was revised and I do not know why
  • A SOW, Work Order, Expense Sheet or Time Sheet was updated and routed to me for approval, but I don't know why it was changed
  • Why was my worker's Work Order, Work Order extension, Time sheet, or Expense Sheet rejected?

Resolution

Please reach out to the PMO at the Buyer company if you have any questions about why a change was made in the system.

Fieldglass technical support is best able to assist with:

  • Break/fix issues on the Fieldglass platform (such as bug reports or errors) 
  • Configuration changes that cannot be made in the UI
  • General how-to guidance on the application

Fieldglass technical support does not have any information about:

  • Past or future actions of its clients
  • Buyer companies' specific processes or internal policies
