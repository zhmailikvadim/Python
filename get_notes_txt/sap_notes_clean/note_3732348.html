Symptom

To change the contact header field labels such as Name, Type, AN Status, Category, Region and Department within the Contacts section of Supplier 360 profile.

Environment

  • SAP Ariba Supplier Lifecycle and Performance
  • Contacts

Reproducing the Issue

Resolution

The contact header field labels are not customizable and therefore the fields cannot be renamed. Also, new or existing header fields cannot be added or removed.

The hard coded labels of contact header fields are as below.

  • Name
  • Type
  • AN Status
  • Category
  • Region
  • Department
