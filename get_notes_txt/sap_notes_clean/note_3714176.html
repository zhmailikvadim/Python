Symptom

Postings created on revenue accounts are not created in MPB.

Reason and Prerequisites

This was an intended change, since we introduced account type check during JE creation. KBA 3399540 was created for that in 2023

Solution

As workaround Schedule Repricing for Projects can be triggered and postings will be created.

Revert the changes for the fix with S4H CE 2602 HFC04
