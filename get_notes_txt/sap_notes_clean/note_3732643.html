Symptom

  • The Peru Sales Ledger report shows tax base amount and tax amount as 0.00.
  • The same behavior can also be observed in the Peru Purchase Ledger report.
  • The report may be generated with status generated with errors, but the log can remain blank.
  • Invoice values, tax values, and other tax-related amounts are not populated as expected.
  • In the reported examples, output lines were generated with all relevant tax amount fields equal to 0.00.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • Statutory Reporting 
  • DRC Peru

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Peru Sales Ledger report in Statutory Reporting/DRC.
  2. Set the reporting parameters.
  3. Generate the report and download the TXT output.
  4. Observe that the tax base amount and tax amount fields are 0.00.

Cause

Incorrect customization entries were maintained in view V_T007Z (Assign Time-Dependent Tax Group Version) for Peru tax group versions SLSL and PURL, which are not used by the Peru Sales/Purchase Ledger reports and caused conflicts in tax box determination, resulting in 0.00 amounts.

Resolution

  1. In SPRO, navigate to: Financial Accounting > General Ledger Accounting > Periodic Processing > Report > Sales/Purchases Tax Returns > Assign Tax Group Version Time-Dependent (view V_T007Z).
  2. Remove the entries maintained for the Peru tax group versions SLSL and PURL.
  3. Save the changes.
  4. Regenerate the Peru Sales Ledger (and Purchase Ledger, if applicable) and validate that tax base amount and tax amount are populated.
