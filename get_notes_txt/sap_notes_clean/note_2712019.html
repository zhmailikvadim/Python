Symptom

You are in a Trade promotion integrated scenario and maintaining the accruals on Rebate agreements in ERP (external accruals). You may notice:

Validated account is not defaulted correctly on the CRM claim items.
Accrual Balance(ERP) is not displayed correctly in claim resolution tree.

Other Terms

External accruals, Business function CRM_CLA_CPM, Variable rebates, Fixed rebates, Enhanced rebates, Accrual balance

Reason and Prerequisites

If any one of the products in the rebate agreement do not have sales volume, this situation will lead to two options for validated accounts- Sold-to and the default Payer(Rebate recipient). The SAP Note is to limit the validated accounts to Sold-Tos.
The correction also cover the scenario if you have manual accruals in ERP for fixed rebates.

Solution

Implement the corrections attached to the SAP Note.
