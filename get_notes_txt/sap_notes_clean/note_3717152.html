Symptom

  • I method to retrieve business partner contact information, such as their addresses, using the Ariba API.
  • The desired data is stored within the Supplier Registration Questionnaire in the Supplier Lifecycle and Performance (SLP) module.

Environment

  • Supplier Lifecycle & Performance - Open API

Resolution

  • You must utilize the GET /vendors/{{smVendorId}}/workspaces/questionnaires/{{questionnaireId}}/qna endpoint, which is part of the Supplier Data API with Pagination, to retrieve the supplier information available in the Supplier Registration Questionnaire.

See Also
