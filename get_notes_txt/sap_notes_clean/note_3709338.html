Symptom

When processing high data volumes, the job RFM_DIGITAL_ACCESS may fail with a TSV_TNEW_PAGE_ALLOC_FAILED error.

This runtime error occurs when the system is attempting to allocate a new page for an internal table.

Other Terms

RFM_DIGITAL_ACCESS , Runtime error, TSV_TNEW_PAGE_ALLOC_FAILED , New Page , Internal Table.

Reason and Prerequisites
