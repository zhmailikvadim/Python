Symptom

The field control of the field 'Expected Date of Completion' is based on the customizing node SPRO → Logistics - General  → Product Lifecycle management (PLM) → Change Record → Settings for change records → Define change record type → Dynamic field control . Based on the status against the field 'Expected date of completion', the field is either editable, hidden or mandatory. This should be always the case.

Other Terms

Expected Date of completion, /PLMI/CL_CR_SET_UI_PROPERTY~SET_HDR_DYNAMIC_FLD_CTRL_PROP

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Please implement the SAP note.
