Symptom

When serial number integration is active in SAP Extended Warehouse Management (EWM) for inspection object type 4 (Q-Inspection Product/Batch Inbound Delivery) or inspection object type 5 (Q-Inspection Product/Batch Whse-Internal), and Item Unique Identification (IUID) handling is activated at client level, serial numbers may not be transferred to inspection lot.

Other Terms

IOT 4; IOT5; Serial Numbers; IUID; Item Unique Identification; Inspection Lot; embedded; decentral

Reason and Prerequisites

The system incorrectly determines the relevance of Item Unique Identification (IUID) when serial numbers are transferred to inspection lots in the mentioned context. It considers only the client-level activation of IUID handling and ignores the material/plant-specific setting. As a result, serial numbers are not transferred as soon as IUID handling is activated at client level, even if the material/plant-specific IUID setting is inactive, indicating that IUID handling is not relevant for this material/plant combination.

Solution

Implement Correction Instruction.
