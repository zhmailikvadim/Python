Symptom

According to the recent response received from the authorities, the sick leave allowance should be calculated by decreasing it by one day for each medical certificate issued starting with February 1, 2026, including for the certificates in continuation.

Other Terms

T7RO0C; KLBEU; UNPBE; KBBEG

Solution

For continued sick leaves (connected with KBBEG), the first day of this continued sick leave becomes unpaid. The determination of the law start (date for reading the customization) can be customized in T7RO0C-UNPBE.

Please see the attached document with more details and customizing.
