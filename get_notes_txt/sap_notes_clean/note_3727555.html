Symptom

You created an outbound delivery which countains a delivery group hierarchy. One of the group items is adjusted with 0qty. When you perform the goods issue on TU (Transportation Unit) level, you can identify that the document flow of the adjusted item is not updated with the OD document related entry, however the item is in the created OD and the goods issue has happened well also. Unfortunately the adjusted item remains inconsistent, the delivery can not be closed or reversed anymore.

Other Terms

DLVGRP, no DF is written, no OD is written, 0qty, 0 qty

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the correction instruction or the equivalent support package.
