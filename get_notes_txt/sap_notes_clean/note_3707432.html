Symptom

During invoicing, the local currency amounts from the posting are not passed to the CAII transfer records.

Other Terms

DFKKRA_RAI_MI, DFKKRA_RAI_CO, FKKINV_S, FKKINV_M, FKKINV_MA, BERTH, BETR2, BETR3, FP_RAI_MON

Reason and Prerequisites

This issue happens when there are both positive and negative amounts in the posting and invoicing items, and the aggregated amount is 0.

Solution

Implement this SAP Note or the corresponding Support Package
