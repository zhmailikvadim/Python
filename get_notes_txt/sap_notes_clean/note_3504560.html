Symptom

The End of Supply (Migration) process is missing some configuration at step 1200 to automatically complete the processing at the S4 end. Due to this, the process remains stuck at step 1100 for the check result GPKE_END_CONF.

Other Terms

DE_B_GPKE_EOS_SUP, DE_B_GELIGAS_EOS_SUP, End of Supply for Electricity (SUP View), End of Supply for Gas (SUP View), Migration, SUP View, 2310, 2310_00, S/4, Precondition, US4G, GPKE_SUP

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Please implement the automatic corrections or import higher relevant Support Packages.
