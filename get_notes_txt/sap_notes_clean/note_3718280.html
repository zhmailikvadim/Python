Symptom

In the Manage Customer JIT Calls app, the system displays the created material document under the Business Transaction Document facet after a JIT call is processed. However, the app provides a separate, dedicated Material Documents facet intended specifically for displaying material‑related postings. Material document is shown in the incorrect facet instead of appearing in the Material Documents facet as expected.

Other Terms

Manage Customer JIT calls, Material Documents, Business Transaction Document Reference, JIT S2C

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the correction instructions of the Note or import the relevant Support Package.
