Symptom

An email is received which includes:

  • "Expiring API certificate" as heading
  • A Link to http://<iastenant>/admin/#/applications/.../trust/password. When you open the link, you are redirected to SSLC 100_SD or SSLC 80_SD certificate which status shows that it will expire soon.

Environment

  • SAP Cloud Identity Services
  • S/4HANA Cloud

Resolution

  • SAP will renew the SAP default certificate and rotate it in the IAS tenant. No action is required from the customer side.
  • SAP is working on adapting the email notification.
  • If further clarification on the use of the certificate SSLC_100_SD or SSLC 80_SD is needed, a support Case can be opened under the component XX-S4C-OPR-SRV.

Keywords

SSLC_100_SD, SSLC 80_SD, expiring certificate, identity authentication service, OpenID, certificate renewal.
