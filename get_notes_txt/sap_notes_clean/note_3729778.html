Symptom

In the process "Registration of Meter Operation Service for Electricity (New MOS View)" (DE_B_WIM_REGMOS_MOSN), the MALO supply scenario is not updated in step 0210 when the MALO involves multiple MELOs.

This issue is relevant for the market role: MOS.

Other Terms

WIM, MALO, MELO, supply scenario, BAdI, CHECK_MOS_MALO_UPD, /US4G/IF_BADI_SUSC_PROCESS, /US4G/CL_IM_BADI_SUSC_PROC_WIM, /US4G/CL_UBD_SUSC, CHECK_MALO_WITH_SINGLE_MELO

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because the system did not support updating the MALO supply scenario when a MALO involves multiple MELOs. This is a new special requirement.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system updates the related MALO's supply scenario when the MALO has been identified in the custom BAdI method CHECK_MOS_MALO_UPD (/US4G/IF_BADI_SUSC_PROCESS). You need to implement the BAdI method CHECK_MOS_MALO_UPD (/US4G/IF_BADI_SUSC_PROCESS) based on your custom business scenario. You can verify the fix by checking that the MALO supply scenario is updated in the process DE_B_WIM_REGMOS_MOSN based on your custom special case.
