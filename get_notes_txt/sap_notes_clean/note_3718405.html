Symptom

In the SAP HCM SAP Fiori app "My Leave Requests (Version 3/SAP Fiori 2.0)" available attachments are directly opened once they are clicked by the end user.

The intended behaviour is that an attachment is downloaded instead of directly opened.

Other Terms

GBX01HR5, HCMFAB_LEAVE_REQUEST_CR_SRV, Content-Disposition, HTTP-header

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason:

Program error.

Solution

The corrections contained in this SAP Note ensure that clicked attachments are downloaded instead of opened.

 

Import the relevant Support Package into your system.

For an advance correction, implement the attached correction instructions.
