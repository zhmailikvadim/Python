Symptom

 When payroll is processed after an employee transfer, the PLR information in the BRTAX table is deleted.

Environment

  • HR renewal 

  • SAP S/4HANA 

  • SAP ERP  

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. PLR payment in January:
    The PLR (Profit Sharing) is paid to the employee in January.

  2. Employee transfer in February:
    The employee is transferred to a different company (or department) in February.

  3. Payroll processing after transfer:
    When the first payroll is processed after the employee’s transfer, the PLR information in the BRTAX table is deleted.

Resolution

The system is designed to retain PLR information in the BRTAX table only in the following situations: if there is no change in the employee’s company, or if the employee, after being transferred, returns to their previous company. In any other case where there is a company change and the employee does not return to their original company, the PLR information in the BRTAX table is deleted when payroll is processed after the transfer.

For more information on this behavior, please refer to the following SAP Notes:

  • SAP Note 1931923 - PLR: Incorrect IRRF tax payment when EE changes company
    ** This note resets the BRTAX when there is a company change.
  • SAP Note 2922827 - PLR - Tax payment after two transferences at the same year
    ** This note restores the PLR info in BRTAX when the employee returns to the previous company.
