Symptom

The repository was locked and caused the disruption of operational reports.

The table AL_VERSION in the repository shows as locked.

The issue happens at random times when the status of the repository is inactive.

Other Terms

This issue is tracked in Corrective Measure 002075125900005659432019.

ENVIRONMENT:

  • Sybase 16 SP03 PL07
  • Data Services 4.2 SP09 Patch 05, SP10, SP11, SP12

Reason and Prerequisites

Caused by an incorrect locking mechanism on AL_VERSION.

Solution

The correction is delivered in the release(s) listed in the Support Packages and Patches section of this SAP Note.
