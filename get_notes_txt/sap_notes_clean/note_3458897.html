Symptom

For the migration asset data in table '/kjrtax01/anek' and '/kjrtax01/anek' in releases as of sFIN 2.0 , when take-over date is in current fiscal year and executing the property tax report, migration asset data will be retrieved, but dynamic selections do not work for the migration asset data.

Other Terms

Japan, Property Tax Report, RAIDJP_TAX, Migration Asset, sFIN 2.0

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a correction of legal function.

Solution

 Apply the correction instructions in the note or the corresponding support package.
