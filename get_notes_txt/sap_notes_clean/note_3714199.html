Symptom

In App : F3192 – Scheduling Agreement Consumption  ,the  release order amount is zero.

Environment

  • SAP FIORI
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access app: Scheduling Agreement Consumption 
  2. Search for target Scheduling Agreement
  3. Check the release order amount

Cause

According to help doument  Scheduling Agreement Consumption

Scheduling Agreement Spend corresponds to the scheduled quantity until the evaluation date. It is calculated as follows:

Scheduled quantity = Sum of the schedule line order quantity

Scheduling agreement spend = Scheduled quantity * (Net price amount / Net price quantity)

But for Scheduled quantity , the delivery date should not be after the system date, If the schedule line delivery date is after the system date 
Scheduled quantity will be zero , the released order amount (Scheduling agreement spend) will be zero

Resolution

Check the schedule line delivery date make sure it is not in future

Keywords

P_SchedgAgrmtItemScheduleLines ;ScheduleLineDeliveryDate ;P_SchedgAgrmtItemScheduleLines ; Release Order amount
