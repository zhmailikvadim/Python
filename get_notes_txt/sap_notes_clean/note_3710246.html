Symptom

In SAP Transportation Management System (SAP TM), users observe different charge calculation statuses in a freight order, such as Calculated, Calculation Error, or Not Calculated.

Environment

SAP Transportation Management embedded in S/4HANA 2023 onwards

Resolution

The charge calculation status indicates the result of the charge calculation process as described below:

  • Calculated - Charge calculation was successful for the freight order
  • Calculation Error - Data is missing or incorrect, for example, the freight agreement is missing from the freight order, or the document currency is missing from the freight agreement
  • Not Calculated - Data is missing or incorrect for the charge element, for example, there's no carrier assigned to the freight order, or there's no charges template for the purchasing organization

Keywords
