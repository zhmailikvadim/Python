Symptom

How to configure Validation and Enrichment for receipts?

Resolution

  1. Enable below parameter Intelligent Configuration Manager. 
    • Application.Receiving.AllowReceiptValidation
    • Application.Receiving.AllowReceiptEnrichment
  2. Configure the Process Receipt Externally integration event.
