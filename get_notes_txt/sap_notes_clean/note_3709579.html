Symptom

You are processing an inspection lot in ERP, but EWM inspection is selected to process partial decision. Some part of the stock is accepted and other part is chosen to scrap to cost center, but the entered cost center is not valid.
As a result you may get an error message and a stuck WMQF queue.

Other Terms

stuck WMQF queue, /SCWM/QM_GM_CREATE, scrap to cost center, CC not exist, /SCWM/ACC011

Reason and Prerequisites

Due to code error.

Solution

Implement the correction.
