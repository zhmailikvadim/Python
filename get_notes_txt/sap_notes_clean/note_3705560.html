Symptom

You would like to have the communication arrangement "Price Condition Replication from SAP Business Suite" but you cannot find it, even after checking the new communication arrangements that are available.

 

Environment

  • SAP Cloud for Customer

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the C4C system.
  2. Navigate to the general settings.
  3. Open the communication arrangements.
  4. Select the button 'new' on the header bar.
  5. Search for the "Price Condition Replication from SAP Business Suite" communication arrangement and observe it's not present.

Cause

The scoping question 'Do you want to display the offline pricing price master data from SAP ERP?' is not reviewed in the C4C business configuration.

Resolution

Please scope the question "Do you want to display the offline pricing price master data from SAP ERP?" in your C4C business configuration.

 

Keywords

Scope, Scoping questions, Price condition, Pricing, Business Configuration, Missing
