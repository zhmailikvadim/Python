Symptom

  • Inquiry regarding the possibility of restricting standard Fiori apps at organization levels such as plants, company codes, etc.
  • Inquiry about restricting access to standard Fiori apps at different levels, including display, edit, and create functionalities.

Environment

SAP Fiori Launchpad

Cause

Resolution

Fiori Launchpad authorizations are not granular; users either have full access to an app and all its functionalities or no access at all.
