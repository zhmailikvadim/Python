Symptom

Changes are required to support the inclusion of the BPCA UUID in the balance key figures business event for Prenote Updates that were changed/assigned as a group based on the 'N Prenote-M Payment Item Matching' concept.

Other Terms

Business events, BKF, BPCA UUID on prenote

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Prerequisites:

3587249 - Prenote Changes: N Prenote-M Payment Item Matching

Solution

Import the relevant Support Package for your release. Use the transaction SNOTE to implement the attached correction instructions in advance.
