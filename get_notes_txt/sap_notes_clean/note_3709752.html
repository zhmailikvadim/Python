Symptom

The system's standard IBAN disassemble function is generating incorrect bank key and bank account numbers for Turkish IBANs.

Environment

  • Financial Accounting (FI)
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise
  • SAP S/4 HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

Steps to reproduce the issue:

  1. Execute transaction BP.
  2. Use "Business Partner (Gen.) role.
  3. Go to Payment Transactions tab.
  4. Click the button "IBAN" and paste IBAN number into the field IBAN.
  5. Press Enter, the system pop-up bank master window with Bank country/region "TR" and bank key.

For example:

  1. The IBAN "TR57 0006 2000 3950 0009 0700 33" is entered into the Business Partner (BP) Bank Details, the system disassembles it into a bank key "620003950000" and a bank account number "9070033".
  2. Verified IBAN format from external sources indicates the correct bank key should be "00062" and the correct bank account number should be "0039500009070033".

Cause

The IBAN is used more and more frequently in payment transactions and business partners increasingly quote the IBAN instead of national bank details.

Resolution

Turkish rules for bank details are very complex and bank specific. For domestic payments, a 9-digit bank number (4-digit bank code and 5-digit branch code) and an account number up to 16 characters long are used. However, the bank numbers embedded in the IBAN are 5 digits long and are not compatible with the bank numbers for domestic payments.

The following settings are recommended to maintain bank details for domestic payments:

  1. Bank Account Number Length 16, Checking Rule 1
  2. Bank Number Length 9, Checking Rule 4
  3. Alternative settings for international payments into Turkey only
  4. Bank Account Number Length 16, Checking Rule 1
  5. Bank Number Length 5, Checking Rule 4
  6. International and domestic payments – example
    IBAN: TR29 0001 0008 6040 2987 1050 01
    SWIFT/BIC: TCZBTR2AXXX
    Bank Number: 001000086
    Bank Account Number: 860402987105001
  7. International payments only – example
    IBAN: TR29 0001 0008 6040 2987 1050 01
    SWIFT/BIC: TCZBTR2AXXX
    Bank Number: 00010
