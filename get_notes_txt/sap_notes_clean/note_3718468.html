Symptom

In MD04 after activating "Display Customer" or "Display Vendor" in the user settings, the column did not appear and a wrong error message was displayed.

Other Terms

DSAG, PP, MD04; MD07; MD05; MD06; DISPLAY_CUSTOMER; DISPLAY_VENDOR; kuanz; lianz; T499U; mt61d; mdpsx; matnr; mt61d-matnr; mdpsx-mdmat;

Reason and Prerequisites

The customer/vendor data was not loaded when the user settings "Display Customer" or "Display Vendor" were not activated.

Solution

After activating "Display Customer" or "Display Vendor" in the user settings the system will load available data. The corresponding column or the right message if there is no customer or vendor available is displayed.
