Symptom

You view Country and Region details in Fiori and expect the system to display the correct region based on the selected country. However, the relationship between Country and Region does not work correctly in Fiori and an incorrect region is displayed. The same Country and Region link works correctly in the GUI.

This note corrects the behavior by ensuring that the Country and Region relationship functions correctly in Fiori and displays the correct region.
