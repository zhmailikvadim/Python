Symptom

Visiting Information facet in Account page is not seen for user

Environment

SAP Sales and Service Cloud version 2

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to the navigation menu.
  2. Go to Accounts.
  3. Open any Account.
  4. Observe that the Visiting Information facet is not available.

Cause

The user's assigned business roles do not include the account visit plan business service (sap.crm.service.accountVisitPlanService), so the Visiting Information facet is not displayed.

Resolution

  1. Check the user's assigned business roles for the account visit plan capability.
  2. Add the business service sap.crm.service.accountVisitPlanService to the appropriate business role for account visit plan.
  3. Assign the updated role to the user (or assign a role that already contains this business service).
  4. Have the user sign out and sign in again, then open an Account to verify that the Visiting Information facet is visible.
