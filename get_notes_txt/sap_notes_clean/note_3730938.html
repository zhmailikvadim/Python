Symptom

Freight Booking Confirmation Status is not changing to 'Confirmed with Changes' when adding notes in SAP Business Network for Logistics.

Resolution

The behavior observed is a Standard System Behavior. In SAP Business Network for Logistics , the Confirmation Status is designed to flag structural or negotiable modifications to the Freight Order.

The status "Confirmed with Changes" (or "Accepted with Change") is strictly triggered by changes to core execution parameters, such as:

  • Quantities

  • Prices/Charges

  • Dates and Times
