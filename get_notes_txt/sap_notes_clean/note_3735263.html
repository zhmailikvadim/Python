Symptom

  • Expense sheet is in Pending Supplier Review status
  • Expense sheet has not routed for approvals as it is in Pending Supplier Review status
  • Expense sheet should be in Pending Approval status but it is in Pending Supplier Review status instead

Resolution

Expense sheet will go to Pending Supplier Review status when Allow Supplier Review on Expense Sheet is enabled on job posting template

When this rule is enabled, the supplier will receive the expense sheet for review after the worker submits it. After revision is complete, time sheet will route for approvals and will follow any approval rules configured on the approval group
