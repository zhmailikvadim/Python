Symptom

As described in the regulatory change manager tool under https://app.main.regulatory-change-manager.cloud.sap/index.html#/detail/GSREQWE-5487, this SAP Note provides you with the relevant system changes to address the Glossary Changes for Version 2026/1, valid as of 01/04/2026.

These Glossary Changes have been published by the Belgian Social Insurance Authorities on their homepage Sociale zekerheid/Sécurité sociale/Soziale Sicherheit (www.socialsecurity.be).

Summary of main changes

  • Social Risk Declaration
    • Scenario UN05 => no major changes
    • Scenario AL01 => no major changes
  • DmfA => No major changes
  • Notification file => No major changes
  • Dimona => No major changes
  • Acknowledge receipt file => no major changes 
  • Bijlage 3 - Type bijdrage / Annex 3 - Type de cotisation
    New condition in function module HR_BE_WORKER_CONTRIBUTION:
    Contribution 836 is calculated for employer categories 257 & 357, in combination with  the worker codes 046, 047, 484, 487 & 495.
  • Bijlage 4 - Lijst van de verminderingscodes / Annex 4 - Liste des codes déductions
    • Reduction 1360 "Wage Ceiling Reduction":
      The wage ceiling amount - customized in table T511P with constant SVERE - has been increased from 85.000,00€ to 86.700,00€ as of 01/01/2026.
       
      Constant (T511P) Description Amount Valid from Valid to
      SVERE Employer Contrib. Wage Ceiling 86.700,00€ 01/01/2026 31/12/9999
       
    • Reduction 0611 "Pensioner Health Care":
      The reduction has been extended to Q4/2026:
       
      Reduction (T5BDR) Description Valid from Valid to
      0611 Pensioner Health Care 01/07/2022 31/12/2026
        
  • Bijlage 8 - Codificatie van arbeidstijdgegevens / Annex 8 Codification des données de temps de travail
    The following Service Codes have been added:
    • 36 FULL ABS. NON PAID EQ. TO SERV. MOTIVATION, ANNEX 06/01/2014
    • 37 FULL ABS. NON PAID EQ. TO SERV. NO MOTIVATION,  ANNEX 06/01/2014
    • 80 NON-RECOVERABLE OVERTIME HOURS EXEMPTED FROM SI CONTRIBUTIONS (already existing since 01.10.2015)
    • 81 VOLUNTARY OVERTIME - NOT SUBJECT TO CONTRIBUTIONS

  • Bijlage 27 - Werkgeverscategorieën / Annex 8 catégories d' employeurs 
    New employer categories 257 and 357.
  • Bijlage 44 - Maatregelen tot reorganisatie van de arbeidstijd / Annex 44 Mesures de réorganisation du travail
    New Reorganization Measures have been introduced. The list of fixed values of domain P12_REMES has been extended by 520, 522, 523, 551, 552, 553, 554, 567, 568, 578, 579, 584, 585, 701 & 702.
    As the Reorganization Measures 501, 502, 507 & 508 will be replaced by 520, 522, 523, 551, 552, 553, 554, 567, 568, 578 & 579, these new ones will be handled, as the old ones which e.g. have been used in the processing within the function modules HR_BE_DAQ_SERVICE & HR_BE_CAREER_INTERRUPTION_FT. 
    For the Reorganization Measures 584, 585, 701 & 702 nothing has been done in the processing, as the Social Security asks to continue to use the old ones 7 & 514.

  • Mealvouchers

    Amount of employer contribution

    The employer's contribution to the cost of the meal voucher may not exceed EUR 6.91 or EUR 8.91 (amount from 1 January 2026 - Royal Decree of 10 November 2025 – Belgian Official Gazette of 17 November 2025) per voucher. All vouchers with an employer contribution of more than EUR 8.91 are considered wages.

    Amount of employee contribution

    The employee's contribution must amount to at least EUR 1.09. All vouchers with an employee contribution of less than EUR 1.09 are considered wages.

    For the SAP Model wage types M911 (Meal cheques - Eer part) & M912 (Meal cheques: Ee part) in table Wage Type Characteristics (T511) the maximum and minimum amount is maintained. It turned out, that these amounts have not been maintained since the introduction of EUR on 01.01.2002. The maximum amounts are retroactively maintained as shown in this overview, based upon the information available for SAP. Nevertheless, these wagetype are model wage types, with the purpose to serve as models for the customer specific wage types.

Other changes

  • Workbonus constant LIMLW (table T511P) has been increased to 3.466,44 € starting from 01.01.2026
