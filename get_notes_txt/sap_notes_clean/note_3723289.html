Symptom

I noticed that after a new version of the Supplier Registration Questionnaire was created in SLP, some address details, such as House Number, were missing or changed. 

The supplier did not submit any updates or resubmissions. 

Why did this happen?

Cause

The Supplier Registration Questionnaire template includes both address mappings network.legal.addressExt.postalAddress and vendor.addressExt.postalAddress.

When network.legal.addressExt.postalAddress is mapped, any update synchronized from the Ariba Network (AN) automatically triggers a new version of the Supplier Registration Questionnaire.

Because AN does not have a dedicated house number field, its data model does not align one-to-one with SLP.

As a result, during remapping the house number may be removed or duplicated in the newly generated questionnaire.

This is expected system behavior due to inherent data model alignment limitations between AN and SLP.

Resolution

If you do not require address updates from the Ariba Network (AN), remove the network.legal.addressExt.postalAddress mapping from the Supplier Registration Questionnaire template and republish.

Note: If you keep this mapping, AN updates will continue to take precedence and overwrite changes during subsequent synchronizations.
