Symptom

When sending Service Sheet (SES) documents to the Business Network, they are failing in CIG with the error message: "ErrorResponse: Error: Service Sheet line {0} refers to an unknown PO line {0}". As a result, the documents fail to reach the Business Network.

Environment

SAP Business Network.

Cause

The service sheet documents were submitted prior to the release of Purchase Order (PO) version 2 (or higher). As a result, the system was unable to match the SES item with the PO because the corresponding item is only included in PO version 2, which was issued after the service sheet documents.

Resolution

To resolve this issue, a new SES document needs to be sent. Please note that attempting to resend the SES through CIG will not be successful. Issuing a new SES is necessary.
