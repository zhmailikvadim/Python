Symptom

  • In the notification panel of a custom approval process for sales quotes, a "Reject" button (icon: cross) is displayed for approvers.
  • Users are unintentionally rejecting sales quotes directly from notifications.

Environment

SAP Cloud for Customer

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login to the system.
  2. Click on the notification bell icon.
  3. Observe the presence of the "Reject" button and the inability to disable or hide it from the notification bell icon.

Cause

The "Reject" button is controlled by the standard framework, and modifications to its behavior are not supported by the system.

Resolution

  1. The "Reject" button is controlled by the standard framework, and any changes to its functionality are not supported.
  2. Buttons that cannot be hidden via Adaptation > Master Layout should be considered as new feature requests, as this functionality is not available in the current release.
  3. For enhancement requests or feature suggestions, refer to the SAP Influence portal:

SAP Influence portal  https://influence.sap.com/sap/ino/#/campaign/1868

  1. Alternatively, post questions on the SAP community forum where experts can provide guidance.
