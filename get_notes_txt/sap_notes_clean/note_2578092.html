Symptom

Reports using Semantic Layer Profitability Analysis are not consistent with those in Sales Analysis in SAP Business One Client.

Reproducing the issue

Prerequisites:

  • You have enabled SAP Business One Analysis.
  • You are using Business One Interactive Analysis in Microsoft Excel to connect to Profitability Analysisreports.

or

  • You are using Pervasive KPI and Dashboards based on Semantic Layer Profitability Analysis.

Scenario:

  1. Create an A/R Down Payment Invoice. 
  2. Create an A/R Credit Memo based on the A/R Down Payment Invoice created in step 1. 
  3. Create an A/R Invoice. 
  4. Create an A/R Credit Memo based on the A/R Invoice created in step 3.
  5. Open the SAP Business One Client standard Sales Analysis by Document report for a business partner (BP) in the appropriate time frame.
  6. Open the Interactive Analysis Profitability Analysis report, select Net Sales Amount LC, Document Type and filter by Business Partner in the appropriate time frame.

Actual behavior:

In the SAP Business One Client standard Sales Analysis by Document report for the BP, both the A/R Down Payment Invoice and the corresponding A/R Credit Memo are disregarded.

In the Interactive Analysis Profitability Analysis report, the Credit Memos are taken into account.

Cause

System behavior

Base relationships among documents are not considered in the Profitability Analysis model.

Solution

This is correct system behavior since Semantic Layer would just provide a view of all data. It is not meant to be 100% consistent with the Sales Analysis Report in SAP Business One Client.

In case you do want to filter these documents out, you can use the workaround below.

Workaround: 

Caution:

If you use this workaround, A/R Credit Memos based on A/R Down Payment Invoices will no longer be visible in the Semantic Layer Profitability Analysis. If you would like to restore the original view, you need to Reinitialize the company in SAP Business One Analytics Admin Console.

Workaround for View Profitability Analysis:

  1. Open SAP HANA Studio, and connect to the SAP HANA Server running SAP Business One.
  2. Expand Content → sap → {your schema name} → ar → case → Calculation Views, find SalesAnalysisFact, and double-click it.

  1. In the graphic, find Projection_14, which contains the content ARCreditMemoDetailFact, and click it.

  1. In the middle Details panel, find BaseDocumentTypeCode and click its gray circle icon to make it visible (the circle turns orange).

  1. In the right Output panel, choose Filters → Expression, double-click, and in the displayed Expression Editor dialog, modify the expression as:

    "TaxOnly" = 'N' and "BaseDocumentTypeCode" != 203

  1. Choose OK, and then click the Save and Activate icon at top right.

  1. Wait until the activation completes successfully.

 

Other terms

IA, Calculation View, Downpayment
