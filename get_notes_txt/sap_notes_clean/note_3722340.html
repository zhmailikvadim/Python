Symptom

The IR56E documentation specifies that the Name of Employer field supports up to 70 characters.

In the HHKCTXE0 report, the system truncates entries of 63 characters or more after the 62nd character.

This SAP Note extends the length of the "Name of Employer" so the full value (up to 70 characters) is captured and displayed without truncation.

Other Terms

HR_HK_IR56E2025, PDF Form

Reason and Prerequisites

This is an enhancement.

Solution

Do either of the following:

  • Apply the relevant Support Package.
  • Carry out the attached ManualSteps3722340.pdf
