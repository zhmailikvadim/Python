Symptom

If the absence 0380 (removal of SI liability) or 0381 (removal of SI liability unpaid) is used for the entire duration of an employment relationship (that is, if the employment relationship has not existed in the sense of SI law), the report Create DEUEV Notifications (RPCD3VD0) generates the following error message:

  • 736: Early termination of notification creation.

No DEUEV notifications should actually be created for this period and no error message should be triggered.
