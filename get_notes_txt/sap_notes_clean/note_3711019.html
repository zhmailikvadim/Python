Symptom

For Sovereign Cloud customers (DP & Security: SCDP), the Clock In Clock Out function does not behave as expected even when all mandatory configurations are correctly set. 

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite
  • SAP SuccessFactors Time Tracking

Cause

The Clock In Clock Out functionality is currently not supported in the Sovereign Cloud.

Resolution

This is a roadmap item and is currently being worked on by the engineering team.

Keywords

SCDP, Sovereign Cloud, SAP SuccessFactors, SF, Time Tracking, TTR, Clock In Clock Out, CICO, Time Event Types, Clock In Clock Out Groups
