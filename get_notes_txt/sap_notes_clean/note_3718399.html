Symptom

Dump occurs during the reverse of canceled quantities of line item where the trading contract has more than 9 line items with FLAT price fixation(pricing approach should be Non Hedgeable Flat).

Replication Steps -

- Create a contract with more than 9 line items and Save.
- Create the FLAT price fixation for all the items and maintain the pricing approach as Non Hedgeable Flat(NF).
- Do partial quantity Cancellation on one of the line item and save.
- Edit the contract, go to the cancellation tab and click on the reversal check box. Dump occurs.

Other Terms

Agricultural Contract Management; ACM; Cancelation Reversal; Non Hedgeable

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
