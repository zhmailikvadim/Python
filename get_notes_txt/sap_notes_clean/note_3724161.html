Symptom

In the process of adding a partner in the GTS license, the system currently only supports handling a single standard address, and cannot support business scenarios where a business partner has multiple addresses and any one of them needs to be selected. This results in regulatory compliance violations.

Other Terms

/SAPSLL/LCLIC_MNG, Multiple Addresses, Partner

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Apply correction instructions in the Note or upgrade to relevant SP.

Limitations:

If multiple partner address function is enabled in the system, only one address can be maintained for each partner function of each business partner in the license attributes.
