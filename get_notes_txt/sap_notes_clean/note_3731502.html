Symptom

  • Attempting to extend two production company codes to an additional ledger
  • System displays error: "Project must be used for production company codes" (Message No. FAGL_MIG223)

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Attempt to extend these company codes to an additional ledger via customizing.
  2. Observe error: "Project must be used for production company codes" (FAGL_MIG223)

Cause

  • The assignment of a new non-leading ledger to productive company codes was attempted directly via customizing.  
  • In S/4 HANA, for productive company codes, the system requires using the tool "Subsequent Implementation of a Further Accounting Principle" to ensure data consistency; otherwise, message FAGL_MIG223 is issued.  

Resolution

  • Do not assign a new non-leading ledger to productive company codes via direct customizing.
  • Use the tool "Subsequent Implementation of a Further Accounting Principle" to introduce the non-leading ledger.
  • Refer to the official guidance:  Help: Subsequent Implementation of a Further Accounting Principle
