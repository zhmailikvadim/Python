Symptom

Transport Footprint Calculation only considers old/main locations while supplier has various locations maintained.

Environment

Sustainability Footprint Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Maintain/Generate new/alternative locations in SFM.
  2. Maintain Transport Settings to assign the various addresses to their respective plants.
  3. Perform Transport Footprint Calculation.
  4. Observe that only old/main locations are considered in the calculation.

Cause

The issue occurred because Transport Activity Generation and Route Generation were not re-triggered after modifying the Master Data or Settings used in the calculation.

Resolution

  1. After modifying the Master Data included in the calculation, retrigger Transport Activity Generation.
  2. Re-triggering Route Generation.
  3. Perform the Transport Footprint Calculation again.

Keywords

Transport Footprint Calculation, Alternative Locations, Transport Activities, Master Data, Route, Generation, Retrigger, SFM.
