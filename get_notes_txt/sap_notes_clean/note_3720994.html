Symptom

In the process "Sending of 55670 from Responsible DSO to TSO" (DE_C_MDC_55670_DSO2TSO_SEND), a data provisioning error occurs when 55670 is triggered by a leading point of delivery that is a tranche point of delivery, preventing the message from being sent.

This issue is relevant for the market roles: DSO.

Other Terms

 55670, Settlement Unit, tranche PoD, tranche point of delivery, leading PoD, data provisioning, DE_C_MDC_55670_DSO2TSO_SEND

Reason and Prerequisites

 The error occurred because of incorrect data provisioning.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system provisions data correctly, allowing 55670 to be sent successfully for tranche points of delivery. You need to retrigger the process in the business system. You can verify the fix by checking that 55670 is sent without any error.
