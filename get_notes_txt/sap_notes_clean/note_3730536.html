Symptom

During the employee masterdata replication it is noticed that the following error is occurring within the SLG1 logs

  • Error: "Enter payroll area for period XX.XX.XXXX - XX.XX.XXXX."
  • Message No: HRSFEC_SERVICES137.

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll

Reproducing the Issue

Cause

  • The employee master data replication payload did not contain pay_group for the affected period, which caused the error in the mapping step.
  • The error was raised in class CL_HRSFEC_EE_MDR_ORGASSIGN_XX, method MAP_PAYGROUP.

Resolution

  1. Verify in Employee Central that the Pay Group (pay_group) is maintained for the exact period indicated by the error for the employment being replicated.
  2. If pay_group field is missing within the employees replication payload for that time slice, add or correct it and ensure there are no effective-dated gaps across the period. (The employees masterdata payload can be viewed through the report: "RP_HRSFEC_REPLICATION_STATUS")
  3. Re-run the employee replication and confirm that the error no longer appears.
  4. If Pay group is present in Employee Central for the period but still not included in the replication payload, capture evidence of the maintained data and the resulting payload and contact support for further investigation.
