Symptom

Three dots are displayed when custom background element's label is too long on the latest people profile. If you click on the three dots, a blank box will pop up.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central
  • Background Elements

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open any user's latest people profile.
  2. Navigate to a custom background element card.
  3. Ensure the card’s header label is very long.
  4. Note that there are three dots on the top right order, click on the three dots, a blank box is displayed.

Cause

This has been identified as a defect and will be fixed in 2026 2H release.

Resolution

This has been identified as a defect and will be fixed in 2026 2H release.

See Also

The fix for this issue is planned to be deployed on 1H 2026 release.

For release timelines, please review SAP SuccessFactors Product Release & Road Map Information.
