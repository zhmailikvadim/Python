Symptom

  • How to change the language displayed in SAP for Me
  • Change portal language
  • How to change the language setting
  • Wrong language is displayed
  • SAP for Me is not displayed in the correct language
  • What determines the language displayed in SAP for Me?

Environment

SAP for Me

Resolution

SAP for Me is displayed in English, French, German, Japanese, Portuguese, Simplified Chinese, Italian, Korean or Spanish (see KBA 3291921). If your browser’s language is not included in this list, by default SAP for Me will be shown in English.

To change the language setting within SAP for Me, do the following:

  1. Go to SAP for Me > Portal Preferences (or click the Profile icon top right and select My Settings)



  2. Under General, click the Language drop-down and select a preferred language (default is set to Auto-detect (browser preferences))



    lick Save Settings

  3. Confirm to reload SAP for Me by clicking Yes so the changes can take effect

To adjust your browser's language setting refer to the following:

Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge

  1. Go to your browser Settings > Languages
  2. Click Add languages
  3. Search for the preferred language and click Add
  4. Click the ... and Move to the top
  5. SAP for Me will automatically adapt the language if supported, or default to English

Mozilla Firefox

  1. In the browser address bar, enter about:preferences.
  2. Scroll down to the section Language.
  3. In Choose your preferred language for displaying pages click the Choose button. The Webpage Language Settings interface is shown.
  4. If your preferred language is not listed, click Select a language to add..., choose a language, and confirm by clicking the Add button.
  5. In the languages list, select your preferred language and click Move Up until it is listed at the top. SAP for Me will now be shown in this language.

Apple Safari

  1. On a Mac, choose the Apple menu > System Settings
  2. Click General (in the sidebar)
  3. Click Language & Region (on the right, you may need to scroll down)
  4. You can change the default language of your Mac by switching the sorting of ”Preferred languages” and move your preferred language to the top
  5. SAP for Me will automatically adapt the language if supported, or default to English
