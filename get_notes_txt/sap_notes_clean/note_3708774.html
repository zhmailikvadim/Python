Symptom

The payroll constant SILLO for the year 2026 should be 4147.10, not 4147.09.

This constant is used as the minimal social insurance allowance base.

SAP has always set this constant as the result of deduction of the 3 social insurance premiums that are paid by the employee, from the minimal wage (SILL2):

4806 - [4806 * 9.76%] - [4806 * 1.50%] - [4806 * 2.45%] =

4806 - 469.07 - 72.09 - 117.75 = 4147.09

Article 45 of the act of social insurance allowances, DU.1999.060.0636, speaks about a single deduction at the rate 13.71%:

4806 - [4806 * 13.71%] =

4806 - 658.90 = 4147.10

Other Terms

T511K, SILL2, SILLO, PLZLA.

Reason and Prerequisites

Error in the pre-calculated payroll constant SILLO for the year 2026.

The incorrect value was released with the SAP Note 3662703 - Payroll constants 2026.

Solution

The payroll constant SILLO for the year 2026 has been corrected in the customizing table T511K.

A manual correction of the constant is recommended - using the maintenance view V_T511K.

This correction will be included in the Support Packages listed below.

 

A similar error occurs for the SILLO setting for the year 2022: it should have been 2597.33, instead of 2597.32. However, the correction for the year 2022 will not be included in any Support Package, because such a small correction, after 4 years, should rather be avoided.
