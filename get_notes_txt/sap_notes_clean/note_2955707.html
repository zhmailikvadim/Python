Symptom

While accessing the 'License Compliance Digital Access' App, following info message appears:

"You do not have an active license."

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

Open "License Compliance Digital Access" App

Cause

The message appears if the license for materials 8005687 and 8005688 are expired or not acquired.

Resolution

Details about Digital Access licenses:S4HC Digital Access (8005687), and S4HC Digital Access Add Doc Cap (8005688)

Check the validity of license for materials 8005687 and 8005688 in the License Utilization Information.

See Also

SAP Help Portal - License Compliance Digital Access
