Symptom

In the Fiori Launchpad, when using App "Change PIRs":

After you press save, the sucess message comes up as popup instead of as a status message.  When pressing EXIT in the popup, the app does not return to the entry screen of Change PIRs, but jumps into the launchpad.

Other Terms

Change PIRs, Save, NO1ST, Navigation, MD62.

Reason and Prerequisites

Programming Error. Parameter NO1ST also causes entry screen within App not to be called again.

Solution

The correction eliminates the parameter NO1ST again. Then after save the App Change PIRs returns again to the entry screen.
