Symptom

You have upgraded to S/4HANA, where the old Treasury (Real Estate) Business Partner is obsolete. You would like to archive and delete all Treasury Business Partners in your system using archiving object TRTM_BPAR. However this is not possible for those partners for which indicator BP000-NO_CONV is set to X. Such business partners are not considered during the archiving run.

Other Terms

Treasury Business Partner, Real Estate Business Partner, RE BP, TR BP, TRBP, Treasury, Real Estate, BP001, NO_CONV, Archive, Delete

Reason and Prerequisites

TR BP is obsolete in S/4HANA, deletion must be possible independent of the indicator BP001-NO_CONV

Solution

Attached to this note you find a small report as PDF. By help of this report you can remove the indicator BP000-NO_CONV for all business partners in the system. This is not critical, because the TR BP is obsolete in S/4HANA anyway. After deleting NO_CONV indicator for all TR BP in the system you can execute archive and delete run using archiving object TRTM_BPAR.
