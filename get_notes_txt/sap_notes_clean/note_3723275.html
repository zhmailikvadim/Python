Symptom

When I access the purchase order and go to Create Invoice, after entering all the information and clicking Next, the error: !Required field appears. But there is nothing I can edit and it does not let me submit the invoice.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Sign in to the supplier account. 
  2. Go to Orders.
  3. Go to Purchase Orders or Orders and Releases. 
  4. Search for the order and access it. 
  5. Click on Create Invoice and then on Standard Invoice.  
  6. Enter all the required information, click on Next. 

The error !Required field appears in the Ship To section.

Cause

This happens because your buyer has the rule: Require Ship From and Ship To addresses on invoices active, and when the customer sent the purchase order, some information was missing in the Ship To section.

Resolution

To resolve this, your customer can disable this rule or submit a change order and add the correct information in the Ship To section of the order. You should contact your customer so that they can take action, since the information in the Ship To section appears depending on what the buyer sends.
