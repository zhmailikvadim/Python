Symptom

After integrating Commerce with OPF (Open Payment Framework), you noticed that the terms and conditions acceptance checkbox is missing from the payment page, compared to the OOTB non-OPF sample page.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

SAP Commerce Cloud

Resolution

In OPF, you have the option to set the Terms and Conditions to either Implicit (default)​ or Explicit​ mode. The help document below explains how to switch between the two.

Configuring Terms and Conditions
