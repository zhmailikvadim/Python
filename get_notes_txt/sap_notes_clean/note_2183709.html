Symptom

System is displaying an application error when the user initiates an out-of-sequence Cancel with an effective date the same as the New Business effective date.

Other Terms

AuthoritySuite, AS,OOS, Out-of-sequence, OOS Cancel

Reason and Prerequisites

Steps to Reproduce:

  1. Create a New Business transaction on today - 10 months, select a coverage and issue the policy.

  2. Initiate an Renew transaction and issue the policy.

  3. Initiate an OOS Cancel on today - 10 months (same as NB effective date), select "Pro-rata" as Cancellation Type and "Insured Request" as Cancellation Reason.

  4. Click on the "Next" button in the Cancel Policy - [Enter Cancellation Information] screen and verify the screen.

Cause:

The system doesn't take into account for equality (i.e. same dates) when comparing the Effective Date of the Cancellation transaction with the Effective Date of the New Business in the previous Term.

Solution

Make sure the comparison includes the of when the Effective Dates of the New Business and Cancellation transactions are the same.

Validity

This document is valid for:
