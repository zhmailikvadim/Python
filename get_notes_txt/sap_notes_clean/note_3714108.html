Symptom

During the attempt to send the invoice to the customer, the system raises the error “Email ‘Billing document XXXXXXXXXX’ could not be sent” in the Manage Electronic Documents app.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition
  • SAP Document and Reporting Compliance, Cloud Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Manage Electronic Document App. 
  2. Click on - Customer Invoice: E-Mail.
  3. Select the Source Document. 
  4. Click on Customer.
  5. Error Message appears. 

Cause

The domain of the sender email address is not maintained correctly.

Resolution

The following steps outline the resolution procedure:  

  • Follow below steps for configuring in Manage Your Solution app:
    1. Log on to the SAP Fiori launchpad.
    2. Choose Manage Your Solution.
    3. Select Configure Your Solution.
    4. Enter "Define Allowed Email Sender Domains for email outbound"(configuration step ID 101313) in the Search Input Text smart field, and choose Enter. 
    5. Choose Configure.
    6. Input the correct domain name of the sender email address in the configuration.
    7. Choose Save and exit. 

  • Incase while using SAP Central Business Configuration (CBC) :
    1. Log on to the project experience in SAP Central Business Configuration.
    2. Select the relevant workspace.
    3. Navigate to the configurations activity tab. 
    4. Choose configuration activity: "101313 - Define Allowed Email Sender Domains for email outbound". 
    5. Choose Configure.
    6. Input the correct domain name of the sender email address in the configuration.
    7. Choose Save and exit.
