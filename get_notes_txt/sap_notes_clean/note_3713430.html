Symptom

This SAP Note provides the form for the semiretirement registration (application) that is valid as of 1/1/2026 in statements.

Other Terms

Semiretirement, AMS, Austrian Public Employment Service, General Pension Act (APG) Sec. 4 Para. 2

Reason and Prerequisites

Legal change for Payroll Austria

Solution

The correction is provided by HR Support Package (via CLC if necessary).
You can implement an advance correction by using SNOTE to implement the automatic correction instructions and performing the manual activity (upload BC set and form).

Important additional information:

  • This SAP Note delivers only the registration form HR_AT_AMS_ATZN24 (ID 0328) for agreements with a start as of 2026.
  • The form for the change notification of agreements as of 2026 is delivered at a later point in time. See: https://app.main.regulatory-change-manager.cloud.sap/index.html#/detail/GSREQWE-7601
    • Additional corrections that are relevant in special situations for the forms are also integrated into payroll there.

Outbound Delivery Details:

  • In the form, for point 5 (page 4), the monthly gross remuneration (1) was divided into two different fields 1A and 1B depending on the semiretirement model.
    • 1A: For continuous models, the new statement wage type ATUB is introduced and supplied with the wage type /TUG (additional benefits and overtime are not taken into account). Refer to SAP Note 3718038.
    • 1B: For block time models, the statement wage type ATOG and therefore the wage type /TOG are still supplied (consideration of additional benefits and overtime hours).
  • In the infotype Semiretirement A - Notification Supplements (0662), the following new fields can be maintained:
    • Pension key dates follow-up date (PSTDT)
    • SR Agreement Subsequent Payment Date (ATVDT)
    • Regular retirement pension key date (RAPST)
    • Early retirement pension key date (VAPST)
    • Heavy Labor Pension Key Date (SAPST)
  • Corrections to the form compared to the form that was used for requests up to December 31, 2025:
    • In the "Employer" field, the form of address stored in T536C is no longer printed.
    • In the "Social Insurance Agency" field, the first part of the text from the view V_T5A1I_TR is now printed instead of (incorrect) the SI insurance body.
