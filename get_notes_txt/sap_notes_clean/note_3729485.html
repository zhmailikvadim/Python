Symptom

There is a requirement to implement a digital signature for outbound PDF documents (e.g., invoices, delivery notes) in SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

 

Resolution

As of Release 2602, the BAdI APOC_SIGN_PDF_WITH_CERTIFICATE enables the digital signing of output PDFs with custom certificates for countries supported by SAP localization.

The BAdI APOC_SIGN_PDF_WITH_CERTIFICATE is integrated into the SAP S/4HANA Output Control (CA-GTF-OC) component. It enables the digital signing of PDF files and their duplicates during the email output process, utilizing custom certificates for countries supported by SAP localization.
