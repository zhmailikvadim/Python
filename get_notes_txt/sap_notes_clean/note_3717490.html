Symptom

Unable to add a value-based filter for measures within manage analytics stories application.

Environment

SAP Integrated Business Planning - Manage Analytics Stories Application

Cause

Resolution

This functionality is not currently included in the standard IBP product design. If you would like it to be considered, please submit your idea on the Customer Influence Channel. Ideas with the most votes are reviewed by our Development Team and may be implemented in a future IBP release.

For additional details, see KBA 2479068 - IBP: How to provide enhancement / improvement requests or feedback for Integrated Business Planning product
