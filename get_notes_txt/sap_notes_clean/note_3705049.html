Symptom

In case of monthly periods of exposer we could see in TOC that the periods order is different in dependency on the access language of a user.

English - the order is correct

other language - the order is not correct if no - or not all - texts of the classes CL_CMMF_TOC_CEXIT and CL_CMMF_TOC_QUERY are implemented

Other Terms

Commodity Risk Management; Trade Order Cockpit

Reason and Prerequisites

Not implemented texts, program failure

Solution

 Implement the attached Correction Instruction or the related Support Package.
