Symptom

When you try to select a template when composing an email within an entity e.g. Case, you notice that the Favorite button (star icon) is not displayed alongside the Use Template option, so you are unable to add any of the templates as favorites.

Environment

SAP Sales and Service Cloud Version 2.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Logon to the SAP Sales and Service Cloud Version 2 environment.
  2. Navigate to Cases work list.
  3. Open any case.
  4. Click on Send Email button.
  5. Click on Add Template.
  6. See the start icon to add a template to the favorites is not displayed.

Cause

This is caused when the respective business service / app is not present in the agents business role.

Resolution

To ensure the business service is maintained, follow the steps below:

  1. Click on the User Profile.
  2. Click System Settings.
  3. Navigate to Roles under Users and Control.
  4. Open your business role.
  5. Scroll down to Business Services.
  6. Ensure the sap.crm.service.favoriteService is added to the business role without restrictions.
  7. Scroll down to App List.
  8. Ensure the Favorite app is also enabled.
  9. Click Save.
