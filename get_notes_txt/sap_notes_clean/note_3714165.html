Symptom

When creating EZ FI and EZ Legacy documents using EZ transactions, the fields POC Partner and Authorized Partner only allow selection of contact person master records.

This leads to inconsistencies between G-Invoicing Sales Order processing and EZ FI processing, and prevents agencies from selecting the same partner records across both processes.

Other Terms

EZ FI, EZ Legacy, Contact Person, POC Partner, Authorized Partner

Reason and Prerequisites

The EZ transactions are designed to use contact person master records for POC and Authorized Partner.
