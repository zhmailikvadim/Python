Symptom

There is currently no option to release the condition in F9K2. The standard condition that was created is also not visible at the account level.

Environment

  • SAP ERP Release Independent
  • Financial Supply Chain Management
  • FSCM In-House Cash

Reproducing the Issue

1)Access T-code F9K2
2)Open the bank account->Check the conditions

Cause

"XXX" condition created is not assigned to any condition group while the account is tagged to the condition group of "XXXX"(Interest-bearing)

Resolution

1)Remove the condition group from the account.
2)Maintain the standard condition for group "XXXX" to tag it to all the accounts having that group. This can be accomplished using transaction F98E, which is the recommended method as it will update all relevant accounts with the standard condition.

Keywords

IHC,F98E,F9K2, Release Condition, Standard Condition, Condition Group
