Symptom

In the Create Injury/Illness Log Entry app, when you add an aid/follow-up treatment, you can enter any date regardless of the date of the injury/illness entry. As a result, you can enter aid/follow-up treatment dates that are earlier than the date of the injury/illness entry, which causes inconsistent data in the system.

Other Terms

EHS, Incident Management, Aid/Follow-up Treatment, Date Validation, Injury/Illness Log, Consistency Check, F4026, EHHSS_INC_REC_OIF_V3

Reason and Prerequisites

The app doesn't enforce a validation to ensure that the Aid/Follow-up Treatment date is on or after the Injury/Illness Log Entry date.

No standard consistency check exists for this scenario, which allows users to enter logically incorrect dates.

Solution

Validation checks have been implemented to ensure data consistency between Injury/Illness Log Entry and Aid/Follow-up Treatment dates.
