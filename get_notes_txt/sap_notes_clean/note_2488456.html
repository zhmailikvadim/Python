Symptom

You use a trip provision variant for the public sector in Rhineland-Palatinate. You create a separation allowance event with an external stay. In the period of the separation allowance per diem, an absence with retention of the remunerated accommodations is entered and deductions for meals are entered on the same day.

This is the case, for example, if a several-day absence starts at 2 p.m. and breakfast and lunch are provided beforehand.

For deductions, the separation allowance per diem for breakfast is reduced by 15% and for lunch and dinner by 30% of the meals per diem, but always by at least the payment in kind value. The remaining 25% is the accommodations portion of the meals per diem.

If the payment in kind value is higher than the percentage share of the meals per diem to be reduced, the reduction can also reduce the accommodations share in the separation allowance per diem in the constellation described above (absence with retention of remunerated accommodations), which is incorrect.

Other Terms

pubsec_germany, Rhineland-Palatinate, trip provision variant DP, deduction, reduction, accommodations portion, trg_av, external stay, trip home

Reason and Prerequisites

Enhancement of SAP Note 1990734

Solution

Import the specified HR Support Package or implement the attached correction instructions.

As a result, the accommodations portion is always retained in the separation allowance per diem even for deductions on days with a partial-day absence and retention of the remunerated accommodations.
