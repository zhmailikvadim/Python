Symptom

  • Database/device is filled even automatic expansion policies on segment/device had been set.

Environment

  • SAP Adaptive Server Enterprise (ASE) 16.0 for Business Suite
  • SAP Adaptive Server Enterprise (ASE) 15.7 for Business Suite
  • SAP NetWeaver (NW) - All versions

Cause

Automatic expansion policy on segment/device will not work without the expansion threshold on the segment in the target database.

Resolution

Set the space expansion threshold by command:

sp_dbextend 'set', 'threshold', <dbname>, <segmentname>, <freespace>
go

For example:

sp_dbextend 'set', 'threshold', pubs2, logsegment, '100m'
go
