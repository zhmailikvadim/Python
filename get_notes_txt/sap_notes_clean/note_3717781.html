Symptom

In the Migration Cockpit using the Direct Transfer approach for the scenario SAP Apparel and Footwear Solution (AFS) to SAP S/4HANA, we are migrating the following objects:

  • Sales Orders
  • Sales Contracts
  • Purchase Contracts

To enhance customer understanding and make the selection criteria more visible, the pre-selection logic has been moved to the Application Selection step.
