Symptom

SAML Single Sign On (SSO) remains functional even when the certificate/keystore has expired. 

Environment

  • SAP Business Objects Business Intelligence Platform 4.x

  • All operating systems

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to BI  Launchpad: http://<webserver/hostname>:<Portno>/BOE/BI  or  https://<webserver/hostname>:<Portno>/BOE/BI
  2. Logins work fine with SAML SSO despite expired certificate on server.

Cause

This isn't specific to Business Intelligence (BI) , BI uses a third party library to do the SAML logon

Resolution

To verify the expiration date, you can use the command "keytool -list -v -keystore <keystore-filename.jks>" to check the expiration date.

This process is not exclusive to BI as it relies on a third-party library for SAML logon. 

Keywords

SAML SSO, Expiry certs, Expiration date , single sign on
