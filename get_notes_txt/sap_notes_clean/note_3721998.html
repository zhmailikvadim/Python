Symptom

Customers may identify functional gaps or improvement opportunities while working with SAP Cloud Identity Access Governance and would like to propose enhancements or new capabilities for consideration in future product updates.

Other Terms

IAG, CIAG, SIAG, Cloud Identity and Access Governance

Reason and Prerequisites

SAP Cloud Identity Access Governance follows a structured product lifecycle and roadmap planning process. Feature changes and new capabilities are evaluated based on formally submitted customer feedback and enhancement requests.

Solution

To ensure that enhancement requests are reviewed and considered by SAP Product Management, customers are encouraged to submit their proposals through the SAP Influence program.

SAP Influence is the official channel that allows customers to:

  • Propose new features or functional improvements
  • Vote and comment on existing enhancement requests
  • Provide direct input into SAP’s product roadmap planning

Enhancement requests submitted through Influence are evaluated as part of SAP’s standard product governance process.

The relevant Influence campaign can be accessed using the following link:
https://influence.sap.com/sap/ino/#/campaign/1739

For a complete and up-to-date overview of the currently available features, supported scenarios, and configuration options, please refer to the SAP Cloud Identity Access Governance Help Portal. The help portal contains the full product documentation and describes the existing capabilities in detail:

https://help.sap.com/docs/SAP_CLOUD_IDENTITY_ACCESS_GOVERNANCE?version=CLOUDFOUNDRY
