Symptom

• Error message: Navigation to this application is not supported occurs when clicking on the Verified Journal Entries card in the General Ledger Overview app.  

Environment

• S/4 HANA On-Premise.  
• S/4 HANA Private Cloud.  
• Transaction Apps.  

Reproducing the Issue

1. Open Fiori launchpad.  
2. Open the General Ledger Overview app.  
3. Click on the "Go" button.  
4. Click on the Verified General Entries or Journal Entries to Be Verified card.  

Cause

• The target mapping AccountingDocument-requestGLDocument2 is not assigned to the user's account.  

Resolution

  • When you click on Verified General Entries or Journal Entries to Be Verified, it should redirect you to the Verify General Journal Entries - New Version app. 
  • Assign the target mapping AccountingDocument-requestGLDocument2 to the user's account.
