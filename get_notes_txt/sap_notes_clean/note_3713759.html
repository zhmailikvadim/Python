Symptom

You run the Sibuin Reports - Straight-Line Method (transaction RFKRASL) or Declining-Balance Depreciation (transaction RFKRADEC) report for South Korea. And you have the Update Sibuin Amounts radio button selected. The posted Sibuin amounts for the fixed assets (which are updated through the BUIN, Deemed Depreciation Upload for South Korea (transaction RFKRAUP) report) are increased by a factor of 100.

Other Terms

South Korea, Sibuin Report, 100 times, RAIDKRA10, RAIDKRA07, RAIDKRA08, RFKRAUP, RFKRASL, RFKRADEC, KR

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a program error.

Solution

Install the relevant Support Package or implement the correction instruction attached with the SAP Note.
