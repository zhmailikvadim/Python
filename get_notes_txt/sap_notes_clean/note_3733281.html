Symptom

While raising an unblock request, Message R111 067 is raised during unblocking of Business Partner.

Other Terms

BUT_UNBLK_REQ, BUP_REQ_UNBLK, R111 067, R111 069, Unblocking request, BUPA_UNBLOCK_MD

Reason and Prerequisites

During the unblock request creation, the existence of the unblock request is checked. However, the unblock request with Approval status is missed.

This Note corrects the correction provided in the Note 3341942.

Solution

Implement the Note.
