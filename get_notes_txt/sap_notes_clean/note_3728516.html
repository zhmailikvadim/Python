Symptom

 The standard class determines the fiscal year using SY-DATUM(4), which assumes that the fiscal year aligns with the calendar year. In cases where this alignment does not exist, the derived fiscal year may be incorrect, leading to inconsistencies in data processing and reporting. As a result, transactions and calculations that depend on the fiscal year can produce inaccurate or misleading outcomes, especially when operating across periods that do not follow the standard calendar structure.

Other Terms

SAP Service and Asset Manager, Fiscal Year, S4MOBADDON-8383

Reason and Prerequisites

 To address this issue, the fiscal year determination logic was adjusted to no longer rely on SY-DATUM(4). Instead, the system now derives the fiscal year based on the year provided by the application in combination with the values configured in the relevant filters. This approach ensures that the fiscal year used in processing accurately reflects the intended business context, improving consistency and reliability without requiring changes to the standard class.
