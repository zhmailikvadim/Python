Symptom

When the tax report is run, neither the original marketing document nor its cancellation is displayed.

Environment

SAP Business One

Reproducing the Issue

Scenario:

  1. Go to Sales - A/R → A/R Invoice.
  2. Create an invoice with complete details, including the tax code and tax amount.
  3. Once the A/R invoice is added, right-click and choose Cancel to cancel the invoice.
  4. Go to Financials → Financial Reports → Accounting → Tax → Tax Report.
  5. Run the tax report for the period during which the invoice and its cancellation were recorded.

Expected behavior:

Both the original invoice and its cancellation appear in the tax report when it is run for the given date range.

Actual behavior:

Both the original invoice and its cancellation do not appear in the tax report when it is run for the given date range.

Resolution

Whether marketing documents and their cancellations appear in the tax report or not is determined by enabling or disabling the "Display Canceled and Cancellation Marketing Documents in Reports checkbox" under Administration → System Initialization → Document Settings → General tab. 

  • When the checkbox is selected, both the original marketing document and its cancellation are included in the tax report.
  • When the checkbox is not selected, neither the original marketing document nor its cancellation is included in the tax report.
