Symptom

In the process "Receipt of Deregistration Request for Gas (SUP View)" (DE_C_GELIGAS_DEREG_SUP), the system rejects a deregistration request from the distribution system operator via a negative APERAK message because of a point of delivery not exist error, even though the point of delivery exists on the contract end date.

Other Terms

GELIGAS, DE_C_GELIGAS_DEREG_SUP, APERAK, deregistration request, point of delivery, PoD, contract end date, proc key date, EP 1000010643, gas

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because of a logic error in the system's validation of the point of delivery.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system approves deregistration requests when the point of delivery exists. This fix requires emergency patch EP 1000010643 to be deployed on the cloud. You need to wait for the deployment of EP 1000010643. You can verify the fix by rerunning the deregistration scenario for gas and checking that the system approves the deregistration request when the point of delivery exists.
