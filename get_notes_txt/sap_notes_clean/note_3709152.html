Symptom

The customer wants to know the exact moment when an Firefighter ID (FFID) validity expires.

Environment

  • SAP Access Control 10.0
  • SAP Access Control 10.1
  • SAP Access Control 12.0

Resolution

The time component of the validity is based on the system’s configured time zone and the time zone rules where the Firefighter Session would be initiated. 

The validity period starts as soon as the system date became equal to or later than the valid-from date, and ends when it becomes equal to or earlier than the valid-to (until) date.
