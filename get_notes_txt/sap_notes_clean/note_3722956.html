Symptom

  • An error occurs while maintaining the Bank Account through transaction FI12 .
  • When executing FI12, selecting the respective Company Code, choosing the House Bank, and clicking on the option to Maintain Bank Account, the system fails to redirect to  NWBC for Bank Account Management as expected.
  • The system throws the error message: “FPM application WDA_FCLM_BAM_ACC_MASTER was started with an invalid or without configuration.”

Environment

  • SAP R/3
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Execute transaction FI12 
  2. Select the respective Company Code.
  3. Select the House Bank.
  4. Click on the option to Maintain Bank Account.
  5. Observe that the system fails to redirect to NWBC for Bank Account Management and displays the error message: “FPM application WDA_FCLM_BAM_ACC_MASTER was started with an invalid or without configuration.”

Resolution

  1. Check for any missing configuration in the Bank Account Management setup, specifically the "define basic settings".
  2. Make sure the setting of cash scope is "full scope".
