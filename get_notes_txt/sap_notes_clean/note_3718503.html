Symptom

When opening the Business Partner Overview, the interaction record's appearance differs depending on the logon language. In German, the description and notes are missing. In English, both are displayed correctly.

Other Terms

  • BP-TIMELINE
  • BP-Overview
  • OTR
  • CRMS4_IU_UI_FORMS_BP/BTQRACTDESC,
  • CRMS4_IU_UI_FORMS_BP/BTQRACTDFLTBODY
  • CL_CRMS4_IU_UI5_BP_TIMELINE
  • FILLUP_ITEMS_INTERACTION_REC
  • GET_FORMATTED_ITEM_TEXT (CL_CRMS4_IU_UI5_BP_TIMELINE)
  • GET_FORMATTED_ITEM_TEXT (CL_CRMS4_IU_TL_CUSTOMIZING)
  • GET_TEXT (CL_CRMS4_IU_MISC)
  • GET_OTR_TEXT (CL_CRMS4_IU_MISC)

Reason and Prerequisites

The cause of this is a faulty translation of the following OTR text objects.

Pakage: CRMS4_IU_UI_FORMS_BP
Alias: CRMS4_IU_UI_FORMS_BP/BTQRACTDESC

Pakage: CRMS4_IU_UI_FORMS_BP
Alias: CRMS4_IU_UI_FORMS_BP/BTQRACTDFLTBODY

Solution

Follow the steps from the attached manual correction instruction.
