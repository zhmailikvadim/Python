Symptom

  • An error occurs in the RTI Inbound queue in IBP with the message: "Could not change orders. SQL-Code: 689".
  • Reprocessing the queue or using the provided methods does not resolve the issue.

Environment

SAP Integrated Business Planning

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to bgRFC Monitor app.
  2. Check the error queue, and the error message states "Could not change orders. SQL-Code: 689 -1".
  3. The error queue cannot be reprocessed by clicking on Restart button.

Cause

The issue is caused by manual deletion of bgRFC units without proper handling of errors, leading to inconsistencies in the system.

Resolution

1. Deactivate your integration model on ERP side.
2. Execute a reconciliation job for the affect orders
3. Clean up the orders on IBP side via reconciliation results
4. Turn on your models again to send over the documents
