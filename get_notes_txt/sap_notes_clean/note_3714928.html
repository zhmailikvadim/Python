Symptom

How to view Disk volume details in HANA cockpit.  

Environment

  • SAP HANA Cockpit

Resolution

To view the Disk Volume information:

a. Navigate to SAP HANA Administration with SAP HANA Cockpit.

b. In the Monitoring or All view, on the Database Overview page, click the (More) icon on the Disk Usage card. 

c. Select Disk Volume Monitor.

d. For further details, refer to Disk Usage: Monitor Disk Volume

See Also

SAP Note 3672851 - Data volume information of indexserver is visible in SYSTEMDB from HANA Cockpit, but not visible in Volumes tab of SYSTEMDB from SAP HANA Studio
