Symptom

This KBA explains how to pay superannuation when the payday falls after the termination date.

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll
  • SAP S/4HANA

Resolution

According to the requirements specified by Comsuper, no contribution should be made if the employee is no longer in the organization on the payment date; however, this may vary from one super fund to another. Please refer to note 1632880.
As a workaround, use infotype 0015 to manually adjust the super contribution.
