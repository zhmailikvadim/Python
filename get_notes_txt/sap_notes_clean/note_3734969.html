Symptom

 In the processes "Decision Tree Diagram E_0608 (Registration of an Assignment)" (DE_B_GPKE_DSO_NIN_DTD_E0608) and "New Installation (DSO View)" (DE_B_GPKE_NIN_DSO), step 0230 overwrites any check result from the customer extension in step 0115.

Other Terms

New Installation, change of supplier 24, DE_B_GPKE_NIN_DSO, customer extension, check result, pod identification, step 0115, step 0230, step 0235

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because step 0230 ignored the customer extension result.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

Activate BC set according to your system:

S/4HANA 2021 SP07 service pack, BC set is /US4G/3_0_DSO_SP07_NOTE_3734969
S/4HANA 2021 SP08 service pack, BC set is /US4G/3_0_DSO_SP08_NOTE_3734969
S/4HANA 2025 SP00 service pack, BC set is /US4G/3_0_DSO_SP00_NOTE_3734969

After implementing this SAP Note, step 0230 triggers step 0235 when a customer extension is detected. You need to implement pod identification in step 0235. You can verify the fix by checking that step 0230 doesn't provide a check result, step 0235 is triggered, and pod identification is performed by step 0235 instead of step 0230.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.
