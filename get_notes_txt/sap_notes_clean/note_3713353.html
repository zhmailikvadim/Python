Symptom

The "Index rate table" column is missing in the Freight Agreement template and Calculation Sheet template.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA 1809 and Higher
  • SAP Transportation Management 9.1 and Higher

Resolution

As per SAP standard behavior, the “Index Rate Table” column is not available in Freight Agreement templates or Calculation Sheet templates in SAP S/4HANA or SAP TM. However, it is available and can be maintained directly in the Freight Agreement and the Calculation Sheet.
