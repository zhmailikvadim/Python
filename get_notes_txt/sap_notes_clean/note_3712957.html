Symptom

During the Revoke for the Phased Rollout functionality for Employees, some employees are getting deactivated because of an old company/personnel area assignment despite currently being assigned to a company/personnel area assignment that’s still included in Phased Rollout.

Other Terms

Concur Integration

Reason and Prerequisites

Wrong selection period was set for Revoke Job.

Solution

Please apply the correction instructions or upgrade to the newest support package.
