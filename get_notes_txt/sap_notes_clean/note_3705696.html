Symptom

You need to output a Corrective Tax Invoice made on or after January 1, 2026, against down payments posted before 2026.

The change is intended to support the procedures for the transitional period as outlined by the Federal Tax Service, referencing the logic from the previous rate change (FNS Letter No. СД-4-3/20667@) and ensure correct tax calculation for Corrective Tax Invoices for down payments posted in 2026.

Other Terms

FI_CORRINV_2021_RU, FDP_FI_CORRINV_2021_RU_SRV, FDP_FI_CORRINV_2021_RU, CL_FIRU_FDP_FI_CORR_INV_HELPER, Corrective Tax Invoice, DP19, Corrective Tax Invoice, Downpayment Corection

Reason and Prerequisites

Legal Requirement.

This SAP Note provides you with the relevant system changes to address the legal change: 

Correction downpayment invoice with increased VAT rate 22%

Solution

Install the appropriate Support Package mentioned in this SAP Note or implement the attached manual implementation steps.
