Symptom

Following error message occurs when executing F.05/FAGL_FCV:

 "&&&&/&&&&&& &&& belongs to business area X, not Y"

Message no. KI188

Environment

  • Financial Accounting (FI)
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA

Cause

The account assignment object is assigned to business area "X"in the system configuration. However, an explicit assignment to business area "Y" is made in the posting row, leading to the error message KI188.

Resolution

As recommended by the system, you may perform one of the following actions.

  • Make an explicit assignment to business area "X" . To do so you must set the "Business area" field as entry-ready in Customizing.
  • Enter an account assignment object assigned to business area "Y".
  • Change the assignment of accounting assignment object (&&&&/&&&&&& &&&) to business area "X". Note the document number and continue processing the document after changing the assignment.

Or make the message a caution or deactivate it entirely in Customizing. Use the Implementation Guide under "Controlling: General" and choose Change message control. Enter area KI and message number 188.
