Symptom

You are working in an A/R or A/P document that causes inventory movement when working with Items, after entering Quantity, using Tab to exit the Quantity field you get the following system message: 

Quantity defined causes inventory to fall below defined minimum; in "Quantity", enter valid value

Environment

  • SAP Business One
  • SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. You are creating an A/R Invoice. 
  2. You enter Item A and quantity 10. You tab out of the quantity field. 

The scenario is valid for other A/R and A/P documents that move inventory. 

Cause

  • Item A has a defined minimum inventory. 
  • You can find it in Inventory → Item Master Data → Inventory Data tab: Minimum. 
  • The quantity entered in the document will cause the inventory for Item A to go below the minimum level defined. 

Resolution

Decrease minimum inventory level or decrease item's Quantity in the document.
