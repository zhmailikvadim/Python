Symptom

Optional IOS portal package enterprise apps do not appear in the SAP Afaria Appstore client. Why ?

Environment

SAP Afaria 7.x

Cause

The SAP Afaria Appstore client does not support the optional installation of IOS enterprise apps.

Resolution

Either create and use an Afaria enterprise client or set the IOS enterprise applications in their SAP Afaria application policies to "required".
