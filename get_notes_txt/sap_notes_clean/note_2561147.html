Symptom

At least two goods movements for a sales or purchase order exist. Several price fixation terms exist. An automatic allocation has taken place. You go to the transaction CMM_ALLOC and assign manually the first goods movement to another price fixation terms as the automatic assigned one. After saving you realize that freed fixation terms have been assigned to another goods movements in VLOGP (DB table CMM_VLOGP) but not in CMM Allocation (transaction CMM_ALLOC in displaying mode or check DB table CMM_ALLOC_ITEM).

Other Terms

automatic allocation, quantity distribution routine, 100000

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Apply the note via SNOTE
