Symptom

When you navigate from apps such as Manage Sales Orders, Display Line Items in General Ledger to Sales Orders - Actuals, you will get an error "Attribute 'CDS_F_2CISDSALESORDER' no longer exists."

Other Terms

2CISDSALESORDER, Sales Order, Sales Document

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing mapping of Sales Document to Sales Order in the Technical Catalog SAP_TC_FIN_CO_INV_BE_APPS.

Solution

Please implement correction instructions or the corresponding Support Package.
