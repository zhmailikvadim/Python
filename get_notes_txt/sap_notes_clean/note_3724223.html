Symptom

  • The status of the invoice in Reconciliation tab is not getting changed from 'Accept' to 'None'. 
  • System is throwing error "Invoice is already part of GSTR3B summary file, Please complete the process in the preview tab".

Environment

Digital Compliance Service for India

Reproducing the Issue

  1. In DCS, navigate to Reconciliation Tab.
  2. Search for the invoice number.
  3. Attempt to change the status of the invoice from "Accept" to "None."
  4. Observe the error "Invoice is already part of GSTR3B summary file, Please complete the process in the preview tab".

Cause

The issue arises when the invoice is being included in GSTR3B already and later attempting to exclude it for GSTR3B returns by changing the status.

Resolution

  1. Open GSTR3B and select the GSTIN and reporting period. Ensure that the ITC Accepted Period in the Reconciliation screen is provided as reporting period in GSTR3B.
  2. Navigate to Preview in GSTR3B.
  3. Use the "Change Status" option. System will show the pop up saying ""Change Status" allow you to skip SAVE action and allow you to perform "Payment of tax"". Click on 'OK'. 
  4. Go to the Reconciliation screen in GSTR2B, change the status from "Accept" to "None," and save the changes.
  5. Regenerate the summary in GSTR3B.
