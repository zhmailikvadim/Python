Symptom


  • Rejector comments are not included in email notifications when new requests in the Bank Account Management workflow are rejected.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue


  1. Navigate to the Manage Bank Accounts Fiori tile.
  2. Submit a new request for approval.
  3. When the approver rejects the request and adds comments, the rejection email notification does not display the comments.

Cause


The email template FCLM_BAM_REQ_REJECTED_EMAIL does not have a designated field to display rejector comments.

Resolution


  1. The standard system does not support displaying rejector comments directly in email notifications.
  2. To view the rejector comments, navigate from the email to the Manage Bank Account application. Follow this path: View History → Revision History → Approval History → Notes.
