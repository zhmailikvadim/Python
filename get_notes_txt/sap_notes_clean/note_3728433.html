Symptom

When I am creating a Paper Invoice in Ariba Buying&Invocing (B&I), the field Service Period is not available. 

Cause

This is a limitation of the system. This field is only available for Supplier Invoices (Invoice Submission Method: Supplier).

Resolution

This functionality can be achieved through a customization process offered by SAP Services. To explore this option, you can contact your Customer Success Partner (CSP), who can guide you through the process and assess the feasibility of implementing this customization.
