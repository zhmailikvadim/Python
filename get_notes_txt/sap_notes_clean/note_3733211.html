Symptom

When running the Inventory and Balance Ledger (Format 3.17) report for DRC Peru, you encounter the following issues:

  • Column 13, which should only show Asset balances, displays balances for all accounts.
  • Columns 14 (Liabilities), 15 (Loss), and 16 appear empty, even though the accounts have balance data.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Document and Reporting Compliance service
  • DRC PERU

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Run the DRC Peru report “Inventory and Balance F3.17”
  2. Review the generated output and observe that column 13 shows balances for all accounts and columns 14–16 are empty.

Cause

The root cause of this issue is an incorrect configuration of the Financial Statement Version (FSV) hierarchy used to generate the report. G/L accounts are not structured correctly under their proper nodes (e.g., an expense account is grouped under an asset node). The report populates its columns based on the hierarchy structure and the G/L account numbering range.

Resolution

To resolve this, you must review and correct the structure of the Financial Statement Version (FSV) hierarchy used for the report. Ensure that G/L accounts are assigned to the appropriate nodes according to the logic the report uses.

The report populates the columns based on the following rules for the alternative G/L account:

  • Column 13 (Assets): Filled if the alternative G/L account begins with 1, 2, or 3, or if it begins with 5 and has a debit balance.
  • Column 14 (Liabilities): Filled if the alternative G/L account begins with 4, or if it begins with 5 and has a credit balance.
  • Column 15 (Loss): Filled if the alternative G/L account begins with 6, or if it begins with 8 and has a credit balance.

After validating and adjusting the FSV hierarchy to align with these rules, re-run the report to confirm the columns populate correctly.
