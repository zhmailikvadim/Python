Symptom

When SAP Ariba Sourcing is hosted in UAE data center and SAP Business Network is in EU data center, SAP Ariba Sourcing related transactions will not reach transaction tracker due to different data centers.

Cause

SAP Ariba Sourcing and SAP Business Network are hosted in different data centers.

Resolution

To enable standard document flow between SAP Business Network and SAP Ariba Sourcing, ensure that both solutions are hosted in the same data center region.

  1. Verify the hosting region of SAP Ariba Sourcing.
  2. Verify the hosting region of SAP Business Network.
  3. Align both solutions to the same data center region.

If the solutions remain in different regions:

  1. SAP Ariba Sourcing related documents from SAP Ariba Sourcing to SAP Business Network does not work.
  2. You must design SAP Ariba Sourcing processes to bypass SAP Business Network via the parameter SRC_DIRECT enabled in SPRO.
