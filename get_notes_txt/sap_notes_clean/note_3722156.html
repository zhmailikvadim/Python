Symptom

Users observe that creating or updating a Blanket Agreement does not change the item’s Committed or Ordered quantities.

Environment

SAP Business One 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create or update a Blanket Agreement for the relevant item and period.
  2. Confirm that the item’s Committed and Ordered quantities remain unchanged after saving the agreement.

Cause

Correct System behavior

Resolution

In SAP Business One, Blanket Agreements do not, by themselves, increase an item’s Committed or Ordered quantities.

A Blanket Agreement is a contractual framework (quantity/amount over a defined period) and a planning reference. It does not reserve inventory or create incoming supply; only transactional documents (for example, Sales Orders and Purchase Orders) affect those quantities.

For a comprehensive list of all documents that affect Committed and Ordered quantities, run the Inventory Status Report. For more details see KBA 3372506: How to check ‘Committed’ and ‘Ordered’ values.
