Symptom

In the Manage Mass Maintenance app, for the data type Fixed Demand and Receipt (06), the field corresponding to the Negative Indicator in the SAP GUI (transaction /SAPAPO/SPPFIXREQ) was confusingly labeled as "Demand Indicator".

Additionally, the associated filter field behaved in a way opposite to what was expected, selecting the Receipt option returned Demand records, and vice versa.

Other Terms

eSPP Simulation, Manage Mass Maintenance, Fixed Demand and Receipt, Fixed Requirement, Demand Indicator, Negative Indicator

Reason and Prerequisites

The naming discrepancy caused confusion. This has now been completely resolved by replacing the "Demand Indicator" label with "Negative Indicator", making it identical to the corresponding field in the SAP GUI transaction for Fixed Demands and Receipts (Fixed Requirements).

In addition, the domain values were incorrectly configured, which led to the filter behaving in the opposite way than expected. This has also been corrected.

Solution

1. The "Demand Indicator" label has been replaced with "Negative Indicator".

2. Domain values have been corrected so that an active negative indicator now corresponds to Receipt, and an inactive one corresponds to Demand.
