Symptom

  • Initialize business rule not populating fields on the requisition detail page.
  • Initialize business rule works only on Short Requisition Create Page.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Cause

The Initialize business rule is not supported on the job requisition detail page. This rule is only supported on the Short Requisition Create Page where it is required to populate the Internal Job Title, Form Due Date and Approvers.

Note: The Form Due Date field can’t be updated using the rules engine, even if write permissions are applied to the field.

Resolution

If you think this would be a good feature for Recruiting Management, please, submit your idea on our Influence page for an Enhancement Request. You can also check the following KBA for more information on requesting an Enhancement: 2090228 - How to submit enhancement requests for SAP SuccessFactors products.
