Symptom

 You are using FIXMG. After the BOP results are updated in ERP and the permanent order numbers are sent back to APO/PPDS (key completion process), the dates in APO are scheduled incorrectly.

Other Terms

fixmg, bop, key completion, key_completion_eket

Reason and Prerequisites

Fault in design of key completion process of the FIXMG orders after BOP. The semi change transfer/key completion is processed. There is change transfer processed with data filled for key completion process. Note set the process back to classic key completion as in case of BOP for STO's without FIXMG.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the Support Package relevant for your release.
