Symptom

App W0017 Change Maintenance Order deleted Material appears under "Materials" Tab. In GUI IW32/IW33 it does not appears anymore.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA 2021
  • SAP S/4HANA 2022
  • SAP S/4HANA 2023
  • SAP S/4HANA 2025
  • 2025 (Private Cloud)
  • 2025 (On Premise)
  • SAP ERP

Reproducing the Issue

  • App W0017 Change Maintenance Order 
  • Under Operation Data, Under Materials the deleted Material still display
  • IW33/IW32 Components Tab the deleted Material does not appears 
  • Edit, Display deletions -> On in IW32 show deleted Material

Cause

Data inconsistency. 

Resolution

Table "RESB" deletion indicator XLOEK is not set.

SAP KBA 2881684

provide more information and Correction Report.
