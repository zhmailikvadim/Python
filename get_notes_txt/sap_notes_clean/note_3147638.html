Symptom

  • While trying to access Cloud Integration (former: CPI) tenant on Neo environment, you get the below error:
    You are not authorized to perform this operation. Contact your tenant administrator for permission.

  • After this, following error is returned:
    Autorefresh was terminated due to multiple failed server requests. Refresh page to update data.

  • You were able to access your tenant. There is no change in permission. Even the tenant administrator could not access the tenant suddenly.

    Environment

    • SAP Integration Suite
    • Cloud Integration

    Reproducing the Issue

    Attempt to access SAP Cloud Integration tenant with URL (https://xxx.hana.ondemand.com/itspaces)

    Cause

    This is caused by a faulty tenant management node.

    Resolution

    To resolve this issue, the operation team for Cloud Integration has to restart your tenant. For this, open a case under the component BC-CP-IS-OPS and provide the following information. If all necessary information is provided, a developer from the operations team will address your request directly. 
    Note: If the required information is not provided when opening the Case, this will likely result in a delay in processing your request. 

    • Your CPI/Integration Suite tenant URL
    • A 15-20 minute time slot for a restart, including your time zone. (Example: Restart time: 28/08/2025 between 01 am and 7 am CET )
    • Cite this SAP KBA
