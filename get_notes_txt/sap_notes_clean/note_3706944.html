Symptom

  • Have Business Service Management Configured with event actions (email notification) in SAP Cloud ALM
  • Scheduled a maintenance service for system
  • The Event has been created and reflecting in our Event Calendar of Business Service Management page
  • Received an email from Business Service Management that the activity has been completed and another email for post-processing activity also being completed
  • Does SAP update the Business Service Management according to the timeline of the scheduled event or it will be updated after the completion of activity?

 

Environment

SAP Cloud ALM

Resolution

Business Service Management configuration of the event Sent email to allows multiple Condition settings to allow control over 'when' a notification is sent (options are Announcement, Before Start, Completion, Start, Status Update):
