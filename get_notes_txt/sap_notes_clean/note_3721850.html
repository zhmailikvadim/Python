Symptom

When using MTM versioned logistics data, certain records (such as inventory-related transactions like MATREX) may get processed even though price fixation is not applicable for them.

In such cases, the field:CMM_VLOGP_C_GRP-SPREAD_CALCULATION_GROUP will be blank (not maintained) in the pricing procedure.

If the system attempts to calculate Spread to Market for these records, it results in a runtime dump.



Other Terms

MATREX, spread calculation , CMM_VLOGP_C_GRP-SPREAD_CALCULATION_GROUP,

Reason and Prerequisites

To prevent dumps:

  • Implement a validation check before spread calculation.

  • If CMM_VLOGP_C_GRP-SPREAD_CALCULATION_GROUP is initial (blank):

    • Skip spread-to-market calculation.

    • Do not trigger MTM spread logic.

  • Ensure pricing procedure configuration clearly differentiates:

    • Transactions with price fixation

    • Inventory / non-price-fixation transactions
