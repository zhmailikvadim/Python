Symptom

I am unable to create additional entities on my account. When I go to the Additional Entities tab on my Company Profile, the Create button is grayed out.

Environment

SAP Business Network for Procurement

Reproducing the Issue

1. Click on the initials and select Company Profile
2. Click on Additional Entities
3. Create button is grayed out

Cause

The current account is setup as a child account under Connected Accounts

Resolution

You need to unlink the account from its Parent account and update details for Additional Entities independently.

See Also

To unlink your child account, please refer to How do I unlink the company profile of a child account?
