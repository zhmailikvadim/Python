Symptom

  • I am trying to update the number of positions in a Job Posting as a buyer user but the field is not editable.
  • The field 'Number of Positions' is not available for editing.

Cause

  • The Job Posting is not in Halted or Submitted status.
  • User Role Permission 'Edit Job Posting Number of Positions after Creation' is not enabled.
  • The Job Posting is a Named Job Posting.

Resolution

In order to edit the Number of Positions in a Job Posting:

  • The Job Posting must be in Submitted or Halted status.
  • User Role Permission 'Edit Job Posting Number of Positions after Creation' needs to be enabled.

Notice that, this field cannot be edited for a Named Job Posting and cannot be edited for Shift Manager Job Postings. 

If all the necessary conditions are met:

  • Log in to your SAP Fieldglass account
  • Open the Job Posting in question 
  • Click Actions > Click Edit Job Posting
  • Submit the Number of Positions required > Update
