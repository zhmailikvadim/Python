Symptom

Why are suppliers now receiving Supplier Qualification notifications that weren’t sent before?

Cause

The Supplier Qualification questionnaire has been configured to expire, which triggers notifications to be sent to suppliers according to this configuration.

Resolution

Update the configuration in the questionnaire template by setting the Can expire? option to No
