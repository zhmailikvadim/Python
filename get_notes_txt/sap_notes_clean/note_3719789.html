Symptom

When adding a Landed Cost and in the process flow also adding a Business Partner (BP) of type Customer, you receive the following error message:

Selected business partner is not a vendor.  Field: Vendor

Environment

  • SAP Business One
  • SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Purchasing - A/P → Landed Costs
  2. Tab out of the Broker field and click New button, Business Partner Master Data window opens. Create a Customer and click Add button. 

Cause

Only Vendor type BPs can be used in Landed Costs documents. 

Resolution

When adding a BP in this manner, it must be of Vendor type. 

This is standard behavior.
