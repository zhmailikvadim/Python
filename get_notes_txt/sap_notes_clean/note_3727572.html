Symptom

Why I am Seeing Error message: No space left on device when processing a transaction via SAP Integration Suite Managed Gateway for Spend Management and SAP Business Network?

Environment

SAP Integration Suite, managed gateway for spend management and SAP Business Network

Resolution

  1. No space error could indicate underlying performance or resource constraint issue. To resolve reprocess the Transaction from Transaction Tracker
  2. If the error: No space left on device occurs frequently, it is recommended to contact SAP support for further investigation and assistance.
