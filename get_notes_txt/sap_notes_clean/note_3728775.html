Symptom

The mainstream maintenance period of SAP Liquidity Management for Banking 6.3 expires on October 31, 2026.

Other Terms

Liquidity Management, LMS, mainstream maintenance, customer-specific maintenance, maintenance end

Solution

The mainstream maintenance period for SAP Liquidity Management for Banking 6.3 concludes on October 31, 2026. Starting November 1, 2026, the product will enter customer-specific maintenance.

For further information on customer-specific maintenance see SAP Note 52505 - Support after end of mainstream maintenance or extended maintenance - SAP for Me.

Alternative go-to product: Fioneer Intraday Liquidity Management Solution.
