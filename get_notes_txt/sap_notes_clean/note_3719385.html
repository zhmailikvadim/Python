Symptom

For GST India, when sales export invoices or the proforma invoices are posted in foreign currency, the amount in local currency is to be stored in AFD fields in database table GSLOG_LFD.

The local currency details are stored at the Billing item level.

Other Terms

GSLOG_LFD, Local currency, DRC_INVC_CVCRCY, NETAMT,

TAXAMT, LOGIN_DRC_LOCLAMT_LFD, LOGIN_EXP_RECD_TYPES_DATA_LFD, BDI, GST India

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Corrections.

Pre-requisite DDIC note: 3650718 - IN SD Billing Foreign currency : Enabling invoice amount in local currency DDIC pre-requisite

Solution

Please implement the correction instructions.
