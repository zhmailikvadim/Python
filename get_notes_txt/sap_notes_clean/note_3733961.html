Symptom

  • Activities that are visible in Planning and Dispatching board do not appear on the Service Map.

Environment

  • SAP Field Service Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in to the Shell Application.
  2. Open Planning and Dispatching app and go to Service Map.
    Expected Behavior:
  3. Activities on the planning and dispatching board are visible on the service map.
    Actual Behavior:
  4. Observe that activities visible on the dispatching board are missing on the map.

Cause

The service call status mapping is incorrectly configured so that the "Cancelled" status is mapped for the "Released" status, causing activities to be treated as cancelled and excluded from the map.

Resolution

  1. Review the status mapping for service calls and activities in the settings and configuration app under shell.
  2. Remove the mapping "Released" to "Cancelled" and ensure that "Released" is not mapped for "Cancelled". Optionally, leave the "Cancelled" status unmapped.
  3. Save the changes.
  4. Refresh the Service Map and verify that:
  5. Current activities are visible.
