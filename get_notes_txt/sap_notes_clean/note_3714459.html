Symptom

  • The freight condition type with calculation type C - Quantity is not functioning as expected.
  • Instead of calculating based on a fixed value per piece, the system calculates the freight condition based on the weight of the product.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a Sales Document and enter the necessary information.
  2. Observe the freight condition type.

Cause

  • In the pricing procedure, the condition base of the freight condition is set as 13-Net Weight, causing the system to pick the net weight as the condition basis.
  • As a result, in the sales document, the net weight of the product is used for the calculation instead of the quantity.

Resolution

  1. Access the configuration activity "Set Pricing Procedure" and locate the related pricing procedure.
  2. Clear the alternative condition basis of freight condition in the pricing procedure.
  3. Save the changes.

Keywords

Freight Condition Type, Pricing Procedure, Set Pricing Procedure, Calculation Type, Net Weight, Quantity, Freight Calculation, Alternative Condition Basis.
