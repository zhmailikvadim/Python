Symptom

As described in the regulatory change manager tool under Revised statutory minimum wage rate, this SAP Note provides you with the relevant system changes:

  • Delimiting the Statutory Minimum Wage per Hour (HKSMW) constant to update the minimum hourly wage to HKD 43.1, effective from May 1, 2026.

Other Terms

 T511P, HKSWM

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a legal change.

Solution

Do either of the following:

  • Import the relevant Support Package into your system. 
    Ensure that you have the correct constant entry (HKSMW) in view V_T511P.
  • Follow the steps in the attachment "ManualSteps3734059.pdf".
