Symptom

In the process "Start of Supply for Electricity (DSO View)" (DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SOS_REG), when a negative APERAK is received while sending deregistration to the old supplier in step 1300-0200 of the cloud process DE_C_GPKE_DSO_SOS_REG, and you refetch the data through a remote exception in the business system, incomplete data is sent through step 0850 of process DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SOS_REG.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

GPKE, Start of Supply for Electricity, DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SOS_REG, DE_C_GPKE_DSO_SOS_REG, BM_GPKE_DSO_SOS_DEREG_REQ, /US4G/CL_PRST_DSO_DEREG_DATA, UBD, APERAK, refetch scenario, deregistration, old supplier

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because step 0850 of process DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SOS_REG sent only supply scenario UBD data.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, step 0850 sends complete data, and the system creates a successful TDoc for business message BM_GPKE_DSO_SOS_DEREG_REQ. You need to activate the following BC set and trigger the refetch scenario through a remote exception. You can verify the fix by checking that the system sends the correct data to deregister the old supplier.
