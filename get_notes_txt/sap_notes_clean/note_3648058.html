Symptom

There are unnecessary scroll bars appearing on the product details page; this phenomenon only occurs in Windows.

Reason and Prerequisites

Unnecessary CSS setting: overflow: scroll for editorarea tabpanels. This will cause the scroll bar to keep appearing when browsing on Windows.

Solution

Change editorarea tabpanels style from overflow: scroll to overflow: auto.
