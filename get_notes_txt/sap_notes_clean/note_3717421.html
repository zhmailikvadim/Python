Symptom

Rejection button is missing for DCDs related to Service Order/Service Contract/Subscription Order in the Manage Documented Credit Decisions App (F5587)

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • Financial Accounting (FI)

Resolution

The rejection button functionality for Documented Credit Decisions (DCDs) related to Service Orders, Service Contracts or Subscription Orders is currently not supported in the Fiori interface. This functionality is planned for future development but is unavailable at present.

See Also

  • 3608572 - Reject Option is not available in Manage Documented Credit Decisions (F5587) in SAP S/4HANA
