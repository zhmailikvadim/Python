Symptom

While running employee master data for a Romanian employee, the following replication error raises: 

  • Wrong:  <Form of addr> or <Birth date> or <ID num (first digit)> - Message No. HRBASRO006

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Integration to SAP Business Suite
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central integration with SAP Human Capital Management for SAP S/4HANA
  • Employee Master data replication
  • Business Integration Builder (BIB)

Cause

The system validates the national ID (PERID) against the gender (GESCH) and birthdate (GBDAT) in Function HR_RO_PNC. An inconsistency in this data will lead to the error. 

Resolution

  1. Verify the employee's gender, birthdate and national ID to ensure it is compliant. See following examples: 
    • For gender "male" (GESCH = 1) and born before the year 2000, the first digit of the national ID must be "1".
    • For gender "female" (GESCH = 2) and born before the year 2000, the first digit of the national ID must be "2".
  2. Correct the data in Employee Central to the real values.
