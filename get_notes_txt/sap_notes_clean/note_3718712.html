Symptom

I would like to know what is the transaction number for a PurchaseOrderExportRequest or a PurchaseOrderChangeExportRequest on Managed Gateway for Spend&Network (ISMG) and I would like to check this in SAP ERP

Resolution

In SAP ERP, you can check the transaction number from a Purchase Order (PO) that originated from the SAP Ariba Buying and Invoicing via ISMG integration by checking the field EKKO-UNSEZ from a specific PO. 

  1. Go to SE16 or SE16N to view a table
  2. Choose table EKKO
  3. Filter for Purchasing Document (EBELN) of the PO you want
  4. Execute
  5. Check what is the UNSEZ field value

See Also

The table ARBCIG_POHISTORY in SAP ERP may also be helpful in this search of Purchase Order document numbers and their correlation between SAP Ariba Buying and Invoicing, ISMG and SAP ERP.
