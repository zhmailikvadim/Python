Symptom

Manage Cost Centers (Version 3) app not visible in the system even after the necessary roles and catalogs are assigned.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Resolution

IAM Apps are not Automatically Activated.

If IAM apps are added to a business catalog with a new release, they are no longer automatically activated. Even if the business catalog is already in use, these IAM apps remain inactive until it is choosen to enable them.

    • To explore IAM apps, open the Display IAM Apps app.
    • To check if a business catalog supports IAM apps, open the Business Catalogs app and use the Supports IAM Apps filter.
    • To assign IAM apps, use the Maintain Business Roles app. For multiple roles, the Mass Change Wizard within the same app supports bulk addition and removal of IAM apps.
    • Manage authorizations for business users more flexibly by activating or deactivating individual IAM apps after an upgrade according to your business requirements.
