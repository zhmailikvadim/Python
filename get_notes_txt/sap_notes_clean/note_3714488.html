Symptom

When clicking Selection, the following error message is displayed: "No bill of exchange liability accounts found for selection".

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the app Returned Bills of Exchange Payable.
  2. Attempt to process a bill of exchange liability account selection.
  3. The following error message is displayed: "No bill of exchange liability accounts found for selection". 

Cause

The bank subaccount was not maintained  in the configuration activity "Set Up Bank Determination for Payment Transactions" (ID: 101045).

Resolution

Please ensure that the entered G/L account is maintained in the bank subaccount under the configuration activity 'Set Up Bank Determination for Payment Transactions' (ID: 101045).

Keywords

bill of exchange, liability accounts, returned bills of exchange payable, error message FW023, G/L account, bank subaccount
