Symptom

  • Where is the roadmap for Crystal Reports?

  • What are the future plans for Crystal Reports?

Environment

  • SAP Crystal Reports 2020
  • SAP Crystal Reports 2025      

Resolution

  • What is the Crystal Reports Road Map?
              
    The roadmap includes SAP Crystal Reports, and Developer Resources, and describes how the product capabilities are planned to progress over time. The road map provides information on the recent innovations, and planned innovations for the product.
               
          
  • Where is the Crystal Reports Road Map?
            
    The Roadmap for Crystal Reports is included in the SAP Analytics On-Premise Solution Road Map.
       
    1. Go to the SAP Road Map Explorer for SAP Analytics On-Premise Solution
        
    2. Login, and it will show the road map for SAP BusinessObjects BI platform, which includes Crystal Reports.
