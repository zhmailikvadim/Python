Symptom

In the process "Calculation of Energy Amounts (MOS MaLo)" (DE_B_MET_ENERGY_AMOUNT_CALC_MOSMALO), a clarification case was raised via standard exception E281 (/US4G/PROC_METERING) in step 0350. However, the energy quantity was not recorded in the message of the clarification case.

This issue is relevant for the market role: MOS.

Other Terms

 MET, DE_B_MET_ENERGY_AMOUNT_CALC_MOSMALO, exception E281, /US4G/PROC_METERING, /US4G/CL_UBD_CALC_FORMULA, Calculate Energy Amount, clarification case, energy quantity

Reason and Prerequisites

 The error occurred because the calculated energy quantity was not filled in the message.

Solution

 Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system records the energy quantity in the message of the clarification case. You need to retrigger the process. You can verify the fix by checking that the exception is raised with the calculated quantity.

The error message is shown in English only. If you would like to translate it, proceed as follows:

LIMU MESS /US4G/PROC_METERING281

Package: /US4G/PROCESS_METERING

English Text: Calculated energy quantity &1 for &2 cannot be less than 0
German Text: Berechnete Energiemenge &1 für &2 darf nicht kleiner als 0 sein

  8. Choose Back (F3) to leave the translation editor.
