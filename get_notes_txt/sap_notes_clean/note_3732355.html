Symptom

After initial sync in SAP Service and Asset Manager, Service Order item Business Partners are not displayed, although they exist in the backend. Header-level Business Partners are shown correctly.

After adding a Business Partner on item level and syncing again, all backend Business Partners become visible.

Other Terms

Programming Error.
