Symptom

Child and Child-Rearing Support Fund Contributions are calculated for an employee who is exempted in IT0140.
“Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.“

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create an exemption record in IT0140 effective from 2026/04/01.
  2. Run the monthly payroll for period 2026/05.
  3. In the payroll results, the SI contributions /301 and /303 are not calculated, but wage types /607, /608, and /609 are still calculated.

Cause

The customer is using a custom rule (Zxxx), and the rule was not updated according to the correction delivered in SAP Note 3709289.

Resolution

In the standard SAP solution, rules J141 and J14X handle the 免除 from SI premium.
Please update your custom rule based on the rules J141 and J14X in SAP Note 3709289 to correctly process the 免除 case.
