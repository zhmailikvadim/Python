Symptom

A backordered requirement is not selected by the Backorder Processing (BOP) filter when the "Backorders (Date/Time)" option is active.

Other Terms

Filter, /SAPAPO/BOP, "ATP, BOP: Backorders", GF_BCKOR, GF_CNFWO

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisite:

  • BOP variant is using a filter variant with the option "Backorders (Date/Time)" is activated in the "Confirmation Situation" tab.

Solution

Any requirement with a material availability in the past (earlier than today) will be marked as a backorder when the "Backorders (Date/Time)" option is active. The other flags in the "Confirmation Situation" will be subsequently evaluated.

Implement the attached correction instructions.
