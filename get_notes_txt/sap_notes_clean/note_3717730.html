Symptom

In the Manage Cash Concentration app (App ID F3265), navigating to the detail page section does not display Bank account details.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA 1809 onwards

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Manage Cash Concentration app (App ID F3265).
  2.  Click on Go to display data.
  3. Navigate to the detail page section by selecting any entry.
  4. Observe that Bank Accounts details are not displayed.

Cause

Cash pools are not linked to their respective bank account numbers.

Resolution

  1. Open the Manage Bank Accounts app. 
  2. Assign the cash pools to the respective bank accounts.
