Symptom

A master cost center assignment or profit center assignment is shown in the Controlling facet but the name of the master cost center or profit center is missing in the assignment. This can be the case if the assignment becomes valid first in the future or is already outdated and the master cost center or profit center is not valid for the current date.

Other Terms

S/4 HANA Defense & Security, Manage Force Elements, F4112, Controlling, Profit Center, Master Cost Center.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the correction instruction.
