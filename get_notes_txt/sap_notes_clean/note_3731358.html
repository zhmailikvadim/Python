Symptom

  • The below error appears when trying to open a webi document:

       "Action cannot be performed" 

  • The document contains a duplicated query.

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 4.3 SP4 Patch 10
  • Web Intelligence (WebI)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login to BI Launchpad. 
  2. Open a webi document containing a duplicate query.

>> Error: Action cannot be performed

Cause

This issue is caused by a regression in the product, however there is no official bug logged for it.

Resolution

Issue does not occur in SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform BI 4.3 SP 04 Patch 12 and higher versions. 

Keywords

unable, corrupted, corruption
