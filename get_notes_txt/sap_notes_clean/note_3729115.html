Symptom

When performing the rebase in the parallel line system, the error messages below occur:

  • Error: Maintain Condition Exclusion for Pricing Procedures - Table/view V_T684S: higher-level entry *** is missing.
  • Error: Set Pricing Procedure Determination - Entry *** does not exist in T683 (check the input).

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Perform rebase on the Parallel Line.
  2. Observe the activation errors listed in the symptom section.

Cause

If the modification in Maintain Condition Exclusion for Pricing Procedures and Set Pricing Procedure Determination are involved when performing the rebase in the parallel line system, the double maintenance for the configuration activity-Set Pricing Procedures is needed.

If the entries in tables relevant for pricing procedures in Maintain Condition Exclusion for Pricing Procedures and Set Pricing Procedure Determination are missing, the errors will occur.

Resolution

The workaround below can be considered:

  1. Double maintain the missing entries via the configuration activity 101117 - Set Pricing Procedures in Parallel Line system for Set Pricing Procedure Determination and Maintain Condition Exclusion for Pricing Procedures.
  2. Reactivate the failed activity.

Keywords

Activation Errors, Parallel Line Rebase, Pricing Procedures, Missing Entries, SSCUI 101117.
