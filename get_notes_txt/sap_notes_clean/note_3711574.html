Symptom

  • An error occurs when attempting to post a change in EWM directly to move a product from plant A to plant B.
  • The error message displayed is: "Batch &1 does not exist in plant &2." Message no. M7042

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP ERP Central Component (ECC)
  • SAP Service Parts Management (LO-SPM)
  • Logistics Execution (LE)
  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management (SCM-EWM)

Cause

The issue occurs because the batch master data is not created in the target plant, preventing the post change from being successfully posted.

Resolution

Immediately integrate the batch master data with ERP after creating the batch in EWM to ensure consistency in stock data and prevent discrepancies between systems.
