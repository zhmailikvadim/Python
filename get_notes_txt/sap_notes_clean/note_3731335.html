Symptom

  • Values from KNA1-STCDT and LFA1-STCDT are not present in the BusinessPartnerSUITEBulkReplicateRequest_Out payload.
  • KNA1-STCDT and LFA1-STCDT are not getting replicated via business partner outbound replication.
  • No specific error messages were provided.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Maintain STCDT in customer (KNA1) and/or supplier (LFA1) master data.
  2. Replicate the Business Partner using DRFOUT with BusinessPartnerSUITEBulkReplicateRequest_Out.
  3. Observe that the outbound XML does not contain STCDT, and the target system does not update KNA1-STCDT/LFA1-STCDT.

Cause

STCDT is not transmitted directly by BusinessPartnerSUITEBulkReplicateRequest_Out; it is derived by CVI from a Business Partner tax category (for example, AR1A) under Identification > Tax Numbers, and mapped to STCDT for country AR.

Resolution

  1. Maintain a Business Partner tax number using the appropriate tax category (for example, AR1A) under Identification > Tax Numbers for the Business Partner.
  2. Ensure the Business Partner’s country is set to AR so that CVI can derive STCDT from the tax type (such as AR1A).
  3. Replicate the Business Partner again via BusinessPartnerSUITEBulkReplicateRequest_Out; the tax numbers are sent in the payload.
  4. Allow standard CVI mapping to populate KNA1-STCDT and/or LFA1-STCDT in the target system. 
  5. If additional adjustments are needed, BAdIs MDG_SE_BP_BULK_REPLRQ_OUT and MDG_SE_BP_BULK_REPLRQ_IN for outbound/inbound XML mapping can be used, and/or BAdI CVI_CUSTOM_MAPPER to influence CVI mapping.
