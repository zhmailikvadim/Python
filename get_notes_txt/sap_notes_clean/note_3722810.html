Symptom

Manual pricing conditions added in a Sales Quote are not copied to the Sales Order automatically during order creation.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Cloud Version 2
  • SAP Sales Cloud and SAP Service Cloud Version 2

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Sales Quote object work list.
  2. Open a Sales Quote A.
  3. Check the pricing procedure as B.
  4. Add a manual pricing condition C.
  5. Simulate the pricing.
  6. Create a Sales Order from the Quote A.
  7. Simulate the pricing for the Sales Order, now pricing procedure is also B.
  8. Observe that the manual pricing condition C is not copied from the Sales Quote to the Sales Order.

Cause

  • Manual pricing conditions are copied only when creating a follow-up Sales Order from a Sales Quote, not during simulation.
  • The pricing procedure of the follow-up sales order was not the same as the pricing procedure of the sales quote during order creation, which prevented the manual conditions from being copied.

Resolution

  1. Ensure that the pricing procedure associated with the document types of the Sales Order and the Sales Quote is the same.
  2. Maintain a pricing determination rule in SAP Sales Cloud Version 2 so that the same pricing procedure is determined on the Sales Order during creation.
