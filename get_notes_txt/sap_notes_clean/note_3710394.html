Symptom

With this NOTE, Synchronization of Material price changes during delta update will be controlled by a switch maintained in table /ISDFPS/CDFPSK1.

Field             : value
DFPS_KEY     : DFS_PRICE_CHNG
DFPS_VALUE : X (When X then price sync should happen. If blank then no price sync during material update)

Initial creation of Material at inbound side will not be checked for this switch, hence price change deatils(Table MBEW) will get created.

Other Terms

Defense disconnected S4, MR21, Material, Price change, MBEW

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions provided with the NOTE.
