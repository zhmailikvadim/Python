Symptom

Customer tries to delete Cost Objects and we do not find the corresponding com log entries.

Other Terms

Cost Objects, Internal Order, Sales Order, Maintenance Order, Network, Network Activity, Project WBS ELements.

Reason and Prerequisites

Deletion not exported due to missing com log entries.

Solution

Execute Report CTE_FIN_CREATE_COM_LOG_CO. (Package: CTE_FIN_SUPP_IMP)
