Symptom

in Oklahoma Tax Report, when the PUN (Property User Number) information is updated and saved in the system, no prompt is displayed asking whether the report should be re-reported or invalidated. So that, it results in the system allows changes to a tax rate that is not acceptable to the report.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition 2023.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to O3ut5Pun_info and display the document.
  2. Update GP (General Purpose) tax rate and save.
  3. No popup is received asking to re-report nor does it invalidate the report.

Resolution

For that issue you are not asked to re-report  because during the development, the Reporting PPA functionality was decided to be implemented in the t-code O3UT5_PUN_INFO for the change in the fields - Exempt NRI 1 Crude, Exempt NRI 5 Natural Gas, Exempt NRI 6 Natural Gas Liquids and Approved Exempt Override. Meaning, if you change any other field except these, you won't be asked to re-report nor invalidate it.

Keywords

OK TAX; PUN (Property User Number); Tax rate.
