Symptom

The user intends to subscribe to the "SAP Joule for developers, ABAP AI capabilities" service in the subaccount, but it is not listed in the Entitlements. 

Environment

  • SAP BTP, Cloud Foundry runtime and environment
  • Joule

Resolution

  • SAP Joule for developers, ABAP AI capabilities supports you in the end-to-end development using generative AI and can be integrated into ABAP development tools for Eclipse. It's currently only available for customers using SAP BTP ABAP environment, SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition, or SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition. 
  • To leverage SAP Joule for developers, ABAP AI capabilities, user need to purchase an additional license. Refer to Getting Started.
  • Contact the SAP technical representative to support the purchasing/activation of SAP Joule for Developers. To know the account executive follow the steps as per KBA 2626344 - Who is the SAP Account Manager/Executive for my company? 
  • After purchasing the service, check the prerequisites and follow the steps from Subscribing to SAP Joule for Developers, ABAP AI Capabilities.
