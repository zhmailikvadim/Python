Symptom

  • Comments added during the approval process do not appear in the "Activity" section on the right-hand side of the workflow details page.
  • This issue occurs when the approver clicks "Update", writes a comment, and then clicks "Approve".

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the workflow details page.
  2. Click "Update".
  3. Write a comment.
  4. Click "Approve" without clicking "Publish Comment".
  5. Observe that the comment does not appear in the "Activity" section on the right-hand side.

Resolution

Product Engineering is investigating a solution.

Click on star to bookmark this article to receive updates about this issue. For more information about updates from subscribed KBAs, you can refer to the KB Article 2171560 - How to be notified of new or updated SAP Notes or KBAs.

Workaround

Please Post Comment first before clicking the Approve button.
