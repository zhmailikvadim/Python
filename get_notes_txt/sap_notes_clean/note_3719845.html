Symptom

In the process "Receipt of Invoice" (DE_B_CP_INVC_RECV), step 0110 fails the DTD E_0243 check because the municipal discount is incorrectly determined when bill lines with empty line types exist.

This issue is relevant for the market roles: SUP, MOS.

Other Terms

US4G, DE_B_CP_INVC_RECV, Receipt of Invoice, DE_B_CP_EMBED_REV_INVC_DTD_CHK, DTD Check for Reversal Invoice, /US4G/CL_PRST_DTD_REV_INVC, municipal discount, bill line, line type, DTD E_0243

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system correctly determines the municipal discount even when bill lines with empty line types exist.
You need to trigger a DTD recheck.
You can verify the fix by checking that no municipal discount is fetched in step 0110-0040.
