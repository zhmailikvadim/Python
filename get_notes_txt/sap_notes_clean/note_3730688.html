Symptom

In SmartRecruiters, some functions are not available due to the integration with SuccessFactors. In these cases, you will see the message: "This data is managed in SuccessFactors".

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

SmartRecruiters for SAP SuccessFactors

Cause

Once the integration with SuccessFactors is activated, some functions like "Add new user" and bulk upload of Job Fields are restricted, as the data is managed only in SuccessFactors.

Resolution

Administrator must managed and sync data from SuccessFactors to SmartRecruiters.
