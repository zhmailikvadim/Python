Symptom

In the custom business objects (new version) app the "Publish" operation fails. The custom business object has a key field of type "Code List".

Other Terms

Custom Business Objects (New Version), CBO2, Publishing Failed

Reason and Prerequisites

Due to a programming error the key indicator isn't correctly considered during the publishing of a custom business object if the key fields has the type "Code List". The publishing is inconsistently treating the field as a key or non-key field in different places.

Solution

Correct the programming error by correctly considering the key indicator for fields of type "Code List". The key indicator needs to be passed to the logic which generates the CDS view entity which is used to model the business object.
