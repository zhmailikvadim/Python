Symptom

 Additional validation of operation not initial in check /APEU/CL_CHK_MCM_PROCESS=>CHECK_SLP_INPUT

Other Terms

APEU ,2508

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error , SLP input validation

Solution

Added the additional check in the check class /APEU/CL_CHK_MCM_PROCESS=>CHECK_SLP_INPUT for all the operations where we have create, change or Terminate.
