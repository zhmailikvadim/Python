Symptom

While performing goods movement, the below error occurs 

Error: No Matching Inventory Items Found 

Environment

SAP Business ByDesign

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Internal Logistics work center.
  2. Choose Goods Movement under common tasks.
  3. Enter the mandatory details Site, Planning Area, Product, Move Quantity, and Source Logistics Area.
  4. Choose Next.
  5. The error appears: "No matching inventory items found".

Cause

The product in stock overview is categorized under stock type warehouse, and it is associated with a warehouse order ID.

Resolution

  • It is not possible to perform a goods movement for a stock that is linked to a warehouse order.
  • This is the standard system behavior.
