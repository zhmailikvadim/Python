Symptom



  • In Create Golden Tax Documents-China APP, whether the Net Amount and Tax Amount fields display values before or after applying discounts.


Environment


  • GTI
  • China

Reproducing the Issue


  1. Search for and open a billing document.
  2. Check the displayed Net amount and Tax amount fields.
  3. Determine whether these values are shown before or after applying a discount.

Cause


  • Consulting question regarding the GTI design.

Resolution

The "Net amount" and "Tax amount" fields reflect the amounts after applying any discount.
