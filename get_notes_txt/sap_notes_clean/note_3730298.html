Symptom

Following issues were identified related to Reference date Routines in Advanced CPE.

1. Old routine 9999995 is mid date routine and is incorrectly mapped to new routine 19004, it need to be mapped to 19002.
2. Old routine 9999997 is incorrectly mapped to new routine 19002, new routine is required in Advanced CPE to provide same
    functionality like old routine 9999997 (End date or current date , whichever is higher).
3. Advanced CPE Migration of old routines 9999995 and 9999997 need to be corrected.
4  Mapping of old routines (9999995 and 9999997) to new routines is incorrect in Report - /ACCGO/CPE_DTA_CNVRSN_CSTMZG.

Other Terms

Agricultural Contract Management; ACM; Advanced CPE; Data migration; Reference Date Routine

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).

New routine is added in Advanced CPE to provide same functionality like old routine 9999997 (End date or current date , whichever is higher).
