Symptom

  • Statements of Works (SOWs) are not auto-closing despite the company configuration being set to close them automatically
  • Why the SOW did not being be picked up for closure?

Resolution

  1. Verify the company configuration Auto close statement of work when end date exceeds (# of days) is enabled
  2. There is a 30-day buffer period. SOWs are eligible for closure only after their end date has passed and they meet the configuration threshold, with the threshold date falling within the last 30 days from the current date. 
  3. SOW will remain open if they contain items that are either Pending Approval or incomplete
  4. Any SOW with items in a Pending Approval status or Approved but not yet Invoiced will not be picked up for closure by the system agent. Only when all such items are either fully approved and invoiced or completed will the SOW become eligible for closure.
  5. If the agent attempts to close an SOW and the closure fails, the agent may attempt to close the SOW up to two times. If it still fails, the agent will not attempt to close the SOW again.
  6. Buyer user can manually close the SOW from Actions > Close Statement of Work
