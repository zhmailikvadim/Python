Symptom

  • When selecting a specific goal plan template in Objective Management Data for a Story report, the Category column is not visible and cannot be pulled.
  • The Category can be pulled when “All Templates” is selected in Objective Management Data.

Environment

  •  SAP SuccessFactors Performance & Goals

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Reporting.
  2. Create a Story report.
  3. Use the specific goal template table under Objective Management Data.
  4. Observe that the Category column is not visible.

Cause

The issue arises due to limitations in accessing the Category field when selecting a specific goal plan template in Objective Management Data.

Resolution

Workaround:

  • Add the control-category-element into the XML https://help.sap.com/docs/successfactors-performance-and-goals/implementing-and-managing-goal-management/defining-category-and-default-category?version=2505
  • Or, Include all templates when creating the report.
