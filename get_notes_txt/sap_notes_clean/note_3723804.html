Symptom

  • Access to the Learning section in the Finance & Legal dashboard is missing.
  • The required authorizations to access the Learning section are present, but the section is still not visible when simulating the user's S-user ID.

Environment

SAP for Me

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the Finance & Legal dashboard in SAP for Me.
  2. Attempt to access the Learning section.
  3. Observe that the Learning section is missing despite the required authorizations being present.

Cause

Resolution

The content previously located in the Learning section within Finance & Legal has been relocated. You can now access the SAP Learning Hub contract under Finance & Legal, Cloud -> Contract section. 

See Also

Refer to: Required S-user authorizations to access Learning tab in Finance & Legal dashboard - SAP for Me
