Symptom

You process order RAIs with Final invoice indicator (FINAL_INVOICE_IND = F). During a contract change, a performance obligation's final invoice flag turns into 'X' even the invoice RAIs have not been processed yet. As a consequence, the SSP, transaction price and allocated amount becomes incorrect value.

Other Terms

FARR, Revenue Accounting and Reporting, OCM, Final invoice

Reason and Prerequisites

It happened when the final invoice has been billed in SD side. In the next order change, SD integration component send requests to RAR to set(keep) the final invoice flag on POB, which ignore if the invoice had been processed or not.

Solution

Apply this SAP Note.
