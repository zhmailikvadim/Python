Symptom

ReferenceSystem_code values longer than 5 characters received from MCM are truncated in the backend, resulting in data loss.

Other Terms

 APEU, UCOM, /APEU/S_MCM_ACTOR_EXT_REF

Reason and Prerequisites

APEU 2602

Solution

Increased the field length of ReferenceSystem_Code in S/4  to support 20 characters, ensuring the full value is stored without truncation.
