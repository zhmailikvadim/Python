Symptom

Updates to documentation after publication

Location: SAP S/4HANA and SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition--> Sales -> Order and Contract Management -> Basic Functions -> Checking Availability in SAP S/4HANA

Reason and Prerequisites

The following statements are incorrect:

  • The preponement of the requested delivery date doesn't affect the FCFS priority.
  • The postponement of the requested delivery date updates the FCFS priority.

The corrected statements are as follows:

  • The preponement of the requested delivery date updates the FCFS priority.
  • The postponement of the requested delivery date doesn’t affect the FCFS priority.

Solution

The above mentioned page has been corrected in the attached pdf file and will be published on the SAP Help Portal with the next SAP S/4HANA release.
