Symptom

You create a returns order where the sold-to party and ship-to party are not identical. During inspection creation with MSR_INSPWH the header tab shows the correct partner in field 'Ship-to Party'. However, if you save the inspection and later use MSR_INSPWH to change or display it you find that the sold-to party is shown instead.
