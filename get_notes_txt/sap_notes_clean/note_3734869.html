Symptom

SAP Note 3577306 (PY-GB: Residency Status 2025) introduced changes to List A and List B.

One of those changes included the introduction of a List B Group 2 document as follows;

"A Certificate of Application (non-digital) issued by the Home Office showing that the holder has made an application for leave to enter or remain under Appendix EU to the immigration rules (known as the EU Settlement Scheme), on or after 1 July 2021, together with a Positive Verification Notice from the Home Office Employer Checking Service."

This document was introduced on January 17th 2022 as the 5th document in List B Group 2, but changed to the 2nd document in later publications. In SAP it is has been given a unique identifier of B26 (i.e. the 6th unique instance of a different document in List B Group 2). However, the start date of the document was defined as 15.02.2025, when it should have been 17.01.2022.

This SAP Note corrects this entry to make it available from January 17th 2022.

Other Terms

Right To Work, Proof of ID, V_T5G_PRFID

Solution

EITHER

Apply the relevant HRSP

OR

Make the following manual change to B26 (View V_T5G_PRFID).

Current Entry

GB03 B26 15.02.2025 31.12.9999 HO issued Certificate of Application (post 01/07/2021) + PVN

Amended Entry

GB03 B26 17.01.2022 31.12.9999 HO issued Certificate of Application (post 01/07/2021) + PVN
