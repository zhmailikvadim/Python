Symptom

When blocking an item in Central Contract and distributing it into connected system, the item in distributed agreement is not blocked.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA 2025

Cause

Support Package level of connected system is below the minimum required.

Resolution

Make sure that the connected system has the minimum required SP level according to section Resolution and section "Installation and setup information for SAP S/4HANA 2025 hub system" of Note 3651377.
