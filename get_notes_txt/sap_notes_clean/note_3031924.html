Symptom

You use the trip provision variant for the Austrian public sector to enter duty allocations.

You call the transaction "Travel Expense Manager" (PR05) and navigate to the "Overview Incl. DZ" to display all trips and duty allocations for the relevant personnel number in a dialog box. When you do this, you select a trip or duty allocation and open this trip or a period of the duty allocation in change mode. If you now call the form (simulation), a rate that is too high may be used in some cases due to the concatenation logic that is not taken into account. As a result, the amounts displayed for the overnight fee and the per diem fee are incorrect.
