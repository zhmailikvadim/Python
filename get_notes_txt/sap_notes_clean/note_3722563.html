Symptom

New statistics ISCP 1-04 has been published by Statistical office of the Slovak republic. The new statistics is valid since year 2026.

Other Terms

Report HSKCTRX0, ISCP 1-04, form TREX.

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Slovakia (HRSK).

Solution

Report HSKCTRX0 updated to reflect latest legislation. Previous version of report HSKCTRX0 backed-up to report HSKCTRX0_2025. Form TREX updated and backed-up to form TRE6.

On selection screen of report HSKCTRX0 in section "Ostatné parametre" field "Kód Ekonomickej činnosti" was enlarged from 3 digits to 5 digits.
