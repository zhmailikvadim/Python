Symptom

When user selects Forecasts from Scheduling Agreement, the report 'ARBERP_BUS1001_EXTRACT_PROA' considers all delivery schedules and transfers them as forecast. The  Firm and trade-off Schedule Lines  should not be included in the ProductActivity cXML that is sent to Ariba Network.

Other Terms

ARBERP_BUS1001_EXTRACT_PROA, ProductActivityMessage, PROA, Ariba Network, SAP Add-On, Forecast, Scheduling Agreement, SAR, Schedule lines, delivery schedules, Firm , Trade-off, Firm/Trade-off zone .

Reason and Prerequisites

Functionality was not available.

Solution

Implement the code changes provided in the note or SP13
