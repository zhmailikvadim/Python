Symptom

In Manage Central Purchase Contracts app, when copying an existing central purchase contract, the header fields Purchasing Organization, Supplier, and Company Code are greyed out and cannot be edited.

Environment

  • SAP Fiori
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the app to manage central purchase contracts.
  2. Search for and open an existing central purchase contract.
  3. Use the copy function to create a new central purchase contract from the existing one.
  4. In the header of the copied contract, try to change Purchasing Organization, Supplier, and Company Code; the fields are non-editable (greyed out).

Cause

This is a standard system design to ensure data integrity. These header fields act as the structural foundation for the contract. Changing them would invalidate dependent data carried over from the original document, including:

  • Pricing conditions and tax calculations.
  • Partner functions and plant assignments.
  • Output and text determination.

To prevent inconsistencies between the header and item-level data, the system requires the copied contract to maintain the same structural framework as the source.

Resolution

If is necessary to change these fields, consider the following:

  • Create New: Select Create instead of Copy to manually enter the new organizational details.
  • Custom Enhancement: While not recommended due to the risk of data corruption, requirements for editable fields during a copy may be addressed via BADI or In-App Extensibility after a formal impact analysis.
