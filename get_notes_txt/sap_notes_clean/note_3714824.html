Symptom

  • Missing nodes specifically, nodes with types "M", "K" and "A" are filtered out by the standard CDS View I_GLAccountHierarchyNode, which is based on the table HRRP_NODE_N.

Environment

  • SAP Fiori
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Extract GL Account Hierarchy using standard CDS View I_GLAccountHierarchyNode.  
  2. Observe that nodes with Nodetype M, K, and A are filtered out and do not appear.  
  3. Attempt to view the hierarchy in RSH1 where the nodes are missing. 

Resolution

  • Please be advised that the nodes 00BSLOSS, 00BSPROFIT and 00PLPROFIT are special nodes which are calculated based on the GL accounts from the nodes 00ASSET and 00LIAB_EQUIT. They are not returned by the CDS views I_GLAccountHierarchyNode and C_GLAccountHierarchyNode.
  • These CDS views are used by analytical tools and if the nodes 00BSLOSS, 00BSPROFIT, 00PLPROFIT are there, the GL accounts in it would be aggregated twice in the result (once as a part of 00ASSET or 00LIAB_EQUIT, and for the second time as a part of 00BSLOSS, 00BSPROFIT, 00PLPROFIT). Please check the help page Special Nodes which describes how the special nodes are calculated.
  • If complete FSV structure is needed, there is a CDS view I_FINANCIALSTATEMENTHIERNODE available.
