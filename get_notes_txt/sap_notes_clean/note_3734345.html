Symptom

In Interview Central, when accessing candidates for the rating process, users are unable to view more than 10 candidates on the Interview Assessment page for review. Despite having additional candidates in the review stage, only a maximum of 10 candidates display for rating.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to Interview Central
  2. Open a candidate from the list to access the Interview Assessment page
  3. Verify that only 10 candidates are available for rating, despite additional candidates being in the review stage

Resolution

This is currently being investigated by our Engineering team.
