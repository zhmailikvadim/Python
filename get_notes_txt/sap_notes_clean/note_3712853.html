Symptom

The deferment account number (field BEKNR) was extended in the communication structure by SAP Note 3663188. As a result, SAP Note 3394236 added the field BEKNR_LNG to the IDoc for the transfer of the long account number in the messages to the customs authorities.

As a result, the field BEKNR_LNG is filled for all localizations. For localizations in whose mappings the field is not known in Seeburger BIS, an error therefore occurs during the processing of the message.

This symptom occurs, for example, for import messages to the customs system in Great Britain (CDS).

Other Terms

BEKNR_LNG, BEKNR, CDS, GB, import, deferment account
