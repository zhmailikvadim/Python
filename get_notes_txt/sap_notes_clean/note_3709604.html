Symptom

You use one of S/4HANA Fiori applications "My Travel and Expenses for Business Traveler", "Travel and Expenses for Travel Assistant". You are using the split receipt wizard. The following situations are adjusted:

  • At private expense deduction step, you could not input the whole available amount
  • At divide receipt step, the divide action shall act on the whole available amount

Other Terms

Travel Expenses, MTE, Expense report, F6190, F0584B, F6190A, F0584C

Travel Request, F6015, F6015A, F0409B, F0409C

Reason and Prerequisites

 Program error.

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instructions or apply the corresponding Support Package.
