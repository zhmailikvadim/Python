Symptom

You have activated Asset Accounting (new) (FI-AA (new)).

If you carry out a transfer posting for a fixed asset that has only one acquisition in the current year, the system posts a transfer posting document in both the previous year and in the current year. However, the document in the previous year with the amount 0 must not be posted.

Other Terms

ABUML

Reason and Prerequisites

Transfer posting from assets

Either you use EHP7 (617) and have activated the business function FIN_FAA_PARALLEL_VAL and FI-AA (new),

or you use Release EA-FIN 700.

Solution

Implement the corrections specified below in your system.
