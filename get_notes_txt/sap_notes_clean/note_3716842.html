Symptom

  • An issue in the display of the dropdown list of time slots when attempting to add a custom slot in interview scheduling.
  • The background appears in the foreground, obstructing the dropdown list display.

Environment

Success Factor Recruiting

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to Recruiting.
  2. Proceed to Interview scheduling.
  3. Start scheduling an interview.
  4. Add an interviewer.
  5. Click on 'Add Custom Slot'.
  6. Observe the dropdown list displaying incorrectly.

Cause

Known Issue

Resolution

The issue has been addressed by the Engineering team The fix will be available in Preview and Production as part of 2605 release. 

  • Preview 13-Apr-2026
  • Production 15-May-2026
