Symptom

Inquiry regarding whether SAP SuccessFactors and Microsoft Teams have a standard integration for field mapping.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite

Collaboration and Work Tech 

Cause

Resolution

The Engineering team confirmed that SAP SuccessFactors only supports mapping of the fields "email" and "username" between SAP SuccessFactors and Microsoft Teams.

To have a different system behavior, it would be possible to have an enhancement request created for it, where the SuccessFactors product management team can further review and assess the requested functionality to be considered in a future release.
