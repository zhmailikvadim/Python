Symptom

The SAP Digital Manufacturing system does not support the management of routings with operations in different plants.

Environment

  • SAP Digital Manufacturing
  • SAP ERP

Cause

The SAP Digital Manufacturing system is designed to be plant-dependent, and cross-plant operations require APM/CPM decisions.

Resolution

  1. Acknowledge that the SAP Digital Manufacturing system does not support the management of routings with operations in different plants.
  2. Utilize routings created in SAP ERP, which will also be executable in SAP DM.

Keywords
