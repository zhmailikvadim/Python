Symptom

The "Redistribute and Create ISD Invoices" app is not available for access.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Resolution

To resolve the issue, assign the following business catalogs to the relevant user business role:

  • SAP_FIN_BC_GLO_GSTISD_IN_PC – Globalization - Input Service Distribution for India
  • SAP_BR_PURCHASER_IN – Purchaser for India
