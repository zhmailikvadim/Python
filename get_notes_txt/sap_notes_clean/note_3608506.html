Symptom

What are the possible Manager - Direct Reports relations displayed in Latest Org Chart?

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite

Resolution

The latest Org Chart shows the direct and Matrix reporting relationships in a tree structure.

It can display up to 1,000 reports under a single manager.

Second Manager or Custom Manager are not displayed in Latest Org Chart.
