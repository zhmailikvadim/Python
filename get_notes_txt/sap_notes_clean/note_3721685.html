Symptom

How do I compare scenarios?

Resolution

To compare scenarios, please follow these steps:

  1. Choose Compare scenario and select the scenarios you wish to compare. You can select up to six scenarios.
  2. After selection, choose Compare scenario
  3. The Compare scenario page will display a summary of the selected scenarios.
  4. Scenarios are listed in the top right-hand corner of the page, and you can remove any by clicking the x button next to the scenario name.
  5. To close the Compare scenario page, choose Cancel.
