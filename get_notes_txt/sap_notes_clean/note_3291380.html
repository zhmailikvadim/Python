Symptom

You choose UTIL_IC role from Web UI. You confirm a business partner and premise. You click Contract Management link. If this Business Partner has already one or more contracts, click the link of Division Description. You click tab called "Documents". The documents buttons are enabled when it should be disabled.

Repeat the same above scenario with creating a contract, the buttons in the tab "Documents" are disabled when they should be enabled.

Other Terms

GS_CM, Folder, URL, IUICCOND

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the relevant Support Package.
