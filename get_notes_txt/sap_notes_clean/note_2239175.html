Symptom

If you change the master data for the rating of consumption items, the system fills the contract account incorrectly in the rated consumption items.
As a result, you are unable to navigate from billable items to the corresponding consumption items (and vice versa).
The system is also unable to find the relevant consumption item during the reversal of billable items.

Other Terms

FKK_BIX_RATING_MODULE

Reason and Prerequisites

There is a program error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified Support Package.
