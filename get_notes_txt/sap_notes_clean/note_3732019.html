Symptom

You notice there exists different currencies in FD33 and FBL5N and you want to identify if it is a consistent data in 

Environment

  • ERP Financials - Account Receivables - Credit Management
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise 4.7
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

1.T-code FD33/FD32

enter Customer and Credit Control Area

Check Credit exposure amount e.g. 10.000,00 USD

2.T-code FBL5N 
enter Customer and company code 

Check   Amt in loc.cur. e.g. 9.235,74   EUR

Resolution

1.Please compare the amount in T-code EWCT.



You can see that after exchanging the  amount should be equal.

There is no inconsistent data.

2.If the amount is not equal.

Please execute program RFDKLI20 to fix the inconsistency if none of the causes/reasons applies to your case. Refer to SAP KBA 2057405 to see how the program works.


Keywords

FD32, FD33. FBL5N, customer, Credit Control, EWCT, exchange rate.
