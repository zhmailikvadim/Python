Symptom

After implementing note 3636804 you experience warnings "Bank details with the same account number will be lost in the customer" (CVI_VO 001) and "Bank details with the same account number will be lost in the supplier" (CVI_VO 002) in transaction BP even though the bank data is not identical as it differs by IBAN (and the bank account number field is empty, i.e. the IBAN-only scenario is active).

Other Terms

Validation, CVI_VO001, CVI_VO002

Reason and Prerequisites

In the BDT scenario the comparison was not done by relying on CVI_BP_MEMORY like in the validation object, which generates the technical account numbers from the IBANs, starting with <IBAN>, but by simply trying to delete duplicates and seeing if anything was deleted. For the duplicate deletion the IBAN was not taken into consideration.

Solution

 Apply this note or the SP.
