Symptom

There are occasions when an attachment from Supplier Invoice are saved at Performance at the servicing side upon execution of Supplier Invoice Out edocument.

Other Terms

Attachments Supplier Invoice, G-Invoicing Hub Performance Attachments

Reason and Prerequisites

In G-Invoicing, users can attach documents in the Supplier Invoice linked to a performance (i.e. in MIR4 or MIR7).

If this is done before executing Supplier Invoice Out, this attachment will be included in the Performance document in Requesting side upon this execution.

There are instances where this attachment is saved instead in the Servicing Performance.

Solution

Implement the correction instructions to fix this issue.
