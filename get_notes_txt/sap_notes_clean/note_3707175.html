Symptom

  • Not able to click Manage PO Numbers button
  • Manage PO Numbers button grey-out to click
  • Manage PO Numbers function is deactivated
  • Manage PO Numbers is disabled
  • Why I cannot click 'Manage PO Numbers' button even though my S-user has all the required authorizations?

Environment

SAP for Me > Finance & Legal > Cloud 

Reproducing the Issue

S-user is not able to click Manage PO Numbers in Finance & Legal dashboard -> Cloud tab, even though S-user has authorization 'Display Order Information in SAP for Me' and 'Purchase Licenses' assigned:

 

Cause

No any missing PO numbers.

Resolution

Check Finance & Legal > Overview tab: 

  • If there is no missing PO numbers and Counter is at 0 for both cloud /on-prem, then the Manage PO Numbers feature will be disabled and not clickable:



  • If there is any missing PO as the sample screen shot below, the Manage PO Numbers feature will be active and clickable:



    Clicking on the missing PO line will lead the user jump into the Manage PO Numbers application:

Important: 

In case the contract has missing PO, but no any missing PO is shown in the Overview tab of Finance & Legal dashboard, or there is no way for customer to add the missing PO in SAP for Me, very possible the corresponding contract is currently not eligible for PO submission via SAP for Me.

For additional support in submitting the PO number, please use the 'Get assistance' option available under the 'Manage Contract' menu on the last column of the Contracts table. The submitted form will reach the PO Collection team directly and they will be able to assist further.
