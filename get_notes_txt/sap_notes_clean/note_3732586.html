Symptom

You would like to understand how the recipient role Responsible Team for Case works in Autoflow and Approval business flow.

Environment

SAP Sales and Service Cloud Version 2.

Resolution

When using the recipient role as Responsible Team for Case, the Manager of the Org Unit that is assigned to the case will be the one acting as the recipient

It is also recommended that the Primary toggle is enabled for the manager in the Org Unit.
