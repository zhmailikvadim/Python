Symptom

When you create or edit a central purchase contract of type 'Value Contract' with items of category 'Material Group' in the Manage Central Purchase Contracts app, the system displays an error message prompting you to enter a valid target value, even when you have entered a correct value.

Other Terms

Manage Central Purchase Contracts, Central Purchase Contract, Material Group, Item Category, Value Contract, Target Value, Error, F3144

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instruction.
