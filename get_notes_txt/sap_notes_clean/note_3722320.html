Symptom

Error is raised upon updating planned finish of current decision point less than planned finish of previous decision point when planned start of previous decision point and planned start of current decision point are initial.

Other Terms

Planned finish of current decision point, Planned finish of previous decision point, Planned finish, /RPM/CL_DECISION_POINT_API====CP, /RPM/IF_PROVIDER_ACCESS~MODIFY.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

 Implement the corrections attached to the SAP Note.
