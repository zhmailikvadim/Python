Symptom

When you receive goods from RF in EWM, the goods receipt posts in S/4HANA but the material document doesn't update with medication kits. This happens because the medication kits aren't created and saved in the medication kit master table.

Other Terms

ISLSUI, Intelligent Clinical Supply Management

Reason and Prerequisites

You use SAP Intelligent Clinical Supply Management.

Medication kits aren't created and saved in the /CTCO/MEDNO table because one of the determinations in the /CTCO/MEDNO business object fails.

Solution

Implement the attached correction.
