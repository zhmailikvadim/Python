Symptom

When you activate or use the OData service LE-SHP Quick Create: Delivery Without Reference (LE_SHP_QC_DLVNOREF_SRV), the system displays the following error:

“Association 'TO_BPDATACONTROLLERUSAGE' of SADL structure 'I_BPCustomerMultiAddrVH' with binding '_BPDATACONTROLLERUSAGE' does not exist. Cleanup of the project is needed.”

Other Terms

LE_SHP_QC_DLVNOREF_SRV, I_BPCustomerMultiAddrVH, I_BPDataControllerUsage, /IWFND/MAINT_SERVICE

Reason and Prerequisites

The association to I_BPDataControllerUsage is obsolete due to SAP Note 3535818. As a result, the OData service LE_SHP_QC_DLVNOREF_SRV refers to outdated or inconsistent metadata. The OData project for LE_SHP_QC_DLVNOREF_SRV was not regenerated after the changes in the SAP Note.

Solution

Perform the manual steps explained in this SAP Note. This corrects the metadata inconsitencies.
