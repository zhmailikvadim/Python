Symptom

In standard transactions ME22N, ME23N the Item overview layouts are different(having different columns and changed positions) for STOs with and without Change Authorization.

This is controlled by the implementation class /ISDFPS/CL_IM_PROCESS_PO~IF_EX_ME_PROCESS_PO~FIELDSELECTION_HEADER(for Enhancement Spot ME_PROCESS_PO) on purpose to restrict deletion of item entries that don't have change protection in the current system.
