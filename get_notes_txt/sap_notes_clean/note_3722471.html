Symptom

  • The “Manage Cash Concentration” app displays a balance before amount which is different from cash flow analyzer app as mentioned in this KBA 2978537. Example : Manage cash concentration app displays Amount of EUR -245,049.83 as of 20 October 2025  comparing the app Cash Flow Analyzer shows a starting balance of EUR -248,379.83, resulting in a difference of EUR 3,330 .
  • Why The balance calculation in the app manage cash concentration  must be calculated based on the bank account ID and not based on the house bank. 

Note: In App Manage cash cocentration 'Balance before amount gets calculated as mentioned in this KBA 2978537.

Environment

S4CORE 106 107 108 109

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the “Manage Cash Concentration” app.
  2. Observe the balance before amount as of 20 October 2025, which is EUR -245,049.83.
  3. Compare this balance with the starting balance in the Cash Flow Analyzer, which is EUR -248,379.83.
  4. Note the discrepancy of EUR 3,330 between the two balances.

Cause

  • The house bank assignment is mandatory for physical cash transfers and payment processing in cash concentration scenarios. Flows without house bank assignment are excluded from the balance calculation. So flows without House bank will not be add to the balance before.

Resolution

  • Make sure field FQM_Flow-House_bank is updated for the corresponding flows becasue flows without House bank will not be add to the balance before as per below explaination.

To Define the Cash Pool Hierarchies, in the Fiori “Manage Bank Accounts”  For a Bank Account you have to tick Under the Tab Cash pool the flag 'Use in Cash pool'.

This flag is ticked in the house bank under the connectivity path.  

  1. Cash Pool Flag Purpose: The "Use in Cash Pool" flag serves as a master switch to enable/disable bank account participation in cash pooling operations 
  2. House Bank Connectivity Requirement: House bank assignment is mandatory for physical cash transfers and payment processing in cash concentration scenarios
  3. Flow Selection Logic: System automatically filters cash flows based on both bank account and house bank cash pooling flag status using standard database joins
Business Justification
  • This design ensures that only properly configured bank accounts with actual payment capabilities participate in cash concentration
  • The dual-level flag system (bank account + house bank) provides granular control over cash pooling participation
  • House bank connectivity requirement aligns with the business need for actual fund transfers in cash concentration operations

This design is intentional and cannot be modified. The system requires proper house bank connectivity to ensure that cash concentration operations can execute actual fund transfers between participating accounts, which is the core business purpose of cash pooling functionality.
