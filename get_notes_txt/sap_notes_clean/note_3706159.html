Symptom

When deleting an emergency contact in the "Manage my Data" tile via Quick Actions on the home page, the system displays a save message as “???ECT_Save_Success???” at the bottom of the screen. 

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central
  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite

Reproducing the Issue

  1. On the Home Page, go to Quick Actions and select Manage My Data;
  2. Click Change Emergency Contact;
  3. Select the contact you wish to delete;
  4. Click Delete;
  5. Confirm the deletion;
  6. Observe and record the save message displayed as “???ECT_Save_Success???”.

Resolution

The update is planned to be delivered as part of the 1H2026 release, which will occur around April and May, 2026.
