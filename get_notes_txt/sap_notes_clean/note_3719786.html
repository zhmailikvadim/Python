Symptom

  • Total volume calculation discrepancy compared to tare volume and loading volume. 
  • Total volume of closed packaging materials remains constant regardless of contents. 

Environment

  • Logistics - General
  • Handling Unit Management
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise 4.7
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA 
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Pack the handling unit (HU) in transaction VL02n for example
  2. The total volume appears incorrect when looking at the tare volume and loading volume. 

Cause

Packaging material is a box/container - it does not change its volume when material is added and the box is closed. 

Resolution

  1. Verify the configuration of the packaging material in the Material Master.
  2. Check the field MARA-KZGVH (Packaging Material is Closed Packaging) located on the Sales: General/Plant tab.
  3. Confirm that the packaging material is configured as a "closed packaging material". 
  4. Understand that for closed packaging materials, the total volume is not increased by the loading volume of the contents because the volume of a closed box remains constant whether it is empty or filled.
  5. If the configuration is correct, no further action is required as the system is functioning as designed. Otherwise, it is possible to reconfigure the packaging material type (tcode VHAR).
