Symptom

Occurs during creation of the EU sales list for Hungary following error.
The sign amount of accounts payable positions (control type " V " = pre-tax) are wrong.

Other Terms

RFASLD15, Hungary, Hungarian localization, EC sales and purchase list, Tax declaration, VAT reporting

Reason and Prerequisites

If tax rate settings deviates from a standart then a program check needs to verified.

Solution

The signs changing for pre-tax are disabled if a tax rate settings diverges from standard (transaction: ftxa -> ESA+ and ESE-).
