Symptom

BAPI_MATERIAL_PLANNING do not support Industry specific solutions like DIMP MPN & SUS material exchange for MRP.

Other Terms

BAPI_MATERIAL_PLANNING, MD03 , MD04 , MD05 , ADEC , MPN , SUS.

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a consulting note for IS Aerospace and Defense.

Solution

MPN, or Manufacturer Part Number, is part of the IS Aerospace and Defense solution.

BAPI_MATERIAL_PLANNING do not support Industry specific solutions like DIMP MPN & SUS material exchange functionality in MRP run. That means system does not consider the stock and requirements of other parts in an MPN-MRP set as cumulative when performed MRP run using BAPI_MATERIAL_PLANNING.


Any problems or inconsistencies arising out of the usage MPN functionalities in BAPI_MATERIAL_PLANNING, will not be supported by SAP.
