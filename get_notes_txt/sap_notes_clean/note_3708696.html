Symptom

During the formatting of addresses, the city and country of the recipient is not formatted in uppercase letters if the "Print Foreign Name" option of the address formatting is activated.

The corrections in this SAP Note adjust the address formatting in the SAP Fiori apps in accordance with DIN5008 and format the city or country in uppercase letters.

Other Terms

Preference, long-term supplier’s declaration, LTSD, V_T005_BAS, DIN5008

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions.

In addition, implement the version SFPF5 or higher of the print form /SAPSLL/PF_EU_LTSD from SAP Note 3387020.

If you use the print forms /SAPSLL/PF_EU_GB_LTSD_NEG or /SAPSLL/PF_EU_GB_LTSD_POS, also implement the version SFPF1 from SAP Note 3708898.
