Symptom

  • For example in a chain transaction to cover special business needs the tax departure country is changed in the sales document.
  • The change of the tax departure country does not update the material tax classification (VBAP-TAXM1, KOMP-TAXM1). 

Environment

  • SAP R/3
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create e.g. a sales order in VA01.
  2. The tax departure country is 'AT' determined from delivering plant and material tax classification '1'.
  3. At header level on the billing document tab add Tax departure country (VBAK-LANDTX) 'DE'.
  4. Check the material tax classification at item level and KOMP-TAXM1 of the tax condition in the pricing analysis.
  5. Neither VBAP-TAXM1 nor KOMP-TAXM1 is updated, the initial value determined from country 'AT' remains.

Cause

The material tax classification is determined during document creation based on the country of the delivering plant. A manual change to the tax departure country does not automatically trigger a redetermination of the material tax classification.

Resolution

  1. Refer to section 1/e of SAP note 872449 for guidance on manually updating the material tax classification at the item level on the billing document tab.
  2. Alternatively, implement an extension in USEREXIT_CHECK_VBAP (include MV45AFZB) to ensure the material tax classification is redetermined based on the manually updated tax departure country.
