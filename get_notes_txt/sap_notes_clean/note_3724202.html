Symptom

Soap messages are failing to deliver, and the Soap receiver channel shows that the messages are being cancelled due to error: "Failed to call the endpoint: Error in call over HTTP: HTTP 429 Too Many Requests"

Environment

Product version: SAP NetWeaver 7.5

Cause

Th error means that you're making more calls than what the target can handle at a certain time or what is allowed to process at a giving rate, leading to the rate limit reaching its threshold and by consequence, a possible connection closure.

This is not necessarily a limitation from PO, as it can be established by the target system to avoid any security or resources risks such as bottlenecks/Dos attacks/etc...

Resolution

You can implement a minimal request transfer rate by configuring the below ICM parameters:

  • MIN_RECEIVE_RATE
  • CHECK_RECEIVE_RATE_AFTER
  • MAX_RECEIVE_TIMEOUT

For more information on the above parameters and how to configure them, please refer to the following SAP Note: 1981955 - Enforcing minimal request transfer rates in SAP Web Dispatcher and ICM.
