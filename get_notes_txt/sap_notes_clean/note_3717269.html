Symptom

Supplier is unable to update few questions in the supplier registration questionnaire from their Business Network Account.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login through the supplier account.
  2. Attempt to edit the affected question.
  3. Observe that the field is unchangeable and cannot be updated.

Cause

The buyer has configured an editability condition for such questions at the template level. The field can only be updated once the condition set by the buyer is met.

Resolution

  1. Contact the buyer to verify the editability condition configured for those questions.
  2. Ensure the condition is met as per the buyer's configuration.
  3. Once the condition is satisfied, update the field accordingly.

Keywords

supplier registration, questionnaire, editability condition, unable to update, field unchangeable, SAP Ariba
