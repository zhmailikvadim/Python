Symptom

Why are the next approvers not seeing my approvable in their To Do list or receiving any approval notifications?

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Submit an approvable for approval with two or more serial approval nodes.
  2. Ask the approvers in each node to look for the approvable in their To Do list and check their inbox for notifications.
  3. Only the approvers in the first node see the approvable and receive notifications.

Cause

The approval flow is designed to operate in sequence, activating only one serial node at a time. While the first node is active, the approvers in the following nodes are not considered yet. Because of this, they do not see the approvable in their To Do list and do not receive notifications until the approvers in the first node take action.

Resolution

If you require more than one approval node to act at the same time, you can configure your approval flow to use Parallel approvers. All parallel nodes become active together, allowing all approvers in those nodes to receive notifications and see the approvable at the same time.

Keep in mind that in a parallel scenario, the system moves to the next serial node as soon as one of the parallel nodes approves the approvable. The system does not wait for approval from every parallel node.
