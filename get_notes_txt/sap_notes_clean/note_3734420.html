Symptom

Webdynpro based Fiori Sales Analytics apps are not appearing in Business Workzone

Other Terms

BWZ, SAP_TC_CEC_SD_BE_APPS, W0139, W0140, F4572, F1249, F1250

Reason and Prerequisites

There are missing Transaction Codes for Webdypro apps in table SUI_TM_MM_APP

Solution

Please review the following manual instructions:

  1. Go to transaction /UI2/FLPAM
  2. Click Edit button
  3. Search for technical catalog SAP_TC_CEC_SD_BE_APPS and update the apps in the following table with the missing TCodes:
