Symptom

Supplier user has updated the email address in their Business Network account, but it did not synced to SLP.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Update the contact’s email address in the supplier’s Business Network account.
  2. Wait for the standard synchronization from SBN to update contacts in SLP.
  3. Check if the email has been updated in sourcing side.
  4. Observe that the contact email in SLP remains unchanged.

Cause

Duplicate email addresses exist under the same supplier account in SLP.

Resolution

Supplier Management don't support two users with the same email address under one account. You can have duplicate contacts in sourcing side, but it won't sync back to SLP. As there is already one user with the same email address in SLP contacts tab, the revised email synced from SBN to sourcing, but did not updated in SLP. Whenever you do such updates, please make sure there won't be any contact with the same email address in SM database.
