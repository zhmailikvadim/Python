Symptom

You have created a third party purchase contract with zero tolerance, then LDC is created for quantity greater than contract open quantity and provisional analysis. Application document created in Provisional status for quantity available in contract and remaining unapplied quantity moved to RTA line item. When Link to Contract is performed on provisional line item, it incorrectly updated the quantities leaving a fraction of qauntity open in the contract.

Other Terms

ACM, Agriculture Contract Management, Partial application, Link to Contract

Reason and Prerequisites

Issue is happening because LAQ was considered for qauntity adjustment

Solution

Implement the Correction Instruction to fix the issue.
