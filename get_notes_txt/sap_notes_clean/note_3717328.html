Symptom

Currently, after Dangerous Goods check execution in TM, the resulting DG indicator or DG status for items is propagated to parent items. However, in case of shipment items (item category SHP), this propagation is not working correctly.

Other Terms

Dangerous Goods Check, DG Indicator, DG Status

Reason and Prerequisites

There is a program error.

Solution

Implement the corrections of the attached correction instructions or apply the corresponding support package.
