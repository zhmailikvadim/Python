Symptom

Documents which cannot determine legal regulations have status 'Not Checked/Blocked'. They are not available in 'Managed Blocked Documents' app.

Other Terms

F4714, fcg.lls.blkdccd.manage, I_CstmsCmplncDocBlkdItem

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Apply correction instructions in the Note or upgrade to relevant SP.

After applying this Note, the 'Not Checked/Blocked' documents with empty legal regulation and activate area are displayed in the app. User cannot navigate to the block detail page due to missing check result.

Make sure Frontend Note 3724633 and prerequisite Note 3710953 are also implemented together with this Note.
