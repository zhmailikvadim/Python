Symptom

You use PDF Preview as default Viewer for SAPscript and Smart Forms documents in your system.

If you open some SAPscript and Smart Forms documents one after another (e.g. in TA SP01), second or any followed document will not showed in PDF-Preview. PDF Preview appears empty.

Other Terms

Smart Forms, SAPscript, PDF Preview, SP01, VF03

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Please apply the correction instructions of the note or implement the corresponding support package.
