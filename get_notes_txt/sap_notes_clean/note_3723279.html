Symptom

  • Classic Planning Model workbooks in Analysis for Office (AFO) need conversion to SAP Analytics Cloud (SAC) add-in, pointing to the new SAC model.
  • Heavy calculations are referenced in other Excel tabs linked to AO workbooks.
  • Existing SAC add-in workbooks pointing to the Classic model require switching the data source to the new SAC model type.
  • Users seek a workaround or the fastest method to convert AO workbooks to SAC add-in without rebuilding from scratch.

Environment

SAP Analytics Cloud, add-in for Microsoft Office

Cause

  • The technical basis of Analysis Office and SAC add-in differs significantly (e.g., direct vs tunnel connection, BICS vs Firefly).
  • Not all features available in Analysis Office are supported in SAC add-in.

Resolution

  1. A migration/conversion tool from Analysis Office to SAC add-in is not available.
  2. Users are encouraged to vote for the request on the influence portal to prioritize development of a migration tool. https://influence.sap.com/sap/ino/#/idea/278332/?section=sectionDetails" SAP Influence Portal /a 
  3. As of now, feature development for SAC add-in is prioritized over migration support investigation.
