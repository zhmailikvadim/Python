Symptom

Scheduling Agreement cannot be edited in Manage Scheduling Agreement app and the following error shows in /n/iwfnd/error_log:
"No key table found for content-id-reference ‘id-*‘"
Component MM-PUR-OA

Environment

  • Materials Management (MM)
  • SAP FIORI
  • SAP S/4HANA 2020
  • SAP S/4HANA 2021
  • SAP S/4HANA 2022
  • SAP S/4HANA 2023
  • SAP S/4HANA 2025

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open a scheduling agreement in Manage Purchase Scheduling Agreement app.
  2. Click edit button.
  3. The scheduling agreement cannot be edited.

Cause

Note 3627703 - "Scheduling Agreement not editable via Manage Purchase Scheduling Agreement App" is missing.

Resolution

Implement note 3627703.

See Also

The error can happen especially when number range for scheduling agreement has less than 10 digits and note 3585049 has been implemented. 

Keywords

F2179, scheduling agreement, An exception has occurred, An exception was raised
