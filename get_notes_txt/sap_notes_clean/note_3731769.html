Symptom

  • Inconsistent behavior of deep link URLs
  • Some URLs from List of SAP SuccessFactors Deep Links | SAP Help Portal are working, and some are not
  • Deep link URL is not working in Android and iOS

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite
  • SAP SuccessFactors Mobile

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login to your SF Mobile App
  2. Go to Browse
  3. Under Links, try opening "View My Profile" (or any reported deep link)
  4. Notice that nothing will happen after selecting the quick link

Cause

  • Incorrect URL was configured
  • Mobile App is outdated

Resolution

The engineering team has figured the resolution and a functional fix has already been deployed.
To correct this behavior from your end, kindly ensure:

  • Correct URL is configured, following the format from this Help Guide: List of SAP SuccessFactors Deep Links | SAP Help Portal
  • Update your Mobile App (as of today: March 2026- app version should be 16.1.2)


Note:

The fix for iOS will be deployed in July 2026. 
If this functionality is critical to your business prior that period, please inform the SAP team by submitting a portal case to LOD-SF-PLT-MOB.
