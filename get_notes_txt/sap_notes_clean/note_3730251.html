Symptom

During settlement rule maintenance for Service/Maintenance Orders, the system does not allow proper maintenance of the settlement rule when the receiver is a Profitability Segment. The Plant (WERKS) field is missing, leading to incomplete derivation of the profitability segment.

Other Terms

Settlement Rule, Plant (WERKS), Service Order, Maintenance Order, CO-PA, Profitability Segment

Reason and Prerequisites

The issue occurs because the Plant (WERKS) field is not being populated in the settlement rule maintenance structure during the execution of the settlement rule dialog.
As a result, the system is unable to derive or maintain the profitability segment correctly.

Solution

Implement the note.
