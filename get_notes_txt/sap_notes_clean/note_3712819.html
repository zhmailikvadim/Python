Symptom

In note 3698287, the EXKID constant from table T511K was released in the amount of 3.525,37 CZK. The correct value of the EXKID constant is 3.525,38 CZK.

Other Terms

Table T511K, constant EXKID for year 2026

Reason and Prerequisites

  •  This SAP Note is relevant for the Czech Republic only.

    Changed value in the constant EXKID (Exekuce - částka na děti) in the table T511K for year 2026

Solution

We have prepared new values of the constant EXKID = 3.525,38 CZK in the table T511K for year 2026

In the case of closing payroll for the month 12/2025 in January 2026, it is necessary to use the new constants for wage garnishments EXKID effective from 12/1/2025.
