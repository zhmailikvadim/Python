Symptom

Introduce the triggering operation and timing for Material Document and Billing Document in Scope Item 5MQ.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Resolution

Material Document - Good Issue for OD into SiT (GIOD):

Triggering Operation: Post Goods Issue (PGI) for the Outbound Delivery manually.

Triggering Timing: It is determined by the PGI time.

Material Document - Post GI from SiT to Customer (GITC):

Triggering Operation: Automatically triggered by the system after completing PGI.

Triggering Timing: It is determined by "External Transfer of Control Dates".

Billing Document - Customer Invoice (CISC)： 

Triggering Operation: Created with reference to the Outbound Delivery manually.

Triggering Timing: It is determined by the Invoice creation time.
