Symptom

During the clearing of a document from a reconciliation posting, the system issues error message FI 278 "Line &1 in document &2 &3 &4 does not contain any commitment items".

Other Terms

F-03, KALC

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason: Program error. Since postings using the reconciliation ledger should be not updated in Funds Management and therefore do not contain an FM account assignment, you want the check for critical business transactions to be deactivated for documents of this kind.

Prerequisites: You do not use CO-FI real-time integration of the new general ledger. One of the FI documents involved in the clearing is a CO-FI reconciliation booking and does not contain any commitment items.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified Support Package.
