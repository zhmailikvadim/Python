Symptom

Brazil Localization is not available in Approve ACM Contracts FIORI app.

Other Terms

LO-AGR; LO-AGR-CC; Agricultural Contract Management; Approve ACM Contracts; CPE; Brazil Localization; F4379A

Reason and Prerequisites

Brazil Localization features are already available in SAP GUI transactions for Contracts.

Solution

Brazil Localization UI5 features are available in Approve ACM Contracts. The facet 'Royalties' is added in the Header section of Contract and 'Brazil' facet is added in the Item section of Contract. Royalties section in the header will have the information related to Royalties Technology, Royalties Percentage, Royalties Category and GMO Relevancy.
Brazil facet in tems section will have the information to the Material Usage.
