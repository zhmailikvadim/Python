Symptom

When decreasing the quantity in a partially delivered Sales Order you receive the following error message:

Cannot decrease quantity more than X, since X were already delivered

Environment

  • SAP Business One
  • SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Sales - A/R → Sales Order. 
  2. Add a Sales Order with Item A and Quantity 5. 
  3. Base a Delivery on the Sales Order in step 2 and deliver 3. 
  4. Go back to the  Sales Order in step 2 and change the quantity to 2. 
  5. Update the document. 

The scenario, error message and Message ID are also valid on the A/P side where goods have been received for a Purchase Order. 

Cause

The logic of the system is - quantity 3 of Item A has already been delivered, hence when changing the quantity to 2 in the original document the quantity would be lower than what has already been delivered. 

Resolution

Change the quantity to what has already been delivered or a higher quantity.
