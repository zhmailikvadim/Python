Symptom

New statistics PRACA/A 2-04 and PRACA/B 2-04 have been published by Statistical office of the Slovak republic for year 2026.

Other Terms

HRSK, HSKCPRA0, HSKCTRX0, PDF, HR_SK_HSKCPRA0_A, HR_SK_HSKCPRA0_B, HR_SK_HSKCPRA0_A_2025, HR_SK_HSKCPRA0_B_2025

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Slovakia (HRSK).

Solution

This SAP Note delivers:

1. Change of PDF forms

  • HR_SK_HSKCPRA0_A
  • HR_SK_HSKCPRA0_B

2. Employer’s Mandatory Insurance Contributions

  • The mandatory employer contributions for statutory insurance (health and social insurance) are shown in line R.20 in Praca/A 2-04 and line R.11 in Praca/B 2-04 of the PDF output.
  • These amounts also include the employer’s mandatory contributions to supplementary pension savings.
  • Employer contributions to supplementary pension savings are required by law for employees classified in work categories 3 and 4 (i.e., those with Infotype 0253 - P0253-SELKV set to X, 3, or 4).
  • (ISCP 1-04 field 25 ('cenaprace') should contain this Employer contributions to supplementary pension savings too)
