Symptom

Profiles are populated with accounts that use email addresses ending in @privaterelay.appleid.com.  

Environment

  • SAP Customer Data Cloud
  • Customer Identity 
  • Social Login

Cause

• Users logging in with Apple can access supported apps and websites using their existing Apple Account.  
• The "Hide My Email" feature in Apple enables users to generate anonymous email addresses for enhanced privacy.  
• These private relay email addresses are unique, randomly generated, and end in @privaterelay.appleid.com.  

Resolution

1. Confirm that the "Hide My Email" feature is enabled for users logging in with Apple.  
2. Private relay email addresses ending in @privaterelay.appleid.com are expected behavior for enhanced privacy.  
3. No further action is required as this is standard functionality.
