Symptom

The machine learning feature in the app Bank Reconciliation - China (App ID: F2382) uses machine learning technology to match data automatically or propose probable matches of journal entry items and bank statement items.

As of SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 2608, the predictive scenario enters the deprecation cycle.

* Deprecation in this context means the intelligent scenario remains available for use but is no longer developed or supported and may be discontinued in upcoming releases.

Other Terms

FI_EPIC_BKRECNCLN; Predictive Scenario for Bank Reconciliation

Reason and Prerequisites

 Low customer utilization of this feature.

Solution

The planned deprecation applies for the predefined predictive scenario FI_EPIC_BKRECNCLN (Bank Reconciliation).

You don't need to take any action.
