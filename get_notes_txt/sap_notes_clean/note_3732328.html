Symptom

In the process "Trigger of Master Data Changes" (DE_B_GPKE_TRIG_MDC_ASSGN_DSO), step 0610 (Determine Technical Resource Change Data) did not run despite step 0600 having the check result MDC_TECH_RSRC_TRIG_REO. Step 8999 completed even though step 0605 (Deadline step) was still in Deadline Active status.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

MDC, DE_B_GPKE_TRIG_MDC_ASSGN_DSO, Technical Resource Change Data, step 0610, step 0600, step 0605, step 8999, MDC_TECH_RSRC_TRIG_REO, Deadline Active

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because step 8999 completed even though step 0605 (Deadline step) was still in Deadline Active status.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, step 8999 runs only when step 0605 status is set to completed along with other preconditions. The BC sets are /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP00_NOTE_3732328, /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP07_NOTE_3732328, and /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP08_NOTE_3732328. You can verify the fix by rerunning the scenario and checking that step 0610 runs correctly when step 0600 has the check result MDC_TECH_RSRC_TRIG_REO and step 0605 is completed.
