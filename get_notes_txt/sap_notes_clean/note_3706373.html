Symptom

For all applications within Sustainability Footprint Management, is not possible to limit users to operate and query data corresponding to their assigned Plant or Company. 

Environment

Sustainability Footprint Management

Resolution

Currently, SFM only manages application access through Business Technology Platform (BTP). Improvement of permission control for all sustainability footprint management applications based on organizational units is being worked on. The estimated timeline for implementation is H2 2026.
