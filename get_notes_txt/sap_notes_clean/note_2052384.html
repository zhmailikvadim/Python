Symptom

Main note

Other Terms

Address Management, Turkey, Business function, FIN_LOC_TR_1, CRM_LOC_TR_1, ADDR_TR_SWITCH, CMD_VMD_FS_EXT_SFW_01, CMD_VMD_TR_SFW_01, RSADRCTUPD

Reason and Prerequisites

New development, as address management was missing fields for county (Ilce) and township (bucak).

Solution

In this localization of address management for Turkey, two new address fields, County (Ilce) and Township (bucak), were added in SAP for Utilities (IS-U) and SAP Customer Relationship Management (SAP CRM). These fields represent the hierarchy levels between Region and City in the address structure used in Turkey.  Further, this enhanced address data is now integrated between IS-U and SAP CRM.

County data can be uploaded with the assigned Region, and the Township data can be uploaded with the assigned Region and County.

This functionality is delivered via ERP EHP7 SP6 and CRM EHP3 SP6.
