Symptom

According to latest legislation, Ministry of defence SR should have a specific process of directing a portion of paid income tax (as stated in SAP Note 3696927). Further modification of this functionality regarding recalculation is needed.

Other Terms

Public sector, wage type M81A, payroll rule SK94.

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Slovakia (HRSK).

This SAP Note is relevant only Public sector - Ministry of defence SR.

Solution

This SAP Note delivers modification of payroll rule SK94 to properly handle recalculation of 'Directed portion of paid income tax'.
