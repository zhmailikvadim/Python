Symptom

Why are some questions mapped to generic custom fields in Supplier Registration questionnaire not appearing in the ERP data tab of Supplier 360 Profile after syncing the Supplier?

Cause

A visibility condition applied to the relevant registration questions prevents those questions from being shown/answered. When the condition is not met, the values are not captured and therefore are not synced to the SM/ERP data area. This behavior is expected with the current configuration.

Resolution

To make all the questions mapped to generic custom fields in Supplier Registration questionnaire appear in the ERP data tab after syncing the supplier, change the template configuration to remove the visibility conditions for these questions.
