Symptom

You are using Inbound Cargo Registration (ICR) and recognize missing packaging items in the packaging hierarchy after packing JIT items using button Pack Per JIT Call. However, the packed quantity and the unused quantity of the items are updated accordingly. This leads to an inconsistency between the displayed packaging hierarchy and the item quantity information, which may confuse the user.

Other Terms

ICR, inbound cargo registration, packing dialog, JIT

Reason and Prerequisites

You use SAP S/4HANA for manufacturing logistics or this SAP Note is a prerequisite of another SAP Note.

Solution

Implement the attached correction.
