Symptom

  • My company is not currently interested in rating Job Seekers, Workers or Suppliers in SAP Fieldglass
  • How can I completely disable the Rating functionality in SAP Fieldglass

Resolution

Based on the current design of this functionality, users can be restricted from having the possibility of rating Job Seekers, Workers or Suppliers, by disabling the User Role Permission Evaluate from the respective module. However, there are no existing options to disable this functionality completely, as it was designed to be native and always available for users with the correct permissions.
