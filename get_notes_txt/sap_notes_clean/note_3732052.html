Symptom

 In the process "End of Supply (DSO View)" (DE_B_GELIGAS_EOS_DSO), the embedded process "Process for G_0007" (DE_B_GELIGAS_DSO_EOS_G_0007) fails at step 310. This embedded process is triggered by the main process at step 1200.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

GPKE, EOS, GELIGAS, End of Supply, DE_B_GELIGAS_EOS_DSO, DE_B_GELIGAS_DSO_EOS_G_0007, CHKGRP_GPKE_DSO_EOS_OTHERS, CHKGRP_GPKE_DSO_EOS_BUS_TEST, check group, embedded process

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because the check group CHKGRP_GPKE_DSO_EOS_OTHERS was incorrectly configured with the child group CHKGRP_GPKE_DSO_EOS_BUS_TEST as an extension.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, step 310 of the embedded process completes successfully. You need to retest the process. You can verify the fix by checking that step 310 of the embedded process runs without errors.
