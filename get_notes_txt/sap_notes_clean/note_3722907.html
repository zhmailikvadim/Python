Symptom

After Create and approve a Purchase Requisition, geneate a Purchase Order from this Purchase Requisition. Making new changes in Purchase Requisition to trigger a new approval process and the Purchase Order can still be edited even the Purchase Requisition are in release.

Environment

  • Materials Management (MM)
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Fiori 

Cause

Standard behavior. After making changes in Purchase Requisition and have it in status = in release, only changes in Purchase Order item quantity which surpassed PR quantity will stop the PO from saving.

Resolution

Implement custom check with BADI ME_PROCESS_PO_CUST. 

Keywords

ME52N, PR, PO, change, Release, In release, approval, reset,
