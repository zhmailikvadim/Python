Symptom

When Bank account is created/modified via workflow and once some processor claims that request from their My Inbox app, No data appears in Next Processor list in My sent Request app for the user.

Other Terms

CL_FCLM_BAM_WORKFLOW_UTILITY , GET_WORKITEM_BY_CR_ID , My sent Request , Next processor , F1371A

Reason and Prerequisites

User Name not shown in My sent request app in the next processor column after claimed in My inbox app.

Solution

 Implement the corrections of the SAP Note using the transaction SNOTE in your gateway back-end system.
