Symptom

A goods movement with special stock indicator E/F or Q/R is entered. The special stock valuation indicator differs in E from F or Q from R. Both should be in sync correspondingly.

Other Terms

M7242, M7-242, M7 242, SOBKZ, MSSA-KZBWS, MSSQ-KZBWS, MATDOC_EXTRACT-KZBWS, subcon, sales order stocks, project stock

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason: Program error
Prerequisites:

Recursive subcontracting (A&D subcontracting) is used in the process (special stock F or R).

  1. A goods receipt for any of the special stock combination E/F or Q/R was made. The stock got consumed (stock was made to zero again).
  2. The special stock valuation indicator (KZBWS) in the sales order or project was changed.
  3. A goods receipt was entered for E/F or Q/R which was not yet used in 1).
  4. The goods receipt in 3) took the current KZBWS from the sales order/project instead the already existing one from the material master from the prior posting 1).

Solution

Implement the attached corrections or import the relevant Support Package.
