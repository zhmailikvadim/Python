Symptom

You are in the "Classify Products" app and select a foreign trade classification number that is used as a reference number in a number set. However, the referenced numbers do not have any validity and are not transferred.

Other Terms

/SAPSLL/PR_CLWB_CUS, /SAPSLL/PR_CLWB_002, /SAPSLL/PRODUCT_CLASS_WB_CUS, reference numbers

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the correction instructions relevant for your release.
