Symptom

When you create an in-house service in the Manage In-House Services app and enter the sold-to party, the system determines the CRM Sales Organization incorrectly. The system returns inherited organizational units such as the sales office and the sales group in addition to the original CRM Sales Organization. As a result, the system determines an incorrect value for the CRM Sales Organization, which leads to incorrect filtering in subsequent fields which use value helps.

Other Terms

Sales Organization determination, Enterprise Management Layer (EML), Organizational Unit Inheritance, Value Help

Reason and Prerequisites

This issue occurs when the Enterprise Organizational Model is disabled and the system operates in the CRM organizational model. When the inheritance for the sales organization is enabled in Customizing, the system copies the sales organization to organizational units defined under the sales organizational unit, such as the sales office and the sales group. The value helps for the respective fields do not filter out these inherited organizational units, so the system includes them in the partner determination logic.

Solution

Implement the SAP Note to solve this issue.
After implementing this SAP Note, the system correctly determines the CRM Sales Organization when you create an in-house service. The system excludes inherited organizational units from the determination logic and displays only the correct CRM Sales Organization in the value helps of the corresponding fields.
