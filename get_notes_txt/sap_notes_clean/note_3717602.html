Symptom

After replicating material master changes from your MDG system to a target system, you notice that there are period gaps in tables CKMLPP and CKMLCR in the target system. This may lead to error message C+007: "No master record exists for period ... of fiscal year ...".

Other Terms

C+007, C+ 007

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Please implement the attached program correction.
