Symptom

DSO can't send meter reading results to MOS MeLo if DSO is MOS MaLo.

Other Terms

IDEXDE, MSCONS, 13017, Electricity, DSO, DMOS MaLo, /IDEXGE/CL_MSCONS_MALO_MELO,  GET_SENDER_RECEIVER_ELE, GET_MELO_MALO_RECEIVER_SENDER, GET_SENDER_RECEIVER_MELO, /IDEXGG/CL_IM_ISU_IDE_DATEX, IF_EX_ISU_IDE_DATEXCONN002~DATEXCONNECT_MREADING_OUT, /IDEXGE/ESE_MSCONS_MALO_MELO

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Please implement the correction instructions mentioned in this Note or import higher Support Packages.

Note: You might adjust your own implementation for method IF_EX_ISU_IDE_DATEXCONN002~DATEXCONNECT_MREADING_OUT of BAdI ISU_IDE_DATEXCONN002 (our implementation class /IDEXGG/CL_IM_ISU_IDE_DATEX) and enhancement section /IDEXGE/ESE_MSCONS_MALO_MELO to prevent sending meter reading results back from DSO to MOS MeLo after MOS MaLo (DSO is also MOS MaLo) receives them from MOS MeLo.
