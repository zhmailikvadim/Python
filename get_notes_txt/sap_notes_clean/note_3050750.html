Symptom

This note provides the solution for the following case:

The employee was transfered from company 1 to company 2, when you run the SSO 6-09 for this employee for company 1, the name of company 2 displays on the form. This is not correct, the name of company 1 should display.

Other Terms

HTHCINS0

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a correction of legal function.

Solution

Apply the support package specified in this note.

Alternatively, you can apply the correction instruction attached to the note.
