Symptom

  • Job postings created in SuccessFactors are marked as active and posted to the job board, but the job is not visible on Arbeitsagentur

Environment

SAP Successfactors Recruiting Posting

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Post job requisition within SuccessFactors.
  2. Verify that the job posting is marked as active and posted to the job board.
  3. Click the link to the job posting on the job board.
  4. The job is not visible on teh job board

Cause

Resolution

The Arbeitsagentur job board have the policy of removing the job from the job board any time even if the job is not unposted by customer. It is up to the job board when they want to remove the job board.

If the job is posted but is not visible on the job board , please reach out to the job board and they should be able to assist further why and when the job was removed from the job board.
