Symptom

You have created and approved a change request for resources with planning/forecast based on cost, revenue or both cost and revenue. Upon execution of period-end forecasting, EAC values are incorrectly set to zero.

Other Terms

Change Request, EAC, Approved Change Request, Forecasting Based on constant EAC, /CPD/PFP_M_A1_PF49, Period-End Forecasting, Monthly Planning, Monthly Plan.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error.

Solution

Implement the manual Correction Instruction attached to this SAP Note.
