Symptom

When a single BoM (of Material and Plant) is passed to the report RFM_PP_BOM_CONSISTENCY_CHECK, no issues are detected. However, when multiple BoM entries are processed together, the report incorrectly raises error RFM_PP_BOM_CONST_CHK008 even though each BoM validates successfully when checked individually.

Other Terms

RFM_PP_BOM_CONST_CHK008, BoM Consistency Check, Multi‑BOM Processing, Variant Article BoM, BoM Validation, Variant Article BoM &1 does not exist

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason 

This problem is caused by a program error.

Solution

Implement the correction instruction provided with this SAP Note.
