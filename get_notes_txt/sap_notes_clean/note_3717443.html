Symptom

As per the Union Budget 2026 announcement, the "Supply of Manpower" is now classified as "Work" to avoid misclassification under professional services.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Resolution

In S/4HANA, this requires the following adjustments:

  • Definition Update: Manpower supply must now be processed under section 194C (Work Contracts) rather than section 194J. 
  • Rate Correction: Systems must be configured to prevent the higher 10% deduction, fixing the TDS rate at 1% (Individuals/HUF) or 2% (Others).

Keywords

Union Budget, 2026, Manpower supply, TDS, 194C, Individuals/HUF
