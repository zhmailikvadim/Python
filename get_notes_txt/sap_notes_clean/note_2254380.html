Symptom

Error when opening a Web Intelligence report from BI Launchpad in "MODIFY" mode: "The action cannot be performed. (WIS 30650)"

Other Terms

webi, webintelligence, Zabo, WRC, Rich Client, modify, WIS 30650, desktop, preferences

Reason and Prerequisites

Environment:

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 4.2
  • Web Intelligence
  • Desktop

Steps To Reproduce:

  1. Logon to BI LaunchPad
  2. Set Web Intelligence preferences to Desktop
  3. Open any Web Intelligence report in Modify Mode
    1. >> The error "The action cannot be performed. (WIS 30650)" appears in Web Intelligence Rich Client

Cause:

This issue is caused by a defect in the product.

Solution

This issue is fixed in the patches listed in the "Support Packages & Patches" section below.
The "Support Packages & Patches" section will be populated with the relevant patch levels once they are released.
For Business Intelligence Platform maintenance schedule and strategy see the Knowledge Base Article 2144559 in References section.
