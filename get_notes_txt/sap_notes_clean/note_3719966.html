Symptom

Billing document with zero net value (free goods) is not captured in settlement document or Business Volume Report.

Reproducing the Issue

Condition Contract Management in SAP S/4HANA

Cause

In standard Business Volume table V_WB2_VBRK_VBRP_1, only billing document with VBRP-PRSFD = X will be selected. For free goods item, it's VBRP-PRSFD = B.

Resolution

Standard Design, create and use custom Business Volume table/CDS instead.

Keywords

Billing Document, BV, Business Volume, V_WB2_VBRK_VBRP_1, VBRP-PRSFD, zero net value
