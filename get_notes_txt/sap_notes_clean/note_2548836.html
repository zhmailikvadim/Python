Symptom

You want to update the 'Actual price calculation' (CO transaction KSII) or the 'Revaluation at actual price' (CO transaction RKLN) into Funds Management (FM).

However, you cannot select the corresponding business transactions for the integration in the customizing.

Environment

  • Financial Accounting (FI)
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise 4.7
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • PSM-FM Add-On is active (transaction SFW5, EA-PS is on) for this system/client

Reproducing the Issue

You do not find the business transactions KSII or RKLN in the Implementation Guide of Funds Management (transaction SPRO) -> Public Sector Management -> Funds Management Government -> Actual and Commitment Update/Integration -> Integration -> Integration with Cost and Project Controlling -> Choose Business Transactions for Integration.

Cause

The update of these CO transactions KSII and RKLN into Funds Management is not released.

Resolution

You can integrate the business transactions 'Actual price calculation' (CO transaction KSII) or the 'Revaluation at actual price' (CO transaction RKLN) by the following modification of standard:

1. In the ABAP Dictionary (Transaction SE11), change the value range of * FM_VGROUP by adding the following fixed values:

Fixed value Short text
KSII Actual price calculation
RKLN Revaluation at actual price

2. In the Implementation Guide of Funds Management (transaction SPRO), enter in the following path:

Public Sector Management -> Funds Management Government -> Actual and Commitment Update/Integration -> Integration -> Integration with Cost and Project Controlling -> Choose Business Transactions for Integration.

3. Select either 'All transactions' or the transactions 'Actual price calculation' and 'Revaluation at actual price'.

Caution:

This is considered a modification of the SAP standard system. If you want to use this modification, you have to make your own tests to ensure that the business transactions in question are updated into Funds Management according to your requirements.

Bear in mind that this modification will not be covered by SAP support for future issues related to this modification and not available in any future package.

Please read carefully the note below that talks about modification of the standard:
 
170183 - What to consider when applying modifications

You should only implement such a change at the beginning of a new fiscal year, or at least at the beginning of a period. Note that otherwise a data inconsistency between CO and FM may occur since the actual postings of the transactions listed above carried out prior to the implementation of this modification are not updated.

Keywords
