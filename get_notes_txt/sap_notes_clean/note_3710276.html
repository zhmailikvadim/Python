Symptom

You are processing RF Putaway and during WT confirmation a split exception is triggered. On the split exception screen the UoM is changed and we return to the Putaway screen to finish the WT confirmation. If the product has only 1 SUoM relevant UoM, then you may see and error message:
Unit of measure &1 is not stock-specific

Other Terms

/SCWM/L3870, SUoM, SPLT

Reason and Prerequisites

Due to code error.

Solution

Implement the correction.
