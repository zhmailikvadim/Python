Symptom

How do I trigger payload for Suppliers where initial payload was not triggered by automatic synchronization due to other errors?

Resolution

To manually trigger the payload:

  1. Verify that the Supplier has met all the requirements for initial automatic synchronization.
  2. Click Manage > SM Administration > ERP Integration > Initial Supplier Synchronization > Choose Manual Synchronization.
  3. Access the Supplier 360 Profile and navigate to ERP Data and click Sync Now.
  4. Confirm that the payload is sent and the supplier data synchronizes successfully.
  5. After synchronization completes, revert the integration setting back to Automatic.
