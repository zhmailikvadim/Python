Symptom

While renaming the Global Account in the SAP BTP Cockpit according to the Change the Display Name of Your Global Account, you would like to know whether this update also changes the Global Account name in the Global Account settings within the BTP Cockpit and in SAP for Me.

Environment

SAP Business Technology Platform

Resolution

  1. Refer to the SAP Knowledge Base Article 3444790 "How to change the Global Account's Display Name" and follow the instructions provided in the article to update the display name of the Global Account.
  2. Once done, this will update the display name of the Global Account in the Account Explorer immediately, but the name inside the Global Account settings in the Edit Global Account dialog box located in Directories & Subaccounts view will be updated after some time.
  3. Once this name is updated the corresponding name for this Global Account will also reflect in the SAP for Me
