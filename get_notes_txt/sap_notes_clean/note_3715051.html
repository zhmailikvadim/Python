Symptom

In the process "Receipt of EEG Transfer Time Series from SC (PRS View)" (DE_C_EQS_ETTS_SC_RECEIVE_PRS), the system selects the point of delivery (PoD) from the wrong view because it does not consider EQSPODVIEW when selecting data from the PDTSV table.

This issue is relevant for the market role: PRS.

Other Terms

US4G, EQS, PRS, DE_C_EQS_ETTS_SC_RECEIVE_PRS, EQSPODVIEW, PDTSV, EEG Transfer Time Series, PoD, process cluster, process step

Reason and Prerequisites

The issue occurs because of a program error.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system selects the correct PoD data from the appropriate PoD view.

To verify the fix, rerun the process and check that the system selects the correct PoD data.
