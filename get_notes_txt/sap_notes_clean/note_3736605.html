Symptom

In SAP S/4 Hana, the Business Partner is the leading master data. For every Customer/ Supplier/ Contact Person, there should be a corresponding Business Partner linked to it. In some systems, it's found that the Customer/ Supplier/ Contact Person is without its corresponding Business Partner, which is an inconsistent state.

Other Terms

CVI Link, CHECK_CVI_MAPPING, CLS4SIC_LO_MD_BP_2, SUM check, Contact Person

Reason and Prerequisites

Due to some inconsistencies during the system upgrade from ECC to ECC or S/4 HANA to S/4 HANA, some Customers/Suppliers/ Contact Persons were not linked to their corresponding Business Partners. With the code corrections in the current OSS note, such inconsistencies are identified during an ECC to ECC or S/4 HANA to S/4 HANA upgrade. This note will make sure such inconsistencies are reported during an upgrade.

Solution

Implement Note for S4CORE 109 or above releases.

For below releases from S4CORE 108, Implement note 3249240.
