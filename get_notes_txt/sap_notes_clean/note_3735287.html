Symptom

What is the maximum number of tax values that can be created in SAP Business Network?

What is the limit on Tax values in SAP Business Network?

Resolution

The rule Require suppliers to choose from your list of valid tax values when enabled, you can create up to 20 tax values in your SAP Business Network account

See Also

To configure tax values in your account, please refer How do I configure tax categories for my suppliers to choose from when invoicing?
