Symptom

Your WBT (Web-based training) course can't be played out with expected contents from Learning Portal. We need to eliminate the reason of course content itself.

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP Learning Solution 6.0

Cause

Resolution

Download the sample content from attachment, which is SCORM format with name 'RuntimeBasicCalls_SCORM12.zip'. Import and publish the Zip file into your LSO test system to check whether it can be played out correctly.

  • If this sample content can be played successfully, then the WBT can't be played issue is caused by your own course contents.
  • If this sample content also can't be played, then need to check other settings of LSO content player.

More information: 

This sample content is from the official website of SCORM in the following URL. And it's in version of 'SCORM 2004 V2'. 

https://www.scorm.com/scorm-explained/technical-scorm/golf-examples/
