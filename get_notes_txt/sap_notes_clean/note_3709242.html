Symptom

This SAP Note provides you with changes to CDS views for Mexico statutory reports in the Run Statutory Reports app for SAP Document and Reporting Compliance. The updates to CDS views address the enablement of the  Type of Business field (J_1KFTBUS column) present in the Supplier Master table (LFA1).

The Type of Business field enables you to identify the operation type of your suppliers as required by the Mexican tax authority, SAT. The Type of Business (Order Type) field is mapped with the transaction type and the tax grouping version in the MXTVO customizing table.

Other Terms

DRC, Mexico

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Mexico.

Solution

After implementing this SAP Note, the Type of Business field is enabled in the CDS View I_MX_BusinessPartner. As a result, you can identify the operation type of your suppliers in the Type of Business field within the Supplier Master table (LFA1) for Mexico statutory reports.
