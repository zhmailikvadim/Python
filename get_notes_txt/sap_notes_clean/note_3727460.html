Symptom

  • Users are unable to share tickets after completing the booking workflow as a guest.
  • When booking with an account, the ticket sharing functionality works correctly, and a pop-up appears to share the marked ticket.
  • When booking as a guest, the workflow ends without displaying the pop-up to share the marked ticket.

Environment

SAP Event Ticketing - Online Shop

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Find an event where tickets can be shared with others.
  2. Add at least two tickets to the shopping cart to enable sharing one of the tickets.
  3. Mark one ticket as the ticket to be shared with another user.
  4. Complete the booking workflow as a guest.
  5. Observe that after a successful booking, no pop-up appears to share the marked ticket.

Cause

System works as designed.

Resolution

  1. Contact the Customer Engagement (CE) team for further assistance.
  2. Open a feature request via the influencer tool to address the functionality gap for guest users.
