Symptom

A dump CX_VCM_INVALID_INPUT occurs while saving the Sales Order after item modification.

This issue occurs when items are deleted using the report VCM_RT_CLEANUP_ITEM. During this process, there is no verification of the VCM linkage in the VBAP table.

This oversight can lead to inconsistencies where item details exist in VBAP but not in VCM. As a result, errors occur during subsequent Sales Order processing, such as item addition or modification, leading to the dump.
