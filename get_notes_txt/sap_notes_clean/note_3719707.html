Symptom

When executing WB21, the system returns the following errors:

  • Error WV185 "No basic purchase price relevant to pricing found with schema YGP010": occurs when entering sales price.
  • Error WV186 "Calculation schema YGS010 of appl. V is not capable of price calculation": occurs when entering the purchase price.

When updating to "released" status in WB22, the sales price appears blank.

Environment

  • Settlement Management
  • Agency Business
  • Condition Contract Management
  • Condition Contract Settlement
  • Trading Contract
  • SAP S/4 HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access transaction WB21.
  2. Register free purchase details.
  3. Enter sales price.
  4. Encounter error WV185 "No basic purchase price relevant to pricing found with schema YGP010".
  5. Update to "released" status and register.
  6. Access transaction WB22.
  7. Observe that the sales price is blank.
  8. Register free sales details in WB21.
  9. Enter purchase price.
  10. Encounter error WV186 "Calculation schema YGS010 of appl. V is not capable of price calculation".

Cause

For the item category, the item class has been set as "B" (Free of Charge Item without Price Entry).

Resolution

  1. Ensure that SAP Notes 3326430, 3513119, 3533513, and 3554526 are implemented.
  2. If the item category contains free-of-charge items without price entry, remove the "required" flag from the corresponding pricing procedures.
  3. Change the item class from "B" (Free of Charge Item without Price Entry) to "A" (Free of Charge Item with Price Entry) and set the price to zero.
