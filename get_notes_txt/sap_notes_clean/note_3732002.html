Symptom

Purchase Orders in SAP ERP or SAP S/4HANA show the amount as quantity and the unit price as 1 after transfer from SAP Ariba B&I.

Cause

The Purchase Order is an amount-based PO in SAP Ariba B&I. This can be confirmed by checking the payload sent from SAP Ariba B&I to CIG, where the <ReceivingType> field is set to 3 for this type of PO.

Resolution

This behavior is expected and standard. SAP Ariba B&I  allows exporting amount-based Purchase Orders to SAP ERP or SAP S/4HANA.

For amount-based POs:

  • The total amount is mapped to the quantity field in ERP or S4/HANA
  • The unit price is set to 1

 For example, if the amount of the purchase order in the SAP Ariba B&I is $95, it is exported to SAP ERP or SAP S/4HANA with a quantity of 95 and with a price of $1.
