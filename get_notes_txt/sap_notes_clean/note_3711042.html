Symptom

When you run payroll for a terminated employee who receives payments (such as bonuses) which are subject to supplementary NHI after their leaving date, the system calculates the supplemental National Health Insurance (NHI) premium using the current NHI amount instead of the NHI amount on the leaving date.

According to Article 6 of the Regulations for the Withholding and Payment of Supplemental National Health Insurance Premiums (https://law.moj.gov.tw/LawClass/LawAll.aspx?pcode=L0060027), when a regular employee receives payments which are subject to supplementary NHI after termination, the system should calculate the insurance premium using the NHI amount on the leaving date, rather than the NHI amount on the payment date.

For example:

- Leaving date: 2024.04.01
- NHI Class in IT0355: 15
- NHI Status: Transfer out, Quit or Stop
- NHI Amount at leaving date: 53,000 TWD
- Bonus payment date: 2026.01.15
- NHI Amount at 2026.01.15: 55,400 TWD

In this scenario, when you run payroll for the bonus payment, the system uses 53,000 TWD as the NHI amount.

Other Terms

SNHI Premium, NHI Amount, Transfer out, Quit,

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a correction of legal function

Solution

Apply the corresponding HR Support Package or correction instructions in this SAP Note.
