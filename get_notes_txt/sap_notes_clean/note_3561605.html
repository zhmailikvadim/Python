Symptom

  • HANA_UMML is installed in S/4HANA backend
  • A role is missing that HANA_UMML should have created: sap.hana.umml.umml4hana.Roles::ManageModels 

Environment

  • S/4HANA On Prem 2019/2020/2021/2022/2023
  • Predictive Analytics integrator (PAi) - HANA_UMML
  • Intelligent Scenario Lifecycle Management (ISLM) - F4470
  • Intelligent Scenarios - F4469
  • SAP HANA Automated Predictive Library (APL)
  • Application Function Library (AFL)

Resolution

  • sap.hana.umml.umml4hana.Roles::ManageModels role is not used in HANA_UMML 1.12.x version. You can grant sap.hana.pai::ExecutePAI role to S/4HANA Schema DB user. 
  • Before above, make sure Pre-Requisites of ISLM is done. And you can also refer to 2868156 - Error: "Version Incompatibility" when opening ISLM app
