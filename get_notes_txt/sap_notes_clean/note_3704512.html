Symptom

Unable to add the custom fields to the bank details of the BP with customer or vendor roles.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Transaction BP.
  2. Under the Payment Transactions tab.
  3. How to add custom field in the Bank Details.

Cause

The enhancement category of the Business Partner structure BUS_EI_BANKDETAIL has been set to "cannot be enhanced" because the current external interface of the business partner and the inline APIs do not support the data transfer for the new/custom fields. 

Refer the SAP NOTE 2984675

Resolution

SAP standard system behavior


See Also

2984675 -  Business Partner external interface structures cannot be enhanced

Keywords

Bank details, Custom, fields, custom fields, BUS_EI_BANKDETAIL, Payment Transactions
