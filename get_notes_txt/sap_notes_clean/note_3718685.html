Symptom

When having a column with quote total shown in quote list, there is a mismatch between that value and the one shown inside the quote.

Environment

SAP CPQ

Reproducing the Issue

Define quote total as a column in quote list. 

  1. Open a quote
  2. Check its total
  3. Go back to quote list, quote total differs in column.

Cause

Calculations being done in scripting can cause such issue with improper saving method is used.

Resolution

It is important to have Quote.Calculate() at the end of any script which is responsible for performing calculations.

Keywords

Quote Total, Quote List, Column, Calculation, CPQ
