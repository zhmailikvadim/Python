Symptom

In the process "Calculation of Energy Amounts (MOS MaLo)" (DE_B_MET_ENERGY_AMOUNT_CALC_MOSMALO), step 0350 calculated an incorrect energy amount for complex installations.

This issue is relevant for the market role: MOS.

Other Terms

MET, Calculation formula, Complex installation, Consumption, Feed-in, OBIS code 1.9.*, OBIS code 2.9.*, MSCONS 13019

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because of a program error.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system calculates the correct energy amount for complex installations in step 0350. You need to initiate a new process of DE_B_MET_ENERGY_AMOUNT_CALC_MOSMALO. You can verify the fix by checking that the calculated energy amount value is correct.
