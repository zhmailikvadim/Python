Symptom

You created a service contract for a vehicle and added a coverage item with a range defined in units different than distance/mileage (e.g. hours). Now you want to create service order with relation to that service contract. Even if given vehicle fullfills the contract criteria, system incorrectly shows the contact item as invalid. Instead of reading engine hour counter from vehicle master data, system tries to compare mileage odometer to contract coverage range and is unable to convert distance value into time-based unit.

Other Terms

Service Contract, VSS vehicle, contract coverage, mileage range

Reason and Prerequisites

This is due to program error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction.
