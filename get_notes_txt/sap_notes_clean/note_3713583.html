Symptom

In Business User Management we provided business partner grouping BPEE for internal number range and business partner grouping BPEX for external number range. In case this business partner groupings were not used we detected the standard business partner groupings based on the business partner customizing (Maintenance View V_TB001, Transaction S_ABA_72000040).

This pre-delivered business partner groupings are not sufficient for some customers and they requested the possibility to use self-defined business partner groupings for business users.

Other Terms

TB001 BUM BP Grouping FS4BPU_B_BP_GROUPING_ENH

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing BAdI Definition for self-defined business partner groupings.

Solution

Implement the correction instructions. Alternatively import the support package.

This SAP Note contains manual activities which you need to execute.

Afterwards use transaction SE20 and create a BAdI implemention based on BAdI Definition FS4BPU_B_BP_GROUPING_ENH.
