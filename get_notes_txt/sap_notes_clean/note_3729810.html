Symptom

In Service Order ODATA V4 API, the user creates a service order and partner with document based partner address in the post request, but the partner address from the payload is saved. Instead the partner address from master data is saved in the database.

Other Terms

Service Order ODATA V4 API, Partner address in service document

Reason and Prerequisites

The issue occurs only in the create when partner address is created during the creation of the service order. In update case, it works as expected.

Solution

Please apply the attached correction instruction or the relevant support package.
