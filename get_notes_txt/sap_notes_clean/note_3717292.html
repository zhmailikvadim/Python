Symptom

I need to create a user role that gives an individual the ability to view job postings, workers, and work orders in one business unit

Resolution

The Business units can be managed on user level, not on User role level

When you are creating user role there is no option to limit the visibility for business unit

During the creation of new user you can limit the visibility on Cost object, Business units and Sites

Keywords

limit, user role, specific business unit, one, BU
