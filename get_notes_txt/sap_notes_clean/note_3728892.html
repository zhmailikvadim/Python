Symptom

After enabling the feature PL-16983 – Enable Act As, I am able to Act as Enterprise Users. However, the requirement is that I should only be able to act as Supplier Users and should be prevented from acting as Enterprise Users.

Environment

SAP Ariba Buying

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Sign into your SAP Ariba application.
  2. Navigate to User Manager > Users.
  3. Search for an Enterprise or Supplier user.
  4. The Act As button is not available for the user. 

Cause

The feature PL-16983 – Enable Act As allows you to Act as other users under User Manager > Users, including Enterprise Users.

Resolution

After enabling the feature PL-16983 - Enable Act As, users who belong to either the Customer Administrator or Act As group can Act as both Enterprise and Supplier users.
Users are also able to act as Enterprise Users, which is intended behavior. The Act As feature is designed to allow users to act as other users under User Manager > Users, including Enterprise Users.

If the requirement is to restrict users from acting as Enterprise users, this functionality can be achieved through a customization process offered by SAP Services. To explore this option, please contact your Customer Success Partner (CSP), who can guide you through the process and assess the feasibility of implementing this customization.
