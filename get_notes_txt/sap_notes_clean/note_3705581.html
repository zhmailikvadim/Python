Symptom

  • DCD status is updated to 'Rejected' for the blocked sales order.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a Sales order.
  2. DCD is generated for the sales order due to credit block.
  3. Observe that the DCD status is updated to "Rejected" due to the blocked sales order.

Resolution

On apply a reason for rejection for every item in the sales document. The DCD status is updated to "Rejected".
