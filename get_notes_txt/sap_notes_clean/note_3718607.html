Symptom

You have set up a charge condition with date categories relative to the periodic settlement.  You have set up an amount limit condition to limit the charge condition. The key date of the settlement time period is changed before the next settlement due date (for example, from day 3 to day 7). Directly before the next settlement run, you make a retroactive change for accounts with such a condition setting. After executing the account settlement run, you notice that the full charge amount has been posted. The amount limit condition has not limited the charge amount.

Other Terms

bca_acbal_acc_m, BDC,

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

The problem occurs if

  1. Product Attribute 'Recalculate Immediately When Change is Made' is selected in the product
  2. The charge condition has date categories relative to the periodic settlement
  3. The key date of the settlement time period is changed (for example, from day 3 to day 7 of a month) after last settlement
  4. A retroactive change is applied immediately before the next account settlement run

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction (CI) or the relevant Support Package.
