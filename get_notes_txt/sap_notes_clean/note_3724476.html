Symptom

When creating a billing document from the "Create billing documents" app, the TTX1 is not calculated automatically, while using pricing procedure A11001 (Services GB). 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the "Create billing documents" app.
  2. Select a document from the list
  3. Click on the "Create Billing Documents" button.
  4. Observe that the temporary invoice page opens, and the tax amount is displayed as 0 GBP, despite the tax classification being maintained in the customer and product master.

Cause

In the pricing procedure A11001, TTX1 is set as a manual condition.

Resolution

The TTX1 condition is not automatically called in the documents because, in the standard pricing procedure A11001, it's configured as a manual condition type. 

In this case, according to the standard configuration, TTX1 must be added manually in the pricing procedure for the tax to be calculated.
