Symptom

For master data maintenance of telephone numbers or mobile phone numbers, they are all stored in table ADR2. It is important to know how to distinguish them.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

  • Sales and Distribution (SD)
  • Logistics Execution (LE)
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP NetWeaver

Reproducing the Issue

Maintain telephone numbers or mobile phone numbers for business partner using t-code BP.

Both of them get saved in table ADR2.

Resolution

Field ADR2-R3_USER is used to register whether a phone is a mobile phone or not.

More detailed information can be found in help document https://help.sap.com/docs/ABAP_PLATFORM_NEW/a10e348e95cd4b679cc727859578c3ac/4e4a2d4fe8801ecfe10000000a42189b.html
