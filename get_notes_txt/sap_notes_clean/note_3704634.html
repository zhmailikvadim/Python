Symptom

How to request for the activation of Joule AI on your Incentive Management environment. 

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Sales Performance Management
    • Incentive Management

Cause

Resolution

  1. Work with your Account Executive to obtain a BTP subaccount with proper entitlements.
  2. Ensure the tenant is associated with a cost center, as this is required for setting up the AI bot.
  3. Once the BTP subaccount is ready and the cost center is associated, open a new support ticket and request for Joule activation.
