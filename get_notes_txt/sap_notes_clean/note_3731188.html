Symptom

When running the Bulgaria VAT (BG_VAT_DCL) the vendor VAT registration in protocols is incorrectly displayed as 999999999999999 for EU countries.

Other Terms

Bulgaria, Bulgaria VAT Report, BG VAT Report, BG, DRC, VAT Registration number, 999999999999999, Greece, GR, EL

Reason and Prerequisites

This note is only valid for Bulgaria.

Solution

A correction to the method for determining the VAT region in the case for protocols was created.

A special case was added for Greece due to their unique VAT number starting with EL and not GR.
