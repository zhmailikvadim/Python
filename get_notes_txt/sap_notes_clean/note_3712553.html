Symptom

You are trying to replicate employee master data, but the following errors are triggered:

  • Entry &1 &2 does not exist in T001P (check entry)
    Message No. 00058
       
  • Pernr &3: Infotype 0001 with error
    Message No. ECPAO_IN_PROCESSING076

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central integration to SAP Business Suite;
  • Employee Master Data replication;
  • Business Integration Builder (BIB);
  • Point To Point (PTP).

Cause

You are replicating a Job Information record that contains a combination of Company/Personnel Area and Location/Personnel Subarea that is not configured in T001P.

Resolution

Check T001P to see the available combinations of personnel area and subarea, then change the current combination of the affected Job Information record to a valid one. In case that the combination that is triggering the error is required, maintain T001P to make this entry available.

Keywords

Employee, Master Data, Replication, BIB, PTP, IT0001, 00058, ECPAO_IN_PROCESSING076, T001P.
