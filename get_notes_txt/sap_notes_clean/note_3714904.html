Symptom

I do not see the option to message my buyer in my SAP Business Network account. How can I get this option?

Resolution

In order to see the Message tool in your SAP Business Network account, there are some requirements:

  • Your buyer must enable this feature for your supplier account. If your customer confirms that the feature is enabled for your account, see below.
  • Your account must be at the enterprise level. If you see that you have a standard account, you can upgrade

When both requirements are met, you will see the messaging feature available in your account.
