Symptom

Users need to understand the estimated provisioning time for various services, including SCI, Suite foundation services, S/4HC, and SuccessFactors (SF), when initiated through SAP For Me. 

Environment

  • SAP managed Workforce Management

Resolution

The following table provides the typical time required for provisioning various applications and services necessary for SAP Managed Workforce Management:

Application/ServiceTypical Time for ProvisioningRemarks
SAP Cloud IdentityUp to 5 minutes 
Foundation For SAP Business SuiteUp to 15 minutes 
SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public EditionUp to 24 hoursMore than 24 hours can be considered an issue.
SAP SuccessFactors HCMUp to 1 hour 
Integration for Workforce Management30 minutes
