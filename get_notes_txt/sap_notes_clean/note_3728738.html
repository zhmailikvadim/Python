Symptom

In the process "End of Supply (DSO View)" (DE_B_GPKE_DSO_EOS), the market role "NB" (grid operator) was missing in the header section.
This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

GPKE, End of Supply, DE_B_GPKE_DSO_EOS, NB, grid operator, header section, market role

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because the market role "NB" was not included in the header section of the process DE_B_GPKE_DSO_EOS.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system includes the market role "NB" in the header section of the process "End of Supply (DSO View)" (DE_B_GPKE_DSO_EOS). The BC sets /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP07_NOTE_3728738 and /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP08_NOTE_3728738 contain the necessary corrections. You can verify the fix by retesting the scenario and checking that the market role "NB" appears in the header section of the process.
