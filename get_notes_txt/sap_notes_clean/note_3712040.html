Symptom

I approved a Self Registration Request, but the supplier did not receive any email notification of the approval.

Resolution

The system does not notify the supplier when a Self Registration Request is approved. It only sends notifications when the request is submitted or rejected by the customer.

See Also

After approving the self-registration request, invite the supplier to complete their registration.

The invitation email serves as the approval notification.
