Symptom

The company code is located in the country (T001-LAND1) with the national currency (T005-WAERS) = UNI and has the local currency (T001-WAERS) = USD. You want to use the program RFUMSV00 for the tax report and the tax authorities of the country require the currency UNI.

Other Terms

RFUMSV00, S_ALR_87012357

Reason and Prerequisites

The declaration to the tax authorities must typically be made in the national currency (T005-WAERS). However, most of the company-code-specific reports from SAP use the local currency T001-WAERS. This leads to the following problem:

In RFUMSV00, you can use the "Alternative Local Currency" function. The option (parameter) ALCUR is not always visible and depends on the setting of the "Alternative local currency activated?" indicator (T000F-XALHW). However, the functionality is for static exchange rates that do not change over time. It was developed at the time of the introduction of the euro for the joining currencies, when the exchange rates between the joining currencies and the euro were already fixed. However, the alternative local currency functionality usually means that the documents must be converted and translated twice: UNI -> USD -> UNI. But this calculated amount is often not identical to the original amount WRBTR, which is usually not permitted and leads to problems.

It is also possible to activate the extensive plants abroad functionality (T000F-XWIAA). For more information about this, see the release notes in SAP Note 882132 - Plants abroad: Release notes, tax base, cash discount base. Then, the values are updated in the LW* amount fields in BSET in the national currency UNI and can be directly reported in RFUMSV00 (indicator: Nat.Crcy Instead of Local Crcy (PAR_XSTW)). One disadvantage of this workaround is that the LW* amount fields can be filled relatively 'freely' (BTE 2051); there is no link from our side to amounts in accounting (for example, second or third local currency).

Important: The fields LWBAS and LWSTE do not exist in BSEG; they only exist in BSET. Therefore, they do not influence the balance of a document.

Solution

In such cases, SAP recommends that you choose the local currency (T001-WAERS) = UNI and use a second local currency = USD as the group currency.
