Symptom

This SAP Note provides you with changes in NF_IMPOSTO_CALCULO HANA view to address the reporting of Oil and Gas tax condition amounts in PIS and COFINS taxes.

The NF_DOCUMENTO HANA view was also changed to address the reporting of CPF of one time customers in Nota Fiscal.

Other problem covered by this SAP Note is an error regarding ECD_J210 register. When you generate this register, transitory accounts (COD_NAT = 09) are being reported.

Other Terms

TDF, Brazil, SPED, EFD Contribuições, PCO, Oil and Gas, NF_IMPOSTO_CALCULO, PIS, COFINS

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Brazil.

You have implemented SAP Note 2825102.

You have upgraded your system to SAP TDF Support Package 13.

Solution

After implementing this SAP Note, the following fields of NF_IMPOSTO_CALCULO HANA view will receive amounts for Oil and Gas tax conditions.

Also view ECD_J210 will be updated to not consider accounts with COD_NAT = 09 as result accounts.
