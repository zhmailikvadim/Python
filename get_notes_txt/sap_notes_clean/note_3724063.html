Symptom

The release of an account terminates with an error. The short text of the error message is "Local currency amount exceeds the largest postable amount".

Other Terms

B_WHGNR

BETRH_KK

/MSG/RVTGANTKO-BET_VTGNR

>U262

CX_SY_CONVERSION_OVERFLOW.

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisites:

  • When account A1 is released, the system generates account A2 for a participating treaty.
  • The release process the generated accounts in the sequence A1, A2.
  • Account A1 has an original currency or an account currency that matches the local currency of the company code.
  • Account A2 has an original currency and an account currency that are both different from the local currency of the company code.
  • Account A2 has a local currency that differs from the local currency of account A1.
  • The account A2 has a posting with an amount that exceeds 13 digits translated into the local currency.

Reason: The system only takes into account the currencies of account A1. Therefore, the system does not split the amount of account A2 that is too high.

Solution

History:
March 9, 2026: New

Troubleshooting Description:
The system takes into account the currencies of each account to be released and splits the amount that is too high into smaller amounts before it calls the connected current account system.
