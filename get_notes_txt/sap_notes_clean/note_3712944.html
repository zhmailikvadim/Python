Symptom

  • If a Data Action has more than 100 lines in the script of an Advanced Formula, the cursor is not visible when positioned at the beginning of the line
  • Also the beginning of all lines are displayed incorrectly

Environment

SAC Analytics Cloud 2025.21.11

Reproducing the Issue

Create a Data Action with a script that content more than 100 lines

Resolution

The solution of this issue is currently under investigation. Please follow this KBA for updates.
