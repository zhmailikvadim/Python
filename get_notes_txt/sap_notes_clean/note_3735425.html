Symptom

  • For raw materials with planning procedure 3, safety stock and planned independent requirements (PIRs) are included in the availability check.
  • In the Product View (/SAPAPO/RRP3), entries such as “SStk Req.” (safety stock as requirement) and “FC req.” (planned independent requirements) are considered in net requirements.

Environment

  • Product: SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition
  • Supply Creation-Based Confirmation (SBC)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Product View using transaction /SAPAPO/RRP3.
  2. Select the relevant planning version, the affected product, and location.
  3. Review the time series and observe that “SStk Req.” and “FC req.” entries are included in the net requirements calculation.

Resolution

SBC initiates the availability check within PP/DS and does a net requirements calculation. This net requirements calculation considers safety stock and planned independent requirements as relevant requirements.
