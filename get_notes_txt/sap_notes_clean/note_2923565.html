Symptom

You usе My Inbox configured to load tasks from BPM task provider through OData. You configure an unplanned substitution rule specifying a substitute user. In My Inbox of the substitute user, he or she selects "Substitute for" from the menu and switches on the substitution. You have claimed a task and you are now the owner of the task. Your substitute user is able to see the task and has an active "Release" button, even though he shouldn't be able to release the task as he is not the actual owner.

Other Terms

SAP BPM, Business Process Management

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

To correct this problem, apply the patch matching your support package version as listed in the "Support Packages & Patches" table below. Follow the instructions in the SAP NetWeaver Support Package Stack Guide.
