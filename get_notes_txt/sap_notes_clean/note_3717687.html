Symptom

 JAWS announces "Press spacebar to open the sorting and filtering menu" on every cell of the cellerator.

Other Terms

 JAWS, accessibility, table, cellerator, column header, rows, aria-description, aria-label, cell

Reason and Prerequisites

The aria-label of the header is "Item, Press spacebar to open the sorting and filtering menu", which makes every cell column to repeat additional information that are irrelevant.

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction or the mentioned Support Package.
