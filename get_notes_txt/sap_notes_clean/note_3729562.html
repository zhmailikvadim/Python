Symptom

System is re-calculating the Split Amount based on the Expected Amount field being changed by the User.

Environment

Production and Test

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a Service Requisition with Expected and Maximum Amount of 100.
  2. Create Split Accounting based on Amount totaling 100.
  3. Update the Expected amount on the line item to 1.
  4. Observe that the Split accounting is re-calculated based on 1.

Cause

Expected behavior.

Resolution

Refer to the Purchasing Guide for Procurement Professionals for details on the current system design: The accounting splits for service line items are configured based on the Expected amount.
Any changes to the Expected amount result in recalculation of the splits.
