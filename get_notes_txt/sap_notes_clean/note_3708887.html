Symptom

How do I subscribe to certificate update notifications and alerts as a supplier on my SAP Business Network supplier account?

Resolution

Here's how you can subscribe to certificate notifications and alerts:

  1. Sign in to your SAP Business Network supplier account
  2. Click [User Initials] > Settings 
  3. Under Account Administration, click Notifications > General tab
  4. Under Other Notifications, select the check box for each notification type you want to activate and add required email address under the following fields:
    • Network Service 
    • Certification Expiration Notifications
    • Other Notifications 

Please note: You may enter up to three comma-separated email addresses per field.

You may receive all these notification emails from: sapcloudsupport@alerts.ondemand.com

See Also

To access the Notifications page, you must be the account administrator or a user with the Company Information permission enabled for you by the administrator. 

Contact your account administrator if you need additional assistance in setting the notification preferences.

For more information on what each of these notification types mean, refer to: SAP Help Document: General Notifications
