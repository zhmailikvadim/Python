Symptom

Reporting tasks are available for other countries but not for a specific country, even after providing the correct details in the Run Statutory App.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA On premise
  • SAP Fiori
  • SAP S/4HANA 2023 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Fiori app Run Statutory Reports.
  2. Enter the required details: Reporting Entity Name, Report Category Name, and Reporting Year. Click the Go button
  3. Observe that no reporting tasks are displayed, and a message box with the warning "Warning No Data" appears on the screen.

Cause

The issue occurred due to the fiscal year variant not being maintained for that country in the system.

Resolution

  1. Please maintain the Fiscal Year Variant (for example : QT) in the following path:
    SPRO → SAP Reference IMG → Financial Accounting → Financial Accounting Global Settings → Fiscal Year → Maintain Fiscal Year Variant
  2. Once maintained retest the issue.
