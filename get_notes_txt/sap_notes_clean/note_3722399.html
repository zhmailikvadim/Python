Symptom

When clicking "Software constraints" in app Maintain Bills of Material, the error message "Navigation to this application is not supported" is displayed.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the app Maintain Bills of Material.
  2. Click on the "Software constraints" button.
  3. Observe the error message "Navigation to this application is not supported".

Cause

Resolution

The 'Software Constraints' functionality is not applicable in the Public Cloud environment and is planned to be removed in a future release. It is suggested to review the app Maintain Bill Of Material/F1813A(Version 2), in which this button has already been removed. 

Keywords

navigation to this application is not supported, software constraints, Maintain Bill Of Material
