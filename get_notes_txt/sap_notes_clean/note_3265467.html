Symptom

Transport of access policies and custom roles/role collections are not possible in CPI.

Environment

  • SAP Cloud Integration
  • SAP Integration Suite

Cause

The Transport of access policies and custom roles/role collections are not supported as for now.

Resolution

The Transport of artifact level and package are supported in CPI as for now.
