Symptom

The consistent failure message observed is: "Negative stock not permitted in loa, material, istock"

Environment

SAP Business ByDesign

Cause

Negative stock during the "Applying Inventory Changes" step while saving GoodsAndActivityConfirmation.

Resolution

Manually maintain stock to resolve the consistent error message: "Negative stock not permitted in loa VP03-000, material VP-FS-SPRITZE-1950, istock 07/2025."
