Symptom

You are creating sales orders out of service documents.

1)      If the sales order does not have the same transaction number as the service document, then the service document is not displayed in the sales order document flow.

2)      The predecessor service document is always shown with status ‘Archived’ in the docflow of the sales order item. In the docflow of the Sales Order header the status is displayed correctly.

Other Terms

VA03, VBFA,

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement attached correction instruction.
