Symptom

I have edited a custom field on a worker record, but the worker is not included in the next Active Worker Delta Single Line Download transaction even though Delta Compare is enabled.

Cause

When the Delta Compare setting "All enabled columns Defaulted as Delta Compare Fields" is active, custom fields added to the connector after the feature was enabled may not be automatically recognized for delta comparison due to a system limitation.

Resolution

You must manually define the delta compare fields to ensure all columns, including custom fields, are tracked for changes. Follow these steps:

  1. Sign in with your Configuration Manager account.
  2. Click Connector Catalog.
  3. Open the connector Active Worker Delta Single Line Download.
  4. Click Actions > Define Delta Compare Fields.
  5. In the bottom section, unselect All enabled columns Defaulted as Delta Compare Fields.
  6. In the top section, select all fields you wish to track, then click Add Selected.
  7. Click Update.
