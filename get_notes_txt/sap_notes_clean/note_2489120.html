Symptom

When entering a Distribution List  domain @exchange.sap.corp the following message appears. 


When looking into the Outlook address book, it looks like the distribution list can be used with different format DL_xxxxxxxxxxxx@sap.com.

Unfortunately, the mails are not delivered to the members of the DL, though the DL appears in the header of the mail message delivered.

Environment

  • HCI-DS

Reproducing the Issue

Add Distribution List (DL) for your notification .

Resolution

To use DL one need to provide an email address that is accessible from outside the corporate SAP network.
