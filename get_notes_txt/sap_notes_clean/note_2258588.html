Symptom

When ATLAS data for a delivery are shown, country of origin and country of destination (the "Ch. route" tab) are always empty.

For a created delivery also transit countries are not shown.

In a notification record these countries (the "Tran. route" tab) are shown correctly.

Solution

Implement changes according to the correction instructions.
