Symptom

RMK instance was created on the wrong datacenter. How to move the RMK instance from one datacenter to another?

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Marketing

Resolution

The Support Team cannot perform instance migrations.

Sales Account Executive or CSP must be engaged to book a DC migration order. They will then contact the Cloud Operations Migration team to start the process.
