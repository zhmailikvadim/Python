Symptom

When you check the schedule line in the sales order, you find that the material availability date is later than expected even though ATP is not active for this sales order and there are no special settings for the shipping point.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

There is an individual purchase requisition for the sales order. When the system calculates the material availability date, the goods receipt processing time, purchasing department processing time and planned delivery time in the individual purchase requisition will be considered. 

Resolution

Check the relevant time parameter settings in the individual purchase requisition. To know more about the calculation, refer to SAP Note 210455 - Confirmation date of TAB / TAS items.

Keywords

Schedule line date, Confirmation, MBDAT, TAS, T399D-BZTEK, Scheduling, Individual requisition, Material availability date, Postpone, Goods receipt processing time, Purchasing department processing time, Planned delivery time.
