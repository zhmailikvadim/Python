Symptom

  • High number of lock entries in SM12
  • Locks are not released for an extended period
  • The maximum number of locks has been reached
  • Locks are generated even after logging off from the system
  • Locks remain even after the browser is closed

Environment

  • SAP Transportation Management
  • S/HANA embedded TM

Resolution

Read and follow the suggestions in the Support Content page Lock Handling.
