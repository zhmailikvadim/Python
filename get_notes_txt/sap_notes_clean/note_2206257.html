Symptom

You perform the "CBC Amount Payer" business transaction for a policy. You then execute an additional change retroactively. The "Change CBC Amount Payer" business transaction appears in the scheduling dialog. In this case, a default setting is not provided for the business transaction, neither in the column "with user interaction" nor in the column "without user interaction".

Other Terms

Cash-Before-Cover

Reason and Prerequisites

Implement SAP Note 2221547 in advance.

You use the Cash-Before-Cover scenario.

Solution

The corrections are delivered in a Support Package. For an advance correction, perform the manual tasks.
