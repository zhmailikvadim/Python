Symptom

Audits or internal reviews flag SAP-delivered “service users” on AS Java/NWA.

Need for a single reference that explains who creates these users, their purpose, and the security policy applied by default.

Environment

SAP NetWeaver AS Java 7.5 (including systems with NetWeaver Administrator and optionally SAP Enterprise Portal, KM, MII and others).

Cause

SAP-delivered internal service users are created by specific AS Java services/components to perform technical background tasks and internal APIs.

They are not intended for end-user logon.

They do not expose information to end users and are not intended for interactive use.

Locking, deleting, converting to dialog, or forcing password rotations outside SAP guidance can break system operations.

Resolution

Do not delete, convert, or assign dialog/interactive logon to these users.

Do not force password-expiration policies that break technical communication.

Keep role assignments as delivered. Changing roles can break the owning component.

If a required service user is missing or locked, redeploy/repair the owning component or restore default settings rather than recreating it manually.
