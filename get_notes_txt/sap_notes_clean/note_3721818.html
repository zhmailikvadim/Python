Symptom

When documents are posted into the future (posting date greater than today), they do not get processed properly regarding the balance reconciliation and the update of the clearing fields when they are reversed.

Other Terms

FINS_ACTIVATE_OIM, FINS_ACTIVATE_XLGCLR, FINS_SWITCH_TO_OPEN_ITEM, FINS_SWITCH_XLGCLR

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Limitation

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or upgrade to the latest support package.
