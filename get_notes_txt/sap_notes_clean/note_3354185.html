Symptom

  • There are no WBS field available in the Process Receivable apps.
  • There is a requirement to filter the Process Receivable by WBS (project Code).

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to App Process Receivables.
  2. Press the Adapt Filter button.

Resolution

WBS Element filter option is not available for the App Process Collections Worklist and App Process Receivables.

As given in the FAQ document help portal document link, the Manage Customer Line Items app displays line items from a financial operations point of view. It shows the invoiced amount, when this amount is due to be paid, and whether the invoiced amount has been dunned already. Usually, there is exactly one line item per invoice, which shows the full invoiced amount and its payment terms.

Information about profit centers, WBS elements, and segments is related to General Ledger (G/L) Accounting. To display this type of information, use the Display Line Items in General Ledger app instead.

Refer, the help portal document for the App Process Receivables. Purpose of the App is to display information about the customer open receivables, credit memos, credit information, and the contact history.

Similarly, for the App Process Collections Worklist it is designed for the collection specialist to collect open receivables. Refer the help portal documentation.
