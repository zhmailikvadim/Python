Symptom

Canceling an Invoice document in Ariba network is resulting in error: InvoiceCancelFailed: Delayed Processing Requested

Reproducing the Issue

1. Create an invoice.
2. Approve the invoice in Ariba B&I to generate the Invoice Reconciliation document and approve it to Reconciled status.
3. Initiate cancellation of the the invoice from Business Network. Cxml DOC-54 error is issued: InvoiceCancelFailed: Delayed Processing Requested

Resolution

Suppliers can cancel invoices that do not have an invoice status of Reconciled, Paying or Paid. This has been mentioned in the documentation Cancel Invoices.

Keywords

Cancel Invoice, invoice cxml error, DOC-54
