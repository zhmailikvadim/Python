Symptom

 CPE terms are not calculated correctly after amending a deal.

Other Terms

/DMBE/;CPE Redesign; Advanced CPE; Deal Capture; Commodity Pricing Engine; Amend Deal; Formula Mapping; Term Calculation.

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason: The mapping logic in the CPE Redesign Adapter fails to correctly set update indicators for new terms when a formula is switched during an amendment.

Prerequisites: Advanced CPE (Redesign) is active and the system is configured to use the CPE Populator for deal amendments.

Solution

Adjusted the ABAP logic in the CPE Data Mapper and Term Handler to ensure proper identification of new terms.
