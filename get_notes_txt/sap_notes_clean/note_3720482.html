Symptom

You run HR-HK: Periodic MPF contribution report (format: eMPF) (HHKCPFE0) report to generate eMPF data and encountered the following issues:

  • For existing members without date of employment, date of employment was displayed as initial value '00000000' instead of blank in downloaded files.
  • When checking the checkbox "Separate termination file" for terminated members, a termination file and a contribution file were generated. However, the date of employment was not optional and always displayed in contribution file although status is 'E'.
  • When checking "Include Credit YOS from Previous Service" and "Intra-Company Transfer: No MPF Holiday" in IT0346 for employees who have done intra-group transfer and do not have MPF holiday, employee's mandatory contribution(eemco) should be displayed. However, eemco was blank.

After applying this note:

  • Date of employment is displayed as blank instead of '00000000' in downloaded files for existing members without date of employment.
  • When checking the checkbox "Separate termination files", date of employment is optional for existing members in contribution file.
  • For employees who did intra-group transfer and do not have MPF holiday, employee's mandatory contribution is displayed.

Other Terms

HHKCPFE0, eMPF, Date of employment, Employee's mandatory contribution

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a correction of legal function. You have applied Note 3702711 and 3680053.

Solution

Apply the Support Packages or implement the corresponding correction instructions.
