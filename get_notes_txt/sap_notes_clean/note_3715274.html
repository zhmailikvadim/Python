Symptom

In the construction savings contract display (transaction /IBS/BSC_SHOW), the system issues the error message "WOP data inconsistent". When you switch to the "WOP Data" tab page, an uncontrolled termination occurs.

Reason and Prerequisites

In the formatting of the data for the display, some local data is not reset. This results in inconsistent data constellations.

Solution

In the BO "Wop in Building Contract" (/IBS/CL_PRM_BO_BDS_IFM_BSC, method WOP_DATA_FILL), the local data is reset.
