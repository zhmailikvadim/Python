Symptom

In the process "Receipt of 55616 from Responsible DSO to SUP" (DE_B_MDC_55616_DSO2SUP_RECV), the Status of Remote Controllability for PoD relationship attributes could not be updated based on inbound master data values from the market partner in step 1600-0200.

This issue is relevant for the market roles: SUP, DSO.

Other Terms

MDC, PoD relationship attributes, Status of Remote Controllability, /US4G/CL_MDX_UBD_POD_PERIOD, /US4G/CL_UBD_DATA_POD_PERIOD, /US4G/S_UBD_POD_PERIOD_REQ, DEFAULT_VALUE, 55616

Reason and Prerequisites

 The error occurred because of a code defect in Class /US4G/CL_MDX_UBD_POD_PERIOD, Method /UCOM/IF_MDX_MARKET_DATA_ACC~FILL_PROC_STEP_DATA, Class /US4G/CL_UBD_DATA_POD_PERIOD, Method GET_POD_RELATIONSHIP, and Structure /US4G/S_UBD_POD_PERIOD_REQ, field DEFAULT_VALUE.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

If you are DSO role, you need to follow post-implementation activities to activate the BC set US4G_3_0_DSO_SP07_NOTE_3711024.bcs.

After implementing this SAP Note, You can verify the fix by checking that after step 1600-0200 runs, the Status of Remote Controllability for PoD relationship attributes is updated with the inbound values.
