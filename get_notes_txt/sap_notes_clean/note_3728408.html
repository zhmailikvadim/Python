Symptom

  • You use DRC report HU_VAT_DCL tool Analyze Data
  • Customer Invoices are duplicated in the Analyzer
  • The ESA/ESE inverse posting documents show incorrect sign regarding the tax base amount and the tax amount in the Analyzer.

Other Terms

Hungary, VAT reporting, DRC, HU_VAT_DCL, HUF, duplication, Analyze Data, ESA/ESE, inverse posting

Reason and Prerequisites

  • The duplication occurs when the system retrieves VAT analytics data. 

Solution

  • After applying this SAP Note, the system correctly displays each customer invoice only once in the VAT analytics report and ESA/ESE amounts sign.
