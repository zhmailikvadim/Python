Symptom

Item Master Data in the Direct Materials Sourcing (DMS) database is not providing updated values after submitting a payload.

Cause

The ItemMasterSync scheduled task has not run on the realm since the payload was sent to Ariba.

Resolution

Item Master payloads are not processed in real-time therefore the results are not available immediately. After the ItemMasterSync task completes, the items are loaded into the DMS database. Webservice calls extract data from this database.

See Also

The ItemMasterSync task is scheduled to run at 7 AM and 7 PM PST daily. Once the Item Master is loaded, it is first indexed to Arches from the DMS database.
