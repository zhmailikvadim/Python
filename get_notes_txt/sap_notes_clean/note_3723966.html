Symptom

What prevents a supplier from seeing their questionnaire when the questionnaires have Not Created status ?

Cause

There might have been an attempt to upgrade the Supplier Registration Project that failed, leaving the status of the questionnaires as Not created.

Resolution

  1. Navigate to SM Administration > Template Upgrade > Upgrade Status to verify the failed template upgrade attempts.
  2. Cancel the current supplier registration project.
  3. Reinvite the supplier to the registration project. This action will automatically pull the most recent version of the template.
