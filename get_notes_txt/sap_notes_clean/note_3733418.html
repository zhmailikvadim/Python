Symptom

The app Manage Customer Line Items does not support controller extensibility in BAS due to synchronous loading.

Other Terms

F0711, fin.ar.lineitems.display, Business Application Studio, BAS, controller extension.

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing feature.

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction or the mentioned Support Package.

After implementation of the note please recalculate the index of the app (like in the attached note 2449635) for project FIN_CUST_LITS (tab 'Single SAPUI5 Repository Only'). It deletes the front-end caches.
