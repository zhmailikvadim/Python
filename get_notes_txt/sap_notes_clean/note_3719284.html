Symptom

When and how can IT0038 (Fiscal Data CH) be delimited?

Environment

  • Personnel Administration Switzerland
  • Payroll Switzerland

Solution

1. If the employee leaves the company (IT0000, employment), IT0038 must not be delimited, but must exist until ENDDA = 12/31/9999.
(V_T582A, IT0038, time constraint = 1)

Otherwise, errors such as the following may occur in payroll RPCALCC0:

  • Table T5C2C cannot be read. Canton XX, Maintainability:Y
    CHQST The calculation step could not be executed.

  • Table T5C2B cannot be read. Canton XX




2. In the case of changes, IT0038 must not be delimited mid-month, exception Entry/Reentry. Changes can only ever start on the first of the month (01.XX.YYYY).

For example, if the employee changes the place of residence in IT0006 mid-month and therefore IT0038 must also be adjusted, the IT0038 record starts with the new municipality number with the first of the following month.
After the F1 help, in the field 'Alt. Residential Arrangements" in IT0038 (P0038-DIF_MUNIDLIVING).


3. However, the table ST - Tax of the payroll cluster RPCLSTRC can have mid-month delimitations after the first of the month and before the last day of the month. The period represents those in which the employee was active ((IT0000, employment).

Example:
04.08.2025    29.08.2025    BS    2701    4    SFN
-> The employee joined the company on August 4, 2025 mid-month and on August 29, 2025 mid-month.
