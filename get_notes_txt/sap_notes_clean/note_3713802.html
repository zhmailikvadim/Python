Symptom

In the process "Receiving Delivery Note (DSO View)" (DE_DELVNOTE_DSO), step 0080 cannot determine the business partner correctly when the business partner has already moved out and the delivery note is sent after the moving date.
This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

DE_DELVNOTE_DSO, step 0080, /IDXGL/CL_CHECK_METHOD, DET_BP_BY_POD, ISU_GET_OBJECTS, IDEXDE, delivery note, business partner determination

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the corrections of SAP Note 3713802 and 3712961.

After implementing SAP Note, the system correctly determines the business partner in the process DE_DELVNOTE_DSO.
You need to retest the process DE_DELVNOTE_DSO.
You can verify the fix by checking that the business partner is determined correctly.

Dependency Note: 3712961
