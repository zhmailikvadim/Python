Symptom

With S/4HANA 2022 release, BAPI_INCOMINGINVOICE_CREATE1 and BAPI_INCOMINGINVOICE_PARK  are enhaced with parameters SELECTPO, SELECTDELIVERY and SELECTSERVICE_LEAN. The purpose of these parameters are not well documented.

Other Terms

SELECTPO, SELECTDELIVERY, SELECTSERVICE_LEAN, BAPI_INCOMINGINVOICE_CREATE1, BAPI_INCOMINGINVOICE_PARK.

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing Documentation.

Solution

When Supplier Invoices need to be parked (or) held with reference informaton (e.g. Purchase Order reference, Delivery reference, Lean Service reference) without Supplier Invoice items, these parameters can be used. When these are filled, Supplier Invoice item details must not be filled. With this reference information, the Supplier Invoice can be processed later with MIR4 or Fiori app Manage Supplier Invoices.

When the reference details are filled, the corresponding reference can also be seen in MIR4 (or) Manage Supplier Invoices Fiori app against the reference type (without supplier invoice items) in the supplier items section.
