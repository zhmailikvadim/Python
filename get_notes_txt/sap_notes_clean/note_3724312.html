Symptom

Will the system send notifications for the auto-closure of Statements of Work?

Cause

Resolution

Fieldglass will send notification when Statement of Work auto closes when the Messaging settings are enabled 

  1. Navigate to Messaging > Module: Statement of Work > Action: Close
  2. Enable the messaging settings 

Important: Users with the Configuration Manager role do not receive message notifications, which is expected system behavior
