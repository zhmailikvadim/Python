Symptom

New forms for the sick pay notification and maternity pay notification have been published on the OEGK website. These forms are delivered with this SAP Note for printing from the infotype "Work and Remuneration Confirmation A" (0526).

Other Terms

IT0526, print, AW notification, AK notification, P6, P7

Reason and Prerequisites

Legal change

Solution

Import the corresponding HR Support Package into your SAP system.

For an advance correction, proceed as follows:

  • Only systems based on SAPK-604: first implement the advance corrections described in the manual instructions.
  • Then upload the attached correction instructions to your SAP system using transaction SNOTE.
  • Then perform the post-implementation steps described in the manual instructions. You can find the form templates in the attachment to this SAP Note.

We will check the following inconsistencies with the ÖGK and ELDA:

Maternity pay notification:

  • Non-Working Days Area
    The sample for the non-working days known from the sick pay notification (DM Org, chapter E.10, field AFRT) is not provided for the maternity pay notification (DM Org, chapter E.11).
  • Reason for Dissolution of Employment Area:
    • With reference to DM org. chapter D.22, reasons that are not provided for in the ELDA notification are listed here, for example, 01 - Termination by Employer
    • With reference to DM org. chapter D.22, reasons that are permitted in the ELDA notification are not listed here, for example,  22 - Unjustified early leaving
  • The start and end of pay in lieu of notice and payment in lieu of unused leave are not provided for the maternity pay notification by ELDA (DM Org, chapter E.11).

If you notice further discrepancies, contact SAP with a customer message. SAP will only populate the form fields that are part of the ELDA field structure.
