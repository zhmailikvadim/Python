Symptom

Even though withdrawal WTs exist (1st step in a 2-step wave), it is possible to delete wave items with the same stock attributes from the wave. This leads to a mismatch in quantities for wave items versus the quantity of WTs, and could even lead to the deletion of the wave, if the last item gets removed.

Other Terms

/SCWM/ORDIM_O-WAVE Unassign Reassign Wave Item /SCWM/WAVEITM

Program Error

Solution
