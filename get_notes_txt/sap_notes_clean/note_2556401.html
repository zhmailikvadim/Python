Symptom

You run the Account Document Output (transaction IDCNDOC) report for China. On the Accounting Document screen, there is no data in the Official Doc. No. column for reversal documents.

Other Terms

Accounts Document Output, IDCNDOC , RFIDCN_ACC_DOC

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a correction of legal function.

The reasons are as follows:

  • The original documents and reversal documents are from the integrated asset posting.
  • The original documents and reversal documents are from the MIGO transaction.

The logic cannot find the above-mentioned original documents, so the ODN cannot be generated.

Solution

Apply the corresponding Support Package or implement the correction instructions in this note.
