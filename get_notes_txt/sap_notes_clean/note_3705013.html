Symptom

  • An error occurs when attempting to create or import bank account balances using the "Manage Bank Account Balances" app .
  • Error Row 2: Cannot update balance for account ******** by importing a spreadsheet.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

SAP S/4HANA 2023

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the Fiori app "Manage Bank Account Balances" .
  2. Import the balances from the spreadsheet.
  3. Click on import
  4. Error Occurred

Cause

The error occurred because of the missing customization in the Define bank account settings App.

Resolution

  1. Open the App "Define Bank account settings" APP ID F5488 in DS9200.
  2. Open the Bank account.
  3. Enable the below check box :
    • Enable balance from Manual Entry.
    • Enable balance from Spreadsheet import.

    4. Import/create the balance in the Manage Bank Account Balances app Again.
