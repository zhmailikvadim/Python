Symptom

Settlement/Rebate documents cannot be submitted to KSeF; the system prevents successful transmission of these documents.

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Document and Reporting Compliance, Cloud Edition
  • SAP S/4HANA cloud, Private edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a customer rebate/settlement document that results in an eDocument for KSeF.
  2. Open the eDocument cockpit and submit the document to KSeF.

Cause

Customer rebates/settlement management documents are not supported by the standard Poland localization for KSeF

Resolution

If this process is required, implement the BAdI EDOC_ADAPTOR to modify the relevant fields of the KSeF invoice accordingly.
