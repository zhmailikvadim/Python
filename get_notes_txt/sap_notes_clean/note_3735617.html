Symptom

  • The system does not generate a new tranche in step 1200‑0180 when processing an SOS with transaction reason BusinessTransaction3 on a feeding pod with attribute Z90.
  • A negative APERAK is returned during SoS Registration Confirmation at step 1700 indicating a missing mandatory segment for BMID 55078, for example:

"ERC+Z29'"

"FTX+ABO+++Fehlendes /M_UTILMD/G_SG4/G_SG8[S_SEQ/D_1229=(?'Z15?')] beziehen auf /M_UTILMD[1]/G_SG4[1] Bedingung [[477]XOR[478]]AND[2061]'"

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA Utilities Master Data Updates Option for Germany

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Review the cloud messaging at step 1700 and confirm a negative APERAK indicating missing mandatory segment SEQ+Z15 for BMID 55078.

Cause

  • Missing mandatory segment in UBD data: SEQ+Z15 (required for BMID 55078 per BDEW AHB). The corresponding information (DATA_USAGE_PERIOD[]/DATAQUALITY_&_USAGE_PERIOD_ID) is not sent from the backend in step 1300.

Resolution

  1. Review step 1300 of the S/4 backend process and ensure the UBD payload includes DATA_USAGE_PERIOD[]/DATAQUALITY_&_USAGE_PERIOD_ID.
  2. Populate and transmit the SEQ+Z15 segment for BMID 55078 in alignment with BDEW AHB requirements.
  3. Re-run the process: send the SoS Registration Confirmation, verify that the APERAK is positive at step 1700, and confirm that a new tranche is created in step 1200‑0180.
  4. Monitor subsequent runs to ensure the UBD data remains compliant and the tranche creation proceeds as expected.
