Symptom

After executing the SNP Heuristic job the results are being checked in the product view. It can be noticed that some of the purchase requisitions have different delivery and end times meanwhile, they were planned on the same source and destination locations. The differences can specifically observed between the winter and summer time changes.

Environment

  • SAP Supply Chain Management (SCM)
  • SAP Advanced Planning and Optimization (SCM-APO)
  • SAP Supply Chain Management Supply Network Planning (SCM-APO-SNP)
  • SAP Supply Chain Management Supply Network Planning Heuristic (SCM-APO-SNP-HEU)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Execute the SNP Heuristic job either in the background or the foreground
  2. Go to RRP3 Product View
  3. Select the product location which was planned by the Heuristic
  4. When checking the generated purchase requisitions it can be observed that some of them has a delivery time of 18:00, the other ones have 19:00.

Cause

The variation in delivery times is attributed to daylight saving time adjustments. Daylight saving ensures that the planning results are accurate by considering the time zone differences and seasonal changes. The actual differences are coming from the fact that the results are being checked in local time zone and not in UTC. 

Resolution

Please make sure that the times are being checked in UTC time zone. In UTC, the time will be the same where in local time zone it is considering the daylight saving.
