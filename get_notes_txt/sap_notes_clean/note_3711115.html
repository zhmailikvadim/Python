Symptom

You edit a business partner in transaction BP or using an API. The business partner has maintained a business partner as a contact person in the relationships that is the contact person for a large number of other business partners. When you save the business partner, you notice that the process takes an unusually long time.

Other Terms

BP, performance, contacts, CVI, customer, vendor

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction or the mentioned Support Package.
