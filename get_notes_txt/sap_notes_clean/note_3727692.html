Symptom

In SAP Document and Reporting Compliance (DRC) Statutory Report for Germany's Advance VAT reports (DE_VAT_ADV_RTN, DE_EXT_ADV_RTN, DE_PAY_ADV_RTN) for year 2025 and 2026, descriptions for DE language are missing but are available in earlier years' reports.

Changing the logon language does not solve the issue.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • S4CORE 108

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log into the Fiori application with DE language settings.
  2. Navigate to the Run Statutory Reports (F1515) application.
  3. Attempt to generate any of the Advance VAT reports for 2025 or 2026.
  4. Observe that the descriptions are displayed in English instead of German, while DRC options are in German.

Cause

Lower support package (SP) release.

Resolution

Translated texts for are delivered with the latest support package on S4CORE 108, which is SP04. 
By implementing SP04 the missing translations will be available.
