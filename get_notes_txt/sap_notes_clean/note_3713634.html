Symptom

In case you process a Business Partner of type Organization with Communication Remarks, having a Business Partner Contact Person with work address Communication Remarks in a Consolidation or Mass Processing process, the process might be stucked in Validation step with status Waiting and runtime error ITAB_DUPLICATE_KEY in program SAPLBUPA_INTERFACE_OUTBOUND.

Other Terms

MDC; CMP; ADRT; MDG; PERSNUMBER; ITAB_DUPLICATE_KEY; SAPLBUPA_INTERFACE_OUTBOUND;

Solution

Implement solution according to correction instructions.
