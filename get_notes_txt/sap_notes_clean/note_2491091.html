Symptom

You use business trip accounting for the German public sector. If multiple trips are chained on one day, the wrong reimbursement and tax-free amounts can be determined in the following cases:

  • The absence in total is 24 hours because the trips to be chained fit together seamlessly.
  • The entire absence of the trips to be chained is more than 10 hours but less than 11 hours (for LRKG North Rhine-Westphalia).
  • The entire absence of the trips to be chained is more than 13 hours but less than 14 hours (for LRKG in states for which a higher reimbursement applies after 14 hours).

Other Terms

pubsec_germany, BRKG, business trips, multiple trips on one day, chaining, betfz, betfa

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Import the specified HR support package or implement the attached correction instructions.
