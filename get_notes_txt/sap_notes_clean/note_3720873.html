Symptom

Your are using the B2A-Manager to process the DmfA (DMFA) & DmfA Update (DMUP) declarations.
A SQL error occurred while updating the status handler tables (T5F99SR or T5F99SE). This is because the records in your declaration contain inconsistencies or duplicate entries.

Other Terms

DMFA, DMUP, T5F99SR, T5F99SE, CL_HR99S00_B2A_DOCUMENT_ADMIN, CL_HRPAYBE_B2A_DOC_ADMIN_DMCA, CL_HRPAYBE_B2A_DOC_ADMIN_DMFA, CL_HRPAYBE_B2A_DOC_ADMIN_EGOV

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Belgium.

Solution

This SAP Note improves the logging in case of an SQL error in the processing of the DmfA & DmfA Update declaration (DMFA/DMUP) when inserting records in the status handler tables T5F99SR or T5F99SE. The logging has been improved for foreground and background (reports RPCDMFB0/RPCDUPB0) processing.
When there are non-unique records in the declaration, the log displays the information (TYPRO, GLBID, ESSID) of up to 10 records of those duplicate records in the B2A log.
