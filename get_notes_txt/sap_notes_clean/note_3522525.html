Symptom

When using the Fiori Launchpad of SAP Cloud for Real Estate, the user gets logged out after 15 minutes of inactivity.

Resolution

The Fiori Launchpad of SAP Cloud for Real Estate is based on SAP Business Technology Platform's Cloud Portal Service. The default session timeout is set to 15 minutes.

Due to legal requirements, there is a maximum of 20 minutes as described in https://help.sap.com/docs/cloud-portal-service/sap-cloud-portal-service-on-cloud-foundry/session-and-security-policy-configuration?locale=en-US.
