Symptom

You use the e-mail integration of R4CM. You notice that under rare circumstances, an incoming e-mail is not saved.

Other Terms

Ticket 1000007368, ADHOF, cash, communication

Reason and Prerequisites

R4CM requires the MAIL_ID generated by the mail server so that the customer's e-mail can be assigned correctly when a reply is received. This is not generated until it is sent and does not exist in the SAP system. To receive this, R4CM sends itself the e-mail as BCC with an indicator as an HTML comment to assign the MAIL_ID to the internal e-mail. When responding to this email, these HTML comments are usually removed.

In rare cases, this HTML comment is not removed and passed back to our system. In these cases, the system may assume that the e-mail has been sent by our system. As a result, this email is not saved.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions.
