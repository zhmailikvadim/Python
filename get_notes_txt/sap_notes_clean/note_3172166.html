Symptom

I am trying to create a contract-based invoice, but I am unable to click Line Item Actions > Add Catalog Items.

Environment

  • SAP Ariba Buying and Invoicing

Cause

When you punch-in to your buyer's system to create a contract invoice, you must select a contract number before you add the line items.

Resolution

Select a value for Contract and you will be able to add line items to your invoice.

Keywords

Contract invoice, unable to add line items contract invoice.
