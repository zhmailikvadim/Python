Symptom

Why don't I see the button Edit&Resubmit on my rejected Service Entry Sheet?

Environment

SAP Business Network

Cause

The buyer has the transaction rule 'Allow suppliers to reuse service sheet numbers' disabled.

Resolution

Supplier must click the button 'Copy SES' in the rejected SES, which will copy all the info from the original document, edit the SES number (as they are not allowed to reuse it) and update the fields as necessary.

Another option would have the buyer enabling this rule 'Allow suppliers to reuse service sheet numbers'.
