Symptom

  • Users experience issues logging into SAP cloud systems (C4C, CPQ, Success Factors).
  • Error message displayed: "Access is denied because you are connecting from a country or region where this product or service is not available."

Environment

SAP Cloud for Customer core applications

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Attempt to log in to SAP cloud systems (C4C) from an office located in Ukraine.
  2. Observe the error message: "Access is denied because you are connecting from a country or region where this product or service is not available."

Cause

The issue is caused by geo-blocking restrictions applied to the region where the office is located.

Resolution

If you believe you are in a permitted location, contact your SAP administrator or raise a case with SAP support using component XX-SER-ECO-GBA.
