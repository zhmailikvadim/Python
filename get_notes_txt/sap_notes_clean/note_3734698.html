Symptom

When registering impairment losses in Tr-cd: ABAWL, the "New Book Value" in the asset master record is automatically updated with the post-impairment book value.

Asset net book value which updated in asset master data is incorrect after asset impairment.

Other Terms

Japan Asset, Asset Revaluation, Net Book Value, impairment, ABAW, AS91, NAFAP, AMDP.

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a correction of legal function.

Solution

Apply the correction instructions in the SAP Note or implement the corresponding Support Package.
