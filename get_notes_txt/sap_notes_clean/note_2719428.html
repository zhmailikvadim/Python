Symptom

When you change a retail article via the transaction MM02,  Message no. MH091 (You must use retail functions to process the material XXXX) is not raised anymore.

Other Terms

MM02, MM03, ISR_RETAILSYSTEM, ISR_RETAILSWITCH, SFSW_FASHION, SFSW_FASHION_02, SFSW_FASHION_03, SFSW_FASHION_04, FSH_APERS

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason: Program error.

Solution

The program corrections are contained in the tab Correction Instruction of this note. You need to implement the corrections in your system.
