Symptom

SAP is launching an Early Adopter Care Program for selected customers for ‘SAP managed Operational Procurement’, covering SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition, SAP Ariba Buying, and SAP Ariba Invoicing. In principle, all SAP customers are eligible to register for this program. Participation in the Early Adopter Care program is voluntary and at SAP’s sole discretion. It depends on several prerequisites described in this note. This note is subject to change. Please review it regularly for updates.

Solution

General Information

'SAP managed' refers to a delivery approach where SAP takes responsibility for key technical and operational aspects of the solutions, reducing complexity for customers and ensuring a consistent, best-practice experience. SAP handles all aspects of the software lifecycle, including provisioning, technical integration, monitoring, automatic updates, backups, off-boarding. SAP as a solution provider not only manages single applications but also the life-cycle dependencies between these applications.

Key aspects of SAP managed:

  • Landscape: Standardized, best-practice landscape setup designed and managed by SAP, reducing variability and integration complexity.
  • Provisioning: Business-driven activation of components for a seamless suite experience, avoiding repetitive technical steps.
  • Identity & Access Management:Centralized and streamlined user provisioning, authentication, and authorization across SAP cloud ERP applications through SAP Cloud Identity Services as the single point of management.
  • Integration: Delivery of technical integration for specific business scenarios.
  • Monitoring: SAP assumes full responsibility for technical integration and stability, eliminating the need for customer-operated monitoring.

This specific Early Adopter Care Program covers the business scenario 'SAP managed Operational Procurement' which includes SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition, SAP Ariba Buying, and SAP Ariba Invoicing.

Registration the Early Adopter Care Program

Every SAP customer can register for the Early Adopter Care program for SAP managed Workforce Management, which triggers an SAP review of the required prerequisites. Partners and SAP employees may register on behalf of a customer.

Link to registration page on the SAP Customer Influence Portal.

Prerequisites for Participation in Early Adopter Care Program in 2602 Release

In principle, all SAP customers are eligible to register. Participation in the Early Adopter Care program is voluntary and at SAP’s sole discretion. It depends on the fulfillment of the following prerequisites for the 2602 release.

  1. Purchased bundles and solutions: Customers who have purchased SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition, SAP Ariba Buying, and SAP Ariba Invoicing through:
    • either the SAP Finance Premium (8019461) bundle
    • or the SAP Finance Base bundle (8019322) with SAP Ariba Invoicing (8018575).

  2. Implementation approach
    • Simultaneous implementation and unified go‑live of all solutions.

  3. S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition
    • Greenfield customers with no existing tenant of SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition.
    • No Managed Service Provider model (Partner Managed Cloud / Business Process Outsourcing).
    • No request of SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition, disaster recovery (8012323).
    • No request of SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition, Test Data Refresh (8011406).
    • No request of SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition, three-system landscape, parallel line.
    • No requirement for system extensions.
    • Only one landscape of S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition is ordered by customer.
    • Test & Go-Live SKUs are not part of the customer order.

  4. Identity & Access Management
    • No usage of shared email addresses.
    • Preferred: No existing SAP Cloud Identity tenant.

  5. Other prerequisites
    • No customers located in Mainland China.
    • No planned takeover of the company by another company in the foreseeable future.

Further Information

  • For the starter system of SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition, the starter customizing tenant (100) is provided. As of 02 March 2026, this is applicable to all starter system of SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition. No starter development tenant (080) is provided.

Conditions: Customer Feedback & SAP Support

The customer receives enablement, proactive support, continuous project monitoring, and direct collaboration with SAP Product Management through a dedicated, assigned SAP expert acting as a single point of contact. This SAP expert, together with additional SAP teams, actively supports the successful adoption of SAP managed services particularly during potential issues and critical phases, such as tenant provisioning, integration provisioning, and go-live activities.

The program is specifically focused on new SAP managed capabilities. Accordingly, SAP support is strictly limited to topics that are directly related to these managed services. Project management and consulting services are explicitly excluded. Customers and their partners remain fully responsible for the project execution and overall success.

As part of the program, SAP expects customers to provide regular feedback, which is used to drive continuous improvements.
