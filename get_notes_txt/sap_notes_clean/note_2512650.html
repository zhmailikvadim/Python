Symptom

In Browser Access, while you are closing a Sales Order or a Sales Quotation form with the Analytics tab expanded in the sidebar, the screen freezes. 

Reproducing the issue

Prerequisites:

You are connecting to SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA from Browser Access. 

Scenario:

  1. Go to Modules → Administration → System Initialization → General Settings → Display tab, and ensure that Enable Sidebar in windows to display recommendations and linked dashboards is selected.
  2. Open Modules → Sales A/R → Sales Order or Sales Quotation form. 
  3. Select the Analytics tab to expand the Analytics tab in the sidebar.
  4. Close the Sales Order or Sales Quotation form. 

Expected behavior:

The Sales Order or Sales Quotation form is closed. 

Actual behavior:

The system keeps waiting, and the screen freezes. 

Solution

SAP intends to provide a patch or patches in order to solve the problem described.

The section Reference to Related Notes below will list the specific patches once they become available. The corresponding Info file of the patches in SAP Service Marketplace will also show the SAP Note number.

Be aware that these references can only be set at patch release date.

SAP will deliver patches only for selected releases at its own discretion, based on the business impact and the complexity of the implementation.
