Symptom

The following APIs were enhanced with fields for International Trade Management (ITM):

oData V4 for scheduling agreements: API_SCHED_AGRMT_PROCESS_2

  • New header fields:
    • OverallTrdCmplncEmbargoSts
    • OvrlTrdCmplncSnctndListChkSts
    • OvrlTrdCmplncLegalCtrlChkSts
  • New item fields:
    • TrdCmplncEmbargoSts
    • TrdCmplncSnctndListChkSts
    • OvrlTrdCmplncLegalCtrlChkSts

Enhanced BAPIS for scheduling agreements:

  • BAPI_SAG_CREATE
  • BAPI_SAG_CHANGE
  • BAPI_SAG_GETDETAIL
  • BAPI_SCHEDULE_MAINTAIN

Enhanced BAPIS for contracts:

  • BAPI_CONTRACT_CREATE
  • BAPI_CONTRACT_CHANGE
  • BAPI_CONTRACT_GETDETAIL

The BAPI-field names are the same at header level (structure BAPIMEOUTHEADER) and item level (structure BAPIMEOUTITEM):

  • EMBARGO_STATUS
  • SCREENING_STATUS
  • LEGAL_CONTROL_STATUS


The new fields can only be read. They cannot be maintained from outside.

Other Terms

Trade Control, Embargo Status, Total Embargo Status, Screening Status, Total Screening Status, Total Legal Control Status

Reason and Prerequisites

New Development

Solution

It is recommended to implement the assigned Feature Package.
