Symptom

Sales Quote That Are Replicated From ERP Are Read Only

Environment

 SAP Cloud for Customer 

SAP ERP

Reproducing the Issue

1)Go to Sales Work Center

2)Go to Sales Quote Work Center View

3)Open the Sales Quote 

4)Give the following message "Sales document does not allow local edits due to the Extern indicator."

Cause

Quote replication from ERP to C4C has few restrictions. Any quote replicated from ERP to C4C is read-only. It cannot be further updated/edited by user or replication.

Resolution

This is current system behavior
