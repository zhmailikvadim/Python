Symptom

Other Terms

ISLS, Manufacturing, Intelligent Clinical Supply Management, PI Sheet, OES_LOG_HEADER'

Reason and Prerequisites

You use SAP Intelligent Clinical Supply Management. The issue is due to the side effect of another SAP Note 3711576

Solution

Implement the attached correction.
