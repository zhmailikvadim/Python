Symptom

When generating the sourcing request analytical report, the supplier field appears as unclassified or remains blank.

Cause

The supplier has not been populated in the sourcing request header fields.

Resolution

To ensure the supplier appears correctly in the analytical report, you must fill in the Supplier field at the header level of the sourcing request. 

Please note that if the supplier is added only within the sourcing request items, this information will not be reflected in the supplier field at the sourcing request header level and therefore will not appear in the report.

See Also

If the supplier field is not available in your sourcing request, refer to: How can I enable Suppliers as a custom header field for sourcing projects and requests?
