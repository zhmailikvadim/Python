Symptom

Error occurs in CFIN target system during the replication of CO documents (top-down distribution).

The entry is successfully posted in CFIN source system.

Error code: F5 842

Error text: Balance in 1 currencies

Environment

  • Financial Accounting (FI)
  • SAP S/4HANA 
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Try to replicate CO documents (top-down distribution) from CFIN source system to CFIN target system.
  2. Observe the error message "Balance in 1 currencies" during replication.

Cause

In CFIN source system, the original source document (top-down distribution) has been split into several ones because of the huge number of line items.

For the individual documents have non-zero balance, but the amounts sum up to 0 for the several documents together.

As a result, in CFIN target system, the individual document is facing error F5842.

Resolution

Unfortunately for such specific scenario, there is no standard suggestion can be provided,

Please review the point #28 in the FAQ note 2959532:

###

28)    Can CO documents that do not balance to zero (for example, due to document splitting in the source system) be replicated to the Central Finance system?

    Individual documents that do not balance to zero are replicated, but cannot be processed in the Central Finance system. This also applies to cases where the source system creates split CO documents. Although these split documents may together balance to zero, they do not balance to zero individually, and therefore we are not able to process them in the Central Finance system.
