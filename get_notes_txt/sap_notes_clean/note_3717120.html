Symptom

Is it possible to delete a Tax ID or the whole External Document Service configuration?

Environment

SAP Business Network

Cause

Resolution

Once configured and enabled, the external document service can only be disabled, not completely deleted. 

In order to disable one of the configurations, follow the steps below:

  1. Sign in to your SAP Business Network buyer account
  2. Click on the [User Initials] > Settings 
  3. Navigate until External Document Service Configuration
  4. On the External Document Service Configuration page, choose Actions  > Disable
  5. The confirmation dialog box appears. Choose Yes to disable the External Document Service Configuration

See Also

You can find further information on our dedicated page: Disabling External Document Service Configurations
