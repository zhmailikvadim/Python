Symptom

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Utilities Master Data Updates Option for Germany

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Send message 55691 to Test MaCo Cloud.
  2. Observe the received negative APERAK response.

Cause

The segment the segment SG4 IDE is missing from the received payload.

Resolution

  1. Review the transfer document related to the received negative aperak in the FTX segment.
  2. Analyze the missing segments in the payload received.
  3. Refer to the missing segment report, which includes the relevant BDEW handbook references along with their translations.
  4. Verify the missing segment report and ensure the segment SG4 IDE is required for the case.
  5. Confirm the correctness of the message payload and validate the absence of errors.
