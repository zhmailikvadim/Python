Symptom

You use the report /SCWM/RP_LICENCE_SELF_AUDIT to select and review a list of tracked warehouse numbers based on their usage of basic or advanced functions, and the compliance of that usage throughout the last 12 months. This report is now enhanced additionally for decentralized EWM systems to show those systems as 'DECNTRAL' in the 'Actual Usage' field. Attention: Decentralized systems are always considered as Advanced Warehouse Management. Consequently this will be transparent in field 'Acknowledged Usage of Warehouse Management'.

The reports of /SCWM/RP_COMPLIANCE_CHECK and /SCWM/RP_LICENCE_ANALYSIS however are still not supported in EWM decentralized systems. There will be an error message occurring once those reports are executed in a decentralized system. The error message text is improved to make this limitation transparent. For decentralized systems the self auditing report of /SCWM/RP_LICENCE_SELF_AUDIT is the only supported report.

Other Terms

Extended Warehouse Management, EWM, License Measurement, Self Auditing, /SCWM/RP_LICENCE_SELF_AUDIT

Reason and Prerequisites

Advance Development

Necessity for self auditing functionality for decentralized warehouse management systems.

Solution

Apply this note if you are using Extended Warehouse Management (EWM) on S/4HANA or classic EWM.
