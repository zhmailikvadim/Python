Symptom

You convert an STR (stock transport requisition) into an STO (stock transport order). The STR has an account assignment and therefore belongs to an MTO segment.

After the STO has been converted, you notice that it has lost its account assignment and now belongs to the MTS segment.

You can also correct this inconsistency by doing an initial transfer, e. g. FM CIF_REFRESH_PURCHASE_ORDER, or report /SAPAPO/PPDS_DELTA_ORD_TRANS.

Other Terms

N/A

Reason and Prerequisites

The STO is an intercompany STO.

Programming error in FM ME_APO_PREPARE_PO.

Solution

Implement this note or the corresponding support package.
