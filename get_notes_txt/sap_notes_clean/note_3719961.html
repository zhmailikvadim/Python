Symptom

How to find reconciliations related to a specific invoice.

Environment

SAP Business One

Cause

By design

Resolution

  1. Open the invoice in question.
  2. Right-click on the invoice and select Reconciled Transactions.
  3. In the bar menu, click on Form Settings and set the Reconciliation No. column as visible.
  4. Click on the golden arrow beside a reconciliation number to view the full details of that specific reconciliation.

Keywords

manual reconciliation, reconciliation details, A/P Invoice, A/R Invoice
