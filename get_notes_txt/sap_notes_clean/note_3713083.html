Symptom

Why can't I filter by status under Your Requests page in guided buying?

Cause

The Your Requests page in guided buying does not have the filter of status in its initial (old) format, you can only search by:

  • Type
  • Last Date
  • Request ID

Resolution

In order to have the filter by status under the Your Requests page you'll need to enable the parameter:

  •  PARAM_ENABLE_NEW_YOUR_REQUEST_PAGE

This parameter will enable more filter options to the page, including the status filter.

If you also wish to have the Request for Quotes and Custom Form page you need to also enable the parameter below:

  • PARAM_ENABLE_ENHANCED_YOUR_REQUEST_2
