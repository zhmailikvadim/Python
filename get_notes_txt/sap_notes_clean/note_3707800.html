Symptom

You use SAP Travel Management for S/4 HANA Fiori application "My Travel and Expenses for Business Traveler" or "Travel and Expenses for Travel Assistant". You have a trip with receipts. You deduct private expenses on a receipt having already some split receipts. You get a short dump with exception CX_SADL_DUMP_APPL_MODEL_ERROR on CL_TRV_MTE_VIRTUAL_ELEMENT_S4.

Other Terms

Travel Expenses, MTE, Expense report, F6190, F0584B, F6190A, F0584C

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

 Implement the attached Correction Instructions or apply the corresponding Support Package
