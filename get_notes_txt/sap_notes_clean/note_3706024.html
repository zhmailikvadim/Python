Symptom

In the process "Registration of Meter Operation Service for Electricity (New MOS View)" (DE_B_WIM_REGMOS_MOSN), when processing a supply scenario with a MeLo that has two MaLos, the system incorrectly assigned the supplier of the first MaLo to the second MaLo in step 0210 "Update Supply Scenario".

This issue is relevant for the market role: MOS.

Other Terms

WIM, MeLo, MaLo, /US4G/CL_UBD_SUSC, /US4G/IF_UBD_SUSC~UPDATE, Supply Scenario, Supplier, DE_B_WIM_REGMOS_MOSN

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because of a program error.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system correctly updates the supply scenario data. You can verify the fix by checking that the supply scenario data is updated correctly and that each MaLo is associated with its appropriate supplier.
