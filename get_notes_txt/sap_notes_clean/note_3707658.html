Symptom

Version 2: Improved typing error in manual correction: 170 (correct) instead of 150 (incorrect) 

Lt. National Council Resolution of the 20th January 2026 must:

  • the payroll parameters for payments according to EStG Sec. 68, 2: For wage payment periods that are after the 31st December 2025 and before the 1st January 2027, the bonuses for the first 15 (old 10) overtime hours in the month are tax-exempt to a maximum of 50% of the basic wage, but a total of no more than EUR 170 (old 120).
  • In the same way, according to Sec. 68, 1, the public holiday remuneration is now to be calculated tax-exempt.

Sec. 124b line 440 stipulates that the employer must perform a recalculation for his or her employees in accordance with Sec. 77 Para. 3 as soon as possible, but at the latest by 31. May 2026 if the technical and organizational options are available for this.

The publication in the Federal Law Gazette of the Republic of Austria is still pending.

Other Terms

Overtime bonus, public holiday work

Reason and Prerequisites

Legal change

Solution

This SAP Note delivers the two constant changes. SAP then checks whether the tax handling of the public holiday remuneration is mapped correctly.

Perform retroactive accounting for the affected personnel numbers accordingly.

Implement the attached manual corrections or import the specified HR Support Package/CLC to correct the error described above.
