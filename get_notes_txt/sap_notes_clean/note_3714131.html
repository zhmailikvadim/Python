Symptom

The requirement is to check the current stock availability for multiple materials across multiple plants simultaneously. 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

In apps like "Monitor Product Availability" (CO09) and MD04, the view of products is limited to single material stock analysis. 

Resolution

  • The Display Product Availability app checks the availability of relevant Available to Promise (ATP) products, based on the Product Availability Check (PAC) algorithm, for various orders such as production, sales orders or outbound deliveries. It is possible to check the availability quantities by plant or storage location, in a base and alternative unit of measure, with a view of all locations.
  • For more details, refer to the community link - Get to Know Display Product Availability 

Keywords

display product availability, application, multiple stocks, CO09, availability check, PAC, advanced available to promise.
