Symptom

Column specified without its table name when you double click on it in the Table Comparison filter smart editor.

Other Terms

This issue is tracked in Corrective Measure <002075125800000466252020>.

Keywords:

Double click , missing part , missing section , incomplete name , Table comparison filter, filter smart editor

Reason and Prerequisites

Environment

Data Services 4.2 SP11 and higher

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to a Table_Comparison query.
  2. Click '...' in the top right of the filter section.
  3. Double click one of the columns from a table in the data section on the left.
  4. See that the column name is specified without its table name.

Solution

This correction is delivered in the release(s) listed in the Support Packages and Patches section of this SAP Note.

Workaround:

Drag and drop the column from the section on the left of the Filter Smart Editor into the filter.
