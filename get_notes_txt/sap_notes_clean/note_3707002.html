Symptom

  • The Invite anyone for feedback functionality is not working as expected in SAP Signavio.
  • Business stakeholders without SAP Signavio Collaboration Hub licenses are unable to comment on shared process models.
  • Error message received when login.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Select the process model intended for sharing with business stakeholders.
  2. Click on Invite anyone for feedback.

Resolution

  1. Verify that the business user has received the email invitation.
    • Subject: XXX invited you to comment on the diagram DDD
    • From: noreply@signavio.com
  2. Instruct the business user to log in using the link provided in the email invitation, ensuring they do not use the standard SAP Signavio login link.
