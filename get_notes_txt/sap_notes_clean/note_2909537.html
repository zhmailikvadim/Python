Symptom

Product Properties and Notes of product specification are not visible in Purchase order

Environment

SAP Business ByDesign

Cause

In standard ByDesign we do not show product specification property details on Purchase Order screen.

Resolution

We made this product specification BO (PRODUCT_REQUIREMENT_SPEC) PSM enabled.

 So that you can configure the same from PDI studio.(Contact your partners to help you with it)

 The path to be followed  to view product specification properties in PDI :

 PRODUCT_REQUIREMENT_SPEC ( ProductRequirementSpecification ) -.

 ROOT --- association --- SpecificationFolder --- association -- SpecificationFolderProductPropertyValuationList ( target node: SpecificationFolderProductPropertyValuationList ) --- association -- PropertyValuation --- association -- PropertyValuationPropertyValue( PRP_VLN_PRP_VAL )

 The above details are PSM enabled only after your system is upgraded to version 2005

Keywords

Product specification, Property details
