Symptom

The "Submitted on" field  appears empty on the Home page for Job Requisition Approvals.
That field is supposed to show a date.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management 

Cause

Misconfiguration in the translation of the KEY:
UXRHP_SVC_COMMON_APPROVAL_SUBMITTED_ON

Resolution

  1. Access Admin Centre > Manage Languages
  2. Click on the locale where the problem has been observed, for example "en_GB" under the "Custom Translation" column
  3. Search for KEY: "UXRHP_SVC_COMMON_APPROVAL_SUBMITTED_ON"
  4. Ensure the translation contains the argument {0} at the end.
    For example, it should be something like this: "Submitted on {0}"
  5. check the same for other languages

Keywords

"Submitted on" , Home page, Job Requisition Approvals
