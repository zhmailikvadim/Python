Symptom

  1. The activation of certain ACM Fiori applications and related services fails because the corresponding Expense Management switch must be enabled to activate the required services and UI components. If the switch is not activated, customers may be unable to proceed with the activation of the ACM content, which can result in the process being blocked.

  2. When a contract contains multiple line items, application displays the same 'Delivery Period From' and 'Delivery Period To' values across all rows in the list view, even when the line items have different delivery periods.

Other Terms

ACM; Agricultural Contract Management; Manage ACM Contracts; NextGen CPE; Expense Management; F4379A

Reason and Prerequisites

Activation fails because the expense switch needs to be activated to activate the services & BSP application. Now if customers do not want to activate the switch they get into a situation where they can’t even activate the ACM content and eventually get blocked.

Solution

Import the support pack or implement the attached Correction Instruction(s).

The dependency on the Expense Management component has been removed from the manifest file. The component will now be loaded dynamically at runtime rather than during the application launch.
