Symptom

  • An error occurs while uploading physical inventory using transaction /SCWM/PI_UPLOAD.
  • Error message: Batch XXXX does not exist for product XXXX.
  • Message no. /SCWM/PI_APPL_REPORT033.

Environment

  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management (SCM-EWM)
  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management Physical Inventory (SCM-EWM-PI)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Execute transaction /SCWM/PI_UPLOAD.
  2. Upload results/CSV file.
  3. Observe the error message: Batch XXXX does not exist for product XXXX (Message no. /SCWM/PI_APPL_REPORT033).

Cause

  • The products listed in the log from the PI upload are missing their Valuation Category configuration in the Material Master.

Resolution

  1. The issue occurs because the valuation Category is not maintained in the Material Master for the affected products.
  2. Due to the missing Valuation Category, the system does not process these products during PI upload, and therefore they do not appear in the upload log.
  3. Maintain the required Valuation Category in the Material Master for the affected products.
  4. After maintaining the configuration, execute the PI upload again.
