Symptom

When rejecting a purchase order in the inbox, two email notifications are sent to inform the creator about the rejection instead of one.

Environment

  • SAP fiori
  • SAP S/4Hana

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Reject the flexible workflow of a purchase order.
  2. Observe that two email notifications are sent to the creator instead of one.

Cause

The issue occurs due to the configuration of the notification step in the Manage Workflows app, which duplicates the email notifications.

Resolution

  1. Remove the notification step currently maintained in the Manage Workflows app.
  2. Clear all system caches.
  3. Retest the functionality to ensure that only one email notification is sent upon rejection of a purchase order.
