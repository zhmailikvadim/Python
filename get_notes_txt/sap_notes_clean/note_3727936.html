Symptom

What is the sorting order of Performance History full profile card and Trend Information card in the latest People Profile?

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Performance Management

Resolution

Currently, for both Performance History full profile card and Trend Information card, records are sorted in descending order based on their last modified dates (latest at the top).
