Symptom

In the component overview, you can maintain the purchasing data even though the item category used is not flagged for external procurement (DBSKZ).
These are the following purchasing fields in EAM Simplicity:

  • GL_ACCOUNT (G/L Account)
  • MATL_GROUP (Material Group)
  • PUR_GROUP (Purchasing Group)
  • INFO_REC (Purchasing Info Record)
  • PURCH_ORG (Purchasing Organization)
  • UNLOAD_PT (Unloading Point)
  • TRACKINGNO (Requirement Tracking Number)
  • VENDOR_MAT (vendor material number)
  • RECIPIENT_LOCATION_CODE (Recipient Location Code)
  • GR_PR_TIME (Goods Receipt Processing Time in Days)
  • GR_RCPT (Ship-To Party)
  • PREQ_NAME (Name of Requester)
  • AGREEMENT/ AGMT_ITEM (Outline Agreement/Contract)
  • PREQ_NO/ PREQ_ITEM (purchase requisition)
  • VENDOR_NO (Vendor)
