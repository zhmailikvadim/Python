Symptom

  • Team graders are unable to view supplier Comments and/or Attachments provided in the comment section during grading in guided sourcing events.
  • Supplier comments and attachments within the comments are not visible in the Grade as team member view of the event.

Resolution

Supplier comments and attachments within the comments section are not designed to be visible in the Grade as team member view.
