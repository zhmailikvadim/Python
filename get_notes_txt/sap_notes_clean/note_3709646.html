Symptom

This SAP Note contains an enhancement of the OData service /MSG/API_BPM_BP_READ_O4 for reading business partners that is required as a technical pre-implementation step for further development.

The service refers to application data that is relevant for the assignment of business partners to a loss. The main purpose of the service is to enable the consumer of the service to read business partner data and generate value helps for business partners.

Other Terms

Web Service

Remote API

Reason and Prerequisites

Retrieval of the application data that is relevant for the assignment of business partners to a loss.
