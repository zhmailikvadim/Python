Symptom

Two issues are covered in this correction.

Problem I: creation and installation of an equipment results in equipment deletion.

The problem occurs when the organizational structure is defined. If an equipment is also assigned to a functional location when it is created, the system generates duplicates. Two iDocs are stored for the company ID and two for each organizational level. In iDocs created for the organizational level, the DELETED field is set to X. When the same equipment is later sent from transaction /PACG/ECM_EQUI, no duplicates are created, and the DELETE field is also not marked there.

Problem II: equipment and functional location statuses incorrectly communicated at the organizational level.

Equipment and functional location statuses defined in transaction /PACG/ECM_EQS work incorrectly for the company ID and organizational level. There was a logic flaw, and the organizational level was incorrectly handled during iDoc generation. Detailed information on the status definitions can be found at the link /PACG/ECM_EQS (Equipment Status).

Reason and Prerequisites
