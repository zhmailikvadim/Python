Symptom

One of the below errors occurs when attempting to assign a Delivery Plant to a Distribution Chain in the Product Master app.

  • The value 1234 is not allowed for the field Delivering Plant. 
    Message no. MG142
  • The plant 1234 does not exist for sales organization 5678 / distr. channel 90

* Where 1234 represents a Plant, 5678 represents a Sales Organization and 90 represents a Distribution Channel.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Manage Product Master Data (F1602) app.
  2. Navigate to the Distribution Channels tab.
  3. Create a new entry.
  4. Enter Sales Org, Distribution Channel, and Delivery Plant.
  5. Error appears.

Cause

There's a configuration activity that needs to be maintained for this called 102287 Assign Sales Organization - Distribution Channel - Plant.

Pending Org Structure changes in Central Business Configuration(CBC) can lead to data not carrying over to 102287 Assign Sales Organization - Distribution Channel - Plant. Once the Organizational Structure has been confirmed and completed the changes will carry over to 102287 Assign Sales Organization - Distribution Channel - Plant.

Resolution

102287 Assign Sales Organization - Distribution Channel - Plant can be found as follows;

  1. Access the Manage Your Solution application.
  2. Navigate to the Application Area -> Organization.
  3. Navigate to the Sub Application Area -> Organization.
  4. Locate "Assign Sales Organization - Distribution Channel - Plant."

See Also

KBA 3602410  - How to assign Plant to new Distribution Channel in SAP Central Business Configuration provides instructions on activating the Organizational Structure in Central Business Configuration(CBC).
