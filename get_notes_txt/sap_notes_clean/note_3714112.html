Symptom

The Payment Form in your frequency term is defined as 'Mid-Period' and the frequency start is defined as the condition start. This means that if a condition begins on the 30th of a month, the due date should be the 14th of the following month. Similarly, if the condition starts on the 31st, the due date should fall on the 15th of the next month.

However, from the period where February 28th or 29th is the calculation end date onward, the due dates for the calculation periods are being incorrectly calculated.

Other Terms

Mid-Period, Due date, February, cashflow, VICDCFPAY, VICDCFOBJ

Reason and Prerequisites

This is caused by a program error.

The payment form is 'Mid-Period', the frequency start is the condition start date on the 30th or 31st of the month, and the value date is February 28 or 29.

Solution

 Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified support package.
