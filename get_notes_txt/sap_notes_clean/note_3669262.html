Symptom

Error below is thrown when trying to deploy an iflow in SAP CPI, without further details in the UI or in the LJS traces:

<Iflow name>' deployment failed

Environment

  • Cloud Integration
  • SAP Integration Suite

Cause

The Iflow designtime artifact is locked by another user. 

The lock appears under "Manage Designtime Artifact Locks" tile in CPI WebUI Monitoring Overview.

Resolution

  1. Go into the CPI Web UI;
  2. Click on "Monitor" on the left side tab;
  3. Go to the bottom of the page and click on "Manage Designtime Artifact Locks" tile;
  4. Click on padlock icon to release the lock;
  5. Try to deploy the Iflow again.
