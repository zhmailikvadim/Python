Symptom

I am trying to update the SSL certificate for the Ariba endpoint configuration, but the SSL entry field is read-only and not allowing manual updates in Ariba site.

Environment

SAP Ariba Buying 

Reproducing the Issue

1. Login to SAP Ariba. 
2. Navigate to Manage > Core Administration > Integration Manager > Endpoint Configuration.
3. Attempt to update the SSL certificate in Ariba site.

Resolution

The endpoint configuration is not certificate-based and is managed automatically by Ariba. No manual updates are required, and you can confirm this by checking the certificate’s validity period (expiry date).
