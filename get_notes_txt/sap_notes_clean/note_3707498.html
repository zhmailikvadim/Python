Symptom

Even though an SFC is created with characters listed in KBA 3707514, with some characters it can not be searched for in Manage Time Records app.

Environment

  • Digital Manufacturing 2511
  • Digital Manufacturing 2602

Cause

Fiori elements search bar does not support some characters, specifically ["], [(], [)], [;] as documented here: https://sapui5.hana.ondemand.com/#/topic/3cdebeebb04b4205908140242c9d6817

Resolution

A user must know the exact SFC number and enter it without a search, as long as the SFC number contains characters that can not be searched for.
