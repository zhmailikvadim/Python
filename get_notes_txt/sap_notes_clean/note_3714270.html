Symptom

On detail view of a Space or a Page, the Transports tab is not visible and assign objects to transport not possible in Fiori Launchpad

Environment

  • SAP Fiori Launchpad
  • SAP S/4HANA 2025

Resolution

  1. Apply SAP Note 3691102 - Transport tab doesn't show in app Manage Launchpad Spaces and Launchpad Pages
  2. Clean cache according to 2319491 - How to clean up the cache after applying changes that affect SAP Fiori apps

See Also

3621938 - Spaces+Pages: Save Terminates with Exception
