Symptom

  • The Spanish Tax Agency has published a law that introduces modifications to the annual tax report Model 347 (Declaración Anual de Operaciones con Terceras Personas) on December 3, 2025, Law HAC/1431/2025. 
  • The main objective of this change is to gather more detailed information to improve taxpayer assistance in completing their Personal Income Tax (IRPF) declarations.
  • The modification to Model 347 is expected to include:
    • A new numeric field, NÚMERO DE CONVOCATORIA BDNS (BDNS code), in the declared record (record type 2).
    • This field is meant to capture the call code for subsidies or aid from the National Database of Subsidies (BDNS).
    • The NÚMERO DE CONVOCATORIA BDNS field may only be completed when the operation key for the transaction is "E" (Subsidies, aid ,and other public transfers received from Public Administrations).
    • As a result, the layout of the file for telematic presentation is adjusted. The new field occupies positions 300-305, and the subsequent blank spaces field (BLANCOS) is shifted to positions 306-500.
