Symptom

Working Time Account field is not visible in Employee Time sheet object.

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central - Time Sheet
  • SAP SuccessFactors Time Tracking

Resolution

Working Time Account field contains value if the Time Sheet was submitted manually or by Time Sheet Submit job. 
If the Time Sheet was replaced by Recalculation Job automatically, the Working Time Account field would be empty.
