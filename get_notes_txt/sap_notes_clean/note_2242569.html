Symptom

This article provides information on how to upload and update a custom form

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental." 

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding 1.0

Resolution

Prerequisite

  • Document in Portable Document Format (PDF)
  • Portable Document Format (PDF) Editor

    The editor use in this KBA is PDF-XChange Editor Plus, kindly coordinate with your IT team on what PDF editor is approved in your organization. SAP SuccessFactors is not promoting the product and only using it for demoing and instruction purposes.
  • Although other PDF editors may work, it is recommended to use Adobe Acrobat Professional due to compatibility issues with other editors.


Instruction

  1. In PDF editor, ensure that the text fields are configured and all updates are added.



  2. In [ SAP SuccessFactors HCM > Onboarding > Settings > PDF Forms ], locate the current form that needs to be updated and click 'Choose File' to select the updated PDF.

    In below screenshot, the Form group is 'adi' and the current form is 'Lorem Ipsum'.



  3. Click the 'Update' button to upload the updated PDF



  4. To ensure that you have uploaded the PDF successfully, click the 'View PDF' button and it will pop-up the PDF viewer.





  5. To ensure that PDF field is properly linked to Onboarding keys, verify the 'Input Value'.





    Note

    For Employee or Assignee Signature, please locate if the Sign is populated.



  6. To verify if the updated Form is now showing to employee's print package, create a test candidate and follow the Onboarding process until the candidate click-to-sign page.
