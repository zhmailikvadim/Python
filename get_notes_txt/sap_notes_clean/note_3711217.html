Symptom

You cannot modify the CBS and IBS Tax Situation field in the MASS Maintenance transaction.

Other Terms

Legal Change, Brazil, Mass, Massobj, bus2032, CBS, IBS, Tax Situation, VBAP

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Brazil.

You have SAP Note 3711218 implemented in your system.

Solution

After you implement this SAP Note, you can select and use the Tax Situation for CBS and IBS field in the MASS Maintenance transaction.

The system enables the following field for data entry:

  • Internal Tax Situation for CBS and IBS (J_1B_TAX_SITUATION)

List of Affected Objects:

  • Function Module
    • MAP2E_VBAPKOM_TO_BAPISDITM
  • Includes
    • FV45PFAP_VBAP_FUELLEN_VBAPKOM
    • LVBAKF0C
