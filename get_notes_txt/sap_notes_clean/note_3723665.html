Symptom

Why is customer address dropdown not displayed for suppliers in additional fields during invoice creation in SAP Business Network?

Cause

This behaviour occurs due to following rules configured in Country/Region-Based Invoice Rules, Supplier Group Transaction Rules or Default Transaction Rules

  • Require invoice Sold To Name and Country to match PO Bill To Name and Country (or Sold To information if available)
  • Copy Bill To address on purchase orders to Sold To address on invoices

Resolution

You must disable the following rules to allow suppliers to Choose Address for Customer from the dropdown under Additional Fields, during invoice creation.

  • Require invoice Sold To Name and Country to match PO Bill To Name and Country (or Sold To information if available)
  • Copy Bill To address on purchase orders to Sold To address on invoices

See Also

Please Note:

  • If the supplier is part of a Supplier Group, the rules must be disabled in Supplier Group Transaction Rules.

  • If the supplier’s country is included in Country/Region-Based Invoice Rules, the rules must be disabled there.

  • If the supplier is not part of any Supplier Group and is not included in Country/Region-Based Invoice Rules, the rules must be disabled in Default Transaction Rules.
