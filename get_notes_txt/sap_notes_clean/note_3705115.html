Symptom

You use S/4HANA Group Reporting, you executed "Define Selections" application.

Selections are not retrieved to the screen or the screen is not loaded.

Other Terms

Define Selections, client copy, cleared time-stamps

Reason and Prerequisites

Client copy process incorrectly cleared time-stamps of selections, it is the reason why data cannot be loaded.

Solution

 Import of Support Package or advanced correction by implementation of correction instruction.
