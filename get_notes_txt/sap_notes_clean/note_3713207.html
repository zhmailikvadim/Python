Symptom

Why do I see different Effective and Expiration dates for the Business activity area and the projects?

Resolution

The Effective Date and Expiration Date of the Contract Terms Attributes of the project are converted to server time (referencing the US/Pacific time zone) and then further converted to the user's local time zone (local system time zone) when displayed in the UI. Since this business activity involves date conversions according to the user's system time zone, it is expected that some users will see different dates.

E.g: Thu Jan 08 00:00:00 GMT 2026 will be Wed Jan 07, 2026 – 7:00 PM (EST) Thu Jan 08, 2026 – 5:30 AM (IST)
