Symptom

D112:

The legal change introduces a new technical structure for the monthly D112 declaration, version D112_A7.2.6 (v 7), which is expected to be used for reporting obligations starting with January 2026. A significant modification affects the calculation and reporting of medical leave. Effective July 1, 2026, any corrections to sick leave indemnities from previous months delivered via SAP Note 3703516 HRRO: LC - Medical Leaves January 2026.

 

Other Changes (Payroll Constant Changes):

  • Several economic indicators that influence payroll calculations are updated. For 2025, the minimum gross salary (salMin) is confirmed to increase in two stages: to 4,050 RON from January to June and to 4,325 RON from July to December.
  • The non-taxable amount for employees earning the minimum wage is adjusted for 2026. It is set at 300 RON for the first half of the year and is expected to be reduced to 200 RON for the second half.

Other Terms

D112; HROCDE1; Sick Leave 2026; D_14; D_15; D_16; C2_12x; C2_13x; C2_14x

Reason and Prerequisites

Technical Specifications for changing the XML file, was published here: https://static.anaf.ro/static/10/Anaf/Declaratii_R/112.html .

Solution

  • Calculation of the field D_14, D_15 and D_16 changed. These fields should not contain the unpaid days of the sick leaves (first unpaid day of sick leave).
  • Filing of the fields C2_12x; C2_13x; C2_14x updated based on the latest changes in customization for table T7RO0C delivered in SAP Note 3703516 HRRO: LC - Medical Leaves January 2026.
  • Annex 1.2 update: Added field 28 in section D with the sick leave percentage.
  • Payroll constant changes starting 01.07.2026:
    • The minimum gross salary (salMin) for 2025: 4,050 RON (Jan-Jun) and 4,325 RON (Jul-Dec).
    • The non-taxable amount for minimum wage earners for 2026: 300 RON (H1) and 200 RON (H2).
