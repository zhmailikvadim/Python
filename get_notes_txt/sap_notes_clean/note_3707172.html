Symptom

In app My Inbox, you are trying to verify journal entry, and you would like to view its journal entry posting date in this app, which is currently not visible in the app.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Resolution

App My Inbox does not support this functionality.

Use app Verify Journal Entries in General Ledger - For Processor (Inbox) and app Verify General Journal Entries For Processor (Outbox) instead.

See Also

Refer to: Verify Journal Entries in General Ledger - For Processor (Inbox) and (Outbox) | SAP Help Portal.
