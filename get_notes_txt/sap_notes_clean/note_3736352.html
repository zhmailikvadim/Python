Symptom

  • I am a supplier or buyer user and I cannot update the worker's personal information
  • I am a supplier or buyer user and I edited the Workforce Personal Information but it is not reflecting on the existing Worker record
  • I am a worker and I would like to update my Personal Information

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Sign in as Supplier
  2. Open Worker
    • Observe that "Address 1" is empty
  3. Click on Actions > Edit Workforce
  4. Enter a value for "Address 1" field
  5. Click Continue > Update
    • Workforce shows the value on "Address 1". 
  6. Go back to Worker and "Address 1" is still empty on worker record.

Cause

 Workforce Personal Information changes apply to future Job Seeker/Worker submissions only. Existing Workers retain the data from the time of their original submission, preserving the integrity of what was submitted to each buyer.

Resolution

To update an existing Worker's Personal Information, the Worker must update their information directly by following these steps:

  1. Sign in as the Worker
  2. Click on My Profile (icon with their initials on the top right corner)
  3. Click Settings
  4. On the "User Information" card, click Edit
  5. Enter the necessary information
  6. Click Update
