Symptom

When an invoice is paid by multiple payments, the accumulated amounts will be incorrect in report "A pagar e a receber."

Image/data in this SAP Note is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Reproducing the issue

Scenario:

  1. Create an A/P Invoice with a total of 5000.
  2. Create two Outgoing Payments to pay the A/P Invoice (one pays 2000 and the other pays 3000).
  3. Open Banking → Banking Reports → A pagar e a receber.
  4. In Selection Criteria, set as below:
    ('Periodo de data de' Due Date range should cover the Invoice and Payments Due Date)

Expected behavior:

The accumulated total amounts and paid amounts are 5000.

Actual behavior:

The accumulated total amounts and paid amounts are doubled, 10000.
