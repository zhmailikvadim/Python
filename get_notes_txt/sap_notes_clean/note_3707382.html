Symptom

POC is not transferred to Revenue accounting using KKA2 transaction (Result Analysis)

Environment

Revenue Accounting and Reporting

Resolution

  • If you are using Optimized Contract Management ,  POC-based fulfilments with Result Analysis integration are out of scope.

You can find it in section 8.1 "Detailed Comparison", in "Fulfillments" Functional Area section (Check the release note for your version)

  • This is also explained in the Frequently asked Questions (FAQ) consulting Note below (check the attachment in the Note) :

3015027 - Frequent Asked Question(FAQ): From classic inbound processing (CIP) and contract management (CCM) to optimized inbound processing (OIP) and contract management (OCM)

  • For OCM , Fulfillment by POC van be done by following configurations of the POBs in the BRFplus decision table for POB attributes (DT_PROCESS_POB):

            -Fulfillment type = O (Percentage of Completion)

            -Event type = MA (Manual Fulfillment) or PX (Fulfillment by External PoC)
