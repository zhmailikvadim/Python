Symptom

  • The Time Sheet Status does not change sequentially as expected.
  • The Time Sheet Status remains "Draft" despite being rejected.
  • The sequence displays "Approved" even though the Time Sheet is rejected.

Resolution

This behavior is as designed and expected from the system. For tracking purposes, all information remains recorded in the system. When Worker submit their Time Sheet again, the system will create another sequence to record all information that Time Sheet had.
