Symptom

The purchase document was created and modified before the SP30 upgrade became active. After the SP30 upgrade, the system updated the EKKO-FRGRL field to "true," which caused the purchase document status in HUB to change to "In Approval."

Other Terms

Central Purchasing, Purchase Order status, EKKO-FRGRL, Subject to Release;

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the note to resolve this issue.
