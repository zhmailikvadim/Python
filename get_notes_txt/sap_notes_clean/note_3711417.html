Symptom

An SD invoice is generated and submitted to a public-sector customer via Spain SAP Document and Reporting Compliance (DRC) web service. The submission completes successfully in SAP and displays status 1200 – Registered, including a registration number.

However:

  • The public recipient’s portal/platform does not display the invoice.
  • No acknowledgement or response is returned from the recipient system.
  • The public entity confirms that the invoice was not received.
  • No error message is visible in DRC monitoring.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition
  • SAP Document and Reporting Edition, Cloud Edition
 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Submit the invoice via DRC web service.
  2. Confirm SAP status as Registered 1200 and capture Submission ID/UUID.
  3. Call "get status" in DRC.
  4. Check the recipient's public portal/platform for invoice visibility and acknowledgment.

Cause

Missing eDocument process configuration in Business Partner.

Resolution

  1. Go to Maintain Business Partner application.
  2. Search for relevant business partner or create a new one.
  3. Go to Address > Communication > Other Communication... and choose Secure Store and Forw..
  4. In Notes, enter ES_INV_WS and in SSF-ID, enter web_service_address_test.

For more details, refer to SAP Help portal : Configuring the eDocument | SAP Help Portal.
