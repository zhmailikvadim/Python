Symptom

If a customer is using S4CORE 2025, the business function ACRA (LOG_CMM_ACRA_BF) must not be activated unless the following prerequisites are met:

  • The dependent business function LOG_CPE_SD_MM (Logistics – Commodity Pricing Engine) must already be active.
  • For AGRI clients where the ACM switch is enabled, the new ACPE business function "/ACCGO/NEXTGEN_CPE_ADOPTION" is also required.

Once all prerequisites are fulfilled, the business function LOG_CMM_ACRA_BF can be activated.



Other Terms

S4CORE 2025, LOG_CMM_ACRA_BF, LOG_CPE_SD_MM (Logistics – Commodity Pricing Engine), new ACPE business function "/ACCGO/NEXTGEN_CPE_ADOPTION"

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a new development.

Solution

Implement the attached correction.
