Symptom

  • New application cannot be found in the Fiori Launchpad Search despite of having the business catalog assigned to the business role.
  • "No authorization to start the UI for transaction" for new activities despite of having the business catalog assigned to the business role.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 2602

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Attempt to use the new configuration activity.
  2. Observe the error message: "No authorization to start the UI for transaction".

  1. Attempt to use/find an new application.
  2. No result.

Resolution

The following SAP Note contains the IAM Apps that have been added with 2602 release: 3659244 - Identity and Access Management (IAM): Central Change Overview for SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 2602 - SAP for Me 

Attachment "Delta_S4CE_2602_2508.xlsx" > "IAMAppsAdded" tab

    

The new IAM Apps are automatically included in the business role, but they are not automatically activated: IAM Apps Not Automatically Activated | Identity and Access Management (IAM): Business Role Design with IAM Apps | SAP Help Portal 

   

The new IAM Apps need to be activated as per How to Activate or Deactivate IAM Apps (App Authorization Variants) | SAP Help Portal
