Symptom

  • Invoices arriving from Business Network (BN) are associated in SAP Ariba Buying & Invoicing (B&I) to a different supplier than supplier referenced in cXML.
  • Invoice Reconciliation raises an Unmatched exception.

Example:

Supplier submitted an online invoice with the following From credentials in Header of cXML:

<From>
  <Credential domain="NetworkID">
     <Identity>ANXXXXXX</Identity>
   </Credential>
   <Credential domain="VendorID">
      <Identity>0001</Identity>
    </Credential>
    <Credential domain="PrivateID">
       <Identity>acm_123___0001___0001</Identity>
     </Credential>
</From>

After submission, the supplier linked to invoice in Ariba B&I is 0002

Environment

SAP Ariba Buying and Invoicing

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Configure multiple supplier records in Business Network that share the same Network ID (ANID). (e.g. ANXXXXXX)
  2. Submit a online invoice that references the intended supplier. 
  3. Observe the invoice is associated to a different supplier than the referenced supplier and that an Unmatched exception occurs in Invoice Reconciliation.

Cause

When an ANID/networkid is populated for the supplier in SAP Ariba Buying & Invoicing while multiple supplier records in Business Network share that same ANID, even if I have the correct configuration for use PrivateID to identify the supplier in B&I, the system prioritizes ANID for supplier resolution and does not fall back to PrivateID, so the chosen supplier will always be the one who has the ANID configured.

Resolution

  1. In SAP Ariba Buying & Invoicing, remove the Network ID (ANID) from both the common supplier and the supplier location for the affected supplier so that supplier resolution uses Private ID in duplicate ANID scenarios.
  2. For invoices already submitted and mismatched, perform Manual Match to align them with the correct supplier (refer to: KB0408430).
  3. After removing the ANID, future invoices should resolve to the intended supplier automatically via Private ID.
