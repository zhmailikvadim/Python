Symptom

When you ask for Change of payment terms in the sales order ,  the list creation with relevant F4 help fails due to huge list .

Other Terms

API_SalesDocuments_AI_SRV,Change of Payment Terms

Reason and Prerequisites

When a user requests a change to the Payment Terms in a sales order by providing a description, the system fails to display the relevant F4 help values due to an unrestricted search result.

Because of the large volume of data returned, the list generation fails, and the user is unable to view the F4 help entries containing the appropriate payment term codes.

Solution

Implement the Specified Correction.

Post this correction, When you Ask for the change of payment terms in a  Sales order  on Joule, it displays the data as below.

When you ask for Change of payment terms in the sales order  it displays a list of 30 relevant codes that match the given description.
