Symptom

Slovenian EBS algorithm (FM FIEB_804_ALGORITHM) does not correctly find the payment reference in the memo line.

Other Terms

 Slovenia, FIEB_804_ALGORITHM, Electronic Bank Statement, regex, regular expression

Reason and Prerequisites

Changes in memo line persistence caused different position of new line and space characters in the memo line.

Solution

This change enahances regular expressions that detect the payment reference so that they are robust to white space characters positions around them.
