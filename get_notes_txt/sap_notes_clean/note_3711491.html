Symptom

When I try to upload the KSeF certificate to my supplier account in SAP Business Network I receive the error: The certificate must be of type .12 or .pfx file format.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in to my SAP Business Network supplier account.
  2. Go to the Initials and then click on Company Profile.
  3. Select Country Configuration.
  4. Choose Create.
  5. Select the Country/Region as Poland and the Company Name.
  6. Choose Continue.
     

Select the KSeF certification type, then upload the certificate and the error The certificate must be of type .12 or .pfx file format display.

Cause

This happens because the platform only accepts the PFX/P12 format.

Resolution

If you are using another format, the certificate must be converted to the format accepted by the platform and then you should try to upload it again. If you are uploading in the PFX/P12 format and are experiencing any errors, you can contact our support team to verify internally.

See Also

Please note that our support team does not perform certificate conversions; you will need to carry out that conversion yourself or contact someone in your company who can assist you.

For more information about Tax Invoicing in Poland, you can view the guide: Country Configuration for Suppliers in Poland.
