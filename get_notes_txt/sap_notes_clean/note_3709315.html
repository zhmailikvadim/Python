Symptom

Editing the XML for the Invoicerequest_in interface is not possible on recipient side due to the lack of forward error handling (FEH) integration.

Environment

  • SAP Transportation Management 9.6
  • SAP Transportation Management embedded in SAP S/4HANA 1709 and above releases

Resolution

This is standard behavior. 

  • InvoiceRequest_In is intentionally not FEH-enabled in TM. The reason is mainly an invoice request is considered a legally and financially relevant document coming from an external party. TM is not supposed to alter its business content after receipt.
  • FEH is typically enabled for interfaces where:
    • TM is the process owner, or
    • Technical corrections are acceptable without changing the business intent of the message.
