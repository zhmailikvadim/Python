Symptom

When executing Model Mix Planning (or Sequencing) with Genetic Algorithm for a flat line structure (01SAP_GA02) no orders are processed.

Other Terms

MMP, GA02

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisite:

You have implemented note 3540877 or a corresponding support package relevant for your S4CORE/S4COREOP/SCM/SCMAPO release.

Reason:

A side-effect of the note 3540877.

Solution

Implement this note or a corresponding support package relevant for your S4CORE/S4COREOP/SCM/SCMAPO release.
