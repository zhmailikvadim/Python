Symptom

  • After a 'Test Data Refresh (TDR)' in the test system, no output documents are visible.
  • The output documents visible in the production system are missing in the test system after 'Test Data Refresh (TDR)'.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

Output documents fall under 'Document Management Service (DMS)' and are not within the transfer scope of 'Test Data Refresh (TDR)'.

Resolution

Output documents are part of 'Document Management Service (DMS)' and are not within the transfer scope of 'Test Data Refresh (TDR)'. Therefore, the output document details are not copied from the production to test system during 'Test Data Refresh (TDR)'.
