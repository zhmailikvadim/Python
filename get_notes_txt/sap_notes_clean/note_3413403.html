Symptom

SAP Solution Capability names of SAP S/4HANA Asset Managementand SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition, Asset Management are changed.

Other Terms

Maintenance Planning,  Tactical Asset Management Planning , Resource Scheduling, Asset Management Resource Planning, Maintenance Demand Processing , Asset Fault and Incident Response Management , Asset Spatial Enablement, Asset Data and Information Management for Spatial Enablement, Asset Costing and Valuation, Asset Performance Analysis, Asset Data and Information Management, Asset Configuration Management, Asset Refurbishment

Reason and Prerequisites

SAP Solution Capability names changed to align to Institute of Asset Management – Conceptual Model : https://theiam.org/knowledge-library/asset-management-an-anatomy/

Solution

Here are the previous names and updated names:

  • Maintenance Planning (previous name) –  Tactical Asset Management Planning (updated name)
  • Resource Scheduling (previous name) –Asset Management Resource Planning (updated name)
  • Maintenance Demand Processing (previous name) – Asset Fault and Incident Response Management (updated name)
  • Asset Spatial Enablement (previous name) - Asset Data and Information Management for Spatial Enablement (updated name)

The updated names of solution capabilities can be found in SAP Roadmap Explorer replacing the previous names.

For the taxonomy alignment, the following new solution capabilities are explicitly added. The functionality has existed in previous releases, but was not explicitly described as solution capabilities of SAP S/4HANA and SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition. This is only a change to the taxonomy and the way SAP describes the functionality. This note does not constitute any change to the features and functions of the product.

  • Asset Costing and Valuation
  • Asset Performance Analysis
  • Asset Data and Information Management
  • Asset Configuration Management
  • Asset Refurbishment
