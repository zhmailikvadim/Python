Symptom

Inspection is not created after 3PL inbound delivery is released, although inspection plan is maintained for the 3PL Site.

Environment

SAP Business ByDesign

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Third-Party Logistics Work Center.  
  2. Go to Purchase Orders View.  
  3. Search and select the relevant Purchase Order.  
  4. Choose 'New Inbound Delivery'.
  5. Fill the mandatory details.  
  6. Choose 'Release'
  7. Go to Document flow tab.
  8. The inspections is not created.

Cause

The quality inspection is not supported for 3PL Site (Externally Managed Warehouse).

Resolution

This is standard system behavior.
