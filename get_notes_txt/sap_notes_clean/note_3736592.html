Symptom

What do Effective Date and Expiration Date mean on a contract request or contract?

Resolution

Effective Date is the start date of the contract, and Expiration Date (for non-evergreen contracts) is the date it ends.

A contract is opened automatically when it reaches its effective date, and it moves to Closed (release-order contracts) or Inactive (no-release order contracts) when it expires. After this time, new requisitions generally cannot be made against release-order contracts, but expired no-release contracts may still permit receipts (where the date predates the contract expiration) and/or invoices (handled via invoice exception rules).
