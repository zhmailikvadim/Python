Symptom

The following shortdump occurs during the transfer of a sales order:

Category               ABAP programming error
Runtime Errors         UNCAUGHT_EXCEPTION
Except.                CX_MFLE_MAPPING_ERROR
ABAP: Program          CL_MFLE_CIF_CHK_MAPPER========CP
Application Component  SCM-BAS-INT

Other Terms

VB12, VB13, product substitution, MARA, SPP

Reason and Prerequisites

It can happen when the entered product (VBAP-MATWA) no longer exists in material master and is replaced by its substitute as per what is maintained in transaction VB11. During the transfer to SCM/SPP, the shortdump occurs.
