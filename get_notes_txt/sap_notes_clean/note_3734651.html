Symptom

  • After successfully creating a related business partner, an additional check in CL_CRMS4_IU_BL_UBT->FILL_RELATIONSHIP_DATA uses the relationship category from the selected template instead of the category chosen on the creation screen.
  • An error message "Utilities Business Partner Template : Error occurred. Further information can be found in the log." appears after saving, even though the relationship is created successfully.

    Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental. 

Environment

  • Product: SAP S/4HANA for the releases
    • S/4HANA 2022
    • S/4HANA 2023
  • S4CRM: Utilities Industry

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login into the WebUI with role S4C_UT_IC or a copy of it. 
  2. Confirm any business partner and open the Business Partner Overview.
  3. Choose Create and Create Related BP.
  4. Select any template.
  5. Fill the form for the related business partner and change the relationship category to a different type than the one defined in the template.
    Note: In the current screenshot the filed is already in display mode only.
  6. Save.
  7. Observe that the relationship is created, but an error message appears because the check uses the template’s relationship category.

Cause

  • The relationship category is defined by the selected template and is part of the key of the business partner template business object. Changing the relationship category after selecting the template leads to a mismatch: the post-save check validates against the template’s relationship category, which causes the error.

Resolution

  1. Implement SAP Note 3728852 “Relation type changed when creating related business partner with template” to make the relationship category field non-editable in the “Create Related BP” process.
  2. In customizing, define one template for each relationship type required, and select the appropriate template instead of changing the relationship category during creation.
  3. Avoid changing the relationship category after choosing a template; choose a template that already matches the desired relationship type.
  4. Optional (customer-specific enhancement, use with caution): If editability is needed, re-enable the field by enhancing IUFMBP/VUBuilHeaderTemplate (class CL_IUFMBP_CN_UBT, method GET_I_RELATIONSHIP), and suppress the error by an implicit enhancement in class CL_CRMS4_IU_BL_UBT, method FILL_RELATIONSHIP_DATA (adjust CT_RETURN). Note that suppressing the error may hide relevant errors in other scenarios.
