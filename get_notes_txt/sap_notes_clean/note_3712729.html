Symptom

There is a functionality problem when using an extension installed on Business Application Studio and support will identify the extension.

Environment

SAP Business Application Studio

Cause

Resolution

  1. Open the extension in Extension Marketplace in Business Application Studio (BAS).
  2. Click on it
  3. Find the extension identifier on the right hand side.
