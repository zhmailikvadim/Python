Symptom

Several CDS views that are part of the Environment, Health, and Safety > Foundation for EHS component, (views related to the "EHS Location" entity) have been adapted with analytics annotations for proper support in SAP Business Data Cloud.

Other Terms

I_EHSLocationRevision, I_EHSLocationRevisionNameT

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason: Program error.

Prerequisites: The validity of the correction is maintained in the correction instructions.

Solution

The Support Packages that contain this correction are listed in the "Support Packages" section.
Alternatively, you can implement the correction instructions of this SAP Note.
