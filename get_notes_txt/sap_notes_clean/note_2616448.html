Symptom

Sometimes critical fixes applied to Visual Enterprise products are not correctly applied.

Other Terms

Visual Enterprise Critical Fix
VE CF Issue
Author
Generator
Viewer

Reason and Prerequisites

Downloading files may be blocked by the operating system.

Solution

After downloading a critical fix (or other product)...
-> Select the downloaded file
-> R-click and choose: Properties
-> Tick the box: Unblock

Now unzip and continue the patching or installation process.
