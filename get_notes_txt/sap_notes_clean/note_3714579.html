Symptom

In case of a goods receipt correction the batch tracebility is not correctly updated in ERP.

Other Terms

/SPE/GOODSMVT_CREATE, 309, 310, M721, Deficit of & & & &

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

For each GRC with a batch different from the delivery item, a dummy PC has to be sent additionally before the GRC.  In order the batch tracebility is correctly updated in ERP, the dummy posting change should have the movement type 310.

After applying the note 3697441, the dummy posting change is sent with movement type 310, but the source and target batch fields are not switched, and the message hangs with deficit error.

Solution

Please apply the source below or the relevant support package.
