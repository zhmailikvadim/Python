Symptom

  • After closing an Activity (technically, a ServiceAssignment object) in the FSM Mobile app, the object remains in LOCKED status in the cloud
  • The ServiceCheckout and attachments are successfully synchronized
  • Despite the technician completing the closure on the device, the ServiceAssignment does not transition to Closed
  • Logs may show a synchronization conflict, such as OBJECT_BLOCKED (e.g. "Sync V5 – Error type: OBJECT_BLOCKED – Object: ActivityDTO_V43")
  • This behavior may occur in some cases if multiple updates or merges are attempted on the same ServiceAssignment

Note:

The LOCKED status should not be confused with syncStatus=BLOCKED. A ServiceAssignment (Activity) is considered LOCKED once it has been successfully checked out but has not yet been closed. In the scenario described, although the technician clicked the Close button, the Activity remained in the LOCKED state until the system successfully processed the closure and synchronized with the cloud. 

Environment

SAP Field Service Management 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the SAP FSM Mobile app
  2. Navigate to an assigned Activity (ServiceAssignment)
  3. Perform the required workflow steps for the Activity
  4. Press the Close button for the Activity
  5. Observe the Activity status in the mobile app and in the cloud system after synchronization

Actual behavior:

  1. The Activity (ServiceAssignment) status changes to LOCKED after pressing Close, but does not transition to Closed in the cloud
  2. ServiceCheckout and attachments are synced successfully

Expected behavior:

  1. The Activity (ServiceAssignment) status should change to Closed immediately after pressing Close, with all data synced correctly. 

Cause

  • The activity closure did not persist because the object became blocked during synchronization
  • This resulted in the activity remaining in a locked state, even though the technician closed the job in the mobile app and attachments were synced successfully
  • The system reported the following error in the logs: "Sync V5 – Error type: OBJECT_BLOCKED – Object: ActivityDTO_V43"
  • This potentially occurs due to a merge or synchronization conflict, where the local closure on the device conflicts with updates in the cloud

Resolution

The Activity (ServiceAssignment) remained in LOCKED status due to a synchronization or merge conflict.

To resolve this:

  1. Check the device and cloud for any merge conflicts related to the Activity
  2. Follow the SAP guidance in the Help Portal: Clearing Merge Conflicts 
    This guidance is particularly useful when a device shows a local closure, but the cloud record does not finalize due to a blocked or competing update
  3. After resolving conflicts, re-sync the mobile device to ensure the Activity status updates to Closed
