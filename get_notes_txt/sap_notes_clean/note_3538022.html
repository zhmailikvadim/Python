Symptom

In the Standard Billing FORM printout using the FORM: SDBIL_CI_STANDARD_PL, legal text is not getting printed:
The legal text is: "Simplified VAT invoice – intracommunity delivery pursuant art. 135–138 of VAT act. The tax on the delivery will be settled by the last taxpayer of value added tax."

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA Private cloud Edition,
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Try printing Poland Billing Document using FORM: SDBIL_CI_STANDARD_PL.
  2. Observe the legal text that appears depending on the invoice tax code.

Cause

The Legal Text, appears depending on the invoice tax code.
Missing configuration in the View: GLO_LOG_REAS_V.

Resolution

  1. Access the configuration in the view GLO_LOG_REAS_V.
  2. Maintain the configuration for the required tax code used in Billing Document.
