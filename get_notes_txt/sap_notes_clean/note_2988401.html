Symptom

Segments DTM+752 and DTM+672 cannot be filled in message CH112 in case of electricity.

Other Terms

configuration, CH112_008, AMID 11113, UTILMD_Z19_SG6_DTM+752, UTILMD_Z19_SG6_DTM+672

Reason and Prerequisites

Configuration error.

Solution

Please apply the Support Package mentioned in the SAP Note to your system or implement the correction instruction.
