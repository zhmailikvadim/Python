Symptom

This SAP Note delivers the proxy objects required to process your electronic documents in compliance with the Spain SII HidrocarburosExtraidosDeposito tag addition.

Other Terms

Proxy, Electronic Document, Spain SII, Spain, legal requirement, Document and Reporting Compliance, HidrocarburosExtraidosDeposito

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason

You want to implement the necessary proxy objects to utilize the Spain SII process according to IGIC Legal Change.

Prerequisites

You should have Spain SII solution installed.

This SAP Note is relevant only for Spain.
