Symptom

When trying to run mass maintenance on products and business partners; we encountered a process template field which is mandatory has no entry or options.

Environment

  • SAP Master Data Governance-SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Launch Manage Mass Maintenance-Products/ Business Partners
  2. Create Process
  3. Process template dropdown is empty

Resolution

  • To activate the feature toggle:
    3646136 - SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 2508 - Scope Item 7MI Not Available, Feature BP_CLOUD_READY_MODE, Scope Item 1RK in deprecation
    Follow link 'Successor Mass Load and Mass Maintenance Apps for Business Partners' under recommendation.
    Scroll down to Implementation Details and navigate into 'Activate Features'.
    Here the activation is described.
    Be aware of the mentioned prerequisites: 
    To use this configuration activity, your administrator must first assign the business catalog Feature Toggles 
    - Configuration (SAP_CA_BC_IC_LND_FTG_PC) to the relevant business role.
