Symptom

Agency users are unable to view the job requisition status (open or closed) in the agency portal before forwarding candidates to the job requisition. This limitation may result in candidates being forwarded to closed job requisitions.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in to the agency portal as an agency user.
  2. Attempt to forward a candidate to a job requisition.
  3. Observe that the job requisition status (open or closed) is not displayed.

Cause

Expected Behavior 

Resolution

The current system design does not support the functionality for agency users to view job requisition status before forwarding candidates.

To request a change in this behavior, propose an enhancement request via the Influence Page.Submit a new idea or search and vote for existing ideas on the Influence Page to potentially include this functionality in future product versions.

Refer to the following link for enhancement request submission: SAP Note 2090228 - Enhancement Request Submission
