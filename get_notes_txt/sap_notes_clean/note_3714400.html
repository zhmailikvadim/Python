Symptom

Error message: "There are no revenue rates for resource XXX." occurs when assigning a role with an activity type and billing rate to a project resource in the Customer Projects app, although the related condition records for PSP0/PCP0 have been maintained.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open "Plan Customer Projects" app.
  2. Select an existing project or create a new one.
  3. Navigate to the Plan tab.
  4. Add a new role and assign the role with an activity type and billing rate.
  5. Save the project.
  6. Observe the error message: "There are no revenue rates for resource XXX.".
  7. Check the related condition records for PSP0/PCP0.

Cause

The condition types PSP0 and PCP0 are missing in the applied pricing procedure.

As a result, the related condition records cannot be picked correctly and the revenue rates cannot be determined.

Resolution

The workarounds below can be considered according to the business requirement:

  • If the current pricing procedure is the expected pricing procedure:
    • Add the condition types PSP0 and PCP0 to the pricing procedure to ensure the related condition records can be fetched.
  • If the pricing procedure is not the expected pricing procedure:
    • Modify the configuration in "Set Pricing Procedure Determination" to ensure the correct pricing procedure is determined and the condition types PSP0/PCP0 are involved.
