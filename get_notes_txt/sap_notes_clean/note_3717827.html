Symptom

In the Maintain ACM Trading Contracts app, the system does not properly persist the deletion of a Contract Value Adjustment entry. Even after deleting the entry and saving the contract, the deleted record reappears when the contract is reopened.

-Steps to replicate:
1) Open a trading contract in the Maintain ACM Trading Contracts app that contains multiple Contract Value Adjustment entries.
2) Click Edit and navigate to Item → Contract Value Adjustments.
3) Select any Contract Value Adjustment entry and click the Delete icon.
4) Click Add and then click Save.
5) Reopen the same trading contract in the Maintain ACM Trading Contracts Fiori application.
6) Click Edit and navigate again to Item → Contract Value Adjustments.
7) Observe that the previously deleted Contract Value Adjustment entry is still present.

Other Terms

ACM; Agriculture Contract Management; Maintain ACM Trading Contracts; Contract Value Adjustments

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
