Symptom

The Miscellaneous project type is not available in the process type dropdown when initiating a process from the Qualification tab

Resolution

Any qualification initiated will be displayed on the Qualification tile of your Supplier Management dashboard. However, Miscellaneous processes will not appear on the dashboard because miscellaneous processes are designed to appear under the Processes tab in the supplier profile, not the Qualifications tile. Miscellaneous projects are not tied to specific qualification lifecycle stages. 

Therefore, kindly initiate the qualification process from the Process tab for Miscellaneous project types
