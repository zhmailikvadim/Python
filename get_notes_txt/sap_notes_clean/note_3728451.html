Symptom

Signavio does not flag task names that contain multiple verbs when the verbs are separated by an ampersand (&).
For example: “Check & Request List.”

Resolution

The current conjunction checker does not detect & and skips it during validation. As a result, Signavio interprets the label as a single Verb–Object structure. This behavior follows the logic described in SAP Note : https://me.sap.com/notes/3719825/E
