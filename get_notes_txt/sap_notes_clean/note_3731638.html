Symptom

  • The issue occurs during the migration validation process from ECC to S/4HANA.
  • Customer and vendor are linked to each other and mapped to the same BP.
  • In the first step, when synchronizing the customer to BP, the system creates a BP, and the customer's industry is synced to BP.
  • In the second step, when synchronizing the vendor and updating BP, the vendor's industry overwrites the industry of BP.
  • No error message is recorded in the case.

Environment

  • Sales and Distribution (SD)
  • Logistics Execution (LE)
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Synchronizing the customer to BP, the system creates a BP, and the customer's industry is synced to BP.
  2. Synchronizing the vendor and system will update BP which created in first step, the vendor's industry overwrites the industry of BP.

Resolution

It is system standard design, the one synchronized to BP later will overwrite the industry's BP field. It is advisable to confirm which industry should be maintained for the customer/vendor and ensure the industry values remain consistent between them.
