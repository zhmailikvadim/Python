Symptom

I would like to obtain a complete list of all available worker roles shown in the dropdown when adding a worker, but I am unable to find an option to view or export this list.

Resolution

Worker roles available in the system are configured and managed by each buyer organization, according to their specific business requirements.

To obtain the list of available roles, you should contact the buyer’s Program Management Office (PMO), as they are responsible for maintaining these configurations and can provide the roles currently available for your account.
