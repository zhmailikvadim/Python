Symptom

In Spain plastic packaging tax, the figures reported under concept 1 of the producer's inventory book currently don't consider weight of exempted fractions.

Other Terms

Spain, Plastic Packaging Tax, Plastic Tax, ES Plastic Tax, ES PPT, RDP, SAP Responsible Design and Production, Producer Book, Concept 1, exempted fractions

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for the Spain Plastic Packaging Tax report.

Solution

With this release, the figures for concept 1 will consider weight of exempted fractions. We recommend you check your reporting periods and evaluate the need for recalculation to get the updated figures in the Run Compliance application. Recalculation happens for most not submitted periods.
