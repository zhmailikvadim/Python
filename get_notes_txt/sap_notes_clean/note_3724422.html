Symptom

  • In F.13, after applying the variant in the parameter screen, clicking on multiple customers to exclude a few customers results in the program being executed directly and hanging.
  • This issue occurs only in the FIORI App and not in the WEBGUI or SAPGUI.

Environment

SAP Fiori Launchpad User Interface.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Apply the variant in the parameter screen of F.13.
  2. Click on multiple customers to exclude a few customers.
  3. Observe that the program executes directly without allowing further selection or exclusion of Customer or GL Account and hangs.

Cause

  • Configuration issue.

Resolution

  1. Maintain DYNP_SKIP_SEL_SCREEN = 0 as per Note Note 3196972.
