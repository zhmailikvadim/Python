Symptom

The “When the Following Applies” option on the Terms tab is not available in Approval Templates for Blanket Agreement document type. 

Environment

SAP Business One

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in to SAP Business One.

  2. Navigate to Administration → Setup → General → Approval Templates.

  3. Create a new approval template.

  4. In the Approval Templates – Setup window, select Blanket Agreement as the document type.

  5. Go to the Terms tab.

  6. Observe that the option “When the Following Applies” is not available.

Cause

Correct System Behavior 

Resolution

In SAP Business One, the availability of query-based conditions in Approval Templates depends on the selected document type.

When Blanket Agreement is selected:

  • Query-based conditions are not supported

  • The option 'When the Following Applies' under Approval Templates - Setup window, tab Terms is not available.

This is expected system behavior. Blanket Agreements are treated as master data–type documents, not transactional marketing documents. Approval queries in SAP Business One are designed to work only with transactional documents and their associated tables, which are not available for Blanket Agreements.
