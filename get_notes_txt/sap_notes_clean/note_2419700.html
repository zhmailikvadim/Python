Symptom

In the SDI Data Provisioning Design Time Object Monitor and Task Monitor, youvcan schedule a date/time to decide when a design time object/task will be executed. To do so, you select the values through a dialog which allows you to select the date/time, according to their local time (from your browser). However, the value(Xscron) that you see after scheduling is in UTC.

Other Terms

Environment: SAP Hana 1.0 SPS12 Rev2 (122.02) and SAP HANA SDI 1.0 SP3 (1.3.x)

Reason and Prerequisites

To schedule a date/time to decide when a flowgraph will be executed is done through a dialog which allows you to select the date/time according to your local time (from their browser), but the value(Xscron) that you see in the list of schedules is in UTC.

Solution

This correction is delivered in the release(s) listed in the Support Packages and Patches section of this SAP Note.
