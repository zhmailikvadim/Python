Symptom

No need to trigger process 'DE_B_MDC_CALCULATE_ARTICLE_ID’ in MPM process in case of no pod relationship existing.

Other Terms

/US4G/CL_CHK_MDC_PROCESS, CHK_IF_TRIG_CALC_ART_ID

Reason and Prerequisites

Solution

Please implement the correction instruction in the SAP Note or import the higher version of Support Package.
