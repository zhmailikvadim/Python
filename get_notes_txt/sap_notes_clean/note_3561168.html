Symptom

When creating an intercompany invoice with reference to delivery, error VF484 "Cannot create an intercompany invoice (billing type &1) for delivery &2." is raised.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to VF01
  2. Enter the delivery number and select intercompany invoice type
  3. Press Enter

Cause

In the referenced delivery, the Sales Organization for Intercompany Billing (field LIKP-VKOIV) and the Intercompany Billing Status (field LIKP-FKIVK) are empty. 

Resolution

Please select an outbound delivery that is relevant for intercompany business processing.
