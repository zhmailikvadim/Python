Symptom

In SAP Subscription Billing, the 'ProductEffective' notification is not triggered when a subscription is created directly in Active status, although it works when transitioning from Pending to Active or when changing the product in "Manage Subscriptions" app(F6801).

Environment

  • SAP Subscription Billing
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

In SAP Subscription Billing, although the' ProductEffective' event is always generated, its corresponding notification is suppressed if the event timestamp is older than 24 hours.

Resolution

This behavior is standard by design to ensure the subscription start date falls within the 24-hour notification window if a 'ProductEffective' notification is required, as no system correction is necessary.
