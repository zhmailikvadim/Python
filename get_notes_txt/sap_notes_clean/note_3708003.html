Symptom

The suppression formula in SAP Crystal Report Designer 2025 disappears after saving and reopening the report.

Reproducing the issue

Prerequisites:

Using Crystal Report Designer for SAP Business One 2025 SP0 P1 and earlier versions.

Scenario:

  • Open Crystal Report Designer for SAP Business One.
  • Open a report with a subreport.
  • Right-click the subreport in the design page and select "Format Subreport."
  • In the Common tab, open the Suppress formula.
  • Add any logic, such as "1=1."
  • Save the formula.
  • Save and close the report.
  • Reopen the report.

Expected result:

The formula should be saved correctly.

Actual result:

The formula is blank, and the setting was not saved.
