Symptom

In the Fiori application Revise Payment Proposals, when blocking or unblocking some payments/exceptions, unjustified error message appears: No G/L account has been specified for & /& for the bank, even though correct entries are maintained in table T042I.

Other Terms

F0771, Revise Payment Proposals, FZ018, FZ 018, block, unblock

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction or the mentioned Support Package.
