Symptom

As described in the regulatory change manager under Microgeneration REN 1.059/2023:

  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP ERP Central Component

This SAP Note provides you with system changes to enable the Maintain Energy Credits Manually (EECA_UPD_BR) transaction to use the Credit Classification (CRED_CLASSIFICATION) and Credit Division (CREDIT_DIV) fields. The EECA_UPD_BR report that handles manual updates for the credits should display these new fields allowing customers to retrieve information with correct classification. However, it is not possible to modify the credit classification once it has been created.

The Brazilian National Electric Energy Agency (ANEEL) establishes three classification categories for distributed generation contracts:

  • GD I: Consumer contracts connected or with protocol at Energy Distributors until January 7, 2023.
  • GD II: Contracts connected or with protocol at Energy Distributors from January 8, 2023.
  • GD III: All microgeneration contracts starting their connection between January 8 and July 7, 2023 (valid from 2031); for all others, classification valid from 2029.

These classifications affect how energy credits are distributed and must be maintained throughout all credit operations.

For more information, see also the REN 1.059 published on February 2023 and law 14.300 published on January 6th of 2022.
