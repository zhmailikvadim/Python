Symptom

For a newly created loan, the INVID of the previous loan remains in the transfer to the cash flow check for the new loan. As a result, the system then tries to read the loan in the position.

Reason and Prerequisites

In the cash flow check, this INVID was not deleted after checking the loan that already has INVID. Therefore, this check was executed incorrectly for newly created loans with the INVID of the previous loan.

Solution

In the cash flow check, a deletion of INVID has been added after each loan check.
