Symptom

According to Solução de Consulta 258 (published by RFB on December 31, 2025), amounts paid as seniority benefits to employees aged 65 or older are exempt from income tax.

However, the system currently processes these amounts incorrectly in the following scenarios:

  • Income Tax Calculation
    Seniority values are included in the taxable income when they should be exempt
    It happens in the following wage types:
    • Rend. Tributável (/TI1)
    • Rend. Tributável (Férias) (/TI2)
    • Rend. Tributável (13º) (/TI3)
  • Simplified Income Tax Calculation
    Seniority values are incorrectly included as legal deductions in the simplified income tax calculation, impacting the amount of the following wage types:
    • Dif. IRRF Simp (Regular) (/SI1)
    • Dif. IRRF Simp (Férias) (/SI2)
    • Dif. IRRF Simp (13º) (/SI3)

Other Terms

/IS1, /IS2, /IS3, Ded de Senioridade IR, Seniority Deduction, Income Tax, Simplified Income Tax, Solução de Consulta 258, RFB, Exemption

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason

The system uses the following wage types for employees aged 65 or older:

  • Ded de Senioridade IR (/IS1)
  • Ded. senior. IR (férias) (/IS2)
  • Ded. senior. IR (13º) (/IS3)

Currently, these wage types are:

  • Incorrectly included in the taxable income calculation.
  • Incorrectly considered as legal deductions in the simplified income tax calculation.

When you install an SAP Note, some prerequisites might be required. The prerequisites are SAP Notes that have already been released and that change the same context of the current one. Then, even if these prerequisites are not necessarily related to the symptom of this SAP Note, you must implement them due to technical reasons.

Solution

The system handles the seniority wage types /IS1, /IS2, and /IS3 as follows:

  1. Income Tax Calculation: Exclude seniority wage types (/IS1, /IS2, /IS3) from the taxable income amount (/TI1, /TI2 and /TI3 wage types).
  2. Simplified Income Tax Calculation: Do not consider seniority wage types (/IS1, /IS2, /IS3) as legal deductions in the simplified income tax calculation.

This adjustment ensures compliance with Brazilian tax regulations for employees aged 65 or older who receive seniority benefits.
