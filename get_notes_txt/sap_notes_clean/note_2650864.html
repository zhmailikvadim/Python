Symptom

  • Corrupted Web Intelligence document with orphan section after a Save or migration issue cannot be open in the Web Intelligence Interactive Viewer.

Other Terms

webi, webintelligence, fiori, fiorified, empty, loading, load, lost, navigation

Reason and Prerequisites

Environment:

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform 4.2
  • Web Intelligence Interactive Viewer

Steps To Reproduce:

  1. After a Save or migration issue a Web Intelligence document may be corrupted and have no expression associated anymore to a section
  2. Open the corrupted document using Web Intelligence Interactive Viewer
    1. >> The document fails to be displayed

Cause:

  • This issue is caused by a defect in the product.

Solution

This issue is fixed in the patches listed in the "Support Packages & Patches" section below.
The "Support Packages & Patches" section will be populated with the relevant patch levels once they are released.
For Business Intelligence Platform maintenance schedule and strategy see the Knowledge Base Article 2144559 in References section.
