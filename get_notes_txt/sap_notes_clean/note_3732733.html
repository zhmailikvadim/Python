Symptom

How do I find a "carrier onboarded on BNL"?

Resolution

To check if a carrier is onboarded in BNL, follow the steps below:

  1. Sign in to your Shipper account.
  2. Click Discover Business Partners application >Search for the carrier using the legal entity name.
  3. Review the search results:

  • If the carrier profile is visible, the carrier is onboarded and available.

  • If the carrier profile is not visible, the carrier is not yet onboarded.

See Also

For more reference, please check SAP Business Network Freight Collaboration >Application Help >Shipper Apps and APIs >Discover Business Partners
