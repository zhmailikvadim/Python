Symptom

In the Fiori app Monitor Capacity Utilization (F1523), shift start and end times appear shifted or inconsistent and this issue is also affecting the Downtimes.

Other Terms

F1523, Monitor Capacity Utilization, Time Zone Handling, Shift Definition, Capacity Profile, Factory Calendar, UI5 Date Handling, JavaScript Date, APO-PPS, SCM-APO-PPS-CP

Reason and Prerequisites

The reason is a program error. Client-side date/time conversion used UTC-based JavaScript Date objects without fully applying backend-provided time zone metadata.
As a result, application logic incorrectly relied on the user’s local workstation time zone during rendering of shift intervals.

Solution

Apply the attached Correction Instruction.

No additional manual or post‑implementation steps are required.
