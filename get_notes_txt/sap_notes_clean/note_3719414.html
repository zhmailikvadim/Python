Symptom

While accessing https://account.<region>.hana.ondemand.com, you could see that the certificate is expiring, and you want to know how to renew it.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

SAP BTP, Cloud Foundry runtime and environment

Cause

Validity period of the certificate is ending.

Resolution

The certificates will be automatically renewed by the team responsible prior to the expiration.  They get updated with the regular Update Cycle. This is usually 1-2 weeks before the current one would expire.
