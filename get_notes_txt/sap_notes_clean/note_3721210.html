Symptom

  • Intercompany Invoices Posted via EDI (Electronic Data Interchange) are excluded from the Domestic Sales and Purchases List Report RFIDHU_DSP

Other Terms

RFIDHU_DSP, RFHUDSP, Domestic Sales and Purchases List, VAT returns, AWTYP, IBKPF, EDI

Reason and Prerequisites

The report must also include intercompany invoices (IBKPF) in the output to meet legal requirements.

Solution

  • After implementing this SAP Note, the system includes intercompany invoices posted via EDI in the Domestic Sales and Purchases List Report RFIDHU_DSP.
  • The report now meets the legal requirement for complete VAT reporting.
