Symptom

When executing any Asset Accounting transaction, the system raises error:

FAA_POST018

“Depreciation area &2: More than two open fiscal years”

The posting is terminated and cannot be completed.

Environment

  • Financial Accounting (FI)
  • Asset Accounting (FI-AA)
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise 4.7
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4 HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance 

 

Cause

This error occurs when the posting year differs from the currently opened fiscal year by more than one year in Asset Accounting.

If more than two fiscal years are open in Asset Accounting, balance carryforward values may become inconsistent, potentially leading to incorrect follow-up postings (such as partial retirements or incorrect depreciation values).

To prevent data inconsistencies, the system blocks the posting and raises error FAA_POST018.

Resolution

1. Verify the Asset Accounting fiscal year configuration for the affected company code.

2. Check tables T093B and T093C:

    • T093B-LGBJA (Last closed fiscal year)
    • T093C-LGJAHR (Current fiscal year in Asset Accounting)

3. Compare the values in tables T093B and T093C.

The difference between T093C-LGJAHR (current fiscal year in Asset Accounting) and T093B-LGBJA (last closed fiscal year) must be exactly two years.

Example:

  • If T093C-LGJAHR = 2026, then T093B-LGBJA must be = 2024.

In this scenario, fiscal years 2025 and 2026 are open, which is the maximum allowed.

If the difference is greater than two years, this means error FAA_POST018 is raised correctly by the system.

4. Close older fiscal years. After correcting the fiscal year status, repeat the posting.
