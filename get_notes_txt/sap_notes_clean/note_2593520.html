Symptom

You are using IGS 749/753 PL1 on AIX and the following log entries can be repeatedly seen in the IGS portwatcher trace file:

Wed Jan 3 12:57:20 2018
*** ERROR => [Portwatcher::CallInterpreter] Sending Abort due to Interpreter failure. [portwatcher. 633]

Wed Jan 3 14:22:45 2018
*** ERROR => [Portwatcher::CallInterpreter] Sending Abort due to Interpreter failure. [portwatcher. 633]

Wed Jan 3 14:43:16 2018
*** ERROR => [Portwatcher::CallInterpreter] Sending Abort due to Interpreter failure. [portwatcher. 633]

Wed Jan 3 15:35:08 2018
*** ERROR => [Portwatcher::CallInterpreter] Sending Abort due to Interpreter failure. [portwatcher. 633]

Wed Jan 3 15:38:59 2018
*** ERROR => [Portwatcher::CallInterpreter] Sending Abort due to Interpreter failure. [portwatcher. 633]

The issue does not occur on other platforms and only affects IGS 749/753 PL1 specifically.

Other Terms

Abort, CallInterpreter, Portwatcher, AIX, rs6000_64, IGS 749

Reason and Prerequisites

The issue was caused by a program error.

Solution

Please update the IGS 749/753 to patch level 2 or higher.
