Symptom

You get the warning "The increase in the AWf high (or low) premium wage is not equal to €0, while the combination of the Employment Relationship Type Code, Srtlv Code, and the indicators for the type of employment contract imply that the increase in the cumulative AWf high (or low) premium wage should be €0” (L2225) from the Dutch Tax Office after filing the wage return. You receive the warning for an employee, who was rehired, but not per first day of a week and got a different (either permanent or temporary) contract than the contract type was during the previous employment.

Other Terms

NSV01, Awf High, Awf Low, Weekly Payroll, 2225

Reason and Prerequisites

The issues occur only in case of weekly payroll in the above mentioned situation.

Solution

Import the HR Support Package relevant for your release. For more information, see SAP ERP Human Capital Management Support Package Schedules or SAP S4 HCM Support Package Schedules.

As a general rule SAP recommends you to implement the Support Package. However, if you need to install a solution earlier, use the SAP Note Assistant.
