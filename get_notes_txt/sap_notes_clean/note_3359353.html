Symptom

Prenote Debit and Credit Amount for 0.00 is not being displayed and error Displayed in the title on entering incorrect account in Bank Account Display Activity app.

Other Terms

BankAccountCurrency, Bank Account Display Activity, BADA, Prenote Debit Amount, Prenote Credit Amount

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

You can use transaction SNOTE to implement the correction instructions in advance. You do not have to perform any manual tasks if you implement the advance correction.
