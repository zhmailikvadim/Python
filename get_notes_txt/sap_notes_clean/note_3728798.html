Symptom

 A missing precondition in process step 0700 caused an error to occur in step 100-8900 of the subprocess.

Other Terms

- New Installation
- Change of supplier 24
- DE_B_GPKE_NIN_DSO
- GPKE_DSO
- Registration of Assignment
- E_0608

Reason and Prerequisites

 The error was caused by incorrect preconditions in the process configuration.

Solution

After implementing the fix, the process continues normally after step 0700 without triggering the error in step 100-8900.
Customers must activate BC set /US4G/6_1_SUP_SP08_3728798 in addition to implementing the SAP Note.
