Symptom

You use Intrastat solution for Italy in SAP S/4HANA or SAP GTS(Global Trade Services). In 2022 the field control has been changed for Italian Intrastat declarations, for example, the ‘Partner VAT registration number’ and ‘Invoice value in foreign currency’ fields are not relevant for ‘Items’ (section 1) and ‘Correction Items’ (section 2) in receipt declarations starting 2022.

This note provides updated field control for Italy.

Other Terms

Intrastat, S/4HANA, GTS, PTVAT, /ECRS/V_TCIM1, IFVAL, /ECRS/RP_EDIT, /ECRS/V_TCIS1.

Reason and Prerequisites

 This is legal requirement.

Solution

 Implement the attached correction.
