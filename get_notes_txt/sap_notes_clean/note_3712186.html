Symptom

"Paste" button in mass change option disappeared in app "Maintain Business Users" after 2602 upgrade.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 2602

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the application 'Maintain Business Users'.
  2. Select several business users and click on 'Mass Change'.
  3. Select 'Add Business Roles'.
  4. Click on the selection box to display a list of roles.
  5. Observe that the "Paste" option is disappeared.

Cause

The interface has been updated to align with UX guidelines, resulting in the removal of the clipboard option as it no longer adhered to these standards.

Resolution

Click on the "Show Filters" button within the application. Input the business role IDs into the designated "Business Role ID" field.

This method validates the input format and displays errors if the format is incorrect, offering an improved experience compared to the previous clipboard option.
