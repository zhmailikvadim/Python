Symptom

  • My Time Sheet is in Pending Approval status and I would like to know why
  • My Supervisor approved my Time Sheet and its status has not changed
  • When will my Time Sheet be approved?

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Sign in to SAP Fieldglass
  2. Under Time & Expenses > go to Time Sheet
  3. Locate the Time Sheet mentioned
  4. Check if the Time Sheet is in Pending Approval status

Cause

Some buyer organizations configure approval workflows that require multiple approvers for certain Work Items, according to their internal policies

The Time Sheet remains in Pending Approval status because it has not yet been approved by the assigned supervisor or other approvers defined in the approval group. All approvers in the approval workflow must complete their approval before the Time Sheet status is updated

Once all required approvers approve the Time Sheet, the status changes to Approved

Resolution

The Time Sheet will remain in Pending Approval status until it is reviewed and approved by all assigned approvers

Workers should contact their assigned supervisor to request approval of the Time Sheet. If further assistance is required, the Worker may also contact their Staffing Agency or Program Management Office (PMO)
