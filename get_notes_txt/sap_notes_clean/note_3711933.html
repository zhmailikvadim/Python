Symptom

Why are SAP Ariba URLs now HTTPS in downloaded metadata which used to be HTTP?

Resolution

SAP Ariba has migrated URLs from HTTP to HTTPS to align with industry-standard security protocols and ensure data is protected in transit.

Customer needs to update all configurations to replace HTTP URLs with HTTPS at their end. After completing the update, enable feature PS-1444 in Ariba so that Ariba sends the SAML Issuer with HTTPS. Contact SAP Ariba Support to request enablement of PS-1444.
