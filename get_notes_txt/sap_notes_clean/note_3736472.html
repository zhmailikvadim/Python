Symptom

As part of SAP’s ongoing effort to align with cloud industry best practices and to improve scalability and availability, Standard Reporting will be discontinued with the 2604 release. After the release, Standard Reporting features will no longer be available and any workflows that depend on them may be impacted.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
  • Incentive Management on Legacy Oracle

Resolution

What is Standard Reporting?
 
Standard Reporting is a self-service reporting tool that facilitates rapid report configuration and on-demand report generation on commissions data. Standard Reporting is available only to customers using SAP Commissions on Oracle.
 
As a replacement, Business Objects Crystal Reports is available as a reporting tool for creating a comprehensive set of reports.
 
When Will This Occur?

The sunset schedule for Standard Reporting is as follows:

  • 2604 Non-Production Upgrade: April 11, 2026
  • 2604 Production Upgrade: April 18, 2026
     

What Do You Need to Do? 
 
We encourage you to use the replacement solution, Business Objects Crystal Reports, which is available in the SAP Commissions Oracle environments. We understand that software transitions and changes can be disruptive, and we want to assure you that we are making these changes with your best interests in mind.
 
FAQ:
 
Q. How will this impact me?
A. Standard Reporting features will no longer be available and any workflows that depend on them may be impacted.  Services will otherwise be unaffected.
 
Q. How will this impact my contract or warranty?
A. Your account team can help you navigate your contract and warranty questions and concerns
 
Q. What will happen to the report data: will any data be destroyed?
A. Reporting data is part of the existing Commissions schema and this sunsetting will not destroy data that is currently in this schema.
 
Q. Will I have access to the data after the discontinuation?
A. The data used by Standard Reporting is still in the database schema where Commissions resides. The data flows into these tables as part of the Update Analytics process. This data will still be available, as well as the Update Analytics process.
 
Q. How can I transition to similar reports using Business Objects Crystal Reports?
A. Contact your CSM, services partner, or report developer to help you get started on Business Objects Crystal Reports
 
Q. Will I have access to the old reports after the announced dates?
A. Old reports will no longer be available after the announced dates
 
Need Help?
We are committed to your business's continued success and will be available to assist with clarifications or inquiries throughout this process. If you have questions, please contact our support team via the SAP for Me portal, referencing the component CEC-SAL-TBA.
