Symptom

You are a Managed Service Provider (MSP), this means an outsourced third-party company that takes on the ongoing, day-to-day responsibilities, monitoring, and maintenance of a range of tasks and functions for another company—your customer.

You want enable Credit Card and other digital payments using SAP digital payments add-on.

Other Terms

Managed Cloud, Credit Card Payments

Reason and Prerequisites

You are offering your service for S/4HANA private cloud.  
Your customers are represented as clients in the S/4HANA system.

You purchased SAP digital payments add-on for your customers.

Solution

In the S/4HANA system you must create destinations for the "DIGITALPAYMENTS_OAUTH" service for all SAP digital payments add-on tenants as described in SAP Note 2524512. For example:

  • DIGITALPAYMENTS_OAUTH_1
  • DIGITALPAYMENTS_OAUTH_2
  • ...
  • DIGITALPAYMENTS_OAUTH_n

In the S/4HANA system you must create destinations for the "DIGITALPAYMENTS" service for all SAP digital payments add-on tenants for test and production as described in SAP Note 2524512. In case that you want to use SAP digital payments add-on tentants in different regions (data centers), you must create one "DIGITALPAYMENTS" per region.

For example:

  • DIGITALPAYMENTS_TEST_EU10
  • DIGITALPAYMENTS_PROD_EU10
  • DIGITALPAYMENTS_TEST_EU11
  • DIGITALPAYMENTS_PROD_EU11
  • DIGITALPAYMENTS_TEST_US10
  • DIGITALPAYMENTS_PROD_US10

For client-specific configuration you can use transaction FDP4 and assign the corresponding destinations.
