Symptom

  • Provisioning of SAP Databricks is completed on SAP4ME and the access to SAP Databricks works fine.
  • However, the corresponding SAP Databricks Solution is still in "Processing" status on SAP4ME. The "Edit" option for the Solution is grayed out & only the "Delete" option is available.
  • The associated SAP Databricks tenant is in READY state.
  • This behaviour maybe typical when the Formation which includes the SAP Databricks tenant and the SAP Cloud Identity Services tenant (IAS) was (re)created manually after provisioning because it was missing or not created completely.

Environment

  • SAP Databricks

Cause

A timing issue likely occurred while creating the Formation for the CIS/IAS tenant, during or after Provisioning. 

Resolution

This is a known problem which is being worked on by the SAP BDC Development team and the fix will be available soon. 

Workaround: Delete the solution and re-provision SAP Databricks on SAP4ME - make sure each step is rendered and complete, before moving onto the next step.

Keywords

SAP Databricks, solution, processing, status, stuck, failed,  IAS, formation
