Symptom

When BP with vendor/customer role is updated, error below occurs:

Supplier-specific data were not saved due to error R5 520 (see long text!)
Message no. CVI_BDT_ADAPTER051

Diagnosis
While saving the supplier-specific data of the business partner the following error occurred:

Street *** does not exist in city ***
R5 520

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Cause

Resolution

  • Add the missing entries to the regional structure using the SR10, SR20, or SR30 transactions.
  • Disable the "City file" active check in T-code OY07 for the relevant country 

Keywords

R5520
