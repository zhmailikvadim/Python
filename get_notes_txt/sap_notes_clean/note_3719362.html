Symptom

Activation of joule requires SAP Build Process Automation to be updated from standard plan to default plan.

Environment

SAP Build Process Automation

Resolution

  1. Refer to the SAP Help Portal guide for changing service plans: Changing Service Plans | SAP Help Portal 
  2. Ensure that the necessary steps are followed as outlined in the guide to update the plan from standard to default.
  3. Confirm there is no immediate impact to active development by conducting appropriate testing before and after the update.

See Also

  1. SAP Help Page : Changing Service Plans | SAP Help Portal 
  2. SAP Help Page : Upgrade the Service Plans | SAP Help Portal
