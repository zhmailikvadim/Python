Symptom

Transaction: O3UREP_MASTER

Wyoming Form 2 Regulatory Report is reporting Oil Volume as zero, even when the Oil Volume exists.

Other Terms

General keywords: IS-OIL, Industry Solution, PRA, Production and Revenue Accounting, IS-OIL-PRA, Oil and Gas Upstream.

Problem-specific keywords: Reg Reporting, Wyoming Form 2, Flared, Vented

Reason and Prerequisites

In the reporting data preparation, 'Vented' and 'Flared' volumes overwrite the 'Oil' volume records resulting in zero Oil volume being reported.

Solution

Apply the correction instructions attached with this note.
