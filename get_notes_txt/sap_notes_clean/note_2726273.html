Symptom

Symptom

For hourly wage earners, the determination of the net remuneration in the base calculation for the supplement to maternity pay, in the public sector and in the private sector, is currently not determined based on the calendar day.

Other Terms

Supplement to maternity pay
Determination
Each calendar day
Net remuneration
Hourly wage earners
ZMGS: "Supplement to maternity pay: Determination of net remuneration per calendar day for hourly wage earners"

Solution

The solution is delivered in an HR Support Package. For an advance solution, implement the attached correction instructions.

The symptom is corrected starting from the in-period as of which the new subapplication ZMGS "Supplement to maternity pay: Determination of net remuneration per calendar day for hourly wage earners" is valid. The subapplication is delivered with the valid-from date 1/1/2020. You can set the subapplication valid at an earlier time; to do this, adjust the corresponding entry in the table V_T596D.

Incorrect Volume deltas are shown in Report Details for GLO2 for all leases.

Other Terms

General keywords: Add-on, IS-Oil, Industry Solution, PRA, Production and Revenue Accounting, IS-OIL-PRA, Pre-Roadmap.

Problem-specific key words: IS-PRA, Pre-RoadMap, Report Details, /PRA/RP_WORKPLACE, ROIUY3_TX_RPDT,  Incorrect Volume deltas are shown in Report Details for GLO2 for unitized leases.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Apply the automatic corrections of this SAP Note.
