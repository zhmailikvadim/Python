Symptom

  • Quality notification (QN) status is not completed in the Ariba network even though it is marked as approved in the ERP system.
  • Supplier has created QN of type Q7 but did not mark the QN header status as completed.

Environment

Supply Chain Collaboration - Quality Collaboration

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Verify the QN status in the ERP system; it is marked as approved.
  2. Check the QN status in the Ariba network; it remains in-process.
  3. Confirm that the supplier has completed tasks but has not updated the QN header status to "Completed".

Cause

Supplier completed the tasks associated with the QN but did not update the QN header status to "Completed" in the supplier portal.

Resolution

  1. Request the supplier to log in to the supplier portal.
  2. In the supplier portal, locate the relevant QN.
  3. Edit the QN and update the header status to "Completed".
  4. Verify that the QN status is now synced between the ERP system and the Ariba network.
