Symptom

Net Weight is not imported into SAP DM for the materials without EAN number. 

Environment

  • SAP Digital Manufacturing 2511
  • SAP Digital Manufacturing 2602

Cause

The system works as per the current design.

Resolution

Even though Net weight (NTGEW) is mapped from an iDOC and can be seen in a payload in an Integration Message Dashboard, EAN is required to have it imported into SAP DM.

Keywords

NTGEW, Net Weight, Digital Manufacturing, EAN
