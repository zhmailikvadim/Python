Symptom

Not able to delete location products using “Order-Based Planning: Delete Master Data”. Due to warning message:

 

“XX location products still referenced by forecast orders in vers. XXXX”

 

Even thou no forecast is not seen in the “Projected Stock” app for the mentioned Location product.

Environment

  • SAP Supply Chain Management (SCM)
  • SAP Integrated Business Planning (SCM-IBP)

Cause

The Projected Stock app reflects the forecast maintained in the forecast key figure, if the values from the key figure have been deleted, these will not be shown in the Projected Stock app, but the orders still exist, and can be seen in the “Orders” app.

Resolution

The Order itself must be deleted, a new planning run must be executed for the location product to delete the existing forecast if there are no forecast maintained in the key figure.

Refer to the help page bellow for the deletion of master data.
