Symptom

In the process "Receipt of Delivery Note for Grid Usage Billing (SUP View)" (DE_B_GUB_SUP_DELV_NOTE), step 0130-0030 fetches the maximum profile quantity incorrectly from the beginning of the year to the end of the delivery note period.
This issue is relevant for the market role: SUP.

Other Terms

GUB_SUP, Billing Enhancement, Delivery Note, Performance Price, Profile Value, Maximum profile quantity, Calculate Energy Quantity, /US4G/CL_PRST_DTD_DELV_NOTE, ENHANCE_STEP_DATA, DeliveryNoteBasicPrice, CALC_ENERGY_QUANTITY_DTD, UBD, Utilities Business Data

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error when fetching the maximum profile quantity.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.
After implementing this SAP Note, the system fetches the maximum profile quantity correctly and uses it in calculating energy quantity.
You can verify the fix by checking that for document category 'DeliveryNoteBasicPrice' in the line item of the delivery note, CALC_ENERGY_QUANTITY_DTD of the utilities business data (UBD) has a value.
