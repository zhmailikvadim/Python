Symptom

In case of Net Dedesignation the business transaction category #9072 will be created again through Fiori App F5660 (Manage dedesignation request), it leads to double posting.

The previous flows of this category must be removed and the flows must be recreated in case of Net Dedesignation.

The field 'Transaction Code' always gets the Id of the last executed transaction, in this case the Id of F5660.

Other Terms

 Commodity Risk Management; Commodity Hedge Accounting; Bases Adjustment

Reason and Prerequisites

 Program failure

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction or the related Support Package.
