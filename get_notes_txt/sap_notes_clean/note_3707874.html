Symptom

Getting an internal error during the application process:

errorMessage=lnternal error
name=Error
code=lnternalError

The error occurs when "Reimagined Candidate Experience" is enabled and affects only internal applications, not external ones.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management 

Resolution

Our engineering team are providing a fix for this issue and it will be deployed as part of the 1H 2026 release.

Preview: April 13.

Production: May 15 - 16 

Workaround: In the mean time, please consider removing the SM-mappings from candidate profile template which will resolve the issue for now.
