Symptom

When you enter or edit an attendance, a short dump with the exception condition "UNKNOWN_ACTOR_TYPE" occurs. This affects the ESS Leave Request, HR Renewal, or the Time Manager's Workplace (PTMW).

Other Terms

Short dump, UNKNOWN_ACTOR_TYPE, CL_PT_TD_ITATTABS, CL_PT_TD_DEDUCTION, IF_PT_TD_DEDUCTION~INITIALIZE

Reason and Prerequisites

The dump occurs under the following conditions:

  • It is an absence with quota deduction.
  • There is an active implementation of the BAdI PT_BLP_USER.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the relevant support package.
