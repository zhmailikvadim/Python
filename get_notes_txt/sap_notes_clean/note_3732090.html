Symptom

The count of business partners that can be blocked is intermittently higher than the actual number of partners that can be blocked. This occurs when the blocking for business partner data is executed with parallel processing.

Other Terms

BUPA_PRE_EOP, Max. Parallel Processes, Server Group, lt_batch_partner_sysgrp, resource_failure.

Reason and Prerequisites

The issue occurs during parallel processing when all available work processes are occupied, causing a "resource_failure" exception. The retry mechanism appends duplicate records to the batch table, leading to incorrect counts in the log.

Solution

Implement the note.
