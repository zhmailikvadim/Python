Symptom

In GTI outbound application, if net value of one item exceeds amount limit, GTI will split this items into two or more invoices. After splitting, there will be rounding difference in 'Tax Amount' field. This will cause difference between SAP documents and VAT invoices.

This note is for SAP NWBC version of GTI solution only. It does not affect the GUI version of GTI.

Other Terms

XX-CSC-CN-GTI, GTI, Difference, Tax amount, outbound

Solution

Apply this not into your system.
