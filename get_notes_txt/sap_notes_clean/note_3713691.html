Symptom

After the CE2602 upgrade, APP F9067 are listed as "IAM App added inactive" in the change log but are not visible in the IAM-App-Overview and cannot be found in the system. 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. After CE2602 upgrade.
  2. Access the business role BR_AA_ACCOUNTANT.
  3. Verify the change log indicating the addition of the app F9067 as inactive on 01/31/2026.
  4. Unable to find app F9067 in the system.

Cause

Feature toggle off.

Resolution

The F9067 app has not been delivered; its feature toggle is currently off, so it is not available for use. The "What's New" section will be updated when the app becomes available.

Keywords
