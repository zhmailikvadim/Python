Symptom

The bank information sent through the Outbound/Request_In payload from Ariba is not updating the vendor profile (XK03 transaction code) in the ECC or S/4 HANA system. But the same Bank information can be seen in BP transaction.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Verify the bank information sent through the Outbound/Request_In payload from Ariba.
  2. Check the vendor profile for the affected supplier in the ECC or S/4 HANA system.
  3. Observe that the bank information is not updated in the Vendor table (XK03).
  4. Confirm the validity period end date of the bank information in the ECC or S/4 HANA system under BP transaction.

Cause

The bank information created in the ECC or S/4 HANA system has an expired validity period end date of xxxx-xx-xx which prevents it from updating in the Vendor table (XK03).

Resolution

  1. Update the Validity End Date to 9999-12-31 in the ECC or S/4 HANA system under BP transaction.
  2. Trigger an Inbound/Request_Out payload from ECC or S/4 HANA system to Ariba to ensure the validity periods are correctly updated in Ariba.
  3. Trigger an Outbound/Request_In payload from Ariba back to the ECC or S/4 HANA system to update the bank information in both BP and the Vendor table (XK03).
