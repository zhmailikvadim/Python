Symptom

This SAP Note is relevant only for ENBIL customers.

In accordance with Sec. 36h Para. 2 EEG, the values to be created are adjusted with effect from the start of the sixth, eleventh, and sixteenth year following the commissioning of the installation based on the location income of the assets in accordance with Annex 2, point 7 in the five previous years. In the EEG, this means that it is uniquely stored on the date of commissioning of the WEA and not on a different date.

As a result, the changes to the location yield and quality factor and any resulting change to the value to be created are taken into account in forward billing as of an incorrect date. As a result, the allocation according to Sec. 24 Para. 3 EEG and the remuneration amount (depending on whether a new correction factor and therefore a new AW result) is incorrect in the corresponding payroll period.

Other Terms

ENBIL, EEG, EEG billing, section 36h EEG

Reason and Prerequisites

Incorrect date determination for determining the quality factor
