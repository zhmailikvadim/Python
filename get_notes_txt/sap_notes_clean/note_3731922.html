Symptom

When supplier submits an invoice for a PO with lines having different Ship To address, there are two possible scenarios:

  • Header Level Shipping
  • Line Level Shipping

However, the line level shipping information is not being populated when the invoice line type is service, not material.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Sign in to Business Network Supplier account
  2. Open a Service PO
  3. Click to create a new invoice 
  4. Go to the Shipping section
  5. Click Line Level Shipping

Resolution

Copying the ShipTo Address to service lines on an invoice isn’t supported—this functionality is only available for material lines.

However, an Enhancement Request can be raised to suggest this functionality.
