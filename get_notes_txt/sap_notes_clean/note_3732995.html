Symptom

  • Email provider/domain cannot be verified in the console.
  • Error message: "Verification Failed We are not currently able to verify this provider. Note that it can take up to 24 hours from the time you have added the DNS records to your domain before the verification will succeed."
  • DNS records were added long before the attempted verification, but verification still fails.

Environment

  • SAP Customer Data Cloud
  • SMTP

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Attempt to verify a configured sending domain/email provider in the console after DNS records (SPF/DKIM) have been published.
  2. Go to relevant partner ID and open Email provider feature
  3. Choose relevant domain, add required details and click on 'verify'
  4. Got error response: Verification Failed

Cause

The SPF record was unverified and configured with a hard-fail "-all", causing messages to be rejected when SPF could not be applied and preventing successful provider/domain verification.

Resolution

  1. Review the SPF record for the sending domain and change the policy from hard-fail "-all" to soft-fail "~all" to avoid outright rejection during verification.
  2. Ensure the SPF record includes all required mechanisms for the authorized sending service and/or IPs according to your email service provider’s documentation.
  3. Publish the updated DNS TXT record and allow time for DNS propagation.
  4. Confirm that SPF status is verified by your email service provider and that DKIM records are active.
  5. Retry the email provider/domain verification in the console.
  6. After successful verification, consider tightening the SPF policy as appropriate for your email program.
