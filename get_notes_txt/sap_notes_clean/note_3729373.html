Symptom

 In the Fiori app "Display Energy Amounts," the "Amount" field displays only three decimal places. However, gas factor values require four decimal places, resulting in the last decimal place being missing.

Other Terms

 Display Energy Amounts, Fiori app, gas factor, decimal places, Amount field, CDS view

Reason and Prerequisites

 The error occurred because of an incorrect cast statement in the CDS view.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the "Amount" field in the Fiori app "Display Energy Amounts" displays four decimal places. You can verify the fix by checking that the "Amount" field displays four decimal places for gas factor values.
