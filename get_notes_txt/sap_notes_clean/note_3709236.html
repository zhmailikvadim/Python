Symptom

Field "first-name-alt1" and "last-name-alt1" can not be controlled by role-based permission.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Field "first-name-alt1" and "last-name-alt1" have been enabled in "Manage Business Configuration" > Personal info
  2. The user don't have permission to edit these 2 fields in role-based permission
  3. Go to Onboarding Dashboard > Find the new hire > Try to completed "Review New Hire Data" step
  4. The 2 fields are editable

Resolution

There is no separate permission control for these two fields.

If "Employee Central Effective Dated Entities > Personal Information" is enabled in role-based permission, these two fields will automatically become editable.
