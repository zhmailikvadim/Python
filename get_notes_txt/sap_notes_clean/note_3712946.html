Symptom

  • Duplicate postings are displayed on LinkedIn.

Environment

Successfactors Recruiting Posting

Cause

Only one of the jobs is posted via SuccessFactors. LinkedIn appears to scrape jobs from the external career site, resulting in duplicate postings.

Resolution

  1. Confirm that only one job is posted via SuccessFactors by verifying the UTM source in the job link.
  2. Identify the duplicate job that is not posted via SuccessFactors.
