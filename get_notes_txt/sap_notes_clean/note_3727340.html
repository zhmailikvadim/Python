Symptom

I am unable to find workers in Closed status when searching in the Worker list.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Sign into you SAP Fieldglass account
  2. Click on View> Workers
  3. Search for a worker
  4. No results were found

Cause

When workers are in Closed status, their records are moved to the Past & Archive Data section after a certain period of time. Because of this, they may no longer appear in the standard Worker list search results, which primarily display active or more recent work records. 

Resolution

  1. Sign into your SAP Fieldglass account

  2. Navigate to Admin (gear icon)

  3. Select Past & Archive Data

  4. Choose the Worker module

  5. Set the appropriate time period for the worker record

  6. Click Apply Filter

This will allow you to find workers whose records have already been archived.
