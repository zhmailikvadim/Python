Symptom

During amortization within a key date valuation, a flow is created, even when the amount in position and valuation currency is zero. The creation is only expected when the customer uses SAP Treasury and Risk Management, impairment accounting extension for expected losses.

Other Terms

 Amortization, Effecitve Interest, Key Date Valuation

Reason and Prerequisites

You use SAP Treasury and Risk Management and process an amortization where the amortization amount is 0 and an effective interest rate exists.

Solution

 Implement the attached correction instruction or the mentioned support package.
