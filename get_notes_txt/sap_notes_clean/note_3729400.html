Symptom

  • Two API keys/Site were activated in B2B organisation management within 5 minutes
  • One of Sites is not visible on the list in Organisation management

Environment

  • SAP Customer Data Cloud
  • Organization Management (B2B)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Enable the Customer Data Cloud (CDC) B2B for Sites in the CDC Console.
  2. Disable the B2B for Sites.
  3. Check the site list in the CDC administrative interface. Sites are still inactive.
  4. Attempt to create or activate additional sites in the tenant. The system prevents this action due to the tenant reaching the maximum allowed site quota.

Cause

The issue is caused by an inconsistent state between the CDC Console (site management) and the B2B Organization Management environment after the B2B add-on was activated and later disabled. Remaining artifacts from the B2B enablement process were not fully cleaned up, leading to residual sites being counted toward the tenant’s site limit.

Resolution

  1. Ensure that actions to enable, disable, or reactivate sites are performed one at a time.
  2. After performing an action on a site, wait at least 5 minutes for the site status to update correctly before proceeding with the next action.
  3. If the issue persists, contact Customer Data Solution Support for further investigation and manual intervention to remove residual artifacts or repair incomplete activation entries.
