Symptom

In SAP Invoice Management by Open Text when you run the Invoice life cycle report the 'Number of Touches' for a DP are not displayed.

Environment

SAP Invoice Management by Open Text 16.3

Cause

The issue is caused due to program error.

Resolution

The issue is resolved in:

  • SAP Invoice Management by Open Text 20.4 SP3
  • SAP Invoice Management by Open Text 7.6 SP3

Please contact support team with reference to this KBA and KB20771564 if you need more details on the fix.
