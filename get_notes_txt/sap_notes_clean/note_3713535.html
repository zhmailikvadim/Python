Symptom

You declare your exports from SAP GTS to the Swiss customs system Passar.

This SAP Note delivers the check for the following rule:

  • NZ50016 - Required Justification data group (status RELEASED is checked)

Not all possible statuses of the goods declaration export in Passar are taken into account for the check.
The system mainly checks for the status 'RELEASED'. As a result, subsequent statuses of the export goods declaration are also taken into account implicitly in the check of the rule NZ50016.

Reason and Prerequisites

ICC, incompleteness check, message NE014, justification
