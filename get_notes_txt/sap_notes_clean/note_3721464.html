Symptom

Within the Enhanced AFI Logic, there are two possible use scenarios:

  • Traditional Accounting, based on past events, ie. business transactions, and
  • Economic Accounting, based on cash flows, expected or scheduled.

The calculation framework must know which approach applies to a given work package item.

Reason and Prerequisites

This note is relevant for

  • Enhanced AFI Logic, formerly Accounting for Financial Instruments Based on Expected Cash Flows (ECF)

Solution

With this note, the above-mentioned information is provided within the calculation framework and made available in the calculator detail logs. Note that the determerination of the use scenario is based on the presence of Register Offset target values.
