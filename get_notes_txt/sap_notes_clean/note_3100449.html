Symptom

You create a Sales Quotation or Sales Order as a follow-up document from a Sales Opportunity. During this follow-up creation, the pricing conditions set with a Calculation Type equal to 'A' (Percentage) are incorrectly copied from the preceeding document (reduced by a factor of 10).

Other Terms

Price conditions, Copy, Percentage, Divided by 10, Follow-Up, Opportunity, Quotations, Sales Orders.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instruction.
