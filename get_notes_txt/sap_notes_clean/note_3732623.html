Symptom

This article describes functionality introduced in the May 2026 SAP Fieldglass release

When creating or editing a Statement of Work (SOW) in SAP Fieldglass, you may notice the following changes:

  • You can save an SOW as a draft without completing required fields
  • You can move between pages even when required information is missing
  • Some fields or badges display “(No Value)”
  • The SOW Duration card is not displayed when dates are missing
  • Error messages appear but do not stop you from continuing
  • Draft SOWs appear in list views without start or end dates

Reproducing the Issue

You may observe this behavior when you:

  • Save an SOW in Draft status without completing required fields
  • Navigate between SOW pages while required information is missing
  • View an SOW with missing date information
  • Do not see the SOW Duration card while dates are incomplete
  • Review error messages that appear while drafting
  • View draft SOWs in list views that do not include dates

Cause

  • SAP Fieldglass has enhanced the SOW creation process to support a more flexible drafting experience
  • Required fields are now validated only when an SOW is submitted, allowing you to save drafts and complete information gradually instead of entering placeholder values

Resolution

During Drafting

You can:

  • Save an SOW as a draft without completing required fields
  • Move between pages without being blocked by missing information
  • Review error messages that appear for awareness while drafting

Visual Indicators

You may see the following until required information is completed:

  • “(No Value)” displayed for incomplete fields or badges
  • The SOW Duration card hidden until required dates are entered
  • Draft SOWs appearing in list views without start or end dates

At Submission

Before submitting the SOW:

  • All required fields must be completed
  • SAP Fieldglass guides you to any missing information

This behavior is expected and is part of the updated drafting experience
