Symptom

You process delivery with serial numbers. EWM incorrectly registers deletion and creation of serial numbers in the change document for the delivery even when you do not add or remove any serial number from the delivery. Actions can be different, like loading, unloading of handling units containing serial numbers, etc...

Other Terms

CDPOS, /SCWM/DLV_SERI, change document, SCDO

Reason and Prerequisites

This issue is caused by program error.

Solution

Implement the relevant support pack or the attached corrections.
