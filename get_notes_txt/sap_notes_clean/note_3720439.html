Symptom

I am experiencing an issue with our custom timesheet reports. For new SOW-based contractors, the column for Time Sheet Billable Hours [Regular/Hr] incorrectly displays 0 hours, even though hours have been submitted and approved. We have tried several search field combinations, but the results remain zero.

Cause

This behavior typically occurs because SOW-based timesheets may use different billing units (such as daily rates) or custom rate categories that do not map to the standard "Regular/Hr" billable hours field.

Additionally, if the report is filtered by specific submission dates rather than Invoice Approved Dates, data gaps may appear if the contractors' billing cycles do not align with the report's date range.

Resolution

To resolve reporting discrepancies for SOW contractors, you must update the report fields and filters to account for SOW-specific billing logic.

  1. Add SOW-Specific Fields:
    • Edit the impacted report.
    • Add the field Time Sheet Billable Days (by Rate Category).
    • For SOWs submitted on a daily basis, this field captures the data that the hourly field misses.
  2. Adjust Date Filters:
    • If data appears missing for specific weeks, check the Invoice Approved Date instead of the Time Sheet Submission date.
    • Use dates when the corresponding invoices were fully approved (e.g., filtering by the start of the following month) to ensure all processed hours are captured.
  3. Validate Rate Categories:
    • Ensure that the Rate Categories used on the SOW are active and marked as "Billable" in the system configuration.
