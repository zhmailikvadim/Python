Symptom

While performing Change of Stock, system shows error:
“3PL site not allowed for the process.”

Environment

SAP Business ByDesign

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to internal logistics work center.
  2. Select Change of Stock under Common task.
  3. Enter the Site ABC (ABC represents 3PL Site ID).
  4. System throws the error "3PL site not allowed for the process".

Cause

Change of stock transactions are not supported for 3PL Site (Externally Managed Warehouse).

Resolution

This is standard system behavior.
