Symptom

  • During go-live smoke test, the "SC-42365 'COR: Pricing Conditions support in Order Confirmation'" feature is not functioning as expected.
  • The purchase order (PO) modification structure is not being replicated to the Order Confirmation screen.
  • The issue occurs in both test and production buyer accounts.
  • The behavior was working previously and started failing on a later date.

Environment

  • Product: SAP Business Network Supply Chain Collaboration
  • Supply Chain Collaboration - Order Collaboration

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the Order Confirmations area in the buyer portal for a PO that includes pricing conditions or a PO modification structure.
  2. Open the Order Confirmation screen and observe that the PO modification structure does not replicate to the confirmation.

Cause

The transaction rule Allow suppliers to edit allowances and charges to confirmations at unit price level was not enabled, which prevented the CSC-42365 functionality from operating as designed.

Resolution

  1. Verify whether the feature flag CSC-42365 'COR: Pricing Conditions support in Order Confirmation'" is enabled for the relevant tenant(s).
  2. Enable the transaction rule Allow suppliers to edit allowances and charges to confirmations at unit price level for the buyer portal.
  3. Repeat the confirmation process and verify that the PO modification structure (pricing conditions) is now replicated to the Order Confirmation screen in both test and production environments as required.
