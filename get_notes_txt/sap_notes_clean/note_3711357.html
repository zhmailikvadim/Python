Symptom

The determination of location addresses differs in case of advanced shipping/receiving relevant deliveries. When the freight unit is created from the MM document then it is allowed to use beside shipping/receiving points also business partner and one-time addresses. But if the delivery is created by the order as predecessor then only adv. SR relevant shipping/receiving points are taken as for example destination addresses. For all other types an error message is thrown and the delivery creation is prevented.

Other Terms

TM Advanced Shipping/Receiving process; One-Time-Address; Business Partner address; LE-TM-Integration

Reason and Prerequisites

This erroneous behaviour results from an incomplete determination of the adv.SR relevance for a delivery within the LE application. Furthermore in the source and destination determination of the LE-TM integration the correct determination is prevented in case of one-time and business partner addresses.

Solution

Implement the corrections of the attached correction instructions or apply the corresponding support package.
