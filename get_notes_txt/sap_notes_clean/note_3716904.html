Symptom

Law No. 15,270, of November 26, 2025, amended Law No. 9,250, of December 26, 1995, and Law No. 9,249, of December 26, 1995, to establish a reduction of the income tax due on the monthly and annual tax bases and to introduce minimum taxation for individuals who earn high incomes in Brazil.

  • As of January 2026, taxpayers with a monthly income of up to BRL 5,000.00 will no longer be subject to IRPF.
  • The increase in the non-taxable bracket (0% rate) is granted through a mechanism that reduces the monthly IRPF by up to BRL 312.89. The reduction amount is limited to the tax calculated according to the monthly progressive tax table.
  • This change is expected to directly affect all companies in Brazil due to the new monthly tax reduction rules.

For more information, see the Brazilian authorities‘ website.

Cause

Legal change

Solution

SAP intends to provide a feature package or patch to solve the symptom that is described in this SAP Note. The section References below lists the related feature package or patch once they become available. Be aware that References can only be confirmed at feature package or patch release date. SAP delivers feature packages or patches only for selected releases at its own discretion, based on the business impact and the complexity of implementation.

The following changes are planned for SAP Business One and SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA to address this legal requirement:

  • New columns that will enable the setup of the Fixed Deductible Amount and Input Parameters for reduction calculation of services with monthly income between BRL 5,000.01 and BRL 7,350 are planned for SAP Business One in Withholding Tax Definition – Setup.
  • Monthly accumulation should apply in cases of progressive withholding tax calculation.
  • A new calculation method, following the new regulation, will be introduced for withholding tax calculation when value ranges apply.

Planned delivery of legal change solution: May 15, 2025
