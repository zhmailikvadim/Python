Symptom

After upgrading to SAP S/4HANA 2025 SP00, the "Delete" button for ATC exceptions is no longer visible in the exemption editing screens.

Environment

ABAP Check Frameworks - ABAP Test Cockpit, Code Inspector

Cause

Exemptions in 2025 SP0 are transportable objects, requiring formal deletion processes to maintain auditability and consistency.

Resolution

Starting with ABAP Platform 2025 SP00, the “Delete” action for ATC exceptions was renamed to “Archive.” It performs the same function as before.

Use “Archive” to remove an ATC exception. No additional configuration is needed to “enable Delete”.

Keywords
