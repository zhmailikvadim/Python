Symptom

  1. An error occurs when attempting to verify a custom in SAP Identity Authentication Service (IAS) Mail Configuration settings.
  2. Domain verification for other domains is taking time to complete, displaying the message: "Domain verification may take some time to complete. Please try again later."

Environment

Identity Authentication Service

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Configure Mail Configuration settings within IAS to use Amazon Simple Email Service (SES).
  2. Attempt to verify the a custom domain.
  3. Observe the message: "Domain verification may take some time to complete. Please try again later."

Cause

Domain verification may take up to 72 hours to complete.

Resolution

As explained in Configure Amazon Simple Email Service, the domain verification may take up to 72 hours to complete (usually it takes around 5 minutes).

See Also

SAP Help - Configure Amazon Simple Email Service
