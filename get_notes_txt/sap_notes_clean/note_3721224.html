Symptom

When certificate handling for inbound deliveries is active in SAP Extended Warehouse Management (EWM) using inspection object type 4 (Q-Inspection Product/Batch Inbound Delivery), the status "CTCM – Certificate Confirmation Missing" may be assigned incorrectly to inspection lots under certain conditions:

  • In inspection lots created during Acceptance Sampling.
  • In inspection lots created due to partial follow-up goods receipts for the same purchasing document item, provided that a certificate is not required for each goods receipt item (the setting "Certificate Required for Each GR Item" is not set in the relevant certificate profile).

In these scenarios, certificate handling is either not supported (for example, in the case of Acceptance Sampling) or not correctly considered in the context of partial goods receipt processing, which can lead to the incorrect assignment of status CTCM.

Other Terms
