Symptom

- Unable to change Head of Household for Vermont tax form

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Reproducing the Issue

1. Login as user candidate
2. Click compliance forms
3. Go to Vermont State Withholding Form W-4VT
4. The 4. Enter "1" if you plan to file as "head of household" is disabled

Cause

- Unable to change Head of Household for Vermont tax form

Resolution

  • As per Product Management team, this is expected output that based on Candidate Filing status, few values will be defaulted and respective field would turn into non-editable mode.
  • Re-verified all validations in the form, everything is working as expected according to declarations in pdf form.
