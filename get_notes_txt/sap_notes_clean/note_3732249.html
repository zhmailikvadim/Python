Symptom

When SAP-HR attributes (such as AWART) are present in the time record, auto-determination of end time based on start time and recorded hours does not happen.

Other Terms

F3074A, My Timesheet V4, Consolidated Time Recording, CATS_DETERMINE_HOURS, End Time, Start Time, Recorded Hours

Reason and Prerequisites

The scenario pertaining to Consolidated Time Recording was not handled during determination of the required fields.

Solution

Implement the automatic correction instruction attached to this SAP Note. Alternatively, you may implement the relevant support package.
