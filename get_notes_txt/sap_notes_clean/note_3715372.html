Symptom

During the automatic transformation of the link between real estate and receivable, the RBL_ID is formatted incorrectly in the class /IBS/CLFM_TRA_RE_FOB_CREATE-CREATE_RBL_KEY.

The procedure for setting up the RBL ID is usually as follows. BUKRS / RANL / SANLF

In the above However, the method fills the GSART instead of SANLF during the setup of the RBL ID.

Reason and Prerequisites

During the automatic transformation of the link between real estate and receivable, the RBL_ID is formatted incorrectly in the class /IBS/CLFM_TRA_RE_FOB_CREATE-CREATE_RBL_KEY.

Solution

GSART has been replaced by SANLF.
