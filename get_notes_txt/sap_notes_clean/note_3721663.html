Symptom

  • Business partner master data is not being screened against the new legal regulation in the sanctioned party list (SPL) screening process within SAP Global Trade Services (GTS).
  • The SPL legal regulation has been activated and the SPL check should be performed on BP master level but the fact is not.
  • Audit trails confirm that customers are not screened against newly created legal regulation when transferred via "initial transfer of master data".

Environment

  • SAP Global Trade Services 11.0
  • SAP Global Trade Services Edition for Hana

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Transfer customer data via transaction code /SAPSLL/DEBMAS_DIRR3 to GTS.
  2. Verify audit trails for the screening process via Fiori App 'Display audit trail'.
  3. Observe that customers are only screened against existing legal regulations, but not newly created legal regulations.

Cause

The cause of the issue is related to the system retrieving data from the buffer, but the new legal regulation is not included in the relevant function module for customer screening.

Resolution

Please execute transaction /SAPSLL/SPL_RESET after making any customizing changes to ensure the buffer is reset.
