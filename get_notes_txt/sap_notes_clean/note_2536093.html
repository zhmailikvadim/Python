Symptom

An employee has contract information recorded in IT0016 valid until a specific date and is fired through IT0000 before the expected date of contract termination.

When the payroll is executed, the wage type related to the payment of indemnity (“indemnización”) exceeds 30 days and the final value is higher than the indemnity base.

Resolution

It is correct for the system to exceed 30 days of indemnity payment in some cases.

The system takes the fire action BEGDA and the contract expected ENDDA and calculates the number of days between these two dates. If this difference exceeds 30 days, the system will calculate the compensation payment accordingly – which means the wage type will display a value that is higher than the base in /770.

If the value in the indemnity wage type must be changed by any business reasons, this can be done by modifying the contract validity date in IT0016, adjusting it according to the expected results.
