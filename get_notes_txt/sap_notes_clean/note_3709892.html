Solution

We are pleased to announce the release of SAP GRC for SAP HANA 1.0, SP00,  to customers on March 6, 2026, with restricted shipment exclusively available to our Early Adopter Care (EAC) customers.

General Availability (GA), SP01,  is planned for early Q3 2026, at which time all customers will be able to download the new solution for upgrades or as a net-new implementation.

The documentation for SAP GRC For SAP HANA 1.0 can be found here: https://help.sap.com/docs/SAP_GRC_FOR_SAP_HANA
