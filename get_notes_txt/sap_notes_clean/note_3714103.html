Symptom

When the same plant is used both:

- in the Company ID level, and

- in the organizational level assigned to that Company ID,

the Parent ID of the parent object is not included in the equipment outbound IDoc generated for the organizational level. As a result, the equipment is sent without information about its parent object, which leads to inconsistencies between FSM and SAP ECC/S4 systems.

Other Terms

FSM, E4C, Connector, Equipment, Functional Location, Parent ID, /PACG/ECM_EQUI, IDoc, Organizational Level

Reason and Prerequisites

The issue was caused by an incorrect relevance check during IDoc preparation in methods PREPARE_EQUI and PREPARE_TELO of class /PACG/ECM_CL_S_IDOC_O_EQUI2.

Due to this incorrect validation logic, the system determined that the parent functional location was not FSM-relevant and therefore cleared the PARENT_ID field in the outbound IDoc.

Solution

Please, implement this correction instruction.
