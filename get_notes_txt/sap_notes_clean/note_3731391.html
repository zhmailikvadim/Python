Symptom

In the process "Determination of Actions on Meter Reading" (DE_B_MET_MR_MRP_FRAMEWORK), when you select the End Process button in BPEM (category MR01) raised by step 0030 (Execute Actions on Meter Reading), the process doesn't end and remains in an unchanged status.

This issue is relevant for the market roles: SUP, DSO, MOS.

Other Terms

MSCONS, MET, BPEM, MR01, MRP, Execute Actions on Meter Reading, meter reading

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because of missing configuration in the system.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.
