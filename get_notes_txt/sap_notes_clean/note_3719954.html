Symptom

In the Fiori app for malfunction reports (F2023), creation date and time fields are displayed incorrectly after saving a notification.

The issue appears when user time zone and system time zone differ.

The problem occurs without any error message.

Other Terms

F2023, CL_EAMS_BO_NTF_TIMECONV, CONVERT_USERTIME2SYSTEMTIME, CL_EAM_BOPF_NOTIF_HEADER_DEF, EAMS_S_BO_NTF_HEADER, Time zone conversion, TZS1_SESSION_TO_SYSTEM

Reason and Prerequisites

The reason is a program error in the time conversion logic for notification header data.

The conversion routine does not correctly handle user‑to‑system time zone mapping, leading to incorrect timestamps.

Solution

Apply the attached Correction Instruction.

The correction introduces enhanced time zone handling in the conversion method, including consistent mapping of date/time fields and updated logic for system/user time zone determination.

New automated test coverage is also added to prevent regressions.

No manual post‑implementation steps are required.
