Symptom

  • Unable to login to SAP CX AI Toolkit.
  • Encountering a 403 error upon attempting to log in.

Environment

SAP CX AI Toolkit

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the login page at https://ai-assistant-usea-prod.cxai.cloud.sap. or https://ai-assistant-dewc-prod.cxai.cloud.sap
  2. Enter login credentials.
  3. Observe the 403 error message.

Cause

User tries to login to incorrect url (region)

Resolution

Use the correct region-specific URL for logging in: https://ai-assistant-dewc-prod.cxai.cloud.sap or https://ai-assistant-usea-prod.cxai.cloud.sap as per your alotted tenant details
