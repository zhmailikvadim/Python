Symptom

  • A maintenance order assigned to both a cost center and a WBS element is settled to a G/L account as defined in the configuration.
  • However, after executing the settlement, remaining costs continue to appear on the cost center and are not cleared by the system.

Environment

S/4 Hana Public Cloud

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Perform the settlement for the maintenance order.
  2. Subsequently verify the statistical postings on the cost center through the Cost Center – Actuals App.

Cause

Settlement always reads the actual costs and revenues; statistical account assignments are not taken into account when reading the data to be settled.

Resolution

Verify that the cost center assignment is statistical by reviewing it in the Cost Centers – Actuals report by adding "Cost Center is statistical".
