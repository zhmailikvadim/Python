Symptom

  • Error message: "You cannot extend supply chain collaboration to these suppliers in Ariba."
  • Missing "pack items" option in the NextGen supplier account due to lack of SCC enablement.

Environment

Supply Chain Collaboration - Infrastructure

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Enable SAP Business Network for Supply Chain Collaboration for a supplier.
  2. Sign in to SAP Business Network.
  3. Navigate to Supplier Enablement > Active Relationships.
  4. Click Advanced View.
  5. Select the supplier for whom SAP Business Network needs to be enabled for Supply Chain Collaboration.
  6. Click Actions > Enable/Disable Supply Chain Collaboration.

Resolution

  1. Enable the SCC relationship from the active relationship section.
  2. Perform the enablement through the "upload vendor" option in the Supplier Enablement section.
  3. Verify that the SCC feature is activated for the NextGen supplier.

Keywords
