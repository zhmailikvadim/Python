Symptom

  • The company/legal entity drop-down selection box is not available in the latest People Profile.
  • The issue occurs in the Payroll tab of the SF Employee Central profile.

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll
  • Pay Statement / Mashups

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in to Employee Central.
  2. Navigate to the People Profile > Payroll portlet.
  3. Observe that the company/legal entity drop-down selection box is not available in the latest People Profile.

Cause

There are some differences in features that exist in both the legacy and latest People Profile. More details can be found on guide: Notable Differences in Existing Features. For Payroll portlet, there are a change regarding employees with intercompany transfers:

  • Legacy: For employees with intercompany transfers during the same active employment, an option to choose the relevant company or legal entity was displayed.

  • Latest: For employees with inter-company transfers during the same active employment, pay statements from the current company or legal entity are displayed. Intercompany transfers in the same employment are not recommended. For more information, see Intercompany Transfer in the Same Employment with Data Change.

Resolution

Please check note 11 and provide us your feedback if it's considered that this functionality should be reviewed to keep up on Latest People Profile the company dropdown field available on legacy profile.
