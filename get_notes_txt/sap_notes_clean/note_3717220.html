Symptom

You are using external procurement with calender scheduling. No resources are maintained as Handling Resources in the location but there are calenders maintained. Additionally, Goods Receipt, Transport or Goods Issue times are maintained.

You create a Purchase Requisition or Subcontracting Purchase Requisition in PP/DS. The activities are created based on the calender availabilities. When a (Subcontracting) Purchase Requisition is changed in the Product View, you can notice, that the calender scheduling does not happen correctly.

For example you have a Goods Receipt (GR) time maintained as three days and a calender being valid from Monday morning till Friday evening. Your Strategy Setting requires to plan forward. When you create a new Purchase Requisition on a Thursday, you see that the actual GR activity is 5 days long, starting Thursday and ending Tuesday. You would expect, when you change the order date to a Monday, that it finishes on Wednesday. This is not happening, instead the duration of the activity is still 5 days.

Other Terms

GR, GI, TR, Subco, PurReq, SubPurReq, LC, OM_ACT_SCHEDULE

Reason and Prerequisites

The reason for this behavior is the functionality to reschedule activities in liveCache. Since liveCache does not know calenders, activities without resources are handled as dummy activities in liveCache. The functionality to reschedule activities in liveCache for existing orders takes the start or end date of the activity based on the scheduling direction and the number of days and calculates the new start, resp. end date.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instruction or install the corresponding support package.

Make sure, that note 3723101 is implemented and that liveCache build 101.25 is active in your system.
