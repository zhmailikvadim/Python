Symptom

  • District field is removed from the address in ISU after updating the business partner name in C4C.
  • Tax jurisdiction code becomes invalid due to the removal of the district field.

Environment

Integration of C4C with ERP

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the business partner in C4C and enter edit mode.
  2. Update the business partner name and save the changes.
  3. Check the business partner in S/4.
  4. Observe that the district field is removed and the tax jurisdiction code is invalidated.

Cause

Wrong Business System defined in table MDGV_BP_SYS_PAR of the S/4 system.

Resolution

  1. Access table MDGV_BP_SYS_PAR in the S/4 system.
  2. Update the Business System value to the correct entry as defined in tr DRFIMG -> Define Custom Settings for  Date Replication-> Define Technical Settings for Business Settings.
