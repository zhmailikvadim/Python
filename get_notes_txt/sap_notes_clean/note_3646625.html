Symptom

When you click on a contract number in Fiori, you expect the system to quickly navigate to the corresponding contract details screen. However, you may observe a performance issue where the navigation takes a long time to complete, causing delays in accessing contract information.

This note addresses the performance issue by optimizing the navigation logic, ensuring that clicking on the contract number in Fiori loads the contract details screen efficiently.
