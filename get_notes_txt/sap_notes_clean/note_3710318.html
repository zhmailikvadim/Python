Symptom

Indeed announced a strategic shift away from XML-based job feeds for all customer career site integrations and third-party delivery methods. 
They plan to discontinue “single-source feeds” entirely effective March 31, 2026 for organic (free) job postings, and by the end of 2026 for sponsored delivery.
This KBA provides information for customers still wishing to avail of organic job posting to Indeed post March 31st.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting 

Resolution

SAP SuccessFactors does not currently offer an organic multi-source feed option.

Customers who wish to maintain organic job visibility on Indeed must contact their Indeed account representative as soon as possible to begin the transition to a direct API integration. 
Because this is an Indeed-directed integration, all process documentation and enablement are managed directly by Indeed.

Please check the following blog post on the SAP Community for more information : Indeed’s Transition Away from XML Feeds: What SAP SuccessFactors Customers Need to Know
