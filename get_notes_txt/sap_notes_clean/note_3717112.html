Symptom

The customer does not appear in the dropdown menu for invoicing via the CSV upload method, despite having a trading relationship with them.

Reproducing the Issue

To perform the CSV upload for an Invoice document, please follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your SAP Business Network supplier account.
  2. In the top right corner, click on the three dots (...) and select Invoice under CSV Upload.
  3. You will be redirected to the Import CSV Invoice page. In the Customer field, click the dropdown button.
  4. Verify the presence of the customer in the dropdown list.

If the customer is not listed, then verify the current relationship status by following these steps:

  1. Sign in to your SAP Business Network supplier account.
  2. Click on your [user initials] in the top right corner, then select Settings > Customer Relationships.
  3. On the Current Relationships page, search for your customer.
  4. Verify if the relationship status is Pending or Suspended in the Relationship Status column.

Cause

  • Your supplier account itself is suspended, which has resulted in a suspension of the relationship with the customer.
  • If your supplier account is active, then the relationship with the customer has been pending or suspended.

Resolution

  • If your supplier account is suspended, you need to settle any outstanding subscription fees to reactivate and maintain an active customer relationship. 
  • For active accounts with a pending relationship status, need to accept their trading relationship request.
  • For active accounts with a suspended relationship status, review any unpaid subscription fees and the suspension reason by navigating to [user initials] > Settings > Service Subscriptions > Subscriptions tab, in you SAP Business Network supplier account and resolve them accordingly.

Please follow the appropriate action item from the scenarios above to ensure your customer is listed in the CSV upload invoice creation.
