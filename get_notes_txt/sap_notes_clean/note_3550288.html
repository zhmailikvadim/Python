Symptom

When a cost center in previous period is marked as deleted, but not in active, a deletion is sent to Fieldglass.

Other Terms

 /ASADEV/ACI_FG_COSTCENTER_DEL

Reason and Prerequisites

Deletion of cost center in previous period, but not the active.

Solution

Implement oss note to not send deletion of cost centers which are not marked as deleted in current period but in previous.
