Symptom

After deleting all confirmations of a requirement in the Review Availability Check Result UI, the chosen confirmations table is empty and an error message is displayed stating that the chosen confirmations are not available, even though they are available.

Other Terms

ATP; aATP; RACR; Review Availability Check Result

Reason and Prerequisites

This issue occurs if the following conditions are all met:

  • The requirement is checked with Alternative-Based Confirmation (ABC)
  • The respective substitution stratgey is configured to assign the remaining open quantity to the leading substitute
  • The respective substitution strategy is configured either with a sourcing profile or with storage location substitution

The reason for this problematic behavior is as follows: For validating the confimations edited in Review Availability Check Result (RACR), an availability check with the chosen confirmations is performed. This validation requires that at least one confirmaion line exists. Therefore, when deleting confirmations in Review Availability Check Result, it is evaluated if there's at least one confirmation line and, if not, a zero confirmation is created for the requested date and the requested combination of product and location. To create this zero confirmation, RACR evaluates the confirrmations proposed by the system and derives the necessary data (like the shipping dates) from there. In the situations described above, however, the system-proposed confirmations don't have a line for the requested combination of product and location and thus, the creation of the zero confirmation fails.

Solution

Apply the correction instructions for this note or upgrade to an SAP S/4HANA support package that contains this correction.
