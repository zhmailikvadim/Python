Symptom

During assignment of Flexible Material Planning Objects(FMPO) to the Force Element, the minimum and maximum quanity is not calculated correctly based on the assignment quantity.

Other Terms

Manage Flexible Material Planning Objects, Manage Force Elements, Accompanying Parts, Products, FMPO, FE, S/4HANA D&S, S/4HANA Defense & Security

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error. Additional Functionality required.

Pre-Implementation Step:
Implement SAP Note 3708790 - S/4HANA Defense and Security: Minimum Quantity and Maximum Quantity for Accompanying Parts of Flexible Material Planning Objects and Products.

Solution

The annotation has been updated for side effects corresponding to Minimum Quantity and Maximum Quantity fields for Accompanying Parts of Flexible Material Planning Objects and Products.
