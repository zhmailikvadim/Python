Symptom

How can I search and request a relationship with registered suppliers?

Resolution

On the buyer account home page, you can search and request a relationship with registered suppliers, the steps are:

  1. Log into the buyer account;
  2. Search the supplier with the supplier company name or ANID at Trading partners searching area of the home page;
  3. Click the supplier name at searching result page;
  4. Click Requests for Information > Invite;
  5. Select Fulfillment as the relationship type, tick the statement and the click Invite.
