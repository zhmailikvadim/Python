Symptom

  • The progress bar in Job Requisitions page does not receive VoiceOver focus on iOS devices. 
  • The issue is only in IOS
  • Expected behavior: The progress bar should receive VoiceOver focus and announce the progress percentage.

***Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.***

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Recruiting>>Job requisition
  2. Navigate to the progress bar under the “Progress” column.
  3. With VoiceOver enabled on iOS, verify whether the progress bar receives focus and announces the progress percentage.

Cause

Resolution

Engineering team has provided a fix for this, will be patched after 1H release 

Please provide this reference number to your CSM to get updates on this: RCM-159211
