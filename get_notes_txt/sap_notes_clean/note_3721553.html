Symptom

The transport of a document change of an FI document via Central Finance fails with error message RQ 102 - "Planning group not or incompletely maintained".

Other Terms

FQM, RQ102, RQ 102, CASH_FORECAST_CHECK_LEVEL_GRP

Reason and Prerequisites

Cash Management is not active in the company code.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instruction in your CFIN target system. After the implementation of the corrections you should be able to reprocess the document change in the AIF monitor in the CFIN target system.
