Symptom

Error message F2 381"No authorization to change accounts from account group &4" shows when attempting to modify a business partner with a vendor account group, but the system checks for authorization for a customer account group.

Environment

  • SAP Business Partners - Customer Vendor Integration in SAP ERP and SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP Supplier Relationship Management for SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. The business partner has a customer and supplier assigned, but user is only authorized for supplier. 
  2. Attempt to modify the fields for a business partner with a vendor account group.
  3. Click the "Change" button.
  4. Observe the error message: F2 381 - No authorization to change accounts from account group &4.

Cause

The business partner has both a customer and a supplier assigned, but the user's authorization is limited to the supplier.

However, during the transaction in BP, a check is performed on the authorization for the account group of all assigned objects.

This implies that authorization is required for both the customer's account group (authorization object F_KNA1_GRP) and the supplier's account group (authorization object F_LFA1_GRP) to maintain the business partner's data.

No alternative exists, as any modifications to this partner are simultaneously synchronized to both the customer and supplier. If authorization for one of the master data objects is lacking, the change cannot be executed. 

Resolution

Differentiation can be achieved with authorization objects such as F_KNA1_GEN and F_LFA1_GEN, for example. It is advisable to review the text of Note 3244709 thoroughly, as it offers extensive information on what is possible and what is not permitted in this context. 

See Also

 SAP Note 3244709 - CVI_OTH: Authorization concept transaction with respect to customer vendor integration (CVI)
