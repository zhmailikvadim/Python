Symptom

Transaction: O3UCA_UPLOAD_ROA

Error "Owner & does not exists on the venture/DOI" is occuring while uploading the .CSV file. The error is specifically occurring for a Unit Venture which is associated with the uploaded Well (Well No and Well Completion No) data.

Other Terms

Related Keywords

  • Problem-specific key words: IS-OIL-PRA-REV-CA, O3UCA_UPLOAD_ROA, Owner & does not exists on the venture/DOI, Unit Venture, Tract, PUC, OIU_SB_DOIWC, Venture/DOI Well No/Well Completion crossreference, BAPI_OIU_ROA_DETAIL, REVISED_OWNER_AVAILABILITY, OIUCA_READ_DOIMP_DOIWC_DATA, .CSV file, Mesg No:OIUCA231.

Reason and Prerequisites

The system is not able to validate the uploaded owner in the .CSV file with the corresponding owner in the Ownership records of the unit venture's tract information.

Solution

Apply the correction instructions attached in the note
