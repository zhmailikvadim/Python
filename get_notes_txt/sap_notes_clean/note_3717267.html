Symptom

In the Harmonized Planning Area (HPA), when running the optimizer with a subnetwork filter, the following error appears:

Error: Subnetwork filter and Environment Policy are not supported for Optimizer.

Environment

SAP Integrated Business Planning for Supply Chain (SCM-IBP) (Version 2602 and above releases)

Cause

This is the expected system behavior. 

Resolution

The time-series subnetworks in the Harmonized Planning Area (HPA) are currently supported only by the infinite heuristic.

This information are well documented in SAP help: Time-Series-Based Supply Planning
