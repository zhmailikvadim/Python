Symptom

  • Users are unable to locate or activate SAP Build Apps within their BTP subaccount.
  • Activation does not occur automatically; manual steps are required to enable the service.
  • Users need guidance on accessing the service in the BTP cockpit, enabling entitlements, and assigning the required service plans for initial setup.

Environment

SAP Build Apps

Resolution

Introduction

  • SAP Build ecosystem comprises a number of services designed to empower you in fostering innovation. Through a comprehensive suite of solutions, including both low-code and pro-code offerings, the platform caters to a spectrum of developers, ranging from citizen developers to professional developers and business experts. Refer to What Is SAP Build?.
  • SAP Build service is only accessible in the datacenters and regions that are specified on the SAP Discovery Center - SAP Build website. 
  • SAP Build offers two subscription models:
    • Single SKU of SAP Build
    • Individual Service Subscriptions of the SAP Build Services

SAP Build Apps for Customer:

Contact the SAP technical representative to support the activation of Service. To know your account executive follow the steps as per KBA 2626344 - Who is the SAP Account Manager/Executive for my company?.

SAP Build Apps for Partners:

Contact the SAP TDD by emailing to tdd@sap.com to support the activation of Service, as this step must currently be completed manually. 

Procedure:

  • Add the entitlement into the Global account as per the KBA 3348697 - How to add new Entitlements to BTP. 
  • The details outlined in this topic provide guidance for setting up and configuring SAP Build. Depending on which subscription user choose, the setup and onboarding steps will differ. Refer and follow the steps from Initial Setup of SAP Build.
