Symptom

- A Business Partner with the same Vendor Number and Ordering Address number,  the email address maintained in OA is not picked up.
- However, for a Business Partner with different VN and OA numbers, the email address maintained in the selected OA is picked up correctly.

Environment

  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  • Maintained a BP with the same VN and OA number.
  • Observe that the email address maintained in OA is not picked up.

Cause

The issue is caused due to the inherent design principles of SAP, wherein the determination of the email address is governed by the relationship between the Vendor Number (VN) and the Order Address Number (OA).

Resolution

Based on the standard design of SAP, the behavior of email address selection is determined by the relationship between the Business Partner's Vendor Number (VN) and Order Number (OA):

  • When the same VN and OA number are maintained for a Business Partner, the email address maintained at the vendor master level is selected. Thus, the email address maintained in Vendor Address is chosen in the case of identical VA and OA numbers.

  • When different VN and OA numbers are maintained for a Business Partner, the email address maintained at the order level is used. Accordingly, the email address maintained in selected OA is utilized in the case of different VA and OA numbers.

Keywords

Vendor Number, Order Number, Relationship, Email address, Master Data.
