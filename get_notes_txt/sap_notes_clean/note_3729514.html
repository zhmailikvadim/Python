Symptom

The CDS view I_INSURCLMRESERVEAPPBASE returns incorrect results in the field INSURCLMNETRESERVEAMOUNT when the field INSURCLMRSRVTYPE is equal to 'KK' or 'KE'.

Solution

When INSURCLMNETRESERVEAMOUNT is KK or KE & InsurClmLifeCycSts is 2 (Closed) the  INSURCLMNETRESERVEAMOUNT is displayed as 0
