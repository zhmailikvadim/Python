Symptom

To support universities to collect information on student's maternity with the newly create info type 5251. University can utilize these data in different reporting purposes to comply with the laws as well as restrict students in necessary processes. As of now, we have just designed the database with the necessary fields to describe student's maternity and implemented the validation during module/event booking. We will try to enhance the other business processes as well in the coming future.

Below information’s will be captured on maternity:

  • Occurrence no
  • Date the university has been informed about the pregnancy by the student
  • Estimated date of childbirth
  • Expected start of maternity protection period 
  • Expected end of maternity protection period 
  • Actual delivery date
  • Actual start of maternity protection period 
  • Actual end of maternity protection period 
  • Indicator breastfeeding 
  • Protection period of 1 year because of breastfeeding beginning with the date of birth
  • Indicator risk assessment document is given or not
  • Indicator notification of the relevant authority has taken place or not

Other Terms

SLCM, Student's maternity, Customer influence program

Reason and Prerequisites

Customer influence program : Improvement request no: 231800

Solution

Kinldy do not implement the note 2960342. You are requested to upgrade your system to 1909 SP03 / 2020 SP01.
