Symptom

When accessing the "Migrate Your Data" app in SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition to download the standard object templates for "Customer" or "Supplier", the template files do not include the sheet named "BP Roles". 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the "Migrate Your Data" app.
  2. Select object "Customer" or "Supplier".
  3. Download the template.
  4. Verify that the "BP Roles" sheet is not included in the template. 

Cause

The absence of the 'BP Roles' sheet in the "Migrate Your Data" app template is due to its non-existence in the SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition.

Resolution

  • The customer is created with the Customer (FLCU01) role. If company data is provided in the template, the customer is also assigned the Customer (Financial Accounting) (FLCU00) role in addition to the default role.
  • The supplier is created with the Supplier (FLVN01) role. If company data is provided in the template, the supplier is also assigned the Supplier (Financial Accounting) (FLVN00) role in addition to the default role.
