Symptom

AA662 error appears and business wants to change ANLB-PERFY field for solving it.

Environment

  • S/4 HANA 
  • S/4 HANA Finance

Reproducing the Issue

During asset posting, AA662 error is prompted.

KBA 1795678 - Error AA662 / AB720 / AB722 was followed and as per business requirement, ANLB-PERFY change is preferred in place of change at depreciation key level.

Cause

It is important to know that the first posting to an assets fills the field for the period scaling (ANLB-PERFY) depending on the used depreciation key.


If this first assigned depreciation key uses depreciation to the day functionality, the period scaling will be filled with 365, if it does not use depreciation to the day the period scaling will be 12.

Resolution

This setting can no longer be changed once the system reaches productive status (it can be adjusted only in the acquisition year, as long as that year is still open), because it affects both the depreciation calculation and the expired useful life.

Therefore, the standard SAP recommendation is to transfer the asset.

If this approach is not followed, a dedicated consulting project must be established with local SAP consultants to adjust the depreciation key via a program and subsequently correct the period scaling and expired useful life to restore consistency of the asset data.
