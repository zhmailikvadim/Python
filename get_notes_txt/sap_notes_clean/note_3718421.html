Symptom

  • Quick action card names are truncated and do not display the full name.
  • Examples include tiles such as "Manage My Team," "Announcements," "View Reminders," and "View Favorites."

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login and navigate to the latest homepage.
  2. Observe that some quick action card names are truncated and do not display the full name.

Cause

Known issue which development plan to improve in the 1H2026 release

Resolution

  1. It is confirmed that resizing these cards to display the names fully is not possible.
  2. Product Management has confirmed that many of the Quick Action text labels will be re-worded/shortened in the 2605 release. Efforts have been made to fit them into the available horizontal space.
  3. Note that not every label will display without truncation in every language.
