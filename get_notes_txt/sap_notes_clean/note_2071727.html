Symptom

You perform a partial cancellation of a goods receipt for an ERS relevant material before the goods receipt has been cleared. Afterwards, you settle the goods receipt and the cancellation via "Evaluated Receipt Settlement (ERS) Automotive". You set the parameter "New Pricing" to "No New Pricing". The quantity of goods received and the reversal quantity are accumulated correctly, but the price equals to the full price of the goods receipt. The canceled quantity is not deducted from the price.

Other Terms

MRER

Reason and Prerequisites

Because of a program error, a recalculation is attempted, although the parameter "New Pricing" is set to "No New Pricing". In addition, this recalculation leads to an incorrect result.

Solution

Please implement the corrections in this SAP Note in your system.
