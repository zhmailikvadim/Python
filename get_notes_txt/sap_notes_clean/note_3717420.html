Symptom

When settlement is proposed for mass application, application documents are sorted using PR03. This sorting is required to evaluate the consumption of the pricing lot. For vehicle scenario, since pricing lots are already freezed and sorting of application has no impact on that. Hence, skipping the application sorting for vehicle scenarios.

Other Terms

 Vehcile Settlement, Mass settlement, Performance

Reason and Prerequisites

Heavy load processing

Solution

Implement the CI to fix the issue
