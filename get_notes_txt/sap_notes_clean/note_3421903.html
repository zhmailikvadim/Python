Symptom

When contract liabilities and contract assets are posted on POB level, when revenue contracts are early terminated, you may require that the contract liabilities, contract assets and receivables adjustment are written off to different account for different POBs.

However, it is not possible to have an impairment account for impairment of assets on individual Performance Obligations. This is only possible on a Revenue Contract.

Other Terms

FARR, Optimized Contract Management, Impairment of Assets Impairment Account on perfromance Obligation

Reason and Prerequisites

You have Revenue Accounting Contracts, which already post impairment of assets on Performance Obligation.

Solution

If you would like to have an impairment account for impairment of assets on individual Performance Obligations, apply the note.
