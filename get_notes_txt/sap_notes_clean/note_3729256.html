Symptom

This KBA explains how to use infotype 0199 to maintain additional tax deduction.

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll
  • SAP S/4 HANA

Cause

Resolution

Prerequisite: Infotype 0198 subtype 01 must be maintained.

Maintain infotype 0199 based on specific business requirement:

  • Subtype 01 - CP38‑MONTHLY DED.: This subtype represents the monthly CP38 deduction and generates wage type /436.
  • Subtype 02 - CP38‑BONUS DED..: This subtype applies to CP38 deductions on bonuses and generates wage type /437.
  • Subtype 03 - TAX‑ADDITIONAL DED.: This subtype covers additional tax withholding and generates wage type /438.

The details can be checked via Function P0199 in payroll log.

Keywords

/436, /437, /438, CP38, schedular deduction tax, infotype 0198, infotype 0199, tax deduction, Function P0199
