Symptom

Component: Foundation for EHS Management
Module: Workflow Management

You activate Business Function /SHCM/EE_BP_1 and create two business partners for an employee, each with an address. When you run a workflow and the system tries to determine the e-mail address of the initiator, the system displays the error message "User ... not found".

Other Terms

/SHCM/EE_BP_1, address kinds HCM001 HCM003, BUT021_FS, roles BUP003 BUP010, Task, Tasks, BP Business Partner, CM_EHFND_WFF_BASE 006, CX_EHFND_WFF_BASE_USER

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason:
The system cannot determine the e-mail address because multiple business partners exist for the same employee, and the workflow logic does not specify which business partner address to use.

Prerequisites:
For information about the validity of the corrections, see the correction instructions.

Solution

After implementing this SAP Note, the system correctly determines the e-mail address of the workflow initiator when multiple business partners exist for an employee.

The "Support Packages" section lists the Support Packages that contain these corrections.

Alternatively, you can implement the attached correction instructions.
