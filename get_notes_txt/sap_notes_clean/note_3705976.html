Symptom

A warning message occurred while enabling  the fund configuration within SAP Revenue Growth Management.

A warning message is displayed: "The following trade spends and payment types aren't assigned to any fund types. RGM Trade spends."

  • The system behavior indicates that the selected fund type is not associated with any trade spend and payment type.

Environment

Revenue Growth Management

Reproducing the Issue

To configure funds using the in-app configuration option:

1. Choose 3 horizonal lines in the upper-left corner of the SAP Revenue Growth Management application and select Business Configurations  Fund Configuration.

2. Choose Start Setup.

3. Select the year as of which funds shall be enabled.

4. Enable the fund dimensions you want to use.

The toggle for the Customer dimension is grayed out since this dimension must be enabled.

5. If you enabled the fund type dimension in the previous step, add all the fund types you need by repeating the steps below. You must add at least one entry before you can continue to the next step.

Choose Add Fund Type.

Enter a name for the fund type.

Select a trade spend and the corresponding payment types.

Choose Add to add the fund type.

6. Choose Save and Enable Funds.

Cause

The warning popup is just trying to explain that the fund type you've selected, is not associated to any trade spend and payment type.

Resolution

On clicking "Enable Funds Anyway", funds should get enabled. 
The warning popup is just trying to explain that the fund type you've selected, is not associated to any trade spend and payment type.
