Symptom

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Invite suppliers to the production realm.
  2. Suppliers submit the external questionnaire.
  3. Observe that the registration status remains in "In Registration" instead of updating to "Pending Approval".
  4. Error message "java.lang.NullPointerException: Cannot invoke 'String.length()' because 's' is null" is recorded in the history tab of the registration project.

Cause

The error is happening while building or encoding an email, specifically when the system is trying to encode text for MIME.

In this case, below email notification templates were not migrated successfully and had no subject or content.

  • Sent to the approval task owner and any active watchers when the task is fully approved.
  • Sent to the task owner when a task has been started because its predecessors have been completed.

Resolution

Update the email notification templates in the realm by adding subject, email body content and translations. The default template content can be referenced from another environment.

    To update the email templates, follow below steps.

    1. Login as Customer Administrator.
    2. Go to Manage > Administration > Project Manager > Project Email Templates.
    3. Expand Project Type: Supplier Registration Project.
    4. Select the task and click Edit.
    5. Once the Subject, email body content and translatiosn are added, click Save.
