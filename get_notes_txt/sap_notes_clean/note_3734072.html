Symptom

Create a sales order and perform an ATP check. If material in sales order has no enough stock on request delivery date, Two schedule lines are created.

The first schedule line has no date, while the second schedule line has a date.

Environment

  • S/4 HANA
  • Delivery and transport scheduling
  • Sales and Distribution (SD) – Availability check

Cause

Missing Notes

Resolution

Implement Notes 0003582992 and 0003600218. 

See Also

Note  3418673 - Missing delivery date in sales order schedule line - SAP for Me 

Keywords

missing delivery date, schedule line date empty, sales order, VA01, two schedule lines, aATP, transport scheduling, SD scheduling
