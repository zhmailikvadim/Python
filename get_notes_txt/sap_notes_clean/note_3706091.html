Symptom

You need to remove the 'Switch Application' icon displayed in the top-right corner.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

SAP Sales and Service Cloud Version 2

Resolution

  1. Go to System Settings.
  2. Navigate to Roles view.
  3. Open the desired role.
  4. Under the App List, disable the following apps:
        "sap.crm.extensibilityservice.uiapp.buildAppsLauncher"    
         "sap.crm.extensibilityservice.uiapp.appGyverLauncher"
  5. Click on Save.
  6. Refresh the browser. The 'Switch application' icon will be hidden.
