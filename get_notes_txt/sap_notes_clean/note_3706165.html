Symptom

Partially delivered components of a Sales BOM cannot do quantity correlation. Instead, they do a normal delivery group correlation to the latest date of any confirmation.

Other Terms

ATp; aATP; Sales BOM

Reason and Prerequisites

Partially delivered components of a Sales BOM cannot do quantity correlation. Instead, they do a normal delivery group correlation to the latest date of any confirmation.
This Note introduces a possibility for an enhancement which allows to change this behaviour s.t. quantity correlation is executed even in this case.

Solution

Apply the correction instructions for this note or upgrade to a SAP S/4HANA support package that contains this correction.
