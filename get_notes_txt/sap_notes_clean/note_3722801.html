Symptom

The calculation of Lump Sum D within the Termination Organizer is not changed even if the service period for terminated employees change. Consequently, attempts to correct this by reinstating the employee, updating their service years, and then re-terminating them do not result in the Lump Sum D value being updated correctly based on the revised service period.  "Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

Prerequisite: The Lieu of Notice pay wagetype (I406) or the corresponding customized wagetype needs to be configured in V_T5QRP. This configuration involves mapping it to the appropriate redundancy model and marking it as 'Lump Sum D'. 

  1. Process the employee’s termination through to the payment report.
    For the sample employee, with an entry date of 01.07.2023 and a termination date of 15.10.2025, the 'Lieu of Notice pay' (I406) was marked as excluded. The resulting 'Term D' value is 21,739.13. 
  2. The employee was reinstated, all termination infotypes were deleted, and the hire date was corrected to 01.01.2020, which modified their service years. The termination was then re-processed for this employee.
    However, despite the change in service years and with 'Lieu of Notice pay' (I406) remaining excluded, the 'Term D' amount did not update. 

Cause

Calculated lump sum D is less than lump sum D tax free threshold .

Resolution

Lump sum D is assigned the lower value between its calculated tax-free threshold and the specified calculated lump sum D (I406).

In the example, the fixed calculated lump sum D (I406) is 21739.13.

  • For an entry date of 01.01.2023:

    • The service years are 2.
    • The calculated lump sum D tax-free threshold is LSMD1 + (LSMD2 * 2) = 13100 + (6552 * 2) = 26204.
    • Since 26204 is greater than 21739.13, Lump sum D is 21739.13.

  • For an entry date of 01.01.2020:

    • The service years are 5.
    • The calculated lump sum D tax-free threshold is LSMD1 + (LSMD2 * 5) = 13100 + (6552 * 5) = 45860.
    • Similarly, since 45860 is greater than 21739.13, Lump sum D is again 21739.13.

Therefore, in both cases, Lump sum D is 21739.13 because the calculated tax-free threshold always exceeds this amount.
