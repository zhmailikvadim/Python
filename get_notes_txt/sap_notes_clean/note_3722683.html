Symptom

The F4 help in the G/L Account field displays all G/L accounts maintained at the Chart of Accounts level in the Journal Entry Analyzer app (F0956)

Environment

  • Financial Accounting (FI)
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Fiori 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Journal Entry Analyzer app (F0956)
  2. Click the 'F4 help' in the GL account number field.
  3. Notice that all G/L accounts instead of company code-specific accounts are displayed

Cause

The Journal Entry Analyzer app has a general smart filter bar at the top. This filter bar provides all data and the design of this Fiori application doesn't allow to choose G/L Account according other sub-filters. 

Resolution

  1. Use the filter functionality in the smart table below to narrow down G/L Accounts by Chart of Accounts.
  2. Enter the required parameters and press "Go".
  3. Right-click on the G/L Account column, select 'Filter', and then choose 'Select Filter Value'.
  4. In the popup screen that appears, click on the Search Help button in the field Value of G/L Account.
  5. Use the Chart of Account filter available in this screen to refine the selection of G/L Accounts.
