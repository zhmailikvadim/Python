Symptom

In service quotation, in shipping assignment block, incoterms is always determined from sold-to-party.

Environment

 SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a service quotation in the system.
  2. Verify the incoterm determination for the quotation.
  3. Observe that the incoterm is determined based on the sold-to party instead of the ship-to party or other sources.

Resolution

The current system behavior is designed to determine incoterms based on the sold-to party because in function module CRM_SHIPPING_MERGE_MDATA_OW incoterm is determined in FORM MERGE_FROM_SOLD_TO.
