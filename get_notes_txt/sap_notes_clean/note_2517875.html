Symptom

You run the Generate Leave Quota Compensation (HKRULQC0) report to generate leave quota compensation record (generation type being By Record) for multiple employees. You click the Transfer pushbutton in the ALV list to generate records for Time Quota Compensation (IT0416). The generation result is incorrect when both of the following criteria are met:

  • At least one of the employees has more than one leave quota record which uses the same compensation method.
  • Part of the leave quota records can't be generated in infotype 0416.

For example, there are two employees, employee A and employee B.

They both have two records with the same compensation method 2000. The absence quota types in the two records are 21 and 22.

After you click "Transfer" button to generate the record in infotype 0416, it works fine for Employee A: the record with absence quota type 21 can be generated; the record with absence quota type 22 can't be generated.

The generation result for employee B is wrong: the record with absence quota type 21 can't be generated successfully. The subsequent jobs are affected.

Other Terms

HKRULQC0, PROPOSED_VALUES

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a program error. The relevant table parameter is not cleared.

Solution

Apply the HR Support Package or correction instructions in this SAP Note.
