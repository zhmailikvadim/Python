Symptom

You plan to upgrade the MaxDB liveCache on Linux. While planning the update activity in your landscape, you need to keep in mind that liveCache 7.9.11 is not compatible with SAP APO LCAPPS 7.0.

If liveCache with LCAPPS 7.0 is mistakenly updated to liveCache 7.9.11, it will result in the installation of liveCache version 7.9.11 with LCAPPS 10.0. This situation is irreversible.

Other Terms

liveCache, LCAPPS, LCA build, Release Notes

Reason and Prerequisites

Due to technical reasons, the liveCache package with SAP APO LCAPPS 7.0 is not available with liveCache 7.9.11 on Linux.

Solution

Do not update the liveCache with SAP APO LCAPPS 70 to liveCache 7.9.11 on Linux.

The update of liveCache to version 7.9.11 on Linux is only available for SAP APO LCAPPS 10.0, either in the context of a Support Package update or as part of an SCM upgrade activity.
