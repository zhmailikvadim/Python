Symptom

With the corrections from SAP Note 3684092, the system checks whether the business partner is used as an alternative dunning recipient for another customer or vendor, but the system does not check whether the business partner currently checked in the EoP check has entered a different customer/vendor as the alternative dunning recipient.

As a result, the business partner to be checked can be blocked successfully, but the alternative dunning recipient cannot. However, if the business partner is already blocked, the link of the alternative dunning recipient can no longer be removed and the business partner that is used as the alternative dunning recipient can never be blocked.

Other Terms

 Dunning program, KNB5, LFB5, EOP check, ERP_FI, CL_FIN_EOP_CHECK_CUST, CL_FIN_EOP_CHECK_VEND

Reason and Prerequisites

There is a program error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions in your system. The EOP check then checks whether an alternative dunning recipient is entered for the business partner to be checked. To be able to block the business partner to be checked, this relationship must be removed.
