Symptom

  • Freight orders unexpectedly change status from "Executed" back to "In-Execution" when carriers manually mark loads as delivered in the external freight tracking system and the transportation management system receives the UNLOAD_END post-arrival event.
  • This behavior impacts the invoicing process, as only "Executed" loads are eligible for invoicing.

Environment

  • SAP Transportation Management embedded in SAP S/4HANA;
  • Transportation Management Freight Tracking Integration With External Systems.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Manually mark the load as delivered in the external freight tracking system;
  2. Ensure the transportation management system receives the UNLOAD_END post-arrival event;
  3. Observe the freight order status reverting from "Executed" to "In-Execution".

Cause

Program error.

Resolution

Implement SAP Note 3654650 - Execution status changed back to 'In Execution'.

See Also

KBA 3002520 - Incorrect Execution status even after Last Expected Event 'DEPARTURE'.
