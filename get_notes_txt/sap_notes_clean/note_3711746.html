Symptom

The "Replication Scheduled For" column in the Data Replication Monitor (DRM) is displaying an incorrect and strange date of May 16, 3025 for certain records.

Manually reprocessing the records temporarily resolves the issue.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite

Cause

The incorrect date in the "Replication Scheduled For" field may have been manually updated via Manage Data during testing, as there is no audit data available for these entities.

Resolution

Clear the scheduledReplicationTime via Manage Data for the affected records to resolve the issue.
