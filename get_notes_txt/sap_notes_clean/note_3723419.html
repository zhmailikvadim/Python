Symptom

Excel upload in the Reassign costs and revenues (F2009) app results in endless loading after confirming the upload. The system reads the file and shows the items as ready to upload but does not complete the upload process.

Other Terms

F2009, Excel Upload, Endless Loading, Import, Upload Confirmation

Reason and Prerequisites

The upload data was accessed directly from the event object inside an asynchronous securedExecution call, which could lead to inconsistent behavior during the upload process.
