Symptom

You execute a reclassification (RCL) task on posting level 30. A consolidation unit was originally configured with an accounting technique at-equity method. You changed the method and therefore the accounting technique to purchase method.

RCL doesn't recognize the change and still filters postings below posting level 30 when reading the trigger data.

Reason and Prerequisites

The "Remove Predefined Filters" setting is off.

Solution

Implement the related support package. In urgent cases, you can implement the attached correction instruction.
