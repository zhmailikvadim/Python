Symptom

I am unable to choose "Creator Enters" for the “Hours/Days for Estimated and Committed Spend” setting.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Sign in.
  2. Click on the Admin Menu (gear icon).
  3. Click on Contingent Type.
  4. Open the desired Contingent Type.
  5. Click Edit.
  6. Under “Hours/Days for Estimated and Committed Spend,” I cannot select the "Creator Enters" option.

Cause

The "Creator Enters" option is not compatible with the "Simplified Workflow" setting enabled.

Resolution

To resolve the issue, disable "Simplified Workflow" for the relevant Contingent Type.

Keywords

Simplified Workflow, Creator Enters, Enable, Select, Choose, Cannot, can't, Hours/Days for Estimated and Committed Spend
