Symptom

  • Activation issues occur during the upgrade from 1909 to 2025 after standard content changes.
  • The error message displayed is: "Phase: ACT_UPG  Description: DDIC ACTIVATION ERRORS".
  • The issue is related to the removal of the standard table/view "IFIFYEARPERIOD".

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Upgrade the system from S/4HANA 1909 to S/4HANA 2025.
  2. During ACT_UPG, DDIC activation errors occur because the standard view/table IFIFYEARPERIOD was removed.

Cause

The standard view/table IFIFYEARPERIOD was removed in S/4HANA 2025 as part of a designed maintenance arrangement.

Resolution

Use the view IFIFYEARPERIODCC as the replacement for IFIFYEARPERIOD in upgrade and post-upgrade activities.
