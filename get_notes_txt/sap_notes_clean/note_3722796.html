Symptom

In the Data Mapping tool, Users are able to select Objects that are part of a composite/parent-child associations (for example, Legal Entity USA, Legal Entity FRA).

However, composite associations do not represent valid parent-child relationships in SR. These Objects should not be available for selection.

Environment

SmartRecruiters for SAP SuccessFactors

Resolution

Product Engineering is investigating a solution.

In the meantime, avoid selecting objects hierarch's that includes composite relationshiops.
