Symptom

When creating or modifying an FSM Activity, the update process takes significantly longer time than expected. This delay occurs during contact person determination. If a customer has a large number of contact persons, the determination and validation logic consumes excessive memory, leading to performance degradation. This results in delays in FSM data transfer, causing issues with service execution by technicians.

Other Terms

FSM, E4C, Connector, Activity, Contact Person

Reason and Prerequisites

The issue is caused by inefficient contact person determination logic in method PROCESS_E4C_ACTIVITY (class /PACG/ECM_CL_A_ASA_SVO).

Solution

Please, implement this correction instruction.
