Symptom

  • I am using the Business Unit Upload and would like to inactivate a business unit.
  • Upon trying to inactivate a business unit with the connector I am receiving the error: The Business Unit is associated to one or more modules in the company 

Resolution

If you would like to inactivate a business unit, you should use the Business Unit Upload with the modification type as I.

Do not use the modification type D for this action as the connector might fail in case the business unit have been used or associated with any data. 

Please check the section file formatting on Business Unit Upload connector documentation for further details.
