Symptom

In case a delivery group contains an ABC item and a non ABC item and both of them have the same product plant combination and the ABC item has a higher priority, then it can happen that the ABC item takes all available quantity and the non ABC item gets a zero confirmation.

Other Terms

aATP; ABC; Delivery Groups

Reason and Prerequisites

The reason is that the first item gets all quantity first and the ABC item only frees all possible quantity it could take for the next item after it got its final result, which is only available after the delivery group correlation was executed.

With this correction, the non ABC item is checked before the ABC item and hence, can take its quantity before the ABC item.

Solution

Apply the correction instructions for this note or upgrade to a SAP S/4HANA support package that contains this correction.
