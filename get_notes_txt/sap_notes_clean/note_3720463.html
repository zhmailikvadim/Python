Symptom

By Default datepicker picks only date /hrs/mins. Customer have additional requirement to add secs in the same

Environment

SAP Commerce Cloud 

Resolution

This datetime-picker is a component from https://sap.github.io/fundamental-ngx/#/core/datetime-picker.
This component does not have ‘seconds' feature that cannot be configured. 

It will be a product enhancement request to propose the addition of this feature.

We would like to redirect you to the SAP Continuous Influence Sessions for SAP Commerce Cloud on SAP Customer Influence to post or vote for your improvement request. This channel is managed by the SAP Commerce Cloud Product & Engineering team, who are continuously evaluating incoming requests, and utilising the voting system to help triage and prioritise deliverables for future releases.

How to access? 

  • Go to SAP Customer Influence home page
    • Login with your S-User and accept the Terms of Use
  • Navigate to the list of SAP Commerce Cloud Continuous Influence Sessions and register to be able to submit improvement requests.
    • Approval of registration is typically done in <1 business day
  • Follow the session to get notified of new Improvement Requests and blogs
  • Search, vote and comment on Improvement Requests posted by peer customers
  • Submit new Improvement Requests
