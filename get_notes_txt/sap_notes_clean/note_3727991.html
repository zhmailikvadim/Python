Symptom

I want to know if SAP Ariba Procurement solutions can send an informational email to the delegatee immediately after I create a delegation. For example, a notification such as: "You have been assigned as a delegate for user X."

Environment

SAP Ariba Buying and Invoicing

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a delegation in procurement solutions for a user.
  2. Observe that the delegatee does not receive an informational email immediately after the delegation is created.

Resolution

SAP Ariba Procurement solutions do not notify the delegatee when the delegation is created. It is a good idea to inform the delegatee yourself so that he or she will know to expect approval notifications on your behalf.

If you would like to request an enhancement for this feature, submit a ticket through the SAP Customer Influence (CI) program. For more information, see How do I create an improvement or enhancement request for Ariba Procurement?
