Symptom

You are using the SAP Fiori App - Advanced Scheduling Board (ASB) in Production Planning and Detailed Scheduling (PP/DS) for SAP S/4HANA OP2023 (FPS0/FPS1/FPS2) release and you notice that after performing any Order level edit action like Change Source of Supply or Change quantity, in the Resource or Operation chart, drag and drop is not working.
