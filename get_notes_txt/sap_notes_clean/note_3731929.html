Symptom

  • The statistical taxes are adding value to the total value of Nota Fiscal.

  • You flag the checkbox "Statistical Tax" (J_1BDYSTX-STATTX) in Nota Fiscal, but the taxes still being added to total of Nota Fiscal.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP ERP
  • Materials Management (MM)
  • Sales and Distribution (SD)
  • Brazil Localization

Cause

A tax configured as Statistical still composes the Nota Fiscal total, while a Statistical Item excludes the item values from the Nota Fiscal totals.

Resolution

  • When the flag "Statistical Tax" is set, the tax will be included in Nota Fiscal totals.

  • When the flag "Statistical Item" is set, the tax will not be included in Nota Fiscal totals.
