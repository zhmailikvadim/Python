Symptom

Some labels that were previously visible in the Report Center are no longer displayed. When searching for these labels in Report Center, they cannot be found. Analytics does not provide any functionality to identify who deleted, changed, or removed a label.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors People Analytics

Resolution

Analytics does not offer an audit or tracking feature for changes made to Report Center labels. There is no Analytics tool or log that captures who removed, modified, or renamed a label.

Keywords

Analytics, Report Center, Missing Labels, Label Changes, Label Deletion, Tracking Labels, Audit, Reporting Tools, Report Center Labels
