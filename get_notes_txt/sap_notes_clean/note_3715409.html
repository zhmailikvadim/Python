Symptom

An Eightfold error is displayed for Job Applications created with agency ownership even though the ownership is expired.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Cause

The ATS sends external agency ownership data where the expiration/end timestamp is earlier than the start timestamp, triggering a validation error in Eightfold and blocking the synchronization process.

Resolution

The solution of this issue is currently under investigation by Engineering team.
