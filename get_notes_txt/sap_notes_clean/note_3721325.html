Symptom

You use the program RPUQSTC2 (or RPUQSTC2) to read the cantonal withholding tax scales for the following canton of the "Swiss Tax Administration" (ESTV) for the year 2026. RPUQSTC2_ALL) in your system:

  • Canton Uri (UR)

The canton 'Uri' has different minimum tax rates depending on the church tax portion ('Y' = with church tax portion, 'N' = without church tax portion). However, since only a minimum withholding tax amount can be saved for each canton and tax table version (field name STVER) in SAP Customizing (view V_T5C2D_N), the system may process an incorrect tax amount during the calculation of the withholding tax.

Example Rate 'A*'

  • A0N: 6 Fri.
  • A0Y:8 Fr.
  • A1N: 6 Fr.
  • A1Y: 8 Fri

Other Terms

Payroll Switzerland, withholding tax, 'Single Employment' schema 'CH00' (program: RPCALCC0), 'Concurrent Employment' (CE) schema 'CH0C' (program: RPCALCC0_CE, according to SAP Notes 1849119 or 1888961), WHT calculation 2021 according to the monthly model, or Annual model, import report: RPUQSTC2 (transaction PC00_M02_UQST2)

Reason and Prerequisites

Program adjustment

Solution

Import the HR Support Package relevant for your release. If you have to implement the solution in advance, use the SAP Note Assistant to implement the correction instructions relevant for your release. You can find more information about the SAP Note Assistant on SAP Support Portal at https://support.sap.com/notes.

After you implement this SAP Note, the minimum WHT amounts are no longer imported into the table T5C2D for cantons with different minimum tax rates depending on the church tax portion. In the log of the import report RPUQSTC2, these data records are output with a warning and the different amounts (for example: 'A*Y', 'A*N', and so on).

To ensure that the correct minimum tax rate is also processed in the case of different minimum tax rates depending on the church tax portion (currently only in the canton 'Uri'), delete any remaining minimum withholding tax amount in your Customizing (view V_T5C2D_N, field QSMAM). Which scales should be adjusted here are listed in the log of the import program RPUQSTC2. For the canton of 'Uri', this currently affects the scales "A*, B*, C*, H*, L*, M*, N*, P*".

Without the minimum tax amount in Customizing (T5C2D), the system reads and processes the minimum tax amount from the tax scale table T5C2H (depending on the church tax portion or Rate). A minimum tax amount from Customizing (T5C2D) is only processed if this amount is higher than the amount calculated from the withholding tax calculation. Therefore, if the minimum tax amount in Customizing is '0' ('T5C2D'), the minimum tax amount from the tax scale table 'T5C2H' is not overridden either.

Remark:
If you have the requirement to process an individual minimum tax amount for each pay scale (for example, also depending on the church tax portion), you can do this using the BAdI HRPAYCHWHT_CORRBASE or manually by overriding the withholding tax amount in the tax infotype 0038.
