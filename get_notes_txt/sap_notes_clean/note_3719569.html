Symptom

• Determinations (Pre-Hook or Post-Hook) do not update fields when a related object is modified.  
• Updates occur only after manually saving the business object.  

Environment

  • SAP Sales Cloud and SAP Service Cloud Version 2
  • SAP Sales Cloud Version 2
  • SAP Service Cloud Version 2

Reproducing the Issue

Prerequisites:

1. Navigate to Menu and select Settings.  
2. Go to Extensibility and choose Sales Quote.  
3. Create a custom field on a business object (e.g., Sales Quote).  
4. Configure a reference field (if cross-object logic is used, e.g., Account field referenced in Sales Quote).  
5. Create and activate a Determination (Pre-Hook or Post-Hook) for the business object.  
6. Configure determination logic to update a field based on specific conditions.  
7. Ensure required business objects (e.g., Account and Sales Quote) are available with test data.  

Steps to Reproduce:

1. Navigate to Sales Quote (or any configured business object).  
2. Verify that a Pre-Hook or Post-Hook determination is configured and active.  
3. Open a related object (e.g., Account) referenced in the determination logic.  
4. Modify the value of the field used in the determination logic (e.g., Customer field "XYZ").  
5. Return to the Sales Quote without saving it.  
6. Refresh the UI or reload the Sales Quote.  
7. Confirm that the dependent field on the Sales Quote is not updated.  
8. Make a minor change in the Sales Quote (e.g., modify description or add/remove a character).  
9. Click Save.  
10. Verify that the determination executes and updates the field correctly.  

Cause

The determinations (including Pre-Hook and Post-Hook logic) are executed only during the Save lifecycle event of the business object.

They are not triggered by:

  • UI refresh
  • Navigation between objects
  • Changes made directly on related objects (e.g., Account affecting Sales Quote)
  • Background display reload

This is standard system behavior.

Resolution

To trigger the determination logic:

  1. Open the affected business object (e.g., Sales Quote).
  2. Make any change (even a minor/dummy change).
  3. Click Save.

Upon Save, the determination logic will execute and update the relevant fields according to the defined conditions.
