Symptom

The registration questionnaire is not visible in the supplier user's dashboard even though the questionnaire has been sent successfully.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Act as the supplier user.
  2. Open the supplier dashboard and attempt to access the registration questionnaire.
  3. Observe that the registration questionnaire is not visible, and therefore cannot be edited.

Cause

The supplier user is locked in the registration project’s external questionnaire, which hides the questionnaire from the supplier dashboard and prevents editing.

Resolution

  1. Open the Advanced View of the supplier registration project.
  2. Go to the Documents tab.
  3. Open the external questionnaire and navigate to the Participants tab.
  4. Select the supplier user and click Lock/Unlock to remove the lock.
  5. Act as the supplier user and verify the registration questionnaire is visible and editable in the dashboard.
