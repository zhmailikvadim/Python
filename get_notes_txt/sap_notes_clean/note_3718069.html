Symptom

The daily working hours field (ARBST) and other fields are showing unexpected values following the replication of the work schedule to IT0007

Environment

SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Replicate employee from EC to ECP using report RP_HRSFEC_PTP_EE_REPLICATION
  2. Open PA20 and check IT0007

The daily working hours field (ARBST) and other fields are showing unexpected values.

Cause

The Daily Working hours field (ARBST) is calculated by the standard weekly hours divided by Employee Central working days per week. For example, if the value for the standard hours field coming from EC is 40 and the working days per week is 5, ARBST is calculated to be 8.

Resolution

To resolve this, ensure ARBST is calculated correctly by sending the correct hours and working days per week from EC. 

Keywords

ARBST, Daily working hours, work schedule, IT0007, replication
