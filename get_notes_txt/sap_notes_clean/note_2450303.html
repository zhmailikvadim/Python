Environment

  • SAP Adaptive Server Enterprise (ASE) 16.0 for Business Suite
  • SAP Business Warehouse - All versions 

Cause

Transaction log of the <SID> database is full.

Resolution

  • You need to increase the segment log of your <SID> database. Please refer to SAP KBA 1931223 - Error SQL1105 "Can't allocate space for object..." - SAP ASE for Business Suite
  • If the issue persist after a consequent increase of the segment log, it might be possible that table is not partitioned and it is very big. You might need to consider partitioning of E table. Please read this SAP Note 2187579 - SYB: Pros and cons of physical partitioning of fact tables , item "Partitioning of E fact tables".
