Symptom

LOAD NULLFILE is executed two times to truncate a partition instead of only once.

Other Terms

RSDU_TABLE_DROP_PARTITION_DB2
LOAD NULLFILE

Reason and Prerequisites

In special circumstances, function RSDU_TABLE_TRUNC_PARTITION_DB2 is called two times instead of only once.
Then the utility for LOAD NULLFILE for cleaning up the partition is also executed two times.

Solution

The correction solves the issue.
