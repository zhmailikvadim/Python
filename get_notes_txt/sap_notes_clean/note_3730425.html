Symptom

When posting invoices using transaction codes J1B*N, the following fields remain blank on the "NF-e Data" tab:

  • J_1BDYDOC-IND_FINAL – Final Consumer
  • J_1BDYDOC-IND_PRES – Consumer Presence Indicator
  • J_1BDYDOC-ID_DEST – Destination of the Operation

This issue occurs for fiscal models:

  • Model 66 – Electronic Nota Fiscal for Energy
  • Model 62 – Telecommunication Service

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA

Cause

This behavior is by design. These fields are only applicable to fiscal model 55 in the standard solution.

Resolution

Current Behavior: By standard, the fields "J_1BDYDOC-IND_FINAL," "J_1BDYDOC-IND_PRES," and "J_1BDYDOC-ID_DEST" are not automatically populated for fiscal models 66 and 62 (NF-e).

Workaround: Review include LJ1BB2F29 to create a custom enhancement that populates these fields for models 66 and 62.
