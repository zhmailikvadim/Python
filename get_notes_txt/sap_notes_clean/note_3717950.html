Symptom

A new field HWAER (Local Currency) has been added to the structure:CMM_VLOGP_H_KOMK.This enhancement ensures that the Local Currency is properly populated and transferred to:CMM_VLOGP

Other Terms

HWAER , Local currency , CMM_VLOGP , CMM_VLOGP_H_KOMK, CMM_VLOGP_KOMK

Reason and Prerequisites

  1. From S4CORE 2025, Local Currency (HWAER) is required for correct pricing and valuation processing.

Therefore, explicit inclusion of HWAER in CMM_VLOGP_H_KOMK is required to ensure proper data flow.

Pre-Requisite

  • System must be on S4CORE 2025 or higher.

  • Ensure transport of:

    • Structure enhancement

    • Related dependent objects (if any)

  • Validate no custom logic overwrites or clears HWAER during pricing processing.

  • Activation of the following structures is mandatory:

    • CMM_VLOGP_H_KOMK

    • CMM_VLOGP

Solution

Implement the attached corrections . Also activate the structure CMM_VLOGP_H_KOMK and CMM_VLOGP if required separately .
