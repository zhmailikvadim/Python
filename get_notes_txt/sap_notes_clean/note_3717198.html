Symptom

In the event of a U.S. federal government shutdown, certain government-operated services — including E-Verify — may be temporarily unavailable. As a result, integrations between SuccessFactors Onboarding and E-Verify may be impacted during the shutdown period, potentially affecting case processing and status updates.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Resolution

For the latest updates on service restoration, please refer to the blog below. We will continue to share timely information there as progress is made.
