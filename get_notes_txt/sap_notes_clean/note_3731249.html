Symptom

 This note implement additional selection field OBO = on behalf of for the SAP Standard transaction CMM_FX_HRQ_SWAP.

The field is used for the selection of offsetting groups, where it is compared with field 'On Behalf of Company Code' in 'Transaction' header table  (VTBFHA-BEHALF_OF_COMPANY)

The selection field is not obligatory.

Other Terms

 Commodity Risk Management; program 'Create Offsetting FX Hedge Requests for Commodity Swaps'; RCMM_OFFSETTING_FX_HREQ

Reason and Prerequisites

 New requirement

Solution

 Implement the attached Correction Instruction or the related Support Package.
