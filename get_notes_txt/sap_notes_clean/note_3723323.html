Symptom

Error when activate the Fiori application by executing task list SAP_FIORI_CONTENT_ACTIVATION in  STC01:

  • Error messages include:

Service Group: /SAP/UI_CABUSLOCK_DISP 0000 not found
Service Group: /SAP/UI_CADOCUMENT_MNG 0000 not found
Service Group: /SAP/UI_CADUNRUNEXCPTN_DISP 0000 not found
Service Group: /SAP/UI_CAPAYMENTBYLINK 0000 not found
Service Group: /SAP/UI_CAPAYMENTORDER_MAN 0000 not found
Service Group: /SAP/UI_DIRECTDEBITMANDT_MANAGE_V4 not found
Service Group: /SAP/UI_PROVIDERCONTRACT_MANAGE 000 not found

..........

*Above are just examples, the issue could happen on other service group 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA 2025
SAP S/4HANA 2023
SAP S/4HANA 2022
SAP S/4HANA 2021
SAP S/4HANA 2020
SAP S/4HANA 1909
SAP S/4HANA 1809

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login to the system.
  2. Execute STC01 -> SAP_FIORI_CONTENT_ACTIVATION.

Cause

Resolution

Update task lists by implementing the following SAP Notes:

  • SAP Note: 2686456 - Content Activation for SAP Business Roles -  for SAP_FIORI_CONTENT_ACTIVATION.
  • SAP Note: 2813396 - Content Activation for Business Roles -  for SAP_FIORI_FCM_CONTENT_ACTIVATION (if used).

See Also

  • SAP Note: 2686456 - Content Activation for SAP Business Roles
  • SAP Note: 2813396 - Content Activation for Business Roles
