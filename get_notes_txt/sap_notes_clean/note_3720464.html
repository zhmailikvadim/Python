Symptom

While replying to ticket interactions, the "From" email field remains blank. sometimes the previous inbound e-mail is created by PDI.

Environment

SAP Cloud for Customer

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Service Work Center .
  2. Go to Tickets Work Center view.
  3. Navigate to the Interactions tab.
  4. Verify if an inbound email interaction has been created.
  5. Click on "Reply" for the email interaction.
  6. Observe that the "From" field is blank.

Cause

When a ticket is created from a non-email channel, the system determines the sender address based on the organization (business area) data. If an email address is maintained/present in the associated organization, the From field is populated accordingly. If no email address is maintained/present at the organization level, the From field remains empty. (The organization is determined by service and Support team or Seller under involved parties tab)   

Resolution

  1. Go to Orgnizational management Work Center.
  2. Go to Org structures Work Center View.
  3. Go to corresponding organization.
  4. Maintain e-mail address under General tab.
