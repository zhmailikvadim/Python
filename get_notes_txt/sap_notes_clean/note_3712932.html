Symptom

When posting a Supplier Credit Memo, it is noticed that the FI document does not show the same sign for the amount and quantity:

Environment

  • Materials Management (MM)
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA

Cause

The quantity is not considered as a balance field that must align with other lines in the FI document.
It is there for information purposes.

Resolution

This is the standard system's design.

See Also

  • Refer to: 2123259 - Quantity in subsequent FI accounting document of SD billing

Keywords

credit memo, quantity value, FI document, accounting entry, incorrect quantity, logistics invoice verification, MIRO
