Symptom

In the Company segment of the G/L Account the Open Item Mgmt by Ledger Group flag is input disabled for the Company.

Environment

  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 2021
  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 2022
  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 2023
  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 2025

Cause

The affected company is not assigned to any ledger in the backend.

Resolution

In the backend transaction code FINSC_LEDGER the company has to be assigned to at least one ledger to enable the maintenance of the Open Item Mgmt by Ledger Group flag.

Keywords

FINSC_LD_CMP, SAPLSFIN_LEDGER_CUST, SKB1, XLGCLR, SKB1-XLGCLR
