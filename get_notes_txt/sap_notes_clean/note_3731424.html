Symptom

Field "Vendor: Subcontractor Type" does not show in screen when display business partner under vender role.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Start transaction BP and display a business partner in role FLVN00 (FI Vendor).
  2. Open Company Code data, switch to Change mode, and navigate to Account management.
  3. Observe that field "Vendor: Subcontractor Type does not display.

Cause

The Japan subcontracting solution is not activated; the SPRO path “Subcontracting with Chargeable Components” is missing, indicating business function /SAPNEA/J_SCC_BF is inactive.

Resolution

Run T-code SFW5 and activate business function /SAPNEA/J_SCC_BF under “Enterprise_business_functions”.
