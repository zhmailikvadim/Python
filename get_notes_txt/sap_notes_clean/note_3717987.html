Symptom

You declare your exports from SAP GTS to the German customs system AES 3.0.

The adjustments described in ATLAS participant information 0890/25 have not yet been implemented for the incompleteness check of the export declaration (E_EXP_DAT). These are checks for the identification number of the exporter and the declarant, as well as a check for the indication of the country of origin.

Other Terms

ATLAS, export, participant information, exporter, declarant, origin

Reason and Prerequisites

Legal change

Solution

Implement the correction instructions.
