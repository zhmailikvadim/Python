Symptom

After scoping the configuration items 16T and/or 4AN the pre-delivered materials required for Professional Services intercompany scenarios are not deployed.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Import all transport requests containing the initial scope and organizational structure (CBC) into the PRD tenant.
  2. Access the app "Manage Product Master Data" (F1602) in production.
  3. Verify that best practice scope-related data (e.g., T001, T002) is missing.

Cause

As of 2508 and the switch to new project billing for intercompany billing the pre-delivered materials are no longer necessary as standard.

Resolution

Expected Behavior.

If the materials are still a business requirement then they can be created manually. The schematics are available in the Test Script at SAP Signavio Process Navigator.
