Symptom

For two purchase orders with the same prices, the value of the consumption of the subcontracting component in a reversal is different. 
The behavior is explained by the “GR-Based IV” Indicator EKPO-WEBRE.

Environment

Materials Management (MM)
SAP ERP Central Component
SAP ERP
SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
SAP S/4 Hana

Reproducing the Issue

For the two cases below:

 

1,The component is managed with standard price control.

2,In the first partial reception the standard price was 25 EUR/KG. 5 KG were issued at 125 EUR.

3.In the second partial reception the standard price was 20 EUR/KG. 5 KG were issued at 100 EUR.

 

The cancellation of the second reception is posted and the values are determined in a different way:

 

Purchase Order without “GR-Based IV”. PO History with SC Consumption view: The value of the cancellation is 112,50 EUR.

 

 

Purchase Order with GR-Based IV. PO History with SC Consumption view: The value of the cancellation is 100,00 EUR.

 

Resolution

In cancellations or reversals of goods receipts, the value of the consumption of the subcontracting component is determined according to the average values posted so far.

 CASE-1

When the Indicator for “GR-Based IV” is not set in the purchase order item the system does not differentiate the individual receptions and all postings are managed together in terms of valuation:

In total for the component we have received 10 KG with a value of 125 + 100 = 225 EUR and the cancellation is valuated as per the average: 5 KG x 225 EUR / 10 KG = 112,50 EUR.

 

 CASE-2

When the Indicator for “GR-Based IV” is set in the purchase order item the system differentiates the individual receptions each new reception with reference to the purchase order builds a new valuation group. Only postings within the specific GR/IR assignment group are considered. This can be seen in ME23N, in the purchase order history, changing the view to GR/IR assignment:

 

 

 

Here we can see two different GR/IR assignment groups: The one for the first partial reception and the one for the second partial reception.

The cancellation has specifically cancelled the second partial reception.

The valuation is made as per the average values within this group only. We have received 5 KG with a value of 100 EUR and the cancellation is valuated as per the average: 5 KG x 100 EUR / 5 KG = 100 EUR.
