Symptom

  • While navigating the process receivables by business partner app, the screen displays process receivables for all business partners instead of limiting to one business partner.
  • The target mapping configuration refers to the transaction UDM_SUPERVISOR, leading to incorrect details being displayed.

Environment

  • Fiori-UI for Collections and Dispute Management
  • SAP S/4 HANA 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Manage Business Partner Master Data app F3163.
  2. Click on the BP.
  3. Observe that the screen displays data for all business partners instead of limiting to one.

Cause

The target mapping configuration incorrectly refers to other transaction i.e. UDM_SUPERVISOR instead of the appropriate Fiori app for process receivables.

Resolution

  1. Review the target mapping configuration in the system.
  2. Refer to the Fiori apps library for the process receivables.
  3. Update the target mapping configuration to point to the correct Fiori app for process receivables instead of the transaction UDM_SUPERVISOR.
  4. Verify the catalog definition delivered with the app in the Fiori apps library to ensure proper configuration.
  5. Test the updated configuration to confirm that the process receivables screen now limits to one business partner as expected.
