Symptom

During the generation of conditions using the migration module "Migration of Building Savings Contract - Conditions" (/IBS/BSS_MIG_BSC_COND), the same alternative payer is transferred for each building savings contract for all conditions generated from the payment agreements of the respective building savings contract for construction savings contracts with payment agreements that exist for different alternative payers.

Reason and Prerequisites

The service implementation "Migration of Building Savings Contract - Conditions" (/IBS/CL_BSS_MIG_SI_BSC_COND, method MAP_STD) does not take the constellation described above into account.

Solution

The method mentioned above is corrected.
