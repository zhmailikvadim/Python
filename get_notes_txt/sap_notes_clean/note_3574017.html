Symptom

  • An "Invalid combination of sel.screen. parameters or inactive BF for Test-PY" error is encountered during the "Create Test Payroll" step.
  • The error is observed when re-running monitoring simulations.

Environment

  • SuccessFactors
  • Payroll Control Center (PCC)
  • Human Capital Management 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to PCC.
  2. Run the process.
  3. Navigate to SM37.
  4. Enter Job Name as *Simulation* and click on Execute.
  5. Go into the spool of the job.
  6. Error occurs as "Invalid combination of sel.screen. parameters or inactive BF for Test-PY".

Cause

  • Test Payroll Step is ticked for "Test Run".
    OR
  • Selection screen of the payroll driver is filled with value.

Resolution

  1. Simulation payroll step in PCC should not tick "Test run" check box of payroll driver.
  2. The selection screen section of payroll driver screen should be left empty( no value should be given in input).

Keywords
