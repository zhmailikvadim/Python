Symptom

We enabled Guided Buying "PARAM_ENABLE_APPROVERS_AMOUNT_IN_LOCAL_CURRENCY" , but clicking on the amount does not show anything. We expect to see pop-up with text "Amount in your currency".

Resolution

Please make sure also the following parameter is enabled:

  • Application.Common.SupportedClassToIncludeTotalAmountsInApproverCurrency

As documented in How to Display the Total Cost in the Approver's Currency
