Symptom

Standard approach is needed for assigning custom Adobe forms(PDF) for Handling Unit (HU) and Warehouse Orders (WO) in EWM printing.  

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management

Cause

Consulting

Resolution

Implement BAdIs based on custom Adobe form requirements for HU / WO.

  • /SCWM/EX_PRNT_PDF_HU - Data Selection for HU-Based PDF Forms
  • /SCWM/EX_PRNT_PDF_WO - Data Selection for Warehouse Order-Based PDF Forms

Customer can use the above BAdIs to determine the data for customer-specific PDF forms and transfer it to the specified interface parameters.

Keywords

/SCWM/ES_PRNT_PDF, /SCWM/IF_EX_PRNT_PDF_WO, /SCWM/IF_EX_PRNT_PDF_HU, /SCWM/PRINTING
