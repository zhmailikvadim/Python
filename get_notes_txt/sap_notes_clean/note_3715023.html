Symptom

When a sales order with a discount is copied to an A/R invoice and the posting date is changed, the Total (LC) field in document row does not recalculate. Instead, it retains the value from the original sales order. 

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental. 

Environment

SAP Business One

Reproducing the Issue

Prerequisites:

The decimal places setup for 'Percent' is set to 4 under Administration → System Initialization → General Settings → Display. 

Scenario:

  1. Go to Sales - A/R → Sales Order.
  2. Enter the following details in the sales order:
    • Posting Date: 01.01.2026 (a date prior to current system date)
    • Quantity: 1
    • Unit Price: 1000009.00
    • Discount %: 1.0000
    • Tax Code: Exempt (with zero VAT rate)
  3. Modify the Total (LC) field for the item row from the calculated value of 990008.91 to 990009.00 before adding the sales order.
  4. After updating the Total (LC) field, verify that the Discount % field remains unchanged.
  5. Add the sales order.
  6. Create the A/R invoice from the Sales Order by using the Copy To option. The A/R invoice reflects the current system date, and the Total (LC) field for the item row is 990009.00.
  7. Before adding the document, ensure that the posting date in the A/R invoice is set to a date prior to the current system date, and verify the Total (LC) field value.

Expected behavior:

The Total (LC) amount in the A/R invoice is calculated as 990008.91 when the posting date is modified before the invoice is added.

Actual behavior:

The Total (LC) amount on the A/R invoice remains the same as the amount recorded in the sales order.

Resolution

From SAP Business One Version 10.0 FP2405 onwards, any changes to the posting date do not affect the calculation of the Total (LC) field in the document row, which remains as recorded in the sales order.
