Symptom

Why no suppliers displayed when  the user enters the prompt "Show me the list of suppliers in "In Registration" status for more than 100 days"

Resolution

This is because under Manage > SM Administration > Config fields  > Last status Change date is not checked.  As a result it is not able to fetch In Registration suppliers or Invited suppliers.
