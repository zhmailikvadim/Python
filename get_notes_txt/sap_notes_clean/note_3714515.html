Symptom

During the upgrade, SAP Notes are displayed in the SPAU, but no option to adjust them.

Environment

SAP NetWeaver ABAP based system

Reproducing the Issue

Access SPAU during the upgrade.

Cause

The current SUM phase does not reach to the SPAU adjustment phase. It is too early to adjust SAP Notes in SPAU.

Resolution

Please continue with the SUM. The SUM will stop at SPAUINFO phase. Then you can logon to the system to adjust the SAP Notes in SPAU.
