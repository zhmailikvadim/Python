Symptom

This KBA explains how to handle the rehiring scenario in Payroll Korea, where the standard Rehire action is not supported.
 

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll
  • SAP S/4HANA

Cause

Due to localization restrictions, an employee cannot be rehired using the previous personnel number (PERNR). Therefore, when rehiring an employee, it is essential to assign them a new personnel number.

Resolution

To handle the rehiring procedure correctly, please refer to the steps below:

  1. Reiring the employee

    • Access transaction PA40/SF EC.
    • Enter the employee's details and process them as a new hire.
    • Assign a new personnel number to the rehired employee as part of this process.

  2. Link Old Personnel Number with IT0031

    • Use Infotype 0031 (Reference Personnel Numbers) to maintain a connection between the old and new personnel numbers.
    • This ensures a continuity of historical data and allows you to track the employee's previous records.

  3. Maintain Initial Hire Date with IT0041

    • Utilize Infotype 0041 (Date Specifications) to record the initial hire date of the employee.
    • This helps preserve the employee's service history and any associated benefits linked to their original hire date.
