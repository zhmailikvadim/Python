Symptom

In a sales document, a main item is marked complete even though the subitems are still open. It would be expected that the main item cannot be set to complete while its subitems remain open.

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • Sales and Distribution (SD)
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a sales order with a main item and subitems.
  2. Deliver the main item only.
  3. The main item is marked complete while the subitems remain open.

Note: The scenario may vary depending on the item category and billing relevance. Some main items or subitems are not relevant for billing, so their status may be blank.

Cause

The item status is determined for each item individually; the system does not consider subitems when determining the status of the main item.

Resolution

This is standard system logic. To prevent a main item from being completed while subitems are still open, assign a delivery group on the Shipping tab for the related items. With a delivery group set, the main item should not be delivered without its subitems.

Keywords

Main item, complete, subitem status, sub-item status, delivery group.
