Symptom

You declare your exports from SAP GTS to the German customs system AES 3.0.

For a subsequent export declaration, you must specify the exit date, that is, if you specify the customs status EX-nA, EX-nB, EX-nK, or EX-nN (see SAP Notes 1761877 and 3355905). However, the field for the starting date is not ready for input.

Other Terms

ATLAS, AES, subsequent export declaration, date of exit

Reason and Prerequisites

Legal change

Solution

Implement the correction instructions.

To make the document field for the subsequent export declaration changeable, you must also implement SAP Note 3716490.
