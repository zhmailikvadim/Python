Symptom

When you run reports HHKCPFE0 or HHKCDTC0, the system performs authorization checks against all infotype validity periods.

If an infotype period does not overlap the selected BEGDA/ENDDA range, performing an authorization check on that period is unnecessary and may result in misleading authorization failures.

This SAP note skips the unnecessary authorization check to avoid misleading authorization failures.

Other Terms

PCDTIHK0, DBPNPCOM, $rinfo$

Reason and Prerequisites
