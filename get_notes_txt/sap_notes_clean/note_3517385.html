Symptom

How do I reset Multifactor Authentication (MFA)? 

Resolution

 If Multifactor Authentication (MFA) needs reset for a sub-user on the account, the administrator can follow the steps below:

  1. Log in to your SAP Business Network account.
  2. In the upper-right corner of the application, click [User Initials] > Settings > Manage Users.
  3. Click Manage User Authentication.
  4. Select the users (from the table) or whom you want to reset MFA.
  5. Click Reset.

If the Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) needs to be reset for the administrator of the account, please contact Support to have the MFA reset.
