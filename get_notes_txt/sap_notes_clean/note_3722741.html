Symptom

I mistakenly submitted a SOW for Supplier Review and need to retrieve it back to my company’s side.

Resolution

Coordinators with the appropriate permissions can use the Recall button to retrieve the SOW.

  1. Sign into your SAP Fieldglass Account
  2. Navigate to Services > Statement of Work
  3. Search for the SOW ID that is in Pending Supplier Review status and open it
  4. Click Actions > Recall Statement of Work
  5. This will change the SOW status to Pending Buyer Review
