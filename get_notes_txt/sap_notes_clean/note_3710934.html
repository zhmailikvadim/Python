Symptom

Poland Electronic Invoice (KSeF - B2B) doesn't have specific tags on XML for special conditions related to Caffeine, Sugar and Taurine tax.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP ERP

Cause

Not supported scenario.

Resolution

Currently the tags related to Caffeine, Sugar and Taurine tax scenario are not delivered by standard SAP Poland Electronic Invoice (KSeF - B2B).

In order to achieve this scenario, it's necessary to use BADI "EDOC_ADAPTOR" and create a custom code.
