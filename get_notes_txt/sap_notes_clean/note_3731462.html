Symptom

When exporting the "Manage Fixed Asset - New Version" report to Excel, certain added fields, such as "investment project WBS element," are missing from the exported file.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the app "Manage Fixed Asset – New Version".
  2. In Settings, add the field "Investment Project WBS Element" (and any other desired fields) to the displayed columns.
  3. Export the list to Excel and observe that the newly added columns are missing in the generated file.

Cause

This is a known error.

Resolution

The issue will be corrected in hotfix 2602.8。
