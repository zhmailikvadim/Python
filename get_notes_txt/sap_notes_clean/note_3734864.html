Symptom

You create LE deliveries out of Transportation Management. The TM documents have been created based on corresponding ERP order documents. You use stage building on TM side in a way such that either there is no proper stage between start and incoterm location (no stage or just a statistical one) or there is no proper stage for the transport part between incoterm location and destination.

As a result, you face erroneous/unexpected data after delivery creation: For example, freight units are deleted and created anew.

Other Terms

Transportation Management; Logistics Integration; Delivery Proposal; Delivery Creation; /SCMTMS/CL_DLV_PROP_BUILDER_OL; FILL_INPUT_IL( ); Advanced Stage Building; Statistical Stages;

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error. Technically, the reason for the symptom is that the location at the first/last non-statistical freight unit stop has been considered as split criterion during delivery proposal building.

Solution

Implement the corrections of the attached correction instructions or apply the corresponding support package.
