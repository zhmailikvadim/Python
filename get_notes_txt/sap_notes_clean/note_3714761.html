Symptom

No Persona is found on SSAM mobile client and/or Profile option "Switch Persona" shows "None".

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental. 

Reproducing the Issue

1. User onboards SSAM App and performs Initial Synchronization
2. Open the Profile Settings on the Side Menu
3. Try to check or switch the assigned Persona

Cause

Can be caused by missing ConfigPanel settings or missing Offline Configuration e.g. missing upload of the ".ini" file on Mobile Services.

Note: you may see a similar behavior when trying the demo from the App Store, whenever trying to switch Personas and you see that the option is locked / disabled:
 

Please notice that this is an intended design choice due to constraints and limitations of the App iteration in demonstration mode.

Resolution

First, make sure that the Personas are properly configured: Configuring Personas

In case multiple Personas are assigned to the same user, also check: Switching Between Personas

Then, go to your Mobile Services subaccount and confirm that the Offline ".ini" settings file was uploaded to your SSAM Application instance. In case you require guidance on this setup activity, you can check the Section Troubleshoot 2 - Missing Offline Configuration of the following SAP Tutorial: Create an SAP Mobile Services App for the SAP Service and Asset Manager Mobile App
