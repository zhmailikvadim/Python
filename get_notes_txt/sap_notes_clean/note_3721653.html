Symptom

  • Before CE2602 upgrade, it's possible to display all historical inspection lots (for example: inspection lot with ICCO and UD status) in Record Inspection Results app (F1685A). 
  • After CE2602 upgrade, these historical inspection lots are not visible in Record Inspection Results app.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

The Record Inspection Results app works as design in CE2602.

The previous behavior was resulting from a bug which was corrected with 2602 release. A side effect of the bug was that the historical data had a negative impact on the app performance.

Actually, the Record Inspection Results app was not intended to display historical results recording data. Instead, it's designed to record inspection results only for inspection lots which do not have a usage decision or have a usage decision but with open long-term characteristics. 

Resolution

  • To review historical data, use Manage Inspection Lots app.
  • Another alternative way is to use Display Inspection Lots - Worklist (QA33) app. It's possible to navigate to inspection results of an inspection lot.
