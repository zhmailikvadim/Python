Symptom

You identify, that the status is not correctly set for employees with time management status values different than 1 and for employees not taking part in time evaluation in the middle of the period.

Other Terms

PUST/PEST,  HRPY_PROCESS_SET_PERNR_STATUS, HR Process Workbench, Time Evaluation

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a program error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the relevant support package.
