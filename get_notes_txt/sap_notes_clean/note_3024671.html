Symptom

The Personalization of the Items assignment block is configured with "Number of Visible Rows Before Scrolling" > 0 and "Number of Rows Before Paging" = blank. After selecting one or more products with the F4 search help of the Product ID field, the Items assignment block displays blank rows. The user must scroll up to see their selections.

Other Terms

CRM, CRM Sales, CRM Service, Business Transaction, Product, Products, Item, Items, Product Master Data, Product Search, F4, F4 Help, single select, multiselect, Customer Connection, CustConn, Improvement, Request, Improvement Request, Influence, Customer Influence.

Reason and Prerequisites

Implementation of note 2677141 - Enabling multiselect option for Product Search F4 Help popup in CRM Sales and Service or support pack containing changes from note.

Solution

Apply attached correction instructions.
