Symptom

When accessing a Contract Account within the SAP Service Cloud Version 2 environment, you notice that the Payment Plan tile and section within the Payment Program tab is not displayed for certain contract accounts, while it is displayed for others.

Environment

SAP Service Cloud Version 2, Utilities Add-On.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Logon to your SAP Service Cloud Version 2 environment.
  2. Navigate to Contract Account work list.
  3. Open any record.
  4. See in the Central tab, the Payment Plan tile is not displayed.
  5. Navigate to Payment Programs tab.
  6. See the section for the Payment Plan is also not displayed here.

Cause

In the S4 / IS-U, the BB Procedure for the Contract Account is maintained as 00.

Resolution

In order to display the Payment Plan related tiles and sections within the SAP Service Cloud V2, the contract account BB Procedure should be maintained as 03 in the S4 / IS-U side.
