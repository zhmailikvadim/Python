Symptom

  • When customizing view /US4G/V_DISCORDS and setting response codes for corresponding entries, a warning is displayed indicating that the entries are SAP entries and may be overwritten in future updates.
  • The warning is "Do not make any changes (SAP entry)".
  • The response status for these entries are empty.

Environment

  • SAP Market Communication for Utilities
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open customizing view /US4G/V_DISCORDS.
  2. Set response codes for entries.
  3. Save the changes and observe the warning that these are SAP entries and may be overwritten in future updates.

Cause

  • Response statuses for codes 0–29 are not delivered yet.
  • There are currently no plans to deliver them, however in the future these might be delivered.

Resolution

  1. Customize the response statuses for entries (codes 0–29) in view /US4G/V_DISCORDS as required.
  2. Ignore the warning stating that these are SAP entries and may be overwritten in future updates.
  3. Note that there are currently no plans to deliver response statuses for these entries.
