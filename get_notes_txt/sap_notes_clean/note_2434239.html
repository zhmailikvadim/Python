Symptom

  • When Publishing a migrated Web Intelligence report and going to the prompts tab, an Error occurs: "An internal error occurred while calling 'answerPromptsEx' API. (Error: ERR_WIS_30270)".
  • The error occurs when going to the prompts tab.
  • Error: "This error occurred: Failed. Please check the logs or contact your SAP Business Objects Administrator" when doing the same steps in BI LaunchPad.
  • The error doesn't occur when scheduling the same report.

Other Terms

Webi, scheduling, webintelligence, CMC

Reason and Prerequisites

Environment:

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 4.0
  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 4.1
  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 4.2
  • Web Intelligence

Steps To Reproduce:

  1. Logon to Central Management Console or BI LaunchPad
  2. Create a publication based on a HANA Web Intelligence report
  3. Go to Prompts tab
    1. >> One of the above errors is raised

Cause:

  • This issue is caused by a defect in the product

Solution

This issue is fixed in the patches listed in the "Support Packages & Patches" section below.
The "Support Packages & Patches" section will be populated with the relevant patch levels once they are released.
For Business Intelligence Platform maintenance schedule and strategy see the Knowledge Base Article 2144559 in References section.
