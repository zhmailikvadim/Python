Symptom

  • Employees are receiving an error when applying absences in hours;
  • The error message states: "You can't request 07:20 hours because it exceeds your current leave balance of X hours.";
  • Absences for a full day (7.20 hours as per Work Schedule) were successfully applied earlier but started encountering issues recently.

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central
  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Proxy as the user;
  2. Attempt to create an absence for a day with 7:20 hours in the work schedule;
  3. Observe the error message: "You can't request 07:20 hours because it exceeds your current leave balance of X hours."

Cause

The issue is caused by the uneven representation of 7h20m as 7.333333h in the Work Schedule and employee time calculations. This discrepancy is limited to specific configurations of the Work Schedule.

Resolution

  • This is a known issue and our engineering team are targeting to fix this issue on 1H 2026 release, please note that this is a tentative so track this KBA for updates.
  • As a temporary workaround, create absences by splitting them into two separate entries:
    • Absence 1: 7 hours;
    • Absence 2: 20 minutes.
