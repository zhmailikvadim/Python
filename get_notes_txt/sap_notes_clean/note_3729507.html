Symptom

  • I would like to know if I can enable Rate Guidance for sites outside of the United States (U.S.)
  • I would like to llows the creator or approver of job postings, work orders, and work order revisions to view the rates used by my company for a specific position

Environment

Production and Non-Production

Resolution

Yes, it is possible to use the Rate Guidance functionality for sites outside the U.S..

In this scenario the Rate Guidance information displays for the country/region to which the site belongs. The system aggregates data for all sites in the same country/region for the intended job posting.

Marked Types and Cost of Living Indexes (COLI) are not considered
