Symptom

When I send the Purchase Order (PO) from the Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system to SAP Business Network, it fails in the SAP Integration Suite, the managed gateway for spend management, and SAP Business Network, with the error:

BusinessNetwork response is: "Bad Request" and ErrorCode: "400" and ErrorResponse: "No valid From credential was supplied [ANCLS-XXXXXX]. Document failed in Business Network.ErrorCode:CIG-PLT-00644.

Cause

The SystemID field in the From credentials segment in the PO cXML payload is missing or contains incorrect details, in violation of PO standards. 

Resolution

The SystemID field in the From segment is generated by the MultiERP functionality from the SAP Business Network. If the functionality is enabled, do the following:

1- Remove custom mappings done for this field in the SAP Integration Suite, the managed gateway for spend management and SAP Business Network Mapping Tool.

2- Disable and enable the SAP Integration Suite, the managed gateway for spend management and SAP Business Network, in the SAP Business Network. This will ensure that the MultiERP information resyncs.
