Symptom

Error:Cannot change the status of the existing payment proposal from Paid to Schedule. Please contact support with the Error Reference Number: ANERR-XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX for more details 

Resolution

  • Once a Payment Proposal Request (PPR) is marked as "Paid," the SAP Ariba Network treats it as a final state. This is the expected behavior in the system, and the status cannot be changed to "Scheduled."
