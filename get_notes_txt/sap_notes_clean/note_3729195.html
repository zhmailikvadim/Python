Symptom

This note delivers the solution for the following legal change:

The Royal Decree introduces a new income tax exemption for individuals aimed at promoting the use of solar energy and improving energy efficiency in Thailand. It is a part of the country's strategy to achieve energy sustainability and meet its carbon neutrality targets.

Income Tax Exemption for Solar Power System Installation (Individuals)

  • Individual taxpayers may claim an income tax exemption for the actual costs of purchasing and installing a solar power generation system, up to a maximum of 200,000 Baht per system.
  • To be eligible, expenses must be incurred from March 3, 2026, to December 31, 2028.
  • The system must be installed on a building and connected to the grid of the Metropolitan Electricity Authority (MEA) or the Provincial Electricity Authority (PEA).

Other Terms

THTAX

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a legal change.

Solution

The solution includes the following changes in the system:

1. A new model wage type MA09 has been created to maintain the exemption for solar power system installation in IT0015 from 03.03.2026 to 31.12.2028.

2. A new technical wage type /48J has been created to generate the corresponding deduction in the payroll

3. A new constant SOPIN has been created in V_T511P to define the maximum exemption amount.

The corresponding change for PIT91 will be delivered after the PIT91 format for tax year 2026 is published by the end of the year.

Apply the HR support package to get this legal change.

Alternatively, you can first follow the steps in the attached manual instruction then apply the correction instruction.
