Symptom

You launch the IMG structure for "SAP S/4HANA for Group Reporting", IMG activity for "Define Consolidation Chart of Accounts", beneath node for "Master Data". Then the system casts a popup which offers three options:

  • Create cons chart of accounts
  • Change cons chart of accounts
  • Display cons chart of accounts

When you choose the option "Create" or "Change", the system casts another popup with message "Function not supported in SAP S/4HANA Consolidation" (message G01 028).

Other Terms

G01028, Define Consolidation Chart of Accounts, Create cons chart of accounts, Change cons chart of accounts

Solution

Import of Support Package or advanced correction by implementation of correction instruction
