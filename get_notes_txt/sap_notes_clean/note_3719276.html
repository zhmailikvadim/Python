Symptom

  • Workflow participants are not receiving email notifications when it is their turn to approve or update.
  • Email notifications are triggered only after the completion of the entire workflow.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors HCM

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Initiate an MDF Workflow. 
  2. Notice that Workflow participants are not receiving email notifications when their approval step has been triggered. 
  3. Email notifications are only received after the completion of the entire workflow. 

Cause

The "Current Approver" email notification is disabled for this workflow email in "Manage Workflow Email Configuration".

Resolution

  1. Navigate to "Manage Workflow Email Configuration".
  2. Search and locate the workflow email configuration.
  3. Update the "Current Approver" setting under "Workflow Approved" to "ON".
  4. Save the changes.
  5. Test the workflow again to ensure that email notifications are triggered for the relevant approver at each approval step, once the preceding approval is completed.
