Symptom

  • The ship-to party is only available in the partners section of the Manage Credit Memo Request V2 app, and all partner functions default to the sold-to party.
  • The app does not same with other sales document apps, where the ship-to party is included in the general header section, like Manage Sales Orders - Version 2 app.

Environment

  • Sales And Distribution (SD)
  • Fiori
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Manage Credit Memo Request V2 app.
  2. Create a credit memo request
  3. Observe that the ship-to party is only available in the partners section.
  4. Attempt to locate the ship-to party in the general header section; it is not available.

Cause

  • This is a standard design.
  • The Manage Credit Memo Request V2 app is designed to prioritize billing processes over delivery processes for credit memo and debit memo requests. 
  • As a result, the ship-to party is considered less relevant and is not included in the general header section.

Resolution

    Use the "Create Sales Orders - VA01" app, which is the WEBGUI version of VA01, and includes the ship-to party in the general header section.
