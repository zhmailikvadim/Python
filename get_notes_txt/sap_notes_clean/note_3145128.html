Symptom

  • Certain credit exposure liability categories are maintained as 'Statistical credit limit exposure'.
  • In UKM_BP transaction you cannot change between the views to include only 'Normal' or also include 'Statistical' credit exposures, because the button is missing.
  • You do not see a liability category in the credit exposure details, possibly it is set as 'Statistical'.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

  • Sales And Distribution (SD)
  • Logistics Execution (LE)
  • SAP Credit Management (FSCM)
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

1.) Set credit exposure liability category as 'Statistical'
You can maintain the liability categories under the following IMG path:
SPRO -->
Financial Supply Chain Management
    Credit Management
        Credit Risk Monitoring
            Credit Exposure Update
                Define Liability Categories

2.) Check the credit exposure in UKM_BP transaction -> Credit Segment data -> Credit Exposure (i) -> Details

Resolution

The credit exposure liability categories are only distinguished between 'Statistical' or 'Normal' if the "Relevance for Calculation of Credit Limit Utilization" setting is maintained on any of the liability categories.
When that is maintained under the "Define Liability Categories" node, then in the UKM_BP transaction there is an additional button "Change Visibility of Limit Relevance" to switch between the modes to display Statistical/Normal credit exposure.

Check the configuration for the liability category and switch the view according to which credit exposure you wish to check in UKM_BP transaction.
