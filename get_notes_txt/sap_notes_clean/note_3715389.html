Symptom

Thumbnail images are with a blank background in Buying & Invoicing (B&I) and Guided Buying (GB) even though the URL image has a white background. 

Environment

SAP Ariba Catalog Management

Cause

The subscriptions doesn't have a image maintained in the Thumbnail Image field.

Because this field is empty, Ariba automatically converts the image from the Image field to generate the thumbnail version.

When Ariba performs this conversion, the resulting thumbnail displays with a black background. 

Resolution

To resolve the issue is necessary to add the same image file used in the “Image” field to the Thumbnail Image field and import a new version of the subscription. 

Keywords

Image, Thumbnail Image, Background, Ariba
