Symptom

When you execute an escheatment (transaction /IBS/BSC_CHG_CESS_N), the system issues the error message "Minimum charge of [...] is not reached for charge type [...]", even though the charge is to be waived for the relevant charge type according to the settings in the IMG activity "Define Assignment Types" (/IBS/CBSC_CESS).

Reason and Prerequisites

In the service for generating charges, the non-relevant charges are also validated.

Solution

The implementation /IBS/CL_BSC_SI_GEN_FEES of the above Services (method /IBS/IF_BSC_SD_GEN_FEES~CREATE_FEES_CONDITIONS) is corrected.
