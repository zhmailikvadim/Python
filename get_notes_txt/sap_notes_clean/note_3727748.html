Symptom

When only the quantity of a planned order or a manufacturing order is changed in PP, the corresponding PP/DS order is adjusted proportionally.

Other Terms

planned order, manufacturing order, proportional adjustment

Reason and Prerequisites

The described symptom is standard behavior.

Solution

Sometimes, it might be important to switch off the proportional adjustment by default. If you wish to do so, you can use Method ORDER_INB_PROCESSING_DECIDE of BAdI /SAPAPO/CL_EX_CIF_IP to clear variable cv_proportional_change_allowed. In this case the source of supply will be exploded.

Please be aware that the implementation of the mentioned BAdI method is within customer responsibility. Unwanted results or sideeffects caused by customer own implementations, are not covered by SAP Standard Support. Same for questions on how specific additional requirements can be covered in such an implementation.
