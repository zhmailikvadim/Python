Symptom

  • After setting the BI Launchpad preferences and the browser language to a language other than English, the logon page is still always displayed in English. 

Other Terms

BI Launchapd, Logon page, English, non-english,

Reason and Prerequisites

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObject Business Intelligence Platform 4.3 Support Pack 04 Patch 1500+ , BI 4.3 SP5 Patch 3+ and BO 2025 Patch 6+.
  • All Operating System

Steps to reproduce

  1. Set the default browser to a language other than English .
  2. Login to BI Launchpad
  3. Go to locale settings and set: 

    Preferred Viewing Locale: Use browser locale
    Product Locale: Use browser locale

  4. Logoff and open Login page of BI Launchpad
  5. Observed: Logon page shows English Language

Solution

This issue is fixed in the patches listed in the "Support Packages & Patches" section below.

The "Support Packages & Patches" section will be populated with the relevant patch levels once they are released.

For Business Intelligence Platform maintenance schedule and strategy see the Knowledge Base Article 2144559 in References section.
