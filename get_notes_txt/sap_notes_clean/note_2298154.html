Symptom

There are two ways to open dimensions of infoProvider - link of ID of infoProvider and "View All Dimensions" button at "Auditable" column.

Other Terms

View all dimensions, edit model

Reason and Prerequisites

It is caused by UX design match between planning consolidation model. "View All Dimensions" button has been removed to prevent confusion.

Solution

For SAP Business Planning and Consolidation 10.1, version for SAP NetWeaver on 740, upgrade to BW740(Component SAP_BW) Support Package 16.

For SAP Business Planning and Consolidation 10.1, version for SAP NetWeaver on 750, upgrade to BW750(Component SAP_BW) Support Package 04.
