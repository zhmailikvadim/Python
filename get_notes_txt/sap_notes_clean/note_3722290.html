Symptom

Even when the "bank statement needed" flag is not set and the recipients are not maintained for an IHB account, the Business partner assigned is being marked as an Original recipient automatically. This is incorrect behaviour.

Bank statements as notifications are sent when the program is run, if the recipient data exists for the IHB accounts, and those bank statements are visible in the Manage In-house bank account balances app under Bank statements section.

Other Terms

Manage In house bank accounts app, F5942, IHB bank statements - recipients

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a program error.

Solution

Please apply SAP Note or assigned Support Package.
