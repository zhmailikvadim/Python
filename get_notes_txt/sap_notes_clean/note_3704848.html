Symptom

  • In process 8080 with BMID MDCMALO304, segment SG4+STS ZW3/ZW4 is filled unnecessarily for the message.
  • The customizing for the BMID variant appears to be incorrect, and the data provision supply_direction is not required.

Environment

SAP ERP 6.0

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Execute process 8080 with BMID MDCMALO304.
  2. Observe that segment SG4+STS ZW3/ZW4 is filled for the message.

Cause

  • The standard customizing was not compared with the customer customization.
  • This discrepancy led to the incorrect data provision supply_direction being applied. 

Resolution

  1. Add the supply direction for UBD.
  2. Verify the customization settings to ensure they align with the requirements for BMID MDCMALO304.
  3. Test the process to confirm that segment SG4+STS ZW3/ZW4 is no longer filled unnecessarily for the message.
