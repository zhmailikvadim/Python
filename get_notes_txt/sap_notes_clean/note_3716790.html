Symptom

  • Attempting to update future prices of a material in additional currencies (records found in CKMLPR Table) using BAPI_MATERIAL_SAVEDATA and/or BAPI_MATERIAL_SAVEREPLICA..
  • Uncertainty regarding the entries required to achieve this or whether another class/function module is needed.
  • Currently, only the MBEW table is being populated, while other currencies remain blank.

Environment

  • Controlling (CO)
  • Financial Accounting (FI)
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance

Cause

The BAPI_MATERIAL_SAVEDATA was designed by the material master without material ledger and contains only information about the local currency in legal valuation.

Resolution

For changing future prices in all material ledger currencies and valuations, the transaction CKMPRPN can be used. When material ledger is active, the future prices are stored in the table CKMLPR.
