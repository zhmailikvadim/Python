Symptom

  • EMS shows as procured at the global account level.
  • EMS is inactive in the Services section of the BTP cockpit.
  • Cannot create the required PERSON entity/resource in the subaccount.

Environment

SAP Entitlement Management.

Cause

Entitlements were added to the global account but were not assigned to a subaccount. Services must be assigned to a subaccount before they become available there.

Resolution

  1. Sign in to the SAP BTP cockpit and open the global account.
  2. Go to Entitlements → Configure Entitlements.
  3. Add the Entitlement Management Solution plan to the target subaccount.
  4. Switch to the subaccount, open Services (or Service Marketplace) and verify the EMS is active.
  5. Once the entitlement is assigned and the service is active in the subaccount, create the PERSON entity/resource as required.
