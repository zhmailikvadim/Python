Symptom

The logical transport objects of BPC do not have zero downtime option enabled

Other Terms

BPC, ZDO, UJT_AFTER_IMPORT, AIM

Reason and Prerequisites

The logical transport objects of BPC have been up-ported from 730 to SAP_BW 740 and SAP_BW 750

Solution

For SAP Business Planning and Consolidation 10.1, version for SAP NetWeaver BW740,  upgrade to Support Package 09(Component CPMBPC 810)  and upgrade to BW740(Component SAP_BW)  Support Package 14.

For SAP Business Planning and Consolidation 10.1, version for SAP NetWeaver BW750,  upgrade to BW750(Component SAP_BW)   Support Package 02.
