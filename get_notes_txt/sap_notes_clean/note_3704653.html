Symptom

  • Create a New Job in Promotion Management and observe the delay/slowness.

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence (BI) Platform 4.x and 2025
  • Any supported OS

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Split the APS without adding APS VMS service.
  2. Then go to the promotion management
  3. Create a new job
  4. Observe the delay in creating the job 

Cause

  • This issue occurs when the Version Management Service is not present on any running / enabled APS, this service is required for Promotion Management add / remove activities.
  • It is also observed that VMS services includes few PM related service endpoints.

Resolution

  • To configure a split APS in BI, please follow KBA 1694041 - How do you configure the Adaptive Processing Server (APS) for improved performance and scalability? [Video]

  • Ensure the Version Management Service exists on a running and enabled APS
