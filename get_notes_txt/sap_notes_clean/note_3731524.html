Symptom

  • In ECC systems, the MSLVT (balance carryforward quantity) in FAGLFLEXT contained values; after migration to S/4HANA, MSLVT is not populated in FAGLFLEXT (FAGLFLEXT view) and no equivalent cumulative quantity exists in ACDOCA.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Start SE16N.
  2. Open table FAGLFLEXT (FAGLFLEXT view on ACDOCA).
  3. Observe that field MSLVT (balance carryforward quantity) is blank or zero in period 00 for specific base units, whereas periodic quantities exist in ACDOCA.

Cause

  • By design in S/4HANA migration, only G/L‑relevant balances (balance reporting figures) are migrated during the DLT step; quantity carryforwards (MSL/MSLVT) are not G/L‑relevant and are therefore not migrated as cumulative balances.
  • In S/4HANA, FAGLFLEXT is a view on ACDOCA and cumulative balance carryforward quantities are not persisted.

Resolution

Acknowledge expected behavior: cumulative balance carryforward quantity (MSL/MSLVT) is not migrated and not stored as a cumulative figure in FAGLFLEXT or ACDOCA after S/4HANA migration.

During migration of balances (DLT step: MIG_SOURCE = G, BSTAT = C ), we only migrate the G/L relevant balances or Balance reporting figures in all the currencies. MSLVT is not relevant field for G/L balances hence we do not migrate these values.
In S/4 HANA, Balance carried forward of quantity will not be available in FAGLFLEXT(view on top of ACDOCA) and ACDOCA as a cumulative quantity, but you can check in FAGLFLEXT and ACDOCA per period wise. It will give the results per period wise.

There should not be any issue in SAP standard reports/transaction due to these missing value.
