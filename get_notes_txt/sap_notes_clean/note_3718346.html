Symptom

Running the transaction CMM_FX_HRQ_SWAP or CMM_FX_HRE_FWD_AVG for a keydate (e.g. 31.12.yyyy) with Commodity Prices being fixated until 3 days before keydate:

  • Prices Before 3 days before keydate : fixated  (price > 0.00 USD)     (e.g. 29.12.yyyy)
  • Price At next day of running date: unfixed                                       (e.g. 30.12.yyyy)
  • Price At 2nd day after running date: unfixed                                    (e.g. 31.12.yyyy)

The transactions CMM_FX_HRQ_SWAP and CMM_FX_HRE_FWD_AVG allow a pre-processing for up to three days. Doing so, the price-readback follows two defined rules:

  1. Price Determination is only performed for the last fixation-date of a commodity swap/forward, which is in this case keydate only. This results by the design of the transaction to minimize the deviation from fixated prices.
  2. The Price Determination reads-back the price from the previous fixing date - regardless of whether it was fixed or not, which is in this case at next day of runnung date (0.00 USD).

As a consequence, both the fixation dates (e.g. 31.12.yyyy) are considered with an amount of 0.00USD.

The transaction only allows running three days into the future for the use case when there is a holiday after a weekend so that you can run the report on the Friday in advance.

The transactions have to read-back the price for the date before keydate, too. (e.g. 30.12.yyyy), where the prices are not fixed yet.
