Symptom

During EWM inbound processing you observe generated technical serial numbers starting with $.

Other Terms

/SCWM/PRDI, /SCWM/INB_DLV_SAVEREPLICA, ITEM_SERIAL_NO, HANDLING_UNIT_SERNO

Reason and Prerequisites

consulting

Solution

Serial numbers starting with $ can be generated during EWM Inbound Delivery processing in two cases:

One is that provisional serial numbers are enabled for the warehouse, which makes it possible (for products with 'B' or 'C' serial number requirement in EWM) to perform the goods receipt without providing "real" serial numbers. In this case the system generates these temporary placeholders as technical serial numbers, which should be replaced with real serial numbers till the end of inbound processing (final putaway). Of course the immediate update of the GR to ERP with serial numbers is not possible in this case.

The second case is when you have an EWM Inbound Delivery document with a 'C' serialized product in EWM, and this product is packed into one or multiple HU-s. In case the real serial numbers are not provided yet, or they are provided, but in a way that does not make it possible to automatically decide which serial number belongs to which handling unit item, the EWM standard generates these technical serial numbers starting with $ and assigns them to the handling unit. In this case you may end up in a situation where you have "real" serial numbers on the EWM PDI item, without HU assignment, and there are also system generated temporary serial numbers starting with $, assigned to the same PDI item, but assigned to handling unit as well. (These temporary serial numbers are usually not displayed on standard UI-s therefore you can only observe them in the /SCWM/SERI table.)

This second case is the more frequently occurring one, e.g. when an Inbound Delivery from ERP is replicated to EWM with serial numbers, however the serial numbers are sent on delivery item level instead of handling unit level, and EWM is unable to assign the serial numbers to handling unit. If the whole quantity of the delivery item is packed into a single HU, then there is only one possible combination to assign the serial numbers to HU, therefore the EWM standard automatically assigns the serial numbers to HU only in this specific case. If the delivery item is packed into multiple HU-s (or it is packed into a single HU, but there are multiple HU items for the same delivery item, because of different IDPLATE/Stock ID), the EWM system is unable to find out the correct distribution of serial number to handling unit items. Therefore it is recommended to communicate the serial numbers to EWM on handling unit level, in this case the ERP system already sends the necessary information to decide which serial number belongs to which handling unit.
