Symptom

  • A Freight Order is created in SAP Transportation Management, and relevant Business Partners are maintained in the Business Partner tab.
  • However, when checking table /SCMTMS/D_TORPTY (Transportation Order Party), some or none of the maintained partners are found.
  • Why are certain Business Partners not available in table /SCMTMS/D_TORPTY?

Environment

  • SAP Transportation Management 9.1 - 9.6
  • SAP Transportation Management embedded in S/4HANA 1709 and subsequent releases

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a Delivery
  2. A Freight Unit is automatically generated
  3. Plan the Freight Unit into a Freight Order
  4. In the Freight Order, assign a partner in the Business Partner tab (e.g., Carrier)
  5. Access the backend system
  6. Check table /SCMTMS/D_TORPTY in transaction SE16N for the created Freight Order
  7. The assigned partner (e.g., Carrier) is not present in the table

Cause

  • This is standard system behavior. Business Partners are stored in different tables depending on their partner function in the Freight Order.
  • The table /SCMTMS/D_TORPTY contains only specific partner functions, such as Sold-to Party, Additional Agreement Parties, Goods Buyer, Broker Party and etc...
  • Other partner functions are stored in table /SCMTMS/D_TORROT, such as Carrier, Shipper, Ship-to Party, Communication Party, Execution Carrier and Sender Party.

Resolution

To check and/or retrieve Business Partner data, use the appropriate table (/SCMTMS/D_TORPTY or /SCMTMS/D_TORROT) based on the partner function assigned to the Business Partner in the Freight Order.
