Symptom

Customer disappears from device on delta sync.

Other Terms

BP, Business Partner, /MAC/RSFM_BP_DETAIL, REx, REx 3.0, REx 3.1, Retail Execution, SAP Retail Execution, /MAC/RSFM_BP_DETAIL.

Reason and Prerequisites

Relationships in CRM are downloaded both for IS and HAS. Example, A is a contact person of B. This also states that B has a contact person A. Now when downloading the Customers list from backend to device, the Business Partner A will be downloaded both as a customer and as a contact person. The delete adjacent duplicates will delete the Business Partner, which has to be shown as a customer on the device, thus causing the error.

Solution

Implement this note via transaction SNOTE.
