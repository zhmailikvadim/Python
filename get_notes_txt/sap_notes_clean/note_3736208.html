Symptom

After clicking "View Approval Details", the item and approval details view briefly loads, then turns into a blank screen.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Learning

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Learning home page >
  2. Find an item that has a pending approval request >
  3. Click "View Approval Details" >
  4. Observe that the item and approval details view briefly loads, then turns into a blank screen.

Resolution

Our Learning Engineering team has finalized their work on the situation, and the fix is scheduled to be delivered with 2605 patch 1. Additional details are available in the following links: Product Release & Road Map Information | SAP SuccessFactors - Patch Details. 

Workaround: Click the item title or another area of the tile, but the "View Approval Details" button, then select "View Approval Details" to see the approval information.
