Symptom

The sort order of maintenance plans in the statusboard is not optimal with regard to the unit of the remaining value, the remaining value and remaining days. This leads to confusion.

Other Terms

LMSTB1, MPLA, MPOS, /ISDFPS/ST_LM_TERMINATION_DATA

/ISDFPS/LM_CALC_MPOS_REMAINDER

/ISDFPS/CL_LM_MAST_EQUI, INIT_MAINT_POS

/ISDFPS/CL_LM_TEC_OBJ, INIT_MAINT_POS

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason:

Confusing sort order

Prerequisites:

-/-

Solution

Correction via this SAP Note or the corresponding support package.

Sort order of the maintenance plans in statusboard before this correction: unit of the remaining value, the remaining value and remaining days.

Sort order of the maintenance plans in statusboard after this correction: remaining days.
