Symptom

In the Manage Force Elements application, change documents created for assignments during copy to force element were incomplete and therefore the copied FMPO assignments were not in status 'Planned'.

Additionally, the number of objects to be created—displayed in the title of the “Rename the Copied Objects” dialog during the Copy to Force Element in the Background operation—was being truncated.

Other Terms

S/4HANA Defense and Security, Manage Force Elements, assignments

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the attached correction instruction.
