Symptom

Replication of employee's infotypes 0008, 0014 or 0015 getting updated to an unexpected value or to '0', although an specific amount has been maintained in EC for the impacted Pay Component/Wage Type

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central integration to SAP Business Suite 
  • Employee Master data replication 
  • Business Integration Builder (BIB) 
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Attempt to replicate employee data using ECPAO_EE_ORG_QUERY.
  2. Observe that the infotype records of PA are not created or incorrect filled.

Cause

  • Some EC fields are filtered out or adjusted during replication due to conversion rules configured in the system via V_ECPAO_VAL_CONV.

Resolution

Ensure that you have correctly mapped the Compensation infotypes according to the Guides:

  • For IT0008 and 0014: Mapping Infotype 0008 and 0014 from Recurring Payments | SAP Help Portal
  • For IT0015: Replicating BenefitsIntegrationRecurringInfo and BenefitsIntegrationOneTimeInfo Entity Values to IT0014 and IT0015 | SAP Help Portal

Double check if any of the EC field IDs of the respective infotype are being filtered out or adjusted by a Conversion Rule in the customizing:

  • Transaction SPRO: SAP Reference IMG > Integration with SuccessFactors Employee Central > Business Integration Builder > Employee Data Integration > Define Field Mapping for Employee Data
  • Or access directly via transaction SM34 cluster table view VC_ECPAO_MAP
    • Select the used Transformation Template Group
    • Click on "Transformation Template"
    • Select the corresponding Transformation Template
    • Click on "Primary Mapping"
    • Check the EC Fields, select one of them and click on "Generic Value Conversion".

Here you will see all the configured Conversion rules applied on this EC Field and you may validate if they are being filtered out or adjusted.

Options of conversion rule that might be impacting the result are:

  • Check and Remove
  • Check and Replace
