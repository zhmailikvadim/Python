Symptom

You are selecting a date in a calendar control such as the date navigator.

The date is formatted and parsed which results in a different date than previously selected.

Other Terms

Time zone, DST, Locale, Calendar

Reason and Prerequisites

Different APIs may be in place for date formatting, parsing and displaying in calendar controls.

If those APIs use different time zone offsets, the handling may be inconsistent.

Also different calendar controls may be initialized with different locales.

Solution

With this fix time zone and locale settings are synchronized.
