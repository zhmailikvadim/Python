Symptom

In applications that are based on the Floorplan Manager (FPM) and use the GUIBB Form@GL2.0 or the GUIBB Form Repeater, an incorrect context menu might be displayed in rare cases.

Other Terms

Floorplan Manager, GUIBB, Form@GL2.0, Form, FPM_FORM_UIBB_GL2, Form Repeater, FPM_FORM_REPEATER_UIBB

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the attached corrections or import the relevant Support Package.
