Symptom

Privacy by design and default is based on the purposes (describing the reasons) for processing of personal data.

Project Management simplifies respectively supports the determination and assignment of purposes to person-related data by the enablement for the Purpose Determination.

The Purpose Determination is the central instance between the purpose configuration and the applications to asynchronously determine and update the purpose assignment for business documents.

You can find further details in the SAP Help Portal (https://help.sap.com) by searching for Purpose Determination.

Other Terms

Data Protection & Privacy, DPP, Data Controller, Purpose Project, Ukraine

Reason and Prerequisites

Purpose Determination

Solution

You have activated the automatic determination of purposes for application objects in the IMG activity 'Configure Purpose Determination'.
