Symptom

SAP Ariba Invoicing does not provide a UI option or system setting to designate temporary or permanent delegates for approvers.



Environment

SAP Ariba Invoicing 

Resolution

Currently, SAP Ariba Invoicing does not support a delegation feature. We expect to introduce this capability after the 2604 release; however, a confirm date has not yet been set.

Keywords

CIM, Delegation, Central Invoice Management, Authority, Delegate
