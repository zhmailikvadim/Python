Symptom

When you create an inbound delivery that is relevant for decentral warehouse (but not for EWM), normal additional items (no packing items) that refer to another storage location that is not relevant for a different warehouse (LIPS-LGNUM is empty) lead to the fact that the warehouse is deleted from the delivery header (LIKP-LGNUM) is initial, so that the delivery ccannot be distributed to the decentral warehouse.

Other Terms

LIKP-LGNUM, TVLK-LNSPL, LIPS-LGNUM, T340-DECSY

Reason and Prerequisites

The correction of note 1662589 shall only be valid for EWM relevant deliveries, not for deliveries with decentral warehouse. Note 1689867 has solved this issue only for packing items.

Solution

Apply the attached correction.
