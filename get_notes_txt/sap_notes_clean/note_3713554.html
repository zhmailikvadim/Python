Symptom

 An error is issued when accessing a CDI view of Sustainability Integration that indicates issues with the delta mechanism of CDI.

Other Terms

CDI, ODP, SDF, Sustainability Integration, RDP, SAP Responsible Design and Production, SFM, SAP Sustainability Footprint Management

Reason and Prerequisites

CDI delta functionality is invalidated when underlying CDS views are changed.

Solution

To counter such issues early in the process, automated CDI delta ABAP unit tests for providers within Sustainability Data Integration are introduced.

This note doesn’t fix the underlying issues and it doesn’t provide a code correction for them. Its purpose is to detect such issues and to provide a foundation for further development.
