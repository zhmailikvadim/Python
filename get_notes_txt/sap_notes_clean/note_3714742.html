Symptom

When running the MSSFT service with a flat BoD, meaning only one entry location without any sub-locations, the service throws an error. This can lead to unnecessary confusion.

Other Terms

MSSFT, MEIO, Forecast Profile, eSPP

Reason and Prerequisites

The service tries to load and buffer a forecast profile (which isn't used in the following process). As soon as the BoD is flat, it's not possible to load the profile and the error is thrown.
