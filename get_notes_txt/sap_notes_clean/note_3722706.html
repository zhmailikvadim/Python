Symptom

When my Order Confirmation / Ship Notice / Service Sheet / Invoice are sent to SAP Business Network, it fails with the following error message: TO org is already deleted.

Cause

The Buyer's SAP Business Network account is either Deleted or Expired, so documents cannot be sent to it.

Resolution

You need to contact your client and inform them that their account is deactivated. Until it is reactivated, it will not be possible to send documents to them.

Keywords

to org is deleted, org is deleted, org is expired, reactivate the account, reactivate, account deleted, buyer org deleted.
