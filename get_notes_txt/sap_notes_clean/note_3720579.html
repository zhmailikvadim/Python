Symptom

You try to split a serialized shop floor order. After entering the split quantity the system raises error message COOS 102 'Split quantity greater than quantity confirmed in previous operation(s)'.

Other Terms

COOS102, Order Split, Shop Floor Items, SFI

Reason and Prerequisites

For serialized shop floor orders the split operations are determined by the processing status of the shop floor items selected for the split. Therefore the available quantity at the selected split quantity should not be checked.

For release S/4HANA 2025 this problem was fixed with SAP Note 3649851.

Solution

Apply correction instruction.
