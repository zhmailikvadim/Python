Symptom

When performing a status change in a Change Record (Change Notice), the process terminates with a runtime error.

Typical error message:

  • ASSERT condition was violated
  • Component: PLM-CR
  • Service ID: PLMI_CHANGE_RECORD_MANAGEMENT

Other Terms

PLM-42028, /DSCSAG/CL_OBSERVER, /DSCSAG/CL_EX_ECTR

Reason and Prerequisites

The status change processing is executed in a call stack that involves BOBF and RAP framework logic.

In this context, explicit database commits or framework-incompatible operations can lead to inconsistent transactional states and assertion errors.

Solution

In Change Record status actions, commit handling was aligned with RAP and SADL transaction management to avoid framework conflicts.
