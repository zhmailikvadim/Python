Symptom

  • The system does not block a sales order even when its total value exceeds the Letter of Credit (LC) / Financial Document (FD) amount assigned.
  • No credit block or warning is triggered when the LC amount is less than the total sales order value.

Environment

  • Sales and Distribution (SD)
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a Letter of Credit/Financial document with a certain amount.
  2. Create a sales order for a customer and assign this LC document. The sales order value is higher than the LC value.
  3. Observe that the system allows order creation and subsequent processing without showing any failed LC/FD check and setting overall credit status to blocked.

Cause

The Financial Document/Letter of Credit Check is using the credit value (same as Credit Management). If the sales order has no credit value determined, then it would not fail on FD or LC check.

Resolution

Prerequisites for the credit value calculation must be fulfilled to use relevant sales order value for Letter of Credit Check/Financial Document Check.

Relevant settings:

  1. On the sales order select payment guarantee procedure relevant for LC/FD.
  2. If both credit management and Financial Document check is activated, then update group 12 must be used for the credit control area/credit segment. (Avoid using update group 15 as it prevents sales orders from being considered for credit exposure update and results in no credit value.)
  3. Ensure that the items are billing relevant (the field VBAP-FKREL must not be blank or set to D; use transaction VOV7 to maintain the field).
  4. Items must also be credit active. (The field VBAP-CMPNT must be set to 'X'; maintain the field in transaction VOV7 - field 'Credit active').
  5. Confirm that the item has credit price (VBAP-CMPRE). If the field VBAP-CMPRE is set to 0, check the settings in transaction V/08 for the pricing procedure used in the order and ensure a valid condition is maintained with Subtotal = 'A' setting.
  6. For delivery-related scenarios, ensure that there is confirmed quantity on the schedule lines.
