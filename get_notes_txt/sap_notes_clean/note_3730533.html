Symptom

  • Update work processes were occupied, with ABAP wait/onhold. Transactions were blocked with RECORD_LOCK. This occurred when Business Partner (BP) web service replication was used and a new relationship was created after BP creation.
    • Program: SAPLBS_SOA_INAPPSEQ_UPD 
      Current action: Database Update
      Action: BSSOA_IAS_SEQ
  • Critical issues occurred in web service messages for both inbound and outbound communication.
  • Significant mass changes were made to Business Partner master data earlier in the day, leading to issues in message replication to systems such as Ariba, C4C, CPQ, and FSM.
  • The systems are integrated via Data Replication Framework (DRF) and web services.
  • SM50 displayed multiple entries with ABAP_HOLD due to SM12 lock on UKMDB_V78BNSS
  • Lock contention occurred in the productive system due to users running the transaction code MDS_LOAD_COCKPIT to synchronize data.
  • This resulted in locks against the table BSSOA_IAS_SEQ, causing updates to be stuck and leading to performance issues across the system.
  • Many entries were observed in the tRFC queues (SM58) with "Function Module = MDG_BS_BP_OUTBOUND_DRF_CALL, Destination = NONE."

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Perform a MASS upload or update of BPs
  2. The replication is trigger automatically
  3. Observe degradation in the target system's performance.

Resolution

During Mass upload deactivate the replication in DRFIMG and then replicate the data manually via DRFOUT in batches during Business downtime — batch number depends on system data load. Once the mass updates are completed, activate the replication again in DRFIMG.

Dedicate separate application server for Business partner replication process. 

For the replication already triggered which is currently impacting the target system, you can begin by clearing the pending entries from the tRFC queue (SM58) in the source system. Specifically, ensure that the TRFC Function is equal to DRF_REPLICATE or MDG_BS_BP_OUTBOUND_DRF_CALL. This action will prevent the pending Business Partner (BP) replications from being triggered.

In the target system, the inbound destination queue can also be locked using transaction SBGRFCMON or from SRTMONI > Execute search > Queue Monitor (Truck icon). This will prevent further processing and bring system back to normal.
