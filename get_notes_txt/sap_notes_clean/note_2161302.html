Symptom

When a PLU is discounted to ZERO, inspecting the resulting I8 (Item Tax TLOG) record in the TLOG still shows the PLU’s original tax amount.  This tax amount instead should reflect the item’s new adjusted price tax amount of ZERO.

Other Terms

Point of Sale, Zero Tax, Discount, Markdown, Price Override, PLU Itemized Tax

Reason and Prerequisites

Releases prior to SAP POS v10.2.0 SP 17 and lower will experience this issue.  To be more efficient, the POS does not process a PLU’s tax if the taxable amount is ZERO because the tax amount will always be ZERO.  However, when a discount causes the price to drop to ZERO and therefore also cause the taxable amount to drop to ZERO, the POS still needs to update the PLU’s item tax field from its original tax amount to ZERO.  This last step is not being done. 

Solution

Upgrade to SAP POS v10.2.0 SP 17.  To resolve this issue, the POS has been modified to still process a PLU’s item tax even if the taxable amount is ZERO.
