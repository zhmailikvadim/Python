Symptom

Transaction MEIS must report, in France, purchase orders with receiving plants located in Monaco. Monaco, with ISO country code MC, is not an EU country, but there is a legal requirement for Intrastat to report Imports from any EU country to Monaco. In this scenario, Monaco (MC) is assimilated to France (FR) for Intrastat.

Note: Monaco is not an EU country. Configuring T005-XEGLD = 'X' for Monaco is incorrect and not recommended.

Other Terms

S/4HANA, Intrastat, EU, Plants, Monaco, MC, FR, France, MEIS, purchase order, GTS

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a legal requirement for France and Monaco.

Prerequisite Node:

  • SAP Note "3516734 - Manage Providers of Information: not possible to assign plants from Monaco"

Solution

Implement the attached correction or upgrade to the relevant Service Pack.
