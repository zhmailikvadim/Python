Symptom

There is a requirement to add Trading Partner field to the upload template of "Import Supplier Invoices" FIORI app (F3041).

Environment

  • Financial Accounting (FI)
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Fiori

Resolution

As per standard system design, the trading partner field is not part of the scope of the supported fields for the upload template of "Import Supplier Invoices" FIORI app. The reason is that SAP considers that the value of the trading partner will be derived from the vendor/supplier master data automatically during the posting.
