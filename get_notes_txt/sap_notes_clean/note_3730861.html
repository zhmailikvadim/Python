Symptom

During copying the existing quote or converting the sales quote to the sales order, the pricing procedure was not copied. 

Environment

  • SAP Sales Cloud Version 2 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Sales Quotes View 
  2. Open the sales quote A which has the pricing procedure P1(P1 refers to the pricing procedure name) 
  3. Click ... -> Click Copy button 
  4. The copied sales quote B doesn't has this pricing procedure P1 

OR 

  1. Go to Sales Quotes View 
  2. Open the sales quote A which has the pricing procedure P1(P1 refers to the pricing procedure name) 
  3. Convert this sales quote to sales order 
  4. The converted sales order doesn't has this pricing procedure P1 

Resolution

The pricing procedure cannot be copied during the sales quote copy or sales quote to sales order conversion. This is standard system behavior. 

For native pricing(internal pricing) or price estimation, the pricing procedure can only be determined via the setting -> Pricing Configuration -> Determination Rules. Please maintain the target rules under this setting.

Please note that the manual conditions can be copied from the sales quote to the follow-up sales order only if the pricing procedure associated with the document types of the sales order and the sales quote is the same.  For more details, please refer to the help documentation -> Sales and Service Cloud Version 2 What's New -> Manual Condition from Sales Quotes in Follow-Up Sales Orders.
