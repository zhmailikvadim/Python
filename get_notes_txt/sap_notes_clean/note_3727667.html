Symptom

When Product Compliance Event Processing (PCEP) processes product compliance events and product compliance tasks, performance bottlenecks can occur during the processing of compliance assessments due to superfluous authorization checks.

Other Terms

PCEP, Migration Cockpit, Creation of Compliance Assessments

Reason and Prerequisites

The Product Compliance Event Processing (PCEP) is not optimized for the creation of a large number of assessments

Solution

After implementing this SAP Note, the system optimizes authorization checks within the background processing.
