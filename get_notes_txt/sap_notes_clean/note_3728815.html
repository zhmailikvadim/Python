Symptom

You are trying to exchange a sequence in CO02. A pop-up window appears with the message: “Sequence in order XXX cannot be exchanged.” To determine the reason, you click the question mark button to view the long text. However, this long text does not reflect the recent changes to the sequence exchange logic related to PEO shop floor orders and DSC Edition.

Other Terms

CO591

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Apply correction instruction.
