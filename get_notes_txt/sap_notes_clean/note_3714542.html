Symptom

Payment Order and its Payment Items are processed in parallel by different processes. As a result, Payment Items may be posted multiple times.

Other Terms

SMALL_POP

/PF1/PROCESS_ORDER_PP_ARFC

Reason and Prerequisites

Packages are prepared and processed in an RFC process. After each package, a COMMIT WORK is executed. Due to lock parameter _SCOPE = 2, the dialog lock owner is released during the commit.

Solution

Code adjustment.

The enqueue lock is explicitly re-established after COMMIT WORK to ensure consistent locking during package processing.

Please apply SAP Note or assigned Support Package.
