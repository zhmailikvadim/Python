Symptom

A method is needed to return the scope when the material number and location number is provided.

Other Terms

FILL_BUFFER
GET_SCOPE_FROM_MATNRLOCNO

Flexible integration.

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing functionality.

Solution

Two methods have been added.

The method GET_SCOPE_FROM_MATNRLOCNO can be used to determine the scope from /sapapo/matplsta.

The method fFILL_BUFFER can be used to prefell the buffer of class /SAPAPO/CL_PPDS_SCOPE_SRV.
