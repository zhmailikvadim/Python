Symptom

What are possible scenerio supported in Joule Capabilities?

Resolution

Guided buying is now integrated with Joule, SAP’s generative AI copilot.

Users can ask Joule questions about their purchases and receive immediate and accurate answers through generative AI.

To access the Joule panel, users click the new Joule icon at the top of the window.

Joule Capabilities

The following capabilities are available in Joule in guided buying:

Use CaseDescriptionExample
Purchase requisition statusDisplays the status of a specified purchase requisition.Show me the status of PR123
Purchase requisition approversDisplays previous and active approvers for a specified requisition.Approvers for PR123
Purchase requisition shipping detailsDisplays the shipping details of a selected purchase requisition from a list.What is the shipping address
Purchase requisition detailsDisplay details, such as line-item information of a specified purchase requisition.Show me the details in PR123
Recent purchasesDisplays the list of purchase requisitions created recently.Show me the list of requests that I created recently
Your favoritesNavigate the user to the Your favorites page.Take me to my favorite items
