Symptom

Balance Carry Forward (BCF) did not work as expected.

Values were transferred to the opening FS item F00, but the S-ANI-BS amount remained on S-ANI-BS and was not allocated to S-RETAINED-EARNINGS.

For now, the FS item with S-ANI-BS  and S-RETAINED-EARNINGS have client-specific Subitem Category F1 instead of standard 1 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

The FS item remains unchanged because the FS item category is F1, whereas it should be 1 for the BCF allocation to work.

Resolution

FS items for net income and retained earnings use a breakdown category with a fixed category of 1.
