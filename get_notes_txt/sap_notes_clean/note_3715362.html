Symptom

After deploying the content to the deployment target the workspace was decommissioned and cannot be deleted anymore. 
 

Environment

SAP Central Business Configuration

Cause

Deletion of the already deployed workspace is not possible for audit purposes.
 

Resolution

Deletion of the already deployed workspace is not possible for audit purposes.

The workspace is blocked as part of the decommissioning process, and the lock notification "Your workspace is locked since the target system has been invalidated." will remain active during the 30-day retention period while the process is still reversible.

User access to the workspace can be removed by deleting the users from the workspace team tab. Once removed, users will no longer be able to view or use the workspace and can proceed with activities in the new implementation workspace created in the system.

To avoid confusion for users who can see all workspaces due to their assigned roles, the workspace name can also be updated or changed.

The old workspace will be archived later by an automated cleanup job that will be implemented and executed at a later stage.
