Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP BW/4HANA
  • SAP NetWeaver

Cause

Resolution

  1. Check for any open unconfirmed requests deltas, and follow the instructions as per KBA 3006837 - ODP DTP Error: Only restore to state after previous adjustment to selections
  2. If the issue remains follow the instructions as per KBA 3531292 - Detailed steps: How to 'End subscription' in ODQMON
  3. For 0CO_OM_CCA_40, 0CO_OM_OPA_40, 0FI_AP_3 and  0FI_AR_3 extractors/datasources follow also the instructions as per SAP Note 1781533 - BW-BCT: Initialization without data transfer
