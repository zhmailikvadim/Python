Symptom

  • In the mass pricing workcenter(CMM_MP_MASS_PRICING) for Flat2 contracts, certain fields are not populating correctly.
  • The Flat price UOM is incorrect.
  • Provisional FX is not populating.
  • Basis amount is incorrectly calculated.
  • Even after providing the final FX rate, the basis amount is incorrect or not calculated.
  • The issue occurs during the display in the mass pricing work center.

Environment

SAP Agricultural Contract Management 

Reproducing the Issue

Execute the mass pricing workcenter transaction (CMM_MP_MASS_PRICING) for componentized flat(Flat2) pricing approach contract.

Cause

The issue was caused by a configuration setting where the 'Individual Lift on FLAT 2' feature switch was activated. This configuration caused the system to consider the quotation currency as the flat price currency.

Resolution

  1. Disable the 'Individual Lift on FLAT 2' feature switch in the system configuration.
  2. Verify that the system uses the document currency and document UoM, enabling the desired values to display correctly.
  3. Follow the below path to do the configuration.
  • Execute T-Code: wb2b_cus
  • Click on Agricultural Contract Management
  • Select Basic Settings
  • Activate Feature-Switches remove the Active flag from feature switch ID 'IND_LIFT'(Individual Lift on FLAT 2).
