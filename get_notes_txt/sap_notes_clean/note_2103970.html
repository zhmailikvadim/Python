Symptom

In the billing schema, you want to use the direct marketing tranche at the remuneration quantity level for the individual generating unit instead of at the distribution quantity level.

Reason and Prerequisites

This requires new variant programs.

Solution

You can use the new variant programs EEGCAL18 (same as EEGCAL01) and EEGCAL19 (same as EEGCAL07) to take the direct marketing tranche into account at the remuneration quantity level.
