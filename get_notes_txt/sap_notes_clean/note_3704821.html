Symptom

In the process "Termination of Meter Operation Service for Electricity (DSO View)" (DE_B_WIM_TERM_MOS_DSO), the precondition of step 8920 is incorrect,

for shutdown case after step 0310 is completed and process end information is also received from cloud, as a result the process still remains Active and did not complete.

This issue is relevant for the market roles: DSO.

Other Terms

 WIM, DE_B_WIM_TERM_MOS_DSO, MOS termination, shutdown, DSO View, step 8920, step 0310, BC set, process end information

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because the precondition was not met when step 0310 was completed and process end information was received from the Cloud.

The process remained Active and did not complete.

Solution

Please implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import more high version of support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, then activate the BC set /US4G/3_0_DSO_SP07_NOTE_3704821.

The system will evaluates the precondition correctly in step 8920 when step 0310 is completed and process end information is received.

You can verify the fix by checking that the process document reaches the Completed status after click the 'Execute' button of the active pdoc.
