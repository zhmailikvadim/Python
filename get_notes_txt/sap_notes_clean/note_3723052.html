Symptom

Previously, the “Replan Needed” flag in the configuration of confirmation notes was not sufficiently taken into account in the confirmation cockpit. To remedy this and avoid premature confirmation, this note includes a warning that is displayed to the user when confirming a deviation for which the flag is set.

This gives the customer the opportunity to cancel the process in a timely manner.
