Symptom

Automation Script J59 failure for Process Step: “Maintain Business Partner Certificate” & Reverse Individual Documents.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 2602 with HFC02

Reproducing the Issue

Process step test execution will fail or unable to resume the automate or Re-execution may fail, issue at :

  • process step #3 (Maintain Business Partner Certificate) in J59 Scenario for ES Country
  • process step #28 (Reverse Individual Documents) in J59 Scenario for ES country

Cause

Due to Limitation in the Test Automation Tool for J59 in ES country, the changes are not taking effect as expected.

Resolution

The standard script with adaption changes will be available in SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition Release 2602.1.

Keywords

2602, S/4HANA Test Automation Tool, Maintain Business Partner, Manage Journal Entries, J59_ES
