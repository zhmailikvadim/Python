Symptom

Email tokens added to the supplier registration messaging template do not appear in bold formatting, even when formatting is applied.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Modify the supplier registration email template in the system
    Template: Invitation: Register to become a supplier with ***
  2. Add the email token [SPONSOR_EMAIL] in bold format in the email body and save the changes.
  3. Create a supplier and send the registration invitation to the supplier.
  4. Receive the supplier registration invitation email and review the email id.

Cause

Email cannot be formatted since tokens are backend fields with different types.

Resolution

This behavior is expected and by design.

Email tokens such as [SPONSOR_EMAIL] are dynamically resolved at runtime from backend fields. These tokens originate from different backend data types and are rendered as plain text when the email is generated. As a result, any formatting (for example, bold, italic, or underline) applied directly to the token in the email template is not preserved in the delivered email.
