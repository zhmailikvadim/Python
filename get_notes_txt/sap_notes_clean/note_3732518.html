Symptom

In the business transaction for the charges (old), the 'Percentage Markup' button is used to generate an additional flow for an outgoing flow. You want the amount of the outgoing flow to be reduced by the amount of the additional flow.

This SAP Note delivers the new parameter C_FLG_SUBTR_SURCHARGE_FRM_BASE in the method SET_GLOBAL_FLAGS (BAdI FVD_CHARGE). If this parameter is set to 'X' by a customer-specific BAdI implementation, the amount of the outgoing flow is reduced by the amount of the additionally generated flow.
