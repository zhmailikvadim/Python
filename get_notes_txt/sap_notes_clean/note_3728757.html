Symptom

You need to send outbound e-mails from your tenant but you don't know which activation is needed or you have read that DKIM is needed but you are not sure what should be requested to SAP support.

Environment

SAP Cloud for Customer

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Send an email from the specified domain.
  2. Observe that the email is marked as untrusted and quarantined.

Resolution

DKIM : 

DKIM stands for "Domain Keys Identified Mail" and is used to certify that an e-mail was authorized and sent from the owner of the Domain. This technique is used on Business C4C to sign Mass E-mails and Business Mails. 

DKIM (Domain Keys Identified Mail) is used to authenticate outgoing emails so recipients trust that messages are genuinely sent from your domain.

The difference between Mass Emails and Business Emails in terms of DKIM mainly comes down to how and where the signing is configured and applied.

Mass E-mails: 

Mass emails (campaigns, newsletters) are typically handled by SAP’s marketing/email infrastructure.

Key characteristics:

Separate sending domain (often a subdomain like mail.yourcompany.com)

DKIM is mandatory/recommended for deliverability

Marketing campaigns

Bulk communications

Helps avoid spam filters 

Business E-mails: 

Business emails are transactional or individual emails sent from within C4C (e.g., quotes, tickets, replies).

👉 Focus: Authentication of one-to-one or transactional communication
