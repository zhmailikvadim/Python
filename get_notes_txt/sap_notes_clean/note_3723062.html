Symptom

FSM Activities are not generated during creation and update of service order's operation. Additionally, Connector does not trigger outbound Activity IDocs generation. The issue occurs in scenarios where customizing is maintained only for company ‘0000’ in transaction /PACG/ECM_SCSTD. Even though fallback customizing exists for ‘0000’, system does not read it.

Other Terms

FSM, E4C, Connector, Activity, /PACG/ECM_SCSTD

Reason and Prerequisites

The issue is caused by a logic gap in method /PACG/ECM_CL_A_ASA_UTIL=>GET_CUSTOMISING. If /PACG/ECM_SCSTD configuration is maintained exclusively at '0000' FSM Company level, FSM Activities are not generated and outbound Activity IDocs are not triggered during operation updates.

Solution

Please, implement this correction instruction.
