Symptom

You can use the "Simple Savings Contract Change" dialog (transaction /IBS/BSC_CHG_SMP_CHG) to create payment regulations (payment agreements or payment obligations) with a "Valid From" date after the "Valid To" date if the "Valid To" date is in the past.

Reason and Prerequisites

In the "Service for Payment Agreements" (implementation /IBS/CL_BSC_SI_CI), payment regulations whose "Valid To" date is in the past are not validated.

Solution

The method /IBS/IF_BSC_SD_CI~CHECK_MLTP of the above Services are corrected: even payment regulations whose "Valid To" date is in the past are validated if they are newly created or changed in the dialog.
