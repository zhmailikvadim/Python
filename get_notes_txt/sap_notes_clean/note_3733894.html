Symptom

When you process a payment requisition with multiple payment strategies, after the payment for the first payment strategy succeeds, the second payment strategy fails with error message "Item &2 of journal entry &1 has been cleared."

Other Terms

 Mixed Payment; Payment Requisition; Payment

Reason and Prerequisites

Posted payment item is incorrectly included for payment item validation.

Solution

Apply the correction instructions in the SAP Note or implement the corresponding Support Package.
