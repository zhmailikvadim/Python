Symptom

During the execution of a periodic reposting, the error message "The initial value is not valid for characteristic &" (GM094) appears in the log.

Statistical orders or WBS elements are defined as receivers in the cycle, but account assignment objects of the type "Cost Center" are not used as senders or receivers in any cycle segment.

Other Terms

Transactions: KSW5, KSWB
Program: ALxxyyyR

Reason and Prerequisites

The resolution of the statistical receiver double account assignment with statistical orders or statistical WBS elements requires the derivation of the actual cost center to be posted to as defined in the relevant master record. However, since no account assignment objects of the type "Cost Center" are used in the entire cycle, important internal data structures no longer contain the field "Cost Center". The check for the existence of the actual cost center to be posted to therefore takes place with the initial value, resulting in the aforementioned error message.

Solution

  • Workaround,: Define the field "Cost Center" with a non-existent cost center in any segment - as a sender or receiver.
  • To permanently correct the runtime behavior of the periodic reposting, please carry out the following customizing change:

    Call transaction SM30 for the table/view V_T811I.
    Choose "No Restrictions" (default setting) for the data range and choose "Edit".
    On the next screen, choose the "Position" button to position the screen on the table entry relevant for your application.
