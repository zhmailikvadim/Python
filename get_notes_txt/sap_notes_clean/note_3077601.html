Symptom

In archive management (transaction SARA -> Management), you schedule jobs for the destruction of replaced archive files. These jobs are then incorrectly displayed in the job overview for all archiving objects even though each of these jobs processes files of one archiving object only.

Other Terms

Archiving, ADK, SARA, management, RSARC_DESTROY_REPLACED, destruction, replaced, ILM_DEL_REPL

Reason and Prerequisites

There is a program error.

Solution

Import the relevant support package or implement the attached correction instructions.
