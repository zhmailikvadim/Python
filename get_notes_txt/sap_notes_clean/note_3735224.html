Symptom

Why are all amounts in the Purchase Order printout displayed as "XXX" instead of the actual values?

Environment

SAP Ariba Procurement

Resolution

This occurs because the line items have the Hide Item Price From Supplier checkbox selected in the PR.

In this scenario, the price fields are masked with "XXX" in the Purchase Order printout.

See Also

For more information about who can view the Hide Item Price From Supplier field, please refer to Hide Item Price from Supplier field doesn't display for users.
