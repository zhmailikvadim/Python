Symptom

In latest form, when calculating the total weight of sections in the summary section, the system does not include the weight of section whose section weight is read only.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Performance Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. For example, a form has three sections whose weight should total 100%.
    • Section 1: 30% (section weight configured as read-only)
    • Section 2: 30% (section weight configured as editable)
    • Section 3: 40% (section weight configured as editable)
  2. When a user edits the weights for the editable sections (2 and 3), the system incorrectly calculates the total as 70 (ignoring the 30 from the read-only section) .

Cause

This is a Known Issue.

Resolution

The fix for this issue is planned to be deployed on 1H 2026 release.

For release timelines, please review SAP SuccessFactors Product Release & Road Map Information.

Workaround

Either configure all section weight to be editable, or disable latest form experience.
