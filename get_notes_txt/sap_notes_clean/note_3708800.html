Symptom

Together with the umbrella association for statutory health insurance, the German Hospital Company has agreed and published the agreement on the implementation of the billing procedure for the special cross-sectoral remuneration according to § 115f SGB V (hybrid DRG) as part of the data transfer according to § 301 paragraphs 1 and 2 SGB V (hybrid DRG implementation agreement) dated December 17, 2025.

As of 2026, there are hybrid DRGs that are determined by a grouper only if the case has more than one calculation date. This means that hybrid DRGs can also be determined for cases whose length of stay is greater than 1.

For case merges according to section 1 paragraph 3 of the hybrid DRG implementation agreement (the functions of the case merges are described in SAP Note 3461373 - DE: Hybrid DRG - Adjustment in Case Merge area), this may also mean that a hybrid DRG is also determined for a complete readmission case.

This SAP Note adjusts service rule R11 so that the corresponding billable service can also be generated for complete readmission cases with a hybrid DRG.

Other Terms

Hybrid DRG, 115f, §115f, hybrid DRG order, hybrid DRG conversion agreement, readmission, readmission, complete readmission case, case merge, R11, ISH_CHECK_SERVICES_DRG

Reason and Prerequisites

Legal change

Solution

Refer to the correction instructions for your release.

After you implement the correction instructions, service rule R11 is able to generate the assigned billable service for a hybrid DRG of a complete readmission case.
