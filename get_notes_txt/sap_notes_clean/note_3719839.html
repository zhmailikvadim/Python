Symptom

I have a Work Order in Accepted status and the system shows the next step as Need to Activate.

Cause

Auto Activate is disabled so the buyer must manually activate the Work Order.

Resolution

  1. Sign into your SAP Fieldglass account 
  2. Open the Work Order > Click on Activate
  3. The system will ask Do you want to continue? > Select OK

See Also

  • KB1334325 - The Activate button is not available on the Work Order
  • Connector for activating multiple Work Orders in bulk: Work Order Activate Upload
