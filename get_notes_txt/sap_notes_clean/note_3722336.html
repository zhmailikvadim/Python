Symptom

In TU-based TM-EWM integration scenario, the buyer's original document information at FU or FO item level are successfully filled when delivery created. However, after the ‘Departure from checkpoint’ process is triggered in /SCWM/TU(LDAP notification generated), the buyer's original document information at FU or FO ITEM level is cleared.

  • BUYORIG_BTD_ID
  • BUYORIG_BTD_ITEM_ID
  • BUYORIG_DATE 

Environment

SAP Transportation Management SAP S/4HANA 2023 and subsequent releases 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create the LE delivery.
  2. Trigger the ‘departure from checkpoint’ process in /SCWM/TU.
  3. Observe that the BUYORIG_* field at the FU or FO ITEM level is cleared.

Cause

Missing Note implementation: Note 3717646 - "Buyer's original document information is cleared after LDAP Notification".

Resolution

The Note 3717646 - "Buyer's original document information is cleared after LDAP Notification" is currently a pilot Note. To proceed, please raise a case with the TM-INT-EWM component. A SAP Technical Support Engineer will review the case and coordinate with Development Support to add the specific customer number to the pilot customer list.
