Symptom

  • URL based image is not displayed in Crystal Report for Enterprise report.
  • Issue is not URL specific and occurs with any image URL.
  • Issue occurs from BI launchpad as well.

Other Terms

CR4E, picture, picture url, https, image, not displayed, crystal client, crystal enterprise

Reason and Prerequisites

Environment:

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform 4.3 Support Package 05 Patch 00
  • SAP Crystal Report for Enterprise 4.3 SP 05 Patch 00

Step to Reproduce:

  1. Launch Crystal Report for Enterprise Tool.
  2. Create a blank Report.
  3. Click on Insert option from Menu bar > picture option.
  4. Add the picture in 'Page Header'.
  5. A new window will open to select a picture from local disk (Select a picture from local disk and add in the report).
  6. Now right click on the added picture > Format picture > picture > click on 'Conditional format' button under Element.
  7. A window will open. Add/define the image URL inside double quotes "" and click on 'save & close'.
  8. Switch to page mode.
  9. Notice, URLs image is not getting displayed in the report only picture selected from local disk appears.

Cause:

This issue is caused by a defect in the product

Solution

The solution is under investigation by development team.
