Symptom

Requirement to add custom field in 'Display Line Items - Margin Analysis'

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Resolution

  1. In the “Custom Fields” app, create the characteristic using the business context “Accounting: Journal Entry Item”.
  2. In the same app, enable the field for all relevant usages/views of “C_JournalEntryItemBrowser”.
  3. Publish the field and refresh the “Display Line Items – Margin Analysis” app to verify the characteristic is available and visible.
