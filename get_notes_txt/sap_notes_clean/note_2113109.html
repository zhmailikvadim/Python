Symptom

New additional personal income tax deductible item for the year 2014-2015, which is related with the cost of domestic package tour or accommodation, and details are as following:

1. The individual tax payer can claim the actual expenses but not exceed Baht 15,000 paid only to the authorized tour agents and authorized hotel/accommodations during December 16, 2014 - December 31, 2015. (The authorized names can be checked at http://www.rd.go.th/publish/44131.0.html for tour agents and http://www.rd.go.th/publish/44132.0.html for hotel/ accommodation – pending to be updated).

2. If either side of spouse has taxable income, only such tax payer can claim the actual expenses but not exceed Baht 15,000.

3. If both side of spouse have taxable income, each one can claim the actual expenses but not exceed Baht 15,000.

4. Tax payer has to pay for the actual expenses for domestic package tours and accommodation and obtain the official receipt/tax invoices from the authorized tour agents/ accommodation/hotels.

5. The official receipts/ tax invoices must state name of tax payer , amount and date of payment.

Other Terms

Legal Change, Personal Income Tax, Tourism Exemption

Reason and Prerequisites

Legal Change
