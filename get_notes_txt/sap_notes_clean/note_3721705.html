Symptom

The SAP FSM Service Map does not display all technicians.

Environment

SAP FSM - Planning & Dispatching - Service Map

Reproducing the Issue

  • Login Shell with User
  • Access to Planning & Dispatching - Service Map
  • Select Technician view and check technician locations on maps
  • Not all technicians are showing on Service Map

Cause

Service Map need detailed address info (street, street no. etc) to analyze and locate the location, or technician address has latitude and longitude maintained. 

The invisible technicians are missing enough Default Address Details (Street, Street No, ...) and also no Latitude & Longitude maintained.

Resolution

Go to Master Data Management.

Open People in left navigator, choose one technician.

Please remember to disable Preview Mode so that you can see the Latitude and Longitude fields in Address section.

Fill in Address details, or maintain Latitude & Longitude.

Save Address updates.

Back to Service Map, refresh the page and check the technician is visible or not.

Keywords

SAP FSM, SAP Field Service Management, Service Map
