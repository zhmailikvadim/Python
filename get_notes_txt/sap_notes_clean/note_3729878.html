Symptom

User creates a Lean SES using the App 'F2027 - Manage Service Entry Sheets LEAN', the Lean PO contains a mix of service and material items (EKPO_PRODUCTTYPE '1' Material  and '2' Service)
When user enters the Purchase Order reference, the 'Add Purchase Order Items' pop-up displays only the PO item with ekpo-producttype= '2'.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Cause

Validation checks performed for Material Item (EKPO-PRODUCTTYPE = '1' ) in

  • method CHECK_PO_HEADER_REFERENCE (CL_VAL_SES_ITEM_MAT_PO_REF)
  • method CHECK_PO_ITEM_REFERENCE (CL_VAL_SES_ITEM_MAT_PO_REF)

Resolution

CHECK_PO_HEADER_REFERENCE 

*   1 - Only PO's that are released (or not subject to any release procedure or workflow processMMPUR_SES119
*   2 - Only PO's that are not deletedMMPUR_SES118
*   3 - Only PO's that are not put on hold or parkedMMPUR_SES197
*   4 - No PO's with down payments (EKKO-ME_DPTYP)MMPUR_SES196
*   5 - No PO's that are relevant for INTRASTAT reporting (EKKO-INTRA_REL, INTRA_EXCL)MMPUR_SES156
*   6 - No POs where any industry specific fields are filled (retail, fashion, DIMP, oil, SPEC 2000 etc.) 

CHECK_PO_ITEM_REFERENCE

*     7 - Materials  without material master are  not allowedMMPUR_SES189
*     8 - No batch managed materialsMMPUR_SES159
*     9 - No materials with classificationMMPUR_SES166
*   10 - No materials with serial numberMMPUR_SES161
*   11 - No materials with split valuationMMPUR_SES169
*   12 - No materials with manufacturer part numbersMMPUR_SES168
*   13 - No materials where the revision level is filledMMPUR_SES167
*   14 - No retail articles (with subitems in the PO)MMPUR_SES170
*   15 - Only item category blank (standard) - Currently PO item category A (enhanced limit) is not supportedMMPUR_SES157
*   16 - Items with product type group = 1/blank (material) and 2 (service), both for limits and standard itemsMMPUR_SES158
*   17 - Only items where a goods receipt and an invoice receipt are expected (EKPO-WEPOS = X, EKPO-REPOS = X)MMPUR_SES160
*   18 - No items with confirmation controlMMPUR_SES171
