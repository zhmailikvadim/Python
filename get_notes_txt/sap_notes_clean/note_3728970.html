Symptom

Version History:

Version 1: Pilot release (18.03.2026)

Version 2&3: General release (20.03.2026). No changes to Correction Instructions.

Relevance:

This SAP Note is relevant only for those customers who have the Simplified PBIK solution for Company Cars activated.

Switch settings: 

 V_T5GPBS_ALL-TXBN set to 'X' and V_T5GPBS_ALL-REGP set to '01' or '02'.

Scenario Description:

Consider a scenario where the employee (EE) has a company car effective from tax year 2025-26 with validity end date as 31.12.9999 having no regular payments maintained in IT0442. Payroll is successfully processed for all pay periods in tax year 2025-26 until May 2026 of the new tax year. In June, prior to the payroll processing for June, a new car is maintained with regular payments of 100 GBP retrospectively from May 2026.

May in June 2026 run correctly has the regular payment deduction of 100 GBP. Taxable pay calculation for the new car does not happen in retro as the 'No retro' solution has been activated.

In June in June run, taxable benefit is correctly calculated for two cars after reducing the regular payment. However the regular payment amount of 100 GBP is not deducted in June 2026. This is incorrect.

Other Terms

Regular Pay, Simplified PBIK, V_T5GPBS_ALL-TXBN, V_T5GPBS_ALL-REGP, No retro
