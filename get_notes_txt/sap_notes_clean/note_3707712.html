31.03.2026 UK HMRC: CDS 5.1.0 Legal Change in relation to the specification for declaring the data elements 5/15 (country of origin) and 5/16 (country of preferential origin).


As part of Customs Declaration Service (CDS) Release 5.1.0, (delivered on Saturday 28 March.)
HMRC is improving how CDS validates preference codes and origin data for tariff measure derivation under Data Elements 5/15 (Country of Origin) and 5/16 (Country of Preferential Origin).
Please see the attached email from HM Revenue & Customs: "HMRC CDS Country of Origin Comms.jpg"

Some declaration are being rejected due to this lates CDS release.

The following SAP GTS note has been delivered to adjust the data retrieval accordingly and resolve the issue

3734348  GB CDS: Country of origin for preference is not reported

01.03.2026 Postponement of DK DMS Import system switchover- New Go-Live Date Saturday, March 14, 2026.

The Danish Customs Board has chosen to postpone the closure of the Import System until Saturday, March 14, 2026.
According to the plan, the Import System was to close on Saturday, February 28, to be replaced by the new system, DMS Import.
Further Details are available here: https://toldst.dk/erhverv/toldsystemer/dms/faq-om-overgangen-til-dms-import-14-marts-2026 

Regulatory Change Announcement:  available here 
Related SAP Notes: 
3617503 - DK DMS Import: Conversion to DMS
3662845 DK DMS Import: FAQ and corrections

23.01.2026 POSTPONEMENT OF FR Delta T NCTS PHASE 6 - Go-Live date changed to February 2nd 2026 !!

The Legal change Adaptation of DELTA T to UCC standards - NCTS phases 6, published by the Direction Générale des Douanes et Droits Indirects (French Customs) has issued an urgent update.

The date for the commissioning in production was re-scheduled by French customs from January 26th, 2026 to February 2nd, 2026, 10:00am CET.  

Further details can be found on the FR customs site:
https://www.douane.gouv.fr/fiche/le-transit-delta-t 
https://www.douane.gouv.fr/service-en-ligne/declaration-de-transit-douanier-delta-t 

French Customs are asking Clients not to migrate systems to P6 on 26 January 2026, but to remain on P5. 

16.01.2026 EMCS 4.2 for administrative documents in BE, NL and UK

Belgian, Dutch and UK customs authorities announced the introduction of the new Excise Movement Control System (EMCS) version 4.2. From 12 February 2026, EMCS will move to Phase 4.2

16.12.2025 DELTA E: Export from France Regulatory change
Communication with the French customs authorities in the export procedure area is to be converted to the new DELTA Export customs system as outlined in Note "3642494 - FR export: Conversion to the new customs system DELTA export "
Please read thoroughly note 3642494 for information about the activities to enable use of the French IT system DELTA Export

For the newest required corrections and FAQs please see note 3661630 FR export: FAQ and corrections for the French customs system DELTA Export 
In addition, documentation with the most important changes in the application is attached as a PDF file to the FAQ note 3661630
