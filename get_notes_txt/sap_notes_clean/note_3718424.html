Symptom

Process when creating a payoff - statistical condition items are taken into account, which is incorrect.

Reason and Prerequisites

During the creation of the payoff in the PKAP process, statistical condition items (JNULLKON = 3) must not be used to determine the partner number because these have no effect on payment flows and do not represent a valid partner/customer reference.

Solution

The method GET_VZZKOPO_FOR_PAY_REL_INFO (/IBS/CLFM_RO_BL_REPAYM_OFFER) has been adjusted in such a way that statistical condition items are not taken into account.
