Symptom

Business Activity panel is not displayed in the new supplier profile summary.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the New Supplier Profile Summary.
  2. Attempt to locate the Business Activity panel
  3. Observe that the Business Activity panel is not present.

Resolution

The Business Activity area is only available in the classic supplier 360° profile. If your site uses the new supplier profile summary, Business Activity or Sourcing or Contracts cards are not available at this point even though parameters Application.SM.UI.SourcingSummary and Application.SM.UI.ContractsSummary are enabled.

See About the Supplier Profile Summary for cards and information available in new supplier profile summary
