Symptom

Incorrect supplier was determined when invoice in SAP Ariba Invoicing is created via manual upload or API. The issue used to happen but now supplier is getting determined per expectation.

Resolution

Given that the expected supplier is getting determined, it used to not pick the supplier because the supplier did not exist in SAP Ariba Invoicing or the details on the supplier at the time when the initial invoice was created were different than what it is now. Kindly check with master data team in S/4HANA if the supplier had always existed and no changes were made since the time the original invoices did not pick the expected supplier.
