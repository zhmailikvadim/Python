Symptom

  • The employee has a future-dated termination, and the intention is to rehire shortly after the termination date. But the option to rehire with old employment is not showing in the Rehire Verification task, only “Rehire with New Employment” is shown.
  • The Rehire with Old Employment feature is enabled, but the option is still not available in the Rehire Verification task.
  • No error message is displayed.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Set an employee’s termination with a future effective date.
  2. Initiate Onboarding for the candidate.
  3. Open the Employee Profile Selection (Pre-Onboarding Rehire Verification) task.
  4. Review the rehire options.
  5. Observe that “Rehire with Old Employment” is not shown.

Cause

Expected behavior.

Future-dated terminations aren’t supported for “Rehire with Old Employment”; the previous employment remains active until the termination effective date, so the option is not presented.

Resolution

Guide: Rehire with New and Old Employments

As our guide states:

"Future dated terminations aren’t currently supported when rehiring with old employment. Future dated terminations refer to situations where the last working day is planned for a future date."

Ensure the termination effective date is in the past so that the previous employment is inactive; if the termination is future dated, wait until the effective date has passed.

If the termination date is in the past, confirm configuration & permissions: 

  • Admin Center > Onboarding General Settings, enable “Rehire on Old Employment”.
  • Permission Roles > Manage Hire > Rehire Inactive Employee (by ‘match’ in New Hire)
  • Permission Roles > Manage Hire > Rehire Inactive Employee

Read KBA - 3480806, there is another similar issue.
