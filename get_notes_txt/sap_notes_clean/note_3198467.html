Symptom

After GR to an inbound delivery with HU's, you try to make a goods receipt cancellation with MIGO to a non HU managed storage location in the sense of note 1384533. Yout get a dump with message VL616 ("& & have already been packed. Quantity cannot be changed").

Other Terms

MB_POST_GOODS_MOVEMENT, VL616, MESSAGE_TYPE_X, SAPLMBWL

Reason and Prerequisites

The problem occurs only if the CWM switch is activated. System tries to update CW quantity from HU's when delivery document flow is updated from GR.

This leads to a common check of delivery items. If there is an inconsistency, e.g. packed quantity is bigger than delivery quantity, error VL636 is processed.

Solution

Apply the attached coding correction.
