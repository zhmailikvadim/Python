Symptom

The duration of breaks are also added to the daily and weekly total recorded time in My Timesheet (S/4HANA / Version 4) Consolidated Time Recording.

Other Terms

F3074A, My Timesheet V4, Consolidated Time Recording, Break, total recorded time.

Reason and Prerequisites

The calcutaion for total recorded time does not exclude break records.

Solution

 Implement the automatic correction instruction attached to this SAP Note or alternatively you can implement the corresponding support package.
