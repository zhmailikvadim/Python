Symptom

Enable Onboarding Integration is grayed out on provisioning.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access provisioning
  2. Then company settings
  3. Verify that Enable Onboarding Integration and BizX Onboarding Integration (requires BizX Onboarding) cannot be enabled

Cause

Initiate Onboarding is not enabled on Recruiting Management.

Resolution

Please, make sure that initiate onboarding is enabled on Recruiting management as part of suite.

See Also

Prerequisites for Enabling Onboarding
