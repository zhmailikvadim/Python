Symptom

After initiate the Onboarding from Recruiting, the event reason in onboarding is different from the event reason passed from recruiting.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Initiate the Onboarding from Recruiting;
  2. Candidate lands with different reason passed in Recruiting;

Cause

Duplicate event reason labels configured in "Manage Organization, Pay and Job Structures". The system picks the event reason label randomly when multiple labels are identical, leading to incorrect mapping during onboarding.

Resolution

Rename duplicate event reason labels in "Manage Organization, Pay and Job Structures" to ensure unique identification and start a new candidate from Recruiting
