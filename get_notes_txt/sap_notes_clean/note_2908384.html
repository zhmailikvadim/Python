Symptom

In App 'Create Billing Documents - VF04' only one line item can be selected.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Sales and Distribution

Reproducing the Issue

  • Go to App 'Create Billing Documents VF04'
  • Click 'Display Billing List'
  • Click 'Deselect All'
  • Try to select several lines

Resolution

Press the SHIFT or CTRL key on the keyboard for multi-selection.
