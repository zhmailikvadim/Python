Symptom

List of known Vulnerabilities and Exposures (CVE) for SAP HANA Studio

Reason and Prerequisites

You are using SAP HANA Studio to administrate your SAP HANA systems.

Solution

SAP HANA Studio is deprecated as it has been replaced by SAP HANA Cockpit. For details refer to Section 14 of SAP Note 2073112.

Please prefer SAP HANA Cockpit to administrate your SAP HANA Systems.
