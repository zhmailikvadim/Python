Symptom

In Transportation Management (SAP TM), when items are inserted in the Capacity Requirements tab of a Freight Booking (FRB), the items do not copy to the booking items.

Environment

  • SAP Transportation Management 8.0 - 9.6
  • SAP Transportation Management embedded in SAP S/4HANA 1709+

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create Freight Booking or create freight booking from a freight agreement
  2. On capacity requirements tab, click on insert capacity requirements button
  3. Maintain equipment type, equipment group, container count

Cause

Depending on the customizing, Container Item Source maintained in the freight booking type determines the behavior of copying the items in the booking. 

Resolution

Maintain container item source as 'Container Item Is Defined in Booking (blank)' in the Freight Booking type under additional settings.

When container count, an equipment group, and an equipment type in the Capacity Requirements of the freight booking is entered, one main item is created immediately for each container in the table of the Cargo Management tab.
