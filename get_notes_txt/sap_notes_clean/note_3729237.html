Symptom

In the 'Approve ACM Contract' new Fiori application, the system is displaying a different payment term description compared to the transactions WB22/WB23 for the same trading contract.

-Steps to replicate:

1) Open the trading contract in 'Approve ACM Contract' Fiori application.

2) Navigate to the contract details and check the Payment Term description in the contract details.

3) Open the same trading contract in the SAP GUI.

4) Navigate to the contract details and check the Payment Term description.

Other Terms

Agriculture Contract Management; ACM; Approve ACM Contract; Payment Term; Payment Term Description

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
