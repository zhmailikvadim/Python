Symptom

Work Address attributes are not allowed to maintain in Contact Overview page.

Other Terms

Account, Group, Contact Overview, Relationship, Work Address, Overview page.

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason:

  1. Specific check was carried out to avoid maintenance of Work address attributes in Contact Overview page.
  2. This check was carried out especially for the relationship with Accounts of Category Group.

Prerequisites:

  1. Contact person should have relationship with a Account of Category Group.

 

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions.
