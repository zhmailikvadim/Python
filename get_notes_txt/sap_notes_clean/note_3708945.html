Symptom

Getting the error "CVIV_UI015 - Synchronization from vendor to business partner is not active" while executing the MDS_LOAD_COCKPIT from Vendor to Business Partner. 

Environment

  • Sales and Distribution (SD)
  • Logistics Execution (LE)
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the transaction MDS_LOAD_COCKPIT.
  2. Select the direction Vendor to Business Partner.
  3. Now enter the Vendor number.
  4. Click on execute. 

Cause

Synchronization option “Vendor to Business Partner” is currently not activated.

Resolution

Activate the synchronization option following the below steps: 

  1. Open transaction SPRO.
  2. Navigate to : Cross-Application Components -> Master Data Synchronization -> Synchronization Control -> Synchronization Control -> Activate Synchronization Options.
  3. Add the entry “Vendor to BP” and flag the Active Indicator.

After activating the synchronization option, activate the Post Processing Office (PPO) for the direction “Vendor to BP” by following the instructions provided in the below mentioned KBA.
2351694 - How to activate PPO and resolve ASSERTION_FAILED dump during synchronization

NOTE : This error might occur for the below mentioned synchronization options as well, if so then follow the same process. 

  1. Customer to Business Partner.
  2. Business Partner to Vendor.
  3. Business Partner to Customer
