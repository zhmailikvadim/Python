Symptom

  • Unable to create HDI containers in the new tenant under business data cloud.

Environment

  • SAP Datasphere
  • SAP Business Data Cloud

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Attempt to create HDI containers in the new tenant under business data cloud.

Resolution

The process for BDC related Datasphere tenant is the same like documented in Using HDI Containers with SAP Datasphere.
SAP Datasphere does not need to be available in BTP Cockpit for that HDI integration.

Furthermore the pre-requisite is the same that the region must be the same between SAP Datasphere and HDI.
