Symptom

When creating a query based on Compensation Planning in Story reports, you are only able to select tables from within individual templates, and not multiple or an 'All Templates' option as you are in other template-based domains.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite

Cause

This is currently an expected behavior as Compensation Planning Story Reports are template-specific.

Resolution

The Product Engineering team is aware of multiple customers' desire to have the option to select multiple/all templates at once.

If you too believe this would be a useful functionality, please vote on the enhancement requests below:

  • Compensation Story Reports
  • Ability to report all Compensation forms at once via story reports
