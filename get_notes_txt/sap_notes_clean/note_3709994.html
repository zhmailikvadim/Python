Symptom

During Mass Maintenance or Mass Processing of Business Partners. Many Created and Deleted log entries appear in the Change Documents. 

Mainly related to Communication Data.

Environment

SAP Master Data Governance - Consolidation & Mass Processing Business Partners

Cause

Software error

Resolution

Apply SAP Note 3496155 - Optimizing Change Document creation for Current State behavior

Keywords

ADR2 ADR3 ADR6 "Change records"
