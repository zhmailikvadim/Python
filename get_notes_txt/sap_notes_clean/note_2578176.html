Symptom

Customers having installed and enabled the Roadmap version of Revenue Distribution are experiencing the following issue:

While allocating shares to multiple new owners, the owner's Tax Reimbursements are incorrectly allocated, leading to an Out-Of-Balance error in bookings.

Other Terms

Problem-specific keywords:Revenue Distribution, Valuation, Chain-Of-Title, OOB, /PRA/RD_TRANSFR_OWNER_BY_OWNER, Ownership Transfer, Negative Tax Deducts, NGLs.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

System is treating Negative Tax Deducts as Tax Reimbursements, and while re-allocating these low Tax Reimbursement amounts at the Tax Code level, it allocates amounts exceeding the available balance, resulting in an Out-of-Balance error

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions to resolve the issue.

Version 2: Pilot to Standard conversion.
