Symptom

iContent Management tool is not refreshing on the first week of the month as per documented on 3664753 - iContent Management Tool FAQ + Rollout Schedule - SAP for Me

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Learning

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access Learning Administration >
  2. Go to "iContent Management Tool" >
  3. Verify the "Last Changed On" >
  4. Confirm that it has not been updated at the first week of each month

Resolution

This scenario is currently being investigated by the SAP Engineering team.
