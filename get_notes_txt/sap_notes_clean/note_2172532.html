Symptom

You see DBIF_RSQL_SQL_ERROR dumps with RSTRFCIC or TRFC_QIN_EXECUTE.
The deadlocks are related to updates on TRFCQIN and TRFCQSTATE tables.

Other Terms

DBIF_RSQL_SQL_ERROR, DEADLOCK, RETRY, RSTRFCIC

Reason and Prerequisites

The update of the RFC status to RECORDED status is committed by report RSTRFCIC much earlier before the RFC is executed, which can result in another process
to pick up the entry for execution.

Solution

Apply the corrections attached.
