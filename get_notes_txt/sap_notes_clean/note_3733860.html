Symptom

  • Is it possible to shorten retention period for already archived data?
  • Attempts to reduce retention period are blocked or may show message as "expiration date conflict" in the ILM store.

Environment

  • SAP Business Technology Platform
  • SAP Basis release independent

Resolution

  1. It is not possible to shorten the minimum retention period for already archived files in SAP ILM. 

  2. Please refer below the legal rules that made it to conclude that SAP ILM store shall not allow for shortening of retention periods.

    SAP’s software has to ensure that the tightest data protection requirements from various countries and industries can be met. Commonly accepted standards and regulations (like OASIS CMIS or SEC Rule 17a-4(f) incl. Interpretive Release No. 34-47806) prohibit explicitly the reduction of once defined retention times. As we need to ensure compliance with these standards and regulations, providing the possibility to shorten retention times of previously created and stored ILM archive files is no option.
    There is the risk that the technical option of shorting the well-defined retention times is rated as compliance violation.
    Currently to our knowledge there is no proved legal requirement enforcing the possibility to shorten a once defined retention time on archived data. In case such a legal requirement will be introduced, we expect that corresponding standards are adjusted to reflect these changes and to ensure clear guidance for all involved parties.

  3. ILM does not allow shortening the retention period once a policy with a longer retention period has been applied.
