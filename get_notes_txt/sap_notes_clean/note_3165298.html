Symptom

You use CKMDUVMAT to post distribution of usage variance.  You encounter the following error:

M2801 Error determining target currency: SOrg & CoCode &

You check the material storage location and found it's enabled with HUM (Handling Unit Management; T001L-XHUPF=X).

Other Terms

M2801, M2, 801, T001L, XHUPF

Reason and Prerequisites

DUV does not support storage locations that are HUM-active because, for such storage locations, the system posts a delivery instead of material document.

Solution

Use the DUV functionality on HUM-inactive storage locations only.  Alternatively, you may use the BADI CKMLDUVN_CHOOSE_RECV to change to another storage location using the SUBSTITUTE_PARAMETERS method (see note 996310).
