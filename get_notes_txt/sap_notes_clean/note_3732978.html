Symptom

The decimals displayed in AW01N and/or asset reports are incorrect from the business point of view.

Environment

  • SAP ERP 
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

Check the displayed decimals in asset reports or AW01N.

Cause

Incorrect customizing.

Resolution

  1. Verify currency decimal customizing for HUF in transaction OY04 (table TCURX) and ensure the currency has the required decimal places.
  2. If customizing is correct but the report still shows incorrect decimals, refer to: KBA 1679279
  3. Re-run the asset report and validate results against AW01N and AS03 for the same fiscal year.

See Also
