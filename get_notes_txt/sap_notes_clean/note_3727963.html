Symptom

I am receiving e-mails from noreply@us.bn.cloud.ariba.com but not sure whether it is a trusted e-mail sender

Environment

SAP Business Network for Logistics

Resolution

After registering for SAP Business Network for Logistics, you will receive several follow-up emails from SAP Business Network.

These emails are sent from noreply@us.bn.cloud.ariba.com. Make sure your email software doesn’t block these email addresses to avoid them going to your SPAM/Junk Mail box.

See Also

For more information regarding SAP Business Network for Logistics please refer to the Help documentation via Getting Started with SAP Business Network for Logistics via New Onboarding Process
