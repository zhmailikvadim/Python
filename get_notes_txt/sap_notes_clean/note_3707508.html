Symptom

Social Insurance Premiums and Income tax cannot be retroactive across years in Japan payroll schema JP00.

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll

Resolution

This is a system design as per SAP Note 1539668 - Single Year Limitation Enhancement for Net Retro for detailed information regarding this design limitation.

Keywords
