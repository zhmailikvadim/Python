Symptom

In the process "Receipt of 55109 from Responsible SUP to DSO" (DE_B_MDC_55109_SUP2DSO_RECV), the system encounters a short dump in step 1600, causing the process to get stuck.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

 MDC, DE_B_MDC_55109_SUP2DSO_RECV, /US4G/CL_DEF_BADI_CHARGES_INFO, EXEC_DATA_FOR_ENFG_REDUCT_CUR, short dump, master data update, ENFG_REDUCTION_PREREQ

Reason and Prerequisites

 The error occurred because of a code issue in step 1600.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system updates the master data successfully, and the process continues without interruption. You need to reverse step 1600 and rerun the process. You can verify the fix by checking that the master data for ENFG_REDUCTION_PREREQ updates successfully and the process continues.
