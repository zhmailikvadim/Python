Symptom

You complete the processing of a serial number group. For some serial numbers of that group a goods receipt is triggered.

The goods receipt fails if the serial number masters exists and a different batches are assigned to the serial numbers.

Other Terms

 IS_REWORK_ORDER, Rework Order, REWORKED_SFI_ID

Reason and Prerequisites

The existing batch of the serial number master is not considered for the automatic goods receipt. If the batches of the serial numbers differ, per batch one material document item has to be created.

This situation typically only occurs for rework orders since for other shop floor orders the serial numbers just get created from the production process and do not have a batch in the corresponding master record, yet.

Implement the correction instruction:

Afterwards the batches of the inidividual serial number master records are considered if no batches are maintained in the corresponding shop floor order header and there will be one material document item per batch.
