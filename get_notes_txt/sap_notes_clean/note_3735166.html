Symptom

When a manual address is passed to BAPI_PR_CREATE, purchase requisition defaults to the plant address and the manual address is ignored when tax jurisdictions are used.

Other Terms

 bapi_pr_create, sapl2105, l2105i03, lcl_bapi_item, address_insert, mm_delivery_address_insert, ls_header-banfn.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the attached program correction.
