Symptom

The Sales and Purchase Report displays dashes and zero values in columns.

Environment

  • SAP Business One
  • SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to Financial → Financial Reports → Accounting → Tax.
  2. Execute the Sales and Purchase Report.
  3. Select Sales Documents.
  4. Observe that the report Sales Ledger displays dashes in most columns. 

Ex: "---- VOID ----" (----ANULAR----) in the Business Partner Name column, and zero values (0,00) in numeric calculation columns. 

Cause

This is system design.

The Sales and Purchase Report is linked to the Folio Numbering function. If documents are not printed to generate a Folio Number, the report fails to display the expected data.

The reason behind is to meet legal requirements in Chile.

In other countries that used the localization CL, like: Colombia, Peru, Bolivia, Ecuador the Folio Numbering is not a legal requirement. However it should be used in order to get the data displayed in the Sales and Purchase Report.

Resolution

  1. Create an invoice.
  2. Print the documents to ensure the Folio Number is generated.
  3. Re-run the 'Sales and Purchase Report' under Financial → Financial Reports → Accounting → Tax.
