Symptom

in FKKINVDOC_disp you reverse an adjustment invoice and you navigate back to the adjustment request. Then reinvoice the adjustment request. The summarization is failing.

Other Terms

FKKKO, condense, posting document, initialisierung, LFKB0FI0, LFKB0TOP

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error: The fkkko-Blart info should always be taken from the current process, not from a buffered one.

Solution

 Implement this SAP Note or the corresponding Support Package.
