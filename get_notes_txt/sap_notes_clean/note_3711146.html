Symptom

Fulfillment RAIs for different RAR contracts cannot be processed due to error message: FARR_RAI707 – Item SDFI/XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX: Changes to invoices and fulfillments are not allowed.

Other Terms

SD Integration Component, SDFI, FARR_RAI707, duplicate SRCDOC_ID, initial VBFA-MJAHR, CL_FARRIC_SD_DELIVERY

Reason and Prerequisites

A delivery and PGI are created for a service material. In this scenario, the material document year (VBFA-MJAHR) can be blank (empty) for service confirmations. As MJAHR is part of the key field SRCDOC_ID, duplicate SRCDOC_ID values may be generated for SDFI RAIs belonging to different deliveries.

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction (CI) or the relevant Support Package.

This correction ensures that, if the material document year (VBFA-MJAHR) is initial, the value is derived from the creation date (ERDAT) of the corresponding VBFA entry as a fallback.
