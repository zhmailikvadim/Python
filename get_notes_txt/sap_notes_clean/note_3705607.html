Symptom

In SAP Business Client, an incorrect language may be applied, if different Fiori Launchpads use different language settings. This issue happens especially when in the Fiori Launchpad the language option “Browser Language” is selected. A typical case of using multiple Fiori Launchpads in one SAP Business Client connection would be the use of SAP Build Workzone and from there opening a second Fiori Launchpad.

Other Terms

SAP Business Client; SAP BC; NWBC; Language; Translation;

Solution

With the patch attached to this SAP Note the language setting of SAP Business Client is not automatically persisted anymore, in case the language was changed due to Fiori Launchpad connection login.

Please install the patch mentioned in section "Support Packages & Patches" or a newer one (SAP Business Client patches are always cumulative).
