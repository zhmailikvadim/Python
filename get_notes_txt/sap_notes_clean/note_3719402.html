Symptom

In the Latest People Profile, the ‘Is Primary’ field in the Contact Information (Phone Information) portlet disappears in edit mode when the browser zoomed out.

Environment

Sap SuccessFactors Employee central

Reproducing the Issue

  • Navigate to the "Contact Information" >click Edit.
  • Set the zoom level to 175% or higher and observe that the "Is Primary" field disappears.
  • Set the zoom level to 150% or lesser and observe that the "Is Primary" field remains visible.

Cause

Resolution

 As per the confirmation from engineering team this issue will be fixed in the 1H 2026 release.
