Symptom

Manual item discounts and BOGO's are being allowed in same transaction.

Environment

SAP Point-of-Sale - All version

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Scan in articles qualifying for BOGO
  2. Select Manual Price Adjust
  3. Select Item Discounts
  4. Select any of the options {% Off Comp Offer, % Off Comp Coupon, $ Off Comp Coupon, $ Off Comp Offer, Match Comp Offer, $ Off MFG Coupon}
  5. Select article to give discount to (competitor if necessary)
  6. Amount or % of discount
  7. Select Total
  8. Both Bogo discount and manual item discount apply

Cause

The cause of this reported issue cannot be identified at this point.

Resolution

At the time of creating this KBA, the resolution is not known. 

The issue is currently under investigation and as soon as additional details are available, this KBA will be updated with the appropriate information.
