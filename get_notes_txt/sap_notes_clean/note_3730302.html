OR

How to map a classic Goods Receipt process with integrated Quality Inspection and Deconsolidation? 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

This is a consulting query on how to model a multi-stage warehouse process; no product defect was reported.

Resolution

  1. Review the “Quality Management in Warehousing (3M0)” scope item documentation: refer to: Quality Management in Warehousing (3M0) – Process Navigator.
  2. Follow the Test Script in the 3M0 scope item to implement multi-stage warehouse processing (including quality inspection and deconsolidation) in the public cloud without POSC.
  3. If further procedural details are required, consult the community discussion addressing this topic: refer to: Representation of multi-stage processes without POSC in SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud (Warehouse Management).
  4. For ongoing how-to questions, follow the guidance to use community channels: refer to: KBA 2973358.
