Symptom

  • After selecting the add-on under "Install or Maintain an Add-on" and clicking "Confirm Selection," the "Next" button remains disabled, preventing further planning activities.
  • The logs display the message: "Next button will be enabled only after further planning activity."

Environment

Maintenance Planning Tools

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open Maintenance Planner.
  2. Select system.
  3. Click on "Plan."
  4. Click on "Install or Maintain an Add-on " and click "Confirm Selection."
  5. Observe that the "Next" button remains disabled, and the logs display "Next button will be enabled only after further planning activity."

Cause

Next button will be enabled only after further planning activity.

Resolution

After uninstallation option has been chosen, select the other planning options like "Plan a Maintenance" to enable the "Next" button and proceed with generating the stack XML file.
