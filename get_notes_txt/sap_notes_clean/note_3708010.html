Symptom

Stock transfer orders have been created along with the label printing orders created from the SAP Fiori app Clinical Supply Orders. The user then cancels the label printing orders and in the SAP Fiori app Manage Label Printing Orders, triggers the creation of new label printing orders. New label printing orders and stock transfer orders are then created. However, the issuing storage location is not updated in the item area of the new stock transfer orders.

Other Terms

ISLS, Manufacturing, Intelligent Clinical Supply Management, Manage Label Printing, Issuing Storage Location

Reason and Prerequisites

You use SAP Intelligent Clinical Supply Management or this SAP Note is a prerequisite of another SAP Note.

Solution

Implement the attached correction.
