Symptom

  • How to implementing e-Invoicing for Malaysia entity in SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud?
  • What are the certified software providers or integration methods to connect SAP with Malaysia’s e-Invoice platform (IRBM / MyInvois)?

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition
  • SAP Document and Reporting Edition, Cloud Edition
 

Resolution

  • To integrate SAP system with the MyTax application in Malaysia, SAP Document and Reporting Compliance, Cloud Edition (DRCce) is a recommended solution. This platform facilitates the submission of electronic invoices to the Inland Revenue Board of Malaysia (IRBM) by integrating your system with SAP Document and Reporting Compliance. Integration | SAP Help Portal
  • Scope item 78L needs to be active for Malaysia eInvoice implementation.

See Also

Refer to SAP Community - Electronic Invoicing in Malaysia.
