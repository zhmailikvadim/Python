Symptom

During assignment of Flexible Material Planning Objects(FMPO) to the Force Element, the minimum and maximum quanity is not calculated correctly based on the assignment quantity.

Other Terms

Manage Flexible Material Planning Objects, Manage Force Elements, Accompanying Parts, Products, FMPO, FE, S/4HANA D&S, S/4HANA Defense & Security

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error. Additional Functionality required.

Post-Implementation Step:
Implement SAP Note 3712392 - S/4HANA Defense and Security: Side Effects corresponding to Minimum Quantity and Maximum Quantity for Accompanying Parts of Flexible Material Planning Objects and Products for the UI fix.

Solution

Data Model Updation: Fields for Minimum Quantity and Maximum Quantity have been added to the Sub‑Assignment tables and Draft tables, and propagated to all corresponding Core Data Services (CDS) views to ensure end‑to‑end data availability and consistency.

Functional Logic Updation: The scaling logic for Minimum Quantity and Maximum Quantity Values of Accompanying Parts Flexible Material Planning Objects (FMPOs) and Products—with respect to the FMPO assigned to Force Element (FE) context—has been implemented for this functionality.
