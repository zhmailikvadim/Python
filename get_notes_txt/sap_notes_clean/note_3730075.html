Symptom

Invoices in SAP Ariba Invoicing do not update the payment clearing status to "Cleared" after payment is completed in S/4HANA. The status remains "Released for Payment" in Manage Supplier Invoices Centrally.

Environment

  • Product: SAP Ariba Invoicing

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Pay a supplier invoice in S/4HANA so that the invoice is fully cleared.
  2. Open the same invoice in SAP Ariba Invoicing (Manage Supplier Invoices Centrally) and observe that the clearing status does not update to Cleared.

Cause

Resolution

  1. Review the required configuration for transferring clearing status from S/4HANA to SAP Ariba Invoicing and ensure it is completed. Refer to: Next‑Gen SAP Ariba Invoicing: required configuration for clearing status.
  2. In S/4HANA, set up and run the referenced job that sends clearing status updates to SAP Ariba Invoicing. Refer to: SAP S/4HANA Cloud: job to send clearing status to SAP Ariba Invocing.
  3. After enabling the job, perform a full synchronization so that previously cleared invoices are updated in SAP Ariba Invoicing.
  4. Verify that newly paid invoices update to Cleared in SAP Ariba Invoicing. If the status still does not update, review the S/4HANA job’s execution logs and consider the behavior described in SAP Note 3575545.

See Also

  • Refer to: SAP S/4HANA Cloud: job to send clearing status to SAP Ariba Invoicing
