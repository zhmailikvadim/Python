Symptom

The OData service for the app Maintain Time-Dependent Stock Levels contains misleading information.

Other Terms

Material Requirement Planning

MRP

ODATA Service  PP_MRP_TIMEDPNDNT_STK

App ID F5726

Reason and Prerequisites

The OData Service contains misleading information.

Solution

The OData Service has been adjusted.

Implement the security note 3494251 (containing explicit information like Symptoms of the vulnerability, Prerequisites, CVSS information, etc) that was released to customers as a supplement to this SAP Note 3482891.
