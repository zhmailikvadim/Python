Symptom

 SAP Customer Checkout point-of-sale, cloud edition 3.0 2503 PL02

Other Terms

Documentation: https://help.sap.com/docs/SAP_CUSTOMER_CHECKOUT_CLOUD

Partner Edge: https://partneredge.sap.com/en/products/sapcc/scccld/about.html

Solution

 SAP Customer Checkout point-of-sale, cloud edition 3.0 2503 PL02 following issues/features have been fixed or added.

SAP Customer Checkout: Price List

  • Symptom: When a customer-, user-, or role-specific price list contains prices that are only valid in the future or the past (based on ValidTo or ValidFrom fields), the article price is set to 0 instead of falling back to the standard price lists.

  • Fix: Resolved the bug that prevented the system from attempting fallback price lists when specific price lists were outside their validity period.

SAP Customer Checkout: US Local Mode

  • Symptom: A rounding amount of $0.01 is incorrectly calculated and displayed on the receipt.

  • Fix: Fixed the logic error in the US local tax mode that caused these rounding issues.

SAP Customer Checkout: TSE/DSFINV-K Export

  • Symptom: Signature configuration was not working correctly, causing broken exports. Additionally, the handling of required fields was malfunctioning.

  • Fix: Export of signature data and the management of mandatory fields are now working as expected.

SAP Customer Checkout/SAP Customer Checkout manager: TSE DSFINV-K Integration & Receipt Archive Service

  • Symptom: For "No-Sale" receipts, the transactionEndTime was returned as null and no signature was displayed in the SAP Customer Checkout manager > Receipts app > Signature tab.

  • Expected Result: The transactionEndTime should be transmitted with the correct end time to the archive service, and the signature should be visible in the SAP Customer Checkout manager Receipts app.

  • Fix: Resolved the issue where the TSE signature remained empty and the transaction end time was null (Archive -> Integration Suite) for no-sale receipts.
