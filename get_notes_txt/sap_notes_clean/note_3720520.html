Symptom

You use BAPI_ACC_DOCUMENT_CHECK or BAPI_ACC_DOCUMENT_POST and submit CpD data with bank details. These bank details contain a SWIFT, but no IBAN (which is not needed for this specific posting). The BAPI aborts processing with message RW602 "Required field IBAN was not transferred in parameter CUSTOMERCPD" even though the bank details are sufficient.

Other Terms

SWIFT, IBAN, RW602, RW 602

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instruction or corresponding support package.
