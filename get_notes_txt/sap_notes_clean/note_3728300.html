Symptom

You use the report Process Confirmations of Compensation Benefits (RPCEEVD0_IN) to process EEL notifications from the health insurance funds (confirmations and reversals (submission reason 88)).

The following example illustrates the situation:

  • You process the confirmation from the health insurance fund for the amount of the compensation benefit (submission reason 71). For the affected personnel case, the system creates a corresponding data record in infotype 0700, subtype DBHE with the data from data module DBHE of the confirmation.

  • The health insurance fund later reverses this confirmation with the submission reason 88 and at the same time also sends a new notification with the submission reason 71 with the corrected information in the data module DBHE.

  • You start the report Process Confirmations of Compensation Benefits (RPCEEVD0_IN).

  • The system processes the reversal and the new notification from the health insurance fund.

  • After processing, the original data, as well as the new data for the affected absence from the confirmations from the health insurance fund, are not saved or no longer saved in infotype 0700, subtype DBHE of the affected personnel case. This is incorrect.

Reason and Prerequisites

This problem is caused by a program error. The system processes the reversal and the new notification of the health insurance fund in the incorrect sequence.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified support package.

After you import the HR Support Package or implement the correction instructions, you do not have to perform any further follow-on activities.
