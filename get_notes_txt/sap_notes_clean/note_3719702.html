Symptom

  • Liquidity items could not be allocated to flows generated during the foreign currency valuated documents FAGL_FCV.
  • The foreign currency valuation process was integrated with Cash Flow Analyzer following SAP notes 2901748 and 2670040 .

Environment

One Exposure

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Implement SAP notes 2901748 and 2670040 to integrate the foreign currency valuation process with Cash Flow Analyzer.
  2. Attempt to allocate liquidity items to flows generated during the foreign currency valuation process.
  3. Observe that liquidity items are not assigned despite following the recommended steps.

Cause

The liquidity item assignment based on the V_FCLM_FCV_LQ table does not work for documents stored in BSEG, as the table is designed to derive liquidity items only for foreign currency valuation documents stored in ACDOCA with BSTAT = U.

Resolution

Note: Ensure that the foreign currency valuation documents are stored in ACDOCA with BSTAT = U for liquidity item assignment based on the V_FCLM_FCV_LQ table as per Note 2901748.

The Foreign currency valuated document FAGL_FCV  would be visible in the Cash Flow Analyzer only when using the local currency amount view. 
Please refer below the explanations on how it works:

  •  Foreign currency evaluation is done only for bank account, not for non-bank account such as clearing account. Another way to put it: unless you make a payment from your bank account there     is no foreign currency evaluation that comes into picture.
  • The unrealized currency gain/loss is not related to Flow Builder/FQM_FLOW. The data is retrieved from the view PUNREALZCRCYDIFF.                                                                     
  • If the G/L account is not a bank account it won't be displayed on CFA app irrespective of the amounts.
