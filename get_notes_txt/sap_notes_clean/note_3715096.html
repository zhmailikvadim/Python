Symptom

Log with Handle &1 (Param. I_PROTOCOL_HANDLE) does not exist.

This error can also occur when you execute the program PPARCHD1 in transaction SE38 or SA38.
When you archive the process order, this problem may also occur in the deletion run (PRARCHD1).
