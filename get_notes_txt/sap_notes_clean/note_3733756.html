Symptom

This SAP Note provides you with updates for the SAP_BR_GL_REPORTING_US role template for sales and use tax for the United States.

Currently, you cannot access analytical reports using this template.

Other Terms

Document and Reporting Compliance, DRC US, SAP_BR_GL_REPORTING_US, role template, CDS view, analytical report, sales and use tax, United States

Reason and Prerequisites

You have implemented SAP Note 3684090 - DRC U.S. - Sales and Use Tax: Embedded Analytics.

Solution

After implementing this SAP Note, the SAP_BR_GL_REPORTING_US role template is updated with new CDS views. As a result, you can access the analytical reports for sales and use tax for the United States.

You need to update all custom roles that were created using the SAP_BR_GL_REPORTING_US role as a reference.
