Symptom

Source of supply doesn't show supplier name, but only show supplier ID in My Purchase Requisition app.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

Missing authorization for "Supplier Account Group".

Resolution

Please allow read/write authorization for 'Supplier Account Group' for the business role.
