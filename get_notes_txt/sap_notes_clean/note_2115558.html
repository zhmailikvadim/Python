Symptom

This SAP Note provides you with the Customizing activities that are relevant to the localization of countries for HR Renewal 2.0 feature pack 3.

Other Terms

  • Business Function HCM_LOC_CI_83
  • IMG HR Renewal Belgium (BE)
  • IMG HR Renewal Denmark (DK)
  • IMG HR Renewal Great Britain Public Sector (GB)
  • IMG HR Renewal Italy (IT)
  • IMG HR Renewal Japan (JP)
  • IMG HR Renewal Kuwait (KW)
  • IMG HR Renewal Norway (NO)
  • IMG HR Renewal NPO (UN)
  • IMG HR Renewal Portugal (PT)
  • IMG HR Renewal Qatar (QA)

Solution

The following Customizing activities are available on your system. To switch them on, activate business function HCM_LOC_CI_83 in transaction SFW5.

Country                                                                 Path in transaction SIMGH
Belgium Personnel & Organization - Belgium -> Infotypes for Country Version for Belgium
Denmark Personnel & Organization - Denmark -> Infotypes for Country Version for Denmark
Great Britain Public Sector Personnel & Organization - Great Britain Public Sector -> Infotypes for Country Version Great Britain Public Sector
Italy Personnel & Organization - Italy -> Infotypes for Country Version for Italy
Japan Personnel & Organization - Japan -> Infotypes for Country Version for Japan
Kuwait Personnel & Organization - Kuwait -> Infotypes for Country Version for Kuwait
Norway Personnel & Organization - Norway -> Infotypes for Country Version for Norway
NPO Personnel & Organization - NPO -> Infotypes for Country Version for NPO
Portugal Personnel & Organization - Portugal -> Infotypes for Country Version for Portugal
Qatar Personnel & Organization - Qatar -> Infotypes for Country Version for Qatar

To get these changes within your system, apply the HR Support Package(s) indicated for your release in the Reference to Support Packages section of this SAP Note.
