Symptom

Dependent requirements are not confirmed and appear with a confirmed quantity of 0 in transaction CO09.

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4 HANA

Cause

Dependent requirements are typically associated with production orders or planned orders and are confirmed through the execution of an ATP check for these orders. If these orders were not confirmed the dependent requirement will be visible with 0 confirmed quantity in transaction CO09. 

The confirmed quantity in co09 is directly coming from RESB-VMENG.

Resolution

  1. Access transaction MD04, enter the material and plant, and identify the relevant production order or planned order numbers.

  2. Access transaction COHV and execute mass processing for the identified orders to perform the ATP check.

However, the dependent requirements are not getting confirmed even after running COHV and mass processing.
