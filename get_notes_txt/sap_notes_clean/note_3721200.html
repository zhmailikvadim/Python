Symptom

During the supplier quotation approval process, the cost center and GL account are not displayed on the approval screen, requiring approvers to navigate through multiple screens to find the cost center maintained in the purchase requisition.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the supplier quotation approval screen in the Manage Purchase Requisition Professional App.
  2. Attempt to locate the cost center information directly on the approval screen.
  3. Observe that the cost center is not displayed and requires navigation through multiple screens to find it within the purchase requisition.

Cause

This is currently a functionality which is unavailable.

Resolution

This functionality is currently unavailable in the standard system.

See Also

Refer to: Displaying a Cost Center

Keywords
