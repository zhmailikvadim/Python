Symptom

This SAP Note provides the following enhancements to align with the latest adjustments in the Golden Tax System III.

  • The calculation formula of cumulative deductions for normal salary and continuous remuneration for providing services (intern) is now adjusted as follows: 
    • Cumulated standard exemption for current period = standard exemption for current period + cumulated standard exemption for the last period 
    • The field Tax Entry Date in IT0531 is used to address the above-mentioned requirements.
  • Change to tax calculation of special additional deduction (SAD) when prefiling the deductions
    • The system now uses the reimbursement month of supplementary payments rather than employee termination month to calculate the cumulative tax deductions when the following conditions are met:
      • Employee termination month is before the tax period when the payment is made.
      • The salaries are paid retroactively after employees leave the company.
      • The reimbursement month of supplementary payments is the same as the tax period when the payment is made.
    • The wage type /4K* is recommended to be used to cover the above-mentioned requirements.
  • A new subapplication Adjustment of tax accumulation 2026 (TACM) is introduced in the Validity Interval for Statutory Subapplications (V_T596C) view. The new solution is applicable for use when the subapplication is active. The default begin date of this subapplication is set as 20260201.

For more information about case analysis, you can refer to the Solution_Reference_3710991.pdf attachment.

Note:

  1. The State Taxation Administration has not yet issued official upgrade notes or a new operation manual concerning these changes. However, the tax bureau system has implemented the change in the Individual Income Tax Withholding System.
  2. This information is based on legislation and regulations that could be subject to change at any time by the respective authorities. The information will be updated if required.

Other Terms

Cumulative Deduction, SAD, CRPS, IT0531, Tax Entry Date

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a legal change.

Solution

Do either of the following:

  • Import the relevant Support Package into your system.
  • Carry out the manual activities described in the attachment ManualSteps3710991.pdf. Then implement the attached correction instructions in your system.
