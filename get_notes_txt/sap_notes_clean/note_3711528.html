Symptom

When a Customer JIS call request received from the OEM is cancelled, the corresponding forwarded JIS call must also be cancelled; and the cancellation information must be transmitted to the Tier‑2 supplier.

Other Terms

JIT S2P, forwarded JIS call, OEM, JIS, JIT, Tier 2 Supplier, cancel

Reason and Prerequisites

Advance Development.

Solution

Implement the Correction Instructions of the Note or import the relevant Support Package to adjust the system behaviour.
