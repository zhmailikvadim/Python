Symptom

In the process "New Installation for Feed-In (DSO View)" (DE_B_MPES_NIN_DSO), an error occurs at step 0500-0190 when generating the supply scenario. The embedded process "Trigger of Master Data Generation (DSO View)" (DE_B_MPES_TRIG_MD_GENERATION_DSO) determines the business partner in step 0500-0100 but doesn't generate it.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

MPES, New Installation for Feed-In, Start of Supply for Feed-In, DE_B_MPES_DSO_SOS_REG, /US4G/RP_TRIGGER_GPKE_NIN, /US4G/RP_TRIGGER_GPKE_SOS, supply scenario generation, master data generation, trigger report

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because the system determined the business partner in step 0500-0100 of the embedded process but didn't generate a new business partner.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system generates a new business partner in step 0500-0100, similar to the process "New Installation for Electricity (DSO View)". You can verify the fix by triggering the process using the trigger report /US4G/RP_TRIGGER_GPKE_NIN or /US4G/RP_TRIGGER_GPKE_SOS and checking that the business partner is generated successfully and the supply scenario generation is completed.
