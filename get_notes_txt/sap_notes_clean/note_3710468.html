Symptom

You are an Australian customer that has additional security requirements for support and believe that in order to manage risk you have restrictions around locations for SAP support staff accessing your systems. 

This note applies to on-premise solutions only. 

For questions on SAP cloud solutions please contact your SAP Account Representative.

Cause

You are subject to additional Australian cybersecurity regulatory regimes, such as under the Security of Critical Infrastructure Act 2018 (SOCI Act), Foreign Investment Review Board (FIRB) conditions and/or the Australian Government Information Security Manual.

Resolution

As at the date of this KBA, the current cybersecurity regulatory regimes above do not restrict 

  1. the provision of software support from locations outside Australia or 
  2. SAP support staff accessing customer systems (where appropriate and justified). Similarly, the above cybersecurity regulatory regimes are not prescriptive as to the manner in which particular risks are addressed.

 Under the standard support and license arrangements for on premise SAP systems, restriction on the geo-location of support is not included. Remote access to your systems is required to allow SAP support to assist to resolve your incidents if they are classified as High or Very High. This remote connection is controlled and supervised by you as the system owner, allowing for mitigation of potential risk associated with the remote access arrangement. See the related SAP notes at the end of this KBA. 

If you choose to manage risk in this way (i.e. imposition of location restrictions or not accepting remote connection) you may experience much longer times to respond to and resolve incidents raised with SAP as you will have elected to wait longer for processors from a different time zone or country to provide you with support. Support responses for incidents that are subject to these additional restrictions may be outside the scope of Service Level Agreements in the standard support contract. This includes Business Down situations.

Consider engaging consulting services to assist with determining the best approach to manage your risks for your on-premise SAP solution and to determine your best option in a risk-based approach for obtaining support from SAP in a critical situation.

 Consider moving to SAP sovereign cloud arrangement to better manage your risks or engage with SAP’s CI team through your Account Representative to discuss risk mitigation options as part of SOCI Act’s shared responsibility model. Please contact your SAP Account Representative for more information on SAP sovereign cloud.
