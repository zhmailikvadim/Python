Symptom

Function CIF_ADC_TRANSMIT_IPPE_IS gives a syntax error saying the field PPET_TRUE is unknown.

Other Terms

PPET, CIF_ADDON

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisite:
You run on ECC-DIMP 604 or higher and SAP Note 3100323 or corresponding SP has been implemented in your system.

Program error:
Incorrect usage of constant PPET_TRUE (Type Group PPET) in CIF-related functionality.

Solution

Implement this note or corresponding support pack of your ECC-DIMP release.
