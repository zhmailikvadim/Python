Symptom

When confirmation details are entered for the first time under the Confirmation Control tab, the Item change log does not save "old” and “new” values. The action in the item change log and CDPOS shows only “entered”. 

Environment

  • Materials Management (MM) 
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S4 HANA  

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the purchase order (PO). 
  2. On the Confirmations tab in the item details, verify that the supplier has confirmed the order and that the delivery details are present.
  3. However, in the Change Log, the initial entry for delivery details only appears as "Entered" under the Action column; there are no "Old" and "New" values recorded.
  4. When confirmation details are modified a second time, the Change Log correctly records both the old and new values.

Cause

When confirmation details are populated for the first time, the system treats the operation as an Insert (change indicator 'I') rather than an Update. In CDPOS this is recorded with the 'I' indicator, and for Insert operations the system does not capture old and new values. Only when the change indicator is 'U' (Update) does CDPOS record both the old and new values. 

Resolution

This behavior is standard and aligns with the system's expected functionality. 

Keywords
