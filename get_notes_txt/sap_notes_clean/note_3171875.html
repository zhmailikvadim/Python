Symptom

I receive the following error message when uploading a comma-separated values (CSV) invoice:
WARNING: The uploaded CSV uses a deprecated format. Please upgrade to the latest version.

Cause

Your buyer has updated the CSV template, but you are importing a CSV invoice file using an old CSV template version.

Resolution

Steps to Download the Latest CSV Template for Invoice Submission:

  1. Sign in to the SAP Business Network.
  2. In the upper-right corner, click [...]
  3. Click Templates
  4. Under Current Customers, click your customer's name in the Customer column.
  5. Check the type of document
  6. Click Download.



Keywords
