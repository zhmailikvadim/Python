Symptom

Symptom

You are using the Retail Store Fiori application Receive Products and you want to post a trusted goods receipt for handling units assigned to an outbound delivery and you are facing an error.

Other Terms

Fiori, Retail Store, Receive Products, Goods Receipt, Outbound Delivery, Posting, BAPI_GOODSMVT_CREATE, Trusted Posting

Reason and Prerequisites

Wrong parameter type for parameter "goodsmvt_print_ctrl" of function module BAPI_GOODSMVT_CREATE.

Solution

Implement this note or the respective support package.

 

If you do not give any date in this field, the last day of the reporting month will be displayed in the column 'Date of Payment' on the detail page of the PDF form. But in the text file, the payment date is always the pay date in the payroll results.

It may cause inconsistency of the PDF form and the text file.

Other Terms

HTHCTXF1

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a correction of legal function

Solution

After applying this note, if you give a date in the field 'Date of Payment' on the selection screen, this date will be displayed in the column 'Date of Payment' on the detail page of the PDF form. If you do not give any value in this field, the pay date from payroll result will be displayed on the PDF form.

As for the text file, the logic stays unchanged, the payment date is always retrieved from the payroll result.

To get the correction, apply the HR support package specified in the note.

Alternatively, you can apply the correction instruction attached to the note.
