Symptom

When you try to navigate to the Multi-Level Material Simulation for a component using the link in the inspector in F4450 - Simulation Components, the app does not load and you are presented with a communication error.

Other Terms

F4450, Simulation Components, Multi-Level Material Simulation, navigation, inspector, communication error, malformed syntax, pmrp, predictive mrp

Reason and Prerequisites

This is caused by incorrect handling of material numbers with special characters.

Solution

This note ensures that special characters in material numbers are encoded correctly when navigating.
