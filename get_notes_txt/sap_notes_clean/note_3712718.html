Symptom

The /LSCH/ DRF entries are deleted when you upgrade from lower version of ISLS addon to ISLS-400 SP2 or even higher(e.g. ISLS-500), the DRF business objects cannot be replicated automatically to BRH anymore.

Other Terms

BRH, DRF, ISLS 400 SP2

Reason and Prerequisites

In the release of ISLS-400 SP2, the /LSCH/ DRF Switch BC sets are deleted on the basis that they should be copied only if the switch /LSCH/BF_BRH is activated, this is to keep the system clean when the component is not in use(especially after the merging of BRH, ICSM and CGTO into one single addon).

Solution

You need to reactivate the Switch BC sets following the steps:

1. Execute t-code sfw_browser
2. Locate to
/LSCH/BF_BRH

/LSCH/SW_BRH_MAIN

/LSCH/SWITCH_ENH

Switch BC Sets

/LSCH/SBC_IDSTC_TEXT
/LSCH/SBC_SET_DRF_ID_CODE
/LSCH/SBC_SET_DRF
/LSCH/SBC_OUTB_CFG
/LSCH/SBC_SET_DRF_OTC
/LSCH/SBC_SET_DRF_BS
/LSCH/SBC_SET_DRF_ATTR
/LSCH/SBC_SET_DRF_BO
/LSCH/SBC_SET_DRFVC_IMPL

3. Double click on the SBC set
4. Set "Activation Options-Ignore Previous Activations" to Clicked-On
5. Click "Activate Switch BC Set" button
6. Repeat 3-5 for all the Switch BC sets listed above.
