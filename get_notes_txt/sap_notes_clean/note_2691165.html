Symptom

Invoices with non-digital series are shown under the protocol Electronic Invoice for Spain in the Electronic Document Monitor.

Reproducing the issue

Prerequisites:

  • You are working in the Spain (ES) localization.
  • You have activated Immediate Information Supply reporting via Administration → System Initialization → Company Details → tab/Accounting Data.
  • In Document Settings, the Default Generation Type field is set to Generate, and in Business Partner Master Data → tab/Electronic Documents, it is set to either Generate or Not relevant.
  • You have set up the IIS according to SAP Note 2494985 and the how-to guide attached to the note.

Scenario:

  1. Create an A/R Invoice with a non-digital series.
  2. Go to Immediate Information Supply, and send the A/R Invoice created in step 1.
  3. Check in the Electronic Document Monitor for both protocols.

Expected behavior:

The A/R Invoice is displayed only under the protocol Electronic invoice for Immediate information supply.

Actual behavior:

The A/R Invoice is displayed under both protocols: Electronic invoice for Spain and Electronic invoice for Immediate information supply.

Solution

SAP intends to provide a patch or patches in order to solve the problem described. The section References below will list the specific patches once they become available. Any corresponding Info files of the patches in SAP Service Marketplace will also show the SAP Note number. Be aware that these references can only be set at patch release date. SAP will deliver patches only for selected releases at its own discretion, based on the business impact and the complexity of the implementation.
