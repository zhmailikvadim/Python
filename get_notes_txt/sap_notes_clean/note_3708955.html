Symptom

You want to change partner functions for several suppliers during a mass change.

This can sometimes lead to error messages such as CVI_EI 038 - "Partner role with key &1 &2 does not exist."

Other Terms

MDGS, MDG-S

SAP MDG for Suppliers

Partner Functions

WYT3

CVI_EI 038  CVI_EI038

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

 Apply the attached correction instructions or the related support package to the affected system(s).
