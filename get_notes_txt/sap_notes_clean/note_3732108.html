Symptom

The business user wants to filter Agreements based on filters Created on but it is not working as expected

Other Terms

CRMS4C_RF_REPDY ,CREATED_AT, CHANGED_AT

Reason and Prerequisites

Due to incorrect configuration

Solution

1. Please launch SM34 and in View Cluster put CRMS4C_RF_REPDY.

2. Click Find Maintenance Catalog, click OK in the popup.

3. Click Display

4. Click Edit and change the 2 entries as following:

CREATED_AT    <CDS_VIEW>~CREATED_AT_H

CHANGED_AT    <CDS_VIEW>~CHANGED_AT_H

to

CREATED_AT    <CDS_VIEW>~CREATED_AT

CHANGED_AT    <CDS_VIEW>~CHANGED_AT

5. Save
