Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Brazil.

If you have SAP Oil and Gas (IS-OIL) installed on your system, you need to apply also SAP Note 3732340 (listed in Causes - Side Effects Section) to avoid any inconsistencies.

Solution

Solution Description

After you implement this SAP Note, you can update the IPI tax rate with 4 decimal places through the Change Tax Rate 4 Decimal Places (BADI_J1B_TX_RATE4DEC) BAdI in purchasing, goods movement, and sales processes.

Change Tax Rate 4 Decimal Places BAdI:

By implementing the Change IPI Tax Rate 4 Decimal Places (CHANGE_IPI_RATE) BAdI method, you define the IPI tax rate with 4 decimal places, according to your business logic.

To provide the IPI tax rate, fill the tax_rate_4dec field using the values available in the tax_rate_4dec_parameters structure to define the rules.
