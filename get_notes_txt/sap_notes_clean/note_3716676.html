Symptom

  • In the section under the user's name, only the job title is displayed.
  • A comma (",") appears after the job title.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to the offboarding module.
  2. Select a user.
  3. Observe that only the job title with a comma (",") is displayed under the user's name after Job title.

Cause

We do show location after the comma but here user looks like doesn't have access so it stopped after the comma. this cannot be configured

Resolution

This is a minor UI fix will be resolved by ENG Team in future releases.
