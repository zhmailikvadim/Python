Symptom

You notice that the Export button in the Case work list is not visible by the agent.

Environment

SAP Service Cloud Version 2.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Logon to your SAP Service Cloud Version 2 environment.
  2. Navigate to Cases work list.
  3. See the button to Export the cases is not displayed.

Cause

This is the system expected behavior when the role which the agent is assigned does not have the necessary business services.

Resolution

It would be required to ensure the agent business role has the following business service enabled:

  • Business Service ID: sap.crm.service.dataImpexService
  • Business Service Name: dataImpexService
