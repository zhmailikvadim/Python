Symptom

Currently, contract authorizations are restricted to general activity levels such as create, display or change. There is no provision to control authorizations at a granular level for specific pricing-related actions within contracts.

As a result, users with change authorization can perform all pricing actions without restriction, which does not meet business requirements for controlled access.

The requirement is to introduce enhanced authorization control that enables restriction of individual pricing actions during contract creation and change processes. These actions include:

  • Price Component
  • Price Component with CDOTE
  • Lift with Equity
  • Lift without Equity
  • Lift with CDOTE
  • Roll
  • Roll with CDOTE
  • Cancel with Equity
  • Cancel without Equity
  • Cancel with CDOTE

Additionally, the list of controllable actions should be consistent with the actions available in the following applications and processes:

  • Manage ACM Contracts
  • Process ACM Price Lots
  • Contract Create, Contract Change, and Copy with Reference APIs

This enhancement is required to ensure finer authorization control aligned with business-specific pricing operations.

New functionality.
