Symptom

Why is supplier business activity count not visible to all users in Supplier 360° profile?

Environment

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in with a user having a basic role.
  2. Navigate to the Supplier Management tab.
  3. Search for a supplier.
  4. Observe that the count of sourcing and contract projects is not displayed in the Business Activity tiles for Sourcing and Contracts.

Resolution

The visibility restriction is due to Ariba's design, which limits access to Business Activity tiles for Sourcing and Contracts to users with permissions for the respective Sourcing Projects or Contracts.

You also have the option to submit an enhancement request to propose enabling all users to view the Business Activity tiles for Sourcing and Contracts tiles in the 360° Profile, regardless of their role within the projects.
