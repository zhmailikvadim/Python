Symptom

I am unable to send a Trading Relationship Request (TRR) when I search for a supplier under Search for Suppliers as Request Relationship button is greyed out.

Resolution

To establish a trading relationship with a supplier on SAP Business Network, follow the below steps:

1. Login to your SAP Business Network account.
2. On to the Home page you will find a search box with a filter as Trading partners.
3. Search with the supplier ANID/ supplier name in the search box.
4. Click on the supplier name from the search results.
5. On the top right corner select the drop down under Request for Information button.
6. Click Invite.
