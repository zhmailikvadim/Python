Symptom

In the Process Purchase Requisitions Centrally application, users create Purchase Orders from Purchase Requisitions using Expert Mode.

Two key issues exist in Expert Mode PO creation:

1. Limit Items Display & Mapping Issue:

Both Standard Items and Limit Items are displayed under the same Items facet in the UI. This causes incorrect PO creation behaviour.

2. Order Price Unit Field Mapping Issue:

The Order Price Unit field from PR is not being correctly transferred to the PO item. Due to this missing mapping, the PO interprets the item as “Free of Charge.”

Other Terms

CL_MMPUR_EXPERT_MODE_PO, PAYLOAD_STRUCTURE, GET_PO_DETAIL, POST_PO_DETAILS, CL_I_PO_HEADER_ITEM_FIELDS, F3290, F3292.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Corressponding connected system changes are in note 3663401.

Solution

Implement the note.
