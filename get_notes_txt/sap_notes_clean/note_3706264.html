Symptom

Manager Form Eligibility rules excludes inactive or terminated users to be in the worksheet but they are still showing up.

These ineligible inactive or terminated users are NOT in the Employee History page.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Variable Pay

Cause

When applying the eligibility rule, the "Inactive" checkbox under "Exclude the following employees from the eligibility rule application." is checked. This will include the inactive or terminated users.

Resolution

Reapply the eligibility rule, ensuring the "Inactive" checkbox under "Exclude the following employees from the eligibility rule application." is unchecked.

Keywords

inactive, terminated, ineligible users, variable pay ineligible, manager form eligibility
