Symptom

The purpose of this page is to provide a brief overview of Open Enrollment Mentoring Programs and to act as a bridge to any content related to this feature.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Career Development and Mentoring

Resolution

Open enrollment programs allow you to create simple programs that are open to all employees, or to a subgroup that you define.  Some of the benefits of an Open Enrollment Program include:

  • No invitations are required
  • Employees with access can see and sign up for the program at any time.
  • Users can sign up as both a mentor and a mentee in the same Open Enrollment program.
