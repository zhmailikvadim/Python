Symptom

For the process step Register Offset (calculator RU) you want to customize the offsetting needs depending on characteristics of the contracts via the view cluster /BA1/VCBR_CLM_DRV_REG_OFFSET. Reason and Prerequisites

The Offsetting Needs in Register (TV) depending on the accounting methodology is set to "0 Undefined" and have the customizing view cluster available via the note 3628665.
