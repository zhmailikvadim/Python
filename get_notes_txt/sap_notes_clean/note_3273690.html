Symptom

Previously, custom fields for header-level withholding tax lines were not available in simple non-PO invoices in guided buying.

Resolution

Now, if you have standard custom fields for header-level withholding tax lines in SAP Ariba Buying and Invoicing, SAP Ariba can apply an additional customization to display the fields in simple non-PO invoices in guided buying.

In guided buying, the custom fields are shown in the popup for managing details of header tax lines in simple non-PO invoices. You can also have a customization to hide the Amount column in the popup, if needed.

Note: This fix is applicable on guided buying sites that support simple non-PO invoices but not invoice editing and approval, that is, when the guided parameter ENABLE_NON_PO_INVOICE is set to Yes and the parameter ENABLE_EDIT_AND_APPROVAL_OF_INVOICES is set to No.

Customization Details (for SAP Ariba Use)

A new template, BuyerRestAPITaxLineTemplateField, determines whether a custom field belongs at the withholding tax line level. This template works similarly to the template BuyerRestAPIShowInTopSectionTemplateField.

Apply the new template to the InvoiceLineItem custom tax details field you want to modify.

Example:

<inClass name="ariba.invoicing.core.InvoiceLineItem">

<field name="cus_RetentionBase" reportable="true">

<type class="ariba.basic.core.Money"/>

<templates>

<template name="MoneyField" operator="add"/>

<template name="TaxDetailsField" operator="add"/>

<template name="BuyerRestAPITemplateField" operator="add"/>

<template name="BuyerRestAPITaxLineTemplateField" operator="add"/>

</templates>

</field>

</inClass>
