Symptom

In Commodity Swap deals (Product Category 810), it is not possible to manually assign or change the Profit Center.

In OBO scenarios, the intercompany deal created in the exposure company also does not allow manual Profit Center changes.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA

Cause

Profit Center is automatically derived based on:

  • Organizational structure

  • Valuation configuration

  • Account determination settings

Manual selection at deal level is not supported, as this ensures:

  • Consistent hedge accounting postings

  • Correct valuation results

  • Proper alignment between exposure and hedge

In OBO, the intercompany deal is a system-generated mirror deal and does not allow manual changes by design.

Resolution

Behavior is standard and by design.

Keywords
