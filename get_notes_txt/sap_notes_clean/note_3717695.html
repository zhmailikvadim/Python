Symptom

  • User reported receiving email notifications for every update made to the workspace.
  • How to set frequency of email notifications in Work Zone workspace.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

  • SAP Build Work Zone, Advanced Edition

  • SAP SuccessFactors Work Zone

Resolution

Email notifications frequency settings are user-defined and not enforced by the system or affected by system upgrades.

User can configure it following the steps below:

  1. Open the workspace
  2. Go to the 3 dots on the top right of the page
  3. Click on 'Email Notifications' and adjust the frequency as wished:

NOTE: There is audit report available for changes to email notification settings, as these are not considered security or high-impact changes.
