Symptom

How do I display the ERP invoice number on the SAP Business Network invoice page as buyer for a supplier-initiated invoice?

Resolution

For supplier-initiated invoices, if the buyer wants the ERP invoice number to be visible on the SAP Business Network invoice page, the ERP invoice number must be sent through a StatusUpdateRequest.

The buyer system must send a StatusUpdateRequest for the invoice and include the ERP invoice number in the Comments field. Once the message is processed, the ERP invoice number will appear as a comment in the History tab of the invoice on SAP Business Network.

Currently, this is the only supported method to transmit and display the ERP invoice number when the invoice is supplier-initiated and the buyer wants the ERP invoice number to be visible in SAP Business Network.

Below is the StatusUpdateRequest format that can be used to pass the ERP invoice number:

<cXML payloadID="XXX_XX_XXX" timestamp="YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss+TZ">
  <Request>
    <StatusUpdateRequest>
      <DocumentReference payloadID="XXXX"/>
      <Status xml:lang="EN" code="200" text="OK"/>
      <InvoiceStatus paymentNetDueDate="YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss+TZ" type="processing">
        <InvoiceIDInfo invoiceID="MASKED_INVOICE_ID" invoiceDate="YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ss+TZ"/>
        <Comments xml:lang="EN">
          ERPInvoiceNumber=XXXXXXXXXX
        </Comments>
      </InvoiceStatus>
    </StatusUpdateRequest>
  </Request>
</cXML>
