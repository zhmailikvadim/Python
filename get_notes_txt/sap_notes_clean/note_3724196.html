Symptom

 In the process "Receipt of Deregistration Due to End of Supply for Gas (SUP View)" (DE_B_GELIGAS_DEREG_EOS_SUP), an error occurred in step 0900 (Update Supply Scenario) when the same supplier was assigned to both the current and future timeslices.

Other Terms

GPKE_SUP, Update Supply Scenario, DE_B_GELIGAS_DEREG_EOS_SUP, 2310, EOS_MALO_SUSC_UPD_FUTURE, supply scenario data, timeslice, deregistration, end of supply, gas

Reason and Prerequisites

 Program Error

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system updates the supply scenario correctly in step 0900 without errors. The BC sets /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP07_NOTE_3724196, /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP08_NOTE_3724196, and /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP00_NOTE_3724196 are available for implementation.
