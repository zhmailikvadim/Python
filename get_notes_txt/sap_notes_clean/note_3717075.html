Symptom

When creating a field completion rule and the option "Include Keyword or Phrase" is checked or enabled, the keyword or phrase used is not showing in Recruiting Posting field. 

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Posting

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Setup a Field completion rule that will extract the field values from a job requisition depending on the position of the keyword or phrase. 
  2. Check the "Include keyword or Phrase" on the field completion rule and save. 
  3. Try to post a job and check the Recruiting posting field where the field completion has been configured. 
  4. The Keyword or Phrase use is not included on the extract field value

Resolution

The fix for the issue will be delivered on March 23rd in both Preview and Production. Click on star to bookmark this article in order to receive updates about this issue.
