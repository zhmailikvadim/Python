Symptom

  • The fact sheet details page lists a different amount of fact sheets than in the inventory.
  • The discrepancy is due to inheritance, where additional fact sheets are shown because they are linked to lower leveled fact sheets that are associated with the selected fact sheet.

Fact sheet details page:

Inventory filter page:

Environment

SAP LeanIX Inventory search/filtering

Cause

  • The filtering mechanism in LeanIX inventory applies non-strict filtering, which includes fact sheets that are related on a lower level (as a child) to the filtered fact sheet.

Resolution

  1. Currently, there is no direct solution within the LeanIX graphical interface to enable strict filtering.
  2. There is a roadmap idea which can be voted on to include strict filtering for the inventory.
  3. Refer to the roadmap idea for future updates: a href="https://roadmap.leanix.net/c/505-filtering-toggle-between-strict-and-non-strict-filtering-on-relations" filtering: toggle between strict and non-strict filtering on relations /a.
