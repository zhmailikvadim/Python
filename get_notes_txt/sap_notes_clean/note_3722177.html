Symptom

When does the Contract Minimum Commitment Not Met email notification get sent to users on the Contract's Notification List and Edit List?

Cause

The Minimum Commitment notification is triggered by a scheduled task, which runs automatically on weekdays at midnight. The notification is designed to alert users when a contract is approaching its expiration date but the organization has not spent enough against the contract to meet the agreed minimum commitment with the supplier.

The notification is sent when ALL of the following conditions are met:

  1. The contract is in Open state.
  2. The contract has a Minimum Commitment amount configured.
  3. The Minimum Available Amount Notification Days field is configured (specifies how many days before expiration to send the notification).
  4. The contract has an Expiration Date set.
  5. The number of days until expiration is less than or equal to the configured Minimum Available Amount Notification Days.
  6. The Used Amount on the contract is less than the notification threshold, which is calculated as:
    • Minimum Commitment × (Minimum Available Amount Notification Percent / 100)
  7. The notification has not already been sent for this contract (only sent once per contract).
  8. Contract Minimum Commitment Not Met notification is configured accordingly in the Email Notification Manager. 

Example: If a contract has a $100,000 minimum commitment, 80% notification percent, and 30 notification days with an expiration date of April 1, 2026, then on or after March 2, 2026, if the used amount is below $80,000, the notification will be sent to all users on the Edit List and Notification List.

Resolution

To receive Minimum Commitment notifications, ensure the following fields are configured on the contract:

  • Minimum Commitment (Min Amount) – The minimum purchase amount agreed with the supplier
  • Minimum Available Amount Notification Percent – The percentage of the minimum commitment that must be reached to avoid the notification (e.g., 80% means if less than 80% is used, the notification will be sent)
  • Minimum Available Amount Notification Days – The number of days before the contract expiration date when the system should check and send the notification
  • Expiration Date – The contract's expiration date
  • Notification List / Edit List – Users or groups who should receive the notification

In addition to above ensure Contract Minimum Commitment Not Met notification is configured accordingly in the Email Notification Manager.
