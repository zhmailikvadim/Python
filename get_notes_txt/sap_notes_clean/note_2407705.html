Symptom

With SAP Notes 2337448and 2337463, the function was provided that periodic due dates can be retained after the end of the inpayment agreement. After you activate the function, after the end of term, the prices or billing items may no longer be created if the loan temporarily has a balance of zero (payoff amount = 0). This problem occurs because after you activate the function, the periods are no longer split for each due date. As a result, prices may also be calculated at a later point in time. As a result, the calculation of the cash flow must no longer be stopped if the function 'Retain due date after the end of the inpayment agreement' has been activated.

Other Terms

F_KEEP_DATES_AFTER_EOT

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified Support Package.

If you use regular conditions with a fixed condition amount (for example, an account maintenance charge with a fixed amount) or a percentage share of a fixed calculation base (for example, a charge with a 0.5% of the contract capital), then you must use a suitable condition determination to ensure that the calculation of the conditions ends. You can do this, for example, by making the following entries on the 'Condition determination' tab page:

  • Period of use
  • Determination period
  • Calculation base: Here you should select a calculation base that is zero at the end of the term.

If you make these entries, on the 'Calculation' tab page in the field 'Levels (ConDet)', you can select the entry 'Levels for Condition Determination', and then maintain the levels.
