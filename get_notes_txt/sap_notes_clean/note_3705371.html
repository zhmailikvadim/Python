Symptom

Infotype Absence Scheme Override (0572) enables the user to override the absence scheme rules they have previously configured at the employee level.

Using this infotype, the user can assign a new absence scheme to a single person or extend/reduce the entitlements of the employee's current Occupational Absence scheme.

Resolution

IT0572 overrides at 2 levels.

  • Scheme                                                      AB-BEGDA (only)
  • Entitlement Band                                        each day in AB

 

Override at Scheme level                             only occurs when scheme is derived, once per AB split.

Override at Entitlement Band level               occurs per day processed.


Please note;

IT0572 is for a specific absence and for the whole of that absence.

IT0572 is independent from any other IT0572.

 

So,

  • if an administrator wants to give an employee a completely different OxP scheme, then they can do that.
  • if an administrator wants to give an employee a completely different OxP scheme and then amend the rules of that different OxP scheme, then they can do that.
  • if an administrator wants to keep an employee’s normal OxP scheme and then amend the Entitlement Bands of that OxP scheme, then they can do that.

BUT,

  • if an administrator wants to keep an employee’s normal OxP scheme and then give them different Entitlement Bands to that normal OxP scheme, then they cannot do that.
  • if an administrator wants to give an employee a completely different OxP scheme and then give them different Entitlement Bands to that different OxP scheme, then they cannot do that.

 

This means, that;

The IT0572 record must have a BEGDA/ENDDA that matches the BEGDA/ENDDA of IT2001.

The IT0572 record must do an override that is acceptable
