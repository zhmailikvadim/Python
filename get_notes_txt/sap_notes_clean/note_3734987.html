Symptom

During the execution of report /PF1/R_IHB_ACCCLOSURE, a Runtime Error CX_ABAP_BEHV_COMMIT_FAILED occurs

Other Terms

/PF1/R_IHB_ACCCLOSURE, IHB, /PF1/CL_IHB_RAP_ACCOUNT, R_IHBACCOUNTTP, CX_ABAP_BEHV_COMMIT_FAILED

Reason and Prerequisites

Due to incorrect handling of validation errors that occur during the precheck, the results of the commit entities in simulation mode step are not excluded from the list of accounts for processing and sent for commit, which leads to a Runtime Error.

Solution

Correction of the transfer of the list of accounts that failed validation after executing commit entities in simulation mode.

Please apply SAP Note or assigned Support Package
