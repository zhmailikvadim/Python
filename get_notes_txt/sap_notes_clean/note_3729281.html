Symptom

There is an upcoming mandatory legal change related to United Arab Emirates eInvoicing Programme. This KBA provides guidance on where to find the relevant information regarding this legal change and SAP solution.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental. 

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition
  • SAP Document and Reporting Compliance, Cloud Edition

Cause

  • eInvoicing | Ministry of Finance - United Arab Emirates
  • UAE eInvoicing Programme Introduction (February 2025)

Resolution

1. Where can we find information on the SAP solution related to United Arab Emirates eInvoicing Programme egal change?
A: Refer to the following resources:

  • Regulatory Change Manager: Subscribe to RCM entry Regulatory Change Manager
  • WorkZone channel: Stay up to date with the news related to the solution delivered by SAP. Follow KBA 3608110 - How to join the localization/regional collaboration WorkZone Groups and Community? - SAP for Me and join WorkZone channel Middle East and North Africa Localization - UAE eInvoicing.
  • Webinar:
    • Overview and Awareness (60 min) – April 7th [Register here] 
    • Enablement and Deep Dive (90 min) – April 9th [Register here] 
  • Community Blog: First Insights on UAE eInvoicing : What You Need to Know on the UAE DCTCE Model and the Rollout Plan 
  • Official FAQ: FAQs | Ministry of Finance - United Arab Emirates 
  • Mandatory Fields: UAE-Electronic-Invoice-mandatory-fields_V-1.0-23Feb2026.pdf 

2. What is the go-live date for this legal mandate?
A: For official timelines and important dates related to this legal change,

  • Refer to the RCM topic Regulatory Change Manager.
  • In addition to this, if you are looking for official implementation timelines and rollout phases, refer to the 'Timeline' section on the page eInvoicing | Ministry of Finance - United Arab Emirates.

3. When will SAP release the solution?
A: Check the 'Product Impact' section from Regulatory Change Manager to get the planned delivery date for your business system.

4. What are the SAP Notes to implement this solution?
A: Go through 3705171 - Electronic Document Processing for United Arab Emirates - Peppol: Implementation Overview - SAP for Me.

5. What are the pre-requisites for this solution Or, How do I keep my system ready before implementing the actual solution?
A: Refer the prerequisite area of 3705171 - Electronic Document Processing for United Arab Emirates - Peppol: Implementation Overview - SAP for Me. 

6. Where can we find product documentation related to this legal change?
A: Refer following SAP Help guides:

  • SAP ERP: Document and Reporting Compliance | SAP Help Portal 
  • SAP S/4HANA: Document and Reporting Compliance | SAP Help Portal 

7. Which component should be used to report issues related to this solution?
A: Depending on the nature of the issue, use one of the following component while reporting an issue to SAP:

  • For the primary investigation, use component CA-GTF-CSC-EDO-AE (United Arab Emirates Electronic Invoicing). 
  • You can find the complete list of components related to DRC Cloud Edition in our product guide Monitoring and Troubleshooting | SAP Help Portal.

8. What are the available Regions for provisioning SAP Document and Reporting Compliance, Cloud Edition?
A: Check KBA 3661316 - What are the available Regions for provisioning SAP Document and Reporting Compliance, Cloud Edition? - SAP for Me. 

9. Is SAP a Pre-Approved eInvoicing Service Providers?
A: SAP has been officially listed as a Pre-Approved eInvoicing Service Providers, refer Pre-Approved eInvoicing Service Providers | Ministry of Finance - United Arab Emirates.

10. I would like to read more about the officiall notification and nuances of this legal mandate, where can I find it?
A: If you are looking for general information about UAE eInvoicing, refer to eInvoicing | Ministry of Finance - United Arab Emirates. This page provides an overview of UAE eInvoicing initiative.
