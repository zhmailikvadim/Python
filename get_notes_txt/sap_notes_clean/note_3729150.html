Symptom

Delivery document is visible in VF04 but not listed in the "Create Preliminary Billing Document" app (App ID: F2876).

Environment

  • SAP ECC
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Use the "Create Preliminary Billing Document" app (App ID: F2876) to create preliminary billing documents.
  2. Observe that the delivery document is visible in VF04 but not listed in the app.

Cause

The issue occurs because the FKART (Billing type) field in the billing due list table VKDFS is empty for the delivery document. This prevents the document from being displayed in the "Create Preliminary Billing Document" app.

Resolution

  1. Verify the FKART (Billing type) field in the billing due list table VKDFS for the affected delivery document.
  2. If the FKART field is blank, refer to KBA ##2766319 for guidance on resolving issues related to the FKART field in the billing due list table.
