Symptom

During disbursement processing (after notice) for resident owner contracts, the system does not create the "Residential Grid Transactions Detail" data (table /IBS/TRR_WRT_DET) for all WRT IDs.

Reason and Prerequisites

The distinction between the internal and external pension equalization payment during the creation of the detail values is incorrect.

Solution

The method (/IBS/CL_RR_SI_DET_WRT_DATA->DET_LOC_ADJ_DATA) for the pension equalization payment is corrected.

In addition, the new service /IBS/CL_RR_SI_CONTRACT->/IBS/IF_RR_SD_CONTRACT~GET_LOC_ADJ_SOURCE_CONTRACT is implemented and used to determine the original savings contract for the pension equalization payment.

The logic of the method for determining the percentage distribution during the split (/IBS/CL_RR_SI_PA_PSP->/IBS/IF_RR_SD_PA_PSP~GET_PSP) is also simplified and corrected.
