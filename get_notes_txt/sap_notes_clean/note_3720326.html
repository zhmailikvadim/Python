Symptom

When changing the amount of the Commitment Limit on a Business Partner Master Data you receive the error: 

Commitment limit must be greater than credit limit, Field: Commitment Limit

Environment

  • SAP Business One
  • SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Business Partners (BP) → Business Partner Master Data
  2. Open an existing BP
  3. On the Payment Terms tab change the Commitment Limit to be an amount that is lower than the Credit Limit. 

Cause

This is system behavior. By definition the Commitment Limit must be equal or higher than the Credit Limit. 

Resolution

Define the Commitment Limit amount to be equal or higher than the Credit Limit.
