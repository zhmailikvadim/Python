Symptom

When you click on button "Add New Lines” or "Delete Lines" or "Copy Lines" or "Clear values"  in change mode of SGT_DEFAULT transaction without selecting any segmentation strategy,no message is issued.

Other Terms

SGT_DEFAULT,Add New Lines,Delete Lines,Copy Lines,Clear values

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the attached correction instruction and carry out the Manual Pre-Implementation steps.
