Symptom

A supplier accidentally accepted an Early Payment Proposal with discount and wants to know how to reject or cancel the acceptance.

Environment

SAP Business Network

Cause

The early payment and discount agreement is controlled by the buyer, not by SAP or the supplier system configuration.

Resolution

If a supplier has accepted an Early Payment Term Offer by accident, the following rules apply:

  • The supplier must contact the buyer directly to opt out of or cancel the early payment agreement.

  • SAP Support cannot reject or undo the acceptance on behalf of the supplier.

  • Only the buyer can cancel or modify the accepted early payment proposal on their end.

If the buyer requires assistance, they can open a support case via SAP for Me from their side.
