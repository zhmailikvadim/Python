Symptom

When filtering Force Elements (FE) by Plant/Storage Location in Manage Force Element, the system may return incorrect results:

Root FE is selected even though a child FE has a direct assignment to the same Plant/MRP Area and Storage Location.

Newly created storage locations for indirectly assigned MRP areas are not considered correctly.
