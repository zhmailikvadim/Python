Symptom

  • You launched an email and observed contact get unexpected email content

Environment

SAP Emarsys Customer Engagement

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Send an email campaign.
  2. Verify the email content received by the recipient.

Cause

A possible cause is that your email campaign has different language versions, so contact got another version but not the default one.

Resolution

  1. Verify the language versions available for the email campaign. You can open email campaign's Content Creation page and find the Language Versions in the menu on left side.
  2. If there are different versions, then you can check contact's IETF language tag to verify which version contact should get.
  3. You can re-design your email content or update contact data to fix the issue.

See Also

  • Refer to: SAP Emarsys Language Versions Documentation
