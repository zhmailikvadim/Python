Symptom

This note includes the following corrections in the S/4 Defense Disconnected system:

Issue: Goods Receipt against Outbound Delivery for packing material during Delta Load (DL) was not possible.

Resolution: 

  • Enabled Goods Receipt against Outbound Delivery through MIGO where packing material is added in the Outbound Delivery which is not a part of Stock Transfer Order(STO) and serial number details will also be added for the finished goods in the outbound delivery.
  • When Goods Receipt against Outbound Delivery is posted for the second time in REM system, the posting is restricted with error message 'Goods receipt has already been posted for item'.
  • To retain the SAP recommended movement type during Goods Receipt posting against Outbound Delivery through MIGO for packing material in case of local scenarios.

Other Terms

Goods Receipt, GR, Packing Material, Delta Load, DL, MIGO, Stock Transfer Order, STO, S/4 Defense Disconnected

Reason and Prerequisites

Implement note '3709211' as a prerequisite.

Solution

Implement the corresponding CI as per the note.
