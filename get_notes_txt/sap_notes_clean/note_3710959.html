Symptom

IR Stuck in Approving Status due to Cost Center error: Line item X ( VAT Withholding Tax (auto-generated) ), Accounting, Accounting Y: The Cost Center does not match the requirements.

Cause

Auto-generated header level withholding tax lines do not reference existing line items (as they are generated only at IR level), leading to the use of the Requester's user profile Cost Center. The Cost Center of the requester is not mapped with the Company Code of the IR in the CostCenterExport.csv, causing the mismatch error. 

Resolution

Edit the IR:

  1. Navigate to the auto-generated tax lines
  2. Click Accounting> Copy Accounting From Line
  3. Confirm by clicking OK in the resulting dialog box
  4. Click Validate and Exit
