Symptom

This SAP Note provides adjustments for the following situations:

  • An employee has left the company and receives a non-recurring payment in the following year that is not subject to contributions as a March clause. This non-recurring payment is then exempt from contributions. Due to retroactive accounting or due to an explicit specification during the creation of the PPO contribution survey notification using the report Create PPO Contribution Survey Notifications (RPCBOVD0), the previous year is recalculated. The non-recurring payment is incorrectly reported in the last active period. 

  • An employee in the transition area receives a non-recurring payment during a suspension. There is no regular remuneration in the corresponding month. The non-recurring payment is displayed as regular remuneration in the PPO contribution survey. This is usually correct in the transition area - but not if there is no regular remuneration due to a suspension.

Other Terms

 Social Insurance

Solution

In the future, the non-recurring payment will only be shifted to the last active period within the same calendar year. This does not affect March clauses.

In the future, the statement of non-recurring payments in the PPO contribution survey for employees in the transition area during an interruption takes place in the data record fields for the non-recurring payment and is not moved to the regular remuneration.
