Symptom

I need to perform an action in the SAP Fieldglass system but am unable to do so. I would like SAP Fieldglass support to handle this on my behalf.

Resolution

SAP Fieldglass support do not have access or permissions to make actions or changes in the production environment. 

In this case, please contact directly the Program Management Office team (PMO) for your buyer company, since they are the system administrators and can help you further on that matter.
