Symptom

 In OnSite Billing mobile planning it is possible to assign a tour or order to a meter reader which is not assigned to the same meter reading center by entering a reader number manually.

Other Terms

OnSite Billing, Meter to cash process, planning, assignment, meter reader

Reason and Prerequisites

The meter reader id was not checked against the value help of tour or order assignment, and it was only checked, if the meter reader was active.

Solution

Implement the correction instruction.
