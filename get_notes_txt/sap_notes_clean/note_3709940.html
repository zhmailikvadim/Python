Symptom

How to update the label of  "Home | At {company name]"  in the RMK career site (no UDM)?

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Marketing
    • Career Site Builder 

Resolution

The information can be updated in the Career Site Builder by updating the label "At {company name}". See steps below:

  1. Open Career Site Builder configuration
  2. Open "Tools"
  3. Click on "Translations"
  4. In "Resource Key" field search for the label : "lblSearchAtCompany "
  5. Click in the desired language
  6. Enter the text in "Override Text" field
  7. Click on "Confirm"
  8. Click on "Save"
