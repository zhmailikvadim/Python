Symptom

You want to perform an order split and you have defined in the order type dependent parameters that the actual costs shall be distributed. Furthermore, in the order you want to split you use several unit of measures for operations having different conversion factors. When you perform the split error message COOS 102 'Split quantity greater than quantity confirmed in previous operation(s)' is raised, even though the confirmed quantity in the preceding operation is sufficient.

Other Terms

UMREZ, UMREN, AFVC, COOS102

Reason and Prerequisites

 Program Error

Solution

Apply correction instruction.
