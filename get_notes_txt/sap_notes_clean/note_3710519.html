Symptom

Pending workflows in Employee Profile is showing in Rehire Verification.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Reproducing the Issue

1. Go to the Employee Profile of the inactive user.
2. Observe that there are pending workflows in the profile.
3. Go to Rehire Verification for the same user and verify that the pending workflows are also displayed there.

Resolution

If there are any incomplete pending workflows, it is expected that they will appear in Rehire Verification.

Keywords

Pending Workflow, Workflow, Rehire, Rehire Verification, Workflows, OBX, Onboarding 2.0
