Symptom

If an issue occurs during the sending of a mail notification in flexible workflow, the issue is not identifiable by activating tracing or looking at the job log.

Especially the following issues are not traced/logged:

  • None of the recipients have a valid mail address assigned. 
  • There is an unexpected technical issue during the sending of the mail.

Reason and Prerequisites

There is an issue during the sending of the mail notification.

Solution

 Install the corresponding support package.
