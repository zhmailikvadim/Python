Symptom

In the process "Embedded Process for Updating Meter Reading Values" (DE_B_EMBED_UPD_MR_VALUE), the process stopped at step 0030 and couldn't continue to steps 0040 and 0042.

This issue is relevant for the market roles: SUP, DSO, MOS.

Other Terms

MET, MSCONS, DE_B_EMBED_UPD_MR_VALUE, CUSTOMER_EXTENSION_INACTIVE, meter reading values, embedded process, precondition

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because steps 0040 and 0042 had incorrect preconditions.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

Then activate BC-Set /US4G/3_0_COM_SP08_NOTE_3733313 with T-Code SCPR20, make sure option [Deletion Functionality->Switch On] is ticked.
