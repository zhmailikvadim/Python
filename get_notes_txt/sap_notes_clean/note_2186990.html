Symptom

The price of a commodity swap is in a currency that does not have two decimal places. In this case, the quotation amount and payment amount are calculated incorrectly. The decimal point is shifted by the number of decimal places minus two. (100.00 becomes 0.10000.)

Other Terms

Currency translation, TCURX, XCONTRACT_PRICE11, SAPLFTR_COMS, FTR_COMS_FLOWS_UI-XBEBETR01, FTR_COMS_FLOWS_UI-XBZBETR01, CONVERT_TO_LOCAL_CURRENCY

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Import the relevant Support Package or implement the attached program corrections manually.
