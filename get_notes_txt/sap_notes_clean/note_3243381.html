Resolution

The option "Render to full page size" causes the trouble. To fix the issue you have to make one change in the process documentation template:

  1. Open the process documentation template
  2. Go to "1.1 Diagram" and click on the diagram picture
  3. Now click on the gray bar on the right side, labeled with "Attribute"
  4. In the opened attribute bar, click on the headline "More Attributes"
  5. Remove the check mark for the option "Render to full page size". See:
