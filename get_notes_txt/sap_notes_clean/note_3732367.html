Symptom

Support of BusinessObjects Platform 2025 with SAP FC

Environment

  • SAP Financial Consolidation 10.1 SP09
  • BI/IPS 2025

Resolution

SAP BI/IPS platform version 2025 is now supported with SAP Financial Consolidation 10.1 starting FC 10.1 SP09 Patch 31.
