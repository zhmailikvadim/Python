Symptom

Calculation view instead of CDS view is appearing during creating KPI

Environment

SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open Fiori Launchpad
  2. Open Create KPI app
  3. While selecting the data source, Calculation view instead of CDS view is appearing.

Cause

Assigning the wrong PFCG roles

Resolution

  1. Open SAPGUI 
  2. Run PFCG, assign the role SAP_BR_ANALYTICS_SPECIALIST to the end user

Keywords

Calculation view, CDS view, KPI, Smart Business
