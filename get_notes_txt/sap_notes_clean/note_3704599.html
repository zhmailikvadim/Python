Symptom

Environment

SAP HANA Platform Edition 2.0   

Cause

According to the numeric details of the table :

------------------------------------------------
|TABLE_NAME|NUM_LOBS  |PHYS_SIZE_MB|AVG_PHYS_KB|
------------------------------------------------
|TSPADS    |     21661|   612569.39|   28958.54|
|TSPADS    |     16595|      207.43|      12.80|
------------------------------------------------

The AVG_PHYS_KB of TSPADS table is 28 megabytes of which there are 21,661 LOBs

Quick calculation reveals :

NUM_LOBS        AVG_PHYS_KB
----------     X   -----------             =   Average LOB total size is 627,270,934 bytes (627 GB)
     21661      28958.54    

Resolution

LOB tables require housekeeping and archiving just like any other table in columnstore or rowstore in HANA. See KBA 2388483 - How-To: Data Management for Technical Tables
