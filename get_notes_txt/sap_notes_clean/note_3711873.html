Symptom

You execute the balance carry forward task or the consistency check for consolidation of investments (C/I), in the context of transition from EC-CS to SAP S/4HANA for Group Reporting (S/4GR). This results in error message G0 814 ( "FS items &1 to be carried forward/deleted do not exist" ). The error message refers to statistical item for C/I from EC-CS.

Other Terms

G0814, CX6C3, BCF, balance carry forward, C/I customizing check

Reason and Prerequisites

  • You undergo the procedure for transition from EC-CS to S/4GR according to SAP Note 2833748.
  • You are using distinct charts of accounts in EC-CS vs. S/4GR.

Solution

Import of Support Package or advanced correction by implementation of correction instruction
