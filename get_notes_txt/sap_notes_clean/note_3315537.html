Symptom

You notice that is is possible to submit a move in process without agreeing with the Terms and Conditions.

Environment

SAP Service Cloud Version 2.

SAP Service Cloud Version 2, Utilities Add-On.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Click on the Navigation Menu.
  2. Navigate to Premise work center.
  3. Open any premise.
  4. Navigate to Customers tab.
  5. Click Move In.
  6. Fill in all mandatory and relevant details for the Move In steps.
  7. Navigate to Step 4 - Review.
  8. Uncheck the Agree to the terms checkbox.
  9. Click Submit or Submit via MDT.
  10. See the Success message.

Cause

This can happen in case the Terms and Conditions agreement has been set as a Non-Mandatory detail in the system, as the system provides the ability to control this setting.

Resolution

This is the system designed behavior.
