Symptom

  • The error "The action cannot be performed" is displayed when saving as the report after upgrading to SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 2025
  • Publication failed with error "The action cannot be performed"
  • In the logs the error below happens.
    • 2026 02 21 06:57:29:892|C3_DPSerialization:New:"Vendor"; key:(NULL); kind:16384; visibleState:0; in "Header"
    • 2026 02 21 06:57:29:892|C3_DPSerialization:FolderItemExtInfo_StreamUnit_SerializeIn
    • 2026 02 21 06:57:29:892|C3_DPSerialization:>>:Version Major:=1
    • 2026 02 21 06:57:29:892|C3_DPSerialization:>>:Version Minor:=0
    • 2026 02 21 06:57:29:892|C3_DPSerialization:FolderItemExtInfo_StreamUnit_SerializeIn: 0.001
    • 2026 02 21 06:57:29:893|C3_Dictionary:*** a item already exists with this name ***: "Vendor" in "Header"
    • 2026 02 21 06:57:29:893|C3_DPSerialization:CreateChild failed for: "Vendor"; key:(NULL); kind:16384; visibleState:0; role:0; (res:-2147191475)  in "Header"!

Other Terms

webi, webintelligence, save as, publication, failed, run, Apply and Close

Reason and Prerequisites

Environment:

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 2025 Patch 4
  • Web Intelligence

Steps To Reproduce:

  1. Migrate the Web Intelligence report from SAP BusinessObjects BI Platform 4.3 to SAP BusinessObjects BI Platform 2025.
  2. Log in to BI LaunchPad.
  3. Open the migrated Web Intelligence report.
  4. Use Save As to save the report under a different name
    1. >> Note: At this stage, the newly saved report can be opened without any issue.
  5. Navigate to the folder where the report was saved.
  6. Double‑click the newly saved report to open it
    1. >> "Action cannot be performed” error.

Cause:

  • This issue is caused by a defect in the product.

Solution

  • This issue is fixed in the patches listed in the "Support Packages & Patches" section below.
    Due to different internal processes, the "Support Packages & Patches" section of the SAP Note will be populated as below:

    • for fixes delivered in "Support Packages": immediately (even if the "Support Package" is planned to be released several months later)
    • for fixes delivered in "Patches": ONLY once the "Patch" is released AND available on SMP (not before)

    For Business Intelligence Platform maintenance schedule and strategy see the Knowledge Base Article 2144559 in References section.
