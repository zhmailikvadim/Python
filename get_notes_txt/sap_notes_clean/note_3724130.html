Symptom

You do not hold a valid On-Premise Maintenance agreement which entitles you to use SAP Solution Manager 7.2 (incl. Focused Build and Insights 2.0). But you are legally entitled or required to retain Confidential Information in accordance with the respective General Terms and Conditions of SAP Software and Support (“GTC”).

Other Terms

SAP Solution Manager usage to retain Confidential Information.

Reason and Prerequisites

Usage rights to SAP Solution Manager arise out of the respective, valid SAP On-Premise Maintenance Agreements. You may be obligated to retain and access Confidential Information for a specific period of time even without holding a valid On-Premise Maintenance Agreement, including but not limited to access historical data for legal reasons.

Solution

You are obligated to destroy or return Confidential Information stored on your systems in accordance with the respective terms of the GTC. This requirement also applies to SAP Solution Manager and the add-on component ST-OST (SAP Focused Insights and Focused Build). However, if you need to retain documents for some time due to legal regulations, there is an exception stated in the GTC: You may keep Confidential Data and also use SAP Solution Manager to the extent you are legally entitled or required to retain Confidential Information. Please review the GTC applicable to your SAP contract for specific details. Please note that this exception does not entitle you to use SAP Solution Manager including referenced add-on components for any internal business operations. Unless a valid Maintenance Agreement is in place, SAP will not deliver any support for said SAP Solution Manager installation.
