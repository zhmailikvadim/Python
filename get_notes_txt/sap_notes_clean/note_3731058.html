Symptom

The quantity change is performed on the delivery at EWM but no queue got triggered to update the same on the delivery at ERP.

Environment

  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management (SCM-EWM)
  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management Interface (SCM-EWM-IF)

Reproducing the Issue

1. Change the quantity for the delivery at EWM side using process code.

2. Check at ERP delivery and the quantity would not have changed to updated quantity

Cause

There is no error, the system is working as expected.

Resolution

In the standard process, queues are triggered to the ERP system only during specific actions, such as hierarchical changes (batch or delivery split), posting of GR/GRR, posting of PC, and completion of the delivery on the EWM side (i.e., closing indicator queue).

All other changes are transmitted along with the next subsequent queue when one of these major actions occurs.

For example, when GR is posted for the delivery at EWM, the corresponding queue is triggered and includes all prior changes (such as quantity updates). These changes are then updated in the ERP delivery.

If you require changes to be updated immediately, you can follow the KBA below:

0001281404 – Customer Message Log Determination

Caution: Implementing this KBA will trigger queues for every change made on the EWM side. This may lead to increased queue traffic in the ERP system and could impact overall performance.

Keywords

Queue, Delivery, Trigger, quantity change, ERP
