Symptom

Device and Device Information record(DIR) got removed on different date leading to multiple time slice on shutdown.

For Example, Measurement Concept instance has one Device and One DIR. When shutdown is executed on 25th March, device gets removed correctly on 24th march. However, DIR got removed on 23rd March.

Other Terms

MCM, APEU, UCOM, DER, Time Slice, DIR, Shutdown, /APEU/CL_PRST_RMV_DEV_DIR, lv_dev_removal_date

Reason and Prerequisites

 Program error

Release 2508

Once Device(s) are removed, the device transaction date will be date of removal and same leads to business end date on MaLo. During DIR removal, this business end date gets used leading to reducing one more day on DIR removal.

Solution

Please implement the attached correction instruction.

Correct reference date is used to remove DIR.
