Symptom

  • 'Users and Groups' properties ,member of, Account Manager were not accessible after implementing the workaround for the CVE -2024-53677
  • The sections appeared as a 404 missing page error.

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform 4.x, BI 2025
  • All supported platforms

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Implement the workaround mentioned in KBA KB0932230 - Impact of CVE-2024-53677 on SAP BusinessObjects.
  2. Attempt to access Users and Groups' properties in the SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform.
  3. Observe the 404 missing page error. 

Cause

The parameter ` <constant name="struts.multipart.enabled" value="false"/> ` was misspelled in the struts.xml file, causing the 404 missing page error.

Resolution

  1. Check the correct syntax of:<constant name="struts.multipart.enabled" value="false"/>
  2. Verify if there are any spelling mistakes or syntax errors in the given statement that could be causing the 404 Missing Page Error.
