Symptom

When a default telephone number or a default email address is marked as "do not use"in the business partner ,the data is shown on the UI. This should not be the case.

Other Terms

Loan Payoff Manage, LOANPAYOFFMAINTENANCE

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Import the relevant Support Package for your release. You can use transaction SNOTE to implement the correction instructions relevant for your release in advance.

Manual activities are not necessary.
