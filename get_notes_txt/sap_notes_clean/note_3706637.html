Symptom

Recipients submitting spam complaints were not suppressed immediately, allowing multiple complaints from the same individual for different email campaigns.

Environment

SAP Emarsys Customer Engagement

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Perform a data export for recipients who submitted spam complaints during a specific time period.
  2. Review the exported data to identify recipients who submitted multiple complaints for different email campaigns before suppression occurred.

Cause

The delay in suppression was caused by mailbox providers sending feedback loop events with delays or in batches, which is beyond SAP Emarsys' control.

Resolution

1. Understand that complaint notifications are sent by mailbox providers (e.g., Yahoo, Gmail, Outlook) with delays or in batches, and not in real time.  
2. SAP Emarsys suppresses contacts immediately upon receiving the complaint notification from the mailbox provider.  
3. To minimize risks associated with delayed suppression, consider implementing proactive measures such as:  
   • Applying stricter engagement-based suppression (e.g., suppressing inactive contacts earlier).  
   • Reducing send frequency for low-engagement segments.  
   • Reviewing opt-in sources and reconfirmation strategies.
