Symptom

The following Knowledge Base article will help you set up Okta as an IdP for Advanced Workflow.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental. 

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
  • Advanced Workflow

Resolution

  1. Open your OKTA
  2. Type your credentials and log in.
  3. In Admin console, go to Applications > Applications
  4. Click on the Create App Integration button
  5. Choose SAML 2.0 and click the Next button
  6. App name: e.g. "sandbox" 
  7. Click Next.
  8. Fill the following fields in the Configure SAML step, like in the screenshot below:
    • Single sign-on URL: https://<environment>.webcomserver.com/wpm/federatedSSon.do?federationLoginResponse=true
    • Audience URI (SP Entity ID): https://<environment>.webcomserver.com/wpm
    • Name ID format: Transient
    • Application username: Okta username
    • Add two Attribute Statements like in the screenshot below:
  9. Click the Next button 
  10. App type: This is an internal app that we have created.
  11. Click Finish.
  12. On the Sign On tab, on SAML Signing Certificates, on the Active one, click on Actions and then on View IdP Metadata to get the XML file.
  13. On the Assignments tab, click Assign > Assign to People. Near your username, click on the Assign button. Click the Save and go Back button.
