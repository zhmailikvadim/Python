Symptom

In the Mass Change of Service Contracts Fiori app, the following issue occurs:

After executed a "Update Prices" action in  mass change for service contracts app, a background job is generated for Update Prices action and job status change to "COMPLETED". However, update prices in the billing plan or Price details tab in bulk still does not work.

Other Terms

Mass Change of Service Contracts Fiori app Translations, Fiori App ID: F8680, BSP Application: SRV_CTR_MCS1, Update Prices Action, Service contacts Mass Change

Reason and Prerequisites

Code corrections have been implemented to ensure that the Update Prices Mass Change Action is properly updates the Prices details in the Service Contract as requested. After applying these changes, mass change   Update Prices Action works as expected.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instruction.
