Symptom

  • Different behaviors observed for certain customizing points between Quality and Production systems.
  • Certain customization points are restricted for maintenance in Quality environment, despite being editable in the Production.

Cause

Resolution

The 'Current Settings' classification is for maintenance objects that contain system-specific data or require frequent, direct updates. This functionality is intentionally active only in clients with a 'Production' client role, allowing for necessary changes in the live environment. Consequently, these settings have no effect in other clients, such as development or testing systems. SAP provides a standard selection of these objects to cover the core needs of most customers.
