Symptom

Upon performing the customizing activities for France eInvoice, such as Defining Process Step Interface Determination | SAP Help Portal, other processes than 'FRCINV' and 'FRSINV' are displayed in the dropdown list.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Cause

Outdated entries.

Resolution

The only processes that should be utilized for the eInvoice customizing are 'FRCINV' and 'FRSINV', as mentioned in Help pages Defining Process Step Determination | SAP Help Portal, Defining Process Step Interface Determination | SAP Help Portal and Defining Mapping Class Determination | SAP Help Portal.
