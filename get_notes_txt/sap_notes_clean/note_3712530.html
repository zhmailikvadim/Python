Symptom

In the process "End of Supply for Gas (Old SUP View)" (DE_B_GELIGAS_EOS_SUP) within the GPKE_SUP cluster, step 1400 does not wait for step 1350 to complete. This issue occurs in step 1400.

This issue is relevant for the market role: SUP.

Other Terms

End of Supply, GPKE_SUP, SUP, Gas, DE_B_GELIGAS_EOS_SUP, Update Supply Scenario, process step 1400, process step 1350, BC set, Old SUP View

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because of an incorrect precondition in the process configuration.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.
After implementing this SAP Note, the system ensures that step 1400 waits for step 1350 to complete in the End of Supply for Gas process. Activate the BC set /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP07_NOTE_3712530_ or /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP08_NOTE_3712530_ as required. You need to retest the process. You can verify the fix by checking that the End of Supply process completes successfully and step 1400 waits for step 1350.

This SAP Notes is relevant for the following role: SUP
