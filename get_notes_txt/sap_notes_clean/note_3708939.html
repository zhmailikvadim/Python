Symptom

During the creation of EEL notifications, the following legal changes have not yet been taken into account.

For the person group keys

  • 106 (working students)
  • 109 (part-time employees)
  • 110 (Short-Term Employees)
  • 190 (Only UV-Liable)

Notifications can be created with submission reason 03 (Statement of Earnings for Maternity Pay) or 04 (Hospital Inclusion) if the employee has his or her own membership with a statutory health insurance fund.

However, the report Creation of Outbound Notifications for Compensation Benefits (RPCEEVD0_OUT) does not create a notification with submission reason 04 for all person group keys mentioned above or no notification with submission reason 03 for person group key 190, even if the employee has their own membership with a statutory health insurance fund.


In addition, the following program errors are corrected:

  • In the data module DBVO ("Preexisting Conditions") for submission reason 11 ("Transitional Pay") or 12 ("Reinstatement Action"), the previous illnesses linked in the system that were still at least one day in continued pay are reported for privately insured persons. However, all linked preexisting conditions should be reported, even if they are completely outside of continued pay.
  • No preexisting condition requests (submission reason 41) are created for working students (person group key 106). However, according to the procedure description, this is permitted. The report RPCEEVD0_OUT should therefore create the preexisting condition requests.

Solution

Implement the program corrections.

For the person group keys 106, 109, 110, 190, statements of earnings are also created with submission reason 04 (Hospital Inclusion), and statements of earnings with submission reason 03 (Maternity Pay) are also created for person group key 190. However, this applies only if the employee has his or her own membership with a statutory health insurance fund, that is, if the field Type of HI in the infotype Social Insurance (0013) has the value "Own Membership Statutory HI".

For privately insured persons with submission reason 11 or 12, linked preexisting conditions are also reported in the module DBVO if they are already completely outside of continued pay.

Preexisting condition requests are created for working students (person group key 106) if they have their own membership with a statutory health insurance fund.


The correction is delimited to 1/1/2026. This means that the notifications that were previously missing are created only if the benefit starts in 2026 or later.

The corrections are delivered by Support Package. An advance correction is possible (see correction instructions).


Required actions after importing the Support Package or implementing the correction instructions

If you have employees to whom the description applies (person group key mentioned above, absence "Maternity Pay" or "Hospital Inclusion" and your own membership in statutory HI), start the report Creation of Outbound Notifications for Compensation Benefits  (RPCEEVD0_OUT) for these persons with recalculation as of 1/1/2026 to avoid the random generation of remuneration statements during retroactive accounting.
