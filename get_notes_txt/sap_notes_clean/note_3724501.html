Symptom

 Tax, such Payroll Expense Tax (Tax Type 019), is not calculated for NY Metropolitan Commuter locality despite being correctly mapped in the Tax Model. 

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental." 

Environment

  • HR renewal
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll 

Cause

Accurate tax calculations for a NY Metropolitan Commuter, requires the Estimated Quarterly Gross Wages to be specified in the 'EQ:' parameter within the BSI script. 

Resolution

Customize the information passed in the 'EQ:' parameter as follows:

  1. Run transaction SM30.
  2. Edit the view 'VV_T5UX9_ADDINFO'.
  3. Create a new entry and specify the gross wages for the entire quarter in the 'Amount' field.  Please verify the appropriate amount to be entered with your business team.
