Symptom

During DDIC activation, an error occurs related to collective search help. A collective search help /MRSS/SH_SGU_PLANNING_NODES_C belonging to MRSS_NW includes an elementary search help /MRSS/SH_WC_PLANNING_NODE that belongs to the MRSS component. In systems where only MRSS_NW is installed and the MRSS component is not available, this dependency leads to activation inconsistencies. As a result, search help cannot be activated successfully in DDIC.

Other Terms

MRSS,Search Help,/MRSS/SH_WC_PLANNING_NODE,/MRSS/SH_SGU_PLANNING_NODES_C

Reason and Prerequisites

 This was due to a program error.

Solution

Apply the manual corrections.
