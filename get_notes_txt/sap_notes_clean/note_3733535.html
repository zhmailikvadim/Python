Symptom

The video on Integration & Exception Monitoring | SAP Help Portal shows the Monitoring Categories "Queued RFC (qRFC) Messages" and "Transactional RFC (tRFC) Messages" but they are not available in your SAP Cloud ALM Tenant

Environment

  • SAP Cloud ALM
  • SAP S/4HANA (on-prem and private cloud) / SAP Business Suite 7
  • Integration & Exception Monitoring

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open Integration & Exception Monitoring in SAP Cloud ALM.
  2. Navigate to the monitoring configuration for your SAP S/4HANA (on-prem and private cloud) / SAP Business Suite 7 system
  3. Search for "Queued RFC (qRFC) Messages" and "Transactional RFC (tRFC) Messages"

Cause

  • tRFC and qRFC metrics are not in scope for Integration & Exception Monitoring; these metrics are currently provided by Health Monitoring.
  • The referenced video was recorded in a development tenant that showed metrics not available in productive tenants.

Resolution

Use Health Monitoring in cloud ALM to monitor tRFC and qRFC, as these metrics are supported there.
