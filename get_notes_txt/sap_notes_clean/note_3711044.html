Symptom

User does no find Savings Forms in SAP Ariba Procurement Mobile App.

Environment

SAP Ariba Procurement Mobile App.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login into SAP Ariba Procurement Mobile App
  2. Go To Home Screen
  3. Savings Forms are not visible 

Cause

This is an expected behavior, SAP Ariba Procurement Mobile App does not support Savings Forms. Currently, only Requisitions and a limited set of Upstream Tasks are supported.

Resolution

If you believe the feature you're seeking is crucial for your business, we highly recommend that you to Submit an improvement request. Please describe how this feature can enhance your business operations.

The Product Management team will evaluate your proposal and determine if the feature can be integrated into our future updates.
