Symptom

• An error occurs when transferring transactional data from ECC to Sustainability Footprint Management (SFM).

• Error messages are displayed in ECC.

• Attempting to retry the import by deleting the existing Business Transaction results in the error: "The transactional data replication of the footprint inventory scope ****** for the period ***** is in progress and cannot be deleted."

Environment

Sustainability Footprint Management

Reproducing the Issue

1. Push transactional data from ECC to SFM.

2. Observe that the inventory scopes fail with import errors in ECC.

3. Notice that the Import Business Transaction status remains 'In Progress' in SFM.

4. Attempt to delete the Business Transaction to re-import the data from ECC, but deletion is prevented. 

Cause

Business transaction cannot be deleted when the status is In Progress.

Resolution

Workaround to delete the Business Transaction and re-import data from ECC:

  1. Use the API to mark the status of Business Transaction as completed by using the API call `/NotifyofEndofReplication`. Refer to: Notify of End of Replication API.
  2. Perform the deletion of the Business Transaction on the SFM UI.
  3. Start the entire replication process from the ECC system.
