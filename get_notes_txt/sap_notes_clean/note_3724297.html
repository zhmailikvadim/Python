Symptom

  • Space appeared in Employee Export before the Email Address.
  • Space appeared in Employee Profile before the Email Address. 

Environment

  • IAS configurations

  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite

    • Analytics & Reporting (Ad Hoc, YouCalc, ORD)
      • Story Report

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the Instance.
  2. Go to Report Center.
  3. Run Story report. 
  4. Blank and Loading appeared or Your Profile is not correctly configured in story report.

Cause

  • The user is not available in SAC.
  • Space in the email address in both the Employee Profile and Employee Export.
  • In IPS transformation has: isValidEmail($.emails[0].value)] is not fulfilled
  • isValidEmail - a function/condition that check whether the string looks is a properly‑formatted email address.

Resolution

  • Remove the space before the Email address in Employee export and Save. 
  • Do a Resync in IPS Source System.

  • Refer to: 3486565 - Entity condition is not fulfilled - Users are skipped for synchronization from SuccessFactors to SAC
