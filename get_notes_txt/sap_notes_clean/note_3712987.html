Symptom

An error occurs in the Manage Cash Concentration App despite configuration in T018V.

Error message: FIBL_RPCODE065 " No bank clearing account for CoCd XXXX, bank XXXX, account XXX, pmnt method X".

Environment

SAP S/4HANA 1709 onwards

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open Manage Cash Concentration app.
  2. Create a new one and by selecting a Cash Pool.
  3. Select bank accounts from the list.
  4. Check error messages in the Application log.

Cause

Validations from table T018V are performed for the currencies of all the bank account added in a cash pool. Even though the bank account with that currency is not selected, the system checks the table T018V for all.

Resolution

Maintain the entry in table T018V (view V_T018V) for all the bank account currencies added in a cash pool.
