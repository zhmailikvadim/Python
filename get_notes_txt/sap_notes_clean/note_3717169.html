Symptom

You want to remove a tile from view in SAP Cloud ALM

Environment

SAP Cloud ALM

Resolution

1. In the section where the tile is displayed (e.g. 'Implementation'), click on your profile icon.

2. Select 'Edit Current Page'

3. Click the 'X' icon on the top right of the relevant tile 

4. Click 'Close' to exit Edit Mode
