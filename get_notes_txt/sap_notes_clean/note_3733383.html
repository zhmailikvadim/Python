Symptom

On March 20, 2026, the Institute for the Charge System in the Hospital (InEK)

  • The procedure description for the quarterly delivery of structural data and data on medical personnel in accordance with Sec. 21 Para. 7 KHEntgG for the data year 2026 and
  • The overview of SAP Notes and error messages for the data transfer in 2026 in accordance with Sec. 21 Para. 7 KHEntgG from the 20th March 2026

and published on his website.

For the data of the year 2026, the quarterly delivery of structure data and data for medical personnel in accordance with Sec. 21 Para. 7 KHEntgG is to take place at the following times:

  • for the 1st Quarter 2026 to 15th April 2026 (11:59 PM),
  • for the 2nd Quarter 2026 to 15th July 2026 (11:59 pm),
  • for the 3rd Quarter 2026 to 15th October 2026 (11:59 pm) and
  • for the 4th Quarter 2026 to 15th January 2027 (11:59 PM).

Environment

  • Industry-Specific Component Hospital (IS-H)
  • release-independent

Cause

Legal requirements

Solution

The quarterly delivery of structural data and data for medical personnel in accordance with Sec. 21 Para. 7 KHEntgG for data of the year 2026 must be based on the data record description that was created with the update from the 25th November 2025 of the Annex to the Agreement on the Transfer of Data According to Sec. 21 Para. 4 and Para. 5 KHEntgG.

Compared to the year-related data delivery in accordance with Sec. 21 Para. 1 KHEntgG for data of the year 2025 (the submission must be made by March 31, 2025), there are no changes and additions because this data delivery was already based on the same data record description.
