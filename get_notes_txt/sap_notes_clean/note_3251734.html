Symptom

As per new legal change Lei 13.137/2015 art.24 published in June 21st, 2015, monthly accumulation(paragraph 4th, article 31 of the law 10.833/03) is no more applicable and now  while making payment we need to consider daily tax threshold of 10R$. If current withholding tax amount threshold is < 10R$ we should not deduct any tax, however as soon as threshold reaches or crosses this daily accumulation limit we need to deduct tax on complete payment amount which was posted in previous documents + current document payment amount.

Other Terms

Brazil daily accumulation, Withholding tax, Daily tax amount threshold, CL_FWTC_BRAZIL_DAILY_ACC, Lei 13.137/2015 art.24, law 10.833/03.

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason: Legal Change

Prerequisites: You have implemented SAP Notes 1820571, 2235923 and 2238916  in your system before implementing this SAP Note.
