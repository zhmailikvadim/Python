Symptom

  • After conversion to ODE, users are unable to view previously saved personal bookmarks in their analytical application
  • Users with standard planning licenses cannot access their private bookmarks in the converted ODE story, even if they created new bookmarks in the classic analytical application and if they have read, update, and delete rights roles on "BI_Content_VIEW_TST" rights applied
  • Users with admin roles can access their private bookmarks in the converted ODE story without issues

Environment

  • SAC

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the original classic version of the analytical application
  2. Create a personal bookmark by selecting "Bookmark Current State" and saving it
  3. Log in with an admin user and convert the classic analytical application to an ODE story
  4. Log out and log back in with a user having a standard planning license
  5. Open the newly converted ODE story and observe that previously saved personal bookmarks are not visible

Cause

  • Under Investigation by Development 

Resolution

  • The solution of this issue is currently under investigation by develpment
