Symptom

In case the external point of delivery is changed with validate from date which is later than the last day of application month of MeMi document, wrong external point of delivery is filled in segment LOC+172 of MSCONS and INVOIC IDoc for MeMi document.

Other Terms

IDXMM, MeMi, MSCONS, INVOIC, External Point of Delivery, NAD+PD, LOC+172, /IDXMM/CL_DP_INVOIC_OUT, /IDXMM/CL_DP_MSCONS_OUT, POINT_OF_DELIVERY

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

For filling MSCONS IDoc, the external point of delivery is determined based on process date of single MeMi process document.

For filling INVOIC IDoc, the external point of delivery is determined based on document date of MeMi document.

Solution

Please implement the correction instruction in the SAP Note or import the higher version of Support Package.

The period end date of MeMi document will be used to determine the external point of delivery based on internal point of delivery.
