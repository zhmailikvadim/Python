Symptom

  • ABC is configured to use "all available plants" as possible substitutions.
  • The ATP check with ABC substitution does not consider substitution plants outside of the entered sales organization.

Environment

•    SAP Supply Chain Management (SCM)
•    S4 HANA
•    SAP Supply Chain Management Advanced Available to Promise (CA-ATP)
•    SAP Supply Chain Management Advanced Available to Promise Alternative Based Confirmation (CA-ATP-ABC) 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Use APP or transaction VA01 to create sales order.
  2. Create a sales order with one Sales organization and requested quantity greater than stock available.
  3. Verify the result. Quantity will be confirmed only in the original plant or plants from same sales organization.
  4. No substitution via other plants from different sales organization.

Cause

  • System works as designed. This is not a limitation on the ATP area but rather on the Sales Area (SD-SLS-SO).
  • When creating the same sales order but directly inputting the plant from the different sales area, the following error happens:
    Sales from sales organization XXXX, dist. chan.YY, plant ZZZZ are not defined
    Message No. V1369
  • Since the plant is not considered valid by the sales component, it is not passed as a valid substitution for ATP ABC.

Resolution

In order to have this location be used as a substitution, it needs to be defined in the same sales organization.

This is done on the following SPRO path:

  1. Enterprise Structure
  2. Assignment
  3. Sales and Distribution
  4. Assign sales organization - distribution channel - plant
