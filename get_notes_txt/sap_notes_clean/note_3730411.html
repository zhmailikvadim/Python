Symptom

  • Selecting the App F2774 - Mass Schedule Maintenance Plans and navigating to application job results, an error occurs.
  • Error message: "Navigation is not supported for your user on this device. Please open an incident on the application component."

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA, on-premise
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the App F2774 - Mass Schedule Maintenance Plans.
  2. Navigate to Application Jobs.
  3. Select a job and choose Results.
  4. Observe the error message and blocked navigation.

Cause

The user does not have the required business role/authorization to open the job results.

Resolution

Assign a standard role that allows access to job results, for example:

  • SAP_BR_MAINTENANCE_PLANNER
  • SAP_BR_MAINT_SUPERVISOR

Log out and log in again, then retry opening the Application Job Results.

See Also
