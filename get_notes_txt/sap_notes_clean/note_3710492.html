Symptom

In a cross-dock outbound delivery scenario involving 4 cartons, 1 carton is rejected and 1 carton is short-picked in Extended Warehouse Management (EWM). As a result, the final Goods Issue (GI) and LDAP notifications sent back to Transportation Management (TM) reflect only 2 cartons.

The freight order displays an incorrect product quantity at the product level. Although the quantity is correct at the Freight Unit (FU) and Delivery Transportation Request (DTR) levels, it is incorrect in the Freight Order (FO).

Environment

Transportation Management embedded in SAP S/4HANA 2021 and subsequent releases

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Assign/Dock a Transportation Unit (TU) to any available door using transaction /SCWM/TU.
  2. Load the assigned TU.
  3. Delete the assigned delivery.
  4. Complete the loading process against the TU.
  5. Perform and save the Goods Issue (GI) for the TU.
  6. Depart the TU.
  7. Verify the product quantity in the Freight Order (FO).

Cause

Resolution

Switch to hierarchy types for Cargo Management on the Items tab to determine whether the actual quantity have been updated. The default hierarchy type for All Items displays only the planned quantity.

Additionally, make sure to implement the SAP Notes mentioned in the "See Also" section or apply the corresponding Support Package to fix the known issues for quantity update issues.
