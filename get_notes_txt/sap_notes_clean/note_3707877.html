Symptom

Customer is requesting information on the SAP products that are available and ready to be used business AI assistant (Joule).

Environment

  • SAP Cloud for Customer Version 2 
  • SAP Business AI
  • Joule

Cause

The business AI assistant is currently available in SAP Sales Cloud Version 2 and other products listed in the SAP Discovery Center Business AI Features.

Resolution

To learn more about the SAP Business AI Assistant (Joule) and request a demo, refer to the SAP Early Adopter Care Program. This program provides access to early enablement, product details, and guided support for the Business AI Assistant.

Access the SAP Early Adopter Care Program here:
Joule from SAP | Artificial Intelligence Assistant 

The SAP Business AI Assistant is currently available in SAP Sales Cloud Version 2 and other SAP solutions as listed in the  SAP Discovery Center Business AI Features.

For detailed and up-to-date information on product availability, refer to:

  • SAP Discovery Center AI Feature - SAP Sales Cloud Version 2, Joule
  • SAP Discovery Center AI Feature - SAP Service Cloud Version 2, Joule
