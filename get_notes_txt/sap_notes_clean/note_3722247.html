Symptom

Invoices submitted via Peppol are failing validation on SAP Business Network with error: INV-352: A ZZZ charge or allowance is not allowed by the buyer. or INV-352: A charge or allowance is not allowed by the buyer. or DOC-6: A document preprocessing error occurred.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Supplier submit invoices via peppol integration with SAP Business Network.
  2. Observe validation failure on the SAP Business Network with the mentioned error messages.

Cause

The Buyer has enabled the rule Configure list of allowed allowance and charge codes (under External Tax Invoice Rules but has not included `ZZZ` or `95` in the allowed list.

The error DOC-6: A document preprocessing error occurred confirms that the document validation failure caused an exception during preprocessing (ariba.network.service.common.andocrouting.ANInvoiceDetailRouting.processInvoice.)

Resolution

Follow the steps outlined below to update the allowance values under Configure list of allowed allowance and charge codes

  1. Log in to your SAP Business Network account as buyer.
  2. In the upper-right corner of the homepage, click on [Your Initials] > Settings.
  3. Under Transactions section, select External Tax Invoice Rules.
  4. Under the General Invoice Rules section and click on Configure list of allowances and charges.
  5. Click on Create and add ZZZ and 95 to the list of allowed codes.
