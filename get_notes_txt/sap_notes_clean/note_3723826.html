Symptom

The Dados de ausência/presença (V_T5PPBSAB) and Modelos e horários de trabalho (V_T5PPBS9L) table views, as well as the Atualização visão: síntese V_T5PPBSAB (3) and Atualização de visões: detalhe V_T5PPBSAB (4) screens, have English as their original language instead of Portuguese.

Other Terms

Transaction: 

&SIOE (for more information, see SAP Note 2197631)

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisite

The following Support Package (SP) level is the minimum prerequisite:

ReleaseSupport Package
S4HCM 102 HR SP 00
S4HCM 101 HR SP 06
S4HCM 100 HR SP 19

The SAP Note 3690959 is required.

Solution

The V_T5PPBSAB and V_T5PPBS9L table views, as well as screens 3 and 4 of the V_T5PPBSAB table view, have been modified to have Portuguese as their original language.
