Symptom

Need to change the the colour of  icons on the header 

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite suite 2205

Reproducing the Issue

1. Login to the instance 

2. Check the colour of the icons appearing at the top bar

Cause

It can be changed through importing Theme and not in UI.

Resolution

Icon on the homepage their colors are controlled by modulePicker.iconTextColor in the exported theme.
