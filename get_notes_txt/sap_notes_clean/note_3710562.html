Symptom

Implemented RAP Business Events for the Purchase Requisition object (I_PurchaseRequisitionTP) .
However, when running MRP,  the event is not triggered. 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 

Cause

Due to the nature that MRP could create a large number of Purchase Requisitions at once, the Events is not designed to be triggered by MRP due to performance considerations.

 

Resolution

Use Business Add-in  PPH_MRP_PURREQ_CFL alternatively. 

Keywords

PurchaseRequisition~Created,  I_PurchaseRequisitionTP, MRP,
