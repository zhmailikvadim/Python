Symptom

You enter an external service processing in an EAM order with working hours and calculation key ‘Calculate Work’. Afterwards the working hours field is greyed out and can no longer be changed. This is not correct.

Other Terms

PM03, service, COMSRV, AFVGD-INDET, AFVGD, INDET, ARBEI, AFVGD-ARBEI, FUNC CO_VG_CHECK_SWITCH_STEUS, T430-LIEF, T430-SERVICE, maint. order, maintenance order, CS order, service order, PACKNO, AFVGD-PACKNO, CALC_KEY, WORK_ACTIVITY, CL_EAMS_BO_ORDER, SET_ORD_OPER; WDA, Web Dynpro, Create Maintenance Order, Change Maintenance Order.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

After you have implemented the program corrections, for external service operations the calculation key is defaulted with ‘Maintain manually’ and the working hours are cleared and greyed-out.
