Symptom

When attempting to approve an Invoice Reconciliation (IR) in Reconciling status from the user's To Do page using the Approve button, the system displays the following error: "You cannot mass approve these requests".

Cause

Although the Approve button appears in the To Do page, it cannot be used to approve Invoice Reconciliation (IR) documents in Reconciling status. IRs follow a different approval process, where approval occurs only after all exceptions are handled and the document is explicitly submitted. Therefore, selecting an IR in the To Do list and clicking Approve will always result in an error, because this action is not valid for IR documents.

The same behavior does not apply to IRs in Approving status since those have no exceptions to be corrected.

Resolution

To approve the IR, the approver must:

  1. Open My To Do section:
    • Click the IR ID directly from the To Do page, or
    • Navigate to Actions > Reconcile.
  2. Review and fix any:
    • Exceptions
    • Validation errors
    • Outstanding reconciliation issues
  3. Once all issues are resolved, click Submit.
    This action completes the reconciliation process and approves the document.
