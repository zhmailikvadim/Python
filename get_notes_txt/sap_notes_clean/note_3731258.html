Symptom

  • The field WAVWR is missing in the VWB2_VBRKVBRP_4
  • The quantity in the Debit / Credit memo is not considered in the business volume for condition contract.
  • When creating a credit/debit memo, the billing quantity (VBRP-FKLMG) is not updated in the business volume table VWB2_VBRKVBRP_4.
  • Same for the fields FKIMG (Actual Invoiced Quantity), NTGEW (Net Weight), BRGEW (Gross Weight), and VOLUM (Volume).

Environment

  • Settlement Management
  • Agency Business
  • Condition Contract Management
  • Condition Contract Settlement
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4 HANA



Cause

  • Standard behavior , WAVWR field is not present in VWB2_VBRKVBRP_4.
  • It is the standard sales process, that a credit memo referring to a credit memo request does not change the billed quantity (or any other unit-related field) but only the billed amount field. So if the SD-Document type (AUTYP) is "K" (Credit Memo Request) or "L" (Debit Memo Request) the VBRP-FKLMG (Billing quantity in stock keeping unit) will be zero.
  • Check the data definition of the  V_WB2_VBRK_VBRP_1 (taking FKLMG as an example):
          when ( vbrp.autyp = 'K' or vbrp.autyp = 'L') and vbrp.msr_id is initial then
           cast( vbrp.fklmg * 0 as fklmg )
          when vbrp.shkzg = 'X' or vbrp.shkzg = 'A' then
          -vbrp.fklmg
          else
           vbrp.fklmg
          end                      as fklmg,

Resolution

  • Currently, the  field "WAVWR" isn’t included in the standard business volume table VWB2_VBRKVBRP_4. To make it available, you can create a custom business volume table based on the standard one (VWB2_VBRKVBRP_4) and name it, for example, ZVWB2_VBRKVBRP_4.
         After creating the custom table, simply assign it to your profile via transaction WLFA by navigating to:
         Condition Contract Management > Condition Contract Settlement > Define and Configure Profiles for Business Volume Determination.
  • If you do not want this standard behaviour, copy the listed standard CDS views and adapt them to your requirements.
  • If you are using Advanced Return Management (ARM), check note 3146136.
