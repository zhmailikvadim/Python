Symptom

  • The instance is identified as free tier and is scheduled for deletion.
  • Risk of losing all development associated with the instance.

Environment

SAP Integration Suite

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the SAP BTP Cockpit.
  2. Access the subaccount level under Services -> Instances and Subscriptions.
  3. Identify the instance marked as free tier.
  4. Observe the warning messages indicating the instance is scheduled for deletion.

Cause

The free-tier service plan for SAP Integration Suite expires after 90 days, leading to the deletion of the instance and its associated integration content.

Resolution

Below resource provides detailed guidance on the steps required for upgrading your tenant.

Integration Suite - Provisioning and Upgrading a F... - SAP Community . 

Specifically, refer to the section "Seamless Upgrade to Paid Service Plan," which outlines the necessary actions to update your tenant to a paid usage plan. Once the upgrade is successfully completed, you will notice that the expiry icon will no longer appear.
