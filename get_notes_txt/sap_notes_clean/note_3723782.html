Symptom

  • How to control multiple recipient email addresses per sales organization in SAP Document and Reporting Compliance?
  • The system allows only one EDOC_DE email address to be maintained per business partner.
  • The same business partner is assigned to multiple sales organizations, but the system does not support maintaining separate email addresses for each sales organization.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition
  • SAP Document and Reporting Edition, Cloud Edition
 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Configure SAP Document and Reporting Compliance (DRC) in SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition for sending electronic invoices.
  2. Assign the same business partner to multiple sales organizations.
  3. Attempt to maintain separate recipient email addresses for each sales organization.
  4. Observe that only one EDOC_DE email address can be maintained per business partner.

Resolution

  • In the current configuration, only one email address can be designated with the remark EDOC_DE. Additional recipients can be specified as copies (EDOC_DE_C). Refer to : Maintaining E-Mail Addresses of Customers 

  • In situation where it is expected to configure company codes, each represented by a separate sales organization, the recommended approach would be to:

    • Not specify any email address as EDOC_DE.
      Instead, populate the emailrecipientsto parameter directly within BAdI implementation :eDocument Partner Connector: Change E-Mail to Customer V2 Business Add-In | SAP Help Portal
    • This will give greater control over how email recipients are determined and ensure custom logic is consistently applied.
