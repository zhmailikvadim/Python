Symptom

When you use the OData service CUAN_IMPORT_SRV to load interactions, the system issues error message CUAN_INTERACTION 053 ("Product &1 is not in product master; upload with interaction not possible").

Other Terms

CUAN_IMPORT_SRV

Reason and Prerequisites

You use the OData service CUAN_IMPORT_SRV to import contacts and interactions simultaneously. At the product node, the interactions contain, among other things, products that have not yet been loaded as marketing products.

Solution

Import the contacts and interactions separately or import the products first. Alternatively, implement the correction instructions.
