Symptom

SAP Business One Alerts are functioning correctly but are only receiving in the Message/Alert inbox without pop-up notifications. SAP Business One Alerts pop-up notifications function differently when the Standard Cockpit is enabled. 

Environment

SAP Business One

Reproducing the Issue

Prerequisites:

Enable the Standard Cockpit, setup and activate the SAP Business One Alerts. 

Scenario:

  • Login to SAP Business One client with superuser account.
  • Enable the Standard Cockpit from Administration > System Initialization > General Settings > Cockpit.
  • Enable the option Alert Service from Administration > System Initialization > General Settings. 
  • Create and activate an internal alert from the Alert Management module and assign to the user.  
  • Login with that user.

Expect Behavior:

SAP Business One should display a pop-up notification and an alert in the Message/Alerts overview window inbox from the SAP Business One tool bar.

Actual Behavior:

SAP Business One alert displays in the Message and Alert widget without any pop up notification from the SAP Business One tool bar.

Cause

Resolution

This is the correct system behavior.

When the Alert and Message widget is enabled from the cockpit, the "Message/Alerts Overview" window no longer automatically pops up when a new message arrives. Instead, all alert messages are displayed in the alert widget by default.

To receive an internal alerts with pop-up  notification window, disable the  Messages and Alerts widget in the Cockpit. 

Keywords

SAP Business One alerts, internal alerts, cockpit, standard cockpit, widget
