Symptom

Since the 1st January 2026, the application and certification procedure for assignments in agreement states takes place exclusively digitally. The innovation concerns employers who temporarily post their employees to a state with social security agreements. 

Environment

Product version: SAP ERP 6.0

Solution

The A1 notification procedure has been extended to countries with which a social insurance agreement exists.

  • Social security agreements currently exist with 23 countries, including Albania, Brazil, Chile, China, India, Japan, Kosovo, Montenegro, the Philippines, Turkey and the United States.

  • The implementation of the corresponding module is optional for software vendors.

  • SAP has decided to partially implement the module – for the three most important countries, China, Turkey, and the United States.

  • For all other states, it is possible to submit A1 notifications using the SI notification portal for employers.

A specific point in time for the implementation is not yet known. The announcement and the provision will be made in due course via a corresponding SAP Note.
