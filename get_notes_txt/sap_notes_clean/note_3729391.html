Symptom

The user posts a material document, for example, in transaction MIGO. During the synchronization of the material document to raw material controlling, the document incorrectly remains in the status not relevant in the postprocessing application even though the document would be relevant for raw material controlling.

This problem occurs only if the affected work center is assigned to processes for which at least one work station no longer falls within the validity period of the posting date of the document.
