Symptom

In a change quote the sub-items/components are not getting "Quotation Accepted" status set on them after the PPF action CRMS4_SOM_PROCESS_CHANGE_QUOTE is executed. Due to this, the header "Quotation Accepted" status is not being set to "All items accepted". Instead, it reflects "Some items accepted".

Other Terms

BOM, Change Quote; I1079

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error.

Solution

Correction Instruction.
