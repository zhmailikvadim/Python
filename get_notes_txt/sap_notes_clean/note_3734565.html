Symptom

GL Account is getting determined in revenue account determination but is not being reflected in accounting document.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP FIORI for SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a billing document for a sales process where a pricing condition (for example, freight/charge) is configured as Statistical in the pricing procedure and has an account key with VKOA maintained.
  2. Release/send the billing document to accounting.
  3. Open the resulting accounting document and check line items for the statistical condition amount and G/L account.
  4. Observe that, although account determination finds a G/L for the condition, the statistical condition is not reflected in the accounting document.

Cause

A statistical SD condition is not updated in Financial Accounting documents by standard behavior. Even if revenue account determination (VKOA) proposes a G/L account, statistical conditions are excluded from FI posting.

Resolution

This is correct system behavior.
