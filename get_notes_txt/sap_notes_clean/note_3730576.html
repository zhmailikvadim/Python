Symptom

After proxying to an employee, you are not able to see the "Get HR Support" icon on the homepage to access Enterprise Service Management (ESM).

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite
    • Enterprise Service Management (ESM)

Cause

Expected behavior.

Resolution

With the proxy changes introduced in the 2H Release (see Important Notes on Proxy Access | SAP Help Portal and KBA 3667810), ESM no longer supports the use of proxy, and after internal discussions, the decision was confirmed that ESM will continue without proxy functionality.

To create tickets on behalf of another user, please use the "Affected User" field when submitting it. This method provides a more formal workflow and aligns with the way ESM is designed to operate.
