Symptom

Under Account Display, you select tab Transactions. The system displays error >3002: “Contract account &1 for business partner &2 does not exist”.

Other Terms

 Business Transaction, EVL, DFKKEVL, Account Display (FPL9)

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason

An error in the program occurs when the contract account uses an alternative business partner as the correspondence recipient, and that alternative business partner has no relationship for the contract account in the system.

Prerequisites

  • Customizing for Recording of Business Transaction is active
  • Customizing for Recording of Business Process for Correspondence is active

Solution

Implement the corrections in accordance with the attached correction instructions or import the relevant Support Package.
