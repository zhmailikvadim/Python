Symptom

The bonus for the housing support contribution in Vienna (Z14) is incorrectly included in the wage types SI Employee Contributions (/320) and SI Employer Contributions (/360). As a result, the 20 percent rule, among other things, cannot be applied correctly.

Other Terms

WF, V0HGV, 0.25%, 1.5%, 20%, /140

Reason and Prerequisites

You have implemented SAP Notes 3670363 and 3706521.

Solution

The correction is provided by HR Support Package (via CLC if necessary).

You can implement an advance correction by performing the manual activity.

Details

When calculating the 20 percent rule, the shares for SI are used first. The contributions to the Chamber of Labor contribution and housing support must not be included there.
If the employee share exceeds 20% of the relevant gross payments, these are to be taken over by the employer.

The incorrect assignment ensured that the bonus Z14 was taken into account in the 20%. This has now been corrected.
