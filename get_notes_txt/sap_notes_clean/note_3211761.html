Symptom

During the creation of the Purchase Requisition/Purchase order, Item Category 'D' is unable to be selected.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud

Cause

Expected Behavior

Resolution

The Item category 'D' is not available in Pure Fiori standard apps.

The reason for this behavior is that there is a new process "Lean services" for Fiori services, in which item category 'D' is not involved.

This is a service based on material master (material type SERV) not on item category 'D' and Service Master.
