Symptom

Is it possible to customize the standard fields on the Multi‑Line RFQ form in Guided Buying, such as Quantity, Target Unit Price, Title and Unit of Measure ?

Cause

As of current those fields can't be customized on the Multi-Line RFQ Form.

Resolution

As of now, those fields are static fields and we can't add customization on it, such as changes on its label or add custom hint messages.

To add this option in Guided Buying, we recommend submitting an enhancement request: 3176706 - How do I create an improvement or enhancement request For Ariba Procurement?.
