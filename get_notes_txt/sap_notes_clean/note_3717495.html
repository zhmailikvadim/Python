Symptom

In the process "Decision Tree Diagram E_0608 (Registration of an Assignment)" (DE_B_GPKE_DSO_NIN_DTD_E0608), step 0230 overwrites any check result from the customer extension in step 0115.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

New Installation, Change of Supplier 24, DE_B_GPKE_NIN_DTD_E0608, GPKE_DSO, Customer Extension, US4G_POD_IDENT_AGAIN, US4G_POD_IDENT_FINAL, US4G_POD_NOT_IDENT_FINAL, Point of Delivery, PoD Identification, 60-Day Loop

Reason and Prerequisites

 The error occurred because step 0230 had incorrect preconditions, causing it to overwrite the check result from the customer extension in step 0115.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, step 0230 is only triggered if you provide a specific value in step 0115. When you use a customer extension in step 0115, you need to use one of the following three values to signal the process what action to take next:
- US4G_POD_IDENT_AGAIN: Identify the point of delivery again (60-day loop)
- US4G_POD_IDENT_FINAL: Point of delivery is identified (skip 60-day loop)
- US4G_POD_NOT_IDENT_FINAL: Point of delivery is not identified (skip 60-day loop)

You can verify the fix by checking that step 0230 is only triggered when the value US4G_POD_IDENT_AGAIN is provided from step 0115.
