Symptom

  • Inquiry regarding maintaining access to SAP BFC after its end of maintenance in 2030.

Environment

  • SAP Financial Consolidation (FC) 10.1.

Resolution

After the end of the maintenance period, it will still be possible to continue using the product and to keep it installed on the infrastructure for historical consultation purposes.

However, as of that date, the product will no longer be supported by SAP, which means:

  • SAP FC will no longer provide support for the tool post-maintenance.
  • no access to technical support.
  • no fixes, updates, or enhancements.

The use of the product after the end of maintenance will therefore be under the customer’s responsibility, in particular for consulting historical data.
