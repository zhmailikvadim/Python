Symptom

 Availability of the Reject button in the Manage Payment app for payment orders with the statuses Payment Medium Send to Bank (228) and Payment Medium Settled (230).

Other Terms

 Manage Payments, Payment Order, Reject, F3647

Reason and Prerequisites

 The existing visibility conditions for the Reject button do not hide it when the payment order has the statuses Payment Medium Send to Bank (228) or Payment Medium Settled (230).

Solution

The visibility conditions for the Reject button have been updated to hide it for payment orders in the statuses Payment Medium Send to Bank (228) and Payment Medium Settled (230). Additionally, to enable status changes for payment orders, Note 3713533 has been released. After its implementation, the Manage Payment app will include functionality for changing the status of a payment order.

Please apply SAP Note or assigned Support Package
