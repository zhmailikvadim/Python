Symptom

In PMMO Pegging, replenishments are underpegged which are pegged to a stock transport or subcontracting purchase order NHA replenishment which has been only partially received (that is, it is partially still in procurement), whose received portion is, in turn, at least partially assigned to another dependent requirement (that is, another grouped replenishment element, not a top-level requirement).  (For STO's, the STO P.O. item may be only partially in transit, not necessarily partially received.)

(The portion of the replenishment element which should be assigned to the partially received/shipped P.O. is underpegged in the ratio of the quantity of the NHA purchase order which is still entirely in procurement to its total order quantity.)

Other Terms

PMMO_PEGGING, RPMMOPEG, PMMO_PEGGING041, PMMO_PEGGING-041, underassignments, under-assignment, underpeg, under-peg, under-pegging, under-pegged, Consumed out of Group, NHA, ungrouped production order, subcontracting P.O., stock transport PO, STO P.O.

Reason and Prerequisites

This is due to a programming error.

Solution

Implement this SAP Note, or apply the service pack which contains it.

If you have not yet implemented SAP Note 3485416, "Underpegging of stock issued to partially received P.O.'s issued to top-level requirements", then it is recommended that you implement that Note first, if it is implementable in your system.
