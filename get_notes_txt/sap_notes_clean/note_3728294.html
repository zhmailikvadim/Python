Symptom

You complete the processing of a serialized product in a shop floor order e.g., by completing the very last operation activity for that serial number.

If the serial number master exists and a batch is maintained, the automatic goods receipt fails with error IO229 "Batch for serial number differs from document batch"

Other Terms

IO229, IO 229

IS_REWORK_ORDER, Rework Order, REWORKED_SFI_ID

Reason and Prerequisites

The existing batch of the serial number master is not considered for the automatic goods receipt.

This situation typically only occurs for rework orders since for other shop floor orders the serial numbers just get created from the production process and do not have a batch in the corresponding master record, yet.

Solution

Implement the correction instruction: Afterwards the batch of the serial number master is considered if no batch is maintained for the shop floor order header.
