Symptom

Guided sourcing view button is appearing in the classic sourcing project/event for the users who is not the member of the Category buyer group.

Resolution

Guided sourcing view button option will appear by default in classic sourcing to view the event/project in guided sourcing view which is not related to the Category buyer group, hence it does not matter whether user is the member of the Category buyer group or not the Guided sourcing view option will be available for all the users to view the classic sourcing event in the guided sourcing view.
