Symptom

In Fiori app "F2828 - Maintenance Planning Overview" when using filter "Next Week" or "In Two Weeks" then no Overdue Orders are fetched which is correct. However if you drill down to card Overdue Orders then list report of app " F2173 - Overdue Maintenance Orders and Operations" may show orders. This behavior is due to the incorrect Basic Finish date intervals inherited with navigation.

Other Terms

EAM; F2828; Reference date; Overdue order; "Next Week";"In Two Weeks"; F2173; ParameterProvider; Basic finish

Reason and Prerequisites

 Incorrectly inherited Basic Finish Date intervals upon navigation.

Solution

Apply the attached correction instruction or import the relevant support package.
