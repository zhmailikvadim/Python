Symptom

Adjustment of authorization object for the Fiori app Manage Hedge Constellations Tasklist.

Other Terms

Commodity Risk Management; Commodity Hedge Accounting; Manage Hedge Constellations Tasklist

Reason and Prerequisites

The authorization object CMM_CHEX controls permissions for the Fiori app 'Manage Hedge Constellations Tasklist,' but modifying it would impact other apps, including exposure creation. To ensure finer authorization control without affecting other functionalities, a new authorization object, CMM_CHCTSK, has been created.
