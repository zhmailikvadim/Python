Symptom

When replicating a Business Partner Employee from S/4 HANA Private Cloud to SAP FSM using the standard integration flow, an error message appears in the CPI logs:

[{"status":404,"id":null,"externalId":"EXTERNALID","ex":{"error":"CA-26","message":"CA-26: Resource [UnifiedPerson] with id [OBJECTID] is not found.","values":["UnifiedPerson","OBJECTID"],"id":"ERRORID"}}]

Environment

  • SAP Field Service Management
  • SAP Cloud Platform Integration (CPI)
  • SAP S/4 HANA On-Premise

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Starting standard integration to replicate a Business Partner Employee from S/4 HANA to FSM
    Expected behavior:
  2. The Employee has been replicated successfully
    Actual behavior:
  3. The Employee is not replicated to FSM and there is an error message in CPI

Cause

At update time the corresponding employee no longer existed in FSM — the record was soft-deleted or deactivated (possibly by the "Replicate Business Partner Employee" iFlow).

Resolution

To ensure that deleted employee records from S/4 HANA are correctly replicated and restored in FSM, enable the following company setting in FSM Admin: 'SAP.FSM.DataApi.PatchRestoreDeletedPerson'.

This setting allows FSM to reinstate person records that were previously deleted.

    1. Login to FSM Admin
    2. Navigate to the affected company
    3. Open 'Company settings'
    4. Search for 'SAP.FSM.DataApi.PatchRestoreDeletedPerson'
    5. Click on the setting
    6. Click on 'Edit'
    7. Change value to 'true'
    8. Click on 'Update'
    9. Re-trigger replication of the employee from S/4 HANA to FSM

If the company setting is missing, it should be created via the 'Create' button at the top right corner of the Company settings screen.
