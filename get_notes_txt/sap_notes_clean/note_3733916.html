Symptom

Net amount differs between the GUI Golden Tax Interface (GTI) and the Fiori app "Create Golden Tax Document China" after converting currency to CNY.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP ECC
  • China

Reproducing the Issue

Check the Net Amount for same billing document between the GUI GTI and Fiori APP "Create Golden Tax Documents-China'. 

Cause

In S/4HANA, the GUI version of the Golden Tax Interface (GTI) is obsolete and has not been updated for many years, its calculation logic is out of date and differs from the current Fiori app design, causing mismatches when comparing results.

Resolution

  1. Do not use the obsolete GTI GUI transactions in S/4HANA.
  2. Use the Fiori app "Create Golden Tax Document China" and follow its calculation logic as the leading and up-to-date design.
  3. Avoid comparing amounts or exchange rates between the obsolete GUI and the Fiori app, validate results within the Fiori process only.

See Also

refer to: SAP Note 2227963 - Obsolete GTI GUI transactions in S/4HANA
