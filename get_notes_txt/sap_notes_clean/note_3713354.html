Symptom

This KBA explains China payroll driver does not support VAT(Value Added Tax). "Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental." 

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll
  • SAP S/4 HANA

Resolution

  • According to the information provided in the website 中华人民共和国增值税法实施条例 below, it is mandatory for companies to withhold and collect VAT(Value Added Tax) for 'Remuneration for Providing Services', but this law does not apply to the payroll driver in our HCM system for China.


  • It's important to note that 'Remuneration for Providing Services' in the law and 'Remuneration for Providing Services' in our China payroll driver are not the same concept.
    • The payroll driver refers to employment relationships, it does not require VAT to be collected.
      For example, a teacher at a training organization receives the 'Remuneration for Providing Services', he is considered an employee of the company and does not have to pay VAT.
    • The law refers to non-employee independent 'Remuneration for Providing Services' provided to external personnel, it requires VAT(Value Added Tax).
      For example, if the training organization hires external personnel to train this teacher, this is also called 'Remuneration for Providing Services' and requires VAT to be collected, but the system does not support this as the personnel is external.

Keywords

VAT (Value Added Tax) , Remuneration for Providing Services
