Symptom

Missing German description in the process  DE_B_IDEV_CTB_CHANGE and DE_B_IM_DEV_CHANGE

Other Terms

MACO, Metering, US4G, DE_B_IM_DEV_CHANGE, DE_B_IDEV_CTB_CHANGE, Translation

Reason and Prerequisites

Configuration issue

Solution

Please download the attchment and upload these two BC sets.

1. Open transaction 'SCPR3' and upload the two BC set.

2.Open transaction SCPR20 and enter the BC set /US4G/3_0_MOS_SP08_NOTE_3725584

3.Select *Utilities* > *User setting* on the menu

4.On the *Main Transaction* tab, mark the *Deletion Function* checkbox

5.Back to SCPR20 and then active the BC set
