Symptom

In a marketing document, the line total does not match price after discount by quantity.

Environment

SAP Business One

Reproducing the Issue

Scenario:

In a marketing document set as unit price 45.68, quantity 30, discount 66.67%

Actual result:

Price after discount 15.23 / Total (LC) 456.75

Expected result:

Price after discount 15.23 / Total (LC) 456.90 (Price After Discount 15.23 by Quantity 30)

Resolution

This is the correct system behavior.

As explained in the how to guide How to Work with Gross Prices, the net total before discount is calculated first, followed by the calculation of the price after discount.

 

Unit Price 45.68 * quantity 30 = 1370.40 - Net total before discount

1370.40 * (100-66.67)/100 = 456.75432 rounded to 456.75 - Total after discount / line total  / Total (LC)

devided by quantity 30 = 15.225 rounded to 15.23 - Price after discount
