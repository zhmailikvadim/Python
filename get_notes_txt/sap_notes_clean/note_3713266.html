Symptom

  • The Custom Field uses a pick list for its values. It is linked from the Statement of Work (SOW) module to the SOW Worker module
  • The Statement of Work was revised and the value of the Custom Field is updated
  • When revising the SOW Worker, the Custom Field becomes blank.
  • The field does not prepopulate with the latest value of the Custom Field from the SOW module to the SOW Worker revision

Cause

  • The Custom Field on SOW Worker revision value reflects the Custom Field value on the SOW or SOW Revision at the time the SOW Worker is submitted
  • Subsequent revisions of the SOW do not affect workers who have already been submitted
  •  If the Custom Field value (from the SOW or SOW Revision during the worker submission) becomes invalid, the field will appear blank

Resolution

  1. Verify if the value is no longer available or valid in the Custom Field Pick List
  2. Update the Custom Field value on the SOW or SOW Revision, where the SOW Worker was created using the Custom Field Update Upload connector
