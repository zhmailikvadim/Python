Symptom

In contract display, system shows duplicate partner details for same partner function in Maintain ACM Trading Contract fiori application.

Replication steps:
1. Open an existing contract in Edit mode.
2. Navigate to the Partner tab at the header level.
3. Add two entries using the same Partner Function, but assign different Partner details to each.
4. Save the contract.
5. Launch the Maintain ACM Trading Contract Fiori application. Open the contract in Display mode.
6. Navigate to the Partner tab and observe that the system incorrectly displays partner details for both entries, despite different values being saved.

Other Terms

Agricultural Contract Management; ACM; Maintain ACM Trading Contract application; Partner

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
