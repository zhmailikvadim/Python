Symptom

Quality management is not active for the material, and in Supplier Evaluation by Quality (Quality Notifications) the quality notification score displays as 100 for supplier/material/document combinations.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA

Cause

When any scores are returned for a purchase order item (Time/Price/Quantity), the quality notification score is set to 100 by design in the Supplier Evaluation by Quality (Quality Notifications) app.

Resolution

  1. Note that this behavior is working as designed.
  2. If Quality Notifications are not used in the business process, unassign the “Supplier Evaluation by Quality (Quality Notifications)” app from relevant roles/catalogs.
  3. Use the “Supplier Evaluation by Quality (Inspection Lot)” app for quality-related scoring if inspection lots are in use.
