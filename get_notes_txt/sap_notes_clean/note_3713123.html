Symptom

  • Two business partners were created using the webservice BusinessPartnerSUITEBulkReplic with identical address UUIDs in the BUT020 table without any error being raised.
  • The issue occurred during BP replication from a non-SAP client to SAP S/4HANA, tested via SPROXY.
  • The system successfully created two business partners with the same address UUID when the same UUID was intentionally passed in the request payload.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access transaction SE80 in the SAP system.
  2. Select "Repository Browser" and choose "Development Object" from the dropdown.
  3. Enter the service name "BUSINESSPARTNERSUITEBULKREPLIC".
  4. Navigate to the service definition and execute it.
  5. Modify the BP under the BP tag in the payload to use the same address UUID.
  6. Execute the service definition.
  7. Observe that a new business partner is created with the same address UUID in the BUT020 table.

Resolution

  • Confirmed that the reported behavior aligns with the system's intended design.
  • It is acceptable for two business partners to share the same address UUID as long as the combination of business partner and address details remains unique.
  • The system reliably retrieves the correct data without conflicts, as the uniqueness is maintained through the business partner and address UUID combination
  • The presence of duplicate address UUIDs across different business partners has no functional impact, provided the business partner and address UUID pair is unique.
