Symptom

"Last Modified Date & Time" and "Last Modified By" fields in Additional Payments in the Latest People Profile are not visible. 

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central
  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite

Resolution

This is the expected behavior as per our current system design. The Latest People Profile UI does not support visibility of Last Modified Date & Time or Last Modified By for Pay Component Recurring and Pay Component Non-Recurring elements, even if configured in the data model. These fields are intended for internal use only.
