Symptom

After changing the approvable owner using Reassign Approvable or Transfer of Ownership, the approval flow that depends on the Requester/Preparer does not update.

Cause

Changing the owner updates the Preparer/Requester fields on the document but does not trigger a new approval evaluation. Because no resubmission happens during ownership transfer, the approval flow continues to follow the routing determined at the previous submission (when the document was initially submitted or re-submitted after an edit).

Resolution

The approvers will only update after the document is resubmitted. For example, when an Edit Rule with the action Edit with Resubmit is triggered.

Only then does the system re‑evaluate approval rules that depend on the Requester or Preparer.
