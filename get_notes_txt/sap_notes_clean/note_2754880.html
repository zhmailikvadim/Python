Symptom

When searching Resources in the choose-from list, you cannot search according to the Resource Std Cost 1 field.

Image/data in this SAP Note is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Reproducing the issue

Prerequisite:

You have at least two resourcesset up in the system.

Scenario:

  1. In the Resource Master Data window, in Find mode, insert an asterisk (*) in the Resource No. field and press Enter on your keyboard.
    The List of Resources window is displayed.
  2. Go to Tools → Form Settings.
  3. Try to locate the Resource Cost 1 field.

Expected behavior:

The Resource Cost 1 field is available in Form Settings.

Actual behavior:

The Resource Cost 1 field is not available in Form Settings.

Cause

System Limitation

Solution

This is a system limitation.
