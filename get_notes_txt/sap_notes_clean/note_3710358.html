Symptom

I am encountering an ANERR XX000000000000000XXXXXXXXXX error while creating a pack item in the ship notice.
while creating the Pack Item, after completing Stage 5, it redirects back to the ANERR page.

Cause

The Advance Packaging feature is only applicable when you are using the SAP Business Network Supply Chain functionality.
In this case, the Supply Chain relationship between the Buyer and Supplier is not correctly enabled.
  • In the Active Relationship Standard View, the relationship Supply Chain is marked as No, whereas in the Advanced View is marked as Yes, or vice versa.
  • Also verify that the Supplier’s account type is set to Standard.

Resolution

If you are a Buyer, please follow the navigation steps below:

  • Go to Supplier Enablement and then select Active Relationship SAP Business Network.
  • Select Advanced View, then check the box for the supplier
  • Click the Action button below, then select Enable/Disable Supply Chain Collaboration.
  • First Disable Supply Chain Collaboration, and then enable it again.

If you are a Supplier, please follow the navigation steps below:

  • Supplier should upgrade their account to an Enterprise account.
