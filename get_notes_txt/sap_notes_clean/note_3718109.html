Symptom

This SAP Note delivers the following changes for the Scottish income tax rates and thresholds effective from 6th April 2026:

  • The threshold for the Starter Rate (19%) increases to £3,967.00
  • The threshold for the Basic Rate (20%) increases to £16,956.00
  • There are no changes to the remaining thresholds or any of the tax rates.

 

Scottish income tax rates and thresholds effective from 6th April 2026:

           Starter rate            19%                 1 –    3,967
  Basic rate             (SBR) 20%           3,968 –  16,956
  Intermediate rate      (SD0) 21%         16,957 –  31,092
  Higher rate               (SD1) 42%         31,093 –  62,430
  Advanced rate (SD2) 45%         62,431 - 125,140
  Top rate      (SD3) 48%       125,141 >

Solution

T5GT1

The Scottish (tax regime = S) threshold of 2,827.00 has been delimited to 05.04.2026 in view T5GT1.
The Scottish (tax regime = S) threshold of 14,921.00 has been delimited to 05.04.2026 in view T5GT1.
The new Scottish (tax regime = S) threshold of 3,967.00 has been created from 06.04.2026 in view T5GT1 with tax rate 19%.
The new Scottish (tax regime = S) threshold of 16,956.00 has been created from 06.04.2026 in view T5GT1 with tax rate 20%.

The rest of the thresholds remain the same as in the previous year.
