Symptom

To assist organizations in future-proofing their Governance, Risk, and Compliance, SAP is coming up with new version of its GRC platform: SAP GRC For SAP HANA 1.0 (also referred to as the SAP GRC 2026 release).

This new release aims to integrate cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning to enhance decision-making, increase efficiency through automation, and reduce risks. Thus, enabling users to proactively address potential issues before they escalate.

Furthermore, the new release will offer new Fiori applications and reporting tools, allowing users to tailor their GRC processes to better fit their unique needs and regulatory requirements.

A fundamental design principle of the new release is architectural harmonization, which is accomplished by integrating previously independent GRC applications into a unified software component. This unification enables shared services, and standardized APIs, reducing system redundancy and operational complexity.

Other Terms

GRC 2026, SAP GRC For SAP HANA, SAP GRC For SAP HANA 1.0, GRC Release 2026

Reason and Prerequisites

In the Product Availability Matrix (PAM), the end date of mainstream maintenance remains unchanged, until 2027 for SAP GRC 12.0 and SAP GRC 12.0 for S/4HANA releases.

SAP GRC for SAP HANA 1.0 represents the next evolution of SAP GRC, unifying SAP GRC 12.0 and SAP GRC 12.0 for S/4HANA into a single HANA-based platform for future innovation.

We are committed to continuous innovation and new features. And for the same we have planned a feature pack for SAP GRC For SAP HANA 1.0, which is scheduled for the end of 2026.

Solution

SAP GRC for SAP HANA provides governance, risk, and compliance management features tailored for SAP HANA environments. The solution addresses emerging risks with automated key risk indicators, enhances security with access management, and continuous threat detection.

The unified SAP GRC for SAP HANA release includes: 

  • SAP GRC Access Control for SAP HANA

SAP GRC Access Control is an enterprise software application that enables organizations to control access and prevent fraud across the enterprise, while minimizing the time and cost of compliance. It helps organizations control user authorizations, detect and mitigate SoD risks and ensure compliance with regulations by preventing unauthorized access. 

  • SAP GRC Process Control for SAP HANA

    SAP GRC Process Control for SAP HANA is an enterprise software solution for compliance and policy management. The compliance management capabilities enable organizations to manage and monitor their internal controls to ensure ongoing compliance. It supports documentation of organization processes, performing internal control assessments, policy distribution and issue remediation. 
  • SAP GRC Risk Management for SAP HANA

SAP GRC Risk Management is an enterprise software solution through which organizations can identify and assess risks and opportunities, determine a response strategy, and monitor progress. It automates risk recognition and assesses likelihood and impact.

  • SAP GRC Assurance and Compliance (ACS) for SAP HANA

SAP GRC Assurance and Compliance (ACS) for SAP HANA is an end-to-end solution that enables organizations to build and plan audits, analyse information, document results, form audit opinions, communicate results, and monitor progress.

    • SAP GRC Business Integrity Screening for SAP HANA

Enables enterprises across all industries to detect, prevent, and deter fraud in big data environments.

    • SAP GRC Audit Management for SAP HANA

Enables you to build and plan audits, analyze information, document results, form audit opinions, communicate results, and monitor progress.

    • SAP GRC Business Partner Screening for SAP HANA

Enables you to check that your business partners are compliant by screening them against lists of persons and organizations.

    • SAP GRC Tax Compliance for SAP HANA

Enables you to find tax compliance issues and supports you in resolving the problems.

  • SAP GRC UI Logging for SAP HANA

SAP GRC UI Logging for SAP HANA allows detailed logging and meaningful analysis of data access through SAP user interfaces and technical interfaces.

  • SAP GRC UI Masking for SAP HANA

SAP GRC UI Masking for SAP HANA protects sensitive and restricted data at the user interface.

Important Dates

  • We are delighted to announce the release of SAP GRC For SAP HANA 1.0, early March 2026, with restricted shipment to our Early Adopter Care (EAC) customers.
  • General Availability (GA) is scheduled for early Q3 2026. During GA, SAP GRC For SAP HANA 1.0 will be made available to all customers.
  • We aim to achieve continuous improvements through Feature Packages (FP). Our plan is to deliver the first feature pack for this release by the end of 2026.

  • Availability for both on-premises and private cloud users
