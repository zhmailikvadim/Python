Symptom

This SAP Note provides essential guidelines and information for the implementation of the RPFIPL_SAFT_VAT report.

It introduces the National e-Invoicing System (KSeF) reference number into the extraction of JPK_VAT ("NrKSeF"), and considers adding other markers to indicate invoices issued during system failures ("OFF") or those not sent via KSeF ("BFK", "DI").

Other Terms

Poland, JPK, Jednolity Plik Kontrolny, JPK_VAT, RPFIPL_SAFT, RPFIPL_SAFT_VAT, FIPL_SAFT, Legal change, SAFT, SAF-T

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Poland.

Solution

Explanation of Changes Implemented for RPFIPL_SAFT_VAT executed through transaction FIPL_SAFT.

To ensure compliance with the latest Polish VAT reporting regulations, we have made several critical updates to our SAP system under the RPFIPL_SAFT_VAT report.

Below is a detailed explanation of the changes implemented:

Related to KSeF:

  • The system now supports the inclusion of the KSeF Reference Number ("NrKSeF") in the JPK_VAT report. This number is crucial for tracking invoices processed via the National e-Invoicing System (KSeF), enhancing traceability and compliance.
  • We have introduced new tags for invoices, including:
    • "OFF": Applies to invoices referred to in Article 106nf(1) of the VAT Act, which, as of the date of submission of the records, do not have an assigned identification in the National e-Invoice System. This field will can be automatically filled only for customers up to SAP_APPL 606.
    • "BFK": Applies to invoices not sent through KSeF, e.g., in paper form.
    • "DI": Applies to other specific identifiers, such as documents that do not have KSeF numbers, have not been sent to KSeF, and do not fall under "OFF" or "BFK".

These tags allow for precise categorization and handling of invoices under various conditions, improving reporting accuracy.

Extend the table FIPLD_VAT_H with new tags: "NrKSeF,"  "OFF," "BFK," and "DI".

Extend the customizing tables FIPLD_TAX_DATA and FIPLV_VAT_TYPMAP with the DI indicator.
