Symptom

Unable to save the BP after making a change to the customer company code data field "Account Memo" and system gives the popup message "Supplier Specific data were not saved due to error VMD_API002".

Environment

  • Sales and Distribution (SD)
  • Logistics Execution (LE)
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open transaction BP.
  2. Search and open the required Business Partner.
  3. Switch to the role FLCU00 – FI Customer.
  4. Navigate to Company Code Data.
  5. Enter the relevant Company Code.
  6. Go to the Customer Correspondence tab.
  7. Update the Account Memo field as required.
  8. Click Save to store the changes.

Cause

In an S/4HANA system, the Business Partner (BP) acts as the leading object, and any synchronization to the Customer or Supplier master is always performed in the current state of the BP data. This synchronization occurs even when the user has not explicitly made any changes to the supplier specific data. During this process, address information is technically updated on the Customer and Supplier side. As this is part of T055G and T055 tables customizing the system interprets this synchronization as a master data change. And the fields needs to be added in the authorization objects F_KNA1_AEN / F_LFA1_AEN. 

These authorizations do not permit users to directly modify Business Partner data. Instead, they are necessary to allow customer and supplier-specific data to be updated and to ensure that BP data can be synchronized in its current state without triggering authorization errors.

Resolution

Authorization must be maintained for all field groups defined in tables T055 and T055G within the Customer and Vendor authorization objects F_KNA1_AEN and F_LFA1_AEN.

If any field group is missing, the system will trigger an authorization error.
