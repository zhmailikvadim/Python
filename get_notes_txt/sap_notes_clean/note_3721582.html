Symptom

You would like to have a functionality that shows the taxable amount based on the Tax Regime selected by the employee, so that employees could know the amount before choosing their Tax Regime. 

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central - Income Tax Declaration

Cause

Currently, in the Employee Central module, there is no standard feature for this functionality and it is not possible to customize or configure one.

Resolution

If you would like to have this idea considered, we recommend submitting an enhancement request specifically about it. For detailed steps on How to Submit Ideas for SAP SuccessFactors Products, please refer to KBA 2090228.
