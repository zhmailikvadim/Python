Symptom

Concern regarding the potential exhaustion of supplier invoice number ranges in SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition due to high-volume usage.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

S/4HANA Public Cloud uses a several large numeric interval avoiding reach the maximum capacity.

Resolution

In SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud, SAP uses very large numeric intervals for invoice documents which are designed to support high-volume usage. 
Supplier invoice number range is typically an internal 9-digit or higher interval.
