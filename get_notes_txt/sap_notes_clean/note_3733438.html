Symptom

You declare your exports from SAP GTS to the French customs system DELTA Export and want to transfer a location of goods with the ID type 'DX' ("Type of Location": D (Other) and "Qualifier of Identification": X (EORI) ).

In accordance with SAP Note 3693247, a unique reference received from the customs authority (message field 'Additional Identifier') must be entered in the "Location of Goods" field. However, the incompleteness check does not complain if this information is missing.
