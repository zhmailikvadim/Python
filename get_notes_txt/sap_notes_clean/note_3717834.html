Symptom

 Start time changes when rescheduling a recurring Instance

Other Terms

BILP, Second, Minute, Increase, send, destination, Instance manager, BI

Reason and Prerequisites

Environement

SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform 2025 Patch 2

Reproducing the issue 

  1. Connect to BI Launch Pad 
  2. Go to Folder and select any Web Inteligence or Crystal report, right click and click on schedule 
  3. In recurring option choose the start time eg. 10:00:00 am and click on schedule 
  4. Go to History and click on the recurring created, right click on it and select reschedule, note that the start time increased to 10:02:42 am
