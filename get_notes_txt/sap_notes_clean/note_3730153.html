Symptom

I've updated my company logo, but the old one is still shown in my Purchase Orders.

Environment

SAP Business Network

Cause

The company logo was updated under the Company Profile, but not in the Upload Company Logo tab under the Getting Started option.

Resolution

To update the Company Logo under the Getting Started option, follow the steps bellow:

  1. On the homepage, click on the Getting Started tab, located to the right of the Overview tab, above the tiles
  2. Click on Upload Company Logo
  3. Click Choose File, then Upload, and finally Done.

Note that the logo file must meet following criteria to upload successfully:

  • File format: GIF (Graphics Interchange Format)
  • Maximum file size: 200 KB
  • Maximum dimensions: 250 pixels width by 250 pixels height
  • Appearance: Transparency is supported. Animation is not supported.
