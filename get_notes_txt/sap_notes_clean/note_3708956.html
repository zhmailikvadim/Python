Symptom

Partner details not updated on Save, under the Expense tab, in the Maintain ACM Trading Contract fiori application.

Replication steps:
1. Launch the Maintain ACM Trading Contract Fiori application.
2. Search for Sales Contract, which hold expense details.
3. Click the 'Copy Contract' button.
4. Navigate to the Expense tab at the header level.
5. Verify that the reference contract details have been successfully copied into the new contract.
6. Save the new contract.
7. Open the contract in Display Mode. Under the Expense tab, the Partner field is blank.

Other Terms

Agricultural Contract Management; ACM; Maintain ACM Trading Contract application; Expense; Partner

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
