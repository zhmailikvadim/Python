Symptom

The list of business objects enabled for natural language search in Fiori Launchpad (FLP).

Other Terms

Natural Language Search, View Business Objects

Reason and Prerequisites

To view the full list of natural language‑enabled search models

Solution

This AI-assisted feature allows users to use natural language for searching business data. Users can enter their search queries in natural language in the SAP Fiori launchpad search bar to find the information they need.
