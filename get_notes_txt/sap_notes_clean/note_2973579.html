General Information

No service interuption is expected as a result of this upgrade.

SAP recommends to fulfill the new minor version pre-requisites as soon as possible and switch to the new app version. Additional maintenance can only be offered based on the newest app version as corrections cannot be provided on individual older versions.
The minor versions have a backend pre-requisite which must be fulfilled before you can activate the new minor version in your account.

SAP Fiori Cloud is offering the patches for apps based on the released fixes in the corresponding ABAP frontend server product.  The content of the individual patches is described in the general sections of the ABAP notes.
