Symptom

You first call a patient in the patient organizer and then search for another patient, for example, by entering the birthdate, after the patient organizer is called again (transaction N1PATORG).

You notice that the patient number that belongs to the previous patient is preset in the search screen.
After you select the "Find" function, the system displays the previous patient instead of the newly searched patient in the patient organizer. 

Other Terms

N1PATORG, PATNR, RNPA1-PATNR

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

The patient organizer now deletes the field for the patient number when you start a new search.
As a result, the system correctly no longer selects the previous patient when you start a new search.

Import the specified Support Packages to solve the problem.
You can implement the corrections in advance.
