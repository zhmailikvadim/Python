Symptom

Why am I receiving Task cannot be started even though its predecessors have been completed notification if registration approval has been completed?

Cause

  • The approval of the external questionnaire is placed as the first step in the current flow, whereas it is intended to be the last step.
  • Notification tasks are not supported in Supplier Registration Projects  

Resolution

To avoid this message:

  • Edit the notification profile of the approval task instead of adding a notification task.
  • Verify the configuration flow and ensure the approval of the external questionnaire is placed as the last step, as intended.
