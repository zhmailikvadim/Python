Symptom

In the process "Sending of Quotation (MOS View)" (DE_B_MOSB_QUOT_MOS), step 0020 incorrectly determines the time slice change scenario when the time slice is not relevant to the DSO.

This issue is relevant for the market roles: MOS, SUP.

Other Terms

Meter Operator Contract, Time Slice, Price Catalog, Change Scenario, /US4G/CL_DEF_BADI_CTR_DET, /US4G/IF_BADI_CTR_DET~DET_TS_CHG_SCNEARIO, leading MaLo, leading supplier

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system correctly determines the time slice change scenario even when the time slice is not relevant to the DSO.

You can verify the fix by checking that when the leading MaLo, leading supplier, and price catalog for the supplier are not changed, and the time slice is not relevant to the DSO, the change scenario is not determined as "Price Catalog Change".
