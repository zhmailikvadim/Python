Symptom

During the handover of the Source Engineering BOM to the Shop Floor BOM, the system is allowing External Engineering Snapshots instead of restricting the process to Internal Engineering Snapshots only.

Other Terms

source BOM, Source Engineering BOM, target BOM, Shop Floor BOM

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

By implementing this Note, the handover of the Source Engineering BOM to the Shop Floor BOM will allow only an internal engineering snapshot during the handover process.
