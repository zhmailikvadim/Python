Symptom

In certain system constellations, expanding the BOM item container of a versioned material BOM may result in an error message related to material number formatting.

Other Terms

 PLM-42137, ECTR, Versioned BOM, /DSCSAG/MAT_BOM_READ, /DSCSAG/MAT_BOM_READ2

Reason and Prerequisites

In specific scenarios, material number conversion was not executed consistently for versioned BOMs.

The issue may occur in systems where versioned BOMs are used and the material number format configuration restricts certain external representations.

Solution

The material number conversion logic was adjusted to ensure consistent handling for versioned and non‑versioned BOMs.
The following functional enhancements were implemented:

  • In /DSCSAG/MAT_BOM_READ, redundant conversion of the input material number was removed, as the number is already be provided in internal format.
  • In /DSCSAG/MAT_BOM_READ2, the internal‑to‑external conversion was harmonized for both versioned and non‑versioned BOM.
