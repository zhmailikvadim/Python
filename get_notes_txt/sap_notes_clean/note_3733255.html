Symptom

In the process "Receipt of Status of EQS Time Series Version (DSO View)" (DE_C_EQS_STATUS_RECEIVE_DSO), step 0400 stops and does not proceed to the subsequent steps.

This issue is relevant for the market role DSO.

Other Terms

Step 0400, precondition, EQS time series, DE_C_EQS_STATUS_RECEIVE_DSO, BC set, status receipt

Reason and Prerequisites

The system stops at step 0400 because of an incorrect BC set configuration in the process DE_C_EQS_STATUS_RECEIVE_DSO.

Solution

After implementing this SAP Note, the system corrects the configuration by activating the BC set /US4G/1_3_DSO_SP09_NOTE_3733255. The process now runs the subsequent steps as expected.

Re-run the process to apply the correction. To verify the fix, check that the process runs all steps successfully without stopping at step 0400.
