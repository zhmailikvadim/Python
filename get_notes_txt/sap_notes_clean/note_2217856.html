Symptom

The posting window during Allocation Preview is taking time, instead of single update, bulk updates is incorporated

Other Terms

Allocation preview, Allocation of orders, Single update, Bulk Updates.

Reason and Prerequisites

Due to Single inserts in HANA through SOAR application, the posting window of the allocation process is taking time

Solution

The posting window during Allocation Preview is taking time, instead of single update, bulk updates is incorporated during the posting window of the allocation run.
