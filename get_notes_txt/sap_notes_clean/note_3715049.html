Symptom

When attempting to post a Service Entry Sheet in transaction ML81N under budget control by cost center, the system raises error FCO_AVC_CC 202 if the Availability Control (AVC) budget is exceeded. 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Service PO = Created in year 2025
  2. Cost Center budget is available in year 2025
  3. Posting a Service Entry Sheet in ML81n.  Posting Date = 2026 (Invoice Date = 2025)
  4. After SES approval/acceptance Error reaised: “There is no budget available for cost center”

Cause

In fact, there is no budget available to the cost center xxx in the fiscal year 2026. Even though the commitment was posted in 2025, the posting of a GR/IR/SES in the new fiscal year 2026 will reduce the commitment of 2025 and post the actual cost to year 2026. Therefore, the commitment value will return zero the budget in 2025. 

Resolution

The error message FCO_AVC_CC 202 is a correct error. You can either transfer the budget of 2025 to 2026 or import new budget. 

See Also

Pilot Note 3466996 will allow the message FCO_AVC_CC201 to show the amount that exceeds the available budget. 

Keywords

commitment, FCO_AVC_CC202, SES
