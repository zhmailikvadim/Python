Symptom

  • Users are unable to locate future-dated hires in the data replication monitor search, despite other records being visible.
  • No error messages or codes are explicitly mentioned in the case information.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Data Replication Monitor

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the Data Replication Monitor (DRM).
  2. Search for a future-dated hire by name or ID.
  3. Observe that the future-dated hire is unavailable in the search results, while other records are visible.

Cause

External users will appear in search results once the following setting is disabled: Company and Logo Settings -> Hide External Users from search results

Resolution

Go to Company and Logo Settings and uncheck "Hide External Users from search results".
