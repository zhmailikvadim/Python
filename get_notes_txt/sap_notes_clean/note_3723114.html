Symptom

When Advanced CPE Migration was executed for contracts in which multiple cancellations were performed from Maintain ACM pricing applications in background mode,
following issues were identified related to migration cancellation.

Issue 1: Cancellation data not migrated, Price lot quantity was incorrect.
Issue 2: Price quantity and Cancellation quantity incorrect on CPE_PRCFIXGRP and /ACCGO/T_PR_ASP.

Other Terms

Agricultural Contract Management; ACM; Cancellation; Advanced CPE; Data migration

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
