Symptom

  • Fixed assets error due to missing profit center.
  • The filed Profit Center is greyed out in the master data 
  • Error message: "Account object Profit Center differs from that in asset master record" (Message No. AAPO190).

Environment

  • FIN Asset Accounting
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Cause

The "Bal.sheet" indicator is set for the profit center field in the IMG customizing under Asset Accounting > Integration with General Ledger Accounting > Additional Account Assignment Objects > Activate Account Assignment Objects. This configuration prevents editing the profit center field once the asset has been capitalized.

Resolution

  1. Navigate to IMG customizing under Asset Accounting > Integration with General Ledger Accounting > Additional Account Assignment Objects > Activate Account Assignment Objects.
  2. Deactivate the "Bal.sheet" indicator for the profit center field (PRCTR).
  3. Use transaction AS02 to edit the profit center field in the asset master data.
  4. If avoiding organization change transfer posting is required, use program FAGL_ASSET_MASTERDATA_UPD to derive the profit center directly from the cost center after removing the "Bal.sheet" indicator.
