Symptom

In the Report and Repair Malfunction app, selecting 'Pause Work' from the 'Status' dropdown on the 'Job Details' tab (Work Items) and saving does not persist. The status reverts to 'In Process'. In customizing for 'Overall Status Profiles', the event 'Set Job To Interrupted' (RMJI) is not available for selection.

Other Terms

F2023, Report and Repair Malfunction, CL_EAM_BUSINESS_TRANSACTIONS, IF_EAM_BUSINESS_TRANSACTIONS~GET_STATUS_EVENTS, IF_MAINT_ORDER_ACTION_ALLOWED, MAINTJOB, RMJI, Set Job To Interrupted

Reason and Prerequisites

The reason is a program error. Status event determination in class CL_EAM_BUSINESS_TRANSACTIONS (method IF_EAM_BUSINESS_TRANSACTIONS~GET_STATUS_EVENTS) omits handling for the “Set Job To Interrupted” activity (RMJI)

Solution

Install this SAP Note. The correction restores the RMJI (Set Job To Interrupted) business transaction so it becomes available in Overall Status Profiles and can be assigned to statuses. No further actions are required.
