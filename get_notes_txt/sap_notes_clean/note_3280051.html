Symptom

What is the specification of the email address that can be sent from SAP Customer Data Cloud?

Environment

  • SAP Customer Data Cloud
  • Customer Identity

Resolution

The email addresses allowed follows RFC 822 standard. Per SAP documentation: "SAP Customer Data Cloud validates all email addresses per RFC 822 as a minimum requirement, regardless of any custom regex, which is checked in addition to the RFC."

Keywords
