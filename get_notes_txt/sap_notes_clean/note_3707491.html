Symptom

• The invoice is relevant for invoice lists when an invoice list calendar ('01' in the standard system) is defined for the payer in the master data.
• An invoice list type ('LR' or 'LG') must also be defined for the relevant billing type in customizing.
• When the item division differs from the header division, the invoice list date (VBRK-FKDAT_RL) is determined from the invoice list calendar for the payer, maintained based on the item division rather than the header division in the BP master data under BP > Customer role > Sales and Distribution > Billing > Field 'Invoice List Schedule (calendar identification)'.

Environment

  • Sales And Distribution (SD)
  • Logistics Execution (LE)
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise 4.7
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. In BP, maintain the invoice list calendar for the payer for division 00 as Y1.
  2. In BP, maintain the invoice list calendar for the payer for division 01 as Y2.
  3. Create an invoice with item division VBRP-SPART = 01 while the header division VBRK-SPART = 00.
  4. Observe that the invoice list date (VBRK-FKDAT_RL) is determined from invoice list calendar Y2 rather than Y1. 

Cause

Invoice list date (VBRK-FKDAT_RL) is determined based on KURGV-PERRL, which is derived from VBRP-SPART, rather than VBRK-SPART as per standard design. The division at the item level takes precedence over the header level.

Resolution

1. If you wish to modify the standard behavior, explore developing custom coding within the user exit USEREXIT_NEWROLE_XVBPAK_AVBPAK or USEREXIT_NEWROLE_XVBPAP_AVBPAK (program RV60AFZD) to adjust KURGV-PERRL.
2. As this is considered a modification in the standard system, refer to SAP Note 170183 - What to consider when applying modifications.
