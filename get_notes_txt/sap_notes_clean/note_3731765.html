Symptom

How to set up messaging feature in Business Network Buyer Account?

Resolution

To view messaging feature in Business Network account, the administrator of the account must follow the below steps:

  1. Sign in to SAP Business Network Buyer account.
  2. Click your user initials in the top-right corner and select Settings.
  3. Go to Account Administration >> Messaging Configuration.
  4. Click Edit, and select the checkbox Allow this account to use messaging in SAP Business Network.
  5. Click Save.
