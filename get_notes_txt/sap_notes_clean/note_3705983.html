Symptom

In your company, both serialized and non-serialized materials are used.

When sending Quality Certificates to customers, two different form templates are required for the output type QUALITY_CERTIFICATE:

  • Form Template for serialized materials
  • Form Template for non-serialized materials

This article will guide you how to configure this in SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition system.

*Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Resolution

To configure this, navigate to the Output Parameter Determination app and utilize the "Serial Numbers" condition in the determination step Form Template. Below are the detailed steps:

  1. In the App Output Parameter Determination do a right click on Inspection Lot and then click on Refresh Condition Parameters of Application.
  2. Click on Yes in the pop-up window.
  3. The operation will be executed successfully.
  4. Add the column "Serial Numbers" to the condition table of the determination step Form Template.
    1. Enter Edit mode, then click on "Table Settings".

    2. Click on "Insert Column", then select "From Context Data Objects".

    3. Add the Object "Serial Numbers".

  5. Maintain the settings for the "Serial Numbers" column.
    1. The rules are described at the Maintaining Business Rules for Inspection Lot Form Template help page.


    2. Below is an example of what the proper setting looks like.
  6. After activating the rules, system can select different Quality Certificate form templates based on whether a serialized material is used in the inspection lot.
