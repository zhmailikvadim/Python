Symptom

Purchase orders or purchase requisitions are not updated at the APO system. Instead of this queues with SYSFAIL status are created. When checking the details of the queue you will notice the error message 'Ordertype XXX OrderNo XXX ItemNo XXX SchedNo XXX'.

Other Terms

Purchase order, purchase requisition, /SAPAPO/RRP502, SMQ1, SMQ2, inbound queue, outbound queue, status text

Reason and Prerequisites

  • At transaction SFW1 you notice that the switch CIF_SWFS_GEN_ADDON is active at your S4/HANA system.

Solution

Implement the correction attached to this SAP Note into your S4/HANA system or implement the relevant Support Package.
