Symptom

Deletion of Line item not working in extended app Manage Purchase Requisition Professional App (F2229) after upgrade to S/4 HANA 2025

Other Terms

Manage Purchase Requisition Professional App, F2229, Item Deletion, Limit Item Deletion, refreshItemTableOnDelete, Undefined Error

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

With S/4HANA 2025, SAP introduced a new standard method refreshItemTableOnDelete in the ObjectPage controller which gets triggered on the press of “Delete” button. This method directly references the “Limit Item” table . If the “Limit Item” tab has been removed via UI adaptation, the table within it is not available in the view, and the reference returns undefined, leading to the error.
