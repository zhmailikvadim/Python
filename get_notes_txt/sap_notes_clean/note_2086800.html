Symptom

When you delete implausible meter readings (EL27), the system does not correctly delete the entries in the table EABLQDCONV.

When you display the conversion steps in the EL31, these are displayed more than once.

Other Terms

QD QD_RPOC

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

You have switched to quantity determination during meter reading.

Solution

Implement the correction instructions in this SAP Note or import the corresponding support package.

The correction does not only affect quantity determination during meter reading. However, the change should not have any other effects.
