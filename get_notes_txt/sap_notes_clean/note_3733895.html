Symptom

When creating a new subaccount in the SAP BTP Cloud Foundry environment, the EU01 and EU02 region does not appear as an available option, though the user has all the permissions to create a subaccount.

Environment

  • SAP Business Technology Platform
  • Cloud Foundry Environment

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login to SAP BTP Cockpit.
  2. Click on Create Button -> Subaccount
  3. While selecting the subaccount region, the EU01 and EU02 does not appear in the list

Cause

CF EU01 and EU02 region are only available for Global Accounts that are EU_ACCESS enabled.

Resolution

It is a known behavior as the EU01 and EU02 are only allowed to be created under the Global Accounts that are EU_ACCESS enabled.

In case, you are not sure, if your Global Account is EU_ACCESS enabled, the SAP Technical Support team can assist you in confirming the same. Please raise a case in BC-NEO-CIS component.

If the Global Account is not EU_ACCESS enabled and you would like to enable it for the EU_ACCEESS, please reach out to your Account Executive as per the below document

SAP KBA 2626344 - Who is the SAP Account Manager for my company?
