Symptom

The Service Spend Report (Design Studio) F3142 App is Deprecated.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA 2021
  • SAP S/4HANA 2022
  • SAP S/4HANA 2023 

Cause

Most SAP Design Studio apps were deprecated in SAP S/4HANA 2021 and will be deleted from the system as of SAP S/4HANA 2022. 

Resolution

The Web Dynpro apps are the successor apps. We recommend that you switch to the Service Spend (Web Dynpro) app as soon as possible.

Service Spend (Web Dynpro)
