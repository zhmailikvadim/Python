Symptom

You use SAP Travel Management for S/4 HANA Fiori application "My Travel and Expenses for Business Traveler" or "Travel and Expenses for Travel Assistant".

When viewing the Per Diem section of your Travel Expense, you may notice the following typos:

  • “Meals per Diem” should appear as “Meals Per Diem”
  • “Accommodations per Diem” should appear as “Accommodations Per Diem”

Other Terms

MTE, Travel Expenses, Travel Expense Report

F6190, F0584B

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error: Correction to labels for PerDiem which includes Accommodations Per Diem and Meals Per Diem.

Solution

Import the correction instruction or apply the corresponding support package.
