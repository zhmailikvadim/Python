Symptom

  • The fields Reviewer's signature and date are visible in completed forms despite the business rule being configured to hide them.
  • The business rule execution log is empty, indicating the rule was not triggered upon opening the completed form.

Environment

Performance Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Proxy as the user.
  2. Navigate to the performance module and access the completed folder.
  3. Open the form affected form.
  4. Observe that the fields "Reviewer's signature and date" are visible, despite the business rule conditions being met.
  5. Check the business rule execution log, which is empty, indicating the rule was not triggered.

Cause

Currently, the business rule does not support triggering for forms in the signature stage or completed status.

Resolution

  1. The business rule can be configured to trigger only for forms in the modify stage.
  2. Currently it does not support for Signature or Completed forms.
