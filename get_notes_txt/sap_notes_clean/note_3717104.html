Symptom

Can the Bank Control Key field be configured as mandatory only for specific countries?

Cause

 Bank Control Key is a predefined field in the Bank Account question hence you cannot edit the field.

Resolution

Currently, the Bank Control Key cannot be made mandatory, as this field is only imported and is not configurable.

Only the Bank Key can be configured based on requirements, please refer How can I customize questions of type Bank Account?
