Symptom

How do I configure External Tax Invoice Rules in SAP Business Network ?

Resolution

Please follow the steps to configure external tax invoice rules:

  1. Sign in to Buyer account
  2. Click [User Initials] on the top right corner > Click Settings
  3. Under Transactions - Click External Tax Invoice Rules
