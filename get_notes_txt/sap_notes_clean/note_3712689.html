Symptom

This SAP Note contains information related to upgrades to SAP Business One, add-on for ELSTER Patch Level 12, from previous releases.

Solution

You can download the SAP Business One, add-on for ELSTER, either as an installation package or as an upgrade package from the Software Downloads section of Software Center. Download the ELSTER add-on package from Software Center then register and install the new ELSTER add-on patch level from the package that you downloaded.

Compatibility Information

ELSTER Add-on Patch Level You Are Upgrading to a Higher Patch of SAP Business One 10.0 or SAP Business One 10.0, version for SAP HANA You Are Performing a Fresh Installation of SAP Business One 10.0 or SAP Business One 10.0, version for SAP HANA
12

Meets requirements of ELSTER Rich-Client (ERiC) version 43.3.2.0 as described in SAP Note 3690203.

  • All SAP Business One 10.0 patch and feature pack levels, version for SAP HANA

  • All SAP Business One 10.0 patch and feature pack levels
  • All SAP Business One 10.0 patch and feature pack levels, version for SAP HANA
