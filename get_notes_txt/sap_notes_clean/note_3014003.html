Symptom

GR confirmation or replacement from EWM leads to error message /SPE/INBVAL008: "PO items are partially TPOP".

Other Terms

/SPE/INBVAL008, EKPO-PSTYP, TPOP, EWM

Reason and Prerequisites

Inbound deliveries are NOT created with SPM functionality (VL60, Idoc FM /SPE/IDOC_INPUT_DESADV1), but e.g. with classic transaction like VL31N. In this process, you can mix up PO items that are relevant for GR with TPOP items that are not relevant for GR within the same EWM warehouse. The TPOP items have been configured properly with a confirmation control key (no GR assignment) that leads to non-GR relevance (LIPS-NOWAB = X) and non-EWM relevance (LIPS-LFDEZ = X) in the inbound delivery. The other items are distributed to EWM and shall be posted GR.
