Symptom

It is not possible to restrict the aggregation of capacity on pegging area.

Other Terms

/SAPAPO/SDP94, interactive planning, capacity view, resource, product, location, consumption, order.

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing functionality.

Solution

Implement the correction instructions and implement the new method GET_CAPA_USED_PEGGING_AREA of the BAdI /SAPAPO/SDP_INTERACT.
