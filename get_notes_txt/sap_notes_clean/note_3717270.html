Symptom

After returning to Ariba from a punchout session, the customer is receiving the following error message:

“PunchOut Catalog Shopping Unsuccessful: Requisition XXX has not been updated with the items from your shopping cart.”

Reproducing the Issue

SAP Ariba Catalog Management

Cause

The supplier is sending invalid <From> </From> and <To> </To> credentials in the PunchOut Order Message. 

Example: 

<From>
<Credential domain="DUNS">
<Identity>XXXXX</Identity>
</Credential>
</From>
<To>
<Credential domain="DUNS">
<Identity>XXXXXX</Identity>
</Credential>
</To>

Resolution

  1. Ask the supplier to verify the credentials being sent in the PunchOut Order Message. 
  2. Correct the invalid credentials provided in the PunchOut Order Message. 
  3. Test the PunchOut session and ensure the requisition updates successfully with the shopping cart items.
