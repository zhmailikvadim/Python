Symptom

  • The Time Sheet has a Rate Schedule
  • The Time Sheet has been marked as Complete Later by worker
  • The Time Sheet status is Pending Allocation, next step is Need to edit Tasks

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Worker fills in the hours on page 1, Enter Time
  2. Clicks on Continue to proceed to page 2, Edit Tasks
  3. Clicks on Complete Later

Time Sheet status is now Pending Allocation

Resolution

To move forward, the worker needs to click on Edit Task to submit their Time Sheets, or they can click on Edit to adjust the hours on page 1 if needed.
