Symptom

When Transaction F-53 Payment is processed against the vendor invoice along with an advance payment, an unexpected withholding tax line item with is posted, which is incorrect. 

Environment

  • Financial Accounting
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP ERP Central component
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • This is relevant for MENA countries Saudi Arabia Finance Localization. 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Process a vendor advance payment of 1000 SAR with 5% withholding tax, resulting in 50 SAR WHT being posted correctly.
  2. Post a vendor invoice for 5000 SAR, where the withholding tax base is calculated as 4000 SAR for 5% WHT, resulting in 200 SAR WHT being posted correctly.
  3. Process the final payment via F-53 against the vendor invoice of 5000 SAR and the advance payment of 1000 SAR.
  4. Observe that an unexpected withholding tax line item with Dr 50 is generated, which should not occur.

Cause

Incorrect process followed during payment handling in this business case

Resolution

  1. Refer to Saudi Arabia Help Documentation, which states that withholding tax is applicable only at the time of final payment.
  2. Avoid withholding tax calculation during down payment and invoice posting to prevent the generation of extra withholding tax lines.
