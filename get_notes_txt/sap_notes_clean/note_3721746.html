Symptom

  • The issue occurs when attempting to use product bundles in service orders within SAP S/4HANA Service private cloud.
  • Bundle items do not explode to allow selection of parts and services in service orders.

Environment

SAP S/4HAHA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a service order
  2. Attempt to add a product bundle item in service order.
  3. Observe that bundle item does not explode to allow selection of parts and services.

Resolution

Product bundle is supported only in solution quotations. Service orders do not allow this functionality by design.
