Symptom

  • I can only see one task code on my time sheet and there should be more
  • I am missing task codes on my time sheet

Resolution

You can add tasks assigned to you, that are not showing on your time sheet:

  1. Sign into your account
  2. Under To-Do Section on the home page click Complete Time Sheet
  3. Click + Add Task button
  4. From the list, tick the checkbox for the task to add > click Add Task

Task Codes assigned to your time sheet are defined and managed by the company you work on contract for. If you do not see the task code you want to add, contact your supervisor. They can confirm the exact task codes you should select or help add them

Please note: You may need to clear your browser's cache once your Task Codes have been updated in order to see them
