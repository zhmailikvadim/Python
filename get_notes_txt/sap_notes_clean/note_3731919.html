Symptom

  • When converting a subcontracting purchase requisition from the Process Purchase Requisitions V2 app, the resulting purchase order does not contain batch numbers for BOM components.
  • BOM components are determined, but their batch and storage location field are not transferred into the purchase order.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Process Purchase Requisitions V2 app and select a subcontracting purchase requisition (for example, created from a maintenance order).
  2. Convert the requisition into a purchase order (the flow navigates to the Manage Purchase Orders app).
  3. Open the resulting purchase order and check the subcontracting BOM components: batch numbers are not populated.

Cause

Missing feature: the Manage Purchase Orders app, called during conversion from Process Purchase Requisitions V2, does not currently support storage location and batch fields for subcontracting BOM components.

Resolution

Use one of these workarounds to maintain and transfer batches for subcontracting components:

  • Create or process the purchase order in the Create Purchase Order – Advanced (WEBGUI) app.
  • Use the Process Purchase Requisitions app (alternative to the V2 flow) where applicable.
