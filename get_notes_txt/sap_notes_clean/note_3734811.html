Symptom

 Contract status does not change to ‘Awaiting Amendment Approval’ after editing Item fee details.

-Steps to replicate:
1) Open an approved trading contract using transaction WB22.
2) Navigate to the contract item fee details section.
3) Edit or update the fee details at the item level.
4) Save the contract.
5) Re-open the contract in WB22/WB23 to verify the status.

Other Terms

Agriculture Contract Management; ACM; Item Fee; Awaiting Amendment Approval status

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
