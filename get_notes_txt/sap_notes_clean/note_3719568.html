Symptom

In Dynatrace, you may observe that the following process shows multiple restarts:

  • npd-log-counter

  • log-counter

Despite the visible restart count:

  • No Kubernetes pods are restarting

  • No application containers are restarting

  • No SAP Commerce Cloud services are disrupted

  • No business functionality is affected

The behaviour is limited to the infrastructure process itself.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

SAP Commerce Cloud

Cause

The npd-log-counter / log-counter process is a host-level infrastructure component.

It is:

  • Not a Kubernetes pod

  • Not part of the application workload

  • Not related to SAP Commerce Cloud services

  • Not responsible for business logic execution

This process is periodically started and managed at the Kubernetes node level. Its lifecycle behavior, including restarts, is expected and part of normal cluster operations.

Dynatrace monitors both application workloads and host-level processes. As a result, these normal infrastructure restarts may appear in monitoring views, even though they do not indicate instability or failure.

Resolution

No corrective action is required.

If preferred, Dynatrace can be configured to:

  • Exclude infrastructure-level processes such as npd-log-counter and log-counter from alerting

  • Adjust monitoring rules to focus on Kubernetes pods and relevant application workloads

This helps reduce monitoring noise and ensures alerts remain aligned with application health and business impact.
