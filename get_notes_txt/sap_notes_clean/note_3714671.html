Symptom

In the process "End of Supply for Electricity (DSO View)" (DE_B_GPKE_DSO_EOS), in step 2000-0300, messages 55613 and 55670 are not sent to the Transmission System Operator (TSO) from the DSO.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

Transmission, TSO, 55613, 55670, GPKE_TRIG_SETTL_MAIN_PROC, Settlement Data Change, End of Supply, UBD data, DE_B_GPKE_DSO_EOS

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because the Transmission Service Provider was not appended if there was a supplier in the immediate future from End of Supply in the DSO view.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.
After implementing this SAP Note, the system adds the Transmission Service Provider in the UBD data, and messages 55613 and 55670 are sent to the TSO from the DSO. You need to retrigger the process. You can verify the fix by checking that messages 55613 and 55670 are sent to the TSO from the DSO.
