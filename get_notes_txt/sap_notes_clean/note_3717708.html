Symptom

In the Adjustment Hub, during a partial approval scenario, the approved documents are sent to CVPM in a synchronous call for each document individually.

As a result:

A separate synchronous call is triggered for every single approved document.

This may lead to performance degradation when multiple documents are partially approved at the same time.

Increased response time may be observed due to multiple sequential synchronous calls to CVPM.

System resources may experience higher load depending on the number of approved documents.

This behavior occurs specifically when the approval status is partial and multiple documents are involved in the same approval process.

Other Terms

FCFPSL , Financial control, Manual Posting , Business Transaction , Partial approval , Journal Entry Number

Reason and Prerequisites

Programming error
