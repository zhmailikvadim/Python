Symptom

SAP delivers several scores in Predictive Studio with persistence enabled. This means that score values are calculated on a regular basis. Depending on the complexity of the score, this can generate significant load on the system. Customers cannot disable this behaviour directly in Predictive Studio.

Other Terms

Predictive Studio, Persistence, Scores: Sentiment Score, Account Engagement Score, Engagement Score, Interest Affinity

Reason and Prerequisites

SAP‑delivered scores were intended to be non‑changeable.

Solution

After implementing the correction instructions provided in this note, execute report CUAN_SP_DISABLE_PERSISTENCE in the back end.
