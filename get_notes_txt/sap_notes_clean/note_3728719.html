Symptom

When using the Manage Multilevel Bill of Material app (F5238), the Selected Date field (validity date) does not behave as expected for historical lookups.


While the system correctly accepts and displays the BOM structure for future dates, it fails to retain a past date. If a user manually enters or selects a date in the past, the field automatically reverts to the current date (today). Consequently, the app cannot display the BOM components or structure that were valid at that specific point in history, even if the BOM was managed with a Change Master/Number.
