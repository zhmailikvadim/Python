Symptom

Character limits on pre-screening questions within the SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting module.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Reproducing the Issue

Character Limit for Screening Questions in "My Saved Questions" Preferences Section

Cause

Characters limit

Resolution


In SAP Recruiting Management, the character limit for pre-screening questions varies based on the field type. Single-line text fields allow up to 255 characters and upto 4,000 characters.
