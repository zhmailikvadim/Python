Symptom

When you try to enhance the Partner Determination on checklist item level of the Service Order with COM_PARTNER_BADI, there are no values of IV_STEP_ID and IV_CHKLST_ID passed to the BADI.

Environment

  • SAP Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP CRM
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP CRM, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

Set the breakpoint on DETERMINATION_ADD_IN_1 (Or DETERMINATION_ADD_IN_2, or DETERMINATION_ADD_IN_3) of COM_PARTNER_BADI

Reproduce the steps and you can find that no values of IV_STEP_ID and IV_CHKLST_ID are imported.

Cause

The variables that are passed to the Partner Determination BADI are specific to Service Requests and are not populated by the generic call to partner determination in the CRM_PARTNER_DETERM_*_EC function modules.

In FM CRM_SRQM_CHKLST_F_S_DETERM_EC, chklst_id is retrieved by a call to CRM_SRV_REQ_H_READ_OB(Line 70) and step_id is retrieved from calls to cl_crm_srqm_chklst_ver_utility=>get_steps_for_chklst or cl_crm_chklst_img_access=>get_steps_for_chklst (lines 161 - 177).

The CRM_PARTNER_DETERM_*_EC are generic FM that are used for all transaction types in CRM. CRM does not put in specific logic to retrieve from particular transaction types (i.e. Service Requests). It is more efficient that this be done in the BADI.

Resolution

This is the Standard behavior of the system. COM_PARTNER_BADI cannot be used on checklist item level of Service Orders.

Keywords

COM_PARTNER_BADI, IV_STEP_ID, IV_CHKLST_ID, CRM_SRQM_CHKLST_F_S_DETERM_EC, CRM_SRV_REQ_H_READ_OB, Enhancement of Partner Determination on Checklist Item Level.
