Symptom

Serial numbers are assigned to a delivery and to its handling unit (HU) and a goods movement is done. When this goods movement is reversed with transaction VL09, the serial numbers get deleted from the delivery.

Cause

Intended program design.

During a goods issue, the serial numbers are copied from the HU into the delivery (and from there further into the material document). In this process, the serial numbers originally present in the delivery are deleted from the delivery. In the event of a reversal of the goods issue, the program attempts to undo everything, and the serial numbers copied from the HU are removed from the delivery again. However, at this point, it is no longer known which serial numbers were originally assigned to the delivery, and therefore the delivery remains without serial numbers.

Resolution

If HUs are used, serial numbers should rather not be entered in the delivery in the first place, but in the HU only. Consequently, the serialization procedures SDLS "Maintain delivery" and SDRE "Maintain returns delivery" should be set to "02 Optional". (If your specific business process requires mandatory serialization, the procedures SDCC "Completness check for delivery" and SDCR "Completion check IR delivery" should be set to "03 Obligatory" while SDLS and SDRE get the value "02 Optional".)

Keywords

reversal, cancellation
