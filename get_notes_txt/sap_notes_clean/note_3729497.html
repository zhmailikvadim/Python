Symptom

Users attempting to enable Account Segmentation within the Turkey localization are unable to do so when using the predefined CATR Chart of Accounts (CoA). 

Environment

SAP Business One 

Reproducing the Issue

Scenario:

  1. Go to Administration → System Initialization → Company Details → Basic Initialization.
  2. Notice that on field Chart of Accounts Template the CoA is set to CATR.
  3. Try to select the checkbox Use Segmentation Accounts.

Expected Behavior: The checkbox Use Segmentation Accounts is available for selection.  

Actual Behavior: The checkbox Use Segmentation Accounts is disabled (grayed out). 

Cause

Correct behavior 

Resolution

This is the correct behavior. 

In the Turkey localization, account segmentation requires a User-Defined Chart of Accounts (CoA).

The predefined CoA CATR does not use segmentation and cannot be converted into a segmented CoA.   

Therefore, to enable Use Segmentation Accounts during a new company setup, users must select a User-Defined CoA in field Chart of Accounts Template.  

Once a company is created with a non-segmented predefined CoA (such as CATR), segmentation cannot be enabled later.
