Symptom

In transaction AR01, a fixed asset is listed despite having no current values.

Environment

  • Contract and Lease Management
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access transaction AR01.
  2. Execute it with the following parameters:
  • Company code: XXXX
  • Report date: 31.12.YYYY
  • Depreciation area: 01
  • Sort Variant: 0001

Cause

The fixed asset is displayed in AR01 due to its master data containing 'Capitalized on' and 'First Acquisition on' fields, even though it does not have current values.

Resolution

  1. Verify the master data of the fixed asset to confirm the presence of 'Capitalized on' and 'First Acquisition on' fields.
  2. Filters applied in AR01 are designed to retrieve such information based on the asset's master data.
