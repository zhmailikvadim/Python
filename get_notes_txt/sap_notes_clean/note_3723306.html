Symptom

  • Place holders based on personalization mapping objects in publication with Web Intelligence documebts are not visible in email/SFTP destination.

Other Terms

WebI, webintelligence, LaunchPad, CMC, locale, global, Add Placeholder

Reason and Prerequisites

Environment:

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 4.3
  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 2025
  • Publishing

Steps To Reproduce:

  1. Create a publication in BI Launchpad with Web Intelligence documents.
  2. Choose to use dynamic recipient and setup local personalization.
  3. Choose email as destination and configure email content
  4. In Email Subject or Message click button "Add PlaceHolder"
  5. Within the list displayed
    1. >> Notice that there is no placeholder for the personalization mapping object.
  6. This issue arises only when creating the Publication. When scheduling or editing the Publication placeholders related to personalization are displayed correctly.
  7. Issue does not occur from Central Management Console User Interface.

Cause:

  • This issue is caused by a defect in the product.

Solution

This issue is fixed in the patches listed in the "Support Packages & Patches" section below.
Due to different internal processes, the "Support Packages & Patches" section of the SAP Note will be populated as below:

  • for fixes delivered in "Support Packages": immediately (even if the "Support Package" is planned to be released several months later)
  • for fixes delivered in "Patches": ONLY once the "Patch" is released AND available on SMP (not before)
