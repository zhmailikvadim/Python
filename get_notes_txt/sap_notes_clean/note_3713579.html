Symptom

Organizations frequently inquire about automated payslip email delivery functionality within SAP HCM Payroll systems.  

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP ERP Core Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

Requirement: Customers expect a standard SAP-delivered tool that can automatically send payslips or pay statements directly to employee email addresses as part of the payroll processing cycle.

Cause

SAP does not provide standard functionality for automatically emailing payslips due to legitimate security, confidentiality, and technical performance concerns.

Resolution

1. Implement Employee Self-Service (ESS) portals to enable employees to securely request and access their payroll documents individually.  
2. Ensure the solution maintains data security and compliance with data protection regulations.  
3. Reduce system load by leveraging ESS portals for payroll document access.  
4. Provide better audit capabilities through ESS portals.  
5. Empower employees with control over their sensitive payroll information.  

See Also

Other alternatives:

  • Third-party Solutions
  • Encrypted Email Solutions
  • Secure Document Portals
