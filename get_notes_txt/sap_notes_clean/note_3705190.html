Symptom

The standard report IDCNAR (Accounts Receivable Aging) separates items in the report result for the same customer.

Environment

  • China

Cause

The IDCNAR result separates because the items are associated with different KKBER "Credit Control Areas". This behavior is part of the system's design.

Resolution

  1. Confirm that the items in the standard report IDCNAR are associated with different KKBER "Credit Control Areas".
  2. This separation of items is part of the system's intended design and does not require further action.

Keywords

IDCNAR, Accounts Receivable Aging, China
