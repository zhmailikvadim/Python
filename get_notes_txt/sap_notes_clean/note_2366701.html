Symptom

The Mass Activity Revenue Distribution is executed via transaction FP60M. The distribution status is not updated for all processed documents.

Other Terms

FP60M, RDSTA, RDSTB

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error in case of enhanced revenue distribution

This error occurs under the following circumstances:

  • A document is cleared either via a payment or a reversal, transfer etc.
  • The document is processed by the Mass Activity Revenue Distribution and the status is either set to distributed (RDSTA = RDSTB = 2) or to reversed (RDSTA = RDSTB = 3) depending on the type of clearing.
  • The clearing is reset or reversed so that the current revenue distribution status is reset to open (RDSTA = 1).
  • The document is again cleared in a similar way, e.g. by payment or reversal/transfer.
  • The Mass Activity Revenue Distribution is executed again. No distribution is necessary, therefore no entry in table DFKKRDI is created. But the status of the document is not set to distributed or reversed again.

Solution

Take over the attached program correction or implement the according Support Package
