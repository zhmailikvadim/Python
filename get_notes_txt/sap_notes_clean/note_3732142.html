Symptom

Unable to handle additional movement types during material document cancellation.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Resolution

To add additional movement types for material document cancellation, implement BAdI "J_1IG_SUBCON" in the method "ADD_MVMT_CANC" to include additional movement types during material document cancellation.
