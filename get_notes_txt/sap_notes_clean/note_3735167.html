Symptom

The Normalized Load Quantity (NLQ) / product footprint calculation returns incorrect results when the material master's alternative unit of measure (AUOM) is maintained with a denominator (UMREN) different from 1.

For example, if the AUOM conversion is maintained as 10 EPL = 1000 PC (UMREZ=1000, UMREN=10) instead of 1 EPL = 100 PC (UMREZ=100, UMREN=1), the calculated footprint quantity is too high by a factor of UMREN.

Other Terms

NLQ, Normalized Load Quantity, Product Footprint, AUOM, Alternative Unit of Measure, UMREZ, UMREN, Unit Conversion

Solution
