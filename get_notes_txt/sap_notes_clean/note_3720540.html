Symptom

In verification profile configuration, the scan control “Only once at beginning” is used to manage scanning behaviour in the application. In the standard system, this scan control could not be configured independently and always required an additional confirmation attribute together with another scan control. Due to this restriction, customers were unable to use the “Only once at beginning” option on its own, even in scenarios where no supporting scan controls were needed.

Other Terms

NJIT, Next Generation Just-In-Time Processing, Scanning Application, Verification Profile, Scan Control, Process Customer JIT Calls by Scan/ Data Entry

Reason and Prerequisites

Modification

Solution

Implement the correction instructions provided in this SAP Note or import the relevant Support Package to enable the corrected system behaviour.

With this update, the system allows the “Only once at beginning” scan control to be configured independently within the verification profile. Customers can now use this scan control on its own, without requiring any additional confirmation attribute or accompanying scan control. This enables more flexible and accurate configuration of scanning behaviour according to business requirements.
