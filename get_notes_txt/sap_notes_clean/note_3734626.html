Symptom

In Ariba Business Network, a Scheduling Agreement line shows the status “This is the next line to ship” even though:

  • The supplier has already delivered the material
  • Goods Receipt (GR) is posted successfully in the backend system
  • The document is transferred correctly to Ariba BN

The overall Scheduling Agreement may show status as “Received”, but specific lines remain open.

Environment

  • SAP Ariba Business Network
  • SAP ECC (or integrated ERP system)
  • Scheduling Agreements (SA)
  • Supply Chain Collaboration - Fulfillment

Reproducing the Issue

Cause

A mismatch between the scheduled delivery date and the actual delivery date (early delivery) prevents full reconciliation of the goods receipt to the specific schedule line, so the line continues to display as open (“next line to ship”) even though the overall status shows received.

Resolution

  1. Verify that the goods receipt (GR) was posted in the backend system and successfully transferred to the Business Network.
  2. Compare the scheduled delivery date with the actual delivery date to identify an early delivery mismatch.
  3. If an early delivery is confirmed, acknowledge that the open line status is a display/status inconsistency in the buyer portal and note that there is no impact on receipt or inventory.
