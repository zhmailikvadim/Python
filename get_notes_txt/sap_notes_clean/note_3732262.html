Symptom

  • After a certain point in time, multiple “Turnkey schedule for the intelligent scenario …” jobs begin to fail, for example:
  • Turnkey schedule for the intelligent scenario IBP_SEC_COMPL_ASSIST
  • Turnkey schedule for the intelligent scenario INV_EXPL_CLAUDE_SO1
  • Turnkey schedule for the intelligent scenario IBP_DS_GPT4OM
  • Turnkey schedule for the intelligent scenario IBP_DS_GPT4O
  • Turnkey schedule for the intelligent scenario IBP_DM_FCST_EXP
  • Error notifications are not received for these jobs because the run is not marked as Error and may appear as Finished.
  • An error similar to “already switched off for turnkey operations” can be observed.

Environment

  • Intelligent Scenario Lifecycle Management
  • SAP Integrated Business Planning

Reproducing the Issue

Open Application Jobs application, and locate jobs named “Turnkey schedule for the intelligent scenario ”

Cause

  • Turnkey operations for the intelligent scenarios were switched off while existing application job instances remained scheduled/active, causing exceptions when those instances run.

Resolution

  1. In the application job tool, identify “Turnkey schedule for the intelligent scenario …” jobs that are in Scheduled/active status for the switched-off scenarios.
  2. Manually cancel those scheduled instances to stop further failed runs. (Note the scenarios are still active and there's no impact for the functionality.)
  3. A permanent fix to prevent this exception is ongoing and planned to be deployed on with SAP IBP 2608.2 release.
