Symptom

During the GI reversal in EWM there is a simulation call into ERP which checks the result in simulation mode for the GI cancellation in ERP side.

The result of the simulation mode call is handed over to EWM however some of the received information is not displayed in EWM side.

Other Terms

Delivery number is not displayed

Reason and Prerequisites

The result is handed over to EWM and stored in EWM side in a variable which is only 50 characters long , due to that the rest of the received information after the 50 characters is getting lost.
