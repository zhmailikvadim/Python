Symptom

How to promote "promotion jobs" using Promotion Management in BI.

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform 4.x
  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform 2025

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log into Central Management Console (CMC) and navigate to Promotion Management.
  2. Create a new job and add objects.
  3. The "Promotion Jobs" folder under LCM folder appears empty when clicked.

Cause

  • Direct promotion of "promotion jobs" from the source is not supported.
  • Promotion jobs are automatically included when the LCM folder is promoted.

Resolution

  1. Log into CMC and access Promotion Management to create a new job and add objects.
  2. Upon clicking LCM-> Promotion Jobs, the folder may initially appear empty. This is an expected behavior. Simply select the LCM folder directly.
  3. Import the job to the destination system. The jobs are included when the folder is selected, and are present in the destination system.
