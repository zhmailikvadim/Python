Symptom

When you are using the filter and getting records different time from what you want.

Resolution

The issue arises because the filters updatedDateFrom and updatedDateTo are being used in your current configuration or vice-versa.

In the case of UpdatedDate these filters retrieve data based on the date and time an document was last updated by the system.

However, if you need to access the date when this document was initially created, you will need to use the filter createdDateFrom and createdDateTo.  

Keywords

invoice filter, updatedDateFrom, updatedDateTo, createdDateFrom, createdDateTo
