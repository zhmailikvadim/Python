Symptom

You use the Fiori app Manage Malfunction Reports (F2023) and select a notification from the list which has the Status Work is Done. You change one of the dates in the Malfunction Duration section. After the changes are saved, you notice that the Breakdown Duration is not updated according to the dates.

Other Terms

F2023, Manage Malfunction Reports, Report Malfunction, Malfunction Duration, Breakdown, Malfunction Start, Malfunction End, Breakdown Duration, datetime, Notification, eauszt, auszt, CL_EAM_BOPF_NOTIF_HEADER_DEF, IF_EAM_BOPF_NOTIF_HEADER_STRGY~WRITE, downtime

Reason and Prerequisites

The Breakdown duration was not updated from the dates when the Breakdown Duration was already set.

Solution

Implement this SAP Note.
