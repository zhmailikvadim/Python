Symptom

  • The Fiori Application F2878A (Analyze Delivery Performance - Shipped as Planned) calculates delivery performance based on adherence to planned shipping dates. However, it includes weekends, and it should only count business days according to the SAP calendar.
  • Business area expects that Fiori App F2878A consider only the business working days.

Environment

  • Sales and Distribution (SD)
  • Logistics Execution (LE)
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the Fiori App F2878A (Analyze Delivery Performance - Shipped as Planned).
  2. Generate a report on delivery performance based on adherence to planned shipping dates.
  3. Observe that weekends are included in the calculation, contrary to the expected behavior of counting only business days as per business area expectation.

Cause

  • The behavior is intended, the Fiori application F2878A does not consider the factory calendar for delivery performance calculations.
  • The value for an individual delivery is calculated by comparing Planned Goods Issue Date and Actual GI Date – while also including an offset (‘Acceptable Shipping Deleay’). It’s easiest to visualize by including the columns Planned GI Date, Adjusted Planned GI Date, Actual GI Date and GI Delay in Days in the table.

The fields are calculated by:

  • Adj. Planned GI Date = Pland. GI Date + ‘Acceptable Shipping Delay’
  • GI Delay in Days:
    • If Act. GI Date < Adj. Planned GI Date: GI Delay = 0
    • Else: GI Delay = Act. GI Date – Adj. Planned GI Date
  • The difference between the dates is always calculated as actual days (date2 – date1), a factory calendar is not taken into account.

Resolution

  • Confirm that the SAP Fiori application F2878A is functioning as designed and does not consider the factory calendar for delivery performance calculations.
  • If a modification to consider the factory calendar is required, open a development request. Refer to SAP Note ##11 for guidance on submitting a development request.
