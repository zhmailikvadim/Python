Symptom

When performing the Mass Processing for Products, the materials are not extended to plants and no entry created in MARC table after process activation in MDC. Also the inserted plant data is not identified at the edit step of the mass processing

Environment

  • SAP Master Data Governance-Consolidation and Mass Processing

Resolution

  1. Export the Materials from "Export Master Data-Products" app and update the New plant entry and hide the SCM* fields in the upload file.
  2. Extend the Material with Central Governance Change request to get an idea of what fields are required to extend a material to plant ensure those fields are part of excel and also hide the unchanged/not required fields.

Keywords

plant, material extension, products, MDG CMP, Direct activation
