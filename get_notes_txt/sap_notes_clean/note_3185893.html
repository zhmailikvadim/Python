Symptom

How do I create a Puser in SAP Integration Suite, managed gateway for spend management and SAP Business Network?

Resolution

You can create a Puser in SAP Integration Suite, managed gateway for spend management and SAP Business Network by following these steps:

  1. Click My Configurations > Authorization.
  2. Click Create Account for the Transactional User.
  3. A message confirming an email was sent to your email address will appear. Click OK.
  4. On the email you received, click Click here to activate your account.
  5. On the page Please activate your user account, enter your password and click Submit.
 

See Also

If you need to change the email address or password associated with the Puser, return to My Configurations > Authorization and click Change Password or Change Email.
