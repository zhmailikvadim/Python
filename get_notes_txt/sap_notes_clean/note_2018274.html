Symptom

This is an announcement note of corrections in payroll calculation for Kazakhstan, which took effect retroactively beginning March 1, 2014. Respective legal changes were published on April 5, 2014 (got into force from April 15, 2014). Current annonced change is relevant to obligatory Social Contribution only:

  • Additional payments for ecology region residence are no longer out of calculation base. Beginning March 1, 2014 these payments should be taxed with the obligatory social insurance contributions.

Other Terms

Kazakhstan, Legal change, payroll, 2014

Reason and Prerequisites

  • The legal change was published with Government's statement #250 from March 20, 2014.

Solution

  • The delivery date for this legal change is planned for May 30, 2014. Please, follow our news and reference to related notes section. Note that this section will only be updated after the solution is released.
  • To implement this legal change, implement the relevant correction instructions or apply the relevant Support Package when available.
