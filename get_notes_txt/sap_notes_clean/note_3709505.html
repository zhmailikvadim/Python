Symptom

  • The worker can not submit Time Sheet as the start date of the cost center is in the future 
  • The worker is receiving Time Sheet Error: Cost Center is not valid for one or more dates. Please remove time entered for those dates for this Cost Center

Resolution

The dates of Cost center can be changed via Worker Cost Center Allocation Upload
