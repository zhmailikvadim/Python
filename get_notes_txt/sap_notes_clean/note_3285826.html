Symptom

When processing report '/US4G/RP_SEND_GAS_PROPERTY',  choose the mode “DSO to nDSO Gas Property” and having entered a MeLo POD, the system reacts with an error message.

Other Terms

/US4G/RP_SEND_GAS_PROPERTY; Neighbor DSO; MeLo PoD; DSO to nDSO Gas Property.

Reason and Prerequisites

DSO sending Gas property data to another DSO with the report /US4G/RP_SEND_GAS_PROPERTY can only refer to a grid interconnection point.

Solution

The report and its business class are adjusted:

  • For permitting sending Gas property data from a DSO to another DSO specifying MeLo POD only. Specifying any other kind of POD shall lead to an error message for that POD.
  • When processing the report in the mode “DSO to nDSO Gas Property”, the service provider ID of the other DSO shall be either entered on the selection screen by the agent or determined by service provider on POD.
