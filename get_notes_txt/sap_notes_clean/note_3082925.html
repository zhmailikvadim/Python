Symptom

Is there an audit report that can identify if user has accepted or declined the DPCS?

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors HXM Suite

Resolution

The View Audit Log in Data Privacy Statements will allow you to search for an individual user or multiple users to see who have accepted or declined this statement and when.

  1. Go to Admin Center
  2. Click Company Settings
  3. Select Data Privacy Statement
  4. Click View History of the DPCS Name
  5. Click View Log of the Published Date
