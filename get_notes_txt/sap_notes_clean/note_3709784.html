Symptom

I am not an administrator user, and when I try to mark an activity item as complete, the Mark as Complete button is not visible.

Cause

In the user role for both the Work Order and Worker modules, the Manage button is not enabled, and the action Manage Activity/Offboarding Activity/Milestones are also not enabled.

Resolution

Your user role does not have permission to manage activity items, which is why the Mark as Complete button is not visible to you.

To resolve this, please contact the Program Management Office (PMO) team, as they are the system administrators and can assist in updating your role's permissions.
