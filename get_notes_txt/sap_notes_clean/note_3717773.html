Symptom

Planned Revenue gets incorrectly modified with Planned Cost value on clicking 'Valuate' button in planning area or 'Revaluate' button in forecast area.

Other Terms

Planned Revenue, Revenue (Plan), /CPD/FPSR, Valuate, Revaluate, Planning, Forecasting, 0CAL_FUNC_AO_CURR_CONVER, QFP, Forecast, Valuation, /CPD/CL_PFP_PLN_CST_REV_TRNSLT.

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason: Planned Revenue incorrectly overwritten by Planned Cost during Valuation or Revaluation
Prerequisites: Valuation Method 0CAL_FUNC_AO_CURR_CONVER is used in the Valuation Strategy.

Solution

Implement the SAP Note.
