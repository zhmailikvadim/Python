Symptom

When reviewing the workflow details for a submitted timesheet, you notice absence times are included in the recorded time field

Environment

  1. Successfactors Employee Central

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Employee submits timesheet that includes absences
  2. Manager reviews workflow details and notices absence times are included in the recorded time field

Cause

Standard behaviour

Resolution

In the current standard, all times are included in the recorded times field. This includes absences

Keywords

absence, total, recorded, workflow
