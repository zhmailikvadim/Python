EC-CS

The consolidation solution EC-CS can be used for external (statutory) rendering of accounts as well as internal (management) reporting [1]. For the consolidation solution EC-CS, you can no longer define a custom characteristic using the MATNR data element or domain (or any derived value). If you are already using a custom characteristic based on the MATNR data element or domain, you cannot upgrade to SAP S/4HANA. Appropriate transition pre-checks are in place to give you the opportunity to check such a situation already at start release.

CO

  • The Production Cost Analysis (F1780) app supports a maximum of 16 cost component groups in the Cost Component Group overview screen, and cost component assignment for “Cost Comp. Grp 1” only. The app supports Legal Valuation only. Extensibility is not supported.
  • The Work Center View of the Production Cost Analysis (F1780) app includes production orders and process orders only. This view doesn’t support overhead costs for production order at work center/operation level.
  • In Event-Based Revenue Recognition, only project-based sales processes (professional services scenario) are supported. The Revenue Recognition app only supports single-level projects.
  • In Event-Based Revenue Recognition for Projects/ Professional Services, target revenue calculations are not supported.
  • In Professional Services, origin profit centers are not derived.
  • For Sales from Stock processes, event-based Revenue Recognition is not released.
  • The Manage Internal Orders app only supports one asset under construction (AUC) per investment order. You can navigate to the AUC after release and save this investment order. The app does not support multiple AUCs for one investment order.
