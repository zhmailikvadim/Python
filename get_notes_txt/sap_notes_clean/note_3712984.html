Symptom

Filtering business partner relationships using global search does not return any results.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Cloud Version 2
  • SAP Sales and Service Cloud Version 2

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log on to the tenant.
  2. Use the global search bar to search for a Business Partner Relationship (e.g. Z123-1).
  3. No results are returned.

Cause

This is the expected system behavior.

Resolution

Use the Business Partner Relationship Role field under Advanced Filter in Accounts to filter Accounts based on Business Partner Relationships.

See Also

Refer to: 3498271 - New Customer Request for Additional Feature or Function in SAP standard software
