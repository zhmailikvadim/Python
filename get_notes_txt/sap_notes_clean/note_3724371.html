Symptom

As previously mentioned in SAP notes 2739775, 2957532, the country-specific screens are not required for infotypes 0009 and 0011, for Polish personnel numbers.

However, the HR Renewal settings for the infotypes 0009 and 0011 for Poland still select the country-specific screens.

Other Terms

Renewal IT0009, IT0011, V_T588MFPROPS, VC_T77PAOMDCF.

Reason and Prerequisites

The HR-Renewal customizing settings are outdated and should be updated in the customizing tables.

Solution

In the renewal settings of infotypes 0009 and 0011 for Poland, we are switching from country-specific screens to standard screens.

1. The following records have been deleted:

- from maintenance view V_T588UICONVCLAS:

HCMT_BSP_PA_PL_R0011 0011 46 CL_HRPA_UI_CONVERT_0011_PL
