Symptom

  • A direct contact listed in the PO cXML does not receive the expected Purchase Order email notification
  • The PO appears normally in the supplier’s SAP Business Network account, but no email is sent to the direct contact specified in the PO

Environment

SAP Business Network

Cause

When a Purchase Order contains a <Contact role="directSupplierContact"> element, SAP Business Network intentionally suppresses all outgoing email notifications to the direct contact.

This is standard system behavior and is not related to configuration issues, email delivery errors, or supplier account problems

Example of the cXML segment:

<Contact role="directSupplierContact">
    <Name xml:lang="en">Direct contact Flow</Name>
    <Email name="routing" preferredLang="en-US">somebody@sap.com</Email>
</Contact>

Resolution

No action is required from the buyer or supplier.

If a PO contains a <Contact role="directSupplierContact"> element , the system  intentionally suppresses all outgoing email notifications to the specified direct contact. 

This is standard system behavior and is not related to configuration issues, email delivery problems, or supplier account settings.

This behavior applies to Private and Public supplier flow. 

Keywords

DirectContact, Suppressed, overwrite, BN, Integration, EDI, Routing, P2P
