Symptom

  • On app F4024, opening a KPI card's detail view displays a non-editable view selector in the header.
  • Filters applied earlier are lost each time the user navigates back and re-enters the detail view, requiring filters to be reapplied repeatedly.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental." 

Environment

  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open app F4024 – Warehouse KPIs. 
  2. Click a card (e.g., Warehouse Orders or ODO items) to open the detailed list. 
  3. In the header, attempt to change the combo box value; it is not editable. 
  4. Navigate back and reenter the detailed view; filters are lost and must be reapplied.

Cause

The drill down app does not support saving variants, which results in filters not being retained when navigating back into the detailed view. 

Resolution

  1. There is no option to save a variant on the drill-down app.
  2. Save the filter on the app as a new tile.
