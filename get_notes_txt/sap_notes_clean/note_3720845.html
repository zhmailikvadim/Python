Symptom

In a purchase scenario, a provisional settlement was executed with a 100% threshold, followed by a final settlement with a zero net amount.

As the final settlement resulted in a zero net value, no second MM invoice was generated.

However, when attempting to reverse the final settlement, the reversal settlement fails during approval with the following error:

"Reversal quantity greater than quantity invoiced to date."

Other Terms

ACM, ACM Settlement, Reversal error

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement this automatic correction.
