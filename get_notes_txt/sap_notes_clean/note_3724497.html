Symptom

In case new document business partner items are created due to a partial clearing, the change date and change time might not be initialized for the new cleared item.

It is also possible that the change date and time is not updated in case a promise to pay or installment plan is created for the original item.

Other Terms

DFKKOP, CHDATE, CHTIME

Reason and Prerequisites

Programmfehler

Solution

Vorabkorrektur gemäß Korrekturanleitung oder Einspielung des entsprechenden Support Package.
