Symptom

Adding attachment is mandatory when reporting Proof of Pick-up/Proof of Delivery events in SAP Business Network for Logistics.

Resolution

    By design, Proof of Pick-up and Proof of Delivery events are intended to confirm the physical exchange of documentation. Therefore, the attachment requirement for these specific event types is mandatory and cannot be set to optional in SAP Business Network for Logistics.

    If you do not want to be forced to upload attachments, report only Departure or Arrival instead of Proof of Pick-up and Proof of Delivery. These milestones confirm the status without the technical requirement for a file upload.
