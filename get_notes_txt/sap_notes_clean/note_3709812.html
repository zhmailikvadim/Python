Symptom

What's the difference between Submit Response and Submit Entire Response?

Why are there two Submit Response buttons?

There is no place for me to enter my responses to the customer's questions.

Resolution

Submit Response-(Preliminary action) Step must be completed to be able enter responses in the system. 

  1. The Submit Response option is a hyperlink located under Checklist menu.
  2. The Checklist menu contains tasks that must be completed before the buyer will permit you to respond to their event (e.g. acceptance of an NDA).
  3. Submit Response will always be the last item on the Checklist and must be selected for you to be able to enter responses in the event. 
  4. Click Submit Response (the field turns gray).  You may now move to the Events Contents menu and enter your  project responses.

Submit Entire Response-Sends your completed project to the buyer for review. 

  1. Appears as a blue button at the bottom of the event. 
  2. After you've answered all questions and attached all supporting documentation. The Submit Entire Response  button is used to send your completed work to the buyer.
  3. If you want to make changes after you have sent your work to the buyer and time permits; you may edit the document by clicking Revise Response. The document will open, make the desired revisions the click Submit Entire Response again.
