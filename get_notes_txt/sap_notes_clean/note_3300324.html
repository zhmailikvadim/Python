Symptom

  • Data Services scheduled job not triggered on time.

  • Error in AL_RWJobLauncherLog.txt at the scheduled time:

    01-07-2023 18:30:02    Unable to connect to CMS <Server Name>.  A wrong connection is made to @@<Server Name>:<Port>(<Server Name>:<Port>, <Server Name>:<Port>).  Logon cannot continue.
    01-07-2023 18:30:02    JobLauncher failed to initialize session
    01-07-2023 18:30:02    Error to connect CMS. Invalid command line syntax(return failure).
    01-07-2023 18:30:02    RWJobLauncher failed to connect to CMS.
    01-07-2023 18:30:02    RWJobLauncher return code: 180011

Environment

SAP Data Services 4.x

Reproducing the Issue

Schedule jobs with DS scheduler from DS management console.

Cause

The CMS connection information is wrongly configured.

Resolution

Open the DS Management Console > Administrator > Management > CMS Connection, make sure to apply the correct CMS connection information, then try to reschedule the job.

Keywords

scheduling, doesn't work, failed, triggering
