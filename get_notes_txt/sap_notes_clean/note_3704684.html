Symptom

When trying to copy a capitalized interest rate condition for a transaction involved in a mirroring deal error message FTR0161 and FTR_MIRROR008 are raised. 

Environment

Treasury and Risk Management (TRM)

Cause

Programming error

Resolution

Implement SAP Note: 3443496 - Condition Change not Possible in Mirrored Interest Rate Deals 

See Also

3443496 - Condition Change not Possible in Mirrored Interest Rate Deals

Keywords

FTR0161, FTR_MIRROR008, mirror
