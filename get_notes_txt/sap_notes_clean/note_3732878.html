Symptom

You have employees who are subject to expat regulation. The optimization of expat regulation is switched on using parameters 'CAA' or 'CCNA' in the payroll function Determine SI Days and Periods (NL) (NSV01). You notice incorrect settlements, especially when the earnings exceed the maximum allowed due to an increase in the table wage or high special payments. You notice the symptom specifically after you implement SAP Note 3638264 - Expat Regulation: Minor Issues and Improvements.

Other Terms

NSV01, 30% Regulation, Expat Regulation

Reason and Prerequisites

In the payroll function Determine SI Days and Periods (NL) (NSV01), the system calculates free space available due to optimization in previous periods. Here, the system allowed a rounding space of €0.50 between actual cumulative taxable earnings versus the 30% of the cumulative earnings. However, due to rounding differences during optimization, the rounding space needs to be higher. Therefore, we have increased the rounding space to €5.00 to avoid incorrect calculation of free space.

Solution

You may not notice any impact after implementing this SAP Note as the rounding space of €0.50 works in your system. However, it is recommended to implement the SAP Note or relevant HRSP to prevent any inconsistent calculations in future. If the settlements are corrected by this SAP Note, you may need to trigger a recalculation for affected employees as of January 1, 2025.

Delivery

Import the HR Support Package relevant for your release. For more information, see SAP ERP Human Capital Management Support Package Schedules or SAP S4 HCM Support Package Schedules.

SAP recommends that you implement the Support Package. However, if you need to install a solution earlier, use the SAP Note Assistant.
