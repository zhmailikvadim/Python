Symptom

Long text section cannot be loaded in Fiori App Manage Product Master (F1602). Error "This section could not be loaded completely. If the problem persists, contact your administrator." is shown. 

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental. 

Environment

  • SAP ERP Logistics (LO)  
  • SAP S/4 HANA on premise
  • SAP S/4 HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open/create a product in Fiori app F1602 Manage product master data. 
  2. Navigate to long text section. 
  3. The long text section cannot be loaded. Error "This section could not be loaded completely. If the problem persists, contact your administrator." is shown. 

Cause

Odata services for long text functionality are not correctly activated. 

Resolution

Following odata services must be correctly activated in the front end system to make sure long text functionality can correctly work. Make sure the services are correctly activated in t-code /IWFND/MAINT_SERVICE and SICF. SAP Note 2787111 provides more detailed information. 

  • SGBT_NTE_CDS_API_D_SRV
  • SGBT_NTE_CDS_API_SRV
