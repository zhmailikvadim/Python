Symptom

Is it possible to mark job requisitions as favourites for Recruiting Operators in the Dashboard? Are there any such configurations available?

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Resolution

At present there are no options for the Recruiting users to mark job requisitions as favourites.

We encourage you to submit this idea in the influence portal by referring to the KBA.

Based on the number of votes received our Product management will consider to improvise the product in the future release.
