Symptom

In Transportation Cross Docking scenario the Staging bay is incorrectly determined for the PDO document.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management

Reproducing the Issue

For a PDO document in /SCWM/PRDO transaction Header level the Staging bay is incorrect.

Cause

The STADET_OUT uses the field 'DRDTEGR - Staging Area and Door Determination Group'. But this field is empty for the PDO items in the TCD warehouse.

This is empty because the product doesn't have warehouse view for the entitled (see /SCWM/MAT1). 

The product does not have any DRDTEGR field maintained in the table /SAPAPO/MATLWH.

That is the reason why the field Staging Area and Door Determination Group is not considered at the moment for the staging bay determination (/SCWM/STADET_OUT).

Resolution

Please maintain the DRDTEGR field in the table /SAPAPO/MATLWH for the product.
