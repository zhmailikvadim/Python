Symptom

When executing report BUSOBARCH with multiple business partners specified in the select option, the program confirms at the end that all of them can be archived. However, if the same report is run for just one of those business partners, it indicates that the partner cannot be archived, even though it was previously marked as archivable when part of the multi-selection.

Other Terms

BP Archiving, EVENT_ARCH1, FICA_GPART_EVENT_ARCH1, SARA, CA_BUPA, BUSOBARCH, event_arch1, p_chkall.

Reason and Prerequisites

Certain applications fail to report archiving status for all business partners, causing the program to classify them as non-archivable.

Solution

Implement the note.
