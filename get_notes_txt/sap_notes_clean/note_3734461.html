Symptom

What happens when user Accept Expected Amount (Dispute) the PO Line Amount Variance exception on the Invoice Reconciliation document?

Resolution

For a PO Line Amount Variance exception, when you accept the exception based on the purchase order, the amount of the line item on the invoice reconciliation is updated with the amount available to invoice on the order line item. However, the quantity and unit price are not changed; therefore, the amount on the IR line item is not equal to Unit Price * Quantity.

See Also

If the user intends to Reject the Invoice Reconciliation after disputing the PO Line Amount Variance, it is better to not dispute the exception and directly Reject the Invoice Reconciliation. This will ensure the Amount Invoiced based on the Invoice total is reverted back to the Order.

Refer : Line-level invoice exceptions
