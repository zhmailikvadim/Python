Symptom

In the Crystal Reports for Enterprise designer, in the Structure tab, the Ruler display at the top does not update correctly when switching between the "Portrait" and "Landscape" orientations in the Page Setup.

Solution

Previously, the engine would switch the width and height values.  Presently, we hold the width and height as constants, and switch them according to a 'portrait' or 'landscape' flag.  Due to this functionality, we were not detecting the width change corresponding to landscape-to-portrait switches. To solve this problem, a check was added in PrintOutputController.java to see if the orientation or the width has changed when calling setSectionWidth().
