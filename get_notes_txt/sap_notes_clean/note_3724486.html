Symptom

In a Sales Quote, when multiple Schedule Lines are created and quantities are distributed across them, the Quantity field at the item level does not reflect the total quantity.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Cloud Version 2
  • SAP Sales and Service Cloud Version 2

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Navigation Menu.
  2. Go to Sales > Sales Quotes.
  3. Open any Sales Quote.
  4. Add a Product Line Item.
  5. Open the line item in Detail View.
  6. Add a new Schedule Line.
  7. Click Simulate.

Result: The Quantity field shows the quantity from the first schedule line only, even if additional quantities are planned in other schedule lines.

Cause

This is the expected system behavior.

The Quantity field at the item level displays only the quantity from the first schedule line. It does not automatically aggregate quantities from multiple schedule lines.

If you need to see the total requested quantity, this value is available in another field.

Resolution

To view the total quantity requested across all schedule lines, use the Requested Quantity field under Total Values.

  1. To make this field visible:
  2. Open the Product Detail View.
  3. Click Adapt.
  4. Select Edit Master Layout (pencil icon).
  5. Navigate to Total Values.
  6. Set Requested Quantity to Visible.

Note: The Requested Quantity field is read-only and cannot be edited manually. It is automatically calculated by the system.
