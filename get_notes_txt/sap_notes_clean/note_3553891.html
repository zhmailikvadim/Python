Symptom

Create Contract with item having zero price and release it. Since Contract item does not Price assigned, item condition 01CT will not be created.

Create Purchase Order and assign Quantity, Price, Price Per and assign Contract, observe that PO Item's Price Per is overwritten with Contract Item's Price Per. As per standard, if Contract Item does not have condition assigned then PO Item Price Per should not be overwritten and manually entered Price and Price per have precedence over Contract.

Other Terms

Purchase Order, Item Price Per, Contract, Item Condition, lbbp_pdigpf3k.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.
