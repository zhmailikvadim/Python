Symptom

How to enable CP-843: Support for identifying purchase orders for subcontractors in Ariba Buying?

Resolution

Log a case with SAP to enable the feature CP-843 in your site. Once that is done, a member of the custom administrator group can enable the below parameters through the intelligent configuration interface

  1. Application.Procure.EnableSupplierAsSubContractor - This shows a subcontractor field on the requisition to include the supplier as a subcontractor. 
  2. Application.Procure.EnableSubcontractingForReceiving - This includes subcontracting information on the receipts of the purchase orders raised for such a supplier. This parameter only takes effect when Application.Procure.EnableSupplierAsSubContractor is enabled. 

See Also

Please refer to the documentation for all the necessary configurations for this feature.
