Symptom

Consider a scenario where the following events occur in this order:

  1. A contract has been partially fulfilled.
  2. Certain performance obligations have Suspend Revenue Posting marked.
  3. The price allocation has changed as a result of changes made to the contract (such as price changes).
  4. Suspend Revenue Posting has been unmarked.

In this case, the revenue may not be recalculated as expected to address the price allocation changes previously made to the contract.

Other Terms

FARR, Revenue Accounting, Price allocation, Suspending Revenue Posting, Revenue Block, Revenue Unblock

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Follow these steps:

  1. Apply this note.
  2. Execute report FARR_NOTE_2077711.
