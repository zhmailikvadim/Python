Symptom

In the process "Processing of Disconnection Order (SUP View)" (DE_B_GPKE_DISC_DC_ORD_SUP), when you enter a disconnection in transaction EC86, the budget billing plan stops correctly according to the customizing. However, when you perform the process in steps 2800, 2900, or 3000, the customizing is not taken into account, and the budget billing plan doesn't stop as expected.

This issue is relevant for the market role: SUP.

Other Terms

GPKE, DE_B_GPKE_DISC_DC_ORD_SUP, Budget Billing Plan, BBP, EC86, /US4G/RP_MDR_PROCESS_TRIG, disconnection order, disconnection execution result

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because the method UPDATE_MDR_DISCON_DARK lacked the logic to read the customizing settings.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system correctly stops the budget billing plan according to the customizing and the received disconnection execution result when you perform the process DE_B_GPKE_DISC_DC_ORD_SUP in steps 2800, 2900, or 3000. You can verify the fix by triggering the process using the report /US4G/RP_MDR_PROCESS_TRIG and checking that the budget billing plan stops according to the customizing.
