Symptom

In the configuration of the tenant copy, we explicitly set the Recruiting Candidate email address to dummy@sao.com. Even then without errors during testing, we noticed that the actual, real email addresses are available. As a result, real users received emails during tests.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Reproducing the Issue

After the instance refresh, external candidates are receiving invite email notification in Target (Test) Instance

Cause

Even though the email address was provided for 'Mask Recruiting Candidate Email Address' as 'dummy@sao.com' at the time of instance refresh.

Since the anonymization option wasn't choosen at the time of instance refresh, the primary email addresses were copied as is from the source instance and only contact email was updated as 'dummy@sao.com'.

Resolution

The option of 'Mask Recruiting Candidate Email Address' while creating an Instance Refresh Request, doesn't mean that the Candidate's Primary Email Addresses will be masked. This option only updates their secondary (contact) email addresses.

Therefore if you really want the primary and secondary email of candidates to be masked, Request you to choose option 'Anonymize Sensitive Information' to have the email address masked.
