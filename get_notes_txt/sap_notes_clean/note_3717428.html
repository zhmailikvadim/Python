Symptom

The following issues are encountered from March 31st 2026: 

  1. Free job postings have been removed from Indeed 
  2. Organic job feeds to Indeed are no longer collected
  3. Indeed no longer appears as an apply source in Advanced Analytics

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting 

Cause

Single Source job feeds  to Indeed are discontinued from March 31st. 

Resolution

  1. Jobs removed : Customers who wish to maintain organic job visibility on Indeed must contact their Indeed account representative as soon as possible to begin the transition to a direct API integration. 
  2. New jobs not collected : Customers who wish to avail of organic job posting from SF Recruiting to Indeed must contact their Indeed account representative as soon as possible to begin the transition to a direct API integration. 
  3. Indeed no longer a source in Advanced Analytics: Since candidates will apply directly through Indeed and bypass the career site, Indeed will no longer appear as a source in Career Site Builder Advanced Analytics reporting. Customers should consider this reporting change as part of their broader transition planning as they will only be able to view the Indeed source in the candidate reporting within Recruiting Management.
