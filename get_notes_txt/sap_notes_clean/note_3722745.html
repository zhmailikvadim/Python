Symptom

I created a sub-user and assigned a role with administrative permissions, but the tab Account Administration in Settings is not showing up for this user.  

Environment

SAP Business Network

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login with user in SAP Business Network.
  2. Go to Account Settings (top right corner button with your initials).
  3. Select Settings.
  4. Check that the Account Administration tab and section are missing.

Cause

The assigned role is lacking the required permissions. 

Resolution

To enable the tab and section of Account Administration within Settings, the sub-user assigned role must have assigned to it the permission Company Information.

Keywords

Role, Permission, Manage User, Settings, Admin, Administrator
