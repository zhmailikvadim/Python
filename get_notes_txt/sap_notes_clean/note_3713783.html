Symptom

Customer wants to know how to batch unassign the Assignments in Intercompany Matching and Reconciliation (ICMR) application.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Finance for Group Reporting

Resolution

Follow below steps to batch unassign the Assignments in ICMR.

Step 1: Make the relevant Filters (Such as for field "Assignment umber", "Proc. status" or other field) in APP Manage assignment "Assignments" part according to your requirement.

Step 2: Select all the documents which meet your requirement.

Step 3: Click "Unassign" button.
