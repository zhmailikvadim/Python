Symptom

When double-clicking on a payroll result in transaction PC_PAYRESULT, you face the following error:

"Incorrect structure of payroll result XXXXXXXX/XXXXX" Message no. H99_DP_MESSAGES011

Where "XXXXXXXX"  is the personnel number and "XXXXX" is the payroll sequence number.

Environment

  • HR renewal

  • SAP S/4HANA 

  • SAP ERP

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll 

Cause

This error can happen after an upgrade or after importing payroll results from other SAP system.

Resolution

There are some possible causes and solutions for this issue, please confirm if your case applies to one of the following possibilities:

  1.  Confirm if your case is not happening due to a country specific SAP Note. You can try to run the Payroll Driver for your employee to confirm if you are not facing the same DUMP as explained on these KBAs: 

    • SAP KBA 2531042 - PY-GB: Payroll runtime error IMPORT_WRONG_END_POS
    • SAP KBA 2567189 - [CO] Runtime error IMPORT_WRONG_END_POS in HCOCALC0

Keywords

H99_DP_MESSAGES011, H99_DP_MESSAGES 011, IMPORT_WRONG_END_POS, PCL2 cluster
