Symptom

The retention is calculated incorrectly for treaties with their own share not being 100% in the "Proportional distribution" treaty setting.

Other Terms

Proportional distribution

Retrocession

Retention

Own share

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error
Necessary Customizing settings:

Solution

History:

Version 1 from 11/19/2014: New　

Implement the source code corrections.
Support Package SAPK-68008INMSG corrects the problem.　

Solution:

The retention is now calculated on the basis of the net values for treaties with their own share that is not 100% for the "Proportional distribution" treaty setting.

Technical effects:

There are none.

Manual changes:

You do not have to perform any manual tasks.
