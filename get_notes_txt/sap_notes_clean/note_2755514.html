Symptom

  • It has been observed that the Govt Pension Contribution is being calculated and the /3FN wage type is generated even when the "Paid By Govt" checkbox is not selected in the Pension block of Infotype 0587.
  • As per Note No. 2117876, in case of Pension basis more than 15K, for basis above 15K employee has to contribute additionally 1.16 % which used to be contributed by Govt. The employee PF part will be reduced by the same amount.
  • It is unclear whether this adjustment applies to all employees or only to those for whom the "Paid By Govt" checkbox is marked in the Pension Block of Infotype 0587.

Environment

  • SAP ERP Human Capital Management
  • SAP ERP Payroll India
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Execute Payroll transaction PC00_M40_CALC_SIMU.
  2. Enter the payroll area, payrol period, Personnel Number, Schema and Tick Display log.
  3. Check INEPF paroll function for wage type /3FN -Govt Pension Contribution.

Cause

Clarification of SAP note  2117876 

Resolution

  1. Solution as per SAP note  2117876 will work irrespective of the checkbox Paid By Govt in infotype 0587, i.e. in case of Pension basis more than 15K, for basis above 15K employee has to contribute additionally 1.16 % which used to be contributed by Govt. The employee PF part will be reduced by the same amount.
  2. Also the pension contribution for employer will get limited to Rs.1250 or 8.33% of pension basis only when the check box "Eligible Pay or PF basis whichever is less is selected".

See Also

  • The scenario as per the note 2117878 will be incases where you have selected the Pension tab of Infotype 0587,the Basis as PF Basis, cause in this case only the Pension Basis can be more than Rs.15000.00, and the logic as per the note will work.
  • If it is selected as "Eligible Pay or PF basis whichever is less is selected", then in all cases the Pension basis will always be restricted to Rs.15,000.00, and therefore the logic of Pension basis more than 15K, will never get applied.
