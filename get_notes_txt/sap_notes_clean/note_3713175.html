Symptom

You are trying to adjust wage type /RFP via IT0221 but the adjusted amount does not save in RT table.

Environment

  • HR Renewal
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll

Cause

IT0221 is meant to be used for tax wage types. As /RFP is a technical wage type, it cannot be directly adjusted via IT0221.

Resolution

A custom payroll rule may be created to move the /RFP entry to the final RT.
