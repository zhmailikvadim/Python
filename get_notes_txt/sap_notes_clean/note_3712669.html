Symptom

Purchase order is not transferred to Accrual Engine, no matter by Online integration or manually using POAC_MM2ACE_TRANS_V2.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

Start POACTREE03 and look for certain Purchase order, no Accrual object has been created.

Cause

Purchase orders that represent a purchasing to stock (EKPO-KZVBR = <space>) do not need to be accrued, since no costs are associated to such purchase order items.

Resolution

This is correct behavior.

Keywords

Accrual, purchase order accrual, PO accrual, POACTREE03, POAC_MM2ACE_TRANS_V2, transfer
