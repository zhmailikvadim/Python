Symptom

In Advance Intercompany stock transfer process, the creation of supplier invoice fails in few cases with batch split.

Other Terms

SIIC, VCM, Supplier invoice creation failure, Adv STO

Reason and Prerequisites

When inbound delivery has only items with quantity greater than zero, i.e. batch split items with material quantity from outbound delivery, and the intercompany invoices contain all items from the outbound delivery, the items are not transferred correctly for supplier invoice creation.

Solution

Implement the attachd correction instructions.
