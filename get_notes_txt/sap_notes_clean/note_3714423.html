Symptom

  • The system exhibits poor performance during the execution of control sets in the BPC standard environment.
  • From the ST12 trace, most of the time is spent on the DB side:
    • DB: ADBC ADBC Start: FCODE = EI, DBTABNAM CL_SQL_STATEMENT==============CP
    • DB: ADBC ADBC Start: FCODE = EP, DBTABNAM CL_SQL_STATEMENT==============CP

Environment

  • SAP Business Planning and Consolidation (BPC) version for SAP NetWeaver

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the BPC system and navigate to the Control Monitor.
  2. Attempt to run controls.
  3. The performance is not good.

Cause

This is caused by a known HANA issue.

Resolution

Follow the solution in SAP Note 2985771 - Handling of Temporary BW Hierarchy Tables (0BW:BIA:BI0_08*) in HANA.
