Symptom

When trying to connect to a Tosca cloud license server you might see the follwing error message:
"Could not establish secure channel for SSL/TLS with authority license.tricentis.com"

Environment

SAP Enterprise Continuous Testing by Tricentis

Resolution

Check the following knowledge article from Tricentis:

Could not establish secure channel for SSL/TLS with authority license.tricentis.com

Keywords

registry, TOSCA, file, connect, update
