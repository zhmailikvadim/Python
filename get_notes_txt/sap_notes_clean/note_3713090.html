Symptom

A Subscription Contract is created from a Solution Quotation. It is not Technically Active because the Equipment ID information is missing.

The user executes the Maintain Equipment IDs change process to fill the Equipment ID info and the Subscription Contract is activated. The change processes listed in the Processes assignment block are incomplete.

Other Terms

SOM, Change Process, Processes tab, ISTA

Reason and Prerequisites

Information about change processes is buffered when the user first accesses the Subscription Contract Processes assignment block. As the Subscription Contract is not Technically Active, the change processes relevant for active contracts do not appear.

After the Subscription Contract is activated by the Maintain Equipment IDs change process, the buffer of change processes is not updated and the Processes assignment block does not show the changes that are available for active Subscription Contracts.

Solution

Apply attached correction instructions.
