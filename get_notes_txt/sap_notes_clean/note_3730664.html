Symptom

In the PO Printout the Delivery Date Sequence is not correct, it is being displayed out of chronological order.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Manage Purchase Orders app.
  2. Open an affected purchase order.
  3. Go to the Items tab and open an item.
  4. Open Schedule Lines and verify multiple delivery dates.
  5. Return to the header and choose Edit.
  6. Open Output Preview.
  7. Observe the schedule lines are not listed in chronological delivery date order, the most recently changed delivery date appears last.

Cause

System behavior.

Resolution

The system sorts schedule lines in the Purchase Order output by last change time. The schedule line whose delivery date was changed most recently is placed last.

If chronological ordering in the output is required, submit a feature request via the Customer Influence portal.
