Symptom

In the process "Sending of Quotation (MOS View)" (DE_B_MOSB_QUOT_MOS), version 2604, step 0210 incorrectly triggered the billing end of the previous time slice even when the leading supplier or leading MaLo had not changed.

This issue is relevant for the market role: MOS.

Other Terms

MOS, MOSB, DE_B_MOSB_QUOT_MOS, /US4G/CL_PROC_STEP_TRIG_EOB, Trigger End of Billing by MOS Process for Previous Time Slice, quotation, time slice, leading supplier, leading MaLo

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system no longer triggers the end of billing process for the previous time slice when the leading supplier or leading MaLo has not changed. You need to retest the process. You can verify the fix by checking that the end of billing process for the previous time slice is not triggered when the leading supplier or leading MaLo has not changed.
