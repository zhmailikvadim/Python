Symptom

  • Objects such as technical objects or measurement points marked as inactive or deleted in SAP S/4HANA are replicated to SAP APM. 

Environment

  • SAP Asset Performance Management
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Mark a technical object or measurement point as inactive or deleted in SAP S/4HANA.  
  2. Perform synchronization between SAP S/4HANA and SAP APM.  
  3. Verify that the inactive or deleted objects are replicated to SAP APM.

Cause

  • The system is functioning as intended. During synchronization, objects marked as inactive or deleted are included in the replication process between SAP S/4HANA and SAP APM, except during the initial (first) synchronization.  

Resolution

  1. During the initial (first) synchronization of objects between SAP S/4HANA and SAP APM, objects marked as inactive or deleted will not be replicated to SAP APM.  
  2. If an object has already been synchronized and is later marked as inactive or deleted in SAP S/4HANA, it will still be replicated to SAP APM.  
  3. Currently, there is no alternative to prevent the replication of inactive or deleted objects to SAP APM after the initial synchronization.  
  4. These objects are excluded them from display using filters by default. However the downstream applications still shows these technical objects. 
    Users can also apply the filter "Status" in Explore Technical Objects to exclude objects with statuses "Inactive" and "Marked for Deletion" from the results.  
  5. These lifecycle Status Objects are excluded from licensing considerations, even though they continue to appear in APM downstream applications. 
  6. As per Master Data compliance and retention requirements, these objects are not purged as they remain valuable for historical records.
