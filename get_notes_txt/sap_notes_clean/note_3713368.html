Symptom

When you process intercompany logistics for in-house services in the Manage In-House Services or Manage In-House Service Objects app, the system displays errors related to missing authorizations. These errors prevent you from completing the technical follow-up activities.

Other Terms

 in-house repair

Reason and Prerequisites

During the processing of activities in intercompany logistics scenarios, the system performs authorization checks that require privileged mode. The system does not activate privileged mode in all required situations, which causes the authorization errors.

Solution

Implement the SAP Note.
