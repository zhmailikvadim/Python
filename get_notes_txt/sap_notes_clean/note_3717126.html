Symptom

How to use Force Complete Task option in Guided Sourcing?

Resolution

To use the Force Complete Task, follow the steps below:

  1. Open the guided sourcing project, sourcing request or event
  2. On the Tasks tab, locate the task you want to complete
  3. Click three dots > Force Complete Task
  4. (Optional) Choose a value in the Alternative Status field
  5. (Optional) Enter a comment explaining the reason for the action
  6. Click Force Complete

You must be a member of the Customer Task Administrator group to see and use the Force Complete Task option.

See Also

If you don't see the option to force complete a task, refer to Why don't I see the option to force complete a task?
