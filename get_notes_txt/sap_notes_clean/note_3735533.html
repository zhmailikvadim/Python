Symptom

You encounter issues when displaying Engine results in the area Analytics --> Measurable Engines in the SAM4U tool after importing UCM Results. Many entries are listed where the licensed amount and the measured amount are zero.

Other Terms

Software Asset Management, SAM4U Tool, SAM4U, PCE, UCM Data Import, Engine Results

Reason and Prerequisites

The SAM4U tool imports all engines results even for client 000 and also if the result is zero (which is generally a good thing because you can see that the corresponding metering function was successfully executed). This solution will skip the results from client 000 entirely, will still save the records from all other clients, even where the results is zero but will filter them out accordingly in the Engine Results.

Solution

Implement this SAP Note to fix the above-mentioned issue.
