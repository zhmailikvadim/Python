Symptom

What is the splitting behavior and criterion in SAP Ariba Buying & Invoicing?

Environment

SAP Ariba Buying & Invoicing

Cause

The splitting behavior is caused by different splitting criteria, which are hard-coded in the system and can or cannot be modified depending on the system's configuration.

Resolution

Please refer to the following Split Criteria, its behavior, and the configuration required.

  • Different Suppliers: has an automatic split, and it is defaulted by the system.
  • Ship-To Addresses: the Purchase Order is split when the supplier setting is enabled, and can be used once the Supplier Location is configured.
  • Bill-To Addresses: has an automatic split, and it is defaulted by the system.
  • Catalog vs None-catalog items: has an automatic split, and it is optional.
  • Different Contracts: has an automatic split, and it is defaulted by the system.
  • Different Currencies: has an automatic split, and it is defaulted by the system.

 

When:

  • Defaulted by the system, the splitting criterion is hard-coded in the system and cannot be modified or updated.
  • Optional, the splitting criterion can be enabled or disabled by SAP Support.
