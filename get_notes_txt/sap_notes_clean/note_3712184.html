Symptom

You have installed the Intelligent Application Working Capital Insights in a version lower than 1.7.2, and you are experiencing the following issue:

 Total Inventory KPIs may not match the Fiori apps “Material Inventory Values – Balance Summary” and “Inventory KPI Analysis”

Other Terms

Business Date Cloud, BDC, Datasphere, DSP, WCIA, Working Capital Insights, Intelligent Application, Total Inventory, Cost Estimate

Reason and Prerequisites

 The issue was caused by missing filtering of ACDOCA ("General Ledger Account Line Item") line items with initial CostEstimate, which are not relevant for valuated inventory, inventory-managed materials.

Solution

Update to the latest version of Working Capital Insights by clicking the Update button in the BDC Cockpit. This update has no impact on existing data products.

As of verson 1.7.2, ACDOCA ("General Ledger Account Line Item") material records with initial CostEstimate are excluded.
