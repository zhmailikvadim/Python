Symptom

You execute the balance carry forward task or the consistency check for consolidation of investments (C/I), in the context of transition from EC-CS to SAP S/4HANA for Group Reporting (S/4GR). This results in error message G0 815 ( "FS items &1 to be carried forward/deleted have incorrect subitem category" ). The error message refers to generated item for statistical equity holdings adjustments in S/4GR.

Other Terms

G0815, CX6C3, BCF, balance carry forward, C/I customizing check

Reason and Prerequisites

You undergo the procedure for transition from EC-CS to S/4GR according to SAP Note 2833748.

Solution

Import of Support Package or advanced correction by implementation of correction instruction
