Symptom

Symptom

Running a publication of Crystal Report or Web Intelligence documents takes much more time than expected.

Other Terms

Publication, low performance

Reason and Prerequisites

Environment:

  • SAP BusinessObjects Platform BI 4.1 Patch4.10 and above
  • SAP BusinessObjects Platform BI 4.1 Patch5.6 and above
  • SAP BusinessObjects Platform BI 4.1 Patch6.1 and above
  • Regression introduced by SAP Note 2150518

Steps to reproduce:

  • Create a publication with thousands of dynamic recipients on a Crystal Report or Web Intelligence document.
  • The time to complete the publication is much longer than expected

Cause:

The Publishing Service in the Adaptive Processing Server fails to create multiple scope batches and process all recipients in the same scope.

Solution

The issue has been fixed in the patches listed in the "Support Packages & Patches" section below.

For Business Intelligence Platform maintenance schedule and strategy, see the Knowledge Base Article 2144559 in References section.
