Symptom

  • How is the calculation for MSP fee different for buyer-funded and supplier-funded scenarios?
  • What is the difference between discount and markup calculation for MSP fees?

Resolution

1. Calculations Using Rates
These formulas pertain to SOW invoices created from time sheets, events, schedules, and fees do not have rates

  • MSP% is utilized for SOW invoice calculations
    • For buyer-defined SOWs, the calculation begins with the supplier rate to determine the bill rate
    • For supplier-defined SOWs, the calculation starts with the bill rate to determine the supplier rate

For Discount:

  • Buyer-Funded SOW:
    • Bill Rate = ROUND(Supplier Rate / (1 - MSP% / 100), 2)
  • Supplier-Funded SOW:
    • Supplier Rate = ROUND(Bill Rate * (1 - MSP% / 100), 2)

For Markup:

  • Buyer-Funded SOW:
    • Bill Rate = ROUND(Supplier Rate * (1 + MSP% / 100), 2)
  • Supplier-Funded SOW:
    • Supplier Rate = ROUND(Bill Rate / (1 + MSP% / 100), 2)

2. Calculations Using Amounts
These formulas apply to SOW invoices created from time sheets, events, schedules, and fees

  • SOW has an MSP % that is used in calculations for invoices
    • For buyer-defined SOWs, the process starts with the supplier amount to derive the buyer amount
    • For supplier-defined SOWs, the calculation begins with the buyer amount to find the supplier amount

For Discount:

  • Buyer-Funded SOW:
    • Buyer Amount = ROUND(Supplier Amount / (1 - ptr.msp_pct / 100), 2)
  • Supplier-Funded SOW:
    • Supplier Amount = ROUND(Buyer Amount * (1 - ptr.msp_pct / 100), 2)

For Markup:

  • Buyer-Funded SOW:
    • Buyer Amount = ROUND(Supplier Amount * (1 + MSP % / 100), 2)
  • Supplier-Funded SOW:
    • Supplier Amount = ROUND(Buyer Amount / (1 + ptr.msp_pct / 100), 2)

These calculations ensure clarity in how MSP fees are determined based on whether the SOW is buyer or supplier-funded, and whether discount or markup approaches are applied
