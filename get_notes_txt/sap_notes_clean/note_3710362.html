Symptom

Consolidation of multiple unique SAP ECC platforms into one single SAP platform is required.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA

Resolution

  • Refer to the SAP Community discussion - Migrate several ECC 6.0 system to single S4 HANA system by using SAP LT Server.
  • Contact the local SAP consulting team for assistance in engaging a system architect to develop a strategy for consolidation.
