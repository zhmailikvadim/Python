Symptom

1. Issues Observed in Advanced CPE During CDOTE Processing from GUI (WB22)

1.1 When a contract is opened in Edit Mode via WB22, Classic CPE correctly restricts edits to the amount or fixed exchange rate after a CDOTE order is created;
      however, in Advanced CPE, the fields that are expected to remain editable - Basis Amount, Future FX, and Basis FX - are currently not editable.

1.2 Executing Price with CDOTE for an NPE group within the componentized FLAT pricing approach incorrectly changes the pricing approach to AF (Adjustable Flat),
      and the Pricing Status displayed after the Fill process is also incorrect.

2. Issues in Process ACM – Price Lot Application (Single Lot Edit)

2.1 During FPIP CDOTE processing, when the user navigates to the Price Lot, selects Edit, and opens the CDOTE tab, the Subaccount field appears blank and is
      non‑editable for both Intercompany and Intracompany contracts.

2.2 The Price with CDOTE operation is not functioning correctly, as it fails to execute for both full‑quantity and partial‑quantity scenarios.

2.3 The system does not process Partial Lift actions when combined with CDOTE, resulting in the failure of expected operational flows and preventing users
      from completing the required processes.

3. CDOTE Orders Not Displayed in Cancellation Tab

3.1 CDOTE orders are not displayed in the Cancellation tab within the Manage Contract application, even though they are expected to appear as part of the      
      standard cancellation overview.

Other Terms

Agricultural Contract Management; ACM; Advanced CPE; CDOTE; Cancellation; Lift; Price; Manage ACM Contract; Process ACM Price Lots;

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
