Symptom

In SAP Cloud Identity Services, you may observe new diagnostic log entries appearing in the Troubleshooting Logs that contain the string "hana".

Some of these entries may appear with severity Warning or Error. Examples include (but are not limited to):

  • severity="ERROR", location="com.sap.ids.idp.session.HanaSessionDao"
  • "Deleting IDP session with hanaId.."
  • "Saving IDP session with hanaId.."

There is no visible functional impact associated with these logs.

Environment

  • SAP Cloud Identity Services – Identity Authentication (IAS)
  • SAP Cloud Identity Services – Identity Provisioning (IPS)
  • Troubleshooting Logs

Cause

These diagnostic log entries are generated by internal service operations related to backend optimizations and routine system activities.
Although some entries may carry a warning or error severity level, they do not represent functional issues and do not affect customer operations.

Resolution

No action is required. These log entries are expected and do not indicate a service issue. They can be safely ignored unless you experience functional degradation.
If the service continues to operate normally, no further investigation is needed. Otherwise, report a Support Case with component BC-IAM-IDS or BC-IAM-IPS.

Keywords

IAS, IPS, Troubleshooting Logs, diagnostic, Warning, Error, hana, hanaId, HanaSessionDao, IDP session.
