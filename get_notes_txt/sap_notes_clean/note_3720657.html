Symptom

Settlement Term which changed by Mass Update is reverted after Charge Calculation was executed.

Now the Settlement term priority is: Manual > BAdI > TCCS > General Default>Mass Update

This Note will change the priority of 'Mass Update' to equal as the priority of 'Manual'.

After this Note is implemented, the priority is changed to: Manual/Mass Update > BAdI > TCCS > General Default

Other Terms

Settlement Term, Charges, Mass Update

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Implement corresponding correction measure or service package.
