Symptom

  • Employees are receiving I-9 reverification emails every four hours.
  • Review of the provisioning jobs revealed an "Email Framework Reminder Job" running every four hours.

Environment

SAP SF Onboarding

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Employee is hired in version 1.0 and imported into version 2.0 with a reverification expiration date.
  2. Employee receives repeated I-9 reverification emails every four hours.

Cause

  • The system is functioning correctly from the SuccessFactors side, and only two emails were sent as per the configuration: one general email and one reminder email.
  • The repeated emails every four hours are being triggered from the user's domain and not from SuccessFactors.

Resolution

  1. Verify the configuration of the email template and ensure it aligns with the intended trigger conditions.
  2. Navigate to email services click on details for any one of the emails and check with sender address to ensure who is sending the emails, this could be their own domain.
  3. The "Email Framework Reminder Job" in provisioning is configured only to trigger reminder emails as per the system setup and does not contribute to the repeated emails.
