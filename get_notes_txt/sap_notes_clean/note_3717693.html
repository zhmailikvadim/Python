Symptom

I am running the Invoice Buyer Download, but the transaction returns the error message below even though there are approved invoices in the system. This issue started occurring after a Custom Field used for invoice filtering was deleted or replaced. 

Error: Connector returned no data

Cause

The connector uses a customized logic that filters records based on a specific Custom Field. This logic is tied to the field's Internal Reference. If the original field is removed and replaced with a new one, the new field does not automatically inherit the Internal Reference. Consequently, the connector cannot identify the criteria and returns no results.

Resolution

You must manually assign the required Internal Reference to the new Custom Field to restore the connector's functionality.

  1. Sign in with your Configuration Manager account.
  2. Click Internal Reference.
  3. Select the relevant Custom Field.
  4. Under the Internal Reference column, enter the specific reference tag required by your customization (e.g., FG_POPULATE_1).
  5. Click Update.
  6. Trigger the download again to confirm that invoices are now being captured.
