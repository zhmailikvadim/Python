Symptom

During the onboarding process, onboardees receive an email notification with unclear or unreadable content.

  • The email body displays placeholder text such as:
    "???ONB2COMPLIANCE_COMPLIANCE_DOCUMENT_FLOW_WAITING_NOTIFICATION_BODY???"
  • The content is not properly rendered, making the message difficult to understand.

Affected configuration:

  • Template Title: (ONB) Compliance Document Flow waiting notification Template
  • Trigger: (ONB) Compliance Document Flow waiting notification Trigger
  • Email Category: (ONB) Compliance Paperwork Category

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Process a new hire through the onboarding process.
  2. Observe that the onboardee receives the "(ONB) Compliance Document Flow waiting notification Template" email showing "???ONB2COMPLIANCE_COMPLIANCE_DOCUMENT_FLOW_WAITING_NOTIFICATION_BODY???".

Cause

This is a known product issue.

Resolution

  • The fix for this issue is planned to be deployed in the 1H 2026 release.
  • The progress of this fix can be tracked via OBX-42567.
  • For official release timelines and updates, please refer to SAP SuccessFactors Product Release & Road Map Information.

Workaround

No workaround is currently available.
