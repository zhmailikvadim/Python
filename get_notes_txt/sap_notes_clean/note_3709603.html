Symptom

In the app Plan Balance - Raw Material, the quantities for the Production, By-Product and Requirements categories are calculated incorrectly in specific business scenarios, and instead of showing the full daily requirement, the system displays only the remaining open quantity.

Other Terms

Plan Balance - Component, wrong quantity on posting date

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

History:

Version 1 of February 19, 2026: New creation

Install the code correction.

Description of Correction:

The app Plan Balance – Raw Material shows the total planned Production, By-Product and Requirements for the day, regardless of posted confirmations. Production confirmations do not alter the planned quantity displayed.

Functional Dependencies:

There are no functional dependencies.

Manual Changes:

No manual changes.
