Symptom

Does Fiori App F2343 support inspection lots with serial numbers in SAP S/4HANA 2021?

Environment

SAP S/4HANA 2021

Cause

As per standard design, the use of inspections with serial numbers in app F2343(Manage Inspection Lots) is not supported.

This is documented in attached SAP Note 2829920. Although the Note mentions cloud version, it is also applicable for on-premise Fiori apps.

Resolution

This features has been added in later releases. Missing function until SAP S/4HANA 2022. 

As a workaround, classical visually harmonized apps (for example: QA32 or QA02) can be used instead to process inspection lots with serial numbers.
https://fioriappslibrary.hana.ondemand.com/sap/fix/externalViewer/#/detail/Apps('QA32')/S23OP
