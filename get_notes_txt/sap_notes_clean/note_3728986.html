Symptom

In case of an error during Consolidation, e.g. in case the STO scheduleline from the supplying plant to the delivery plant is not ATP relevant, several issues can occur:

  • Even if the Delivery Plant is not an allowed substitute in ABC, it might be used to confirm the Sales Order. In case the Check Horizon with infinity quantity at the end of the horizon is used in the Delivery Plant, even a confirmation will be given from the Delivery Plant.
  • In the Review Availability Check Screen, the Delivery Plant is shown although it shall not be used.

Other Terms

ATP; aATP

Reason and Prerequisites

If the schedule line creation in Consolidation fails, e.g. because of missing ATP relevance for the STO scheduleline from the supplying plant to the delivery plant is not ATP relevant, the Multi Level basic method claims to be not relevant for the current requirement. Then, PAC assumes that it is relevant. In case, RLT is active in PAC, it will even give a confirmation.

Solution

Apply the correction instructions for this note or upgrade to a SAP S/4HANA support package that contains this correction.
