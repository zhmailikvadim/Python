Symptom

How to remove billing blocks (FPLT-FAKSP) from billing plans in mass.

Environment

  • SAP R/3
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

It is required to remove billing blocks from billing plans in mass. This option is however neither available in backed, frontend nor via standard report.

Cause

Standard functionality does not support mass removal of billing blocks from billing plans.

Resolution

  1. Develop a small custom report to update billing plan items based on business requirements, for example by clearing or adjusting the billing block field (FPLT–FAKSP) for selected sales orders/items.
  2. Record the manual removal of the billing block using SHDB and execute the recording to perform the change in mass.
