Symptom

 You would like to display the information in the field "左のうち、支払調書を提出するものの合計" in report RPCNRPJ0.

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Execute report RPCNRPJ0 via transaction SE38.
  2. Select an entry in "Total payment record."
  3. Press the "Print" button.
  4. Observe that the fields in "左のうち、支払調書を提出するものの合計" are blank.

Cause

Since report RPCNRPJ0 does not calculate  役務の報酬. As a result, 支払総額 on the left side of the form and the "左のうち、支払調書を提出するものの合計" field on the right are always the same. For this reason, the system design omits outputting the same data twice in the "左のうち、支払調書を提出するものの合計" field.

Resolution

If you require the amount to be displayed in "左のうち、支払調書を提出するものの合計," please use method EDIT_PDF_TPR in BAdI: PADJP_RPCNRPJ0 as described in KBA 2714544: JA: Legal reports - Tax (pages 84~85).
