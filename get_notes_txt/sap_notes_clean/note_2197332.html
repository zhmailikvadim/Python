Symptom

Clearing desired on an account with special G/L Indicator results in system generating additional line(s) for VAT during the clearing process.

Environment

  • Financial Accounting (FI)
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  • Use transaction F-44 / App Clear Outgoing Payments to clear the vendor

Cause

Down payments can be posted either net or gross, determined by the tax category in the G/L account master data of the special G/L account used for down payments (excluding the special G/L account for down payment requests). Posting a down payment with tax results in an automatically posted tax item as an open item, which the system attempts to clear during the clearing process. Clearing should occur via F-54, referencing the invoice (without tax), and any difference should be manually cleared, following the net procedure.

Posting an invoice without tax requires the down payment to be posted with a zero-rated tax code for expected clearing, as follows:
Cr. Down Payment 10,000 EUR
Dr. Vendor 10,000 EUR

If the down payment is posted with tax, the system generates the following entry upon clearing:
Cr. Down Payment 10,000 EUR
Cr. VAT-input tax 160 EUR
Dr. Vendor 10,160 EUR

Standard posting for down payments includes VAT postings, with invoices/credit memos also containing VAT postings, as per the standard down payments-clearing process.

Resolution

This is the standard design of the program.
