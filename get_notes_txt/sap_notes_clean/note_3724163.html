Symptom

How to configure data masking for personal data fields within the Identification Types and Tax Categories of a Business Partner?

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open Maintain Business Partner app.
  2. Enter  Business Partner number and open the profile.
  3. Navigate to "Identification" tab.
  4. Personal data of the BP is displayed.

Cause

Resolution

Identification Types and Tax Categories maintained for a Business Partner may contain sensitive personal data.

Follow below steps to mask personal data in 'Manage Your Solution' app:

  1. Open 'Manage Your Solution' app.
  2. Navigate to 'Configure Your Solution'.
  3. Search for the ID '500092'.
  4. Choose configuration activity '103654 - Define Identification Types'.
  5. Open the required ID Type to mask.
  6. Click on 'Edit'.
  7. Tick the checkbox 'Sensitive Pers. Data' and save.

Incase while using SAP Central Business Configuration (CBC) :

  1. Log on to the project experience in SAP Central Business Configuration.
  2. Select the relevant project.
  3. Navigate to the configurations activity tab. 
  4. Search for the configuration activity '103654 - Define Identification Types'.
  5. Select the configuration activity to navigate directly to the SAP S/4HANA Cloud system.
  6. Open the required ID Type to mask.
  7. Click on 'Edit'.
  8. Tick the checkbox 'Sensitive Pers. Data' and save.
