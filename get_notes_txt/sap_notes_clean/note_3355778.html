Symptom

Still could schedule to other formats even checking out all kinds of formats in Export To of Customization

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects BusinessIntelligence(BI)4.3
  • BILaunchpad.
  • Web Intelligence(Webi)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login Central Management Console(CMC).
  2. Click Users and Groups.
  3. Right click any user group, for example: UserGroupA.
  4. Right click the user group and then click Customization.
  5. In Features tab, check out Schedule and Publishing, meanwhile Export To is also checked out. 
  6. Click Save & Close.
  7. Login the CMC with a user who belong to the server group UserGroupA.
  8. Locate any report in Folder.
  9. Right click the report, and then click Schedule.
  10. Click Formats, it is notices that all the formats couLd be checked.

Cause

Schedule and Publishing is used to control formats in BILauncpad.

Resolution

  1. Login BILaunchpad.
  2. Locate any webi report, click three dots, and then click Schedule.
  3. Click Report Features tab.
  4. In Formats option, only Web Intelligence option could be selected.
