Symptom

Onboarding processes with uncompleted compliance are cancelled by close job once the closure period configured in SAP_ONB2_ProcessClosurePeriodConfig rule the reached.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Cause

System design

Resolution

If the candidate is hired and has an open compliance process, the job will skip the onboarding process, which means the process will not be marked as Completed.

If the candidate is not hired,  even though the process has an open compliance, the job will cancel it.

Keywords

onboarding auto-cancel, compliance task open, SAP_ONB2_ProcessClosurePeriodConfig, closure period, onboarding cancellation,
