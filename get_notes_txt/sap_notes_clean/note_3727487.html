Symptom

In the Manage Compliance Scenario app, you open an emissions declaration scenario. In the "Activities" section, you select an emitted substance, and then in the corresponding "Emitted Substance Data" section on the right, you hover over the title of a subject. Instead of displaying the standard "Title Details" popup, the system creates a dump.

Other Terms

CL_EHENV_DATA_DEF_Q_SEARCH

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason: Program error.

Prerequisites: The validity of the correction is maintained in the correction instructions.

Solution

This SAP Note resolves the issue, so that when you hover over an emitted substance subject, the "Title Details" popup is displayed.

The Support Packages that contain this correction are listed in the "Support Packages" section.
Alternatively, you can implement the correction instructions of this SAP Note.
