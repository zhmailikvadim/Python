Symptom

When creating a service confirmation, the error "Enter a customer for special stock W" occurs.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a service confirmation
  2. Save the service confirmation

Resolution

  1. Go to customizing activity SPRO->Service->Transactions->Settings for Service Transactions->Integration->Logistics Integration->Enterprise Organizational Management->Define Logistics Scenario for Material Withdrawal
  2. Add an entry as following:
    Tr     Item  Srv.Or        Logistic Scenario  Movement
    xxxx  yyyy  *              P Plant/Storage     291
    where xxxx is the transaction type of service confirmation and yyyy is the item category.
