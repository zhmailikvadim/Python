Symptom

When issues occur in the application, it is often required to search the Application Server Logs for relevant information to continue investigation

Environment

  • SAP Product and Quotation Management for Insurance 2.0
  • SAP Product and Quotation Management for Insurance 3.0

Resolution

  1. Locate the Application Server Log for FS-PRO or FS-QUO
  2. Open log with a log viewer or text editor. The log results will be ordered by timestamp
  3. Search for the relevant information
    • Search by approximate time of action
    • Search by keyword/feature such as "error", "publish", etc
  4. Generate relevant log output
    • Execute relevant action, immediately refresh log, and scroll to latest entry

Keywords

log, error, java.lang, exception, defaulttrace, trace
