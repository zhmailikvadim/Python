Symptom

When a candidate changes their answers to prescreening questions through their application profile after the initial submission, the changes are recorded in the Audit Trail.

If the field "questionResponse = anonymize=true" is configured in the application template, the screening responses will be anonymized in the application screening details and in reports. However, the application Audit Trail is still retaining the original responses.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Cause

Known error or limitation in Legacy workbench

Resolution

Our Engineering and Product teams have reviewed this behavior and confirmed that it is specific to the Legacy Workbench.

As the Legacy Workbench is planned for deprecation in future releases, Ongoing fixes and enhancements are being implemented in the New Workbench
