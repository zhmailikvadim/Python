Symptom

In report HSKCDPRZ, there is an incorrect text in the Confirmation of payment PDF output.

The calculation of the child tax bonus may be incorrect when the employee tax base is lower than 25,740 EUR for one or more children who have two or more periods with a bonus claim, separated by a period without a claim. In such cases, only the first period is calculated, while the subsequent periods are incorrectly ignored.

Other Terms

HRSK, PDF, HR_SK_HSKCDPRZ, HSKCDPRZ

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Slovakia (HRSK).

Solution

This SAP Note delivers corrections described in the chapter Symptom.
