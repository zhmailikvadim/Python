Symptom

Enabling/Disabling the Messaging Feature for a Buyer Account.

Resolution

  1. Login into your SAP Business Network Buyer account.
  2. Click your user initials in the top-right corner and select Settings.
  3. Under Account Administration click Messaging Configuration.
  4. Check/Uncheck Enable Messaging.
  5. Click Update.
