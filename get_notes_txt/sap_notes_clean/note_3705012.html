Symptom

Duplicated sort fields may cause pdf exporting failure in Crystal Reports for Eclipse

Error message: A column has been specified more than once in the order by list. Columns in the order by list must be unique.

Other Terms

duplicate, sort field, crystal reports for eclipse

Reason and Prerequisites

1. create a crystal report in Crystal Reports designer, add several columns to detail section and sort on one of them (say column "A").

2. add a Group on column "A", with group sorting (also on column "A").

3. export the report to pdf in Crystal Reports for Eclipse, get below error:

  • A column has been specified more than once in the order by list. Columns in the order by list must be unique.

Solution

Such duplicated "Sort Fields" is not supported in Crystal Reports for Eclipse.

To fix the issue, please update your report in Crystal Reports designer:

1. Remove one of the duplicated sorting on column "A" (Just keep one sorting on same column, in group section, or in detail section);
2. Then save your report.
