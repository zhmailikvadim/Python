Symptom

When attempting to delete bank details from an Employee Business Partner record, the bank account entry appears in display mode and cannot be selected for deletion. The bank account ID was created directly in the Business Partner master record rather than synchronized from HR master data.

Users are unable to delete the bank account entry using standard Business Partner transaction, which may result in incorrect bank details being used for payment processing.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA on-premise
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Resolution

To resolve this issue and remove the manually created bank account from the Employee Business Partner, execute the Employee BP synchronization report with the override bank option. This will synchronize the bank details from HR master data and remove any additional bank accounts that were manually maintained in the Business Partner record.

Follow these steps:

  1. Go to transaction SE38
  2. Execute report /SHCM/RH_SYNC_BUPA_EMPL_SINGLE
  3. On the selection screen, enter the personnel number of the affected employee
  4. Check the flag "Override Bank with Default"
  5. Execute the report
  6. Verify in transaction SLG1 that no errors occurred during synchronization
  7. Confirm in the Business Partner transaction that the manually created bank account has been deleted and only the bank details from HR infotype 9 remain

Note: The "Override Bank with Default" option will delete all bank accounts maintained in the Business Partner record (including manually maintained entries) and transfer only the bank account information from the employee's HR infotype 9.

If authorization errors occur during execution (particularly related to authorization object B_BUPA_BNK), ensure the user has sufficient authorizations to modify Business Partner bank details.
