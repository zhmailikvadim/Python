Symptom

Custom Business Objects app missing in BTP ABAP

Environment

  • Product: SAP BTP ABAP environment
  • Identity and Access Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the BTP ABAP Fiori launchpad.
  2. Try to launch the Custom Business Objects app or search for the business catalog SAP_CORE_BC_EXT_CBO; the app/catalog is not available.

Cause

CBO app is supported in S/4Hana only.

Resolution

  1. Note that the Custom Business Objects app and the business catalog SAP_CORE_BC_EXT_CBO are not available in the BTP ABAP environment; this is by design and not a scoping/configuration issue.
  2. To create custom business objects in BTP ABAP, develop them directly in the ABAP environment.
  3. Alternatively, create Custom Business Objects in S/4HANA and expose or call them from the BTP ABAP environment.
  4. Refer to: Extensibility on SAP Business Technology Platform.

See Also
