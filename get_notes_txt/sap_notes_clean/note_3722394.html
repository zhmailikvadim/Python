Symptom

Conversion overflow dump occurs during the display of snapshot details at the contract item.

Replication Steps -

- Create a contract with WB21 transaction.
- Create a FLAT price fixation and make sure that the whole number of total amount should more than 7 digits and Save.
- Display the contract using WB23 and click on the snapshot details under the item.
- Conversion overflow dump occurs.

Other Terms

Agricultural Contract Management; ACM; Snapshot

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
