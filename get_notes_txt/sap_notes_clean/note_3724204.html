Symptom

The system is allocating more units than available unrestricted stock, resulting in Delivered + Allocated quantities exceeding physical stock, after STO delivery creation.

Other Terms

VL10C , VJ , VS , OVZ9A , OVZ9

Reason and Prerequisites

This problem is caused because of a program error.

Solution

The program corrections are contained in the tab correction instruction of this SAP Note. You need to implement the corrections in your system.
