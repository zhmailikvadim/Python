Symptom

  • RE-FX contracts or RE-FX objects are managed.
  • It is required to influence the search help functionality in the partner tab.

Environment

  • Flexible Real Estate Management (RE-FX)
  • Contract and Lease Management
  • SAP ERP 
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the partner tab in RE-FX transactions
  2. Open the search help
  3. It is not possible to define additional search criteria to influence the result list

Cause

The issue arises due to the absence of additional search criteria or the need to enhance existing criteria.  

Resolution

  1. Implement the BADI_REBP_PARTNER_SEARCH to influence the behavior of the RE-FX partner search help.
  2. Refer to SAP Note 1540964 - Partner: BAdI for business partner selection for detailed instructions on implementation.

See Also

  • Refer to: SAP Note 1540964 - Partner: BAdI for business partner selection
