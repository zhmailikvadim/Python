Symptom

In the Manage Waste Transportation Requests (F6084) and the My Waste Transfer Requests (F6083) apps, a waste transfer request displays the status Disposal Confirmed even though the outgoing amount and the confirmed amount in the related transportation document differ. The status Quantity Discrepancy is not available in the value help for the Status filter.

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason: Program error.

Prerequisites: The validity of the correction is maintained in the correction instructions.

Solution

This SAP Note introduces the status Quantity Discrepancy for waste transfer requests and updates the status synchronization between transfer requests and their related transportation documents.

The support packages that contain this correction are listed in the "Support Packages" section.
