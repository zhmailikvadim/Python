Symptom

Introduction of Accounting Document Withholding Tax Item Log Table ACCT_SRF_RPIT_WI and CDS View I_StRpWhldgTaxItemLog for Withholding Tax–Based Document Selection in DRC Statutory Reporting

Other Terms

 ACCT_SRF_RPIT_WI, Accounting document log table, DRC, I_StRpWhldgTaxItemLog

Reason and Prerequisites

 This SAP Note is valid for all countries/regions.

Solution

Implement this note to introduce the Accounting Document Withholding Tax Item Log Table ACCT_SRF_RPIT_WI and the CDS View I_StRpWhldgTaxItemLog, which record accounting documents at withholding tax item level during the DRC run, enabling selection and tracking of documents based on withholding tax types for statutory reporting.
