Symptom

When a standard operation for a node is disabled, the corresponding operation can also not be executed via EML in determinations.

Other Terms

CBO2, EML, Determination

Reason and Prerequisites

The behavior is caused by a programming error. Standard operations where incorrectly being omitted instead of being marked as internal in the behavior definition.

Solution

Implement the correction instructions attached to this note to correctly mark disabled standard operations as internal instead of omitting them in the behavior definition.
