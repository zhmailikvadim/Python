Symptom

When a production order has system status PCNF or CNF, the external operation fields "Unit of Measure" and "Conversion of Unit of Measure” are not editable.

Environment

  • SAP ERP Production Planning (PP)
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA, on-premise
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open a production order that has system status PCNF or CNF.
  2. Navigate to the relevant external operation and locate the fields "Unit of Measure" and "Conversion of Unit of Measure".
  3. Attempt to change either field and observe that the fields are not editable.

Cause

This is standard system design: once an operation is partially confirmed or final confirmed, changes to "Unit of Measure" and "Conversion of Unit of Measure" are prevented to avoid inconsistencies with already recorded confirmations.

Resolution

  1. Reverse or cancel the confirmation(s) for the affected operation so that the order no longer has status PCNF or CNF.
  2. Change the required "Unit of Measure" and/or "Conversion of Unit of Measure" values for the operation.
  3. Repost the confirmation(s) after verifying the updated unit settings.

Keywords
