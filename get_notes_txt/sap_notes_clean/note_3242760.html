Symptom


How do I find my the folder I am working in while in SPM editor?




Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Resolution

There are two ways to find your current location;


  1. In the editor, if you click on the Signavio icon in the top left, it will open the current folder in SPM explorer.




  2. In the editor, click the save button in the toolbar, and then you can see the full path by hovering over any of the second line in the save dialogue box.

    You can also change where to save your diagram here by clicking on the paper icon.
