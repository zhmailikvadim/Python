Symptom

PR05 (Travel Expense Manager): Button 'Results: Travel Request' is missing when viewing/ editing an expense report.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

SAP Travel Management - on premise

Reproducing the Issue

PR05: view/ edit travel expense report:


Button 'Results: Travel Request' is missing in your system.

Cause

customizing: Integration Travel Request/Plan with Travel Expenses is not activated

Resolution

Activate Integration of Travel Request/Plan with Travel Expenses in IMG:

'Travel Management - Integration of Travel Request/Plan with Travel Expenses - Activate Integration of Travel Request/Plan with Travel Expenses'

by setting the flag in the checkbox for trip provision variant you are using.

Keywords

travel request, travel expense, integration travel planning, PR05
