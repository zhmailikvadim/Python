Symptom

ATC check S4HANA_READINESS_2023_NO_FLE reports a finding for a syntactically incompatible call of function module BAPI_MATERIAL_EXISTENCECHECK, see Note(s): 0002438131.

Other Terms

PLM-41921, ATC, /DSCSAG/CL_MATERIAL_UTILS, /dscsag/badi_utils

Reason and Prerequisites

Changes in the interface of BAPI_MATERIAL_EXISTENCECHECK may lead to ATC findings if the parameter MATERIAL_LONG is not considered in the call.

Solution

The calls of BAPI_MATERIAL_EXISTENCECHECK have been adjusted to consider parameter MATERIAL_LONG in the relevant methods.
