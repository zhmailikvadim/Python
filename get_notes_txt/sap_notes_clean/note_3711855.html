Symptom

  • The "Is Remote I-9" field is mapped from the Recruiting Management Module (RCM) but does not populate in Form I-9.
  • In the Recruiting Offer Template, the "Is Remote I-9" field is selected as "Yes".
  • After the candidate transitions into the onboarding process, the responsible user completes the New Hire Data Review (NHDR) tasks, Personal Data Collection, and Additional Data Collection.
  • Compliance Form I-9 is triggered, and the user completes and signs Section 1 of Form I-9.
  • Expected behavior: The email notification template "Remote I9 Section 1 Completion Notification Template" should be triggered.
  • Actual behavior: The email is not sent, and the field "Is The New Hire Remote?" appears blank when the I-9 coordinator opens Section 2 of Form I-9.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Map the "Is Remote I-9" field from the Recruiting Management Module (RCM).
  2. In the Recruiting Offer Template, select "Is Remote I-9" as "Yes".
  3. Move the candidate into the onboarding process.
  4. Complete the New Hire Data Review (NHDR) tasks, Personal Data Collection, and Additional Data Collection.
  5. Trigger Compliance Form I-9 and complete Section 1 of Form I-9.
  6. Open Section 2 of Form I-9 as the I-9 coordinator.
  7. Observe that the "Is The New Hire Remote?" field is blank, and the email notification template is not triggered.

Cause

  • Configuration issue: The "Remote I-9" field is incorrectly included in the main data model instead of being country-specific.

Resolution

  1. Remove the "Remote I-9" field from the main data model.
  2. Add the "Remote I-9" field under country specific model and enable it as "Yes".
  3. Test the configuration to ensure the field applies only to the particular country.
