Symptom

When calling MD04 stock-requirements list, and changing the material grouping tab settings,

 a shortdump "GETWA_NOT_ASSIGNED" occurs in program MM61RF50_MDEZX_AUFBAUEN for field-symbol <MDMI>.

Other Terms

MDMIX, <MDMI>, MD04, Stock-Requirements List, MM61RF50.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

The dump is avoided with the corresponding correction.
