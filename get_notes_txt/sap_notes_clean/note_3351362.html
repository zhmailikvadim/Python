Symptom

As of SAP Business One 1.1 PL19 and higher, new content related to browser access for SAP Business One Cloud is documented in the SAP Business One Cloud Administrator's Guide.

The guide How to Deploy SAP Business One Cloud with Browser Access is valid for SAP Business One Cloud 1.1 PL18 and earlier.

Reproducing the issue

Prerequisites:

You are working with SAP Business One Cloud 1.1 PL19 or higher.

Scenario:

When searching for up-to-date information to meet your business needs, you turn to the guide How to Deploy SAP Business One Cloud with Browser Access.

Expected behavior:

You find the updated information.

Actual behavior:

The guide How to Deploy SAP Business One Cloud with Browser Access is only relevant to SAP Business One Cloud 1.1 PL18 and earlier.

Solution

As of SAP Business One 1.1 PL19 and higher, information about using the Browser Access service in SAP Business One Cloud environments is provided in the SAP Business One Cloud Administrator's Guide.

 

This SAP Note is referenced in the document What's New in SAP Business One Cloud 1.1.

Other terms

Cloud Admin Guide, Cloud Browser Access guide
