Symptom

How to filter partner functions replication from S4.

Environment

  • SAP Sales & Service Cloud Version 2 Integration with SAP S4HANA Public Cloud.

Cause

During the initial load, replicate all business partners without including partner functions. Afterwards, perform a reload to include the partner functions.

Obs. If partner functions are cyclical they will make the massage fail. 

                {
                    "code": "mapping.key.notFound.local.8012",
                    "Mapping Error at tempPartnerId: Key mapping not found for ABC, external key $.customer.salesArrangements[].partnerFunctionRelationships[].partnerDisplayId"                },
                {

Resolution

You must implement the filter to do not replicate the partner functions: Cust Sales Area Partner func -> Field Name: Partner Function (PARVW) - Filter: equal to - Value: *

After replicate the Business Partners, remove the filter and replicate the Business Partners again.
