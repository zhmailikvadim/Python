Symptom

The system does not plan a catch-up depreciation for past periods; instead, it counts these past periods as expired useful life and spreads the acquisition through the remaining useful life.

Environment

  • Contract and Lease Management
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Transfer an asset with using ABUMN and transfer posting date of 01.12.XXXX.
  2. Update the fields:
  • Capitalized on 01.12.2025
  • Depreciation Calculation Start Date to 01.08.2025

Observe that depreciation does not begin on the specified start date and no catch-up depreciation is planned for past periods.

Cause

System does not calculate depreciation for any period before the transfer date. To ensure accurate accounting, the amount from the period prior to the transfer date up until the transfer date should be included within the transfer transaction itself.  

Resolution

  • Check the type of acquisition postings being applied to the asset. Specifically, please verify whether the postings are classified as acquisition postings or transfer postings.
  • Additionally, it is important to confirm that depreciation from the source asset has been successfully carried over to the new asset. Ensuring this transfer will help maintain accurate asset accounting and depreciation tracking.
  • If necessary to adjust the depreciation in the target asset, please perform a manual adjustment using manual depreciation or unplanned depreciation.
