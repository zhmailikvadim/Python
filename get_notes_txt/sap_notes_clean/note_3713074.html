Symptom

 The raising of Fiori launchpad notifications for each product compliance event completed by the product compliance event processing (PCEP) scheduler negatively impacts the overall performance of the event processing.

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason:

Performance impact of raising notifications.

Prerequisites:

For information about the validity of the corrections, see the correction instructions.

Solution

This issue is fixed in the patches listed in the "Support Packages & Patches" section below by removing the raising of notifications from the PCEP scheduler.

Alternatively, you can implement the attached correction instructions.
