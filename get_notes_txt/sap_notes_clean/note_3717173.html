Symptom

  • An error occurs when attempting to use Joule within IBP.
  • Error messages include: "Your domain is not trusted. Please contact your system administrator."

Environment

SAP Integrated Business Planning

Reproducing the Issue

Click on Joule icon.

Cause

The domain of the relying application is not trusted by the IAS tenant.

Resolution

  1. Configure trusted domains in Identity Authentication as described in the documentation.
