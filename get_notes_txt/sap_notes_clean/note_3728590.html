Symptom

  • The prerequisite shows as completed on the Learning homepage page, even though users have not completed it.
  • The issue appears to be related to learning homepage assignment card, where older revisions of the prerequisite course are incorrectly recognized as completed.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Learning

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create an item with a prerequisite item of a specific revision (e.g., item A with prerequisite item B rev 2).
  2. Complete an older revision of the prerequisite item (e.g., item B rev 1) and assign Item A to a user.
  3. On the Learning homepage, the assignment card incorrectly shows as Prerequisite completed.
  4. Attempt to register for sessions of the main item (e.g., item A). The system prevents registration, stating the prerequisite is not fulfilled.

Resolution
