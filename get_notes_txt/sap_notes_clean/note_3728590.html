Symptom

In the process "Receipt of 55109 from Responsible SUP to DSO" (DE_B_MDC_55109_SUP2DSO_RECV), when several different correspondence customers of supplier are received within the same contract period, some business partners of the correspondence customer of supplier may not be saved to the database.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO, SUP.

Other Terms

MDC, correspondence customer of supplier, Z04, NaAdrCorrespondenceCustomerOfSupp, /UCOM/CUSTOMER, /US4G/CL_UBD_DATA_ADDRESS, /US4G/IF_BADI_NM_ADDR_PROCESS~ENABLE_BP_FROM_CUSTAB, /US4G/BADI_NM_ADDR_PROCESS.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, you still need to implement method /US4G/IF_BADI_NM_ADDR_PROCESS~ENABLE_BP_FROM_CUSTAB in BAdI /US4G/BADI_NM_ADDR_PROCESS to enable saving and retrieving business partners of correspondence customers of supplier (Z04) to and from database table /UCOM/CUSTOMER. You can verify the fix by checking that all business partners for the name address category correspondence customers of supplier are saved in database table /UCOM/CUSTOMER.
