Symptom

In Central Finance system, PFKBER field of the structure ACCIT is not mapped  resulting in errors related to incorrect PFKBER value

Other Terms

FINS_CFIN_MDGFS, FKBER, PKBER, UNCAUGHT_EXCEPTION , CX_FINS_FATAL_EXCEPTION, /AIF/MES078 , /AIF/MES178

Reason and Prerequisites

Configuration Issue

Solution

The correction applies to the Central Finance system.

Implement the attached correction instructions and execute the included report or implement the corresponding Support Package.
