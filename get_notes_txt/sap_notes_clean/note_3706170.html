Symptom

SAP and Prometheus Group (PG) have enjoyed a long and mutually beneficial, multi-faceted relationship. SAP customers use PG’s solutions acquired via SAP’s Solution Extension partnership model through which SAP sells and supports PG solutions.

As SAP and Prometheus Group’s relationship continues to evolve, Prometheus Group is focused on deepening its market presence by delivering AI-enhanced data management solutions directly to customers, and as a result, SAP and PG have agreed to terminate our Solution Extension relationship.

SAP and PG will continue to work together, and PG remains a partner in good standing with SAP and will continue to list solutions, including the current SOLEX offerings MDG EAM, MDG RFM and AIW, on the SAP Store.  Prometheus Group is committed to continue investing, innovating and supporting the MDG products and offering them directly to the market.

Reason and Prerequisites

As we winddown the Solution Extension component of our broader relationship, SAP and PG are working together to support customers and their needs moving forward, which include the following:

  • Full Support for all solutions were purchased from SAP will continue for all customers (i.e., cloud and on-premise) through December 31, 2026.
  • For customers who purchased a perpetual license from SAP, starting January 1, 2027 until December 31, 2028 only bug fixes will be provided, and no new releases or upgrades will be available.  Effective January 1, 2029, support and/or maintenance will only be provided for an additional fee (subject to a services agreement with PG).
  • For customers who purchased a term license for a cloud subscription from SAP, you will receive support services from SAP through the end of the term of your license. SAP plans to issue non-renewal notices to customers in the near future, and notice of non-renewal requires at least 6 months’ notice under SAP’s support policies.
  • In all cases, customers may also choose to contract directly with PG for outstanding support and licensing matters.
