Symptom

You use SAP Travel Management for S/4 HANA Fiori application "My Travel and Expenses for Business Traveler" or "Travel and Expenses for Travel Assistant".

Upon creation of a receipt, changing the expense type by entering value manually might lead to an incorrect selection. The value-help filtering wrongly returns several entries.

Other Terms

MTE, Travel Expenses, Travel Expense Report

F6190A, F0584C

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Import the correction instruction or apply the corresponding support package.
