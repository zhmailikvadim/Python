Symptom

  • "Material price analysis" button is not displayed in the Accounting 1 view of the material master (MM03).
  • The issue occurs when accessing split valuated material with Valuation Category ‘B’.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the material master data in MM03.
  2. Navigate to the Accounting 1 view.
  3. Observe that the "Material price analysis" button is missing.

Cause

For split valuated materials , if actual costing data or Material Ledger master data does not exist for the mother segment (valuation type blank) in the relevant table, additional Material Ledger currencies and buttons to navigate to the Material Price Analysis or the cost component split will not be displayed.

Resolution

Enter the 'valuation type' when accessing the Accounting 1 view in MM03.

Keywords
