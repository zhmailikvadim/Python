Symptom

Facing below error while accessing Manage Workflow for Supplier Invoice Approval  app in SAP Ariba Invoicing:

Unable to load data: Check your internet connection. If that doesn't help, try reloading. If this doesn't help either, contact your administrator. 

 

Resolution

To enable the use of the Manage Workflows for Supplier Invoice Approval app, you need to do the following:

  • For access to all process instances from all environments, add ProcessAutomationAdmin group to the respective user.

  • Please note that if you add this role ProcessAutomationAdmin you do not need to follow the alternate approach mentioned in the Assigning Administrator Roles/Groups to Yourself or to the Administrator. This is to only the instances that belong to the environment where you are onboarding the package.

See Also

 Assigning Administrator Roles/Groups to Yourself or to the Administrator.
