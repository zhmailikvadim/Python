Symptom

You are going to perform a Remote Client Copy. Before that, you are going to perform a "Test Run". You want to know, whether the source and target clients will be locked during the "Test Run".

Environment

NetWeaver ABAP based system.

Reproducing the Issue

Perform "Test Run" of "Remote Client Copy".

Resolution

During the "Test Run" of "Remote Client Copy", the source and target clients will not be locked. So it doesn't impact the business on the clients.
