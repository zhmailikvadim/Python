Symptom

While performing direct transfer postings via MIGO, user adds one or multiple identical line item entries along with an existing entry in the item list. This payload, when sent from S/4 system, results in "Unique constraint violation error" in BRH due to duplicate entries. This is because, S/4 prepares one entry of Component of Batch record for each line item that exists even though they are identical.

Other Terms

 ISLS, BRH, LSCH_CPNTB, Duplicate Payload, Components of batches

Solution

Clear the duplicate entries from LSCH_CPNTB outbound payload before sending it to BRH.

Please implement this note.
