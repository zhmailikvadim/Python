Symptom

  • Users require the ability to edit content files in Work Zone while being restricted from deleting them.
  • Current permission levels in Work Zone do not differentiate between "Edit" and "Delete" actions, as per the official SAP documentation.
  • Clarification is sought on whether this separation of permissions is technically feasible.

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors WorkZone
  • SAP WorkZone Advanced Edition

Cause

This behavior is by design.

Work Zone content permissions are defined at a high level and do not support granular separation between “Edit” and “Delete” actions.

When a user is granted Full permission for documents, delete rights are inherently included and cannot be removed independently.

Resolution

The requested permission separation (Edit without Delete) is not technically possible in Work Zone.

Content permissions are limited to predefined permission levels and Delete cannot be excluded from Edit rights.

Document and Image Permissions (Content Repository)

Work Zone provides the following permission levels for documents:

Permission

Allowed Actions

FullView, Download, Copy, Edit, Delete, Rename, Move, Mirror
LimitedView, Download, Copy
Read-OnlyView only

There is no permission level that allows Edit while blocking Delete.

📘 Wiki and Knowledge Base Permissions

Edit and Delete are bundled together under Full permission.

There is no option to customize individual actions (such as disabling Delete while keeping Edit).
