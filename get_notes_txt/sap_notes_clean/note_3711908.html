Symptom

  • The supplier has a XML without CBS/IBS conditions, however, the purchase orders created after Brazil Tax Reform go-live includes CBS/IBS conditions.
  • There is a Purchase order that do not include CBS/IBS conditions and its invoice has not been created yet, leaving doubts on how to proceed with invoice creation.
**Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.**

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA

Resolution

Once condition records have validation dates configured in transaction code FV11:



The IBS/CBS will be calculated on purchase orders based on the "Document Date" and the configured "Valid From" date on FV11. 


To include purchase orders that will remain open after go-live, adjust the "valid from" date for the condition records accordingly.

Disclaimer: Remember to revert the "Valid From" date in FV11 to its original configuration after creating the Purchase Order without CBS/IBS conditions. This will ensure that other Purchase Orders are created with the CBS/IBS conditions taken into account.

For purchase orders created without the CBS/IBS conditions requiring invoice creation post go-live, this situation is considered a consulting matter rather than a product-related issue. The next steps for this scenario should be discussed with the business and consulting team, as it involves a business query.
