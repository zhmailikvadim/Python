Symptom

In the Manage Maintenance Plans (F5325) FIORI app, the 'Remaining Period' and 'Remaining Counter' micro chart fields are blank / empty.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

  • SAP R/3
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA, on-premise
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open Manage Maintenance Plans (F5325) App.
  2. Click on the 'Go' button.
  3. Observe that 'Remaining Period' and 'Remaining Counter' fields on the list have no value.

Cause

The scheduling parameters for the Maintenance plan are not or only partially maintained.

'Remaining Period' and 'Remaining Counter' are only calculated if 'Start Date of Scheduling' and 'End Date for Scheduling' fields are maintained under the Maintenance Plan. 

Resolution

Maintain 'Start Date of Scheduling' and 'End Date for Scheduling' fields for the Maintenance Plan.
