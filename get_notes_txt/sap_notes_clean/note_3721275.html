Symptom

  • Users of Fiori app F4542 can add Business Partners (BP) to positive or negative lists without an authorization object controlling this activity.
  • There is no way to restrict access to add partners to the positive list.

Environment

  • SAP Global Trade Services Edition for Hana

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access Fiori app F4542.
  2. Make any selection within the app.
  3. Add a partner to the positive list.

Cause

This is a standard behaviour.

Resolution

  • The authorization check for adding BP addresses to positive or negative lists is the same as that for manually releasing or confirming SPL blocks.
  • Users require Change activity for authorized legal regulations in authorization objects GTS_BP_LRG and GTS_BP_LRG to perform this action.
  • Further restriction specifically for adding to positive or negative lists is not available as a standard solution.
