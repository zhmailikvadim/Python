Symptom

User scheduled story Job and set on Completion but this Notification email not triggered for Story reports Job Schedule

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Core 2505
  • Report Centre story Schedule

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create and schedule for Story reporting
  2. Enable notifications for Schedule on start and on-completion
  3. Received the email notification when the schedule was started
  4. But didn't receive the email notification when the job schedule is completed. 

Cause

This has been identified as enhancement

Resolution

This is an enhancement that has been implemented in our internal environment and is target to be deployed 1H2026 (2605) release.

Keywords

Schedule, notification, on, completion, start, report, centre, export, email, not , received, triggered.
