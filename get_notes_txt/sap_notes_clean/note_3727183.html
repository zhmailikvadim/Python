Symptom

  • The flexible workflow for Purchase Orders (PO) is triggering additional approvers from an unexpected team.
  • The Teams and Responsibility team has been configured to trigger approvals only to members of team A, but members from team B are also determined.
  • Along with the expected approvers, four additional approvers from another team are being triggered.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Private Cloud Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a Purchase Order (PO) the triggered flexible workflow where the agent is determined via Teams and Responsibilities.
  2. Observe the approval workflow triggering additional approvers from an unexpected team.

Resolution

This is the standard design.

In the current design, attributes such as company code, purchasing group, purchasing organization, and supplier are utilized to determine the approver. These attributes are based on the field values maintained within the "Team and Manage Teams and Responsibilities - Procurement" application.

However, it is important to note that attributes such as material, material group, plant, and purchasing category are not considered. This is because these fields exist at the item level within the purchase order, and the design does not account for item-level attributes when determining approvers.
