Symptom

  • When creating a claim with an attachment, it is not replicated to the One-Time Payments (payComponentNonRecurring) portlet.
  • All other claim fields are replicated to payComponentNonRecurring as expected.
  • No error messages are displayed.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a claim that includes an attachment.
  2. Open the employee’s One-Time Payments (payComponentNonRecurring) portlet and verify that claim data is replicated, except the attachment, which is missing.

Cause

Resolution

Expected behavior.
