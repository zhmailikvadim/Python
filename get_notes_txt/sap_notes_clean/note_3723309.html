Symptom

Invoice attachments are not being copied over to auto-generated Service Entry Sheets (SES) in Ariba Buying and Invoicing (B&I).

For Service Entry Sheets auto-generated from invoices in Ariba Network, the attachments are successfully included in the SES.

Environment

Service Entry Sheet

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Enable Auto SES functionality in Ariba Buying and Invoicing (B&I).
  2. Create a service Purchase Requisition (PR).
  3. Submit and approve the PR; a Purchase Order (PO) gets generated.
  4. Navigate to the PO and click on the Invoice button.
  5. Create a paper invoice against this PO.
  6. Go to Line Item > Details > File Attachments > Add attachment > add a test attachment.
  7. Submit and approve the invoice, Auto SES gets generated.
  8. Navigate to the SES and observe that the invoice attachment is not present in the Attachments - Entire Service Sheet section on the SES.

Cause

Line-level attachments are not supported for Service Entry Sheets.

Resolution

Create an influence ticket to initiate the enhancement process for supporting line-level attachments in Service Entry Sheets.
