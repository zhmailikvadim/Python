Symptom

When putaway warehouse task is created before GR in case the inspection rule is inactive or the stock is not isnpection relevant - for example due to BAdI /SCWM/EX_QM_INSP_RELEVANCE - the putaway WT may be created on quality stock instead of free stock. As a result the stock category will be quality instead of unrestricted.

Other Terms

DQP9, WT, Putaway WT , PWP,  /SCWM/PRDI , Warehouse Task, Quality relevance, Inspection Rule, Inactive, Embedded, Decentral, /SCWM/EX_QM_INSP_RELEVANCE

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions.
