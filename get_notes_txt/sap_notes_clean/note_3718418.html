Symptom

The process for requesting a TRR from the Buyer portal has changed.

The previous page where users clicked “Request Relationship” has been intentionally disabled — this is expected behavior.

Kindly advice all the buyers  to follow the steps below:

 

1. Search for the supplier using their ANID in the Trading Partners section in the beginning of the Page

2. Click on the supplier's name from the search results

3. On the right side of the page, you will see "Request for Information"

4. Under "Request for Information," click "Invite" to send a trading relationship request

 

This is all they need to do to send a request as TRR to all your suppliers.
