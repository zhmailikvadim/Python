Symptom

You use inbound cargo registration (ICR) and have an advanced shipping notification (ASN) for a freight order without truck and trailer registration numbers. If you send an update for this ASN via EDI inbound processing with a defined trailer registration number, the app incorrectly maps the trailer registration number to the truck registration field, even though no truck registration number is provided.

Other Terms

ICR, inbound cargo registration

Reason and Prerequisites

You use SAP S/4HANA for manufacturing logistics or this SAP Note is a prerequisite of another SAP Note.

Solution

Implement the attached correction.
