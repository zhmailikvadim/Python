Symptom

The New Zealand government has legislated an increase to the minimum KiwiSaver contribution rates for both employees and employers, effective 1 April 2026.

As per the latest legislative update, the minimum contribution rates under the KiwiSaver scheme will change as follows:

  • Current rate: 3% (employee and employer)
  • New rate: 3.5% (employee and employer)
  • Effective date: 1 April 2026

At this time, the official documentation (PDF/link) from New Zealand authorities has not yet been released.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

SAP is currently working on delivering the updated KiwiSaver-related forms reflecting the new contribution rates.

Customers are advised to monitor this KBA for updates. Internal ticket number OBX-41933. It can be tracked with the Account Executive team, customers can contact them.

Updates on March, 30th: as Onboarding Development team expected to have the updated PDF and Link by Match, 31st, we expect the new version to be patched to customers on April, 2nd latest.
