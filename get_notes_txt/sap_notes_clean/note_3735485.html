Resolution

If you would like to see a functionality/feature included in future updates,  you can submit an enhancement request through our Continuous Customer Influence session (Campaign #4067). 

You can find more details and access the submission portal here.

Please Note: When submitting your request, please ensure that all relevant details are included directly in your submission. 
Please avoid referencing an SAP case alone, as cases are not visible to other customers or partners. This could impact the visibility and voting process for your request, which is essential for prioritization.
