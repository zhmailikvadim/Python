Symptom

/sdf/smon is scheduled for daily monitoring. One or multiple days of monitoring data were not captured.
Job log(s) of /SDF/SMON_SCHEDULER from those days indicate(s) there is  "No more than 1 Watchdog is allowed" info message than Watchdog job does not exist error message.

Other Terms

No more than 1 Watchdog is allowed,  /SDF/SMON_WATCHDOG  does not exist

Reason and Prerequisites

Parallel job's automatic or manual cancellation can lead to this situation.

Solution

Implement the attached correction.
