Symptom

For Pick WTs that are activated during confirmation of the HU to the Pick Point Storage bin, the calculation of the Pick Completion Date may not consider the durations, that are maintained for the follow-up steps in the Process Oriented Storage Control. This also can lead to wrong values for the Latest Starting Date in the Warehouse Order, and therefore to delays in the shipping.
