Symptom

While attempting to create a base, or chain, rule for various approvable types, the same predefined conditions show from the drop-down, but are not working to expectation.

Resolution

Please reference our SAP Help article for Predefined conditions for base and chained rules here.

You will note that only certain conditions apply to all types of approvables, but many are distinctly reserved for just a few approvable types.  Thus, please take note of which approval flow you are editing to ensure you select the correct predefined condition.

Additionally, to meet a business need for a certain configuration, the next step would be to create an approver lookup table with the right conditions, and approvers, configured.
