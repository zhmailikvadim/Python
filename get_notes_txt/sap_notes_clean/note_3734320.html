Symptom

  • Edit button is not available in the app Manage Credit Accounts.
  • When releasing a sales order via the Manage Documented Credit Decisions app, the system shows the error: "Object could not be locked".

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Manage Documented Credit Decisions app.
  2. Select the documented credit decision for the relevant sales order and choose Release.
  3. Observe that the system returns the error "Object could not be locked".

Cause

Changes exist for the business role after system update. Business role displays "Yes, Changes Exist"

Resolution

Follow the steps provided in SAP Help Portal: How to Manage Business Role Changes After an Upgrade.
