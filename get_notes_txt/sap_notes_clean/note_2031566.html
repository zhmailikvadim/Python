Symptom

P45 supplementary report shows incorrect values in Total Supplementary Pay and in Total Tax deducted field if the EE left the company last year and there was some additional payments after the leaving date which was not reported in last p45.

 

Test scenario:

Leaving date: 01.09.2013

Additional payment for 2013: 1000 EUR

Additional payment for 2014: 500 EUR

 

P45 XML shows 2500 EUR in total suppl. pay instead of 1500 EUR.

Other Terms

P45 Supplementary XML, HIECP450

Reason and Prerequisites

The P45 program add the previous year values two times to the total value.

Solution

Please apply the correction instructions attached to note.
