Symptom

Due to a new update from the Ministry of Finance, the checkbox "Income in Slovakia reaches 90%" on the PDF output in the HSKCDPRZ report is now selected if:

  • the employee is a tax non-resident (P0252-DNERZ) and income in Slovakia reaches 90% (P0257-TTFB405),
    OR
  • the employee is a tax resident and income in Slovakia reaches 90% (P0257-TTFB405) and has either applied for a tax bonus, RZD output fields r.09 OR r.10, ... OR r.13 are not initial, OR child tax relief on P0257 (applied child bonus 01/02).

These changes are then reflected in report HSKCDPHP, field 'Income in Slovakia reached 90%' in part V.

Other Terms

HRSK, HSKCDPRZ.

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Slovakia (HRSK).

Solution

This SAP Note delivers corrections described in the chapter Symptom.

The correction for the field 'Pr.SK 90%' in the Detailed PFD output (accessible via ALV or the 'Calculation of RZD' button in IT0257) will be provided in a future Note.
