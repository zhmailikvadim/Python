Symptom

This KBA explains the reason why mid-month transfers are not supported in the Singapore payroll solution.

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll

Cause

  • The Singapore payroll area is executed on a monthly basis, and the standard tables contain only one record for each month.
    Mid-month transfers lack legal support for calculating CPF contributions in the transfer month.
  • Mid-month transfers lack legal support for generating the CPF/MSO monthly report for the transfer month.
  • Separate IR8A files will be generated for different employer reference numbers if the transfer occurs at the beginning of a month.
    Mid-month transfers lack legal support for separating the income for different employers in IR8A.
    An error message 'Mid-month transfer not supported' appears when generating the IR8A form for mid-month transfers.

Resolution

 Ensure employee transfers occur at the beginning of a month.

Keywords

mid-month, transfer, CPF, MSO, IR8A
