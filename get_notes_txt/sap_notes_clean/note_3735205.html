Symptom

In the process "Start of Supply for Electricity (DSO View)" (DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SBS), when triggering the embedded process "Resend ORDERS 17134 Configuration" (DE_B_US4G_EMBED_ORDR_SUPASS_CONFIG) in step 0070, the measurement product is incorrectly determined in the UBD POD data. This causes the MOS to receive an incorrect measurement product in the process "Receipt of ORDERS 17134 (MOS/MALO View)" (DE_B_ORDR_SUPASS_CONFIG_MOSMALO), resulting in a BPEM case in step 0600.

This issue is relevant for the market roles: DSO.

Other Terms

ORDERS 17134, POD, Measurement Product, UBD POD data, DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SBS, DE_B_US4G_EMBED_ORDR_SUPASS_CONFIG, DE_B_ORDR_SUPASS_CONFIG_DSO, DE_B_ORDR_SUPASS_CONFIG_MOSMALO, class /US4G/CL_PRST_SAC_RESEND_FUT

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because of a program error in the determination logic for the measurement product.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.
