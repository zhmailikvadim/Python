Symptom

Reversal of cancellation quantities does not reverse the cancelled pricing lot quantities

Environment

SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create an ACM contract and price fix the item quantity.
  2. Select the line item.
  3. Go to cancellations  tab.
  4. Select cancellations type.
  5. Reason code.
  6. Select pricing lot.
  7. Enter quantity to be cancelled and save. 

Resolution

  1. When the quantity is cancelled without selecting the reversal status, the pricing quantity is updated to 0.
  2. After the reversal status is checked in the Event Information tab, the quantity is successfully reversed to its original value.
