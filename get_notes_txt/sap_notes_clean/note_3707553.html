Symptom

Within the Opportunity, the playbook card (e.g. Survey activity) cannot be rearranged to be the first card in all phases of the playbook. The system automatically places survey cards at the end when other card types, such as tasks, are present.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Cloud Version 2

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to Guided Selling.
  2. Open an Opportunity.
  3. Locate the Survey activity card in the Playbook section.
  4. Observe that the survey card is placed last, and it cannot be rearranged to appear as the first card.

Cause

Resolution

Currently, sequences in Playbooks are not supported. Only mandatory activities and actions are displayed first in the Playbook.

To ensure the survey appears first, it must be marked as mandatory in the Playbook settings. If there are multiple mandatory activities, the survey cannot be guaranteed to appear as the first card.

For sequencing of activities, this functionality is considered a new requirement.
