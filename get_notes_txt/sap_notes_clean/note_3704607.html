Symptom

  • A Carrier Invoice is received and increases the charge item values.
  • After the related Dispute Case is approved and resolved, the increased values are reflected in the Freight Order charges.
  • When a Credit Memo is later received and the Carrier Invoice is cancelled, the Freight Order charges are not reverted to their original values.

Environment

  • SAP Transportation Management Embedded in S/4HANA 1709+

Reproducing the Issue

  1. A Freight Order is created with a charge item that has a Rate Amount of 100.00 USD
  2. The Freight Order is sent to the Carrier
  3. A Carrier Invoice is received with an additional 10% tax, increasing the amount to 110.00 USD. At the same time, a Dispute Case is created
  4. The Dispute Case is approved and resolved
  5. Once the Dispute Case is resolved, the charge item value in the Freight Order is updated to 110.00 USD
  6. A Credit Memo is received to cancel the Carrier Invoice
  7. The Carrier Invoice is cancelled, but the Freight Order charge amount remains 110.00 USD instead of being reverted to 100.00 USD
  8. The user expects the Freight Order charges to be restored to their original value after the Credit Memo is processed

Cause

Standard System Behavior.

Resolution

  • Once the Dispute Case is resolved and the Freight Order amount is updated, the system does not reverse the Dispute Case status and the Freight Order charge update when a Credit Memo is received.
  • The Credit Memo only cancel the Carrier Invoice. Since the Carrier Invoice is an external document sent by the carrier, SAP TM cannot modify its values. If the carrier needs to correct the amount, they must send a new Carrier Invoice with the updated values.
  • If the Freight Order charges need to be updated again, a new Carrier Invoice must be received. This will trigger the creation of a new Dispute Case, which will update the Freight Order charges accordingly.

Keywords

Freight Order, Credit Memo, Carrier Invoice, Freight Settlement Dispute Case, Dispute Management, Cancelled, Rate Amount, Reset Charges.
