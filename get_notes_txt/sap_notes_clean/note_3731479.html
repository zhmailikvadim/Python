Symptom

  • Vendor details are missing and Preferred Vendor field is greyed out in the item-level Procurement tab in a Service Order using the "External Service item" item category.
  • Vendor cannot be entered in the Procurement tab at item level.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA 
  • S4CRM: Service Order 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create or open a Service Order with an item that uses the "External Service item" item category.
  2. Open the item and go to the Procurement tab.
  3. Observe that the Preferred Vendor field is disabled (greyed out) and cannot be edited.

Cause

The Preferred Vendor functionality is unavailable for S/4HANA Service (S4CRM) external service items; the field is disabled by design and does not display or accept vendor data.

Resolution

1. To maintain vendor partner for a service order:  
   a. In customizing (SPRO), add partner function 00000019 (Vendor) to the partner determination procedure used by the relevant service item category.  
   b. In the Service Order item, open the Parties Involved tab and maintain the vendor under partner function 00000019 (Vendor).  
2. The Preferred Vendor feature is not available in S/4HANA, so the Preferred Vendor field in the Procurement tab remains non-editable for external service items.  
3. If this feature is required, submit a development request to have this feature added to future S/4HANA releases.
