Symptom

Post 2H 2024 release, Job Requisition Details page icons are only displaying "Open in a new tab or window" when hovered on instead of it's short description like View full form, Print Preview, Info about this form and so on.

Images captured are from DEMO Instance 

Production Instance:

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login to the instance
  2. Navigate to Recruiting from Home page
  3. Access any requisition
  4. Mouse-over icons on top right corner on the Job Requisition Details page to see the text "Open in a new tab or window"

Cause

2H 2024 release issue

Resolution

The issue is currently under investigation by our Engineering team and a fix will be delivered. 

If you are experiencing this issue, you can submit a ticket to SAP Support or click on the Star icon to bookmark this article to receive further updates. For how to subscribe a KBA, please refer to 2171560 - How to get notified about new or updated SAP Notes and/or KBAs - SAP for Me.
