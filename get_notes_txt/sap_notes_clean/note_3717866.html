Symptom

After the inbound of time confirmations of an order in the receiving system is showing the following issues:

In the receiving system

  • time confirmation counter (AFRU-RMZHL) is not in sequence after cancellation is performed in the sending system.
  • time confirmation counter (AFRU-RMZHL) is not in sequence after an additional time confirmation for the operation is performed in the sending system.
  • time confirmation counter are not created after cancellation is performed in the sending system.

In the receiving system there are already existing time confirmations for an operation of the order in question!

Other Terms

Distribution, redistribution, /ISDFPS/PMCP1, /ISDFPS/PMDIS2.

/ISDFPS/TC, /ISDFPS/WO.

/ISDFPS/WOUPS, /ISDFPS/LWOUPSF02, WO_DIRECT_UPD_CR.

/ISDFPS/TCUPS.

AFVC, AFVC-RMZHL.

AFRU, AFRU-RMZHL.

IW31, IW32, IW33, IW41, IW43, IW45.

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason:

Corrections/enhancements with SAP Notes

  • IS-DFS-PM-DIS        3636922        Redistribution: Confirmation not redistributed (2)
  • IS-DFS-PM-DIS        3518606        Redistribution: Confirmation not redistributed
  • IS-DFS-PM-DIS        3313604        Redistribution: Too many orders selected
  • IS-DFS-PM-DIS        3251975        /ISDFPS/TCUPS_APPLY_SUBLEV - ASSERTION_FAILED
  • IS-DFS-PM-DIS        3119907        Distribution of confirmations incomplete or incorrect

Prerequisites:

  • Usage of EA-DFPS in ECC
  • Usage of the distribution functionality
  • See Symptoms
  • Example:
  1. Day 1: IW31 - Decentral: Create and Release Order with one operation.
  2. Day 1: IW41 - Decentral: Create tree time confirmations for this operation
  3. Day 1: Decentral: Nightly scheduled redistribution takes place to central system
  4. Day 2: Central: In central system the confirmation counter AFRU-RMZHL of all confirmations is in order (conf. one AFRU-RMZHL = 1, conf. two AFRU-RMZHL = 2, conf. three AFRU-RMZHL = 3)
  5. Day 2: Decentral: IW32 - Change order in decentral system in a way that AEDAT of order is updated (e.g. description)
  6. Day 2: Decentral: IW45 – Cancel the confirmation number 3. A new AFRU record is created with AFRU-RMZHL = 4 to document the cancellation of confirmation number 3. 
  7. Day 2: Decentral: Nightly scheduled redistribution takes place to central system 
  8. Day 3: Central: In central system the confirmation counter AFRU-RMZHL of the first three confirmations is in order (conf. one AFRU-RMZHL = 1, conf. two AFRU-RMZHL = 2 , conf. three AFRU-RMZHL = 3), the confirmation record to document the cancellation has got the wrong confirmation counter AFRU-RMZHL = 5!

Solution

Correction via this SAP Note or the corresponding support package.

Important:

  • Before you implement this SAP Note, make sure, that the following SAP Notes are available in your system:
    • IS-DFS-PM-DIS        3636922        Redistribution: Confirmation not redistributed (2)
    • IS-DFS-PM-DIS        3518606        Redistribution: Confirmation not redistributed

    • IS-DFS-PM-DIS        3119907        Distribution of confirmations incomplete or incorrect
    • IS-DFS-PM-DIS        2435271        Deployed Server: Distribution of customer tables via IDOC (Part 2)
  • All these SAP Notes and this SAP Note must be available in all of your systems - central and decentral - as inbound and outbound processes are effected!
