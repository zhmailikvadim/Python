Symptom

1.Create PPS Purchase Order.

2.Maintain any attribute such as Purchasing Organization.

3.Click on Create Business Workspace.

4.Open Business Workspace properties from Business Workspace facet.

You will find Purchasing Organization attribute in Business Workspace is not set with the value maintained in the purchasing organization attribute of PO.

Similar symptom will be observed for any purchase order attribute which is present in Business Workspace.

Symptom is also similar for some other PPS applications which also supports Business Workspace integration like Purchase Contract & Purchase Requisition.

Other Terms

PPS, Business Workspace, Open Text, PO, PC, PR, Attachment

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error.

Business switch 'PPS_NG_OPEN_TEXT' is active.

Business Workspace has been configured to be created manually.

In case of automatic configuration, Step 3 of Symptom is not applicable.

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction.
