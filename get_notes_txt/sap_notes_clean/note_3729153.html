Symptom

I open an invoice, and I see it remains in Reconciling status and does not move forward.

Resolution

  1. Click Manage > Invoice Reconciliation.

  2. Search for the related Invoice Reconciliation (IR).

  3. Open the IR and go to the Exceptions tab.

  4. Identify quantity mismatch exceptions (QuantityMismatchException).

  5. Click the exception and review the Receipt Quantity and Invoice Quantity fields.

  6. Update the quantity or Accept the exception based on business rules.

  7. Click Submit to process the IR and monitor the invoice status.

*Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

  • Sales And Distribution (SD)
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open a SD sales document in VA*1/VA*2/VA*3
  2. Go to header or item tab Partners
  3. Double click on a partner number to open Address dialog

Resolution

Unloading Point is relevant to Ship-to only. It is designed to hide this field if it is not Ship-to party. There is no useexit or BADI provided to change this logic.

See Also

  • Unloading point for Ship-to party can be changed from the Address dialog. Alternatively, it can be changed from tab 'Shipping'.
  • 3516993 - Item level ship-to party is created due to unloading point change at ltem level
  • Technically, screen fields attributes in addresss, its logic is in FM SD_PARTNER_ADDRESS_DIALOG. There is no userexit inside this FM. Thus in order to change the standard logic, modification has to be implemented.
