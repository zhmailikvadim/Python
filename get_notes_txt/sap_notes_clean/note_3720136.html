Symptom

    Business Partner email address updated in Master Data Management (MDM) or at 'Users' in 'Admin', but at 'My Connection' under 'Crowd Workforce' still shows the old address.

    Environment

    SAP Field Service Management

    Reproducing the Issue

    1. Log in 'Admin' or 'Shell' > 'Master Data Management'
    2. Navigate to 'Users' or 'Business Partners'
    3. Update a Business Partners email address
    4. Log in now to 'Shell' > 'Crowd Workforce'
    5. Enter 'My Connection'
    6. Open the updated Partner
      Actual Behavior
    7. Business Partner Email address is not updated
      Expected Behavior
    8. The updated email address should be shown in the Partner Detail Screen

    Cause

    'Crowd Workforce' reads the email address from the Unified Person DTO, and not the Person DTO where the update was made at Master Data Management (MDM) and at 'Users' in 'Admin', so the change is not reflected.

    Resolution

    • At 'People' under 'MDM' update the email address, which is linked to the Unified Person DTO.
    • Only this update will be reflected to Crowd Workforce.

    See Also

    • SAP Help Portal official documentation about 'Person / UnifiedPerson'.
    • SAP Help Portal official documentation about 'People Screen'.
