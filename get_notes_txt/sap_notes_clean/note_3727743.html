Symptom

The detailed log from report RFKKINV_DELETE_SIM_BILLDOCS doesn't contain information about the documents  and the numbers of dependent objects being deleted.

Other Terms

FKKINV519, FKKINV 519, FKKINV520, FKKINV 520

Reason and Prerequisites

The missing information is not passed in the report.

Solution

Implement this SAP Note or the corresponding Support Package.
