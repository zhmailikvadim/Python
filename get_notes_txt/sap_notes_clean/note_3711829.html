Symptom

"Deleted" action for allowance is not displayed in Time Recording Change Document

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Time Tracking

Resolution

The enhancement for Time Recording Change Document to view Delete actions of allowances is planned with 2H2026 release.

For release timelines, please review SAP SuccessFactors Product Release & Road Map Information.
