Symptom

After running an availability check on a sales order item, some of the schedule lines do not have the delivery block anymore.

Other Terms

 tvak-delcf, Commitment dates switch for calculation rule

Reason and Prerequisites

Issue only happens when:

  1. Commitment dates switch for calculation rule is set and
  2. The delivery block is set for a main item of a sales Bill of Material with correlated delivery dates. 

Solution

Implement the correction instruction.
