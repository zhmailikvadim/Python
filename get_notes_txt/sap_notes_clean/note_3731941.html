Symptom

In the North Dakota Tax Oil Report, you noticed 40F product records are not pulling into ND Oil Tax through the process step.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition 2023

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to /PRA/RP_WORKPLACE transaction.
  2. Execute it.
  3. Go to Report details and PRA details tab. 

Resolution

Remitter and Reporter should be set as purchaser for every DOI accounting created for Major product code 4. Then only records will be processed in booking loader while VL document posting. It this referenced in version 28 & 29 of SAP note #3105864 as a legal reporting requirement for North Dakota (ND) state.

Keywords

40F records, /PRA/RP_WORKPLACE, ND LG, North Dakota.
