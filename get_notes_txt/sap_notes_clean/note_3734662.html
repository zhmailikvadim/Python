Symptom

  • Margin health indicators colors are not in the order of colors in design and appearance tab. The expected sequence is lowest margin = red on the left, medium = orange in the middle, highest = green on the right, but this alignment is not observed in margin health boundaries in setup.

  • In the Quote Configuration, the colors display correctly (red on the left, orange in the middle, green on the right).

  • This issue is present on both quote engines.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental"

Environment

SAP CPQ

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log into CPQ Setup.
  2. Go to the Pricing/Calculations.
  3. Click on Margin Health.
  4. Under tab Indicators, click Add New.
  5. Under Margin Health Boundaries see that the order of colors is not the same as in tab Design and Appearance.
  6. The order of colors are orange - red- green. It should be red - orange - green.

Cause

Bug in application.

Resolution

  • This bug will be tentatively fixed in CPQ-2608 release.

  • There is no workaround.
