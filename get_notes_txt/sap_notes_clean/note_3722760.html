Symptom

The page Set Up SmartRecruiters Field Mapping And Data Sync is not showing any details.

Environment

SmartRecruiters for SAP SuccessFactors

Cause

There is an ongoing issue with Role-Based Permissions on this page. The page may be visible to Users even if they do not have the required permissions. However, when they try to access it, no details will be displayed.

Resolution

Product Engineering is investigating a solution.
