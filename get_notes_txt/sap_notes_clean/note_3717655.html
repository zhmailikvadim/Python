Symptom

When generating a monthly license report in Compliance for a selected date range, the report includes data for every year within the month/day range instead of the specific month/day/year range selected.  

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
  • APM Compliance

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the Compliance environment.
  2. Navigate to the "Alerts" tab on the left side.
  3. Go into "Reports".
  4. Click on the plus icon.
  5. Select the date range.
  6. Click on "Generate".

Cause

Application bug.

Resolution

  1. The Engineering team is conducting further investigation to address this matter.
  2. The target to have the fix implemented is the 2604 release. ]
  3. To validate further details regarding the 2026 release schedule, please refer to: SAP SPM 2026 Release Schedule. 
  4. Please note that these dates are subject to change at the discretion of our Engineering team.
