Symptom

Customers have installed and enabled the Roadmap version of Revenue Distribution are experiencing the following issues in 'Chain of title' in reversals:

For the below ownership transfers:

1. Consolidation of funds with owners having low disbusement decimal
2. Condolidated funds transferred to affiliate owner
3. Ownership transfer from the same affiliate owner to another affiliate owner

Valuation fails with Out-of-Balance error in reversals while creating the inter-company payable/receivable account entries.

Other Terms

General keywords: Add-on, IS-Oil, Industry Solution, PRA, Production and Revenue Accounting, IS-OIL-PRA, Oil and Gas Upstream, Roadmap

Problem-specific keywords: IS-PRA-XT, Valuation, Revenue Distribution, OOB, Out of Balance, COT, Chain-of-Title, Affiliate Owner, Income, Inter-company Payable, Inter-company Receivable

Reason and Prerequisites

For the above transfers, system generates multiple income account entries for same affiliate owner. Due to this, system incorrectly calculates inter-company payable/receivable amounts leading to OOB error in reversals.

Program error.

Solution

Implement this note to resolve the issue.
