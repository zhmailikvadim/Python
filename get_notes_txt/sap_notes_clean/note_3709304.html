Symptom

As from January 1, 2026 the legislation significantly strengthens the return-to-work policy. In that context, this SAP note includes change for the Social Risk Declaration - Scenario ZIMA001 (AL01).

With this SAP Note the determination of the begin and end dates of the illness is modified to 56 days (valid for declaration working on 2026 onwards).

It is important to mention that following SAP Notes are in preparation to cover globally this functional change :

  • 3703513 - RPTGENB0 - Strengthened return-to-work policy January 1, 2026
  • 3703515 - DRS/ASR/SRD - Strengthened return-to-work policy January 1, 2026

Other Terms

AL01, SRD, RPCSRDB0, ASR, DRS, MSR, DMFA
