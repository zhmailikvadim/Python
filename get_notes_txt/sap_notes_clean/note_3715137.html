Symptom

In Timesheet Analytics - My Compliance, On resetting all filters, the table kept loading continuously and no data was displayed..

Other Terms

F8342, Timesheet Analytics, My Compliance, RESTRICT_DATA_BY_YEAR_RANGE, HRFIO_B_TIMESHEET_COMPL_EMPL, Table Load

Reason and Prerequisites

Year range defined in method RESTRICT_DATA_BY_YEAR_RANGE of BAdI HRFIO_B_TIMESHEET_COMPL_EMPL was not correctly applied

Solution

Apply corresponding support pack. Alternatively, implement the correction instructions attached to the SAP Note.
