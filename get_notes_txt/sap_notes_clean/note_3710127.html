Symptom

A JVA allocation ends with Production Month Derivation active ends with the T811D-DPOS OVERFLOW dump.

Other Terms

Allocations, JVA, T811D-DPOS OVERFLOW, SORT, PRODPER, Production Month, GJG5, GJF5

Reason and Prerequisites

By the booking of the JVA allocation result, there is a new document created whenever the Production Month differs. In case the line items aren't sorted by the Production Month, there may be a huge number of documents created. That can eventually lead to the overflow dump.

Solution

Apply the Correction Instructions.
