Symptom

Why is ServiceEntrySheet (SES) getting failed due to the Error: Cannot invoke &quot;ariba.common.core.Supplier.getCommonSupplier()&quot; because the return value of &quot;ariba.servicesheet.core.ServiceSheet.getSupplier()&quot; is null. 

Why is the SES getting failed due to the DOC-54: Received cXML response indication a permanent error?

Cause

<Status text="Not Acceptable" code="406">
Error: Cannot invoke &quot;ariba.common.core.Supplier.getCommonSupplier()&quot; because the return value of &quot;ariba.servicesheet.core.ServiceSheet.getSupplier()&quot; is null. Please contact support with the Error Reference Number: xxxxxxxxx for more details.
</Status>

Resolution

The SES created for the specific supplier may either be inactive or not available in Ariba Buying and Invoicing (B&I).

Additionally, this error can also occur if the supplier is in Active status but no information or values are maintained under Partitioned Suppliers.

Keywords

Cannot invoke, getCommonSupplier, getSupplier, DOC-54, 406, Error.
