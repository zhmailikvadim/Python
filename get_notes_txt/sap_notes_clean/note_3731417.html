Symptom

The Goods and Services Tax (GST) system in India has introduced a new valuation method for specific tobacco and tobacco-related products, as mandated by Notification Nos. 19/2025-Central Tax and 20/2025-Central Tax.

Other Terms

Digital Compliance Service, DCS, RSP, Retail Sale Price, Tobacco

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason

RSP-based valuation of notified tobacco goods under GST.

Prerequisite:

You have implemented the SAP Note  3696938 - India DCS: Retail Sale Price based Tax Calculation for tobacco and tobacco-related products (DDIC).

Solution

This SAP note delivers the necessary mapping class changes to facilitate Retail Sale Price (RSP) based valuation for tobacco and tobacco-related products.
