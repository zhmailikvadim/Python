Symptom

  • WTPA forms are currently applicable only to New York and California states in US.
  • Inquiry regarding the feasibility of extending the WTPA forms feature to other states in US.
  • Request for a detailed explanation about the restriction of this feature to New York and California states for communication during a client call.

Environment

SuccessFactors Onboarding

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Verify the availability of WTPA forms in US.
  2. Attempt to enable WTPA forms for states outside New York and California in US.

Cause

The feature is designed to be applicable only to New York and California states in US. No further details regarding the restriction are provided in the case information.

Resolution

  1. Confirm that WTPA forms are currently applicable only to New York and California states in US.
  2. Inform the user that it is not feasible to extend this feature to other states in US.
  3. Provide the user with the relevant KBA link for further reference:
