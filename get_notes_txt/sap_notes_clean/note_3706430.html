Symptom

  • Invoices created by the supplier in SAP Business Network are getting rejected after creation.
  • Rejection reason shown in in Invoice history of SAP Business Network as Document Manually Rejected/Deleted.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create an invoice in the SAP Business Network.
  2. The Invoice is created in Buyer's SAP ERP or SAP S4HANA.
  3. The Invoice status is set to Acknowledged in SAP Business Network
  4. Observe that the Invoice is getting rejected after this status.
  5. Check the history tab in SAP Business Network, it displays Document Manually Rejected/Deleted.

Cause

  • When a user manually delete Parked Invoice from SAP ERP or SAP S4 HANA, The status of the Invoice in RBKP table will get change to 2 - Deleted
  • The program ARBCIG_INVOICESTATUS_EXPORT exports this status to SAP Ariba Business Network via the StatusUpdateRequest transaction 
  • This status will display as Document Manually Rejected/Deleted in SAP Ariba Business Network 

Resolution

  1. Verify with the user who edited the invoice status in the RBKP table to understand the reason for the deletion.
  2. If further investigation is required, connect with your internal SAP FI consulting team or Implementation partner for a deeper analysis.
