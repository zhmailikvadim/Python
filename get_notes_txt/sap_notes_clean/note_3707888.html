Symptom

In FORA yearly reporting for White collars (RPLAMFS0), the limit age for Employees who is covered by agreements "TRR Trygghetsrådet" and "Trygghetsrådet TRS" has been raised from 65 to 66 years for the income data reported within the year 2025. From the year 2026, the limit age will be raised to 67 years.

Other Terms

RPLAMFS0, age limit, T511K, AFORH

Solution

In the final reporting of salaried Employees for the year 2025, their salaries must be included up to the month before they turn 66 years.

- Just to recognize such cases, two new rows were added in ALV output of FORA report to display the income of all salaried Employees up to 66 years of age.

- Relevant texts of particular ALV rows has been changed in a way to keep and distinguish also original amount for Employees up to 65 years of age. This is still visible in the first position of the ALV output.
