Symptom

  • How to enroll in IT3415 maternity leave (lasting 8 weeks, with a minimum of 7 days), which is available to women following a stillbirth?
  • Required information such as the child's first name, last name, gender, and PESEL number is unavailable, as these details are necessary to create a new record.

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise 4.7
  • SAP ERP Core Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA

Cause

The IT3415 system requires mandatory child data (first name, surname, gender, PESEL) for record creation, which is unavailable in cases of stillbirth.

Resolution

  1. In the case of stillbirth, only one absence record is created, and it is either impossible or not required to make the assignment to a child. Additionally, there is no need to show such an absence in the employment certificate, and there is no absence quota to manage.
  2. Therefore, the infotype 3415 should not be used for recording a maternity leave after a stillbirth.
  3. In the case of stillbirth, we recommend that the maternity leave absence should be directly recorded in the infotype 2001, and the period of 36-month employer's exemption from FP+FGSP should be recorded in the infotype 0014, with a special wage type like (copied from) MZ04 (value 'G' in the processing class 29).

See Also

The purposes of the infotype 3415 are:

- to record assignments of parental absence periods to the employee's children,

- to help manage the quotas for these absences per child or children born together,

- to help show only relevant parental absence in the employment certificate.
