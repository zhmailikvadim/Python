Symptom

Error: The unit price currency on invoice line [x] does not match the unit price currency on order line [x] for purchase order [Purchase Order #].

Reproducing the Issue

Send EDI invoice to Ariba Network.

Cause

You did not include the optional currency (CUR) segment at the header level of the invoice, which means that the Summary section of the invoice (Tax Summary and Invoice Summary) uses the default currency configured in your admin account.

Resolution

You can choose one of two methods:

  1. Include the optional currency (CUR) segment at the header level of the EDI invoice.
    For example: CUR*ZZ*USD~
  2. Update the default currency in the admin account to your desired currency and then attempt to send a new EDI invoice to the Ariba Network.
    For updating the default currency refer to How do I change my default currency?
