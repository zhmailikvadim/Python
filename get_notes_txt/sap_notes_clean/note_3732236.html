Symptom

Unable to process additional movement types during subcontracting challan reconciliation.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

As per standard design reconciliation includes 543 O, 542 O and 544 O movement types.

Resolution

To add additional movement types in subcontracting challan reconciliation, implement BAdI "J_1IG_SUBCON" in the method "ADD_MVMT_RECON" to include additional movement types during subcontracting challan reconciliation.
