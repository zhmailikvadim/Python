Symptom

In this KBA Note only general customizing of field OPERA for Purchase / Sales ledgers (new) is described.

Environment

  • SAP ERP 6.0 and higher
  • SAP_APPL 600 and higher
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP_FIN 617 and higher
  • S4Core 100 and higher

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Run transaction FIAPRU_PURCHASE_LEDG or FIARRU_SALES_LEDGER
  2. Fill selection screen and execute
  3. Get unexpected results

Cause

Incorrect or absent customizing

Resolution

Assign Tax Items to VAT Base Columns

In this Customizing activity and in the Assign Tax Items to VAT Columns Customizing activity, you specify which VAT items are included in the Sales Ledger and Purchase Ledger used in Russia.

In this Customizing activity, you specify which tax items are to be included in which VAT base column.

1. Execute the Customizing activity, and, in the initial dialog box, set the country to RU and the tax grouping version to SAL (sales ledger) or PUR (purchase ledger).

2. Enter the tax codes to be covered by the ledger and fill out the Trs (transaction key) and Bal. (balance) fields.

3. In the TxBs (tax base) column, specify which of the following columns in the output list the amount is to be shown in:

Configuring tax codes with 5% and 7% rates requires the implementation of SAP Note 3560322.

4. You can use the ArithmOper (arithmetical operation) field for the following purposes:

Entry

Use

+

Displays the gross amount (tax base + tax) in the tax base field

-

Leaves the tax base amount blank, and overwrites the partner's name, INN, and KPP number with those of the company code. This option is relevant for down payments.

P

Leaves the tax base amount blank, but does not overwrite the partner's name, INN, and KPP number with those of the company code. This option is relevant for down payments.

G

Displays the tax base amount in the Payt Amt Incl. VAT field instead of the sum of tax base and tax amount (done by default).

B

Retrieves the tax base amount from tax transfer documents (ignoring data from table DEFTAX_ITEM)

T

Displays the tax base amount in the Payt Amt Incl. VAT field instead of the sum of tax base and tax amount (done by default). Use this option for payments to customs or tax offices.

N

Displays the tax, tax base and payment amount and changes their sign to the opposite. Use this option for payments to the budget (for example, payments for rent).

V

Displays the company code's name, INN and KPP in the partner's fields. This option is relevant for tax agent operations.

F

Displays the full amount of the invoice (from the customer item of the invoice) in the Invoice Sales Amount Including VAT field of the output XML or PDF file instead of the sum of tax base and tax amount (done by default). This arithmetic operation is only relevant for the Sales Ledger.

I

Displays the full amount of the invoice (from the supplier item of the invoice) in the Invoice Purchase Amount Including VAT field of the output XML or PDF file instead of the sum of tax base and tax amount (done by default). This arithmetic operation is only relevant for the Purchase Ledger.

J

Displays the full amount of the down payment (from the supplier item of the down payment) in the Invoice Purchase Amount Including VAT field of the output XML or PDF file instead of the sum of tax base and tax amount (done by default). This arithmetic operation is only relevant for down payments in the Purchase Ledger. Also displays the tax base amount as zero, and overwrites the partner's name, INN, and KPP number with those of the company code.

Q

Displays the full amount of the down payment (from the customer item of the down payment) in the Invoice Sales Amount Including VAT field of the output XML or PDF file instead of the sum of tax base and tax amount (done by default). This arithmetic operation is only relevant for down payments in the Sales Ledger. Also displays the tax base amount as zero, and overwrites the partner's name, INN, and KPP number with those of the company code.

Assign Tax Items to VAT Columns

In this IMG activity, you specify which tax items are to be included in which VAT column in the Sales Ledger and Purchase Ledger used in Russia.

For more information about this activity and an example, see Assign Tax Items to VAT Base Columns.
