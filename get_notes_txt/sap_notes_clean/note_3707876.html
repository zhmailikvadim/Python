Symptom

  • Partner Admins are unable to see equipment on assigned activities in the Partner Portal.
  • Equipment is visible in the mobile app but not in the Partner Portal.
  • Adjustments to the Partner Portal Modal UI have been made, but equipment visibility remains absent.

Environment

SAP Field Service Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in to the Partner Portal as a Partner Admin.
  2. Navigate to assigned activities.
  3. Observe that equipment is not visible in the Partner Portal, despite being visible in the mobile app.

Cause

Equipment in screen configuration for partner portal was set to visible = 'false'

Resolution

  1. Navigate to admin > screen configuration settings (using the pencil button).
  2. Locate the field for "Equipment Name"
  3. Change the "Visibility" setting for these fields to "Visible".
