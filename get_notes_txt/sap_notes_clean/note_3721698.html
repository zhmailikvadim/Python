Symptom

In the process "Sending of Deregistration Due to End of Supply for Electricity (DSO View)" (DE_B_GPKE_DEREG_DSO), the deregistration process gets stuck after step 8900 and doesn't move to step 8950 in shutdown scenarios.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

GPKE, DE_B_GPKE_DEREG_DSO, deregistration, shutdown, end of supply, electricity

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because the system was missing preconditions required for the deregistration process to complete in shutdown scenarios

Solution

 Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system completes the deregistration process in shutdown scenarios by activating the BC set /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP08_NOTE_3721698. You can verify the fix by checking that the deregistration process completes successfully in shutdown scenarios.
