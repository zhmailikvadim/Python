Symptom

How do I use the Supplier Profile Questionnaire (SPQ) in Supplier Lifecycle and Performance (SLP)?

Resolution

SPQ option is not available in Supplier Lifecycle & Performance.

To collect supplier information, you can use Supplier Registration Project, Qualification Projects and Modular Questionnaires.
