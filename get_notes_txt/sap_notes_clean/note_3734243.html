Symptom

During the costing run, the system is not selecting the costing sheet, even though it is assigned to the valuation variant.

Environment

  • Financial Accounting (FI)
  • Controlling (CO)
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

1. Execute the T-Code: CK11N with the required parameters
2. Navigate to Valuation tab
3. The field "Costing Sheet" is empty

Cause

  • The Valuation Variant in the Costing Variant has plant-specific settings maintained for the Plant used in Costing. The plant-specific settings have a higher priority than the valuation variant's general settings. So the system uses the plant-specific settings instead of valuation variant's general settings.
  • To access the plant-specific settings, Execute the OKKN transaction -> Select the Costing Variant used for costing -> Select the Valuation Variant -> Click 'Valuation Variant/Plant' -> double click on the entry for plant used for costing -> then go to the 'Overhead' tab for costing sheet selection settings.
  • There is no costing sheet maintained in the plant-specific settings of valuation variant for the plant under the 'Overhead' tab. As a result, the costing sheet is not selected during the cost estimate creation in CK11N.

Resolution

Maintain the costing sheet in the plant-specific settings of valuation variant for the plant used for costing and run the costing again.

Keywords
