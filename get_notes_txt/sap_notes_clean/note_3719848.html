Symptom

Error VL295 is shown in COGI when using synchronous goods movement.

Dlv. type (dlv. catgry &1 for mat. &2 and BWART &3) cannot be determined

Message No. VL295

Environment

  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management (SCM-EWM);
  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management Interfaces (SCM-EWM-IF);
  • SAP ERP Production Planning (PP)
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA, on-premise

Cause

Synchronous goods movement is not activated properly.

Resolution

In standard, error VL295 will only be trigged when creating deliveries, if the business scenario is to use synchronous goods movement, check the customizing of the synchronous goods movement according to Integration of Extended Warehouse Management into PP With Synchronous Goods Movements | SAP Help Portal 

See Also

Integration of Extended Warehouse Management into PP With Synchronous Goods Movements | SAP Help Portal

Keywords

Synchronous goods movement, VL295, COGI, EWM, production order
