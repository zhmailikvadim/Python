Symptom

  • Negative unrestricted stock is visible for a material across multiple plants and storage locations.
  • The negative quantity has reached a significant level, impacting inventory accuracy, financial valuation, and operational planning.
  • No stock is available in quality inspection or blocked stock to justify the negative balance.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Logon to the system.
  2. Open the App: Stock - Multiple Materials.
  3. Enter the Material ID, Plants / Prod. Unit ID and the required Reporting Date.
  4. Execute.
  5. Observe that the unrestricted stock is showing negative quantity.

Cause

Backdated goods movement with goods issue exceeding receipts. Goods Issue is created today with Posting Date in the past.

With the 'Stock for Posting Date' report, the Stock on the Posting Date and the Actual Execution Date can be compared.

Resolution

  1. Ensure that receipts and issues are aligned on the selected posting date.
  2. Avoid backdating issues without corresponding backdated receipts. If backdating is necessary, ensure both receipts and issues align with the actual execution date and time.
