Symptom

Error message: "Bus Function. Multiple Business Partner Addresses active in feeder system" appears during BP transfer to SAP GTS after enabling the multiple address function.

Environment

  • SAP Global Trade Services 11.0
  • SAP Global Trade Services Edition for Hana

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access transaction code, for example /SAPSLL/DEBMAS_DIRR3.
  2. Enter specific customer details.
  3. Execute the transaction.
  4. Observe that out of 1 partner, 0 is processed successfully, and error message /SAPSLL/CORE_PARTNER148 is displayed.

Cause

Resolution

To replicate BP to SAP GTS after enabling the Multiple Address Function, it is recommended reviewing SAP Note 3167941. This note provides detailed guidance on the necessary steps to address this scenario effectively. 

See Also

  • Refer to: SAP Note 3167941 - Business Partner replication from SAP S/4HANA to SAP GTS after enabling multiple address function.
  • Refer to: SAP Note 3019669 - Business Partner replication from SAP S/4HANA to SAP GTS using BP SOAP service.
