Symptom

When changing capacity data in the Mass Maintenance of Work Center Capacities app (F5381), values from work center header  are cleared.
The issue occurs after applying changes such as utilization updates; the work center disappears from the app, and CRHD no longer contains the relevant data, related tables (e.g. CRTX, CRCA) still hold data.

Other Terms

F5381, CRHD, UTILIZATION, CAPACITY, MASS MAINTANANCE, HEADER, CL_PPH_BIL_WORKCENTER_API, PROCESS_WORKCENTER_BUFFER

Reason and Prerequisites

 The reason is incorrect functionality. During processing of work center capacity changes, the logic read/update flow for the work center header used inconsistent sources.
