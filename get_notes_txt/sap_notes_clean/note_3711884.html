Symptom

In ME52N tcode when the field "fixed ID" in tab "Quantities/Dates" in purchase requistion is set/cleared, error  "Configuration for instance 'XXXX' was not found, msg no - CUIB1100" is raised. This is noticed in APO scenarios.

Other Terms

APO, PPDS, /SAPMP/MM_CIP_CONFIGURATION_2, ME_CIP_ALLOW_CHANGE, IF_EX_ME_CIP_ALLOW_CHANGE~GET_DISPLAY, Purchase requisition

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

On change of configuration on APO side, the new CUOBJ is not saved to the configuration related DB tables, ending up in error.

Solution

Kindly implement the correction instructions.
