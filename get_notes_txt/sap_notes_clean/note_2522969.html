Symptom

The mandatory field "Volume" of the funding program is used for a limit check during the creation and fixing of new transactions. In practice, this program volume is often not required and is avoided with a high maximum value. Despite this, the limit check is executed and impairs the performance. This correction allows you to leave the field empty for an unlimited funding program, to ensure that the check is not performed.

Other Terms

Funding program

Reason and Prerequisites

Performance optimization

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified Support Package.
