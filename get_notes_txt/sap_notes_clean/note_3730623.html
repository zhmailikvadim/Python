Symptom

  • When attempting to create a renegotiation in the Manage Renegotiations app, the system displays the error message: "Operation is not enabled".
  • Price renegotiation cannot be started from the app.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Manage Renegotiations app.
  2. Select Create.
  3. Observe the error "Operation is not enabled" and that creation is blocked.

Cause

To enable 'Manage Renegotiations', you must activate Central Procurement as a Hub or have a Product Sourcing license.

Resolution

  1. Activate Central Procurement as the hub scenario in the system.
  2. Alternatively, ensure the Product Sourcing license/entitlement is active in the system.
  3. After completing one of the above, reopen the Manage Renegotiations app and try creating a renegotiation again.
