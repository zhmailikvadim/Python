Symptom

In case of AMQP adapter, despite the messages are retried, the error encountered "org.springframework.jms.UncategorizedJmsException:Uncategorized exception occurred during JMS processing; nested exception is org.apache.qpid.jms.JmsSendTimedOutException:Timed out waiting for disposition of sent Message, cause: org.apache.qpid.jms.provider.exceptions.ProviderSendTimedOutException:Timed out waiting for disposition of sent Message" messages are delivered to the AMQP queue, due to which duplicate messages are created at the consumer side due to the absence of duplicate check.

Environment

  • SAP Integration Suite
  • SAP Cloud Integration

Reproducing the Issue

  • Configure an outbound interface in SAP Integration Suite with an AMQP adapter at the receiver side.
  • Ensure the interface is running in production.
  • Observe the behavior when a timeout error occurs in the AMQP adapter: messages enter retry mode every 5 minutes but are still delivered to the AMQP queue, resulting in duplicates at the consumer side.

Cause

The error indicates that the disposition frame is not received by CPI after the transfer frame is sent. Consequently, the error log on the broker may indicate a CPI closing attempt after the timeout has occurred.

Resolution

It appears that the disposition frame is not being sent back by the broker. It is possible that AMQP is not being handled properly by the broker. A configuration problem on the broker side is suspected.

Hence, it is recommended that the following information be checked at the broker end:

  • Are messages from CPI being received by the broker?
  • If yes, how are those messages being processed or responded at broker side?
