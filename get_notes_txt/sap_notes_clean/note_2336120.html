Symptom

When maintaining selection criteria in the forwading orders relevant for internal settlement query, that would lead to no result, instead of no hits random freight documents show up in the POWL result list.

In addition documents with invoicing status completed are filtered out from the POWL, although it is not excluded in the selection criteria.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified Support Package.

Afterwards the documents with invoicing status completed are not anymore filtered out from the POWL, i.e. the appropriate invoicing status can now be filtered out manually by setting/excluding the respective invoicing status.

In order to achieve the same result as before the invoicing status completed has to be excluded for the selection.
