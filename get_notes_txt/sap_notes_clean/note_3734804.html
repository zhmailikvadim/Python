Symptom

How do I add an attachment to my service entry sheet?

Resolution

To attach a document when creating a service entry sheet:

  1. Click Add to Header > Attachment.
  2. In the added Attachments section, click Choose file and select the document.
  3. Click Add Attachment.

Remember that the total size of all attachments cannot exceed 100 MB.

See Also

The following are additional considerations related to invoice attachments:

  • Not every customer accepts invoice attachments. View your customer's transaction rules to see if attachments are permitted.
  • Your customer might also limit the type of files you can attach to service entry sheets.
  • To delete a document that you added to an service entry sheet, select the box next to the document, and click Delete.
  • After you submit a service entry sheet, you can edit the attachments only for service entry sheets in Canceled, Failed, or Rejected statuses. For service entry sheets in other statuses, work with your customer to decide how to send the attachments.
  • If you are still unable to attach your document, verify that you are using a certified browser and go through the common browser troubleshooting suggestions.
  • Please note: the file name should not include special characters. The only special characters allowed are period (.), hyphen (-), and underscore (_). Please refer to the document Attachment File Names 
  • In case that you are unable to upload the attachment and you are getting an error related to the file size, you can compress the file and try again.
