Symptom

When you run payroll archiving, during the HR preparation step, the system displays the error message "Inconsistency between new clusters CU and CA (program error)".

Other Terms

PU22, PA_CALC, PCL2, CU, CA

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason: The system calculates the archive date incorrectly when the last two payroll results in the archive group are voided results.

Prerequisites: This issue occurs when you archive payroll results and the last two results in the archive group are voided.

Solution

After implementing this SAP Note, the system excludes voided results when calculating the archive date for the archive group.
