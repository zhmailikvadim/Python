Symptom

The error VU019  "Missing data: Net Value" is triggered when attempting to save a sales order in a future delivery scenario using pricing procedure RVBRCE.

*Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

SAP ERP
SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

User creates a Sales Order for the Future Delivery scenario in Brazil localization.

Cause

This error happens because the standard pricing procedure RVBRCE has the PR00 condition as statistic and does not include the ICMI (Net Price + Tax) condition.
As a result, the net price is determined as 0.

Resolution

By standard, the Brazil sales for Brazil delivered by SAP works based on a Net price definition and it is delivered via standard pricing procedure RVABRA.
The standard net price condition (PR00) is the first condition of the pricing.
The final net price value (PR00 + surcharges + discounts) will be used in the ICMI condition, which is calculated through the standard formula 320 in RVABRA.
In case your specific business need is for a different approach when working in Brazilian sales orders, so requires a custom development since it is out of the standard SAP delivered solution.
