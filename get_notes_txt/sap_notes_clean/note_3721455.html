Symptom

Not clear how to maintain geocoordinates provided to you by geolocation provider in degree with decimals format into degree with minutes and seconds format.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Latitude and longitude values are provided by a geolocation provider (for example according to SAP Note 1402070 - Geocoding-Survival guide) in degrees with decimal values format.
  2. In the SAP system, tables accept degree values without decimals, while having separate fields for degrees, minutes, and seconds.
  3. It is unclear how to convert the decimal values into minutes and seconds. 

Cause

  • SAP does not officially provide predefined geolocation data for countries or regions. 
  • Customers are required to define or update geocoordinates based on external sources or ISO standards. 
  • SAP does not officially provide mathematical formulas to convert degrees with decimal values into degrees, minutes, and seconds. 

Resolution

Conversion of degrees with decimal values into degrees, minutes, and seconds format is based on common mathematical/trigonometric formulas. SAP does not officially provide mathematical formulas for this conversion. Use open-source information to learn how to convert GIS latitude and longitude information into degrees, minutes, and seconds format. 

 Possible conversion methods (use at your own risk):

  • Manual conversion:
  1. Degrees: The whole number (integer) part is the degrees
  2. Minutes: Multiply the remaining decimal portion by 60. The integer part of this result is the minutes
  3. Seconds: Multiply the remaining decimal portion of the minutes calculation by 60. This is the seconds, which can be rounded to the required precision.

For example, Latitude 10,1694444444 will correspond based on above calculation method:

10 degree (cutting integer part of 10,1694444444)
10 minutes (multiply remaining decimal part 0,1694444444 by 60 = 10,166666664, cutting the integer part)
10 seconds (multiply remaining decimal part 0,166666664 by 60 = 9,99999984 = can be rounded up to 10)

  • Or find an online converter convenient for you, for example found by google search: https://latlongdata.com/lat-long-converter/
