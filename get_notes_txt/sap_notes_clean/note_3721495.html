Symptom

When creating or changing a sales order with items that are relevant for order related billing and are blocked due to credit check results, entries for predictive accounting are simulated and posted to the predictive ledger.

Other Terms

Predictive Ledger, Order based billing, Credit management, Sales Order Block

Reason and Prerequisites

 Coding error

Solution

Implement the corrections of this note.

Starting with release 2025, a log will be created in case sales orders are not relevant for predictive accounting. You can access this log via transaction SLG1 for object "FINS" and sub-object "FINS_PRED".
