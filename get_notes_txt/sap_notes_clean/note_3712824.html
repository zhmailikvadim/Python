Symptom

How do I change the company profile visibility settings of a child account? How do I hide the profile of my child account? 

Resolution

To update the profile visibility settings in your child account, follow these steps:

  1. In the upper-right corner of your screen, click on your [user initials] > Settings > Connected Accounts.
  2. Locate the child account you want to update.
  3. Select the radio bubble next to the child account and click Edit Profile Settings towards the upper right side.
  4. Next to Profile Discoverability, make sure the toggle shows a white minus sign and not a blue check mark.
  5. Click Save.

Please note that your company profile is by default visible to all buyers unless you change your profile settings.
