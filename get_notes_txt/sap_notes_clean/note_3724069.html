Symptom

When an employee transfers from an MPF scheme to an ORSO scheme and remains employed, the system does not support the required monthly remittance statement submission.

According to eMPF guidelines, when an employee transfers from an MPF scheme to an ORSO scheme while remaining employed under the MPF scheme, monthly remittance statements for that employee is required with the employee's relevant income, employer's mandatory contribution, and employee's mandatory contribution set to "0".

After implementing this SAP Note, the system generates monthly remittance statements for employees who transfer from an MPF scheme to an ORSO scheme while remaining employed. The system sets the employee's relevant income, employer's mandatory contribution, and employee's mandatory contribution to "0" in accordance with eMPF guidelines.

Other Terms

MPF, ORSO, eMPF

Reason and Prerequisites

This is an enhancement.

Solution

Apply the Support Package or implement the corresponding correction instructions.
