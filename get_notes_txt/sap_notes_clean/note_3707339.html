Symptom

You complete an Inbound Delivery from a TCD scenario but the PDO is set to Partially Completed and cannot be Goods Issue posted.

Environment

  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management (SCM-EWM);
  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management Cross Docking (SCM-EWM-CD);
  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management Delivery Processing (SCM-EWM-DLP);
  • SAP S/4HANA;

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Complete the Inbound delivery from the TCD Process which has a Packing Item.
  2. The stock for the non packing items are correctly assigned to the corresponding Outbound Delivery.
  3. The Product which is a Packing Item in the Inbound Delivery is not completed on the Outbound due to open Picking and Loading quantity.

Cause

The ERP item type is mapped to an item type which is not a Packing Item (PAC) in EWM.
Thus, the outbound delivery item is relevant for Picking and Loading.

Resolution

  1. Check the mapping of the item type to ensure it corresponds to a packing item.
  2. Correct the item type mapping to associate it with the relevant item category "PAC".
  3. Update the customizing settings to reflect the corrected mapping.
  4. Retest the case with a new TCD-relevant delivery.

Keywords

PDO, TCD, PAC, cross-docking, Packing Item
