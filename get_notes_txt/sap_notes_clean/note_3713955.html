Symptom

  • Unable to use the AI Tool > "Summarize a Meeting" for a particular MS Teams meeting.
  • The AI Tool fails to retrieve meeting transcripts, which are required for its functionality.

Environment

SAP CX AI Toolkit

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login to the CX AI Toolkit tenant.
  2. Go to the AI Tools section.
  3. Navigate (scroll-down) to the "Summarize a Meeting" tool.
  4. Select the meeting intended for summarization.
  5. Click on "Generate".

Cause

The MS Teams meeting recording and its transcripts had expired which is typically saved in Onedrive, preventing the AI Tool from retrieving necessary data.

Resolution

  1. This is an expected behavior of the product functionality as the AI Tool > "Summarize a Meeting" uses the meeting recording transcripts for functioning.
  2. Select Meetings only for existing meeting recording and transcripts (You can extend the Teams Recording Validity period in MS Teams Admin Center)
