Symptom

Following symptoms are corrected by this note:

  1. You create a sales order or STO item and enter a requirement segment for a stock protected article. There are some PIRs or forecast which have to preserved before promising any stock to current SO/STO requirement. These PIRs consume different set of stock segment as compared to requirement segment of current SO/STO item. In such case SO/STO item can go for under or over confirmation.
    Ex : There are 2 PIRs, EC01 and FH01. EC01 can get stock from ZZ13 and ZZ01 respectively while FH01 can get stock from ZZ04 and ZZ01 respectively.
    Now you are creating a sales order of WH01 which can get stock from ZZ04 and ZZ01 respectively. You notice that this sales order item can get incorrect confirmation.
  2. You create a sales order or STO item for a material having scope 2 strategy, you notice that system is throwing a dump.

Other Terms

Stock Protection Standardization, FSH_STP_MODULE, Vertical Stock Protection, Horizontal Stock Protection, Protection Up-to Plan

Reason and Prerequisites

This is caused due to program error.

Solution

Implement the correction instructions attached with this note.
