Symptom

The Personas Health Check shows new information, related to client timestamp

Environment

SAP Screen Personas 3.0, as of SP08

Reproducing the Issue

Access the Health Check tool and click on the System Status tab.

Resolution

As of SP08, new information is available via System Status tab:

  • webgui / WDA client timestamp
  • Slipstream Engine client timestamp

The picture shows the value for both properties:

 

After updating the Client Note (for SP08, SAP Note 2713261), cleaning the MIMEs and accessing the Personas service again, the information is updated:
