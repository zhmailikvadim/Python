Symptom

This KBA explains the system logic on how to display LHDNM State information in EA form. "Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll
  • SAP S/4 HANA

Cause

Resolution

  1. The system retrieves the Income Tax Office information from the original payroll result TAX table.
  2. Based on the Income Tax Office information, the Tax Office text is fetched from Table: T5LTT.
