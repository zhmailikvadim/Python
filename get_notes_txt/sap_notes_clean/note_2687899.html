Symptom

General Resolution 128/2018 published by the Administración Nacional de Seguridad Social stipulates an increase in the maximum and minimum limits for the base remuneration for Social Insurance withholding destined to SUSS (Sistema Único de Seguridad Social, in Spanish), from September 01, 2018.

The limits for withholdings will change as follows:

  • Minimum Limit for Welfare (Obra Social):  6.008,50 ARS
  • Minimum Limit for SIPA, ART and INSSJyP: 3.004,25 ARS
  • Maximum Limit for SIPA, Welfare (Obra Social), ART and INSSJyP: 97.637,14 ARS

For more information, see also https://www.boletinoficial.gob.ar/#!DetalleNorma/190552/20180829.
