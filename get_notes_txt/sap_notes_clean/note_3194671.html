During goods movement for an Oil material, when GR quantity repricing is done, sometimes you notice a difference of 0.001 in QCI quantity values in MIGO due to rounding issue in S4.

Other Terms

General Keywords: Industry solution, IS-OIL, IS-OIL-DS-TSW, IS-OIL-DS-HPM, IS-OIL-DS-MAP, Rounding, MIGO, additional quantities, Rounding issue, GR repricing

Problem-specific Keywords: OIB2_MAP_ADJUST_QUAN_BY_FACTOR, OIC_ADJUST_QUANTITY, RECEIPT_QTY_REPRICE, MSEGO2, MATDOCOIL, ADQNT, ADQNTP, ADQNTP_LONG, GT_MSEGO2, OIB_MM_UPDATE_M07M_APPENDICES

Reason and Prerequisites

During GR quantity repricing, before pricing, conversion factor is applied and MSEGO2-ADQNT is rounded to 15 decimals and once pricing is done, reciprocal conversion factor is applied and MSEGO2-ADQNT is again rounded to 15 decimals

During this conversion process, final MSEGO2-ADQNT is changed, which inturn effecting MSEGO2-ADQNTP_LONG and causing roundig issues while displaying packed value in Additional quantity tab.

Solution

Implement the correction instructions or Import relevant support package.
