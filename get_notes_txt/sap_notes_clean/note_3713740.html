Symptom

When trying to open an existing A/R invoice by document number, the A/R Invoice cannot be opened with the following error:
No matching records found  'A/R Invoice' (OINV) (ODBC -2028)  [Message 131-183]

Environment

SAP Business One

Reproducing the Issue

Scenario:

  1. Go to Sales - A/R → A/R Invoice + Payment, create an A/R invoice and pay it through the opened Payment Means window.
  2. Go to Sales - A/R → A/R Invoice window and change to Find mode.
  3. In No. field, input the document number of the above A/R Invoice created in step 1.
  4. Click Find button.

Expected behavior:

The A/R Invoice is opened without any error.

Actual behavior:

The A/R Invoice cannot be opened with the follow error:
No matching records found  'A/R Invoice' (OINV) (ODBC -2028)  [Message 131-183]

Resolution

A/R invoices created through A/R Invoice + Payment function can only be accessed through the A/R Invoice + Payment window.  
Navigate to Sales - A/R → A/R Invoice + Payment to find the A/R Invoice.  

Keywords

A/R Invoice, Payment, Find, No., No matching records found, OINV
