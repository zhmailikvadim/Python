Symptom

You are seeking information about the possibility of rebalancing purchased Capacity Units between Datasphere tenants from different subaccounts.

Environment

SAP Datasphere

Resolution

It is not possible to transfer Capacity Units between two different subaccounts.

The Capacity Units are tenant specific and each tenant is bounded to a single subaccount.

Thus, the current SAP Datasphere's architecture and licensing model does not allow capacity units to be directly allocated or transferred between different subaccounts. Each SAP Datasphere tenant is provisioned within a specific subaccount, and the capacity units are bound to that particular tenant and subaccount.
