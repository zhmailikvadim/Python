Symptom

In the process "Shutdown/Dismantling of IMS" (DE_B_IM_DEV_REMOVAL), billing attributes are cleared in step 0250.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

DE_B_IM_DEV_REMOVAL, /US4G/CL_IM_BADI_MCM_OPS_AUTO, /UCOM/IF_BADI_DV_OP_AUTO_DATA~SET_ON_RATE_CHANGE, billing attributes, IM4G, IMS

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because of a program error.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system preserves billing attributes during the shutdown or dismantling of the IMS process.

You need to retest the scenario. You can verify the fix by checking that the shutdown or dismantling of IMS completes successfully without clearing billing attributes.
