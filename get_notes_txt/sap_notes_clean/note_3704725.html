Symptom

  • The "Assign doc" button is not visible in the "Create Purchase Requisitions" app (F1048).
  • The "Assign doc" button is available in other apps such as "Process Purchase Requisitions" and "Manage Purchase Requisitions Professional" apps.

Environment

  • Materials Management (MM)
  • SAP Fiori
  • SAP S/4HANA 1809
  • SAP S/4HANA 1909
  • SAP S/4HANA 2020
  • SAP S/4HANA 2021

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the "Create Purchase Requisitions" app (F1048).
  2. Create a new purchase requisition item.
  3. Navigate to the "Attachments" section in the item details.
  4. Attempt to attach a DMS document link.
  5. Observe that the "Assign Doc" button is not visible.

Cause

The "Create Purchase Requisitions" app (F1643) uses an older freestyle UI framework, which does not support the HDM attachment handling framework required for the "Assign doc" button functionality.

The HDM framework is only activated for the newer "My Purchase Requisitions - New" app (F1643A), which uses the Fiori Elements-based UI.

Resolution

Until S/4HANA 2021, it is recommended to use the "Process Purchase Requisitions" app or the "Manage Purchase Requisitions Professional" app where the "Assign doc" button functionality is available.

Alternatively, upgrade to SAP S/4HANA 2022 or later to use the "My Purchase Requisitions - New" app (F1643A).
