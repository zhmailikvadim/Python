Symptom

  • Changes in income tax rules effective from 1 April 2026 -Employer provided Meal & Voucher increase from Rs 50 to Rs 200 per meal
  • Higher tax-free limit on meal cards
  • Exemption upto Rs 200 per meal is applicable effective from 1-Apr-2026
  • The exemption threshold for free food and non-alcoholic beverages provided by the employer increased from Rs 50 to Rs 200 per meal.

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • Payroll India
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP ERP Core Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

Disclaimer: Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

  1. Maintain Meal or Meal Voucher allowance WT in IT 0008 or IT 0014 or IT 0015 effective from 1-Apr-2026 say example Model WT M300.
  2. Maintain Old Regime in IT 588
  3. Execute Payroll using transaction PC00_M40_CALC
  4. Check for the exemption limit matches the Income tax rules effective from 1-Apr-2026

Resolution

  1. Refer Note 3357444 - HINCALC0: Meal Voucher Taxation as Perquisite, maintain Meal Voucher (Say example Model WT M300) - Used to pay meal voucher amount to an employee via Basic Pay (IT0008)/ Additional payments (IT00015)/ Recurring Payments/Deductions (IT00014). 
  2. Maintain exemption amount in V_T7INT9 table effective from 01.04.2026 (say example 200 RS/Meal X 2 times X 22 days = 8,800 Rs as exemption amount)
  3. As per the note 3357444, Payroll will create 2 WT in Payroll Function "INPAE"
  4. Monthly Meal Perquisite (/3MV) - Stores the monthly meal perquisite calculated based on the meal voucher given to the employee.
    Annual Meal Perquisite (/3MU) - Stores the annual meal perquisite calculated based on the meal voucher given to the employee.
  5. Extra Monthly amount paid to employee comparing the value maintained in V_T7INT9 under the Tax Code SMVP is considered as PERK and updated in WT /3MV. Annual Perk WT /3MU value is updated in WT 127 & WT /413
