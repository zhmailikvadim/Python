Symptom

I'd like to process receiving for a non-release contract, but I can't find any composing receipt in the contract.

Environment

SAP Ariba Buying & Invoicing

Cause

The option Allow receiving against contract?  in contract Definitions section is selected as No, thus receipt is not automatically created for the contract, also Receive button is not displayed in the UI.

Resolution

To fix this, contract Requester should follow the steps:

1. Click Change button on the Contract header
2. Change the option Allow receiving against contract? from No to Yes 
3. Submit the contract request

Keywords

Contract Receiving, contract definitions, change a contract
