Symptom

Missing overhead in material cost estimate.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 

Cause

The costing date is not valid within the validity of 'Credit' line of costing sheet configuration.

Resolution

Kindly consider to expand the validity of credit line for costing sheet or change the costing date of material cost estimate accordingly. 

Keywords

Overhead, costing sheet, credit, valid to, validity period, material cost estimate, CK11N, CK13N, overhead credit.
