Symptom

The B2A text for FPS for the tax year 2026-27 still referred to the old tax year 25-26

Other Terms

FPS, RPCFPSG0

Reason and Prerequisites

The text was not updated to the next tax year

Solution

Please import and apply the table entries in this SAP Note or import the relevant SP.

· Once the SAP Note has been downloaded, please Execute report NOTE_3713946 in "Update and Activate" mode (logon language: EN)
· Check that the table entries for Keys 08, RTI, FPSE has the text "Full Payment Submission 2026-27"
· Save the entries to a customizing transport and progress it through your landscape
