Symptom

I would like to understand what actions I need to take if I change the P user email in Managed Gateway for Spend&Network (ISMG).

Resolution

The only action that must be taken is to confirm the e-mail. After updating the email in the ISMG portal, you'll receive an e-mail from SAP (the sender will be ias@notifications.sap.com). This email may take a few minutes to arrive. Once this e-mail arrives, click on the link from it (Click here to activate your account) to confirm the change of the e-mail. 

The email that you'll receive looks like this:


If the e-mail does not arrive after 10 minutes, check internally with your e-mail administrator to make sure that there are no configurations blocking e-mail from ias@notifications.sap.com
