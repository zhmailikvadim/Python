Symptom

Adding new rules to Rules for Analyzing Memo lines result in an Error.

Environment

SAP Business ByDesign

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to the Business Configuration work center.
  2. Select the Overview view
  3. Search and find Global Settings for Payment.
  4. Select "Create and edit rules for analyzing memo lines" notice that the are multiple rules  already defined.
  5. Click "Assign Account to Rule", the "Assign Account to Rule for Analyzing Memo Lines" screen is loaded.
  6. Click Add Row.
  7. Notice it takes a lot of time for the system to "load" and display the new row. And sometimes the system may show a timeout error.

Cause

Sizing-Limitation.

Resolution

SAP Product Management have evaluated this and confirmed that there is a sizing limitation when the number of maintained rules exceeds 500 rules, from which onwards this is not within support - and product maintenance scope.

For a typical company belonging to the SME (Small and Medium Sized Companies) -sector a number in excess of this threshold is not regarded as reasonable.

SAP Recommended Best Practices:

  1. Smaller number of highly specific and relevant rules.
  2. Effective use of patterns and placeholders.
  3. Minimal overlap between rules.
  4. Regular maintenance and deletion of rules no longer in use.
