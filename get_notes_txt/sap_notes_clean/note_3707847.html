Symptom

  • There is a unique user, or a few users, who are being redirected to a login page after attempting to access Incentive Management through their SSO.
  • This issue may have started suddenly or may have never worked properly.
  • Changing the network or not using a VPN might resolve the issue.

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Incentive Management
  • Incentive Management on Legacy Oracle

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Attempt to access Incentive Management through Single Sign-On (SSO).
  2. After the SSO redirect, you will arrive at the Incentive Management login webpage.
  3. If administrative access is required, navigate to Incentive Management > Portal > Global Settings.
    1. Enable the option "Disable session transfer validation."
    2. Save the changes, and the affected user will be able to log in.

Cause

  • The reported issue is caused by the session-transfer validation mechanism.
  • This mechanism validates that an authenticated session is still being used from the same browser and network location as at login time, in order to prevent session hijacking or session reuse from another machine.

Resolution

  1. Ensure that the user's IP address does not change during the session.
  2. Investigate the network configuration to identify and resolve any issues causing the IP address to change during the session.
  3. Refer to the session-transfer validation mechanism details to understand how the validation works and the conditions under which it invalidates the session.
