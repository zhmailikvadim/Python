Symptom

Offset to COGS elimination line in accounting document for goods issue postings in Advanced/Multistage(as of release 2025) intercompany sales/stock transfer process have incorrect profit and partner profit center determined.

Other Terms

advanced ICO, multistage ICO, TP_AC_EXTENSION, TCV, KTOSL

Reason and Prerequisites

This problem only occurs, if profit center in the sales order item in the delivery/intermediate(in Multistage ICO) plant generated by the system and derived from the material/plant profit center is changed manually by the user.

Solution

Implement this note. The change takes effect in a next goods issue posting related to the advanced/multistage ICO sales/stock transfer.
