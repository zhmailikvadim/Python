Symptom

In the Job Requisition Approval tile on the homepage, if the label of an Operator contains an ampersand character "&" it will be incorrectly displayed as its HTML code:  "&amp;" 

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management 

Resolution

This is a defect currently under investigation  by engineering
