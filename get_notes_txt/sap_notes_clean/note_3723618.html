Symptom

You use Transaction Manager of Treasury and Risk Management. You have activated parallel conditions for interest rate instrument. You create a fee condition for a transaction that has a condition for capitalized interest. After the creation you mention, that the created fee condition has a wrong "Direction of Flow" (SSIGN).

Other Terms

sbktyp = 15, sbktyp = 24, condition category TB, Amounts Equivalent to Interest

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction (CI) or the relevant Support Package.
