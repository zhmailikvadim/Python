Symptom

You request PSM release for Action Close of Business Object PRODUCTION_LOT.

Environment

SAP Business ByDesign

Resolution

Action close is not PSM released.

What you want to achieve is currently not possible via SDK or standard web service ManageProductionLotsIn.

Development team don't have any plans at this time to commit to a date for when this may be available at a future time.

Therefore you need to raise an enhancement request for this.

SAP Business ByDesign Customer Influence Forum： https://influence.sap.com/sap/ino/#/campaign/886
