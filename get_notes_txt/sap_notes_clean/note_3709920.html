Symptom

How to Configure the settings relevant to "Collection Management": Step-by-Step Process.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Resolution

  1. There are a Scope Item BFC - Collection and Dispute Management, open the link and download the Test Script documentation.
