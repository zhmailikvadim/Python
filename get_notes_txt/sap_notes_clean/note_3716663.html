Symptom

  • When configuring Define Partner Determination Procedure, the Partner Function '00000056 – Service Employee Group' does not appear in the F4 dropdown list for selection.
  • In Define Partner Functions, Partner Function 00000056 – Service Employee Group is active and correctly maintained.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA 2021 and above

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to SPRO > Service > Basic Function > Partner Processing.
  2. Access Define Partner Determination Procedure.
  3. Attempt to assign Partner Function 00000056 via F4 help.
  4. Observe that Partner Function 00000056 – Service Employee Group is missing from the dropdown list.
  5. Navigate to Define Partner Functions.
  6. Verify that Partner Function 00000056 – Service Employee Group is available and correctly maintained.

Cause

Enterprise Organizational Model is enabled, which requires different configuration.

Resolution

  • Confirm that the Enterprise Organizational Model is enabled (SPRO > IMG > Service > Enterprise Organizational Model.
  • When the Enterprise Organizational Model is enabled, the Service Employee Group is not utilized. Instead, the Service Team in the Organizational Management assignment block is used.
  • Ensure that the "Service Team Profile" is filled and the Service Team data is maintained in a Fiori app.
  • To fill the Service Team Profile: Navigate to SPRO > Service > Transactions > Basic Settings > Define Transaction Types > SRVO > Details > Profiles.
