Symptom

  • The My Purchase Requisitions app does not support attaching files at the header level of Purchase Requisitions.
  • Attachments can only be added at the line item level.
  • During the approval process, approvers must navigate into line items to view attachments in the My Inbox app.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the My Purchase Requisitions app.
  2. Attempt to attach a file at the header level of a Purchase Requisition.
  3. Observe that the attachment option is only available at the line item level.

Cause

Currently, the My Purchase Requisitions app does not support header-level attachment functionality.

Resolution

Refer to the SAP Influence platform to upvote the requirement for header-level attachment functionality.
