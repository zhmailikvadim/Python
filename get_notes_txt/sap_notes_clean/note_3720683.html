Symptom

In SAP for Me, when trying to delete a contact person from the Finance & Legal Contacts card, the following message appears:

“This contact cannot be deleted because it is still used in transactions.”

Environment

  • SAP for Me
  • Finance & Legal tab
  • Finance & Legal contacts card

Cause

The contact person is still linked to existing business transactions (for example, invoices, contracts, or other financial documents).

Due to these dependencies, the system prevents deletion.

This situation can also occur when attempting to transfer finance contacts if the contact is still assigned.

Resolution

Currently, contacts that are still used in transactions cannot be deleted directly by end users.

If you encounter this issue in the Finance & Legal Contacts card:

  1. Submit a support case to the SAP for Me support team.

  2. Provide the following information in your case:

    • A screenshot of the error message

    • Contact person name and S-user ID (if available)

    • Company ID / Customer number

    • Description of the action performed

The SAP for Me team will investigate the issue and help you further.
