Symptom

The manager approve the workflow based leave request via Fiori My Inbox, but the request status remains in status "SENT", while the connected workflow status is "Completed". Sometimes the workflow  status stucks in status "Start" also.

The problem occurs randomly and it can not be reproduced in the system.

Environment

  • SAP ERP 
  • SAP HCM
  • SAP HCM FIORI

Cause

It can occur, when the Duet Leave management BADI implementation OSP_PT_GEN_REQ_INTER is active in the system .

Resolution

The objective of Duet product BADi is to transfer the status of the leave request to Duet (to integrate into MS Office applications). 
Non Duet customers does not need default active implementation. 

An active implementation can cause several problems.

Please check the solution section of the Consulting note 0001565933 "De-activate the Duet leave management BADI implementation" and perform the manual steps to deactivate this implementation.
 
After that please check the occurrence of any new issues.

Note: This KBA is applicable for leave approval from Fiori My Inbox only. The issue will not happen when approving from other approval applications .
