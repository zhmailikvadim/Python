Symptom

You are interested in how long can the backup process can be performed in HANA Service.

Environment

SAP HANA Service

Resolution

SAP employs backup processes and other measures that ensure rapid recovery of business-critical systems as and when necessary.

The retention time for backups is 14 days. This means that an instance can be recovered for 14 days.
