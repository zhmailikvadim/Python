Symptom

Why am I unable to find a parameter in guided buying administration?

Cause

This behavior can occur due to one of the following reasons:

  • The parameter is outdated or has been replaced
  • The parameter requires enablement by Support
  • The parameter is related to a feature that has not yet been released

Resolution

The resolution depends of the scenario.

  1. Over time, system parameters may be deprecated, renamed, or replaced by newer configurations. When an outdated parameter is used, it may no longer be recognized or function as expected. Always verify that the parameter is still valid and supported in the current system version.
  2. Some parameters are not enabled by default and require activation by the Support team. In these cases, the parameter may exist in the system but remain inactive until the appropriate enablement is performed.
  3. Certain parameters are delivered in advance of a feature release and are not functional until the feature becomes generally available. If the feature is not yet released in the customer’s environment, the parameter will not take effect.
