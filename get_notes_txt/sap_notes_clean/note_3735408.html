Symptom

There has a requirement to use Forms Service by Adobe in multiple subaccount under on global account.

Is it possible to use the service like this?

Environment

SAP Business Technology Platform on Cloud Foundry

Resolution

That depends on the number of quota under global account.

A quota represents the numeric quantity of a service plan that you're entitled to consume in your global account and its subaccounts.

See guide Entitlements and Quotas for more information.
