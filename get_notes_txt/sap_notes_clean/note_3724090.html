Symptom

The (WAVE-based) process "Transfer Building Savings Contract" (BSC_PR_CHNG_TRANSF) is terminated with the error messages "Result of term class /IBS/CL_BSC_TERM_RATINGNR_F_TAR could not be determined" and "Repayment-Amount [...] falls below minimum Repayment-Amount [...] (Loan [...]/Interest [...])" even though the repayment contribution is within the limits for the minimum repayment contribution and maximum repayment contribution set in the IMG activity "Rule Type Characteristics for Building Loan Phase" (/IBS/CBSC_T_RTLO).

Reason and Prerequisites

During the calculation of the valuation figure, the values for the repayment contribution and the valuation loan are not checked for adherence to the minimum and maximum limits in the terms for determining the repayment time (/IBS/CL_BSC_TERM_RED_PERIOD, /IBS/CL_BSC_TERM_FUT_RED_P_SIM). In some cases, for example, if the interest charge is higher than a repayment contribution that is below the minimum amount, no repayment time can be calculated.

Solution

The repayment contribution is assumed to be within the defined limits for the calculation in the terms "Repayment Time" (/IBS/CL_BSC_TERM_RED_PERIOD) and "Precalculated Credit" (/IBS/CL_BSC_TERM_FUT_RED_P_SIM). The valuation loan is accepted for the calculation in the term class /IBS/CL_BSC_TERM_LOAN_SUM used in the term "Repayment Time" as not higher than the building savings total minus the minimum credit required for the allocation.
