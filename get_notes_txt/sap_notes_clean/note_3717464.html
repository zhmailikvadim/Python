Symptom

The screen freezes at the role level during the `GRC Risk Analysis` process. 

Environment

  • SAP GRC Access Control 10.0
  • SAP GRC Access Control 10.1
  • SAP GRC Access Control 12.0 
  • SAP Access Control 12.0 for SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to Risk Analysis. 
  2. Attempt to run the "Role Level" screen. 
  3. Observe that the screen hangs during operation.

Cause

  • The issue occurs due to a process in the "Risk Analysis: Role Level" screen that hangs during operation.
  • This may be caused by a conflict or delay in the execution of the foreground process Risk analysis.

Resolution

Implement the note 0003512304, Ensure that Risk analysis is not triggered automatically in the foreground for any changes made on the frontend screen.
