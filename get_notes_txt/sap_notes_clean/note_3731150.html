Symptom

Within advanced Available-to-Promise (aATP) the Alternative-based Confirmation (ABC) is used to create subitems. In this case, additional items are created in the sales document which called the ATP check. These items can point to a source of supply, for instance, a purchasing info record (Third-Party Order Processing). The information about the source of supply is available in the sales document and is used for example to create a corresponding purchase requisition. But these information are not cleared after the processing of a subitem. Therefore, wrong data may be used in subsequent processing.

Other Terms

FV45PABC_SRC_SUBITEM_MERGE  GT_ITEM_ABC_SRC  Business Process Scheduling (BPS)

Reason and Prerequisites

Previously, the source of supply data were not used any more after the creation of a purchase requisition, so deletion was not necessary. Now they are also used for scheduling.

Solution

Apply the attached correction instruction.
