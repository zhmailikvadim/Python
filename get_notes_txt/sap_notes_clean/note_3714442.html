Symptom

The link for "All Jobs" on the career site is not displaying any jobs when clicked, despite no filters being applied.

Environment

SAP Successfactors Recruiting Marketing

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the career site.
  2. Click on the "All Jobs" category page link.
  3. Observe that the page displays "no jobs" despite no filters being applied.

Cause

No Filter condition not maintained in job list.

Resolution

Maintain values in the filter on the job list page under the Category Page list
