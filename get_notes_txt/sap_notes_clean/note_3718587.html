Symptom

You maintain a record in Company Car (0442) infotype, Company Car (COMP) subtype. The first 60 months, the pollution category is respected by the tax office and may remain unchanged. After the 60 months, you need to check the pollution category annually at the beginning of the calendar year.

To control that the right pollution category is assigned, you have to set a new record in Company Car (0442) infotype every year. To that end, a new check was introduced in payroll via SAP Note 3575874.

The error/warning message should not be displayed in case the pollution category is Taxable Benefit 22% (B7) or Taxable Benefit 35% (E), because they represent the maximum taxable benefit rates applicable under Dutch legislation. Therefore an annual revalidation cannot result in a higher taxable percentage.

Other Terms

Company Car, IT0442, P0442-POLLC, Auto van de zaak

Solution

After installing this SAP Note you will not be warned regarding of the pollution category in case the pollution category is B7 or E. The message is still applicable for other categories, and it is displayed as of the first day of the year, after the 60 months. The warning reads "Create a new IT0442 record. You must check the pollution category yearly" (HRPAYNL_EN 078).
