Symptom

You use infotype 0600 ("Employer Statement") to create employer statements for your employees.

For employees with an hourly wage, the amounts printed on the form under "Income for Hourly Wage" (item 20) for the entries "Holiday Pay", "Public Holiday Compensation", "Percentage of 13th Monthly Wage/Gratuity", and "Other Wage Elements" may differ from the values stored in infotype 0008 ("Basic Pay").

A manual correction of the amounts in infotype 0600 is not possible.

Other Terms

General terms and conditions, form, 13th monthly wage, public holiday compensation, holiday pay, other wage elements, IT0008, IT0600

Reason and Prerequisites

During default value determination for employees with an hourly wage, the system previously read only the percentage portions of the wage elements from infotype 0008. The corresponding amounts were not transferred. Instead, they were calculated in the form by a script based only on the basic wage and the percentages. This led to differences if the actual amounts from infotype 0008 did not correspond to the product of the basic pay and percentage. A manual correction of the amounts in infotype 0600 was also not possible because these were calculated exclusively using a script in the form.

Solution

Import the HR Support Package relevant for your release. If you implement the solution in advance, use Note Assistant to implement the correction instruction relevant for your release. Additional information about Note Assistant is available on the SAP Support Portal at https://support.sap.com/notes.

After you implement this SAP Note, the system reads the amounts of the wage elements from infotype 0008 in addition to the percentages during default value determination for employees with an hourly wage and enters these amounts on the "Earnings" tab page of the infotype 0600. These amounts are used directly in the form and are no longer calculated by script. If required, the values can also be overwritten manually in infotype 0600.

Note that existing records of infotype 0600 are not updated automatically. The amounts for "Holiday Pay", "Public Holiday Compensation", "Percentage of 13th Monthly Wage/Gratuity", and "Other Wage Elements" may need to be maintained manually for the affected records on the "Earnings" tab page.
