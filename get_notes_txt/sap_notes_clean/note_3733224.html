Symptom

When the customer open an object(Vendor/Risk/Response/Opportunity/Enhancement Plan/Response prosal), and meanwhile the same object will be also opened by the other colleague. It means that the current object is locked. There will be a remind information raised. It couldn't show the object name or customer's full name. The detail information is as below:

  • For the vendor, the remind information is 'Selected vendor is currently locked by XXX'.
  • For the activity, the remind information is 'Selected activity is currently locked by XXX'.
  • For the risk, the remind information is 'XXX is currently updating the risk'.
  • For the opportunity, the remind information is 'XXX is currently updating the opportunity'.
  • For the response, the remind information is 'XXX is currently updating the Response XXX'.
  • For the enhancement plan, the remind information is 'XXX is currently updating the Enhancement plan XXX'.
  • For the proposed response, the remind information is 'XXX is currently updating the selected #RESPONSE_PROPOSAL#'.

Other Terms

GRC, Governance, Risk and Compliance, Risk Management

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Apply this note.  The remind infomration will be as below:

  •  Vendor 'XXX' is locked by 'XXX'.
  •  Activity 'XXX' is locked by 'XXX'.
  •  Risk 'XXX' is locked by 'XXX'.
  •  Opportunity 'XXX' is locked by 'XXX'.
  •  Response 'XXX' is locked by 'XXX'.
  •  Enhancement plan 'XXX' is locked by 'XXX'.
  •  Response Proposal  'XXX' is locked by 'XXX'.
