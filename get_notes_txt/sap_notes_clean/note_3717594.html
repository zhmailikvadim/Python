Symptom

This SAP Note contains the following corrections/performance improvements that occur during the processing of reissue triggers:

  • If alternative LTSD recipients exist, but an LTSD was issued based on a document address, invoices of the alternative LTSD recipients are also read for the comparison of the threshold value. This may lead to an incorrect preference statement in the LTSD reissue due to irrelevant invoice values. For LTSDs that were issued based on document addresses, no alternative LTSD recipients may be taken into account.
  • During the determination of the standard address for alternative LTSD recipients, performance problems may occur due to a select on the CDS-View I_BPCurrentDefaultAddress. The select has been optimized by the HDB hint 'USE_HEX_PLAN'.
  • Optimized access to the trigger table during processing due to a new secondary key of table type /SAPSLL/RIRTRIGG_T.
