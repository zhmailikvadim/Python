Symptom

Some of the GR Documents were missing Medication Kit(s) assignment after Delivery Confirmation from 3PL.

 No traces of such documents i.e. failed QRFC, SLG1 logs could be found

Other Terms

Intelligent Clinical Supply Management, External Warehouse, Distribution, 3PL, Medication Kits

Reason and Prerequisites

  • External Warehouse managed Inbound Delivery should be Completely processed
  • Goods Receipt for these Deliveries are already posted with Movement '101'

Solution

Please implement the automatic corrections of this Note.

Steps to perform post Note implementation:

Run the report '/CTCO/ASSIGN_MEDKITS_TO_GRDOC' in SE38

Provide either Inbound Deliveries or GR Document(s) as Input

Assign Medkit(s) to GR Document(s) by choosing the right option

Note:

  1. Applicable only for External Warehouse deliveries which are completed and having GR Document posted (101).
  2. Report does not trace back to STO or Outbound Deliveries to proceed
  3. GR Document Items which are already linked to Medkits are excluded from the Update
  4. Upon execution, the Medkit(s) will be assigned to GR documents and setting them as the current document of the Medkit. No updates on the GR Document.
  5. Medkit status will be updated to "Available" since getting linked to a GR Document (101)
  6. Although considers multiple Deliveries / GR Documents, should be executed with single Delivery initially to verify the results.
