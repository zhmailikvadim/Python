Symptom

Freight cost added in the delivery document does not show the exact credit price in LIPS-CMPRE_FLT.

Environment

  • Sales and Distribution (SD)
  • Logistics Execution (LE)
  • SAP Credit Management (FSCM)
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create the Sales Order.
  2. Create Delivery and change/add the freight cost in the delivery document.
  3. Observe that the credit price LIPS-CMPRE_FLT does not have the expected value.

Cause

The logic of SAP Credit Management requires that the same credit amount throughout the entire document chain (Sales Order → Delivery → Invoice) to prevent data inconsistencies. Therefore, the Standard system logic is to reference the credit price determined in the Sales Order (VBAP-CMPRE) when calculating the credit exposure of the subsequent delivery.

Resolution

Ensure that freight cost and VAT values are determined during the Sales Order phase so they are captured in VBAP-CMPRE.

Keywords

delivery document, dynamic credit check, credit exposure, VBAP-CMPRE, SAP Credit Management, Sales Order, pricing procedure, LIPS-CMPRE_FLT, credit price, freight cost, VAT value
