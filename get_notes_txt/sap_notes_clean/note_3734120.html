Other Terms

Reason and Prerequisites

Law requirement: https://monitoruloficial.ro/Monitorul-Oficial--PI--147--2026.html .

This announcement contains information about the legal change Emergency Ordinance 8/2026, on economic recovery measures (Ordonanță de urgență privind instituirea unor măsuri de relansare economică, creșterea investițiilor productive și a competitivității, precum și pentru modificarea și completarea unor acte normative în domeniul fiscal-bugetar), published on February 25, 2026 by the Guvernul României (Government of Romania).

The effective dates are staggered:

  • March 1, 2026 for changes to the VAT cash accounting system and certain personal income tax deductions.
  • March 1, 2026 Income for salary-related deductions.

 

The ordinance introduces a wide range of fiscal measures to stimulate the Romanian economy, support investment, and increase tax compliance. The changes impact multiple business processes across corporate tax, personal income tax, and VAT.

Key changes impacting business processes include:

  • Personal Income Tax (effective from March 1, 2026):
    • The scope of tax-deductible pension contributions is expanded to include occupational pension funds and Pan-European Personal Pension Products (PEPP), with an annual limit of EUR 400 for each. This impacts the monthly payroll calculation.
    • A new annual deduction of up to EUR 400 is introduced for individuals for investments in shares, bonds, and ETFs. As this applies to salary income, it also affects the monthly payroll calculation.

Solution

EE Bonuses

Starting with 01.03.2026 there has been introduced other EE Benefits:

  • Shares, Bonds, and ETFs EE (see example above)
  • Occupational pension EE
  • PEPP (pan-European personal pension) EE

Create the wage type sets for necessary benefits and customize it in new table T7ROB3.


ER Bonus

Starting with 01.03.2026 there has been introduced new ER Benefit for non-taxable income accorded by the employer:

  • Occupational pension ER

This bonus should be customized (if needed) in old table T7ROB1 with ER bonuses. Please create the set of the wage types for this bonus and enter the records into T7ROB1 table (like e.g. ER Gym Cards).

 

Please see the attached document for more details and the customizing examples.
