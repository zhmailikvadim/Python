Symptom

  • In VL02N screen, after manually adding a batch number at the item level and saving the delivery, the item-level packing status is displayed as "Fully Packed" in VL03N screen.
  • No handling unit is created despite the packing status being marked as "Completed Packed".
  • Error VL617 – “Item has already been packed and therefore cannot be deleted” is raised during the delivery deletion even though no handling unit is assigned. 

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • Logistics Execution (LE)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the VL02N transaction and input the delivery number.
  2. Select the line item and click on the "Batch Split" tab.
  3. Enter the batch number and delivery quantity, then press enter and save the document.
  4. Access the VL03N transaction and input the delivery number.
  5. Expand the "+" icon and double-click on the line item (e.g., 90001-line item).
  6. Navigate to the "Processing" tab and observe the "Pack" status as "Completed Packed".

Cause

After implementing SAP Notes 3506547, delivery items with a quantity of zero may have the packing status (PKSTA) incorrectly set to ‘C’ (Completely Packed) when the item is created with zero quantity or when the quantity is changed to zero. As a consequence, you can't delete these items. 

Resolution

  1. Implement SAP Notes 3601915 and 3673188.
  2. Verify the packing process after the implementation of the notes.
  3. Confirm that the packing status behavior aligns with the expected functionality.
