Symptom

The following issues were identified in the XML output report for REGES Romania (HROLREG5):

  • In case the XML output is split into multiple batches due to 100 message limit, contract records for one employee may be included in different batches. If the batches are not sent in exact order, some messages may not be accepted on REGES side due to incorrect order of sending.
  • Message for operation 'SuspendareContract' should not contain element <DataIncetareSuspendare>.
  • Customers requested a possibility to display operation in the ALV output of report HROLREG5 to improve readability and enable filtering of records.

30.01.2026:

  • When user manually marks a contract cancellation record as submitted, the canceled record may not be correctly updated in table T7ROG7. This may lead to repeated reporting of the cancellation.

04.02.2026:

  • Further improvements to manual contract record cancellation are required.

12.02.2026:

  • Record status information in HROLREG5 is not clear enough.

Other Terms

HRRO; Romania; REGES; XML

Solution

Romanian report HROLREG5 has been adjusted to correct mentioned symptoms.

We have added new status values to better describe the current status of each record. At the moment, the available records status options are:

  • Not sent
  • Sent to REGES
  • Accepted
  • Rejected
  • Canceled (contract records only)
  • Confirmed Manually
