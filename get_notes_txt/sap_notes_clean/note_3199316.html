Symptom

A user was getting SAP Cloud ALM Operations notifications, but they stopped after some months

Environment

SAP Cloud ALM 1.0

Cause

  • SAP cloud ALM Operations Notifications need to be revalidated every 6 months.
  • Prior to expiring an email will be send requesting to give your consent to continue to receive notification. Should the validity not be extended by the due date the validation link will expire and notifications will cease.

    Sample email:

Resolution

  1. Once the validity link is expired if you still want to receive Notification, then in the SAP Cloud ALM Launchpad, under Operations, click on the Notification Management icon



  2. Then select the user whose validity link has expired
  3. Click on the paper airplane icon to resend the invitation.



  4. The email will be resent
  5. In the email under the NEXT STEPS section click on the hyperlink “Here” as shown below



    Once validated you will get the following confirmation message and Notifications will resume
