Symptom

The option Ariba Proposals & Questionnaires is missing from the drop-down list on the top left corner of the SAP Business Network homepage. As a result, it is impossible to access Sourcing events as before.

Cause

For some of the newly created supplier accounts, Ariba Proposals and Questionnaires have been integrated into SAP Business Network page.

Resolution

Please click the Proposals & Contracts tab to view all sourcing events. Detailed steps are as follows:

  1. Log into your SAP Business Network account.
  2. Click Proposals & Contracts tab.
  3. Switch among different buyers.
