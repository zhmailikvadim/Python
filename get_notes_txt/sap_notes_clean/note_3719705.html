Symptom

  • Inquiry regarding recovery time objective (RTO) and recovery point objective (RPO) for SAP Incentive Management.
  • Request for details on the time taken to bring up the system in a new data center and the age of the backup if the system is restored from the backup.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
  • Incentive Management

Resolution

  1. Refer to SAP Trust Center for information regarding recovery time objective (RTO) and recovery point objective (RPO).
  2. Reach out to the Customer Success Manager (CSM) for further assistance.
