Symptom

Is it possible to use auto-approval of supplier organization registration tasks in default supplier workspace template?

Resolution

The task template does include an auto-approve option, keep in mind approvers need to be left blank when choosing this option. You can try this option within your test realm to determine if it aligns with your business requirements. 

If the test results do not meet your expectations, connect with your account representative to explore alternatives that may better suit your needs.
