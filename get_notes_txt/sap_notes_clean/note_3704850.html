Symptom

You update My Inbox from one of the versions S/4HANA 1809 - 1.19.x, S/4HANA 1909 - 1.23.x or S/4HANA 2020 - 1.27.19 to version S/4HANA 2021 - 1.31.x or higher. Then you open My Inbox in Expert View and want to define conditions for a string field (for example, the Task Title field). You want to define a Exclude range, but you see only the Include range option available.

Other Terms

Fiori My Inbox, F0862, C_F0862

Reason and Prerequisites

A bug in SAPUI5 during the original implementation of the logic made the Exclude range option unavailable. Fix was introduced in 1.84.9.

Solution

Based on your SAP S/4HANA release, ensure that your system has at least one of the following minimum SAP UI5 versions installed: 1.96.41, 1.108.44, or 1.120.31. Then, apply the referenced SAP Note, corresponding to your UIBAS001 Support Package.
