Symptom

A supplier invoice cannot be posted due to below error messages:

  • Positive total gross amount missing.
  • Only positive total tax amount permitted.

In some cases, adding to above, below error messages also appear.

  • Enter at least one invoice item.
  • Sum of cash discount relevant items is negative. You can only post the supplier invoice if the sum of the cash discount relevant items is positive. Change the cash discount relevant indicator of the supplier invoice items accordingly.

Environment

SAP Business ByDesign

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Supplier Invoicing work center.
  2. Go to Invoices and Credit Memos view.
  3. Open supplier invoice ABC.
  4. Select Check button.
  5. Above error messages be displayed.

Cause

The total amount of the supplier invoice is negative.

Scenario 1

The type of all line items in the Supplier Invoice are Credit Memo Item. Each of the line items has negative value hence the total amount for the invoice is negative.

The type of invoice item could be checked under Items tab - Basic Data subtab.

In this case, error "Enter at least one invoice item" is displayed.

Scenario 2

There are multiple line items in the supplier invoice, some are Invoice Items while some are Credit Memo Items.

However, the total value of the Credit Memo Items (negative) is greater than the total value of the Invoice Items (positive).

Hence the total amount of the supplier invoice is still negative. 

Resolution

Supplier Invoice only supports positive total amount, while Credit Memo supports negative total amount.

Instead of supplier invoice, using credit memo for negative amount.

For existing supplier invoice with the errors, follow below steps.

  1. Void the supplier invoice.
  2. Create a credit memo for the negative invoicing amount.
