Symptom

In a leasing contract with multiple conditions, some conditions may not be included in the initial valuation. If you calculate and save the valuation before considering these conditions, you may encounter the following issue:

If multiple valuation rules exist and you want to consider a condition retroactively to all of rules, the field "Consider Condition" becomes unchangeable after the condition has been considered in one rule.
As a result, it is not possible to check the field “consider condition” to all valuation rules.

Other Terms

CONSiDERCOND, VICECONDRULE

Reason and Prerequisites

Programm error

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified support package.
