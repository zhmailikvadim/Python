Symptom

Can two BDC Cockpits be created—one for PROD and one for NON‑PROD—or must each customer use a single cockpit?

Environment

SAP Business Data Cloud

Resolution

One tenant is provisioned per customer and is created during SAP Business Data Cloud Core provisioning.

See official documentation: https://help.sap.com/docs/business-data-cloud/administering-sap-business-data-cloud/creating-sap-business-data-cloud-formations#sap-business-data-cloud-core-components
