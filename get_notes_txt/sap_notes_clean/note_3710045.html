Symptom

During Mass Processing of Functional Location in MDC, incorrect address numbers(ADRNR) are getting imported into the MDC process address tables and this causing activation is getting failed.

You may also see incorrect address numbers are getting exported into the file during Export process.

Other Terms

MDC EAM, Mass Processing, Functional Location, ADR2, ADR3, ADR6, ADRC, ADRT, ILOA, /UGI/CL_MDC_DATA_FLOC

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Implement the correction instruction
