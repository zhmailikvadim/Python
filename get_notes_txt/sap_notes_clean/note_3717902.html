Symptom

A change to a sales document leads to a runtime error of type ITAB_DUPLICATE_KEY.

Other Terms

ITAB_DUPLICATE_KEY, CL_ATP_SLS_BFFR_SET_4_CTLG_BLDCP, CL_ATP_SLS_BFFR_SET_4_CTLG_BLDCM001, IF_ATP_SLS_BFFR_SET_4_CTLG_BLD~SET_PARTNER_BUFFER

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequsites:

  • Presence of multiple usage of identical partner role for sales documents.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions.
