Symptom

Issue :
At the time of goods issue, the status of the Serialized Material was incorrect, which prevented the goods receipt from being posted.

Solution:
With this NOTE, transfer of Serialized Materials(Equipment without an “X” in field EQUI-S_EQUI) via GDD(Relocation and GDD Transaction DFS_GENERIC_DISTR run) is handled.

Other Terms

Defense Disconnected S4, GDD, Equipment, Serial Number, Goods Issue, S_EQUI, DFS_GENERIC_DISTR

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions provided with the NOTE.
