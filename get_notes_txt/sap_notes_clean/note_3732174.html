Symptom

  • When the Google social login provider is configured with the CDC SDK, cookies like _Secure-ENID observed.
  • No specific error messages or error codes are mentioned.

Environment

  • SAP Customer Data Cloud
  • Social login

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Enable/configure the Google social login provider for a site that uses the CDC SDK.
  2. Open the site where Google Social login is enabled.
  3. Inspect browser storage (for example, in DevTools) and observe that Google cookies (for example, __SECURE-ENID) are present.
  4. Disable the Google provider configuration and observe that Google cookies are no longer set.

Cause

Cookies are generated by Google as part of the social login flow. CDC only initiates the connection and hands over to Google to complete the authentication; CDC does not control or set these Google cookies.

Resolution

  1. Verify that the observed cookies are not CDC cookies by reviewing the CDC cookies list: refer to: CDC cookies reference.
  2. Acknowledge that the cookies are set by Google during the social login flow and cannot be controlled by CDC: refer to: Google cookie policy.
  3. To avoid third-party cookies before consent, remove or disable the Google social login provider configuration so that no requests to Google are made prior to consent.

See Also

  • refer to: CDC cookies reference
  • refer to: Google cookie policy
