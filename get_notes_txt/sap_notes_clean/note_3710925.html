Symptom

Looking for information regarding how to set and use Multi-Layer headers on stories for Embedded Analytics

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
  • Embedded Analytics

Resolution

On Embedded Analytics for Incentive Management, there is no option for setting a Multi-Layered header. However, you can apply Compound Table Filters in order to set your table widgets according to your needs.

Please refer to the Help Page for guidance on applying compound or simple table filters in Embedded Analytics:

Apply Compound or Simple Table Filters (Optimized) | SAP Analytics Cloud Help

For further assistance on structure and designing of your stories, it is recommended to reach out to your Customer Success Manager (CSM) for Optimization Services.
