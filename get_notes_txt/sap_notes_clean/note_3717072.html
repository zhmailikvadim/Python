Symptom

It is not possible to switch off message TPM_TRG 255 'Hedging relationship &1 in acctng code &2 and valn area &3 is affected' during valuation of a hedging relation.

Other Terms

SAP Treasury and Risk Management, TPM100, Hedge Accounting for Positions

Reason and Prerequisites

Customer requirement

Use transaction OBA5 to configure the message control.

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction (CI) or the relevant Support Package.
