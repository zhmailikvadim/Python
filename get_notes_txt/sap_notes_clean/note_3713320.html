Symptom

This SAP Note fixes the following issues:

  • The system incorrectly calculates the deduction for "Amount Exceeding Composite Ceiling on Income Deduction" when an employee has contributions to Investment Association 2 recorded in the Investment Association (E9) subtype of Expense Information (0881) infotype.
  • For a foreign employee who is marked as 'Spouse of Head' in the Household Head/Member field of Year End Adjustment Data (0542) infotype, the system does not calculate the housing saving deduction. Note that version 5 or higher is required for complete resolution of the housing saving deduction issue.
  • Political Donation (20), Home Country Donation (General) (43) and Home Country Donation (Spcl. Dis. Area) (44) are not output in section 4. Donation Adjustment Specification of Donation Expense Receipt if donation amount does not exceed KRW 100,000 and the calculated tax credit is 0.

Other Terms

YEA, 2025

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a program error.

Solution

Apply the relevant HR support package or correction instructions in this SAP Note.
