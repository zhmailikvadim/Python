Symptom


Users are unable to delete active bank accounts in the Manage Bank Accounts app, even if these accounts are no longer in use.

Environment

  • S/4 HANA 2020
  • S/4 HANA 2021
  • S/4 HANA 2022
  • S/4 HANA 2023

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Manage Bank Accounts app.
  2. Adjust filters as needed and click on Go.
  3. Select the bank account and attempt to delete it by clicking on the "Delete" button.

Cause


Active bank accounts cannot be deleted once they are activated.

Resolution

  1. Active bank accounts cannot be deleted once activated; they can only be closed.
  2. To close a bank account, follow these steps:
  3. Open the Manage Bank Accounts app and locate the bank account.
  4. Select one of the following options:
  • Mark for Closing: Use this button to initiate the closing process. The bank account status will change to Marked for Closing.
  • Confirm Closing at Bank: If the bank account has already been closed at its bank, use this button to update the status to Closed at Bank.
  • Send Closing Request to Bank: Use this button to confirm that you are sending or have sent a closing request to the bank. The status will update to Closing Request Sent to Bank.
    Depending on your settings, a closing request letter may be automatically generated and sent to the bank account contact person via email. The recipient can forward the letter to the bank to request account closure. For more details on the required settings for using the bank correspondence function, see Bank Correspondence.
  • Once the bank account is closed at the bank, finalize the process in the system by selecting the Confirm Closing at Bank button.
