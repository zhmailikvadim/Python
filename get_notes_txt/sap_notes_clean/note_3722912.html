Symptom

Gender field is available across the SAP SuccessFactors platform.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Cause

Resolution

  1. The Gender field is available in the Recruiting module as a standard field.
  2. The Gender field can also be configured as a custom field in other areas of the platform based on business requirements.
  3. Within the Recruiting module:
  • The Gender field is completed by external candidates during account creation.
  • Recruiters can also edit this field based on assigned permissions.
