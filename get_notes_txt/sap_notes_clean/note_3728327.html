Symptom

When pressing the "Apply" button in the footer of the overview page of the Review Availability Check Result UI, an ITAB_DUPLICATE_KEY runtime error occurs.

Other Terms

ATP; aATP; RACR; Review Availability Check Result; ITAB_DUPLICATE_KEY; CL_ATP_CHKRR_RECHECK_ORCHESTORCP; CL_ATP_CHKRR_RECHECK_ORCHESTORCM003; RECHECK

Reason and Prerequisites

This issue occurs if the following conditions are all met:

  • At least three requirements are checked at once
  • At least two of these requirements are checked without advanced ATP (but, for example, with global ATP)
  • The confirmations of at least one requirement have been adjusted in RACR

The reason for this problematic behavior is as follows: When applying changes from the Review Availability Check Result UI, an additional availability check is triggered to ensure consistency. Due to a programming error, also the requirements without advanced ATP are included in this additional availability check, leading to various problems including the runtime error described above.

Solution

Apply the correction instructions for this SAP Note or upgrade to an SAP S/4HANA support package that contains this correction.
