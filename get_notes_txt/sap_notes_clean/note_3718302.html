Symptom

How database size and allocation details can be obtained without relying on OS-level commands or isql in SAP ASE for Business Suite and Netweaver environments.

Environment

SAP ASE for Business Suite (all versions)

Netweaver 7.5

Resolution

Database size and allocation details can be obtained through the DBA Cockpit interface, where the information can be accessed without the need for OS‑level commands or isql:
  • Navigate to DBA Cockpit → Database SID → Space → Databases

Keywords

DBA Cockpit, Netweaver, database, size, allocation, space
