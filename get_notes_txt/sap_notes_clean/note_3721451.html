Symptom

  • Converting .unv Universe having Stored procedures to .unx facing issue
  • On performing ‘Refresh Structure’ on .unx universe in IDT reflects ‘Stored Procedures to be missing in the database

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform
  • Universe Design Tool (UDT)
  • Information Design Tool (IDT)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Lauch Information Design Tool
  2. Convert .unv universe including stored procedure to .unx.
  3. Retrieve the .unx universe.
  4. Open Data Foundation Layer.
  5. Perform ‘Refresh Structure’
  6. Observe >> Stored Procedures to be missing in the database.

Cause

  • The tables containing stored procedures are missing the ‘Qualifier’ and/or ‘Owner’ names on .unv and .unx universes.

Resolution

Insert the ‘Qualifier’ and/or ‘Owner’ names on the unv universe and convert the universe to unx.

  1. Launch Universe Design Tool (UDT)
  2. Import the universe.
  3. Right click on the table.
  4. Select ‘Rename Table’
  5. Add ‘Qualifier’ and ‘Owner’ names.
  6. Click OK
  7. Convert .unv to .unx on Information Design Tool (IDT)

Keywords

Stored procedure, refresh structure, missing database, unv , unx, Qualifier, Owner
