Symptom

For Release 600 (including Enhancement Package 1 - 3, that is, all Enhancement Packages lower than EHP4 ), only SAPScript form output is possible for a number of reports of the software (sub)component SAP_HRCAT.

Other Terms

Reporting

Forms

Reason and Prerequisites

Legal changes or publications by the authorities that make it necessary to adjust forms are only taken into account in PDF forms for the medium term.

Solution

The PDF output for the following reports will be delivered subsequently by Support Package:

RPCBETA1        Payroll for Local Tax Office
RPLGLZA0        Creation of Person-Based Wage-Specific Notifications
RPCISTA0        Economic Statistics for Austria
RPCKSJA1        Annual Overview: Payroll City (Municipal) Fund
RPCKSMA1       Payroll for the City (Municipal) Fund
RPCLZSA0        Payslip: Selection Screen Control
RPCPFAA0        Statement on Support Payments Made
RPCSVBA2        Monthly SI Contribution Statement for Regional Health Ins. Funds (A)
RPCSVBA2PBS  Monthly SI Contribution Statement for BVA
RPCPGTA0        Notification of remaining debt as per Section 292L(1) EO

 

The IMG steps for activating the PDF output in Release 600 are missing until further notice. Therefore, all necessary Customizing steps are be carried out directly using transaction SM30.

For general activation, select the option NFWOF in the view V_T5F99K2.

Other required form- and report-specific data will be delivered by Support Package in the following views (see also the attachment).
