Symptom

This SAP Note enables the management of the source of goods for externally procured products.

Key Features

  • SAP Fiori app: Manage Country/Region of Origin (F8875)
  • Report for Processing Material Documents 
    • SAP Fiori app: Schedule Determination of Country/Region of Origin (F9122)
    • Transaction /SAPSLL/PRCOO_CREATE (report /SAPSLL/CREATE_PRCOO)
  • Report for Updating Current Batch Quantity
    • SAP Fiori app: Schedule Update of Batch Stock (F9333)
    • /SAPSLL/PRCOO_BATQTY (report /SAPSLL/UPDATE_PRCOO_BAT_QTY)
  • Transfer of the determined country/region of origin to the S/4HANA feeder system
    • Product Master via OData v2 Service "Product Master (A2X)"
    • Charge via OData v2 Batch Master Record Service
  • Monitoring the transfer and update of the country/region of origin from source documents using AIF interfaces:
    • Namespace: /GTS
    • Interface: PRCOOB_CHG ("Transfer Goods Origin to Batch of Feeder System")
    • Interface: PRCOO_CHG ("Transfer Goods Origin to Product of Feeder System")
    • Interface: PRCOO_LTSD ("Update Country of Origin from LTSD")
    • Interface: PRCOOIMDEC ("Update of Country of Origin from Customs Import Declaration")

Constraints

  • For batch-managed products, the batch must be managed at batch level "Plant". Batch levels "Material" and "Client" are currently not supported. The batch level "Material" is supported by an additional SAP Note that will be published at a later point in time.

Trade policy country of origin, NPO, origin of goods

Reason and Prerequisites

The function requires integrated material documents from an S/4HANA 2021 (S4CORE 106) system or later.
