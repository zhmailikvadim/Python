Symptom

  • Purchase requisition created in ECC is not visible at the source location in product view /SAPAPO/RRP3, even though it is visible in MD04 at both the source location and destination location.
  • The purchase requisition is not being picked up in /SAPAPO/CCR delta report. 
  • The issue occurs for purchase requisitions created in ECC. However, purchase requisitions created in the APO system are working correctly.

Environment

SAP Supply Chain Management 
SAP ERP 
SAP S/4 HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a purchase requisition in ECC using transaction ME51N.
  2. Verify the purchase requisition in MD04 at both, in the source location and destination locations.
  3. Check the purchase requisition visibility in /SAPAPO/RRP3 at the source location.

Cause

  • Missing the Item Category as U (stock transfer) in the item of requistion.

Resolution

When creating the STR in ME51N, it's necessary to maintain the Item Category of the item as U, to indicate that is intend to create a stock transfer requisition.
