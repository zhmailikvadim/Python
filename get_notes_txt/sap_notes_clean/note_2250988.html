Symptom

The change documents for the field 'Main Economic Activity' (LFB1-CIIUCODE and KNB1-CIIUCODE) are not generated.
As a result you cannot assign the field 'Main Economic Activity' to a vendor or customer group (view V_055_B or V_T055).

Other Terms

SAPMF02K, SAPMF02D, CDHDR, CDPOS, OBAU, OB30

Reason and Prerequisites

The 'Change document' flag (DD04D-LOGFLAG) was not marked in data element CIIUCODE.

Solution

Import the Support Package relevant for your release. For an advance correction, perform the manual activities described in the correction instructions.
