Symptom

Historical One-Time Payments (OTP) are visible for Rehire on Old Employment candidates

Environment

SAP SUCCESSFACTORS ONBOARDING

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Perform the Rehire on Old Employment process, ensuring the existing employment record is reused.
  2. Review the candidate data in the Manage Pending Hires phase.
  3. Observe the visibility of historical One-Time Payments from prior employment during the pending hire phase.

Cause

Expected behavior

Resolution

  1. Confirmed that the visibility of Payment Information during the Rehire Data Review process is the current system behavior for Rehire on Old Employment.
  2. It is expected for the Rehire Data Review page to show all the existing data of the candidate without respecting the permissions.
  3. this is the expected system behavior, as the visibility of historical One-Time Payments for a rehire on old employment is managed through Manage Pending Hires.
