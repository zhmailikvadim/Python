Symptom

When attempting to create an Opportunity from Subscription Contracts, the Subscription Contracts are not visible in the Create Opportunity pop-up.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Cloud Version 2
  • SAP Sales Cloud and SAP Service Cloud Version 2

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Subscription Contracts work center.
  2. Open the detailed view for Subscription Contract A.
  3. In the detailed view, under the General tab, click on "Create Opportunity".
  4. Observe that the "Create Opportunity" pop-up opens without displaying any Subscription Contract.

Cause

  • The system only displays Subscription Contracts in the "Create Opportunity" pop-up if their `opportunityCreationStatus` is set to `NOT_APPLICABLE`, `FAILED`, or if `opportunityCreationStatus` does not exist.
  • For Subscription Contract A, the `opportunityCreationStatus` does not meet the required conditions, resulting in the subscription contract not being displayed in the pop-up.

Resolution

  1. Verify the `opportunityCreationStatus` of the Subscription Contract.
  2. Ensure that the Subscription Contract meets one of the following criteria:
  • `opportunityCreationStatus` is set to `NOT_APPLICABLE`.
  • `opportunityCreationStatus` is set to `FAILED`.
  • `opportunityCreationStatus` does not exist.
