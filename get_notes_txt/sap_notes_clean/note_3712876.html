Symptom

When maintaining a Business Partner with the Natural Person flag enabled, the system throws an error, resulting in rejection of CNPJ entries and validation errors during Business Partner maintenance.

Other Terms

BR1, CNPJ, TAX_NUMBER_CHECK

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

To install the solution, use the Note Assistant to implement the correction instruction.

More information about the Note Assistant is available in SAP Service Marketplace under: service.sap.com/note-assistant.
