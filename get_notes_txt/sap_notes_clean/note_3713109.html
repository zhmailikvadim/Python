Symptom

How to Configure the Number ranges for ship notice in SAP Business Network?

Resolution

Follow the below path to configure the number ranges for the ship notice in SAP Business Network.

  • Log in to the Buyer SAP Business Network.
  • Navigate to Account Settings > Transactions > Document Numbering Preference.
  • Advance Ship Notice and create the configuration according to your requirements.

This will ensure to create the ship notice with auto generated number in SAP Supplier Business Network.
