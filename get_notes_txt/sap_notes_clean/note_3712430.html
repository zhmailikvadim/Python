Symptom

You are using the SAP Fiori App—Advanced Scheduling Board in Production Planning and Detailed Scheduling (PP/DS) for SAP S/4HANA OP2023 (FPS3 or higher) release or OP2025 (FPS0/FPS1) release. You are trying to change the Source of Supply for an order. After the change, you try to perform any other change on the same order which results in error- "Incorrect selection of objects to perform selected scheduling function".
