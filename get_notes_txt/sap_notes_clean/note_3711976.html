Symptom

You have implemented SAP Note 3699735 - [MX] Tax Subsidy for Employees - 2026 and you have some questions regarding the system behavior.
This KBA aims to explain the folllowing:

  1. How to configure table T7MX14 for other periodicities, such as SE01 table (weekly).
  2. How the calculation is affected when the month has 31 days.

Environment

  • HR renewal 

  • SAP S/4HANA 

  • SAP ERP  

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll 

Resolution

  1. How to configure table T7MX14 for other periodicities, such as SE01 table (weekly).

In SAP standard solution, we provide entries only for the Monthly subsidy table (ME01) because it is the unique table officially provided by Mexican Government.
If another periodicity is needed, the customer needs to create their own entries.

     2. How the calculation is affected when the month has 31 days.

If a month has 30 or 31 days, that does not affect the subsidy calculation.
