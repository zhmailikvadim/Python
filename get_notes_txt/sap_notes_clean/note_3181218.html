Question

How do I stop sending document copy requests to Ariba Procure-to-Pay (P2P) and Ariba Procure-to-Order (P2O)?

Answer

In order to disable copy requests in your Buyer account, you will have to raise a Case to SAP Ariba Customer Support.

Additional Information

Please note that disabling it will affect all documents sent to Ariba Procure-to-Pay (P2P) and Ariba Procure-to-Order (P2O) which can impact your buyer realms.
