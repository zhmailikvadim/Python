Symptom

You notice that all outbound emails sent from Service Cloud end up in SPAM or quarantined folders.

Environment

  • SAP Service Cloud Version 2
  • SAP Sales Cloud Version 2

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the case work list.
  2. Open any case.
  3. Click on the Send Email button.
  4. Select the From value from the available list.
  5. Add yourself as the receiver.
  6. Add a subject.
  7. Click Send

All outbound emails end up in SPAM or Quarantine folders.

Cause

In the email channel configuration, the Mail-From confirmation is missing. You can create your own custom MAIL FROM domain to address issues involving the outbound email delivery of your emails to your spam or quarantined folders. Then, you need to set up a custom MAIL FROM domain for an email channel and configure the TXT (SPF) record and MX record so that your email can receive any auto-replies (bounce and complaint notifications). 

 

Resolution

To create a custom MAIL FROM domain, follow the steps:

  1. Go to User Menu Settings.
  2. All Settings. 
  3. Navigate to Emails Channels.
  4. Select an active email channel that you want to create the MAIL FROM domain for or create a new channel.
  5. Click Mail-From and follow the onscreen information.
  6. Click on the Confirm button.
