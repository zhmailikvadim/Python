Symptom

  • Ship notices (ASN) are not flowing from Business Network to SAP.
  • Resending the ship notices from Business Network shows success in Ariba and CIG, but the ASN is not updated in SAP.
  • Multiple purchase orders are affected.
  • An attached document shows ASN marked as “Done” for a specific line and quantity in Business Network, but it is not reflected in SAP.

Environment

Managed Gateway for Buyer Business Network SCC

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Send or resend an ASN from Ariba Network.
  2. Verify the message status shows “Success” in CIG/Managed Gateway.
  3. Check the target ERP system and observe that the ASN is not created or updated.

Cause

ASN functionality failure in ERP due to a custom class and method.

Resolution

  1. Confirm the ASN shows “Success” in Managed Gateway/CIG.
  2. If a middleware exists between Managed Gateway and ERP, review it for any message errors or failures.
  3. In ERP, review the corresponding web service/message in transaction SRT_MONI for processing errors.
  4. Check whether a custom implementation  is used for ASN processing.
  5. Engage the internal ABAP team to analyze and correct any issues within the custom ASN logic identified in SRT_MONI.
  6. Reprocess the failed message/ASN after corrections and verify that the ASN is created/updated in ERP.
