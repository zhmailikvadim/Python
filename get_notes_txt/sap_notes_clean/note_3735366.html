Symptom

When you save a physical inventory document in the SAP Fiori app "Manage Physical Inventory Count", the application becomes unresponsive and does not complete the save operation. The issue occurs when the SAP Intelligent Clinical Supply Management business function for physical inventory preprocessing is not active in the backend system.

Other Terms

ISLSUI, Distribution, Intelligent Clinical Supply Management, Manage Physical Inventory Count, Physical Inventory, OData

Reason and Prerequisites

You use SAP Intelligent Clinical Supply Management.

Solution

Implement the attached correction.
