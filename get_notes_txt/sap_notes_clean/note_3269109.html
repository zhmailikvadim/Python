Symptom

When you disable a usage and publish a custom field, you get a message that the field is used externally.

Other Terms

Custom Fields, Where Used, Externally Used, CFD_REPOSITORY_HNDLR074, E074(CFD_REPOSITORY_HNDLR)

Reason and Prerequisites

The Custom Field Framework performs a usage check for all usages that are to be disabled upon publication. The where-used is performed for all usages, not just those that are to be removed.

Solution

Apply this note
