Other Terms

Reason and Prerequisites

SAP is pleased to announce the latest release of SAP FSM WINDOWS WPF version 26.1.0

This release is now signed by an SAP certificate and will require administrator rights on the machine for it to be installed.

Before installing this version, Please sync data on the previous version. The un-synced data will not be carried over to this version.

Solution

SAP FSM Windows Application

Perfect field service moments in one Mobile App/WPF Client. SAP Field Service Management provides industry-leading field service management capabilities in real-time, by bringing together the right data at the right moment to delight customers and drive business results at the most critical points of service.

BENEFITS

  • Send ETA and arrive on time with the right equipment to solve service requests efficiently and meet SLAs
  • Real time optimization for better utilization in dynamic service environments
  • Streamline your cash flow by empowering technicians to generate service reports, capture signatures, or do in-field payment right on the spot
  • Highly flexible checklist improving MTTR
  • Improved visibility for real customer satisfaction and loyalty
  • Improve first time fix rates by providing access to customer, site and installed product information, inventory, warranties and contracts, SLAs,and pricing
  • Reduced administrative costs associated with time-consuming paperwork or debriefing work orders
  • Make your service workforce to in-field sales by providing service technicians selling recommendations and access to current pricing
    Full offline support when out of mobile coverage gives you true mobility
