Symptom

I'm unable to download a report from an email download link due to an error that the report has expired. 

Cause

Any Fieldglass property should not be made available without authentication. Reports should not be downloaded without being Fieldglass Users

Resolution

An active log in session within SAP Fieldglass is required in order to download the report. 

  • Log in to SAP Fieldglass
  • Click on the download link

Keywords

report, download, expired, link
