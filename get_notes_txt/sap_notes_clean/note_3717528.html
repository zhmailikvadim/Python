Symptom

  • Users attempting to access the SAP Incentive Management Portal to view dashboards or reports via Salesforce web tab integration are encountering an issue
  • The web tabs fail to load, on which users are prompted with a pop-up to grant access to their browser storage.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
    • Incentive Management
    • Incentive Management on Hyperscalers

Resolution

This issue can be resolved by enabling third-party cookies in the browser where the application is being accessed.

Steps to Enable Third-Party Cookies:

  1. Open the browser settings.
  2. Navigate to the Privacy and Security section.
  3. Under Cookies and other site data, ensure that Third-party cookies are allowed.
  4. Once enabled, refresh the Salesforce page, and the web tabs should load without issues.

Additional Notes:

  1. This issue is typically caused by browser security settings that block third-party cookies. Enabling third-party cookies will allow Salesforce to properly interact with SAP Incentive Management for seamless dashboard and report viewing.

  2. If the issue persists after enabling third-party cookies, try clearing browser cache or using an incognito window.
