Symptom

If you have maintained regular payment on IT0442, the calculation was not correct for the first period of tax year .

Other Terms

RPCCAR01, payroll, BIK

Reason and Prerequisites

The factoring of regular payment was incorrect for the first period of tax year.

Solution

The calculation of the aliquot factor on the first period of the taxyear is corrected.

To implement the changes in your system, apply the correction instruction of this note. Alternatively, apply the HR Support Package indicated for your release under Reference to Support Packages.
