Symptom

You use SAP Travel Management for S/4 HANA Fiori application "My Travel and Expenses for Business Traveler" or "Travel and Expenses for Travel Assistant".

Any trip with a receipt amount set to zero automatically clears the cost assignment at the receipt level, setting it to zero regardless of the user’s entry.

Other Terms

MTE, Travel Expenses

F6190, F0584B

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Import the correction instruction or apply the corresponding support package.
