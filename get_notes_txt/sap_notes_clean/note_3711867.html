Symptom

After completing all the configurations as per the Test Script BFC - Collection and Dispute Management, the items are still not displayed in the apps "Manage Collection Worklist" and "Supervise Collection Worklist." 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Resolution

The relevant settings need to be maintained in SSCUI 103757 - "Activate Distribution per Company Code."

See Also

KBA 3418918 - Generate Business Partner Collection Worklist manually
