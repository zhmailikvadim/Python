Symptom

Zero lines missing in g/l view (table ACDOCA) despite the lines existing in the entry view (table BSEG).

Other Terms

FAGL_SPLINFO,  DELETE_ITEMS_ACDOC_PROJECTED, BUZEI

Reason and Prerequisites

Zero lines are deleted in the g/l view although BSEG-BUZEI in not initial because the BUZEI is not checked correctly due to a program error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or upgrade to the latest support package.
