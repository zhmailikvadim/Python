Symptom

When you execute the report ELStAM PHI: Process Notifications  (RP_PAYDE_E2_PKV_PROCESS_NOTIFS), notifications with the following error message are not processed automatically:

HRPAYDE_E2_PKV 023: "IT79 (<BEGDA>-<ENDDA>) must be maintained manually due to a zero notification".

Reason and Prerequisites

The affected messages are as follows:

  1. <HI Allowance Monthly Contribution="0"/>
    <HI Pension Monthly Contribution="0"/>
    <CI Allowance Monthly Contribution="0"/>
    <CI Pension Monthly Contribution="<Euro Amount>"/>

  2. <HI Allowance Monthly Contribution="<Euro Amount>"/>
    <HI Pension Monthly Contribution="<Euro Amount>"/>
    <CI Allowance Monthly Contribution="0"/>
    <CI Pension Monthly Contribution="<Euro Amount>"/>

  3. <HI Allowance Monthly Contribution="0"/>
    <HI Pension Monthly Contribution="<Euro Amount>"/>
    <CI Allowance Monthly Contribution="0"/>
    <CI Pension Monthly Contribution="0"/>

  4. <HI Allowance Monthly Contribution="0"/>
    <HI Pension Monthly Contribution="<Euro Amount>"/>
    <CI subsidy monthly contribution="<euro amount>"/>
    <CI Pension Monthly Contribution="<Euro Amount>"/>

In addition, mid-year transitions from the statutory health insurance fund to the private health insurance fund are affected. Their PHI data is as follows:

<KVPVContributions ValidFrom="01" ValidTo="06">

<HI Allowance Monthly Contribution="0"/>
<HI Pension Monthly Contribution="0"/>
<CI Allowance Monthly Contribution="0"/>
<CI Pension Monthly Contribution="0"/>

</KVPVContributions>

<KVPVContributions ValidFrom="07" ValidTo="12">

<HI Allowance Monthly Contribution="<Euro Amount>"/>
<HI Pension Monthly Contribution="<Euro Amount>"/>
<CI subsidy monthly contribution="<euro amount>"/>
<CI Pension Monthly Contribution="<Euro Amount>"/>

</KVPVContributions>

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified support package.

After you import the HR Support Package or implement the correction instructions, you can automatically process the notifications that still have the status To Be Checked  or To Be Processed  by executing the report again. The infotype SI Suppl. Ins.  (0079) is updated accordingly.

Prerequisite for the automatic processing of notifications 1-4:

In the infotype Social Insurance D  (0013), the HI and CI indicator must be maintained with No Insurance Obligation  (0).
