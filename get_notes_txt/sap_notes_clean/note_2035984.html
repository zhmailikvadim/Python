Symptom

In case of a non-accruing contract the interest accruals shall not be posted versus P/L but versus an offset account (1BF_TRANSO). This is not possible.

Also the calculation procedure templates for change of the accruing status (1117 and1118) are not sufficient to cover this use case.

Other Terms

1BF_ACCR, 1BF_TRANSO, 1J08, 1J09, 1JB6, 1JB7

1117, step type 1A05

1118, step type 1A01

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the manual correction instruction (i.e. implement the BC Set) or the respective support package.
