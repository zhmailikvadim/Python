Symptom

I want to enable the data center for India on SAP Business Network for Managed Gateway for Spend & Network.

Resolution

As per the current product functionality, SAP Business Network supports EU/US/CN/UAE and KSA (public and private), except for the India data center due to design level limitation.

If you require this functionality to be available in standard product, feel free to raise an product enhancement request.
