Symptom

Incorrect Product Allocation or Supply Protection quantity assignments after rejection of a sales order item.

Other Terms

aATP; ATP; Product Allocation; PAL; Supply Protection; SUP; Characteristic Catalog; VBKD; Customer Group

Reason and Prerequisites

The characteristic value for the customer group (or other VBKD fields) on header level is not found in the call to the ATP to notify about the rejection.

Solution

Apply the correction instructions for this note or upgrade to an SAP S/4HANA support package that contains this correction.
