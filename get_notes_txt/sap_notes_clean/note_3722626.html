Symptom

When attempting to delete a custom form, I receive the following error message:
"This form is currently active. You cannot delete 'xxxx-xxxx'."

Cause

This issue occurs because the custom form you are trying to delete is still in an Active state. Active forms cannot be removed until they are deactivated.

Resolution

To delete the custom form, first deactivate it by following the steps below:

    1. Navigate to Manage → Core Admin.
    2. In the left navigation pane, select Forms and Extensions Manager.
    3. Click Forms and Extensions.
    4. Locate the form you want to remove.
    5. Click the Actions button next to the form.
    6. Select Deactivate.

Once the form is deactivated, you can return to the same menu and delete it successfully.
