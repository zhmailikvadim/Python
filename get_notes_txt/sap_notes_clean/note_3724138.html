Symptom

This SAP Note contains two changes with regard to personnel cases with voluntary insurance for an agricultural health insurance fund:

  • The option to determine the fixed contributions from the master data file
  • The consideration of the average additional contribution in the allowance calculation for health insurance

Determination of Fixed Contributions from Master Data File

If the contributions for voluntarily insured persons are calculated according to fixed values, these must be entered in the infotype Social Insurance D (0013) in the Cont. Cl./Amt HI and Cont. Cl./Amt PV. For the agricultural health insurance fund, these fixed values are contained in the master data file and are managed in the Payroll Constants table (T511K) as of 1/1/2026 under the following constants:

  • HI Farmer's Fund (Bkl 20) (KVLF0)
  • PV Farmer's Sec. w/o Parents (PVLF0)
  • PV Farmer's Sec. with Parents (PVLF1)
  • CI Farmer's Char. 2 Children (PVLF2)
  • CI Farmer's Day 3 Children (PVLF3)
  • CI Farmer's Day 4 Children (PVLF4)
  • CI Farmer's Day 5 Children (PVLF5)

In the infotype Social Insurance D (0013), after you implement this SAP Note, the fields Cont. Cl./Amt HI and Cont. Cl./Amt CI for infotype records with a start date after 1/1/2026. In payroll, the fixed contributions are then determined from the constants mentioned above. For care insurance, this depends on the number of children and parent characteristic of the personnel case.

Consideration of the average additional contribution for the allowance calculation for health insurance

No additional contribution is taken into account during the allowance calculation for health insurance for personnel cases that are voluntarily insured with an agricultural health insurance fund. As a result, the allowance for health insurance may be too low as of 1/1/2024. Since the agricultural health insurance funds do not have an individual additional contribution rate, the average additional contribution rate is to be taken into account.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified support package.

After you implement this SAP Note, you may have to change the contribution calculation of the agricultural health insurance funds to fixed value calculation as of 1/1/2024. This is defined in view cluster V_T5D11. The Fixed Value Calculation value should be defined in all four entries for the contribution calculation. Up to 1/1/2026, the fixed values for the affected personnel cases in the infotype Social Insurance D (0013).

Perform retroactive accounting for affected personnel cases to 1/1/2024.
