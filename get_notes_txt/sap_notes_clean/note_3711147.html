Symptom

You are working with Treasury and Risk Management. You use BAPI to create or change Total Return Equity Swap deals. The field Planned Amount in tab Dividend cannot be changed or created correctly.

Other Terms

BAPI_FTR_TRES_CREATE, BAPI_FTR_TRES_DEALCREATE, BAPI_FTR_TRES_CHANGE, BAPI_FTR_TRES_DEALCHANGE, VTB_SDIVIDEND_TRES, get_tab_field_mapping, LFTR_APIF01

Reason and Prerequisites

Programming error.

Solution

Please implement the SAP Note or the Support Package.
