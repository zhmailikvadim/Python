Symptom

If a Commodity Management logistics document with CPE condition types is being revaluated , but the respective conditions are not maintained in CMM_MTM_CALC then any error on this document or its subsequent documents should not be triggered via unnecessary queues . These error queues are ambiguous since its non-risk relevant.

Other Terms

CMM_MTM_CALC, non-risk relevant conditions , CMM_VLOGP, SBGRFCMON queues

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a performance improvement correction to avoid trigger of unnecessary queues in case respective condition types are not maintained in CMM_MTM_CALC.

Prerequisites:

You are using Commodity Mangement module S4CORE 1709 and above releases .

Solution

Implement the attached corrections.
