Symptom

In the processes "End of Supply for Gas (DSO View)" (DE_B_GELIGAS_EOS_DSO) at step 1700 and "End of Supply for Electricity (DSO View)" (DE_B_GPKE_DSO_EOS) at step 1500, the system incorrectly ended the future supplier contract when the following conditions were met:

  • The ending contract had a different business partner than the future contract
  • An SBS process existed with a process key date equal to the future contract start date

After ending the future supplier at step 1800, the system sent market message 44038 to the future supplier.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

End of Supply for Gas, End of Supply for Electricity, DE_B_GELIGAS_EOS_DSO, DE_B_GPKE_DSO_EOS, SBS, future supplier, market message 44038, ZT6, ZT7, /US4G/CL_CHK_GPKE_PROCESS~CHECK_FUTURE_SUPPLIER

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because the system was missing functionality to check the transaction reason of the SBS process before ending the future supplier contract.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the EOS process only ends the future supplier if all of the following conditions are met:

  • The future contract has a different business partner than the current contract business partner
  • An SBS process exists with a process key date equal to the future contract start date
  • The SBS process has transaction reason ZT6 (EoGDueToCancellationBySUPO) or ZT7 (EoGDueToCancellationByCustomerOrSUPN)

You can verify the fix by testing an EOS process and checking that the system only ends the future supplier when all three conditions are met.
