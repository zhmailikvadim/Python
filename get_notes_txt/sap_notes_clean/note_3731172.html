Symptom

The warning "Unresolved contact id" is displayed in the account's contact factsheet for deleted contacts in Engagement Cloud.

Environment

SAP Engagement Cloud

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Contacts.
  2. Open the Corportate Accounts.
  3. Open the account where the contact's related were deleted.
  4. Open the Contacts tab.
  5. You see only the ID of the contact with the warning "Unresolved contact id".

Cause

In case the Corporate Account data has been imported via the AEDI integration, the contact is deleted from Engagement Cloud and from Data Integration and the Id is stored in a technical list to prevent an undesired re-replication in case the contact is modified again in Sales Cloud. The Corporate Account data factsheet shows an "Unresolved Contact ID" warning, but this is just temporary.

In AEDI, we bookmark the Corporate Account whose contacts have been deleted, we recompute its attributes (including all contact relations) and we replicate it again in one of the next iterations of the Replication Run.

We can't give you a precise estimate about the time delay between deleting a contact in Emarsys and having everything in-sync again on the Corporate Accounts factsheet. The Replication Runs are executed every ~5 minutes but there might be other Sales Cloud accounts which have been changed in the meanwhile and have to be re-replicated as well.

Resolution

This is the expected system design.

The information about the contact deletion process for AEDI integration is explained in the documentation Deleting a Contact in SAP Emarsys.
