Symptom

  • I am creating a new supplier invitation
  • I want to understand what the Consolidated Invoice for Contingent and Consolidated Invoice for Services fields mean

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Sign into SAP Fieldglass
  2. Navigate to Admin menu (gear icon) > Click Supplier Invitation
  3. Click New > Scroll to the bottom of the page to locate the fields for Consolidated Invoice for Contingent and Consolidated Invoice for Services

Cause

Resolution

These fields determine whether the buyer will pay the supplier via a consolidated invoice or individual invoices

If Yes is selected:

  • You can combine all types of invoices into one Consolidated Invoice record and pay them all together
  • The individual invoices will update to Pending Consolidation status after final approval, then Consolidated status once the Consolidated Invoice is created

If No is selected:

  • This indicates that you would like to pay each invoice to the supplier separately
  • The individual invoices will go into Approved status after final approval, where they can then be marked as Paid

Considerations:

  • These fields can be updated after the supplier registers, but we advise finalizing this decision before the supplier begins invoicing
