Symptom

  • When trying to view the compensation group membership, it is not showing all members.

       

      

Environment

  • Compensation

Reproducing the Issue

  • You are trying to view compensation group membership when going into admin tools->Manage Families And Groups and clicking on the option to show compensation membership you do not see all the members of a compensation group.

Cause

  • The maximum number of items under Show compensation group membership is 1000 entries. You will need to export the groups to see the complete list of members.

Resolution


Currently it is an expected behavior that the maximum number of items under show compensation group will have 1000 entries. If a group has more than 1000 entries, it will not show all the members of the group.

  • Go to admin tools->Compensation group export
  • Export the groups from the above item it will show a complete list of members for each group

Keywords

1000 entries, show compensation group membership, maximum, Manage Families And Groups
