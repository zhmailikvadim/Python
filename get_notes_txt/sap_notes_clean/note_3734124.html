Symptom

Devices are incorrectly classified as Device Information record (DIR) instead of real Device during processing. This occurs when device and device information record (DIR) shares the same serial number, and evaluation is done based on serial number only.

Other Terms

/APEU, /UCOM, Device, Device information record, DIR, serial number, Device category, equipment number, /UCOM/IF_ISU_INSTLN~GET_DEVICE_REGISTER_DATA, /UCOM/IF_ISU_INSTLN~GET_DEVICE_REG_DATA

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error, APEU 2602

Solution

Please implement the attached correction instruction.

Equipment(Device and Device information records) are uniquely identified with combination of Material and serial number. Accessing it's details only based on serial number leads to incorrect results. The correction has been done to consider material number as well to get correct details.
