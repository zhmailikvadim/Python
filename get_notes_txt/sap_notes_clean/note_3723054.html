Symptom

I want overdue receipt reminder emails to begin only after the Need-By Date + 30 days. The reminders should be sent daily and continue indefinitely until the receipt is completed. Additionally, these reminders should not trigger any escalation to the supervisor. 

Environment

Notification

Cause

Overdue receipt notifications are controlled by the Warning Period and Escalation Period configuration, not solely by the Need-By Date.

Resolution

Daily reminders are sent only between the configured warning and escalation thresholds. Once the escalation threshold is reached, the system either sends a one-time escalation email to the supervisor or stops notifications if escalation is disabled or suppressed.

The system does not support sending daily reminder emails indefinitely without escalation, and there is no separate parameter available to define a custom overdue delay period beyond the standard configuration.

If escalation notifications should not reach the supervisor, the supervisor can be added to the No Escalation group. Additionally, there is no configuration or group available in the system that prevents specific users from receiving all email notifications.
