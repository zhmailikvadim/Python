Symptom

  • I have a requirement to increase the API rate limits.
  • We are experiencing issues due to exceeding the current rate limits. We would like to request an increase of the limits for these APIs.

Resolution

SAP Ariba Product Support does not manage requests to increase API rate limits. As outlined in SAP Note 2706322, our technical customer services are primarily focused on addressing technical or functional issues with an SAP product.

The API rate limits are determined by the engineering team to ensure the quality and stability of our services. This practice is standard across REST APIs.

If you have already explored the API to its maximum performance, validated and optimized its usage accordingly and still require a rate limit increase to achieve your objective, you must collaborate with your Customer Success Partner (CSP) or Customer Success Manager (CSM), by following these steps:

  1. Contact your CSP/CSM and provide the following information:

    • Application Key (apiKey): 
    • Realm: 
    • Type of API (e.g., Analytical Reporting): 
    • Justification for the rate limit increase: 

  2. Your CSP/CSM will then communicate these details to the API Product Owner, who will review your request and make a decision. For further reference about the internal processing, SAP Note 3188010 (internal only) can be shared with your CSP/CSM.

  3. If the request is approved, the Product Owner will coordinate the necessary internal adjustments to modify the rate limits accordingly.

You can ask the CSP/CSM to include the ticket processor in copy of the internal email chain, along with the API Product Owner, and this person can also help to coordinate the process.
