Symptom

Within the worklfow log variant "Summary (HTML)" the section "Information objects addressed so far" does not include all notes or SAP-Office attachments, that should be part of the section.

Other Terms

Workflow log, SWWLOGHIST, objects and attachments, SOFM

Reason and Prerequisites

Due to a pogram error log entries that are vital for the determination of relevant objects were partially not written or in other cases not considered.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or install the corresponding support package.
