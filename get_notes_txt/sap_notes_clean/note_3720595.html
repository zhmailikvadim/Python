Symptom

Since year 2025 the field DNDOP for evaluation of income in SK up to 90% is no longer used in ATR due to an update of evaluation process. However, report HSKCDPHP was not updated to reflect this change. As a result, the report may incorrectly evaluate the value from ATR for income in SK up to 90% (P0257-TTFB405).

Other Terms

HRSK, HSKCDPRZ, HSKCDPHP.

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Slovakia (HRSK).

Solution

This SAP Note delivers corrections described in the chapter Symptom.
