Symptom

  • Possibility of starting the Payroll Control Center process instance in the background via a scheduled job instead of manually clicking "Start" under Upcoming Processes in the Process Management screen.

Environment

  • SuccessFactors
  • Payroll Control Center (PCC)
  • Human Capital Management 

Cause

Standard system behaviour

Resolution

Currently there is no standard tool available to start PCC process instance via scheduled job in the backend.

Keywords

payroll control center, PCC, scheduled job, background process, process management, fiori, start process instance, automation, payroll activities
