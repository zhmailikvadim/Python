Symptom

A lead with status as converted is still displayed as an active lead in the account highlights section.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Cloud Version 2
 



Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Navigation Menu
  2. Go to Accounts
  3. Open an account
  4. In the Highlights section, there is an active lead
  5. Click on the active lead
  6. The status shows converted

Cause

The internal status of the lead is "accepted," but it is associated with a custom status named "converted", which causes it to appear as active in the highlights section.

Resolution

This is the current system behaviour. When the lead is converted to the account, only then the internal status changes to Converted. A custom status with name "converted" would not have the internal status as "converted", hence the lead would be considered as an active lead
