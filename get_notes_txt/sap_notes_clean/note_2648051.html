Symptom

The "My Paystubs (Version 3/Fiori 2.0)" SAP Fiori app does not select the HR form from the feature HRFOR ("HR Forms Decision Feature") on the basis of the organizational data from the infotype 0001 ("Organizational Assignment"). These fields were not previously evaluated.

Other Terms

ODATA, GBX01HR5, IT0001, P0001, HRFOR, CESS, PE03, PMEHF, WERKS, BUKRS, PERSG, PERSK

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason:

Program error.

Solution

The corrections from this SAP Note ensure that the relevant fields in the characteristic structure are filled and evaluated.

 

Import the relevant Support Package into your system.

For an advance correction, implement the attached correction instructions.
