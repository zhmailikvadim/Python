Symptom

In the process "Preparation and Waiting for Sending of Meter Reading (MOS MeLo View)" (DE_B_MET_PREPARE_SEND_MR_MOSMELO), step 0300, the wrong meter reading (MR) reason is determined for combined move-out/move-in scenarios with periodic meter reading at the move-out date.

Other Terms

MOS, DE_B_MET_PREPARE_SEND_MR_MOSMELO, Preparation and Waiting for Sending of Meter Reading, process step 0300, /US4G/CL_PRST_DET_MR_ORDER->GET_MR_ORDERS, meter reading reason, combined move-out/move-in, periodic meter reading, MeLo View

Reason and Prerequisites

The wrong meter reading reason is determined in /US4G/CL_PRST_DET_MR_ORDER->GET_MR_ORDERS.

Solution

After the fix is installed, the correct meter reading reason is determined for combined move-out/move-in scenarios with periodic meter reading at the move-out date.

You can verify the fix by reprocessing step 0300 to retrieve meter reading results and checking that the correct meter reading reason is determined.
