Symptom

When using the BAPI_CONDITION_CONTRACT_CREATE, the system returns the following short dump:

Runtime Errors         UNCAUGHT_EXCEPTION
Except.                CX_WCB_FATAL_ERROR
ABAP: Program          CL_WCB_CC_FACTORY=============CP

Environment

  • Condition Contract Maintenance
  • Settlement Management
  • Agency Business
  • Condition Contract Management
  • Condition Contract Settlement
  • SAP S/4 HANA

Cause

The issue occurs due to duplicate entries being passed in CONDITIONKEYDATAIN, which leads to a short dump as the system cannot distinguish between the conditions.

Resolution

Adjust the test data to ensure no duplicate entries are passed in CONDITIONKEYDATAIN.

Keywords

BAPI_CONDITION_CONTRACT_CREATE, UNCAUGHT_EXCEPTION, CX_WCB_FATAL_ERROR, CL_WCB_CC_FACTORY=============CP, CL_WCB_CC_FACTORY=============CM018, SET_FINAL_KNUMH_IN_KNUMH_GUID
