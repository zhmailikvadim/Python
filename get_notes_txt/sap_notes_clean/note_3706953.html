Symptom

  • Maintain Employees app is not working after the upgrade SAP S/4HANA 2020 or higher
  • Maintain Employees V2 app is not loading, it throws error on the screen
    failed to load 'sap/nw/core/changedocs/lib/reuse/changedocscomponent/Component.js' from /sap/bc/ui5_ui5/sap/nw_aps_chd_lib/~XXXXXXX~Y/changedocscomponent/Component.js: 403 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA 2020 or higher

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login to the Fiori Launchpad and search for the app Maintain Employees
  2. Click on the app

Cause

ICF node nw_aps_chd_lib is not activated in the system. 

Resolution

  1. Login to the SAP GUI and execute SICF t-code
  2. Enter the path /sap/bc/ui5_ui5/sap/nw_aps_chd_lib in the Service Path
  3. Click on Execute, right click on the node and click on Activate Service
