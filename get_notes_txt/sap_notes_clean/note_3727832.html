Symptom

When users sign into their accounts in COMP2, a banner appears at the top of the page displaying: This environment is being decommissioned and will no longer be available after 03/31/2026. Click here for details on SAP Fieldglass pre-production environment offerings and contact your SAP Fieldglass representative to discuss alternatives before the decommissioning date. 

Environment

COMP2 US and COMP2 EU

Cause

These environments are being decommissioned and will no longer be available after March 31, 2026.

Resolution

Please make appropriate plans to upgrade to a sandbox environment ahead of the decommission date or access the COMP1 Environment for testing.
