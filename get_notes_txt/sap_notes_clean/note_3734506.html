Symptom

The Worker has already returned an asset and needs to have it removed from their profile.

Cause

To enable the option for Users to remove assets, you will need to adjust the settings in the User Role section to grant the necessary permissions. As you can confirm in the next section.

Resolution

How to give permission to an User to remove Assets:

  1. Sign in as an administrator.
  2. Click on the Admin gear icon.
  3. Select "User Role" under the "User" box.
  4. Search for your User Role.
  5. Click on the "Edit" button.
  6. Search for "Asset Type"
  7. Make the necessary changes.
  8. Click to confirm.

Now you can remove an Asset following the steps below:

  1. Sign in your SAP Fieldglass Account
  2. Search for the Worker ID of the affected Worker
  3. Select "Rates & Assets"
  4. Click on "+Add Assets" 
  5. Unmarked the provided Asset and remove
  6. Confirm the update
