Symptom

You update the WBS element in the general ledger, and you have included the field ZZPS_PSP_PNR in the corresponding G/L journal entries and totals tables. During the selection for these tables with a ZZPS_PSP_PNR initial value, the system does not display any data, even though it exists.

Solution

Implement the advance correction and regenerate the CDS views by executing the program FGL_MIG_REGENERATE_CDS_GL.
