Symptom

The filter by company does not work correctly when the Employment tax (IRPF) reports program (RPC190E0) is run with accounting data retrieved from the QSTRE table and the log shows results corresponding to other companies.

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP ERP Core Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Execute program RPC190E0 filtering by legal person in the 'Selection of results' box and selecting the 'Accounting data' checkbox to retrieve data from the QSTRE table.
  2. The output contains entries which corresponds to a different legal person.

Cause

The unexpected legal person results are due to the configuration in Personnel Area/Subarea (T001P) and Personnel Areas (T500P) tables, which are checked in the following routine:

*---------------------------------------------------------------------*
*       FORM FILL_TAB_BUKRS                                           *
*---------------------------------------------------------------------*
*       Rutina de actualizacion tabla interna TAB_BUKRS               *
*---------------------------------------------------------------------*
FORM fill_tab_bukrs
  CHANGING ct_company TYPE ty_t_bukrs.

* Atención: no se comprueba si un juper de los seleccionados corresponde
* a una sociedad distinta a las seleccionadas por este procedimiento

  SELECT * FROM t001p WHERE juper in juper
                        AND molga EQ pesu0_molga.
*   Selección de todas las divisiones de personal asignadas al centro
*   de abono / persona jurídica seleccionado
    SELECT * FROM t500p WHERE persa EQ t001p-werks.
*     Selección de todas las sociedades pertenecientes a las divisiones
*     de personal asignadas al centro de abono / persona jurídica
*     seleccionada
      COLLECT t500p-bukrs INTO ct_company.
    ENDSELECT.
  ENDSELECT.

ENDFORM.                    "fill_tab_bukrs

Resolution

Check the consistency of the entries in tables T001P and T500P associated with the company indicated in the filter and make the necessary adjustments.
