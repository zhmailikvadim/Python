Symptom

You are in the nursing service entry function and change the time of an ordered service.
You then select this service to perform or release it.
The selection of the service is, however, lost, and you receive the error message:

Select at least one entry. Message no. N1BASE032

You cannot document the service as performed or released, without selecting the line again.

Other Terms

Nursing, service, selection

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Import the specified support packages to solve this problem.
You can implement composite SAP Note 3043577.
