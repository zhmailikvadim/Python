Symptom

Returned with with error "Date is not a workday" & "Start data may not fall after end date" from App ID F4073

Environment

  • SAP ERP Plant Maintenance (PM)
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

Open Fiori App ‘Manage Maintenance Backlog (F4073) 

Click on ‘Orders in Preparation’ sub tab.

Select maintenance order and from change scheduling option click on assign maintenance event.

Enter maintenance event , select check box ‘Copy Dates From Event’ and click on ‘Assign to maintenance event’.

Click on application log and open the latest entry.

You can see returned with error "Date is not a workday" & "Start data may not fall after end date"

Cause

During time zone conversion of end date time, start date time was passed as value

Resolution

Please implement note 3706743Manage Maintenance Backlog returned with error "Date is not a workday" & "Start data may not fall after end date"

Keywords

"Date is not a workday" & "Start data may not fall after end date"
