Symptom

The inbound process of an STO with Advanced Shipping and Receiving may fail when handling units are used. The error can occur when setting the status "Ready for Warehouse Processing" on the freight order. As a result, the inbound delivery remains blocked.

Other Terms

ASR; RfW; /SCMTMS/CL_TM_LE_DLV_COMPARE;

Reason and Prerequisites

Due to a program error, TM triggers a delivery update including HU data during the STO inbound process. Since HUs are in status "In transit" at this point, the update is rejected.

Solution

Implement the corrections of the attached correction instructions or apply the corresponding support package. This prevents the system from attempting to pass HU data during delivery updates, as this is not supported in this process step.
