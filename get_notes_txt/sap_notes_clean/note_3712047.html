Symptom

This SAP Note gives a summary of legal changes, corrections and enhancements that are available with the country-specific legal change packages below for HCM Italy (components PA-PA-IT, PY-IT, and PY-IT-PS):

  • S4HCM ECC 102:SAPK-10201INS4HCMCIT
  • S4HCM ECC 101: SAPK-10127INS4HCMCIT
  • S4HCM ECC 100: SAPK-10040INS4HCMCIT
  • SAP_HR EnhP6.08: SAPK-608F1INSAPHRCIT
  • SAP_HR EnhP6.04: SAPK-604M3INSAPHRCIT

Other Terms

SAP_HRCIT; Support Package; CLC; HR; legal change;

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisite

Prerequisite is the installation of the Synchronization HR Support Package (or the last available CLC Package) that is relevant for your release and listed below:

ReleaseSupport Package
S4HCM 102 SAPK-10205INS4HCMCIT
S4HCM 101 SAPK-10131INS4HCMCIT
S4HCM 100 SAPK-10044INS4HCMCIT
SAP_HR 6.08 SAPK-608F5INSAPHRCIT
SAP_HR 6.04 SAPK-604M7INSAPHRCIT
