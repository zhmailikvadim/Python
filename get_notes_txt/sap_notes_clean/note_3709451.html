Symptom

Update March 19, 2026: Supplement to retroactive accounting in the solution area.

As of 1/1/2026, the allowance calculation in pension insurance changes for persons insured in a professionals pension organization in the transition area. Up to now, the allowance for pension insurance was not calculated in the same way as a person with statutory insurance in the transition area. Only the assessment remuneration GSV contribution was multiplied by half the PI percentage. This now changes and the calculation takes place in the same way as for a legally insured person in the transition area.

Example:

Reg. Remuneration 1280,00 €
Assessment Remuneration GSV Contribution 1174,93 €
Assessment Remuneration EE Share 969,22 €
Total PI Contribution 218,54 €

Allowance calculation:

Allowance up to December 31, 2025 Allowance as of 1/1/2026
€1174.93 * 9.3% = €109.27 969.22 * 9.3% = 90.14 €
  218.54 € - 90,14 € = 128,40 €

Other Terms

Social insurance, transition area, midijob, professionals insurance

Reason and Prerequisites

New version of Sec. 172 a SGB VI due to the second law to strengthen the company pension scheme and to change other laws (Second Company Pension Act).

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified support package. After you import the HR Support Package or implement the correction instructions, you do not have to perform any further follow-on activities.

Retroactive accounting must be performed for affected personnel cases as of 1/1/2026.
