Symptom

In the My Time Events Fiori 2.0 Application, the Edit and Delete buttons are not visible in the Recorded Event List on the Quick Entry tab on small screens in portrait mode.

Other Terms

F1506A, Edit, Delete, Buttons, Mobile Devices, Portrait Mode, Quick entry tab, Recorded Event List

Reason and Prerequisites

Due to limited screen width on small screens in portrait mode in the My Time Events Fiori 2.0 Application, the Edit and Delete buttons are not visible in the Recorded Event List.
