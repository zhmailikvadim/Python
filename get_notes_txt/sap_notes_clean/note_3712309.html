Symptom

This SAP Note is relevant only for the country version Switzerland (CH).

It contains the following adjustments in fee-based billing to support billing of outpatient flat rates:

  • Enhancement of table IS-H CH: Parameters for Outpatient Flat Rates (TNWCHAMBPAU) by adding a tariff column for the tax points of billing and a rate column for the tax points that are to be used for the fees for outpatient flat rate services.
  • Adjustment of fee determination so that the tax point value for fees is used for outpatient flat rate services
  • Adjustment of fee posting: For services for outpatient treatments with flat rates, you must post to the CO order for outpatient treatment. For outpatient treatments without flat rates, you must post to the case-based order.

Other Terms
