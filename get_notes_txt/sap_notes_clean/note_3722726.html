Symptom

While creating invoices, the following errors appear:

"Field is required, Service Start Date"

"Field is required, Service End Date"

Cause

The error requesting for the service start and end date is related to a business rule that is active on buyer side: "Require suppliers to provide start and end dates on service invoices".

Resolution

The rule can be disabled on buyer side in order to proceed without informing the date, or the purchase order can be sent again by the buyer, but informing that this is a material purchase order in the cXML (it cannot be defined as "regular").

Keywords

Field is required, Service Start Date, Service End Date, invoice creation, invoice error, date, service
