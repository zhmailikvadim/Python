Symptom

A major case occurs on HANA Cloud environment and has been fixed. C-RCA (Customer-RCA)is required.

Environment

  • SAP HANA Cloud

Cause

Major case 

Resolution

SAP has provided fix to the major case, you can close the incident as per SAP Note ##1239839 - How to close a support case - SAP for Me.

Regarding the RCA, which serves as the official C-RCA communication, you can check its availability through the Systems & Provisioning dashboard in SAP for Me, or your email inbox. Typically, C-RCA communications are sent to customers via email. If you do not see it yet, we kindly ask for your patience, as it may take a couple of days for the communication to be sent out.  

See Also

1239839 - How to close a support case - SAP for Me.
