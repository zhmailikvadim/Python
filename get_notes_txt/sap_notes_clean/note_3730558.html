Symptom

Synchronization between SAP LeanIX System "***"and Joule System das-application-ias has failed. – Joule System das-application-ias returned a technical error for assignment with ID "****-****":"Formation creation failed. To integrate Joule with "***" system in the EAC (Early Adopter Care) Program, please contact your Account Executive" 

Environment

Joule

Cause

Early Adopter Care Program needs to be enabled for the global account

Resolution

  • It is required to contact Account Executive or Account Owner to enable the `Early Adopter Care Program` for your account.
  • Please note that Technical Support does not have access to enable `Early Adopter Care Program` for your account, as this functionality is exclusively managed by Account Executives. If you encounter any delays in receiving a response from them, feel free to update me here in the ticket.
