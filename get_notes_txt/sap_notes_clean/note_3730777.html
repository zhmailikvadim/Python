Cause

The failover was not caused by SAP HANA itself, but rather by a Pacemaker cluster infrastructure failure that occurred few seconds prior to the HANA System Replication disruption as the traces confirmed.

Resolution

As the traces confirms that the takeover happened after the pacemaker was killed with signal 9 so, please contact your third party cluster team to check this issue further for the root cause why the failover was triggered because the failover is not caused by HANA itself.
