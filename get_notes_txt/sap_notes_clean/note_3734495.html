Symptom

App Change Interest Rate Instrument (TM_52) and Settle Interest Rate Instrument (TM_54) are no longer available or accessible. 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Attempt to navigate to TM_52 (Change Interest Rate Instrument) or TM_54 (Settle Interest Rate Instrument) in the public cloud system.
  2. Observe that these apps are not available or cannot be accessed.

Cause

The classic app Change Interest Rate Instrument (TM_52) and Settle Interest Rate Instrument (TM_54) are no longer available for use in the public cloud.

Resolution

Use app Manage Financial Transactions to create, change, and settle Interest Rate Instruments; the functions of TM_52 and TM_54 are embedded in this app.
