Symptom

During the determination of the preference indicator for billing documents and sales orders, the recipient country is determined incorrectly for document addresses. Instead of the recipient country of the document address used, the system incorrectly uses the recipient country of the standard address of the relevant partner.

As a result, the relevant preference agreements are determined incorrectly, which leads to the derivation of an incorrect preference indicator.
