Symptom

  • Unable to change posting date in nomination ticket processing.
  • The system automatically resets the posting date to previous date .
  • A warning message is displayed when attempting to change the posting date.

Environment

  • S/4 HANA
  • SAP ERP

Reproducing the Issue

1.) Open the Tcode - O4NM and enter the nomination key

2.) Double click on ticket 

3.)  Attempt to change the Posting Date to the required date.

4.) The system automatically resets the date and displays a warning message, not allowing the change to be saved.

Cause

The behavior is caused by an active standard BAdI implementation(OIJ_MODIFY_POSTING_DATE ), which automatically determines and resets the posting date based on predefined business logic.

Resolution

  1. The posting date behavior is controlled by the BAdI OIJ_MODIFY_POSTING_DATE .
  2. If this behavior is not required:
    - Deactivate the standard BAdI implementation, or 
    - Create and activate a custom BAdI implementation as per business requirement(After deactivating the standard one)
  3. Configuration path:
    SPRO > Industry Solution Oil & Gas (Downstream) > TSW  (Trader's and Scheduler's Workbench) > Tickets > Maintain Business Add-Ins for Ticket Processing > BAdI: Modify Posting Date
  4. This allows controlling the posting date logic as per business needs.

Keywords

O4NM, Posting Date, OIJ_MODIFY_POSTING_DATE, SAP Industry Solutions Oil and Gas (IS-OIL)
