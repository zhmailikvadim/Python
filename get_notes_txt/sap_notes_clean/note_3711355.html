Symptom

On the HR Renewal/ Mashup screen of infotype 0088, the 'Leave period 2' field (LVESD2) appears for all subtypes in display mode - with the exception of subtype SNP. This field, however, is specific to subtype SBP (for Statutory Parental Bereavement Leave and Pay) and should not appear or any other screen.

Other Terms

HR Renewal, ECP, mashup, IT0088, infotype 0088, subtype SAP, SCPA, SCPB, SMP, SPPA, SPPB, STPA, STPB, Leave period 2, LVESD2

Solution

EITHER

Apply the relevant HRSP

OR

Carry out the steps in the attached file, "Manual Instructions for SAP Note 3711355.pdf".
