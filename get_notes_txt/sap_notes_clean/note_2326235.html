Symptom

You did contract modification to a Revenue accounting contract with a fully fulfilled POB, after change a flag called 'HAS_PRO_CHANGE' is lost for this POB, you can not find anything wrong from UI until you do following changes, due to this bug revenue calculation will be wrong afterwards.

Other Terms

FARR, Revenue Accounting, Contract Modification, HAS_PRO_CHANGE

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Apply this note.

POB's has_pro_change is marked as 'X', but POB's change flag is still ' ', so this flag is lost during update database.
