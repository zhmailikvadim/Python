Symptom

Customers having installed and enabled the Roadmap version of Revenue Distribution are experiencing the following issue in Dynamic Settlement Diversity:

In the scenario, where the base formula is given precedence over the alternate virtual formula, no heating value is allocated to settlement owners.

Other Terms

General keywords: Add-on, IS-Oil, Industry Solution, PRA, Production and Revenue Accounting, IS-OIL-PRA, Oil and Gas Upstream, Roadmap

Problem-specific keywords: Valuation, Revenue Distribution, Dynamic Settlement Diversity, Settlement Owner, Settlement Heating Value Factor, BTU Factor, Higher-of Virtual Formula, Alternate Virtual Formula, Base Formula.

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Settlement heating value does not populate when the base formula wins over the alternate virtual formula. This leads to zero heating value allocation to the settlement owners.

Corrections are valid for SAP PRA S/4 HANA OP2023 Release and above.

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instructions to resolve the issue.
