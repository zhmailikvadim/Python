Symptom

You notice errors while transferring subcontracting data (scheduling agreement, purchase requisition, purchase orders etc.) to (e)PP/DS.

Known symptoms are

- a short dump in /SAPAPO/CL_MC01_CLCU (MESSAGE_TYPE_X, assert_  application_error).

Other Terms

/SAPAPO/CL_STOWC, /SAPAPO/DM_ORDER_IS_TRANSFER

Reason and Prerequisites

STOWC class does not properly check if the current scenario is a subcontracting scenario.

STOWC is active.

There is fixed pegging.

Solution

Implement this Note or the corresponding support package.
