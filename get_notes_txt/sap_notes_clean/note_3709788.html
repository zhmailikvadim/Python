Symptom

  • Error message: "VIEW V_ICA_DS_CP_T is inconsistent in active version."
  • Error message: "Check view V_ICA_DS_CP_T (xxxx)."
  • Error message: "Field ICA_DS_CP-DERIVE_TCURR does not exist."
  • Error message: "View V_ICA_DS_CP_T is not consistent."

Environment

S/4HANA Intercompany Matching and Reconciliation

Reproducing the Issue

  1. De-implement SAP Notes related to central procurement in S/4HANA version 1909.
  2. Attempt to activate the view V_ICA_DS_CP_T.
  3. Encounter error messages indicating inconsistency in the view and missing field ICA_DS_CP-DERIVE_TCURR in the table ICA_DS_CP.

Cause

  • The inconsistency in the view V_ICA_DS_CP_T is caused by the absence of the field ICA_DS_CP-DERIVE_TCURR in the table ICA_DS_CP.
  • The field was removed during the de-implementation of SAP Note 3004288, leading to activation issues.

Resolution

  1. Verify the implementation status of SAP Note 3004288 in the system.
  2. Re-implement SAP Note 3004288 to restore the field ICA_DS_CP-DERIVE_TCURR in the table ICA_DS_CP.
  3. Ensure that dependent SAP Notes are implemented in the correct order to avoid inconsistencies.
  4. Test the activation of view V_ICA_DS_CP_T after re-implementing the note.
  5. If further issues persist, contact SAP Support for additional guidance.
