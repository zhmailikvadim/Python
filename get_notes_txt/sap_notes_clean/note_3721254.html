Symptom

eDocument generation fails for customers outside Germany when using the Germany Email/XRechnung solution. 

Environment

  • SAP ERP 
  • SAP S/4HANA 
  • SAP Document and Reporting Compliance Service

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Configure the Germany Email/XRechnung solution in the SAP system. 
  2. Attempt to generate an eDocument for a customer located outside Germany. 
  3. Observe that the eDocument is not generated.

Cause

The Germany Email/XRechnung solution is designed exclusively for customers located in Germany, adhering to German regulatory requirements. 

Resolution

  1. The Germany Email/XRechnung solution is exclusively designed for customers located in Germany (DE). 
  2. Sending an XRechnung document to customers outside Germany is not supported in the standard solution due to German regulatory requirements. 
  3. Refer to the SAP Help documentation for further details: Electronic Customer Invoices (Submission by E-Mail) | SAP Help Portal
