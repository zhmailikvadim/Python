Symptom

Error 'Table 163 is not defined for use with condition type RCA1' when creating or changing a condition contract in F5989 Manage Condition Contracts – External Commission Fiori app.

Environment

Condition Contract Management in SAP S/4HANA

Cause

Missing configuration.

Resolution

Go to transaction WLFA > Settlement Management > Condition Contract Management > Condition Contract Maintenance > Condition Contract Types > select '****'(condition contract type) and under control section, change the 'Activate Approval Process' to 'A Approval process is active'.

Keywords

Table, 163, RCA1, Condition Contract, Settlement, Manage Condition Contract, External Commission, F5989, release, FIORI, pop up
