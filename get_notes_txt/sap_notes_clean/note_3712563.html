Resolution

In the NullPointerException we have a call from: com.sap.lm.itsam.ui.floorplan.FloorPlan.class ->com.sap.js.nwa.cfg.Repository.class
 
The involved SCA components are:

  • LMNWAUIFRMRK
  • LM-CORE

If it does not help, re-deploy the SCA's. redownload the above mentioned SCAs from the SAP for Me website and redeploy the same files (same SPS and Patch)
