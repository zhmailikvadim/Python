Symptom

  • The purchase requisition was initially submitted successfully with the Unit of Measure (UOM) set as "X".
  • When attempting to amend the purchase requisition it receives UOM error.
  • Error message: "Unit of Measure error:Y".

Cause

The issue is related to a change in UOM mapping that occurred where the existing mapping "X" to "Y" was deactivated, causing discrepancies in ERP.

Resolution

  1. Reactivate the correct UOM mapping to resolve the issue.
  2. Update the UOM mapping as follows:
  • "UTF-8"
  • Comment, Value, Key, Preferred
  • "g", "Y", "Y", "No"
  • "g", "Y", "X", "Yes".
