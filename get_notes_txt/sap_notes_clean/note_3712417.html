Symptom

 When a promotion is locked or in a non-editable state (e.g. released), it is not possible to view the complete list of products/categories available as of today in the product hierarchy. The products or categories assignment block in the admin section only display the products/categories that are added to the promotion.

Other Terms

Product Picker, read only promotions, read-only category level promotions

Reason and Prerequisites

The product picker is capable of providing the complete list of product/categories however it is not accessible when the promotion is locked or in non-editable state.

Solution

The correction instruction provided in this note enables accessing product picker when the promotion is locked or in non-editable state.
