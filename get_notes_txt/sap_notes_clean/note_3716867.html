Symptom

During the system license measurement process, the measurement module calculates incorrect results for license-relevant quantities in the livestock and production areas of SAP Meat and Fish Management.

This may lead to inaccurate license consumption values.

Other Terms

IS-MFM, License measurement, SAP Meat and Fish Management by msg.

Reason and Prerequisites

Under certain conditions, the system determines incorrect license-relevant quantities during the periodic measurement process.

Solution

History:
SAP Note created on 26.02.2026.

Version 2: Text correction

Description of Correction:
The correction ensures that license measurement is executed consistently and according to the defined license metric for the affected components.

Functional Dependencies:
None.

Manual Changes:
No manual changes are required for end users.
