Symptom

In the CDS view generation of Line Item Browser, the custom amount or quantity fields are not getting generated with the appropriate annotations like @Semantics.amount.currencyCode or @Semantics.quantity.unitOfMeasure. As a result, errors occur during the activation of the CDS view.

Other Terms

Line Item Browser(LIB); FGL_LIB_* ; FAGL_LIB_REGENERATE_CDS;

Reason and Prerequisites

The reason for such error is that, system did not find the reference field used for the custom field in the data model and the appropriate annotation could not be added to the view definition.

Solution

Please implement the note.
