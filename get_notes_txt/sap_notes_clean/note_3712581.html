Symptom

  • A SOW Line Item is in Approved status.
  • The Next Step field states Supplier needs to create an invoice.
  • The Supplier does not manually create invoices for SOW Line Items.

Cause

The Auto Invoice field on the SOW Line Item is set to No.

Resolution

To ensure the SOW invoices automatically, a user with the Reject Approved SOW Line Item User Permission enabled for their User Role or an Administrator can do the following:

  1.  Reject the Line item via the Reject
  2.  Once the SOW Line Item has been rejected, the Supplier can resubmit the SOW Line Item on their end.
  3. During the resubmission process, the Supplier can set the Auto Invoice field to Yes.
  4. After being resubmitted, the SOW Line Item can once again be approved by the Buyer.

Once approved, an invoice will be automatically generated for the SOW Line Item.
