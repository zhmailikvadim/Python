Symptom

When clicking on the notification for Bank Account Approvals in 'My Inbox' the navigation fails with the following error: "Sorry, the app couldn't be opened".

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

The business user does not have the required authorizations to navigate to the target inbox via 'My Inbox'.

Resolution

Assign the business catalog SAP_CA_BC_MYINBOX_PC to the business role that is assigned to the business user.
