Symptom

You use Transaction Manager of Treasury and Risk Management. You execute TBB1 to process posting for a financial transaction. For the payment detail to be used, the bank chain is maintained with IBANONLY without bank account. The system may get incorrect bank chain information for the payment.

Other Terms

TRCA_BANKCHAIN_DETERMINE, FI_BL_BANKCHAIN_DETERMINE, IBANONLY, I_IBAN_HB, I_IBAN_PB

Reason and Prerequisites

Functionality Enhancement.

SAP Note 2670919 and 3565063 have been implemented or included in your system release.

Solution

Preliminary correction via Correction Instruction or implementation of the corresponding Support Package.
