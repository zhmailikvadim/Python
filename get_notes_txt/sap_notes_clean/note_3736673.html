Symptom

The data extraction service (Document Information Extraction) for the "Create Sales Orders - Automatic Extraction" app currently supports purchase order files in English or German.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

Currently, for purchase order files to be uploaded into the "Create Sales Orders - Automatic Extraction" app, only English and German are supported by the data extraction service. 
In addition, the data extraction service is not available in mainland China and Middle East due to local policies and regulations.

Resolution

To support more languages, it is recommended to use the "Create Sales Orders - AI-Assisted Extraction" app to create sales orders from purchase order files. The "Create Sales Orders - AI-Assisted Extraction" app uses the SAP Document AI (Embedded Edition) for data extraction. Compared with the Base Edition, the Embedded Edition of SAP Document AI supports more languages and smarter document processing capabilities.
