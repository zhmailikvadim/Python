Symptom

When posting goods receipts for correction inbound deliveries, the item number is incorrectly mapped for the created Material Document.

Other Terms

ASR@GTS, /SAPSLL/CD_MM0C_R3, CORR_IND, corrections, MM0C_APICUWL_ITEM_FILL, inb_del_item_crrtn

Reason and Prerequisites

The problem is caused by a program error.

Solution

Implement the correction instructions for your release.
