Symptom

You're using Financials Predictive Accounting for sales orders that have multiple delivery dates scheduled for an item under the Schedule Lines tab in sales order maintenance. After you successfully post goods issue documents, you manually change the timelines of the schedule plan.

After having applied note's 3642199 correction instruction or implemented the corresponding service packs, the timelines of deliveries are reflected in financial table ACDOCA and thus the corresponding reductions are recorded with correct BUDAT values. However, dependent date fields like fiscal year (GJAHR), posting period (POPER), and translation date (WWERT) are not changed accordingly, leading to potential inconsistencies between the document dates and the dependent date fields within the predictive ledger(s) only. This can happen if predicted deliveries are re-distributed among schedule lines with BUDAT changes crossing borders of posting periods, or fiscal years.

Other Terms

 FI-PRA-PRA

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason is a program error. This error affects only the prediction ledgers.

Solution

 Apply the attached correction instructions.
