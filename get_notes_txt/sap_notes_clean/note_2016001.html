Symptom

Exception condition 'NOT Qualified' occurs when transferring changes for planned materials from ERP to SCM with the intial load.

Other Terms

1267791, FM /SAPAPO/DM_MAT_GET_MATLOCID, FORM MAINTAIN_REL_MATLOC, CIF, FM /SAPAPO/CIF_PROD_LOC_INBOUND

Reason and Prerequisites

Programming error in form MAINTAIN_REL_MATLOC, as no need to call FM /SAPAPO/DM_MAT_GET_MATLOCID in case internal table lt_matloc_id is empty. Instead, processing of form MAINTAIN_REL_MATLOC should be skipped.
