Symptom

  • Error occurs when attempting to manage team settings in SAC Role: "Search for the available teams has failed. Correlation ID: xxxx."
  • 400 error from sap/fpa/services/rest/security/enterprise-search call in console log and HAR trace

Environment

  • SAP Analytics Cloud Enterprise 2026.2.6

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to SAC -> Security -> Roles
  2. Click on the Role which is causing issue.
  3. Click on "Teams".
  4. Observe the error message: "Search for the available teams has failed. Correlation ID: xxxxxx."
