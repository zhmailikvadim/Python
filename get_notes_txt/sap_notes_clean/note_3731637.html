Symptom

The transaction "Transfer Posting from Building Savings Contract" (/IBS/BSC_MB_REBOOK) does not enable a transfer posting from an active building savings contract to a G/L account. The system issues the error message "No construction savings contract number transferred".

Reason and Prerequisites

A validation in the method "Save/Check (with Simulation) Transfer Posting" of the BO of the transfer posting also checks whether a BSG number was specified for a G/L account as a target account.

Solution

The method SAVE_REBOOK of the BO is corrected.
