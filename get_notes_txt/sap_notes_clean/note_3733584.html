Symptom

Users are not able to see any information related to the Purchase Order Accruals when using My Inbox app. 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open My Inbox app - All items (Fiori app).
  2. Select the PO (Purchase Order) and Click on Review PO accrual item.
  3. No details displayed.

Cause

The capability to review purchase order accruals and propose accrual amounts is delivered via the business catalog SAP_FIN_BC_POAC_WRKITM_PC by standard system design; without this catalog, users cannot access the review work items.

Resolution

  1. Assign the business catalog SAP_FIN_BC_POAC_WRKITM_PC (Purchase Order Accruals – Process Review Work Items) to the roles of users who must review accruals and suggest revised planned costs.
  2. Be aware that there is no alternative authorization path for this function; the catalog is mandatory for access.
  3. The price category of this catalog cannot be changed; this is standard system design.
