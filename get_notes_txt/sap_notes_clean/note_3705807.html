Symptom

Destination's Tab in BTP Cockpit shows "Not Authorized".

Environment

SAP Business Technology Platform

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the SAP BTP Cockpit.
  2. Navigate to the Destination's Tab.
  3. Observe the "Not Authorized" message displayed.

Resolution

This issue has already been identified by the Destination Service team and a permanent solution will be deployed to all regions in the coming days.

In the meantime, as a workaround, open the BTP Cockpit via the URL from the APAC region: https://apac.cockpit.btp.cloud.sap/.
