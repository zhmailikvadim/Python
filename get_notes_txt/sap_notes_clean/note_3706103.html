Symptom

While trying to use the Fiori app navigation from Joule in the Finance area for accounting document/Journal entry, you see that some of the apps are not displayed for navigation.

Other Terms

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition, Joule, Capabilites, 2601, finance, navigation

Reason and Prerequisites

You want to get additional information about a specific bugfix of SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition Joule Capabilities 2601.

There were some applications defined under the scenario 'Accounting Document' and some under 'Journal Entry'. Now they are merged into 'Journal Entry' and additional terms are maintained as synonyms.

Solution

The issue described as the Symptom is solved in the SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition Joule Capabilities 2601.
