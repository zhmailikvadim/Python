Symptom

The current solution uses a schedule line storage (schedlin_i), which allows entries to be made for different types of events. As a result, schedule lines that are not related to ORDER-type events are also considered during Reserved Material processing. This leads to sending incorrect Reserved materials’s quantities to FSM.

Other Terms

FSM, E4C, Connector, S/4 Service Order, Schedule lines, Reserved Materials

Reason and Prerequisites

Incorrect logic in Schedule Lines storage mechanism during processing of Reserved Material.

Solution

The Schedule line check is no more valid for Reserved Materials process. Please, implement this correction instruction.
