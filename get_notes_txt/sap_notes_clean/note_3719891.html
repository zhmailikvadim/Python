Symptom

Business Scenario:

A PF product is admitted into a Foreign-Trade Zone (FTZ) under an HTS code, along with any additional HTS codes, that are valid at the time of admission. Prior to the product's withdrawal from the zone, the HTS codes expire and are superseded by a new ones. U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) mandates that the Forecast and Entry Summary documents include details pertaining to the expired HTS codes, as they were declared during the admission process.

You may meet the symptoms below:

  • When you try to create weekly entiries for FTZ, the system shows error message as "No data selected from table /SAPSLL/CTSHR".
  • The supplementary units of measure are missing from both the UI and the IDoc during communication with ACE

Other Terms

US, Entry for consumption, weekly entry, Privileged Foreign Status, Duty Calcuation, Declaration Date, Import Date, free circulation from third country,

Create Domestic Stock Issues from Foreign-Trade Zone,  /SAPSLL/CUSWLZ_01, /SAPSLL/CTSHR, /SAPSLL/CORE_BASIS202, Tax Date, /SAPSLL/CORE_BASIS

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Implement the correction instructions relevant for your release.
