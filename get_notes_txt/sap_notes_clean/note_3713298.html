Symptom

You define a color for each business status in application "Define Business Statuses". However, in the application "Monitor Transfer Document", the system does not show the predefined color for the business status.

Other Terms

APE, Business Status, Color, F7708A

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error in the status color mapping in the system.

There is also an associated backend Note 3707999 for this issue.

Solution

 Implement this SAP Note
