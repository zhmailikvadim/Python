Symptom

Currently in the system, for IT0185 subtype 01 (National Id), the first digit of National Id (2 or 3) is validated based on IT0185 begin date, whereas it should be on birth year of an employee.

Other Terms

Infotype: 0185

Class: CL_HRPA_INFOTYPE_0185_QA

Module Pool Program: MP018500

Reason and Prerequisites

 Correction of Legal function

Solution

This note corrects the above symptom by validating the ID number of IT0185, subtype 01 (ID card) based on year of birth of an employee.

Example: The ID number must now begin with “2” if the birth year of an employee is prior to 2000 and it must begin with “3” if the birth year of an employee is on or after 2000.

Apply the relevant HRSP to get the changes. Alternatively, apply the correction instructions attached with this note to get the changes immediately.
