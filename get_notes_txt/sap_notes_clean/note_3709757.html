Symptom

  • I need to remove an SOW Revision in Draft status 
  • SOW was Rejected and now I need to Remove the SOW Revision
  • SOW Revision was incorrectly started and I want the SOW to be reverted back to the previously approved revision

Cause

A Buyer user can Remove an SOW Revision in status of Draft or Rejected when:

  • The SOW Defined by is Buyer 
  • The SOW Defined by is Buyer and Supplier and the supplier has not responded to it

Resolution

You can Remove the Draft Revision by following:

  1. Click View > Statement of Work
  2. Open the Statement of Work
  3. Click Actions > Remove
  • If you do not have the Remove action, you can contact your Program Management Office (PMO) for confirm you have the necessary permissions.
  • If you have the necessary permissions and yet is not able to Remove the SOW Revision, click Contact Us from the top left of this article to submit a support case.
