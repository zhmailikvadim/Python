Symptom

In app Manage Chart of Accounts, it shows "No data available" under Chart of Accounts view.

However, these accounts are visible in app Manage G/L Account Master Data.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open app Manage Chart of Accounts
  2. Under Chart of Accounts view, select one of the Chart of Account and enter
  3. It shows "No data available" under the selected COA

Cause

The implement status is set to "N(Not used)" or "I(In Process)" for G/L accounts under the affected COA. 

Resolution

Change the implement to "P" and then save the changes. Then the G/L accounts will be visible in app Manage Chart of Accounts under the COA view. 

Keywords
