Symptom

When running the Cash Flow report one or both following errors are displayed:

Current account cannot be defined as cash account

or

Checking account cannot be defined as Cash account

Environment

  • SAP Business One
  • SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to module → Finance → Financial Reports → Financial → Cash Flow.
  2. Click "OK" to generate the Cash Flow report.
  3. Observe the error message: Checking Account cannot be defined as Cash Account.
  4. The system opens the chart of accounts window, pointing to the accounting account.

Cause

Two main reasons:

  • In the Chart of Accounts window, the system does not allow the Checks Received account to be categorized as a Cash Account.
  • Under Administration → Setup → Financials → GL Account Determination → Sales tab → General tab, the Checks Received and Cash on Hand accounts are defined with the same value.

Resolution

  1. Ensure that the account defined as Checks Received is not categorized as a Cash Account.
  2. Verify the configuration under Administration → Setup → Financials → GL Account Determination → Sales tab → General tab.
