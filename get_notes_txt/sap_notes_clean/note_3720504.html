Symptom

  • My Job Postings in the system show dollar amounts in an unexpected way
  • How can I change the format of numbers in the system?
  • I see commas where I would usually see decimal points on Job Postings, Work Orders, SOWs, or Worker records

Resolution

In order to change the Number Format that governs how numeric values are displayed in your account

  1. Sign in to your SAP Fieldglass account
  2. Click the circle with your initials and select Settings
  3. Scroll to the Language & Region section and click Edit in the corresponding box
  4. Select the desired format from the Number Format dropdown
  5. Click Update
