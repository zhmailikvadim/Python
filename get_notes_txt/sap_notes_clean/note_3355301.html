Symptom

If a medication kit is packed into a handling unit (HU) and the field 'GLN Extension' (GLT_EXT) has a storage bin before the posting of a good receipt (GR), then after posting a GR, the medication kit is unpacked from the handling unit (HU) by mistake and the field 'GLN Extension' (GLT_EXT) in ATTP still has the storage bin and isn't cleared.

Other Terms

ISLS, Intelligent Clinical Supply Management, GLN_EXT, GLN Extension, ATTP, posting GR, medication kit

Reason and Prerequisites

 You use SAP Intelligent Clinical Supply Management or this SAP Note is a prerequisite of another SAP Note.

Solution

Implement the attached correction.

Then, after posting of a GR, the medication kit is still packed in the HU and the field 'GLN Extension' (GLT_EXT) in ATTP is empty.
