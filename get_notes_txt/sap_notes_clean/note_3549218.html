Symptom

On the printout of the spool the back side of the paper is printing mirrored.

Environment

SAP NetWeaver, all releases
SAP S/4HANA on-premise, all releases
SAP ABAP Platform, all releases

Cause

Tumble Duplex(Flip on Short Side) is set in the printing configuration.

Resolution

Validate the Duplex configuration as per SAP KBA 1565113 - Duplex Printing and use regular Duplex(Flip on long side).

See Also

1565113 - Duplex Printing

Keywords

Duplex, regular duplex(Flip on long side), Tumble Duplex(Flip on Short Side), mirror, back page.
