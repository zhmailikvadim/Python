Symptom

When attempting to create payment requisition for non-China business in the app Create Payment Requisitions. The following error message is displayed: "No payment block keys are defined for payment requisition. No reversal reasons are defined for payment requisition."

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the app Create Payment Requisition.
  2. Click Create and the following error message is displayed: "No payment block keys are defined for payment requisition. No reversal reasons are defined for payment requisition.".

Cause

Missing required configuration activities "Specify Payment Block Reason and Reversal Reason for Payment Requisitions".

Resolution

This app standard is tailored for China-based business operations. Cross-country usage is supported with supplementary configuration "Specify Payment Block Reason and Reversal Reason for Payment Requisitions". Since this is non-standard, expert configuration support must be obtained by contacting your CSM/CEE/PE, who will coordinate your request. 

Keywords

payment requisition, payment block key, reversal reason
