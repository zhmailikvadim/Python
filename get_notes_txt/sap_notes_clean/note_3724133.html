Symptom

You declare your exports from SAP GTS to the Swiss customs system Passar.

In Passar, items that do not mix supplementary items with regular packages are allowed. SAP Note 3633877 enhances outbound processing for customs declarations that were created via integration from the feeder system and whose items contain both supplementary items and regular packages. In outbound processing, the supplementary items from these mixed items are reported to Passar as a separate item.

The additional quantity (<supplementaryUnits>) is not distributed to the item created in this way. In this way, the additional quantity is reported several times. This SAP Note corrects this so that the additional quantity is distributed according to the packed quantities for each package.

Solution
