Symptom

During tenant recovery using BACKINT the recovery does not progress and eventually stops with errors similar to:

recovery could not be completed
Found service neither active nor starting for recovery

The issue may occur when executing:

RECOVER DATA FOR <tenant> USING SOURCE '<SID>@<SID>' USING BACKINT ('<backup_id>') CLEAR LOG;

HANA traces may show that the BACKINT response occurs significantly later than the recovery start timestamp.

Environment

SAP HANA DB Version 2.0

Cause

During tenant recovery SAP HANA waits for responses from the BACKINT interface before continuing the recovery workflow.

If the BACKINT implementation responds with a significant delay, SAP HANA may remain in a waiting phase for an extended period. When the internal waiting period is exceeded, the tenant recovery may stop and errors such as:

"Found service neither active nor starting for recovery"

may be reported.

The delay is typically related to the external backup infrastructure or the third-party BACKINT implementation.

Resolution

If the recovery stops with the behavior described above, verify whether the BACKINT interface responds with a significant delay during the recovery operation.

Compare the timestamps between the SAP HANA traces and the third-party BACKINT logs.

Since BACKINT is implemented by third-party backup providers, the backup software vendor must be involved to investigate delays in the BACKINT interface or backup infrastructure.
