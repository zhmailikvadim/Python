Symptom

* WARNING: This is an IS-OIL / IS-MINE specific note. If you DON'T have   
* IS-OIL / IS-MINE installed on your system, this note does not apply to  
* you. If this note is applied & you don't have IS-OIL / IS-MINE installed,
* you could cause serious damage to your system.

When you navigate to a material document from 'Ticket log' tab of a ticketing transaction, it's displayed with most recently executed action and reference type.

Example: After posting a cancellation document in MIGO, you create a ticket that is in error status, even though a material document was posted as part of the process. When you try to view the document from the ticket’s Log tab in the ticketing transaction, the system opens the Cancellation screen instead of the standard Display Material Document view.

Other Terms

Keywords: IS-OIL, Ticket, Ticket log, Material document, O4TGN, MIGO_DISPLAY, Include MOIJTNF04, FORM JUMP_TO_DOCUMENT, Display, Action

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the correction instructions.
