Symptom

Note: This note is only relevant for IDXCH2 Customers!

This note contains the following feature developments:

- Linkage to PoD (hotspot) on tab "PoD Assignment List" for Process 1112

- Additional checks for PoDs:

   (1)  In case the PoD is assigned to another DSO, a new message "PoD assigned to another DSO" should be displayed. (Instead of "Missing PoD in own System")

   (2)  In case the PoD is assigned to another Supplier, a new message "PoD assigned to another Supplier" should be displayed. (Instead of "Missing PoD in own System")

Other Terms

IDXCH2

Reason and Prerequisites

IDXCH2 SP09 or lower

Implement this note or IDXCH2 SP10
