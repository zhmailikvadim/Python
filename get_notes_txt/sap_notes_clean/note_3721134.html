Symptom

  • The e-verify activity was automatically generated on the same day.

Environment

SAP Successfactor Onboarding 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the Onboarding Dashboard in SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding.
  2. Validate the e-verify activity for the employee.

Resolution

  1. Confirm that the "Complete e-Signature on Compliance Forms" step was completed on the specified date.
  2. Verify the e-verify activity generated on the same day.
  3. Complete the e-verify activity for the employee, as per the standard e-verify process flow:

             - Candidate is moved to Onboarding.  

             - Personal Data Collection (PDC) is completed.  

             - Section 1 of the I-9 is completed by the new hire.  

             - Section 2 of the I-9 is completed by the employer/HR.  

             - I-9 is signed and finalized.  

             - E-Verify case is automatically created
