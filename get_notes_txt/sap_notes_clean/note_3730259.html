Symptom

During device removal (DE_B_IM_DEV_REMOVAL), period consumption is populated via SET_BILLING_ATTRIBUTES, leading to a perceived change in period consumption during rate change, which triggers validation errors.

Other Terms

APEU,2508

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error , Device Remoal , SET_BILLING_ATTRIBUTES,

Solution

Adjusted logic to prevent reading/updating of period consumption during rate change; period consumption remains unchanged in this scenario.
