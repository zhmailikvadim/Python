Symptom

Roadmap information towards WCAG compliance from SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence (BI)

Environment

SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence (BI) 4.3 / 2025

Resolution

SAP is committed to improving the accessibility of its products for people with disabilities.
To support this goal, SAP has defined the SAP Accessibility Standard, based on internationally recognized guidelines such as Section 508 of the U.S. Rehabilitation Act and WCAG 2.1. New products are expected to follow this standard as closely as possible, and it will continue to be updated in line with global accessibility requirements.

The prioritization of accessibility improvements is based on usage frequency, customer demand, and technical feasibility.
Functions that are widely used and have the biggest impact on customer experience are addressed first to ensure that the work delivers maximum value.

As products continue to evolve, SAP will gradually enhance accessibility in future releases and deliver these improvements through standard product updates.
This means accessibility capabilities will naturally grow throughout the product lifecycle, and customers do not need to take any additional steps.
