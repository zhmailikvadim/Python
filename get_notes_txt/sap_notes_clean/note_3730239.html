Symptom

You want to understand the logic of Verifying Electronic Supplier Tax Invoices

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Resolution

The system logic is designed to work as follows:

Once SAP DRC receives the Incoming Source Document with the Purchase Order ID provided by the vendor, the user can generate a supplier invoice for the corresponding purchase order by performing the task outlined in Help Portal| Verifying Electronic Supplier Tax Invoices. This process ultimately generates the Accounting Document. 

It’s worth noting that some customers utilize Incoming Source Files without a Purchase Order ID to meet specific business requirements. While this approach may work for certain scenarios, it falls outside the scope of the support we provide.
