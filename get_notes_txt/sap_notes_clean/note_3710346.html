Symptom

  • I got an email with my username from the automated assistant, but when I enter it in the assistant to reset my password I receive the message: I couldn't find an account associated to that username. Check your entry for accuracy and try again or choose Forgot username. You have up to 4 attempts to retry your username. 
  • I know my username is correct, but when I try to reset my password it says there is no account associated to it
  • I retrieved my username but cannot reset my password because my account can't be found

Cause

You may be signing in to a different gateway, where your credentials are not recognized because your account data is not stored in that instance of Fieldglass. 

Buyer users will have a URL that is specific to the buyer: xxxx.us.fieldglass.cloud.sap or xxxx.eu.fieldglass.cloud.sap (where xxxx is your company code). If you are a worker or supplier and you try to sign in to this URL, your credentials will not be recognized.

Resolution

Please confirm you are signing in to the correct URL for your location, user type and company

  • EU Fieldglass: workers will use the EU SAP Fieldglass Network URL: www.eu.fieldglass.cloud.sap

  • US Fieldglass: workers will use the US SAP Fieldglass Network URL: www.us.fieldglass.cloud.sap

  • All AE instances users: https://www.fieldglass.ae/
  • All AU instance users: https://www.fieldglass.com.au/

Please note that even if you are based in the US, if your company's data is stored in the EU instance of Fieldglass you will sign in to this gateway and vice versa. 

If you have any questions about which instance of Fieldglass to sign in to, please contact your staffing agency to confirm this information.
