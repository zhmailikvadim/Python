Symptom

  • In process DE_B_GELIGAS_EOS_DSO at step 8920, the waiting period is calculated incorrectly, causing wrong deadline determination.
  • The expected waiting period is 9 working days before the assignment start when E/G is in the past, but the standard BaDI fallback calculates 6 weeks instead.

Environment

  • sap s/4hana utilities master data updates option for Germany
  • sap s/4hana 2022

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the market communications process document DE_B_GELIGAS_EOS_DSO in SAP Fiori.
  2. Observe that step 8920 is active and that the waiting period is calculated as 6 weeks instead of 9 expected working days.

Cause

The logic in /US4G/BADI_PERIOD_TYPE (fallback method /US4G/CL_DEF_BADI_PERIOD_TYPE=>/US4G/IF_BADI_PERIOD_TYPE~DET_PERIOD_TYPE) sets trans_reason_code_e01 to “MoveInOut” based on UBD data, which results in a 6‑week timeframe. For gas scenarios with other transaction reasons, the duration is 9 days.

Resolution

This behavior aligns with the expected logic from SAP's perspective. For the gas division, however, if the transaction reason falls within the range specified between lines 15 and 18 (other reasons than MoveInOut), the duration would adjust to 9 days, as anticipated in your scenario.
