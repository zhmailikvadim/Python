Symptom

A locked contract in FI-CA becomes unlocked after executing a change process from SOM.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA

Resolution

The lock applied to this contract is the Invoicing Lock on the FICA side instead of through the LOCK/UNLOCK change process from SOM side. This scenario can be checked by the following ways,

1) The contract is existing in table DFKKLOCKS,

Example: Table: DFKKLOCKS -> LOOBJ1: *100000000001
-----------------------
CLIENT   100
LOOBJ1   00000000100000000001
LOTYP    20               
PROID    
LOCKR    V                 <<< example only
-----------------------

2) In T-code: FP_VT3, it has the 'Invoicing Lock' under the 'Invoicing' block of 'General Data'.

3) No entry exists in table: CRMS4D_CONT_LOCK.
 
• Table: CRMS4D_CONT_LOCK > OBJECT_ID: fill the Subscription Contract Document ID > No entry

That is to say, all changes applied to the contract in FICA are purely local and will be overwritten when the contract is redistributed again. If the customer wants to lock a provider contract, the standard process is through the LOCK/UNLOCK change process from the SOM side, and when the contract is locked in SOM, it is not possible to perform any change process for this contract until unlocking it. The details can be found in Lock/Unlock | SAP Help Portal.
