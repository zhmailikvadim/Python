Symptom

When a document is initially sent to the GTS system, the audit trail correctly displays the external partner number. However, the external partner number is missing in the audit trail report after the partner address is overwritten and the document is retransferred.

Other Terms

/SAPSLL/SPL_OAT_DOC, /SAPSLL/SPL_OPT_AUDIT_DOC, /SAPSLL/SPL_OPT_AT_DOC_READ, ADRV

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Apply the correction instruction in this note or upgrade to relevant support package.
