Symptom

As of 2019, it is important for the calculation of wage tax / national insurance contributions ("loonbelasting / premie volksverzekeringen") that all employees have a fiscal area ("Fiscaal gebied"). Determination of the fiscal area is decisive for whether or not the tax part of the tax credits can be applied. To this end, we distinguish three fiscal areas in the world: The Netherlands, EU / EEA Plus Countries (the so-called "de landenkring" mostly made up by the other European Union (EU) countries and countries in the European Economic Area (EEA)), and the Other Countries (the rest of the world).

To assign a fiscal area to a country, you use the table V_T5N77. This table can be found at the IMG Personnel Management -> Personnel Administration -> Payroll Data -> Tax -> Maintain Fiscal Area; or at Payroll Netherlands -> Tax -> Maintain Fiscal Area. SAP delivers this table containing the entries for The Netherlands, and the EU / EEA Plus Countries. Notice that we deliver T5N77 with a preset of countries. Prerequisite for the good behavior of the table is that the countries we deliver, are present in your customizing of table V_T005 (IMG Personnel Management -> Personnel Administration -> Basic Settings -> Create countries/countries of birth/nationalities).

The fiscal area can be maintained in a new field infotype Fiscal Data NL (0060), called Fiscal Country of Residence (P0060-FISCO). You can also use the new parameter Subtype Inforype 6 (SUBTY) for payroll function Import Wage Tax Data (NL) (P0060). You use this parameter to specify which subtype of infotype Addresses (0006) should be read to determine the fiscal area.
Notice that if you enter an invalid subtype, the system will proceed without issuing any warning. In case no country is registered in infotype 0060, nor a valid subtype of infotype 0006 is specified, the system (payroll function Calculation of Wage Tax [LH] (NL) (NST00)) sets the fiscal area to the default value The Netherlands (NL). The country stored in infotype 60 has priority over the one in infotype 6.
The determined fiscal area is stored in a new field in the cluster table ST (PC228-FISCA). Eventually the fiscal area is used in the Tax calculation to determine which tax table is to be used.

Notice that the county for determination of the fiscal area is also available in the Gross/Net Calculation Simulation Tool (NL) (report ﻿RPCNETN0).

Other Terms

MP006000, CL_HRPA_INFOTYPE_0060, PC228, T5N77, PC00_M05_CNET, PC00_M05_CALC

Solution

Import the HR Support Package specified for your release. Check on the SAP Support Portal under SAP ERP HCM Support Package Schedules for the availability of the relevant HR SP and possible dependencies.
