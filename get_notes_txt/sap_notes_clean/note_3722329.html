Symptom

Customers who have installed and activated the Roadmap version of Revenue Distribution are experiencing incorrect tax allocation behaviour.

For the state of Montana, Royalty Tax (RT) is being allocated to an exempt entity, which is not the intended system behavior.

Other Terms

General Keywords: Add-on, IS-Oil, Industry Solution, PRA, Production and Revenue Accounting, IS-OIL-PRA, Oil and Gas Upstream, Production

Problem-Specific Keywords: IS-PRA-XT, Valuation, Revenue Distribution, Montana, Exempt Owner, Royalty Tax, RT, Pay Code, Pay, Suspense

Reason and Prerequisites

Royalty Tax (RT) should only be allocated to Royalty Owners.

However, when the DOI (Division of Interest) deck contains:

  • An exempt entity with Pay Code = Pay
  • A non-exempt royalty owner with Pay Code = Suspense

the system allocates the RT tax difference to the exempt owner.

This issue is identified as a side effect of SAP Note 2301745.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions to resolve the issue.
