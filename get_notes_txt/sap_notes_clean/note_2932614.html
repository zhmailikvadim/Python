Symptom

After transport, Favorite of My View is unchecked.

Environment

Fiori Launchpad

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create Public View and it is in list of My View
  2. Transport to another Client
  3. In the target client, created Public View is not in list of My View
    It exits in Manage Views screen and Favorite is unchecked

Cause

This behavior is designed.

Resolution

If you flag a view as favorite this setting is enduser-specific (that means only valid for your user).
And enduser-specific personalization cannot be transported.
You have to flag the view as favorite in the target system/client of the transport again.
