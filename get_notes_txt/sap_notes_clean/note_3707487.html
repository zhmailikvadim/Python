Symptom

Why isn't the UBL attachment available on Business Network for Belgium tax invoice flow?

Environment

SAP Business Network.

Resolution

For Belgium tax invoices flow, the buyer and supplier account need to be based on Belgium. Additionally, for Invoices submitted from integrated system, the PostalAddress segments on the Contact roles of Invoice need to be of a Belgium Address.

See Also

For more information on country specific tax invoice, please check Tax Invoicing in Belgium | SAP Help Portal.
