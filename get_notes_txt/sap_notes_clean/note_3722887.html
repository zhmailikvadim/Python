Symptom

  • The BAdI EPIC_APPROVAL_CONTROL is enhanced to define the approval workflow for EPIC.
  • When items are submitted for approval, they do not appear in the approver's inbox.

Environment


  • EPIC China
  • China

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Enhance the BAdI EPIC_APPROVAL_CONTROL to define the approval workflow.
  2. Submit items for approval.
  3. Check the approval inbox; the submitted requests are not displayed.

Cause



Resolution

  1. Open the Fiori APP "Manage Workflows for Payment Items Approval-China".
  2. Find the pre-delivered workflow Predefined Workflow Content and deactivate it.
