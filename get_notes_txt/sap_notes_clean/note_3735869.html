Symptom

  • The Time Sheet's hours are supposed to be calculated by day, but the Time Sheets are calculating them by week
  • The Time Sheet's hours are supposed to be calculated by week, but the Time Sheets are calculating them by day

Cause

The buyer company configures the rate schedule for your Time Sheets

Resolution

Please reach out to the Program Management Office so they can adjust the rate category if needed
