Symptom

In HR Renewal, when you maintain IIT Special Deduction (CN) (3422) infotype for an employee, the following errors occur:

  • When you maintain subtype 1 (Children’s Education), subtype 2 (Continuing Education), and subtype 5 (Housing Rental), if you click the dropdown list of month,  the sequence of months are not sorted.
  • When you maintain subtype 6 (Elderly Care), if you click the Only Child checkbox, the input fields under Supporter Information are not hidden.

 This SAP Note helps fix these two issues.

Other Terms

HR Renewal, IT3422

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a program error.

Solution

Do either of the following:

  • Implement the Support Package.
  • Apply the Correction Instruction.
