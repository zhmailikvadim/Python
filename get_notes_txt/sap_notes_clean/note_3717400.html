Symptom

I require the auto-generated Component Receipt Number functionality on SAP Business Network.

We expect the Document ID to auto-generate when creating a Component Receipt on SAP Business Network.

Cause

Auto-generation of Component Receipt numbers is not supported under the standard design of SAP Business Network.

The Document Number Preferences section does not include the Component Receipt document type for assigning a numbering range.

Follow the below path to verify the number ranges for the available document type in SAP Business Network.

  • Log in to the Buyer SAP Business Network.
  • Navigate to Account Settings > Transactions > Document Numbering Preference.
  • On available document Type list the Component Receipt type missing.

Resolution

Auto-generation of Component Receipt numbers is not supported under the standard design of SAP Business Network.
Submitting a Customer Influence Request will be recommended.
