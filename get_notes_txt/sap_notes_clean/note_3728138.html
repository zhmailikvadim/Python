Symptom

 In the processes "End of Supply for Electricity (SUP View)" (DE_B_GPKE_EOS_SUP) and "End of Supply for Gas (SUP View)" (DE_B_GELIGAS_EOS_SUP), a customer extension step at position 150 was missing above step 300.

Other Terms

GPKE_SUP, DE_B_GELIGAS_EOS_SUP, DE_B_GPKE_EOS_SUP, customer extension, end of supply, electricity, gas, BC set

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because the customer extension step at position 150 was not included in the process configuration above step 300.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system includes step 150 as a customer extension step above step 300 in both end of supply processes. Import the BC set /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP08_NOTE_3728138 or /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP07_NOTE_3728138, depending on your support package level. You can verify the fix by retesting the end of supply scenario and confirming that step 150 appears in the process flow above step 300.
