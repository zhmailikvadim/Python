Symptom

The following stock values in MARC and MARD are not calculated correctly after migration to S/4HANA:

MARD-VKLAB MARD-VKUML MARC-VKUMC MARC-VKTRW MARC-VKGLG

Other Terms

S/4HANA NSDM_MIG_CONTROL

Reason and Prerequisites

The migrated material documents in MATDOC were skipped during migration of MARC and MARD when MATDOC-MEINS was not equal to MARA-MEINS (like for value-only materials). As consequence unnecessary MATDOC records of type MIG_DELTA were created to substitute the skipped values.

Solution

During MARC and MARD migration the MATDOC-STOCK_VKWRT is summed up independant of the MATDOC-MEINS.

Implement the correction and migrate again.
