Symptom

An SAP support engineer completed a root-cause analysis of the error encountered during execution of functionality in the Revenue Accounting Engine (triggered by data from the sender component, manual UI actions, or FARR report execution).

The error is a direct result of a pre-existing inconsistency in the Revenue Accounting Items (RAIs) or contract-related data in one or more FARR_D_* tables.

Other Terms

 Revenue Accounting, existing data inconsistency

Reason and Prerequisites

  • There is no evidence suggesting that the existing inconsistency is result of standard data processing.
  • Standard development support does not correct data inconsistencies unless there is evidence they were introduced by standard system logic.

Solution

  • SAP Support will assist with correction if a step-by-step, reproducible workflow showing how the inconsistency is produced by the standard system is provided.
  • If a reproducible scenario is not available, please resolve the affected data using your internal data maintenance procedures or with your implementation partner.
  • If your implementation partner is not available or able to resolve the issue you can reach out to your SAP contact person (either account manager or TQM) and request a quote for paid consulting services.
