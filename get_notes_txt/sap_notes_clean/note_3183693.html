Symptom

I want to prevent my suppliers to send invoices and Service Entry Sheets (SES) with specific characters in the document number in SAP Business Network.

How can I block my suppliers from entering special characters in invoices and service sheets numbers?

Resolution

You can configure invoice and service sheet document numbering preferences for all suppliers or each supplier with whom you have an active trading relationship.

In order to do that, follow the steps below:

  1. Sign in to your SAP Business Network buyer account as the administrator
  2. Click Administration > Configuration > Document Number Preferences
  3. To create your restrictions for invoices, click Create below the Invoice section
  4. To create your restrictions for SES instead, click Create below the Service sheet section

The configuration page will be shown. You can configure these preferences:

  1. only for specific suppliers (Suppliers dropdown)
  2. for a specific supplier group (Supplier Group dropdown) or
  3. for all suppliers (keep the option All Suppliers in the Suppliers dropdown for that)

In this page, you can specify a desired prefix or suffix, maximum length, and allow the inclusion of specific special characters, lower and uppercase letters and numbers.

Additional Information

You can also limit the number of characters for these documents. See How can I restrict the maximum number of characters for service sheet and invoice numbers? for step by step instructions.

This configuration only applies to invoices and service sheets. See Ship Notice Number Preferences if you would like to have this configured for ship notices.

See Document number preferences for invoices and service sheets in SAP Business Network buyer administration guide to learn more about this configuration options and limitations.
