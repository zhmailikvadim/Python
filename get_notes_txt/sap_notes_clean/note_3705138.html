Symptom

When using ABAP Data Integration to replicate data into SAP Business Data Cloud, certain fields in the data product's nodes, expected to be of boolean type, are incorrectly replicated as character fields with length 1.

The annotation @DataIntegration.dataProduct.transformTo is not applied during the generation of the CSN interop effective flavor, leading to incorrect data type mappings.

Other Terms

 S/4HANA, ABAP Data Integration, SAP Business Data Cloud, APRS, CSN

Reason and Prerequisites

 The ABAP-based source system is an S/4HANA 2025 SP01 system.
