Symptom

  • Short dump occurs when deleting meter reading documents in transaction EL37.
  • ABAP runtime error DBSQL_DUPLICATE_KEY_ERROR.
  • Dump context points to program/class "CL_ISU_AMI_MRR_MANAGER========CP", method "LCL_MRC_CREATE_EABLC"; main program "RELEABL8".

Environment

  • SAP for Utilities as Part of SAP ERP, release IS-UT 600 and higher.
  • SAP S/4HANA, release IS-UT 801 and higher.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Reverse meter reading orders in EL37.
  2. ABAP short dump occurs.

Cause

  • Inconsistency between meter reading document tables: entries existed simultaneously in EABL/EABLG and the shadow tables EABLC/EABLGC, which must be mutually exclusive.
  • This led to duplicate-key collisions and the runtime error DBSQL_DUPLICATE_KEY_ERROR during processing in RELEABL8.

Resolution

  1. Review meter reading documents and their shadow counterparts: verify that for any document, entries reside either in EABL/EABLG or in EABLC/EABLGC, but not both.
  2. In EL31, enable the display of shadow tables to identify inconsistencies (refer to the See also section for the relevant note).
  3. Identify erroneous duplicate entries persisted in EABLC and/or EABLGC that correspond to records still present in EABL/EABLG.
  4. Remove the erroneous duplicate entries from EABLC/EABLGC in accordance with your change governance and authorizations.
  5. If the ISU_MR_EHP6 business function is active, be aware that deletions may also populate EABLDEL/EABLGDEL; ensure these exist only when the “original” entries are deleted from EABL/EABLG via EL37.
  6. Re-run EL37 to reverse the affected meter reading orders without dump.
