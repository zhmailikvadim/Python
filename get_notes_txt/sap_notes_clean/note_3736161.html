Symptom

  • Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune and Ahmedabad cities eligible for 50% HRA exemption Changes in income tax rules effective from 1 April 2026
  • Changes in income tax rules effective from 1 April 2026 - Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune and Ahmedabad cities eligible for 50% HRA exemption 
  • Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune and Ahmedabad cities eligible for 50% HRA exemption under the old tax regime
  • Expansion of cities eligible for 50% HRA exemption under the old tax regime.

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • Payroll India
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP ERP Core Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Maintain HRA in IT 581 effective from 1-Apr-2026 say employee belongs to Bengaluru
  2. Maintain Old Regime in IT 588
  3. Execute Payroll using transaction PC00_M40_CALC
  4. Check for the HRA exemption limit matches the Income tax rules effective from 1-Apr-2026

Resolution

For Expanded list of cities Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune and Ahmedabad cities eligible for 50% HRA exemption under the old tax regime, maintain "Metro" in Infotype 581 (City Category) for applicable employees so that payroll calculates the correct exemption.
