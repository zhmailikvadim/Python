Symptom

  • You need to know who can activate/deactivate supplier users
  • You need to have your user connection reactivated on buyer site 
  • You need to have your user connection approved

Environment

Resolution

Buyer organizations are responsible for managing supplier users information .

As a buyer you can manage supplier users in Supplier/Customer Manager for solutions that include or do not include  SAP Ariba Supplier Information and Performance Management (Classic Architecture) . You need to be a member of either :

  • Commodity Manager
  • Event Administrator
  • Sourcing Manager
  • Supplier/Customer Manager

Use Supplier 360 profile or Supplier contacts data import task in SM Administration, if your solution includes SAP Ariba Supplier Lifecycle and Performance or SAP Ariba Supplier Information and Performance Management (new architecture). 

To access SM Administration you must be a member of:

  •  SM Ops Administrator
  •  SM ERP Administrator
  • Customer Administrator group
