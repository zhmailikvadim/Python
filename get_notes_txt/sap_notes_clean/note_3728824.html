Symptom

Why is the supplier unable to see line items while creating an invoice against an approved service sheet?

Cause

The current version of the Purchase Order (PO) is in an obsolete status, or the approved Service Entry Sheet (SES) is associated with an obsolete version of the PO

Resolution

If the approved SES is associated with an obsolete version of the PO, a new SES against the current (active) PO version must be created. Once this SES is approved, the supplier can create invoice for lines against that approved SES

See Also

Buyer transaction rules and configurations may vary across accounts. Ensure that you review the system configuration enabled for specific suppliers and also verify the control keys defined in the POs.
