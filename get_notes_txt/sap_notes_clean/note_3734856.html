Symptom

In the Product Profitability Review Booklet, the Product Hierarchy fields are not displayed.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

“Not assigned” is displayed in the Product Hierarchy field because this information is not maintained for the product in the material master data.

Resolution

In the Product Profitability Review Booklet (F8348), products can be displayed in a hierarchical view. To enable this, a product hierarchy must first be created and the products must be assigned to the appropriate hierarchy.
This information can also be verified in the “Manage Product Master Data” app.

Keywords

Profit Center, Product Hierarchy, Product Profitability Review Booklet
