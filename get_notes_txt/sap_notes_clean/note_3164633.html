Symptom

You manage installment bonds like asset backed securities or mortgage backed securities. You require the parallel condition framework of the FIN-FSCM-TRM application to enable the functionality of risk free rates. This collective SAP Note provides a list of SAP Notes to enable the parallel condition framework for installment bonds.

SAP Note 2932789 provides more information about the parallel condition framework of the FIN-FSCM-TRM application with respect to the EU Benchmark Regulation and risk free rates.

Other Terms

ABS, MBS, Risk-Free Rates (RFRs), RFR, 042, IB

Reason and Prerequisites

Market requirement.
