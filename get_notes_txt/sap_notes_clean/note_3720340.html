Symptom

Users with certain business roles can see the "My Network" app, although it should not be available to these roles as per the business requirement.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Cloud Version 2

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login with a user assigned to the affected business role. 
  2. Navigate to the "My Network" app.
  3. Observe that the app is visible despite the business requirement to restrict access.

Cause

The service "sap.crm.service.accountEngagementService" controls access to the "My Network" application and is assigned to the given roles.

Resolution

Remove the service "sap.crm.service.accountEngagementService" from these business roles to restrict access to the "My Network" application.

Keywords

SAP Sales Cloud, My Network app, NGCS_AE, NGCS_AE_MGR, restrict access, business roles, accountEngagementService, hide application, Fiori launchpad, configuration changes
