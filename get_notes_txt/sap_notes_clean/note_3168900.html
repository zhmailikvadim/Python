Symptom

Marketing email open tracking might not work in all situations if email client do not load the email in one step but load only the first part and then wait for user confirmation before loading the remaining part of the email.

Other Terms

marketing email campaign; email open interactions; marketing email open trigger

Reason and Prerequisites

Tracking pixel is inserted at the end of email body.

Solution

Apply note to have the tracking pixel being inserted at the top of email body.
