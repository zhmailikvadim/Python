Symptom

When the partial submission feature was enabled, items discarded during upload were not appearing in the History tab of the worklist. Additionally, once the workflow for submitted items was completed (approved or rejected), those items were still visible under My Items.

This note resolves both issues. Discarded items are now correctly displayed in the History tab, and items whose workflows are completed are no longer shown in My Items.

Solution

Please implement this SAP Note to resolve the symptom.
