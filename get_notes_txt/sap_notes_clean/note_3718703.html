Symptom

Can fields on a Custom Email Form be edited/rearranged when it is part of a Classic Approval Flow? If so, how can changes be made to the Custom Email Form in question?

Reproducing the Issue

Customer's Custom Email Form was originally created for Classic Approval Flow, which then was later edited, and New Fields were added. 

Cause

Customer's Custom Email Form was originally created for Classic Approval Flow, which then was later edited and New Fields were added.  Once the customer makes a change or adds a field to their Custom Email Notification Form to Classic Approval Flow, this prevents the customer from being able to alter the form further without fields appearing out of sequence.(Referenced in Knowledge KB1286416  Why are header fields in email notifications out of order for my Custom Form? ) This can also affect the approval Flow behavior depending on how the customer had previously established their approval edit rules and behaviors. 

Resolution

Yes, fields can be edited/rearranged when it is part of a Classic Approval Flow. However to process edits or changes you will need to receive assistance from SAP Customizations Team to alter the Custom Email Form that is part of a Classic Approval Flow. For customization request please refer to Knowledge KB0537649 What is the current process for customization requests? 

As referenced in Knowledge KB1286416 Why are header fields in email notifications out of order for my Custom Form? Customer's Custom Email Form was originally created for Classic Approval Flow, which then was later edited and New Fields were added.
