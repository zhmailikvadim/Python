Symptom

On the Account Timeline, there is no option filter for certain objects e.g Email.

Environment

SAP Sales and Service Cloud Version 2

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Accounts.
  2. Open an Account.
  3. Open the Timeline.
  4. There is no filter for Emails.

Cause

The business role assigned to the affected user does not have the correct ui app assigned to use the email filter on the timeline.

Resolution

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Open Business Roles.
  3. Select the affected Business Role.
  4. Navigate to App List.
  5. Assign the App, sap.crm.emailservice.uiapp.emailInteraction.
