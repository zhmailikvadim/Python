Symptom

You are executing Form 24Q (Transaction code: PC00_M40_F24Q) for employees working in your organization. It is observed that, in salary and tax details of the employee, the value of 'Commuted value of pension under section 10(10A)' is not considered while determining the total exemption under section 10.
