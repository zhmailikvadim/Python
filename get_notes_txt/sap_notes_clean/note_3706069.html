Symptom

Attendance and utilization fields are blank under Labor Management > Labor Utilization in the warehouse monitor. 

Environment

  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management 
  • SAP S/4HANA 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the Warehouse Monitor (/scwm/mon). 
  2. Select Labor Management > Labor Utilization. 
  3. Enter the Date and Processor. 
  4. Observe that the attendance and utilization fields remain blank. 

Cause

  • Missing implementation or inactive Business Add In (BAdI). 
  • Attendance is calculated through the active implementation of the BAdI Attendance Times for Labor Utilization /SCWM/EX_EWL_ATTENDANCE. 

Resolution

  1. Implement the BAdI Attendance Times for Labor Utilization /SCWM/EX_EWL_ATTENDANCE.
  2. Ensure the BAdI is active and properly configured in the system.

Keywords

/SCWM/IF_EX_EWL_ATTENDANCE, /SCWM/ES_EWL_ATTENDANCE, /SCWM/EI_EWL_ATTENDANCE_TATT, /SCWM/EWL_ATTENDANCE, IT_PROCESSOR, IT_PROCESSOR_EWL, Processor Group
