Symptom

 The activation of a Payroll Function/Operation raises the error P0826: The form routine assigned to the function/operation could not be found.

Other Terms

PE04, Payroll Functions, Payroll Operations

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisite:
You have created the subroutine corresponding to the Payroll Function/Operation, but you have implemented it in an enhancement.

Reason:
The syntax check does not read the enhancement and cannot identify the implemented form routine.

Solution

Implement the corresponding correction instruction or the Support Package.

See help.sap.com for further information regarding Payroll Functions and Operations.
