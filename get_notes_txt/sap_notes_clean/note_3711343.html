Symptom

During deployment in extended Service Parts Planning, ATP checks for non-leading members of an FFF class do not apply customer-specific ATP logic, resulting in incorrect determination of distributable quantities and missing consideration of business-specific ATP rules.

Other Terms

 /SAPAPO/SPPDEPL

Reason and Prerequisites

During deployment for non-leading members of an FFF class, the ATP check is executed without the correct product/location context being available in the BAdI processing. The existing call flow does not provide sufficient canonical context information for characteristic determination in the deployment BAdI, which leads to incorrect or incomplete ATP-related logic execution.

This is due to the fact that the BAdI interface did not provide explicit ATP-relevant product and location identifiers for this execution path.

Solution

Please implement the Correction Instruction or import the relevant Support Package.
