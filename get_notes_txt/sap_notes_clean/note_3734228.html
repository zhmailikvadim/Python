Symptom

When calculating the length of stay of a TARPSY case, the values 40 and 44 of the BfS variable "Stay After Leaving" are not taken into account as psychiatry values. The retention period is determined too much by one day.

Other Terms

TARPSY, VWD, stay after leaving, psychiatric clinic

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisites:

  • Country Version CH (Switzerland)

Causes:

  • Error

Solution

This enhancement is available for the specified Support Packages of the relevant releases. You can perform the adjustments prematurely by implementing the attached correction instructions.
