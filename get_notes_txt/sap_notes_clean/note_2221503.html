Symptom

You want use rule based certificate mapping to logon. The X509 client certificate of your users contain the EMail address and you have maintained the addresses in user management system.

Other Terms

X509, CERTRULE, Certificate, SUSR_CERTRULE, login/certificate_mapping_rulebased

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing mapping caused the problem.

Solution

This note is a base correction to allow the mapping of an email to a user name in ABAP.
The mapping is available as of SAP_BASIS 750 SP2 .
A downport of this feature to SAP_BASIS 740 and SAP_BASIS 731 is provided with SAP note 2722739 .
