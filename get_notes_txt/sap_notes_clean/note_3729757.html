Symptom

Forwarding Agreement Quotation header Time zone Adjustment cannot sync to its owned TCCS and rate table.

Other Terms

FWAQ time zone. TCCS time zone. Rate table time zone.

Reason and Prerequisites

FWAQ adjust dates function synced timestamp to TCCS and rate table but not sync header time zone.

Solution

Implement this SAP note to enhance adjust dates function to sync quotation header time zone to its TCCS and rate table.
