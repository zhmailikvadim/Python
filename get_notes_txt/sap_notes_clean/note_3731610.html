Symptom

  • During the tenant decommissioning of the Oracle-based SAP Incentive Management system, end users and administrators continue to receive reminder emails related to ongoing documents and disputes.
  • Following the completion of O2H (Oracle-to-HANA) migration and transition of operations to the GCP instance, customers observe that email notifications are still being triggered from the decommissioning Oracle tenant. This leads to confusion regarding the source system generating these emails.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
      • Incentive Management on Legacy Oracle

Cause

  • Reminder notifications for documents and disputes were configured and scheduled in the Oracle tenant prior to decommissioning. These background jobs or notification mechanisms remain active and continue to trigger emails even after the tenant is taken down or during the transition phase.
  • Since these configurations are not automatically disabled during the decommissioning or O2H migration process, the legacy Oracle system continues to send email notifications independently of the GCP environment.

Resolution

To prevent multiple reminder emails from being sent from the Oracle system during the decommissioning process, it is recommended to disable system-generated emails at the global level.

Customers should enable the option “Disable site-wide system generated emails for end users” in the Oracle instance before initiating tenant decommissioning.

Steps to Enable the Setting:

  1. Navigate to the SAP Incentive Management portal (Oracle instance)
  2. Go to Global Settings.
  3. Under the General Settings tab, enable the option: “Disable site-wide system generated emails for end users.”

Enabling this setting ensures that the system stops sending reminder emails for documents and disputes during the decommissioning phase. This prevents duplicate or unexpected email notifications while operations are being transitioned to the GCP environment.
