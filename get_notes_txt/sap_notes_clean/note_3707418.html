Symptom

The accounting document of a SD billing document has been cleared. And the fiscal year of the clearing document is different with the fiscal year of the accounting document. When you reset clearing document via FBRA transaction, the billing document clearing status (VBRK-CLRST) still remains as C (cleared).

Other Terms

FBRA, CLRST, VBRK-CLRST, SDBIL_UPDATE_CLRST_00001040, CL_SD_BIL_UPDATE_CLEARING_STAT

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the program corrections or import the corresponding support package.
