Symptom

This SAP Note describes the delivery and support strategy for the Brazilian tax reform solution related to the alphanumeric CNPJ changes.  It also lists the SAP solutions that address updates related to CNPJ changes from the Brazilian tax reform.

The Brazilian tax reform requires several adaptations in SAP solutions to comply with the new tax regime changes under Constitutional Amendment 132/2023 and Complementary Law 214/2025. You can find more information in the regulatory change manager under the following items:

  • Tax Reform

  • Tax Reform - Alphanumeric CNPJ - Normative Instruction No. 2229, of October 15, 2024

  • Tax Reform - Joint Technical Note 2025.001 - Alphanumeric CNPJ

For more details about SAP deliveries concerning the tax reform, refer to SAP Note 3561376 - Tax Reform Leading SAP Note. Alternatively, you can find further information by visiting our Workzone Brazil - Tax Reform Localization. For additional support, also refer to the KBA 3642384 - Brazil Tax Reform Troubleshooting Guide, which provides detailed troubleshooting instructions.

Note that this SAP Note is periodically updated and refined to include the most recent information.

For more information, see also: Nota Técnica Conjunta CNPJ Alfanumérico 2025.001, INSTRUÇÃO NORMATIVA RFB Nº 2.229, DE 15 DE OUTUBRO DE 2024, CNPJ Alfanumérico Information Page, and Complementary Law No. 214. Solution

Supported Releases and Maintenance Strategy: We support Tax Reform deliveries for all releases within the valid period of Mainstream Maintenance or equivalent Extended Maintenance at the time of SAP Note delivery. All SAP Notes related to Tax Reform deliveries are categorized as "Legal Change". For maintenance and incident handling in the context of Tax Reform, check SAP Note 3563053.

Special Conditions: Customers with special conditions, such as Extended Maintenance or Cloud Safekeeper, if no specific SAP Note is released for your release, then contact SAP for further details. You can create a Case in the same component of the relevant SAP Note to contact SAP. If your solution release is under Extended Maintenance, only customers with this maintenance status have access to the Tax Reform solutions. For further details on Extended Maintenance or Cloud Safekeeper check SAP Note 52505.

Tax Reform Alphanumeric CNPJ Impacts:

In accordance with the planned implementation outlined in the regulatory change manager (RCM), referenced under link Tax Reform - Alphanumeric CNPJ - Normative Instruction No. 2229, of October 15, 2024, SAP plans to modify the following ABAP objects from numeric to alphanumeric (char) type to support the alphanumeric CNPJ update. Certain ABAP objects mentioned may not be altered directly, but could be impacted by modifications within the same context. Refer to the lists below to assess potential impacts on your custom solutions. It is recommended that each customer review their own packages for references to these ABAP objects and plan any required adjustments accordingly.
