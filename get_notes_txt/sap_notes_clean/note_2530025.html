Symptom

In SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA, in the Choose Company window, if you log onto SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA with a Chinese user code longer than 12 Chinese characters, the following warning message appears, and you cannot use the analytical features: "Cannot connect to SAP Business One analytics powered by SAP HANA".

Reproducing the issue

Scenario:

In SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA, in the Choose Company window, you log onto SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA with a Chinese user code longer than 12 Chinese characters.

Expected behavior:

The logon process is smooth without warning, and after the logon, you can use the analytical features.

Actual behavior:

The following warning message appears, and after the logon, you cannot use the analytical features: "Cannot connect to SAP Business One analytics powered by SAP HANA".

 

Solution

SAP intends to provide a patch or patches in order to solve the problem described. The section Reference to Related Notes below will list the specific patches once they become available. The corresponding Info file of the patches in SAP Service Marketplace will also show the SAP Note number. Be aware that these references can only be set at patch release date. SAP will deliver patches only for selected releases at its own discretion, based on the business impact and the complexity of the implementation.
