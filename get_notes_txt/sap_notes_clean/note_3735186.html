Symptom

Is it technically possible in SAP CDC to allow multiple accounts with the same email address but different usernames?

Environment

  • SAP Customer Data Cloud
  • Login Identifier

Resolution

In SAP CDC email is not necessarily unique. Login Identifier have to be unique for all the accounts and Login Identifier can be set either as Username or Email

1. Go to CDC console > Connect > Self Registration > In settings you can choose your login identifier.

2. Accordingly, add username/email field in Registration and Login screens.
