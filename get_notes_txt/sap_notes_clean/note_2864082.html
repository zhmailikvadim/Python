Symptom

Using Asset Accounting in SAP S/4HANA or planning to transition to SAP S/4HANA you have certain questions about or seeking for general information on Asset Accounting (FI-AA).

Other Terms

FI-AA, New Asset Accounting, EA-FIN, RAFABNEW, New Depreciation Calculation, Conversion, S/4HANA Finance

Reason and Prerequisites

Asset Accounting in SAP S/4HANA is already implemented or you are planning/are currently in a S/4HANA transition project.

Introducing a New Depreciation Area - RAFABNEW obsolete:

Q: I need to introduce a new Depreciation Area. Report RAFABNEW is obsolete with S/4HANA. What is the alternative?

A: Start a Subsequent Implementation of a Further Accounting Principle project for a new valuation (new accounting prinicple).

If you require an additional depreciation area (statistical) of an existing valuation with S/4HANA 1809 or higher you may use Report RAFAB_COPY_AREA.

Please read the following note for detailed information on your possibilities:

2403248 - FI-AA (new): Availability of RAFABNEW.

Insurance Values

Q: How do I map insurance values?

A: Insurance values need a seperate depreciation area. Conversion customers may use RAFABNEW before Conversion in SAP ECC (Introducing a new Depreciation Area).

Please read note:

2398938 - No insurance fields in asset accounting.
