Symptom

After a Disaster recovery the following error shows in SSI: "Configure Root Cause Analysis: System Analysis Exception: Unexpected error occur"

Environment

SAP Focused Run

Reproducing the Issue

- Execute SSI after Dissaster Recovery

Cause

The primary and secondary are running in two different customer networks and associations across different namespaces in the LMDB are not maintained

Resolution

Maintain the cross-data center replication association as mentioned on Landscape Management

After a failover, this association must be adjusted as the SR roles are changed.
