Symptom

  • Configured Alerting and Notifications in Integration Monitoring based on a Business Service
  • Within a Business Service, there can be multiple technical systems
  • Within the notification alert, the information about the technical service/systems where the issue occurs is not available and they do not know the problem service/system

Environment

SAP CLOUD ALM

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Launch SAP Cloud ALM
  2. Click Operations > Monitoring and Analysis > Integration and Exception Monitoring
  3. In configuration, define a Business Service to include desired services/systems
  4. In Events, configure the Business Service to trigger a Notification when an Event occurs
  5. Check the notification that is sent and there is no technical service/system mentioned in the email -> Problem

Cause

This is intended behavior that the notification does not contain the technical system/service information.  The workflow is oriented around the "need-to-know" aspects of the event. In other words, there might be 2 or 3 services involved in the larger business process which are impacted by an integration monitoring issue. So all the Ops teams for all those services might get the "general" Business Service notification that there's a problem somewhere in the chain and someone is looking at it. 

Resolution

In order that the relevant people are alerted to the issue in the specific system/service, at that system/service level, be sure to also configure an Event to notify relevant business users so they can review and resolve the failure in the affected source.
