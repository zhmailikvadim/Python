Symptom

Business Partner records with "&" in the BP Code fail to display in the SAP Business One Sales app.

Business Partner records with "&" in the BP Code fail to be chosen in sales order in the SAP Business One Sales/Service app. 

Reproducing the issue

Prerequisites:

You are using SAP Business One Sales/Service app for iOS.

A Business Partner with BP code having "&" has been created, for example BP code "A&b1". 

Scenario 1:

  1.  Login SAP Business One Sales app for iOS.
  2.  Go to Customers, click code "A&b1". 

Expected behavior:

Business Partner detail can be displayed without error.

Actual behavior:

 Error shows up "Processing Failed - No matching records found".

 

Scenario 2:

  1.  Login SAP Business One Service app for iOS.
  2.  Go to Ticket → Sales Order, click "+".
  3.  Click Business Partner, choose code "A&b1". 

Expected behavior:

Business Partner can be selected into order.

Actual behavior:

 Error shows up "Processing Failed - No matching records found".

Solution

SAP intends to provide a feature package or patch to solve the symptom that is described in this SAP Note. The section References below lists the related feature package or patch once they become available. Be aware that References can only be confirmed at feature package or patch release date. SAP delivers feature packages or patches only for selected releases at its own discretion, based on the business impact and the complexity of implementation.
