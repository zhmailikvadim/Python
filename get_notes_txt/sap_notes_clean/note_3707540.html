Symptom

During Business Partner replication from SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition to Sales Cloud v2 (or other external systems) using standard replication mechanisms (e.g. SAP_COM_008, Replicate by Replication Model), the replication fails with the following error:"Value is not a valid date in accord with the XML format for ABAP".

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. In SAP S/4HANA Cloud, maintain a Business Partner with Payment Card (Credit Card) data under:

    • Business Partner → Payment Transactions

  2. In the credit card details:

    • Valid To Date is maintained

    • Valid From Date is left blank

  3. Trigger BP replication:

    • Via Replicate by Replication Model

    • Or during initial load to Sales Cloud v2 / C4C

  4. Replication fails before outbound XML is successfully generated

Cause

  • The issue occurs due to the "VALID_FROM" date field in the Business Partner data being set to "01/01/0001 00:00:00" in the table BUT000. This invalid date format causes the XML conversion error during the replication process.

Resolution

Maintain the “Valid From Date” for all affected Payment Card entries:

  1. Open the Business Partner
  2. Navigate to Payment Transactions → Credit Card Details
  3. Enter a valid Valid From Date, for example, If the "VALID_FROM" date is set to "01/01/0001 00:00:00", update the field to a valid date format, such as "01/01/1900".
  4. Ensure that the "VALID_FROM" date is properly maintained in the credit card details under payment transactions for the Business Partner.
  5. Retest the replication process using the standard app "Replicate by replication model" to confirm the resolution of the issue.
