Symptom

After changing the Departure Date for Advanced Shipping and Receiving relevant Freight Order the sel_time field is not updated for the Warehouse Loading Stop. It causes issues for time based Freight Order selection: You specify dates as selection criteria in Freight Orders - Worklist or Transportation Cockpit and Freight Orders with departure date outside of the selection criteria will be listed.

Relevant for S4CORE 107 and 106:

Plan_trans_time field is not updated for ASR sub-stop after departure date is modified.

Other Terms

sel_time, DET_ROOT_AM, POWL

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

 Implement the attached correction instructions or apply the corresponding Support Package
