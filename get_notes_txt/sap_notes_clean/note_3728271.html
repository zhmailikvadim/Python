Symptom

When attempting to extend the BP role FLCU00 (Customer Financial Accounting New) for a Business Partner created under BUP010 (Non Employee Business Partner), the system shows the error message R1304 indicating that an entry is required in the 'Correspondence Lang.' field. The field appears but is not editable.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA on-premise
  • SAP S/4HANA private cloud edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Attempt to extend the BP role FLCU00 for a Business Partner created under BUP010.
  2. Observe the error message R1304 indicating that an entry is required in the 'Correspondence Lang.' field, which is not editable.

Cause

The correspondence language is automatically filled by the HR-BP sync reports from the language field in employee's infotype 2. Later, the customizing in view PTRV_VENDOR_SYNC can be adapted so roles vendor and customer are added to the Business Partner. The report /SHCM/R_EMPL_HANDLE_BPROLES should be used to add roles FLVN00 and FLCU00 automatically to the Business Partner.

Resolution

To resolve the issue, follow these steps:

  1. Ensure that the correspondence language is maintained in the employee's infotype 2.
  2. Adapt the customizing in view PTRV_VENDOR_SYNC to add the vendor and customer roles to the Business Partner.
  3. Enter a Business Partner in the field "Ref. Business Partner" within the view PTRV_VENDOR_SYNC. This Business Partner should have the roles FLVN00 and FLCU00 maintained with the necessary data, including the correspondence language.
  4. Execute the report /SHCM/R_EMPL_HANDLE_BPROLES. This report will automatically add the roles FLVN00 (Vendor) and FLCU00 (Customer) to the Business Partner. It will read the correspondence language of the reference BP maintained in view PTRV_VENDOR_SYNC and copy it to any new customer for that company code.
  5. Note that it is not possible to manually add a vendor or customer role via transaction BP to a BP created from HR. The standard procedure is to create or add the customer role automatically via the report /SHCM/R_EMPL_HANDLE_BPROLES.
