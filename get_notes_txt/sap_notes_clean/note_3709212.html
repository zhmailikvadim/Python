Symptom

User is added as an additional approver of job requisition and was added in the route map as U user. Despite not being assigned as any operator role in the job requisition, user can still access it even after requisition is approved. Is there a way to revoke the job requisition access of U user (additional approver)?

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Add a user as additional approver (ensure user is not assigned to other operator / operator team role)
  2. Complete the job requisition approval phase
  3. Proxy as the user and it can still access the job requisition

Resolution

By design, users who are part of the requisition approval process will retain visibility of the requisition in their "My Recruiting" view after approval. Operators and/or approvers retain access to job requisitions they were involved in approving.

At the moment, there's no option or feature that removes job requisition access from the U user.

Consider raising an enhancement idea through the Customer Influence tool for functionality to manage requisition visibility after the approval process. Refer to 2090228 - How to Submit Ideas for SAP SuccessFactors Products for detailed instructions on submitting improvement requests.
