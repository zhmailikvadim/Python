Symptom

How can I use the confirmed delivery date filter on the Items to Ship tile?

Resolution

You can use find Confirmed delivery date filter by following steps: 

  1. Log in to your SAP Business Network account. 
  2. Navigate to Orders > Orders and Releases > Items to Ship.
  3. Locate the Confirmed delivery date filter.
  4. If the filter is not visible, click Show more to display additional options. 
  5. Select desired date range based on your requirements.
