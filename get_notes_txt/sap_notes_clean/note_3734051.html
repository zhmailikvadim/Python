Symptom

You execute the Realignment job using the Schedule Jobs for Consolidation Tasks app and in the job log you get a warning message, that "No integration due to different group currency in version ZZZ.", even though you entered a different version (For example: YYY) at the selection screen, when you scheduled the job.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Finance for Group Reporting

Cause

Designed behavior of the program based on customer configuration.

Resolution

The realignment function operates in accounting. The entities you enter on the realignment screen like version, are not used directly in the execution. Instead they are translated into entities known in accounting. In case of the version you entered, it is used to identify the relevant company codes and ledgers. The checks works on company codes and ledgers. If they are shared between versions you get check messages for more versions than the one you entered in realignment function.

For example, if Consolidation Versions: "YYY" and "ZZZ" both has Preparation Ledger: "0L" assigned in transaction: FINCS_VERS_DEF, then both of these versions are checked.
