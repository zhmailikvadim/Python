Symptom

The "Calculated Amount" field is not visible in the 'Pay Component Non Recurring' and 'Compensation Information' card under the Compensation category in the Latest People Profile.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Employee Profile: Full Profile

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the Latest People Profile of an employee.
  2. Navigate to Compensation -> Pay Component Non Recurring.
  3. Observe that the "Calculated Amount" field is missing in the Latest People Profile.

Resolution

The "Calculated Amount" field is no longer supported in PPX as per the current system design. Instead, its value is directly posted to the "Amount" field.
