Symptom

When running a footprint inventory calculation, the system shows the following error in 'Show Results' for the failed footprint items: 'Cannot find the energy bill or the emission factor for calculation method for Scope 2 in emission factor period for the energy source'.

Environment

Sustainability Footprint Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the 'Calculate Footprint' app.

  2. Select the footprint inventory to calculate.

  3. The calculation completes with errors.

  4. Open 'Show Results' in the top‑right corner of the calculation.

  5. Search for footprint items with an 'Error' status.

  6. Open a footprint item and go to the 'Error and Warnings' tab.

  7. The following error appears: "Cannot find the energy bill or the emission factor for calculation method for Scope 2 in emission factor period for the energy source."

Cause

The issue occurs when the configuration is incorrect or the required emission factors are not maintained.

Resolution

  1. Determine which Scope 2 Calculation Method is required for the calculation variant used for footprint calculations. The available options are Location‑based or Market‑based.

  2. After selecting the Scope 2 Calculation Method, ensure that the corresponding emission factors are maintained for the relevant energy sources in the Model Energy Flows app.

  3. In the Energy Sources tab, select the energy source and open the assigned Emission Factor Period. Verify that the emission factors are maintained for the Scope 2 calculation method defined in the calculation variant.

  4. When the emission factors are maintained for the required Scope 2 calculation, the error no longer appears and the calculation completes successfully.
