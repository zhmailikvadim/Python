Symptom

In report HSKCDPRZ, according to Guideline of the FRSR č. 44/DZPaU/2025/MÚ, the calculation of the child tax bonus does not correctly handle negative values when an employee has a high tax base. This issue occurs in cases where the child’s age changes during the year, which affects the applicable tax‑bonus amount.
Additionally, when the report is executed for multiple employees, the system incorrectly accumulates the child tax bonus across employees.

Other Terms

HRSK, HSKCDPRZ

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Slovakia (HRSK).

Solution

This SAP Note delivers corrections described in the chapter Symptom.

Example of calculation of the child tax bonus:

The employee income is 36.960,00 EUR and insurance is 4.952,64 (tax base = 32.007,36) in year 2025.
PMMSR=1.430,00.
The tax bonus is claimed for 1 child for the whole year, which is 14 years old (turning 15 in 07/2025).

32.007,36 > 25740,00 (0,1*(32.007,36 - 25740,00)) = 626,74/12=52,23
1. child - (100,00*7 - 52,23*7) + (50*5 - 52,23*5) = 334,39 + 0 = 334,39

Child bonus is 334,39 EUR.
