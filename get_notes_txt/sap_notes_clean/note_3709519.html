Symptom

  • The staging area determination on inbound is not functioning as expected and does not follow the system setup.
  • The issue appears to be that the "Determine Staging Area inbound" functionality does not consider the warehouse process type during Goods reciept referring Purchase order without delivery.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Perform a goods receipt using APP Post Goods Movement for Purchase Order.
  2. Go to Warehouse Management Tab, the storage bin is not expected even the bin is set at APP Determine Staging Area - Inbound with putaway Warehouse Process Type.

Resolution

The behavior is intentional: the warehouse process type (WPT) cannot be determined and is therefore not considered as one of the determining factors.
