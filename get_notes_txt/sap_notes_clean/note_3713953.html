Symptom

How do I enable Hypertext Markup Language (HTML), Extensible Markup Language (XML) attachments for Receipt in SAP Business Network?

Resolution

  1. Sign in to your SAP Business Network.
  2. Click [User Initials] > Settings > Electronic Order Routing
  3. Select Email as Routing Method, for Receipt
  4. Enter the Email address (A maximum of five email addresses can be entered, separated by commas) where you want to receive Receipt notifications.
  5. Tick the following checkboxes for attachments in the Receipt email notification, based on your requirements: 
    • Attach cXML document in the email message (for .xml attachment)
    • Include document in the email message (for .html attachment)
  6. Click on Save

See Also

To learn more about the configuration of routing methods and available options on the Electronic Order Routing, please refer to Configuring Electronic Order Routing
