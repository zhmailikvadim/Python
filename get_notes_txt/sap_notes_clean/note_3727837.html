Symptom

I am a supplier user, and I would like to access the list of workers in the SAP Fieldglass system.

Resolution

Vertical view:

  1. Sign into your SAP Fieldglass account
  2. Click on Worker > Worker

Horizontal view: 

  1. Sign into your SAP Fieldglass account
  2. Click on View > Worker

If are still unable to see workers after following these steps, please contact the Program Management Office team (PMO), since they are the system administrators and can help you further on that matter.
