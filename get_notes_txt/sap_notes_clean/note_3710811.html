Symptom

This KBA explains how to configure the Content-Security-Policy (CSP) for SmartEdit to work in local Composable Storefront. In certain cases the missing or misconfiguration of it can lead to unexpected behaviors, such as SmartEdit being unable to display a Composable Storefront inside it, causing an empty screen with only the error message "This content is blocked. Contact the site owner to fix the issue.".

Environment

  • SAP Commerce
  • SAP Commerce Cloud, Composable Storefront

Resolution

The configuration is possible through two ways:

  • The property smartedit.response.header.Content-Security-Policy. One can follow the instructions from Content Security Policy - Target Policy to configure its value:
    • smartedit.response.header.Content-Security-Policy=default-src 'self' 'unsafe-inline' {commerce-occ-api-host} {smartedit-host}; img-src 'self' {commerce-occ-api-host} data:;frame-src 'none'; frame-ancestors 'self' {commerce-occ-api-host} {smartedit-host}; style-src 'self' 'unsafe-inline' fonts.googleapis.com use.fontawesome.com; font-src fonts.gstatic.com use.fontawesome.com
  • Meta tag with http-equiv. This is possible by configuring a Content Security Policy <meta> tag in your index.html file:
    • <meta
        http-equiv="Content-Security-Policy"
        content="default-src 'self' 'unsafe-inline' {commerce-occ-api-host} {smartedit-host}; img-src 'self' {commerce-occ-api-host} data:;frame-src 'none'; frame-ancestors 'self' {commerce-occ-api-host} {smartedit-host}; style-src 'self' 'unsafe-inline' fonts.googleapis.com use.fontawesome.com; font-src fonts.gstatic.com use.fontawesome.com"
      />
