Symptom

When you create an in-house service by inserting service objects in a service order or service quotation in the Manage Service Orders or Manage Service Quotations app in the In-House Service Objects assignment block, the system maps only the sold-to party and the employee responsible to the newly created in-house service. The system doesn't map additional partner functions, even when they exist in both the service order or quotation and the in-house service. This applies to SAP delivered partner functions, such as Ship-To Party and Contact Person and to partner functions that you've customized for both service orders or quotations and in-house services.

Other Terms

Define Partner Determination Procedure in Service

Reason and Prerequisites

The partner selection process doesn't consider all partner functions that are maintained for both service orders or quotations and in-house services.

Solution

After implementing this SAP Note, the system considers all partner functions that are maintained for both service orders or quotations and in-house services. The system maps all partners that are defined for service orders or quotations and in-house services, not only Sold-to Party and Employee Responsible. This gives you more flexibility in partner determination.
