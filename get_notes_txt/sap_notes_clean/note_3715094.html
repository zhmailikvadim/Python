Symptom

SAP Ariba Buying does not support the following:

  • Purchase orders are not sent to suppliers through SAP Business Network. Currently this functionality is not supported. 
  • Catalog items do not support UNSPSC, commodity codes, or eclass values.

Other Terms

Purchase Order, SAP Ariba Buying, User Management

Reason and Prerequisites

  • SAP Ariba Buying Release 2602 does not support the integration of sending purchase order to SAP Business Network. As a result, purchase orders cannot be sent to supplier on Business Network.
  • SAP Ariba Buying does not support UNSPSC, commodity codes, or eclass values for catalog items. Catalog items must be only associated to the material groups.

Solution

  • To send purchase orders to suppliers, do the following:
    • Go to the purchase order created and open it in your browser.
    • Choose the Print option available in your browser and then save the purchase order as a PDF file.
    • Download the purchase order saved as a PDF file.
    • Send the PDF file as an email attachment to the supplier.
  • Catalog items must be only associated to the material groups.
