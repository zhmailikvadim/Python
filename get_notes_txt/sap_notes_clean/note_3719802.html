Symptom

When clicking to open a Business Partner (BP) location on a map, you receive the following error message:

No map service has been defined. Ask an authorized user to specify the map service in the General Settings window.

Environment

  • SAP Business One
  • SAP Business One, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

Go to Business Partners → Addresses tab, click "Show Location in Web Browser"

Cause

In Administration → System Initialization → General Settings → Tab Services. In the field "Map Service", no selection has been made. 

Resolution

In Administration → System Initialization → General Settings → Tab Services. In the field "Map Service", either create or select one of the pre defined map services and click "Update".
