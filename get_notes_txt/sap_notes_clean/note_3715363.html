Symptom

Application error occurs during rehire an inactive employee, either in Onboarding and/or Employee Central.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental." 

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central
  • SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Cause

System is unable to process one or more files associated with the employee profile due to invalid or corrupted attachments.

Resolution

  1. Review the attachments related to the impacted employees either in Onboarding (PDC) or in the previous employment records;
  2. Delete any problematic or corrupted attachments;
  3. Re-submit the rehire.

Support Engineer: please refer to the internal memo for sample backend logs.
