Symptom

Cannot enable supplier evaluation product for SAP Ariba SLP product.

Reproducing the Issue

Resolution

Please refer to the documentation: Enabling Supplier Evaluation: A Checklist and perform the steps mentioned. 

Note that Currently, Supplier Evaluation is available for customers with SAP Ariba hosted in United States and European Union data centers only.
