Symptom

Why are private suppliers not receiving an invitation to the Invoice Status Portal?

Cause

The vendor Collaboration Type is not configured as Invoice Status Portal or Account type is selected as Enterprise account, which is preventing the buyer invite email to be not sent to the supplier.

 

Resolution

Please follow these steps to set the Collaboration Type as Invoice Status Portal.

  • Login to Buyer SAP Business Network account.
  • Navigate to Supplier Enablement > Manage Vendors.
  • Click on the vendor to whom you wish to send the invitation.
  • Update Account type as Standard account.
  • Scroll down to find the Invitation Letter Routing Information section.
  • In this section, select the Collaboration Type as Invoice Status Portal and click on Save. 

After these steps, return to the Manage Vendors page:

  • Select the vendor by selecting the checkbox and scroll down, click on Actions tab.
  • Select Invite Supplier to Invoice Status Portal.

    Now the invitation will be sent to the supplier. Please note, the Invitation Letter Routing Information option is only visible for Private suppliers or when a relationship has not yet been established.
