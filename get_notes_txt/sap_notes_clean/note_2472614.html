Symptom

You have set up a CO validation. You want to use information from the CO document header but the information is not provided when importing a CO document from a sender system into the Central Finance system.

Other Terms

CFIN;

SAP Simple Finance add-on for SAP Business Suite powered by SAP HANA, S4Hana Finance

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing functionality

Solution

This correction concerns the Central Finance system.
Implement the correction instructions or import the relevant Support Package.

Note: The CO document header is an optional parameter for the CO line item validation and may not be filled when FI documents are imported
