Symptom

  • I cannot access the system via Fieldglass.net
  • I get an error that the certificate of Fieldglass.net is expired
  • Complete TLS verification is not possible for Fieldglass.net

Cause

The SAP Fieldglass Network was migrated to hyperscalers resulting in a new URL

Resolution

Please see below to confirm the correct URL for your user type and region, and update any bookmarks accordingly

  • US

    • US suppliers and workers should use the US SAP Fieldglass Network URL: www.us.fieldglass.cloud.sap

    • US Buyer users have a URL that is specific to the buyer: xxxx.us.fieldglass.cloud.sap (where xxxx is your company code)

  • EU

    • EU suppliers and workers should use the EU SAP Fieldglass Network URL: www.eu.fieldglass.cloud.sap 

    • EU Buyer users have a URL that is specific to the buyer: xxxx.eu.fieldglass.cloud.sap (where xxxx is your company code) 

  • All AE instance users: https://www.fieldglass.ae/

  • All AU instance users: https://www.fieldglass.com.au/
