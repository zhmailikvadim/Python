Symptom

You are running regular payroll for current period and payment amount for bank transfer (wage type /559) is rounded correctly as you expected.
But if execute the payroll run with retroactive accounting of the previous period the rounded amount in /559 is different.   

For example, employee has been hired in 01 period with salary 104 000 RUB, rounding is customized to 100 RUB:
...
Payroll data in 03 period:
/550 Statutory net pay    87.034,00
/559 Bank transfer          87.100,00
/560 Net pay                   87.104,00
/56A Claim of company            4,00
/56B Claim of company          70,00

Payroll data in 04 period:
/550 Statutory net pay    87.035,00
/559 Bank transfer          87.000,00   <-- rounding is ok
/560 Net pay                   87.039,00
/56A Claim of company          39,00
/56B Claim of company            4,00

Payroll data in 04 period with retro-accounting of 03 period:
/550 Statutory net pay    87.035,00
/559 Bank transfer          86.900,00    <-- rounding is different
/560 Net pay                   86.969,00
/56A Claim of company          69,00
/56B Claim of company            4,00

Other Terms

PC00_M33_CALC HRUCALC0_CE RU0C /551 /552 RURND ORT_PERSON

Reason and Prerequisites

  • This SAP Note is relevant only for Russia and it affects only the Concurrent Employment (CE) localized payroll solution.
  • Value of rounding rule for bank transfers (processed in the payroll rule RU64) is specified in the feature 33RND.
  • XNET, XRETR, RUPAY functions are used to evaluate final payments includes retro runs and generate BT_PERSON records. RUPAY function is responsible for rounding of bank transfers wage type /559 and generates /56A and /56B wage types.

Solution

  • As a general rule, SAP recommends that you install a solution by applying a Support Package.
  • However, if you need to install a solution earlier, you must carry out manual activities that are valid for your release.
