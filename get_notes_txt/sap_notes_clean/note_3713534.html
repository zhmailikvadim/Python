Symptom

In Payroll Austria, a termination occurs in the garnishment if the field "Basic Amount Garnishment Monthly" (P0527-GBMFACH) is set in the infotype "Payment Upon Leaving A", but no information is set in the field "Months Garnishment". (P0527-ANGAB). As a result, a division by zero occurs.
