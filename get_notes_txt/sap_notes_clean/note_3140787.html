Symptom

The Audit details require a clean up procedure

Environment

SAP Identity Management 8.0

Reproducing the Issue

Check Audit Tables MXP_Ext_Audit and MXP_Audit to determine if the desired records are being cleared properly.

Cause

The statements are not deleting the audit logs past a particular time on Tables MXP_Ext_Audit and MXP_Audit

Resolution

Please follow this blog which covers the procedure to set up cleaning audit records and this SAP Help article.

Keywords

SAP IDM, 8.0, Audit, CleanUpOldAudits
