Symptom

On overview pages, scrolling can cause some screen elements to be displayed incorrectly. The tab bar at the top of the overview page, directly below the title area, is particularly affected. Instead of remaining fixed at the top of the screen as usual, it scrolls out of view along with the rest of the page.

Other Terms

  • CL_CRMS4_IU_UI_AS_VCO_BASE 
  • CL_CRMS4_IU_UI_OV_VCO_BASE
  • CL_CRMS4_IU_UI_VCO_BASE
  • DO_RENDER_CONTENTAREA_BEGIN
  • crms4iu_UI_contentArea
  • crms4iu_UI_runtimeData
  • div class="crms4iu_UI_contentArea"
  • div class="crms4iu_UI_runtimeData"

Reason and Prerequisites

 The cause is an incorrectly generated style="position: relative;" tag within the various view controllers.
