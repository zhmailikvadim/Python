Symptom

In transaction BP, you maintain the company code data of a business partner in a customer/vendor role (FLCU00/FLVN00). On the Payment Transactions tab page, you make entries in the dialog box for Permitted Payee/Permitted Payer. You maintain duplicate entries. The system does not display an error message due to duplicate entries.

Other Terms

BP, CVI, company code, SAPLCVI_FS_UI_CUSTOMER_ENH, SAPLCVI_FS_UI_VENDOR_ENH, 0012, 0010

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction or the mentioned Support Package.
