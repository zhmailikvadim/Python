Symptom

  • Inquiry about the existence of a template function for cases.

Environment

  • SAP for Me

Resolution

Strictly speaking, there are no "templates" available for creating cases.
However, you can use the "Draft Case" function as described in KBA 0002399199 - "How to use the Draft Case function - SAP for Me"

Draft cases made by you are visible for your colleagues who have the necessary authorizations.

For general information about how to open cases, and what authorizations are needed, please refer to KBA 0001296527 - "How to create a support case for SAP Support - SAP for Me".

You may also note that, there are components in SAP where opening cases on "High" or "Very High" priority is not possible. For further details please see KBA 0001522544 - "How to change the priority of a support case - SAP for Me".

In case of emergency situations, you can always reach out to CIC as per SAP Note 0000560499 - "Customer Interaction Center: Hotline - Email - Chat". (Even with an already opened Support case, or if there are issues with the case opening.)

If you have any installations that are supported by SAP Partners, it is also a good idea to take a note of those installations, as that has an effect on the support process. You can find the related information in the Installation Management application, and you may also refer to KBA 0002929126 - "How to add or update the partner listed in the 'Supported By' for an installation number - SAP for Me" for further details.
