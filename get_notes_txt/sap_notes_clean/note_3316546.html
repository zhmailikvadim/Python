Symptom

You create a purchase requisition and enter a vendor that has not yet been created in the system. You hold the document. Unfortunately you can no longer change the held document, e.g. with transaction ME52N. The transaction won't switch to Edit mode.

Other Terms

ME52N, ME53N, DPP, MM_DPP_SFW_01

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

You have activated business function ERP_CVP_ILM_1 - ILM-Based Deletion of Customer and Vendor Master Data.

Solution

Implement the correction instructions valid for your release into your system.
