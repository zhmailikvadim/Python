Symptom

The loan amount selected as part of an election allocation is not taken into account as a fixed loan amount in the (FAIR-based) process "Discharge AKD After Allocation" (/IBS/BSC_ALLOC_NCLCAL). Instead, the fixed loan amount is calculated on the basis of the credit.

Reason and Prerequisites

In the implementation /IBS/CL_BSC_SI_LOANSUM_CALC_V2, method CALCULATE_LOANSUM of the service "Service for Calculating Loan Amount" (/IBS/IF_BSC_SD_LOANSUM_CALC_2), the selected loan amount is evaluated only if the allocation option "Loan Total" (4) exists.

Solution

The implementation of the service mentioned above is adjusted in such a way that the selected loan amount is also taken into account if the selected loan amount is greater than zero and there is no allocation option.
