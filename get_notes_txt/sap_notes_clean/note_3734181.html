Symptom

While creating a Work Permit in PTW (component PM-FIO-WCM), document attachments added during the creation phase are not persisted:

  • Attachment is visible before pressing Create
  • After pressing Create, the attachment disappears
  • On entering change mode and adding a new attachment:
    • The previously added attachment is also created and becomes visible

This results in inconsistent attachment behavior and missing document records during initial creation.

Other Terms
