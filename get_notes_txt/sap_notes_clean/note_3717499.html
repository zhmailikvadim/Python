Symptom

When you use the Capture Field Data app (Fiori ID: F1041), the system responds slowly during data entry and navigation.

Other Terms

General keywords: IS-Oil, IS-OIL-UOM, UOM, Industry Solution, Oil and Gas Upstream, Upstream Operations and Management, Performance

Problem-specific keywords: Capture Field Data Performance.

Reason and Prerequisites

The system processes data inefficiently in the Capture Field Data app due to a program error.

Solution

After implementing this SAP Note, the system processes data more efficiently in the Capture Field Data app. You experience faster response times during data entry and navigation.
