Symptom

Mobile app (Android, iOS and Windows Client) ignores “Locked” state set on the Planning Board, handover and reassignment succeeds with no error message.

Environment

SAP Field Service Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in 'Shell'
  2. Navigate to 'Planning & Dispatching Board'

  3. On the Planning Board assign an activity to a technician

  4. Lock the assignment

  5. Log in mobile app

  6. Open the assigned activity

  7. Tap the three dots and select 'Handover to person'

  8. Select another technician

  9. Save the changes

Actual Behavior:
The activity can be handed over to another technician even when it is locked.

Expected Behavior:
It should not be possible to hand over the activity to another technician if the activity is locked

Cause

  • The 'Locked' feature only works on the "Planning Board" and "Partner Portal". Technically the handover does not consider the field "technicianFixed" in ServiceAssignment DTO.
  • The original idea of LOCKED feature was mainly for the dispatcher, who could lock an activity by time, technician, or both.
    This was meant to make sure that once the dispatcher and the customer agreed on a date or time, it would stay fixed and no one else or the system could change it.

Resolution

There are two solutions to prevent an handover:

  1. Business Rule: Implement a validation business rule to prevent the handover on mobile app (link at 'See Also'). 
  2. Screen Configuration: Hide the handover action in the mobile app (link at 'See Also').

See Also

  • SAP Help Portal official documentation about 'Validation Business Rule' and 'Screen configuration'.
