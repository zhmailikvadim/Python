Symptom

In the Job Requisition, the Job Profile tab is not showing the Role name.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Cause

  • The field jobRole is not configured in the Job Requisition Template.
  • The operator does not have permission to the jobRole field in the Job Requisition Template.

Resolution

The Role entry in the Job Profile tab is controlled by the jobRole field in the Job Requisition Template.

In order to see this value, it is necessary to configure the field in the Job Requisition Template, and grant the users at least read permission to read on it.
