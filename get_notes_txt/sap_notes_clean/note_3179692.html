Question

How do I create a Pricing Alternative?

Answer

Your buyer must allow alternative bidding, and you can only submit an alternative bid for the items you have already bid on in your primary bid. After you have submitted your first bid:

  1. Go to the event checklist in the left pane and click Review Event Details.
  2. Click Create Alternative > Pricing Alternative.
  3. Enter a value in the Alternative Name field.
  4. Select the items you want to include in your alternative bid.
  5. Click OK when you are finished selecting your items. The pricing alternatives will show in a new tab.
  6. Modify your alternative bid as necessary. Keep the following in mind as you create your pricing alternative:
    • Update Totals will update your alternative bid totals.
    • Add or Delete in the Pricing menu will add or delete items from the current pricing alternative bid.
    • Sync Primary Bid will reset your alternative bid values to the values from your primary bid.
    • Reload Last Bid will clear any changes in the bid entry boxes and display the last valid bid you submitted.
  7. Click Submit Alternative Response when you are ready to submit your alternative bid.
Additional Information

You cannot delete an alternative response after it has been submitted. After an alternative response is submitted, only buyers can delete alternative responses after the response is submitted. You can delete alternative bids before you submit your response.
