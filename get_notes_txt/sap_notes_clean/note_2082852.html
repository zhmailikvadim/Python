Symptom

Dump occurs when you execute article/material maintenance dialog transactions (MM01|MM41) in systems where switch "SFSW_FASHION_02" is not available

Other Terms

Fashion switch check, swich SFSW_FASHION_02

Reason and Prerequisites

This is caused due to program error

Solution

The program corrections are contained in the tab Correction Instruction of this note. You need to implement the corrections in your system.
