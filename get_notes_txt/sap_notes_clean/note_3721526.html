Symptom

In the SAP Fiori app Manage PCNs, stock in multiple depots can be involved in relabeling, and orders may be processed at different times, beginning with Reserve Medkits. But when the GR has been performed for stock in the first depot, the PCN status changes to "In Quality Inspection" , and a user wanting to relabel stock in a different depot isn't shown the Reserve Medkits button. Currently the button is only shown for PCNs with status "In Preparation" and "Revision in Progress". It should also be possible to reserve medication kits for relabeling when the PCN status is "In Quality Inspection".

Other Terms

ISLS, Manufacturing, Intelligent Clinical Supply Management, Manage PCNs, Reserve Medkits.

Reason and Prerequisites

You use SAP Intelligent Clinical Supply Management or this SAP Note is a prerequisite of another SAP Note.

Solution

Implement the attached correction.
