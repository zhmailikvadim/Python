Symptom

After upgrading to SAP S/4HANA 2023 version, the application F0940 "Cost centers-Actuals" displays the field "Cost center (Node)" instead of "Cost center (plain field/without a prefix)" in the selection screen. 

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP Fiori
  • SAP Fiori for SAP S/4HANA Finance

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to "Cost centers-Actuals" Fiori application.
  2. Observe that the cost center has the prefix "NODE" as highlighted in screen shot in yellow. 

 

Cause

Standard system behaviour.

Resolution

  • Implement SAP Notes 3521368 and 3645610 to enable input of the Cost Center without a prefix.
  • After implementation, ensure the Controlling Area (CAC) is assigned to the user. This behavior also applies to G/L Accounts.
  • If the issue persists, adjust the annotations in the relevant CDS view, or alternatively add a new field (for example, Cost Center 2) in the cube and then in the query.

Note: A single field can have only one annotation; therefore, hierarchy and single-value input cannot be combined on the same field.
