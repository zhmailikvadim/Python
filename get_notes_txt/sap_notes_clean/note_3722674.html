Symptom

As described in the Regulatory Change Manager under Update of family benefit amounts for 2026, this SAP Note provides you with the relevant system changes to address the Additional Amount Calculation for Family Allowance presented in Portaria n.º 60/2026/1, published on February 5, 2026.

For more information, also see https://diariodarepublica.pt/dr/detalhe/portaria/60-2026-1038101444. The website that can be accessed using this hyperlink is an external website which is not part of SAP's offering.

Solution

As of January 1, 2026, the additional amount for numerous families is updated for each dependent in the Família/pessoa de referência (0021) infotype who is between 12 and 36 months old.

  • If the employee has one single eligible dependent for Family Allowance, the additional amount is not calculated.
  • If the employee has two eligible dependents for Family Allowance, the additional amount is calculated according to the Escalão de Rendimentos Familiar (FISCA) field presented in the Tax PT (0331) infotype, as described below:
    • First Family Income Scale (FISCA = 1): EUR 64,96
    • Second Family Income Scale (FISCA = 2): EUR 57,64
    • Third Family Income Scale (FISCA = 3): EUR 54,35
    • Fourth Family Income Scale (FISCA = 4): EUR 39,28
  • If the employee has three or more eligible dependents for Family Allowance, the additional amount is also calculated according to the FISCA field:
    • First Family Income Scale (FISCA = 1): EUR 106,96
    • Second Family Income Scale (FISCA = 2): EUR 92,32
    • Third Family Income Scale (FISCA = 3): EUR 85,76
    • Fourth Family Income Scale (FISCA = 4): EUR 55,70
  • If the employee is in the Fifth Family Income Scale (FISCA = 5), the additional amount is not calculated for the dependent.

Example:

Proceed with the following steps to reproduce the symptom mentioned in this SAP Note.

Description

Family Allowance - Additional amount calculation for 2026

Preparation/Background

1. Hire an employee on 01/01/2026 in the Third Family Income Scale (FISCA = 3).

2. Add a dependent born on 01/02/2023.

3. Add another dependent born on 01/02/2023.

4. Add a third dependent born on 01/10/2024.

Execution

1. Run payroll for 01/2026.

Result

1. In the Result Table (RT) table in the payroll log for period 01/2026, you will find the following:

1.1. The Benefício para Filho (FA01) wage type with the amount of EUR 644,97.

1.2. The Abono monoparental (FA09) wage type with the amount of EUR 322,50.
