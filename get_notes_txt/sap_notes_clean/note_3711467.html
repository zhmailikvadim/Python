Symptom

  • Inquiry about the end of mainstream maintenance date for SAP BPC 11.1 for SAP BW/4HANA 2.0.

Environment

SAP BPC 11.1 for SAP BW/4HANA 2.0

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Refer to SAP Note 3362512 and access the link 'BPC11.1 PAM'.
  2. Observe that the link redirects to the PAM, displaying SAP BPC 2021 for SAP BW/4HANA.
  3. Note that the end of mainstream maintenance date for SAP BPC 11.1 for SAP BW/4HANA 2.0 is not listed in the PAM.

Resolution

The SAP Business Planning and Consolidation (SAP BPC) Strategy Update blog, confirms the end of mainstream maintenance date as 31.12.2025.

SAP Business Planning and Consolidation (SAP BPC) Strategy Update
