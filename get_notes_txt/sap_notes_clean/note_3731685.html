Symptom

You create or change a retail article and assign a special procurement type for stock transfer or production in other location.

While saving the article, the system does not create a corresponding transportation lane for planning in PP/DS.

Other Terms

MM41, MM42, MARC-SOBSL, /SAPAPO/SCC_TL1

Reason and Prerequisites

You have activated Advanced Planning for the given article.

Missing functionality.

With this new development, the lanes are already created during assignment of the special procurement type in the corresponding article. In case of generic articles, the corresponding lanes are also created for all variants (if feasible).

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction or the mentioned Support Package.
