Symptom

Salesforce has announced that new connected apps can no longer be created from 2026 Spring. 

Environment

Sales Cloud CPQ integrated with SalesForce

Reproducing the Issue

-

Cause

Changes in Salesforce

Resolution

  • The announced changes regarding external apps by Salesforce will not affect current usage of CPQ, as Salesforce will be keeping providing support for existing implementations that uses Connected Apps
  • Changes on SalesForce side will be required in the future, and CPQ documentation will provide the needed information when available.
