Symptom

Issue with product, provisioning, or integration during the end to end HR process 

Environment

  • SAP managed Workforce Management
  • Early Adopters

Resolution

Log a case for Customer Support to assist:

1. Go to the Get Support application in SAP for Me.   

2. Log a case under the following Component:  XX-PART-TST-RIG-EBS

Note:  Follow KBA 3364668 - How to select/enter support component manually when creating a case - SAP for Me  to get to the Component field to select it.

Refer to the KBA 126527 - How to create a support case for SAP Support - SAP for Me for step by step information.
