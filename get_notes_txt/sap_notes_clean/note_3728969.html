Symptom

You execute Template Allocation using parallelization.

In some cases you can find in the header of the Spool List the following misleading comment: 'Update executed only partially due to errors'

However, Template Allocation got posted for all orders of the selection with appropriate status.

Other Terms

Paralleization

Transactions: CPAE, CPAS, CPPE, CPPS, CPTA, CPTB, CPTD, CPTE, CPTG, CPTH, CPTJ, CPTK, CPTL, CPUA, CPUB, CPUK, CPUL

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error
