Symptom

  • The Underdelivery Tolerance flag (UNTKZ) is inactive in the Product Master
  • The field Underdelivery Tolerance is not editable in transaction /SAPAPO/MAT1

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • Extended Production Planning and Scheduling
  • Production Planning and Detailed Scheduling

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open transaction /SAPAPO/MAT1 for the affected Location-Product
  2. Navigate to the "Lot Size" tab and confirm that the Underdel. Tol. flag is inactive

Cause

This setting in the PP/DS Product Master is automatically mapped from the Underdelivery Tolerance maintained for the Lot Sizing Procedure assigned in the MRP 1 view of the Material Master

Resolution

  1. Verify the Lot Sizing Procedure assigned in the MRP 1 view of the Material Master, then ensure the Underdelivery Tolerance flag is active for it
