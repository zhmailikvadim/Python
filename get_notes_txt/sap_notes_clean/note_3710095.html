Symptom

For migrated cases, the system does not display the position copies and the error with cartinality of the role occurs.

Reason and Prerequisites

In these cases, the field INVESTED_PROCESS is not filled in TFM_FIB, which is why the position copies are not displayed.

Solution

A new DI was provided that is called before the query for the field INVESTED_PROCES. The customer then has the option of prefilling/adjusting the field for the migrated cases.
