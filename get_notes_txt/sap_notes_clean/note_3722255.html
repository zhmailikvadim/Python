Symptom

  • Clarification requested regarding whether standard SAP provides functionality for storing customer-specific additional data (addenda) in the context of electronic invoicing requirements in Mexico.
  • Inquiry about whether standard ECC system captures or stores addenda information as part of the billing process or within related Sales objects.

Environment

  • Document Compliance Mexico
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Document Reporting and Compliance service

Cause

  • In standard SAP ECC, there is no built-in functionality that captures or stores "addenda" (customer-specific commercial data) in SD billing or Sales objects.
  • For Mexico electronic invoicing, addenda is an optional, commercial free-text node in the XML and is not part of SAT's technical layout.
  • SAP does not deliver a standard field, table, or form in ECC to maintain or persist addenda content as part of the billing process.

Resolution

  1. Addenda must be injected into the eInvoice XML during eDocument generation via custom ABAP logic.
  2. Use the eDocument Mexico BAdI EDOC_ADAPTOR (method SET_OUTPUT_DATA) as the typical entry point to build and insert the Addenda node into the XML.
  3. If the process requires an addenda check/trigger when sending to customers, use BAdI EDOC_PARTNER_CONNECTOR (method IF_EDOC_PARTNER_CONNECTOR~TRIGGER).
