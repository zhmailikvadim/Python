Symptom

I am getting an error: Your attempt exceeds the free publishing limit. Unlock unlimited publishing by adding the promote subscription to your account while publishing a Network Catalog in SAP Business Network

Cause

You have exceeded the maximum number of publishing actions on network catalog products without Promote subscription.

Resolution

  • You can perform a maximum of 1000 publishing actions on network catalog products without Promote subscription.
  • With Promote subscription, you can publish an unlimited number of products on your network catalog to showcase your full suite of products and services to potential customers.
