Symptom

  • During the EWM Quality Inspection process, when attempting to record a negative partial Usage Decision (e.g., Scrap or Blocked) in QA11, the system triggers a requirement for repacking.
  • The error message displayed is: "Repack. insp. items in HU && that have no putaway or post. chg. decision" (Message no. /SCWM/QUI_WRKST104).
  • This means, that if the internal action (FUINT) linked to the follow-up action is anything other than from 'Posting Change' or 'Putaway', repacking is mandatory.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management (SCM-EWM)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to QA11, press "EWM Inspection" button and post a negative Usage Decision for part of the stock in the HU.
  2. Click on the "Close HU" button.
  3. Observe the error message: "Repack. insp. items in HU && that have no putaway or post. chg. decision" (Message no. /SCWM/QUI_WRKST104).

Cause

Standard system behavior. In case partial decision is required with different decision types, the system does not allow to have multiple items within a single HU during partial UD and it is not possible to move one HU into two different places during the follow-up action processing (e.g. one to the destination bin as a free stock, and one to the scrapping zone or somewhere else where it would belong as a rejected stock), therefore repacking is necessary. 

Resolution

  1. During the partial Usage Decision process (on EWM Inspection screen), repack the rejected stock part into a new Handling Unit (HU) first.
  2. After repacking the rejected part, post and trigger the follow-up action and close the source HU.

If sample split process is used, and repacking is not possible (due to business reasons), alternatively, use a sample quantity equal to the HU quantity in order to avoid the repacking requirement. Refer to SAP KBA 3496217 - Change split sample quantity.
