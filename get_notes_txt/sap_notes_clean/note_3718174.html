Symptom

Invoice document coming from SAP Business Network to S4HANA is failing in Managed gateway for spend management and SAP Business Network with below error.

Fault response received from: ANXXXXXXXXXXX-T for:ANXXXXXXXXXXX-T with Status:FAILED and ErrorResponse:Could not send Message... Suggested Action : Based on the details of error response, please review following Support KBA for solutions: https://support.ariba.com/item/view/KB0402310 or https://me.sap.com/notes/3181644. If you continue to experience problems, please contact support with the error code CIG-PLT-00676.

Environment

S4HANA

Cause

In active IDOC Inbound SOAP service in S4HANA.

Resolution

To Resolve the issue follow the below steps.

    Navigate to SRTIDOC transaction code in ERP/S4HANA.

    1. Choose cancel registration button and execute it.
    2. Re-Execute the SRTIDOC services by choosing Register button.
    3. Inbound IDOC services has been activated.
