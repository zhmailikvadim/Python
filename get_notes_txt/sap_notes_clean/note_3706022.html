Symptom

Joule picks incorrect scenario for given prompts related to sales order information.

Some example prompts which are fixed in this release:

What are the issues of sales order number xxxxxx?

Who is the sold-to party in sales order xxxx?

What is the overall billing status of the delivery xxxxx?

When will the sales order xxxx be delivered?

Does the sales order xxxx have incomplete data?

What is the sequence of documents in the document flow for sales order xxxxxx?

Reason and Prerequisites

You want to get additional information about a specific bugfix of SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition Joule Capabilities 2601

Solution

The issue described as the Symptom is solved in the SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition Joule Capabilities 2601
