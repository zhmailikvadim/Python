Symptom

  • In collections management, the error "Business partner was not translated into a customer" appears.
  • Message no. FDM_COLLECTION108.
  • The error occurs when working in UDM_SPECIALIST or when navigating via UDM_SUPERVISOR to process receivables for a business partner.

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • Collections Management
  • SAP Enhancement Package for ERP

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Start UDM_SPECIALIST and attempt to access/process receivables for a business partner.
  2. Alternatively, open UDM_SUPERVISOR, go to Process -> Process Receivables -> Other Business Partner, enter the business partner and segment.
  3. Observe that the system displays: "Business partner was not translated into a customer" (Message no. FDM_COLLECTION108).

Cause

The business partner is not created with the required FI Customer role FLCU00, so the business partner is not recognized as a customer in Collections Management.

Resolution

In collections management, the BP need to be created as a customer using a BP role such as FLCU00. This is the two basis roles to be used on Collections Management:

  • FLCU00 -> FI Customer
  • UDM000 -> Collections Management

Solution:

  1. Verify the roles assigned to the business partner in your system.
  2. If the FI Customer role FLCU00 is missing, create/extend the business partner with role FLCU00.
