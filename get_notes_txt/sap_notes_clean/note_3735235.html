Symptom

  • The hours on my Time Sheet are calculating a different rate than I am supposed to be paid
  • The Time Sheet is not calculating the correct pay rate

Resolution

As a contract worker, the amount on your Time Sheet may differ from the payment you receive directly. Please reach out to your staffing agency (the company that recruited you to work on contract)

Keywords

payment, wrong amount on time sheet, different rate, miscalculated rate, rate calculation, time sheet calculation incorrect
