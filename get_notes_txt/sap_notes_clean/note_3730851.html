Symptom

This KBA explains how to generate the new SPL quota after implementing note 3557781 - LC: New Shared Parental Leave (SPL) Scheme on April 1, 2025.

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll

Resolution

The previous Shared Parental Leave (SPL) scheme was a quota type that allowed a working mother to share up to 4 weeks of her 16 weeks of Government-Paid Maternity Leave (GPML) with her husband. This meant it was only applicable to male employees.

The current SPL scheme, which will cease from April 1, 2025, will transition to a new type of quota shared by both mother and father. This means it can now be applied to both female and male employees.

Quota capping:

  • Phase 1: Eligible working parents of Singapore citizen children born on or after April 1, 2025, will be entitled to 6 weeks of SPL.
  • Phase 2: Eligible working parents of Singapore citizen children born on or after April 1, 2026, will be entitled to 10 weeks of SPL.
