Symptom

 Error occurs during device replacement process once a new change process is executed after cancelling an existing change process

Other Terms

 APEU, MCM, device replacement, cancellation, /APEU/CL_MCM_COMMON_UTILITY=>GET_INSTANCE_DATA_FROM_OBJECT

Reason and Prerequisites

 Program error, SP validity(10206-10207), 2504

Solution

Relevant API has now been adjusted to ignore records corresponding to the cancelled version of the Instance.

As a result, only the relevant records are now being fetched and passed on to the follow on processes.
