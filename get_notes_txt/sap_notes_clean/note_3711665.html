Symptom

You cannot edit the Material Staging Date or Material Staging Time fields in an outbound delivery after the delivery is distributed to Extended Warehouse Management (EWM).

Other Terms

Outbound Delivery Item, Late Changes, MBDAT, MBUHR, IEW, IDW, ERP can change delivery after distribution, Deliv.Chg

Reason and Prerequisites

This is expected behavior.

The delivery is not editable after distribution unless you configure it to allow changes as described in the documentation for Integration with Extended Warehouse Management (EWM). However, Material Staging Date and Material Staging Time are not currently included in the list of editable fields and are not editable even if the Customizing is correctly configured.

EWM is the leading component for the process and determines which fields are editable.

Solution

Implement this SAP Note or the relevant feature package.

This SAP Note adds the Material Staging Date and Material Staging Time fields to the list of fields that you can edit after distribution. Changes to the Material Staging Date or Time are not communicated to EWM or Transportation Management (TM) after distribution. The update is effective only in Logistics Execution (LE).
