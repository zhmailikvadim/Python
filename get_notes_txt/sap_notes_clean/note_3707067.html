Symptom

• After archiving the /SAPSLL/SPLAUD table using the SPL_AT archive object, 
• There are entries left behind with blank month and year.

Environment

• Global Trade Services (GTS)
• Global Trade Services / Logistics Services (SLL)
• SAP GTS Edition for Hana.

Reproducing the Issue

• Go to transaction SARA in GTS.
• Archive the /SAPSLL/SPLAUD table using the SPL_AT archive object.
• Observe that entries with blank year remain in the table after the archiving process.
 

Cause

Entries with blank YEAR values are associated with Business Partner data

Resolution

• The entries with blank year in the table /SAPSLL/SPLAUD is related to Business partner data and not related to document data. 
• And the year field refers to the document year that was screened. 
• So, the year field can be expected it to be filled only if the audit record refers a document.
• For the business partner there would be no document year can be displayed.

Keywords

SLL-LEG, GTS-CORE, SARA, SPL_AT, /SAPSLL/SPLAUD, blank year, Archiving object.
