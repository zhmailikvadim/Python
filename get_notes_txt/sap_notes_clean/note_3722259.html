Symptom

The Navigation Panel is missing in saved custom tiles, and previously saved bookmarks cannot be opened after SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 2602.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 2602

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the "Custom Tile" and "Preview" 
  2. Attempt to open saved bookmarks.
  3. Observe that the navigation panel is missing, values in filters are missing, and bookmarks cannot be opened.

Cause

Resolution

A fix will be delivered with HFC06(14/15 March).
