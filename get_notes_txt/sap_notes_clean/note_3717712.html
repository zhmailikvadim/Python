Symptom

When a buyer clicks the 'Social Message' (chat) icon on a record such as a Work Order or Job Posting, the icon indicates there are messages, but none are displayed.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Sign in to your SAP Fieldglass account;
  2. Select a record such as a Work Order;
  3. Click on the chat icon on the right

Cause

Chat messages where sent on the Supplier tab and user is looking into the My Company tab.

The 'Social Message' (chat) functionality will always display two tabs (with the exception of the Job Posting Module):

  • SOW, Worker and Work Order modules:
    • My Company: Chat message will be sent to the record owner, and will be visible to anyone in your company who can view this record. 
    • Supplier: Chat message will be visible to your company and to users from a specified Supplier Company
  • Job Posting module:
    • My Company: Chat message will be sent to the Job Postings owner and Program Office, and will be visible to anyone in your company who can view this Job Posting.
    • Supplier: Chat message will be visible to your company and to users from a specified Supplier Company
    • All Suppliers: Chat message will be visible to your company and all the Suppliers that can view this Job Posting.

 

Resolution

Select the correct tab to see the respective messages.
