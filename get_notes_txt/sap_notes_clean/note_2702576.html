Symptom

Who should I contact at SAP CDC to discuss details about my account or contract?

Environment

SAP Customer Data Cloud

Cause

For more information about the tasks that the Account Manager can assist with please refer to CDC's Scope of Support

Resolution

For assistance with business matters related to an account or contract the customer should contact their Account Manager/CEE.  If the SAP contact is not known, open a ticket with CDC Support for assistance.

Keywords

Gigya, features, license, account manager, CEE
