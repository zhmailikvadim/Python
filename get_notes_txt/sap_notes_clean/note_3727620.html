Symptom

You use SIL to implement a new accounting principle and execute the cockpit "Implementation of an Accounting Principle". The following situations may occur:

  • In the step SLP, among other things, a document from a legacy data transfer is subsequently posted. If a retirement has already been entered for the affected asset, this may lead to error AA478 - "Posting transaction not possible (asset value date not allowed)" during subsequent posting.
  • A retirement was entered for an asset, e.g. A partial retirement. Afterwards, a new acquisition was posted to this asset, but no further retirement followed. When you subsequently post this constellation in the step SLP, the last retirement date (ANLA-ABGDT) may be deleted for this asset. This is incorrect.
       

Other Terms

SIL, SLP, AA478, AA 478, ANLA-ABGDT, ANLB-ABGDAT_B

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the specified corrections in your system.

Then reset the step SLP and execute it again.
