Symptom

An Time-Series supply operator is executed and the freeze horizon maintained in attribute PFREEZEHORIZON (In the production source header master data table) or TFREEZEHORIZON (In Location Source master data table) that should be considered in days (as per the SAP Help – Production Source of Supply Header and Location Source of Supply) but is being considered in the operator time profile level, like weeks or months.

 

Example:

Freeze Horizon = 21 days

Operator time profile level = Week.

 

Instead of considering the freeze horizon as 21 days, the operator is considering as 21 weeks.

Environment

  • SAP Supply Chain Management (SCM)
  • SAP Integrated Business Planning (SCM-IBP)

Cause

The planning grid has an unknown periodicity, in the business log of the operator execution can be seen the warning message number 8079 “Freeze horizon conversion isn't possible (reason: Unknown planning periodicity type).”

This may happen in case there are periods that are maintained in different patterns during the whole horizon, for example, all weeks starting on Monday and ending on the Sunday – but some start on Monday and ends in the next Monday – such different creates such unknown periodicity and the freeze horizon cannot be converted.

Resolution

There are 2 possible solutions:

1 – Adjusting the planning area periods so such difference doesn’t happen.

2 – Use parameter additional parameter PLANNING_PERIODICITY in the operator with value as per the periodicity you are using (CALENDAR_WEEK or MONTH, for example).
