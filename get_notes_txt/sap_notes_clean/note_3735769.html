Symptom

User needs to reprint a document after the initial print job has been completed, or needs to reprint the document with different print selection than were used in the original print.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Resolution

  1. Open Post Goods Movement (MIGO) app;
  2. Choose "Change" "Material Document";
  3. Enter the material document that is needed to reprint;
  4. Press "Execute";
  5. Go to the output tab and press Display Output;
