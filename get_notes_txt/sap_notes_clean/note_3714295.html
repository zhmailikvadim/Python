Symptom

Following the 2602 upgrade, SAP announce a new product offering, which introduces BusinessObjects Crystal Reports integration with SAP Incentive Management. It allows users to generate detailed, formatted, pixel-perfect, and customizable reports based on sales compensation data for strategic insights. Users can design and publish SQL-based reports in PDF/XLS/Crystal formats through seamless integration with Incentive Management. 

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
  • Incentive Management on Hyperscalers

Resolution

By transforming compensation information into meaningful insights, these reports help drive improved performance and profitability, making them ideal for documents such as invoices or financial statements that require high visual accuracy.

  • Crystal Reports is a new SKU (available in the 2602 release) that must be added to your account via a contract amendment or a new contract.
  • Once the order is processed, the environment(s) can be configured according to the entitlement to Crystal Reports integration in Incentive Management
  • Your Account Manager (AM), Sales rep, or Customer Success Manager (CSM) will provide details and prepare for the required amendment or contract.

Recommended next steps:

  1. Please reach out to your AM, Sales rep, or CSM to request adding the Crystal Reports SKU to your contract. They are best equipped to handle pricing, billing, contract language, PO requirements, and timelines.  
  2. To help your account team process your request, please provide these basic details (they will handle the rest):
    • Company name and tenant(s)
    • Product names:
      • SAP Incentive Management, Enterprise Edition - 8020863
      • SAP Incentive Management, Enterprise Edition Additional Non-Production Tenant - 8020912
  3. Desired effective date and billing preferences
  4. Sales will coordinate the amendment or new contract and will process the Sales Order and once the contract or amendment is executed, Crystal Reports integration on your Incentive Management environments will be enabled.  Your account team can give you a more specific timeline.
  5. While the contract work is underway, you can prepare your report developers:
    • Purchase SAP Crystal Reports (Crystal Reports 2025 Patch 300 recommended):  
      • SAP Crystal Reports

How can SAP Support Team help:

  • We are happy to draft a concise email you can send to your account team with the required details and a request for pricing, the amendment, and an estimated enablement timeline.
  • If you prefer, we can also reach out to your AM, Sales rep, or CSM directly on your behalf.
