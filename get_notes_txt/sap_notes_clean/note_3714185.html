Symptom

I want to report the Team tab groups in Analytical Reports by using the Contract Workspace and Project Group facts to be able to see the team members and groups related to the contracts.

Resolution

You can add Project Group as a fact in your Data Sources tab, when creating the report.

The fields Project Group Name and Member Name from the Project Group fact will bring the data related to the Team tab.
