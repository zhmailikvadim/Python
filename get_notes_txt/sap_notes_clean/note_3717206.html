Symptom

An issue occurs during the Group Reporting ledger realignment process.

Certain G/L accounts fail to map correctly to their corresponding FS Items, despite being properly maintained in the "Map FS Items with G/L Accounts" configuration.

Realignment log shows: "No data found. Please, check the input. Processing stopped."

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

The specific G/L account  like 12300000 does not exist for the specified company codes (e.g., 2304) with period 011/2025, ledger 0L. As a result, the realignment job cannot access these accounts.
