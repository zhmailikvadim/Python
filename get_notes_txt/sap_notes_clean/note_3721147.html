Symptom

In the process "Receipt of Status of EQS Time Series Version (PRS View)" (DE_B_EQS_STATUS_RECEIVE_PRS), the process gets stuck at step 0400 and does not proceed to step 0500.
This issue is relevant for the market role: PRS.

Other Terms

Settlement, MaBiS, EQS, check status, DE_B_EQS_STATUS_RECEIVE_PRS, PDoc, precondition, time series version

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because of an incomplete configuration.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system processes the complete workflow by correcting the precondition in step 0500.
Import the BC set /US4G/3_0_PRS_SP09_NOTE_3721147 to apply the fix. You need to rerun the PDoc for process DE_B_EQS_STATUS_RECEIVE_PRS. You can verify the fix by checking that the PDoc completes successfully.
