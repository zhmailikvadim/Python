Symptom

  • In the BI application, within the history of a Web Intelligence (WebI) or crsytal report, when right-clicking on a recurring schedule, the context menu appears as expected.
  • However, when attempting to cancel the action by left-clicking outside the context menu (on the same schedule row), the schedule is unintentionally triggered/executed.This leads to unintended execution of the recurring schedule.
  • No error message is displayed.
  • The behavior appears to be incorrect UI interaction handling.
