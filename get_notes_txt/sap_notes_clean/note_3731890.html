Symptom

  • When using Export Invoices to Spreadsheet in the invoice list, the export is limited to a maximum of 5000 records (.xlsx).
  • Is there a way to increase this limit or is there other tool to export more records?

Environment

SAP Ariba Invoicing

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the invoice list in Manage Supplier Invoices Centrally
  2. Choose Export Invoices to Spreadsheet
  3. Observe that the generated .xlsx file contains at most 5000 records

Resolution

The maximum number of rows that can be exported is 5000. You can narrow down the list of invoices by using filters as needed prior to exporting it, which can help you narrow down your invoice list to fewer than 5000 invoices and ensure that the exported spreadsheet contains all relevant entries.
