Symptom

When maintaining TAN-Based Exemption details in the Business Partner application (India), an error during saves. After entering the exemption details with a withholding tax type, the following error message is displayed: "Enter the correct withholding tax_type".

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Business Partner application.
  2. Navigate to India, TAN-Based Exemption section.
  3. Enter exemption details with withholding tax type.
  4. Attempt to save the configuration.

Cause

An incorrect or incomplete configuration of the withholding tax type maintained in the TAN-Based Exemption section

Resolution

  1. Ensure that the relevant withholding tax type and corresponding tax code are accurately defined and properly maintained within the withholding tax configuration activities.
  2. Assign the withholding tax details (withholding tax type, tax code) in the "Supplier: Withholding Tax" tab and save it.
  3. Navigate to the "Supplier: Country-Spec. Enh." tab and provide the details for "TAN-based Exemption" and save it.

Note: Ensure that exemption details (e.g., exemption number, reason, to/from date, and percentage) are maintained only in the "Supplier: Country-Spec. Enh." tab and not in the "Supplier: Withholding Tax" tab.
