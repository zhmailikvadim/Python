Symptom

Component: Foundation for EHS Management
Module: Environmental Management

Ranges in Environmental Limits are displayed as follows: <lower_limit> <= <subject> <= <upper_limit> <unit>

If no subject is given, instead the dimension of the unit should be displayed.

Example: 10 <= Temperature <= 20 °C

Other Terms

CL_EHFND_REQ_HELPER, GET_LIMIT_COMBINED_VALUE, GET_LIMITS_COMBINED_VALUES

Reason and Prerequisites

The symptom occurs due to a program error.

Prerequisites are available in the relevant correction instructions.

Solution

After implementing this SAP Note, the system determines the dimension of the unit to display if no subject is given.

Alternatively, import either of the corresponding Support Packages for the component extension of SAP EHS Management or the Feature Packages Stacks for SAP S/4HANA.
