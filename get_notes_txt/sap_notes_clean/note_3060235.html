Symptom

Personal data is often replicated from SAP S/4HANA systems into other systems. According to some Data Protection and Privacy regulations, this data must be deleted or depersonalized both in the source and the target systems when its primary business purpose has ended. Nevertheless whenever data is blocked or deleted during ILM processes in a system, the key information of this data is not available for other connected systems.

Other Terms

Data Protection and Privacy,  Information Lifecycle Management, ILM_NOTIFICATION, ILM Notifications, Data Protection Notifications, ILM Events, Just-In-Time, JIT, LE-JIT-S2P ILM Notification

Reason and Prerequisites

The following business functions must be activated in your system:

  • ILM
  • ILM_NOTIFICATION

Solution

To support customers in this requirement, ILM Notification Framework provisions the intelligence to capture the key information during ILM enabled data processing. ILM notifications contain information about ILM events based on ILM objects (for example, data archiving or data destruction for a business object instance, like a sales order).
