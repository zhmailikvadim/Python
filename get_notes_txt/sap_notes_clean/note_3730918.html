Symptom

In the process "Metering Point Modification (MOS View)" (DE_B_WIM_MPM_MOS), step 300 blocks the process for RLM meters. The step checks whether meter readings exist, but meter readings are not relevant for RLM meters. As a result, the system always determines that meter readings don't exist and blocks the process.

Other Terms

US4G; MPM; DE_B_WIM_MPM_MOS; /US4G/CL_CHK_WIM_PROCESS; CHECK_MR_VALID; /US4G/CL_PRST_DET_DC_MR; ENHANCE_STEP_DATA

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.
