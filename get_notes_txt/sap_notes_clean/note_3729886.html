Symptom

A country-specific requirement is raised at SAP Support. The solution, clarification or answer is referenced in a document or web portal specific to the country.

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA

Resolution

  1. Review the referenced link provided in the ticket for further details.
  2. Access the official documentation published by the respective country.
