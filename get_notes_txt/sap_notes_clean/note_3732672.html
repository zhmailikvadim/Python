Symptom

Tax calculation in HongKong payroll is handled by IRD.

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll
  • SAP S/4 HANA

Resolution

This tax calculation process is completely independent of SAP production system.
HongKong payroll operates under the following mechanism:

  1. No Preliminary Tax Withholding: HongKong does not implement a preliminary withholding tax (预扣预缴) system.
  2. IR56 Report Generation: The payroll driver only processes wagetypes related to the IR56 report, which is the mandatory employer's return of remuneration.
  3. Government Tax Administration: The generated IR56 report is submitted to the HongKong government's Inland Revenue Department (IRD).
  4. IRD Tax Calculation: The IRD independently calculates tax obligations for employees with submitted IR56 report and directly deducts the tax from their remuneration.

Keywords

IRD, tax calculation, IR56, HKTAX
