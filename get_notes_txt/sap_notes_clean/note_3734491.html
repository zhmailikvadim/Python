Resolution

Background:

Due to a recent policy change issued by the Tianjin Tax Authority (国家税务总局天津市税务局关于公布失效废止的税务规范性文件目录的公告), the fixed medical insurance amount (RMB 21-22) is no longer eligible for tax exemption.

Those who maintained this fixed amount in Infotype 0532 (Basic Medical Insurance) may experience incorrect tax calculations because the data within Infotype 0532 continues to be treated as tax-exempt.

Under the new policy, the fixed medical insurance amount (RMB 21-22) must not be stored in any of the following Infotypes related to medical insurance:

  • 0532: Basic Medical Insurance
  • 3532: Supplementary Medical Insurance
  • 0015: Major Disease Deduction

This amount was previously maintained in Infotype 0532, which automatically applied tax exemption, contrary to the updated regulation.

Resolution Steps:

To address this issue, please follow these steps:

  1. Remove Fixed Medical Insurance from Infotype 0532:
    Ensure the fixed medical insurance amount (RMB 21-22) is deleted from Infotype 0532 to prevent incorrect tax exemption.

  2. Create a Custom Wage Type:
    Develop a custom wage type, drawing on the configuration of the standard wage type M520. Ensure this wage type is tailored to meet specific organizational needs.

  3. Configure Wage Type Accumulation:
    Set the accumulation for the custom wage type to only include Wage Type /110. Ensure it does not accumulate to Wage Types /101 (Total Income) or /103 (Tax Base).

  4. Maintain Fixed Medical Insurance:
    Use the newly created custom wage type to maintain the fixed medical insurance amount (RMB 21-22), ensuring compliance with the updated policy.
