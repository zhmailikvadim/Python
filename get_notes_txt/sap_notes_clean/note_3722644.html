Symptom

An error occurs when attempting to send an EPS to HMRC via PB2A Manager:

"The entry in [TAXMONTH] of MM is only valid if the submission is made between 6 MM and 19 MM inclusive of the [RELATEDTAXYEAR]."

The EPS submission is rejected at the HMRC Gateway with "Business error".

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP ERP Core Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central integration to SAP Business
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll

Cause

The EPS was submitted after HMRC’s deadline of the 19th of the following tax month. HMRC validates the submission period based on the reported tax month and rejects EPS files submitted late. 

For example a file for tax month 02 (6th May to 5th June) would only be accepted at the HMRC gateway until 19th June, any attempt to submit for tax month 02 after this will result in the file being rejected. This validation applies to all files submitted for tax month 01 – 11 in a tax year. The only EPS file that can be submitted later is for tax month 12 for the current tax year and up to 6 previous tax years to amend the total values.

Additional guidance is available on HMRC's website:

Running payroll: Reporting to HMRC: EPS - GOV.UK

Resolution

Submit the EPS within HMRC’s required timeframe to avoid rejection and ensure statutory reclaim amounts are accepted. 
 
Additional guidance is available on HMRC’s website:
What happens if you do not report payroll information on time - GOV.UK
