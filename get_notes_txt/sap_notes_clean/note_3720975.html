Symptom

I'm sending a ContractStatusUpdateRequest cXML document through SAP Business Network, but it fails and return the error No relationship with Supplier, even though I was able to send the same document in the past without having a trading relationship with the supplier.

Environment

SAP Business Network

Cause

The Contract Status Update Request (CSUR) has credentials pertaining to an existing vendor record in the buyer account with which the buyer has no trading relationship. 

Resolution

There are two ways to resolve this behavior:

  1. You can remove the vendor record associated with this ContractStatusUpdateRequest from your SAP Business Network buyer account, hence the CSUR won't be validated against the vendor. 
  2. If you want to maintain the vendor record, you can send a Trading Relationship Request (TRR) to the supplier associated with the vendor record to which you are sending the ContractStatusUpdateRequest document, so that when validating the vendor and ANID it will find the matching relationship.

Note: Please be aware that removing vendor records from your buyer account can have cascading effects if you have other documents or configurations linked to this vendor, so your business and account administrator have to review your current scenario to make sure there will be no other impacts resulting from this deletion.
