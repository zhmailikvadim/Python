Symptom

This article describes functionality introduced in the May 2026 SAP Fieldglass release

You may want to create or upload:

  • A Distribution List without suppliers
  • A Job Posting Template that uses a Distribution List without suppliers

However, when you try to submit a Job Posting, SAP Fieldglass displays an error indicating that the Distribution List does not contain any suppliers

Reproducing the Issue

You may see this behavior when:

  • You create a Distribution List without adding any suppliers
  • You upload a Distribution List that does not include suppliers
  • You create or update a Job Posting Template that uses a Distribution List with no suppliers
  • You upload a Job Posting Template that uses a Distribution List with no suppliers
  • You attempt to submit a Job Posting that uses that Distribution List
  • The system displays an error stating that the Distribution List has no suppliers

Cause

  • SAP Fieldglass allows Distribution Lists and Job Posting Templates to be created or uploaded without suppliers so that customers can complete setup and configuration earlier, even before suppliers are fully onboarded
  • However, submitting a Job Posting still requires at least one active supplier in the Distribution List
  • If no supplier is added, SAP Fieldglass prevents the Job Posting from being submitted

Resolution

To submit your Job Posting:

  • Open the Distribution List used by your Job Posting Template
  • Add at least one active supplier to the Distribution List
  • Save your changes
  • Submit the Job Posting again

After a supplier is added, the Job Posting will be submitted successfully
