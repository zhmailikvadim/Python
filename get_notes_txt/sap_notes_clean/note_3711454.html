Symptom

  • Scheduled recurring jobs (weekly, monthly, yearly) for both EC/Non -EC instances , in which the selected timezone is non-UTC, are not executing at their configured schedule times.

For example, jobs scheduled to run at 08:00 (UTC+9, Asia/Tokyo) are instead executing at 23:00 (UTC+9).  23:00 should be the UTC time and not UTC+9.(Aisa/Tokyo timezone)

  • Scheduled recurring jobs (weekly, monthly, yearly) are not executing at their configured schedule when the scheduled time is converted to UTC. 

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors HXM Suite

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in to instance -> Scheduled Job Manager .

  2. Navigate to Monitor Jobs.

  3. Search for the scheduled job

  4. Click on the job to observe that the scheduled time differs from the actual execution (run) time configured at the time of job scheduling.

Cause

The issue is caused by a time zone configuration change affecting job scheduling.

Resolution

 This is a known issue with the Engineering team. A fix is scheduled to be deployed to the Preview environment on February 24 and to the Production environment on February 26, 2026.
