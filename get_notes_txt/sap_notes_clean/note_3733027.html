Symptom

  • In the delta view, duplicate cash flows appear in app Cash Flow Analyzer when it's sorted by Planning level and currency and grouped by Planning level.
  • In the Cash Flow Analyzer application, when user setting is 'converting to account currency', the same cash flow is repeated for each currency and planning level combination.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA 1709
  • SAP S/4HANA 1809
  • SAP S/4HANA 1909
  • SAP S/4HANA 2020
  • SAP S/4HANA 2021
  • SAP S/4HANA 2022
  • SAP S/4HANA 2023

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open app Cash Flow Analyzer
  2. In the app Cash Flow Display, in 'Switch Views', choose 'Delta View'.
  3. Then go to 'Settings'
  4. In the Group tab, it's grouped by 'Planning Level' first and then second is 'Currency'
  5. Duplicate flows appear for each Planning level for all currencies.

Cause

There is a fixed grouping defined using Currency. It is applied even if not selected from the UI. So, Currency should be the first grouping field.

Resolution

There are two suggested solution that could be preferred by customer depending their business requirements

  1. In the app Cash Flow Analyzer -> Settings -> Group: Keep the Currency as first Grouping field and then Planning Level (or any field as per requirement). Then the Planning level will be shown once for each currency.

  2. In the app Cash Flow Analyzer -> Selection parameters: Filter on Currency from the filter bar and then only group with Planning Level
