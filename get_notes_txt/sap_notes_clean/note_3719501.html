Symptom

You declare your transit procedures under NCTS Phase 5 from SAP GTS to the national customs system.
In transit receipt, you enter non-compliant items for message IE044 (compliance check).

This SAP Note corrects the following error in the incompleteness check:

  • You want to report an item as a supplementary item and correctly remove the gross weight. Afterwards, the system issues the error message of the incompleteness check "Item &1: Specify a net weight that is less than the gross weight" (/SAPSLL/CUS_NCTS_EU 641). 

This SAP Note is also relevant if you still use NCTS Phase 5, the German transit procedure under Atlas, or the Swiss transit procedure under Passar.
