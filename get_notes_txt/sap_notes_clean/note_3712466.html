Symptom

Part of the legislation around Statutory Payments (SxP) requires that, under certain circumstances, an employer must continue to pay statutory entitlements even after the employee’s contract of employment has ended. This applies, for example, to Statutory Maternity Pay (SMP), Statutory Adoption Pay (SAP) and Statutory Paternity Pay (SPP).
 
SAP supports paying SxP to leavers, but specific configuration steps described in SAP Note 820506 must be followed to ensure correct processing during leaving periods and to avoid errors.

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP ERP Core Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • Employee Central Payroll
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central integration to SAP Business
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll

Resolution

  • Follow the configuration and processing steps outlined in SAP Note 820506 – HRGB: GBSxP: Payments to leavers, which provides the full procedure for enabling SxP payments to continue after an employee has left. The note details the required adjustments to schema processing, PCR logic, absence delimitation, OxP scheme overrides, and payroll control record settings needed to ensure correct SxP handling for leavers.
 
  • SAP standard processing does not support Lump Sum SMP payments. For further background on Lump Sums in the GBSXP solution, refer to SAP Note 820506.
