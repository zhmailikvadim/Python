Symptom

Report MB5T displays a double quantity after a goods issue posting.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create an STO for quantity 10 PC (for example).
  2. Post a Goods Issue against the Outbound Delivery for quantity 10 PC.
  3. Check report MB5T - transit stock quantity is displayed as 20 PC instead of 10 PC.

Cause

The hotfix will be delivered with HFC08 (on 20.04.26).

Resolution

Refer to the app Overdue Materials Stock In Transit which displays the transit stock quantity correctly.

Keywords

EKET, MB5T, - transit stock, doble, incorrect, wrong, Overdue Materials Stock In Transit
