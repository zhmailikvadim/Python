Symptom

You have a Business Completed Billable Maintenance Order and a connected Service Order with status Business Completed or Archived. You try to reverse the Business Completion on the Billable Maintenance Order. The system prevents the reversal but the error message says "Message not found (in main memory)" (BL209).

Other Terms

Plant Maintenance, Billable Maintenance Order, BAPI, Manage Maintenance Orders, Cancel Business Completion

Reason and Prerequisites

Issue is caused by a program error

Solution

Implement the attached correction or the corresponding support package.
