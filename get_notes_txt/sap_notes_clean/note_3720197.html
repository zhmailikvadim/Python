Symptom

During the submission of HMRC RTI reports such as FPS, EPS, FPSA, or EYU, the status may remain as “Waiting: Poll again”.

Environment

  • SAP ERP Core Component
  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP ERPSAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central integration to SAP Business
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll

Cause

This status appears after the system attempts to retrieve a response from the Government Gateway three times without success. The lack of response is typically caused by the Gateway being overloaded or unable to respond within the expected timeframe. After the three automated polling attempts fail, the system transitions the record to manual polling and displays the status “Waiting: Poll again.”

Resolution

Once the record shows “Waiting: Poll again,” manual polling can be performed.

Steps:

  1. Select the submission record with the “Waiting: Poll again” status.
  2. Highlight the record.
  3. Select Execute again to trigger a manual polling attempt.
  4. The system will retry retrieving the response from HMRC.
  5. The status will update once a response is received.

Recommendation

Manual polling is more likely to succeed during quieter periods, such as after 6pm, early morning hours, or other times when Government Gateway traffic is typically lower.
