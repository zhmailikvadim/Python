Symptom

• When attempting to simulate posting in the Post General Journal Entries app, only simulation in the entry view is available.  
• The simulation is limited to the ENTRY VIEW level.  
• The option to "Simulate in General Ledger" is not supported.  

Environment

• S/4 HANA On Premise  
• S/4 HANA Private Cloud  
• Transaction Apps  

Reproducing the Issue

1. Open the App F0718.  
2. Click on the simulate option.  
3. Observe that the general ledger simulation cannot be displayed.  

Cause

• The Fiori app Post General Journal Entries does not support 'Simulate in General Ledger' in its current design.  
• This feature is missing and would require a complete redesign of the posting apps for implementation.  

Resolution

 Use the WebGUI version of the FB01 transaction as a workaround for this scenario.
