Symptom

The Service Levels assignment block is available in the following master data

  • Service contracts
  • Service product items and products
  • Business partners (sold-to party)
  • Service organizational units

Any other requirement (e.g. SLA from vendor or based in any other object) can be achieved using the BADI CRM_SLADET_BADI to determine the SLA profile and BADI CRM_SRV_SLA_CALCTIME in case you need any special calculation.

and can be used in SLA determination to assign the correct service profile and response profile to service request transactions such as service requests. However, it's difficult to find the places where the SLA can be updated.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA

Resolution

  • Service Contract

FIORI App "Manage contracts", Details of service item

  • Service Product

 

    • FIORI App "Display /Change Material".  Material type "SERV" has active the option

    • Transaction MM01/MM02/MM03 "Sales: General/Plant Data"

  • Sold to Party

FIORI App "Account" contain the assignment block "Service Level" where you can update the SLA data.

  • Organizational data

App "Search Organization", Response Profile and Service Profile are attributes in the scenario "Service"
