Symptom

Employer costs may be calculated, even though these are not permitted.

Other Terms

PFAGK

Reason and Prerequisites

An entitlement to cost reimbursement of the garnishment creditor to the third-party debtor is not mandatory. Likewise, the third-party debtor cannot claim an entitlement to cost reimbursement from the borrower.

Boewer No. 319:
"The German Federal Labor Court (BAG) has denied an obligation of the garnishment creditor to reimburse the costs of the third-party borrower for the explanation in lack of a legal basis.
Likewise, the third-party borrower has no entitlement to cost reimbursement toward the borrower."

Solution

The solution is delivered in an HR Support Package. For an advance solution, implement the attached correction instructions.

The symptom is corrected as of the in-period as of which the new subapplication PFAD "Deactivate employer costs" is valid.
The subapplication is delivered with the valid-from date 1/1/2017.
You can set the subapplication valid on an earlier date. However, do not set the subapplication valid retroactively because this may result in repayment differences in the principle of origin.

the technical name specified in the SAP Note under "Support Packages".
