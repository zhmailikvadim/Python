Symptom

The response times for the display of a customs warehouse stock list are insufficient if the reference that caused the customs warehouse receipt posting has been archived in the meantime. This applies in particular to the stock list for displaying customs warehouse receipts and customs warehouse issues.
The symptom occurs because the read access to the archive takes place with selection criteria that are not contained in the archive infostructure of the archiving object CUSWLD.

Reason and Prerequisites
