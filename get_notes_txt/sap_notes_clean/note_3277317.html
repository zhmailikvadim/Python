Symptom

  • JAWS ("Job Access with Speech") screen reader assistive tool is not announcing filter titles of search results from BI Launchpad and titles are not accessible
    Titles : Fields , Location , Refresh Time and Type

Other Terms

JAWS,508,title,accessible,search,filter,results,home,BILP

Reason and Prerequisites

Environment:

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform 4.3
  • 508 Accessibility

Steps To Reproduce:

  1. With JAWS ("Job Access with Speech") configured, test BI Launchpad for 508 compliance.
  2. Logon to BI Launchpad
  3. Click on search , enter a string and hit enter
  4. A page loads with all search results
  5. Notice filter tiles are not accessible by JAWS and JAWS don’t announce it

Solution

This issue is fixed in the patches listed in the "Support Packages & Patches" section below.

The "Support Packages & Patches" section will be populated with the relevant patch levels once they are released.

For Business Intelligence Platform maintenance schedule and strategy see the Knowledge Base Article 2144559 in References section.
