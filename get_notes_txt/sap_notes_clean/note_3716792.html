Symptom

How to rename the  organization name shown in the top-right for a child realm

Resolution

Organization name changes for child realm cannot be performed by support, they must be handled by the realm enablement team. Please contact your customer success partner (CSP), who will engage the Realm Enablement team on your behalf.
