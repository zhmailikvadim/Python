Symptom

You use a BAPI of the function group ACC4, for example, via SAP Ariba. You want to post a cash discount invoice in the net procedure. Although this is updated successfully, it incorrectly does not contain a reduction amount, which may lead to errors during clearing, for example.

Other Terms

ACC4, BAPI_ACC_INVOICE_RECEIPT_CHECK, BAPI_ACC_INVOICE_RECEIPT_POST, KZBTR, WSKTO, SKFBT

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the attached corrections or import the relevant support package.
