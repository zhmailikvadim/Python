Symptom

Changes applied to the Supplier Business Network account (supplier data, contact details, and sub-users) are not synchronized to the Supplier account in SAP Ariba SLP and SAP Ariba Sourcing.

Reproducing the Issue

In the Supplier Business Network account:

  • Create a new sub-user, entering all relevant data and details.
  • Save/create the new sub-user account.

In the Buyer’s SLP realm:

  1. Access SAP Ariba SLP and check the supplier’s users/contacts.
  2. Observe that the newly created supplier sub-user is not available in SLP (synchronization issue).

Cause

    • Multiple Ariba systems and cloud platform components are involved, with system jobs and alerts mediating data flow in a complex integration.
    • The supplier-side change may require validation or approval before synchronization.

    Resolution

    • Refresh the browser cache to ensure you are viewing the latest data
      • see KBA KB0759298 - How to clear Cache and Cookies of Browser
    • Synchronization delay is expected in this integration:
      • Typical delay: up to 30 minutes for changes in the Supplier Business Network to appear in SLP/Sourcing.
      • Maximum expected delay: up to 24 hours, depending on system load, jobs, and alerts across SAP Ariba systems and the cloud platform.
    • For users created via a Sourcing invitation sent by the buyer, the supplier administrator must approve the user before synchronization
      • see KBA 3689455 - How to approve users in SAP Business Network?.
