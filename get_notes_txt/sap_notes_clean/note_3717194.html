Symptom

The SOAP iFlow for replicating service orders from SAP S/4HANA to SAP Field Service Management fails with the error message “Could not find property with name: 'to_Qualification'.”

Environment

  • SAP Field Service Management
  • S4 On-Premise via CPI Connector

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a Service Order in S4 System without Qualifications filled
  2. Save the order in S4
  3. Observe the iFlow Replicate Service Order from SAP S4HANA to SAP Field Service Management SOAP
  • Expected: Replicate Service Order from SAP S4HANA to SAP Field Service Management SOAP iFlow is successful & service order is replicated to FSM 
  • Actual: Replicate Service Order from SAP S4HANA to SAP Field Service Management SOAP fails with Error Message:
    CPI: org.apache.olingo.odata2.api.uri.UriNotMatchingException: Could not find property with name: 'to_Qualification' 

Cause

  • Replicate Service Order from SAP S4HANA to SAP Field Service Management SOAP iFlow has "Enable Qualification Requirement" Parameter Enabled
  • This results into error as Service Order sent from S4 does not have Qualifications

Resolution

Disable the Parameter "Enable Qualification Requirement" for Replicate Service Order from SAP S4HANA to SAP Field Service Management SOAP iFlow.

Keywords

Service Order, Qualifications, iFlow, UriNotMatchingException
