Symptom

Transaction ABUML results in depreciation undergoing two write-off entries.

Environment

  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open transaction ABUML.
  2. Attempt to transfer 1/3 of fixed asset
  3. Fill in both the value and percentage fields.
  4. Observe that the depreciation undergoes two write-off entries:
  • The first entry is correctly calculated at 1/3 of the asset value.
  • The second entry is calculated with a higher value, causing discrepancy.

Cause

Resolution

1. The following workaround to transfer exactly 1/3 of asset:

  1. Option 1: Set the percentage to 33.3333% and leave the "Amount" field blank.
  2. Option 2: Set the transfer amount to 1/3 of the APC and leave the "Percentage" field blank.

2. Avoid filling in both the value and percentage fields simultaneously during the transaction.
