Symptom

A Supplier is enabled for both ERSAllowed ( Evaluated Receipt Settlement) Yes and also Goods Receipt- Based Invoicing preferred True, is this supported in the system?

Environment

  • Product: SAP Ariba Buying and Invoicing
  • ERS Invoices
  • Good Receipt-Based Invoice.

Resolution

Goods Receipt-Based Invoicing feature does not support ERS Invoices. A Supplier can be configured for both, however, during Requisition creation ERS Allowed has to be set to No, for Goods Receipt-Based Invoicing feature to work smoothly.
