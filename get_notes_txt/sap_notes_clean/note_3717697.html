Symptom

When the filter bar field "Customer Reference" is used to lookup Sales Documents, Items, and Schedule Lines, the results in the Schedule Lines tab are not accurate. The results are restricted to matches on the Customer Reference value at the header level of the document and do not consider the item level customer reference values. This can lead to missing or unexpected schedule lines being returned in the tab. This problem also occurs in the Fiori App "Mass Change of Sales Orders without Charge"

Other Terms

F5211, F5336

Reason and Prerequisites

A program error requires correction.

Solution

Implement the Support Package for your release as specified by this SAP Note.  In urgent cases you may choose to implement the attached Correction Instructions.
