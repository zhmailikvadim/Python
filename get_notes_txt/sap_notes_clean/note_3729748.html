Symptom

When navigate to the 'Cost' of PCC in the app "Maintain Product Cost Collectors(KKF6N)", it is noticed Target Costs column shows zero. 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

Target costs of event‑based order cost details are not available in app "Maintain Product Cost Collectors(KKF6N)". 

Resolution

Navigate to the app "Product Cost Collector Details - Event-Based(F6965)" to check the target costs. 

Keywords

PCC, repetitive manufacturing, product cost collector, order details, event-based, kkf6n, zero target costs, Maintain Product Cost Collectors, Product Cost Collector Details - Event-Based, F6965
