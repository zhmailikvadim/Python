Symptom

As described in the Regulatory Change Manager, under Base remuneration for Social Insurance withholding - RG 38/2026, this SAP Note provides you with the relevant system changes to address General Resolution 38/2026 published by the Argentine government's social insurance agency (Administración Nacional de La Seguridad Social).

Effective March 1, 2026, the base remuneration maximum and minimum limits for social insurance withholding are set as follows:

  • Minimum Limit for Welfare (Obra Social): 248,962.98  ARS 
  • Minimum Limit for SIPA, ART, and INSSJyP: 124,481.49 ARS 
  • Maximum Limit for SIPA, Welfare (Obra Social), ART, and INSSJyP: 4,045,590.45  ARS 

For more information, check the official announcement issued in the Boletín Oficial de la República Argentina.
