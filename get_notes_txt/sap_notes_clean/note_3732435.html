Symptom

  • Users need to transport settings for:
  • Define Queries for Liquidity Item Derivation.
  • Assign Queries to Query Sequences.
  • Liquidity Calculation: G/L Classification.
  • Define Default Bank Accounts for One Exposure.
  • No error messages are provided.

Environment

Product: SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

Cause

The requested apps are not relevant for transport request.

Resolution

The settings can be maintained directly in the Productive tenant.

refer to: SAP Help Portal – Cash Management (public cloud) guidance
