Symptom

Even though changing of the first budget billing due date to the invoice due date is activated in Customizing, the system assigns a separate due date to the budget billing amount.

Other Terms

ABRABS

Reason and Prerequisites

There is a program error in SAP Note 2042291 that occurs if items with the amount '0' exist for the first due date and the budget billing plan is created in invoicing.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the relevant Support Package.
