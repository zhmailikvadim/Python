Symptom

You use SAP Travel Management for S/4 HANA Fiori application "My Travel and Expenses for Business Traveler" or "Travel and Expenses for Travel Assistant".

An ABAP dump with exception LCX_ABAP_BEHV_DETVAL_ERROR occurs after changing the cost assignment and selecting an internal order.

Other Terms

F6190, F0584B, Cost Assignments, LCX_ABAP_BEHV_DETVAL_ERROR, RAISE_SHORTDUMP

Reason and Prerequisites

program error

Solution

Import the correction instructions or apply the corresponding support package
