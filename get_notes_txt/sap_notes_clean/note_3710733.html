Symptom

 You use SAP ACM to issue the PPA samples/DiMus and want the system to display the corresponding section name when you select the LANR.

Other Terms

 PPA forms, digital samples, specialty group coding

Reason and Prerequisites

 Functionality enhancement

Solution

If you select a physician to whom more than one valid LANR is assigned, the system also displays the corresponding specialty group name next to the LANR in the selection dialog. The section name is derived from the last two digits of the LANR.

The change affects the following document categories in their current version:

  • N3KVMST01 - Electr. Work Incapacity Certificate (eAU)
  • N3KVMST02 - Order Hospital Treatment
  • N3KVMST04 - Order for Patient Transport
  • N3KVMST06 - Referral certificate
  • N3KVMST10 - Referral certificate for laboratory examinations
  • N3KVMST12 - Prescription for Home Health Care
  • N3KVUNIRP - Universal Prescription
  • N3KVMST13 - Drug Prescription
  • N3KVMST19 - Emergency/Substitution Certificate
  • N3KVMST21 - Medical certificate for the payment of sick pay for illness of a child
  • N3KVPTV11 - Individual patient information for outpatient psychotherapeutic consultation
  • N3KVMST62A - Survey of ventilator weaning or Decanuction Spot (62A)
  • N3KVMST62B - Order for External Intensive Care (62B)
  • N3KVMST62C - Treatment Plan - AKI (62C)

The system displays the selection dialog incl. Section names at the following points:

  • When creating PPA samples/DiMus
  • After selecting another physician in the relevant PPA samples/DiMus
  • In the eAU monitor (when the issuing or responsible physician is initially opened or changed)
  • In the signature overview (when the issuing or responsible physician is initially opened or changed)

The function is available with the specified Support Packages.
