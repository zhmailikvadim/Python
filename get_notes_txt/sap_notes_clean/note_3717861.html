Solution

What’s New for SAP GRC Risk Management for SAP HANA (Support Pack - 00)

    • Fiori Launchpad: Unified access to all the GRC applications
    • New Fiori Applications:
      • Asset Category
      • Manage Assets
      • Impact-Forecast-Based Risk Analysis
    • Enhanced Fiori Applications (new features and sections)
      • Manage Risks
      • Manage Vendor Risks
      • Manage Risk Validations
      • Manage Activity Validations
      • Risk Manager Dashboard
    • New Risk Analysis Type: New Analysis method to get the best of corporate and operational risks. Assessment of multiple horizons for multiple impacts with different assessment methods.
    • Asset Management: Creating a new asset entity for BCM, Cyber Intelligence, and providing an API integration option. Possibility to define an asset entity and link it with existing Risk Management objects and include it into Business Continuity Management process.
    • Flexible Workflow Management: You can now adapt to business needs with customizable workflows by utilizing the Multi-Stage and Multi-Path configuration framework.
    • Vendor Risk Management: Assessment of the vendor risks using surveys with automated customer-specific valuations.

For more information on SAP GRC Risk Management for SAP HANA, please refer to https://help.sap.com/docs/SAP_GRC_FOR_SAP_HANA
