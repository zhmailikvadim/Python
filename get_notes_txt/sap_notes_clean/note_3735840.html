Symptom

How can the Export options be limited to only a specific type? 

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform 4.3 / 2025
  • Windows / Unix

Resolution

  1. In the CMC / Users and Groups, right-click a group or user and choose Customizations.
  2. In the "List of Features available for customization", expand the "Export to" section.
  3. Uncheck the file types that are not desired.
  4. Click "Save and Close" to accept the changes.

Keywords
