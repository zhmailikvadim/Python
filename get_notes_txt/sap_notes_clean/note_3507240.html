Symptom

You're updating Business Partner Address using MDG change request, after the change request activation, you realize that the address detail is updated in ADRC properly. However there is no update to the Supplier/Customer address in table LFA1/KNA1.

Environment

  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 1511
  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 1610
  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 1709
  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 1809
  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 1909
  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 2020

Cause

After applying note 3056266, the synchronization address to Supplier/Customer level does not happen anymore.

Resolution

Please apply note "3073896 - CVI_VAL: Address not synchronized or error CVI_API 003 "Bank Key is a required entry field" even though it is filled during creation of new BPs in customer/supplier role" to fix the issue.

Keywords

Address Synchronization, LFA1, KNA1, ADRC
