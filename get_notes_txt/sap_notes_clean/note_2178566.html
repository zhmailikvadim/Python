Symptom

Comment report calls wrong service for most Recent and Full histroy;

 The Most Recent Comments returns the historical comments;

Full history of Comments returns only the most recent comments.

Other Terms

Comment, Audit

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

For SAP Business Planning and Consolidation 10.1, version for SAP Netweaver, upgrade to (Component CPMBPC 810) support package 08 and upgrade BW740(Component SAP_BW) to support package 13
