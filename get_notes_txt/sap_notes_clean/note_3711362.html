Reason and Prerequisites

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified support package.

  • The payroll account indicates whether an employee is covered by the rules for the active pension. Special indicators are used for employees if the active pension is used for tax class 6 or the recognition of the active pension is specified manually using the infotype Fiscal Data D (0012). 
  • In the "Tax-Exempt Payments" section, you specify the amount of the tax exemption due to the active pension for regular and non-recurring payments.
