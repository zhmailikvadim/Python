Symptom

Fiori My Home page is not working on mobile devices.

Environment

  • Product: SAP S/4HANA 2025 FPS00
  • SAP Fiori Launchpad ABAP Services

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the SAP Fiori Launchpad from a mobile browser. (Not applicable to Mobile Client)
  2. Observe that the page does not display correctly and the Launchpad becomes unresponsive.

Cause

  • Mobile functionality is affected on the S/4HANA 2025 FPS00 initial release level.

Resolution

  1. Update SAPUI5 to the latest available.
  2. After applying the update, restart the Fiori Launchpad session and test the "My Home" page on a mobile device.
  3. Confirm that the page renders correctly and that the Launchpad no longer freezes.
