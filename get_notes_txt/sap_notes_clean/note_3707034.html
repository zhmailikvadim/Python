Symptom

How can I unsubscribe from Joule if I no longer need it?

Environment

SAP Ariba Procurement

SAP Ariba Sourcing

Resolution

If you no longer need the Joule subscription or its service instance, you can delete them.

When you unsubscribe from Joule, all new configurations will be removed. All services associated with the Joule setup will be unsubscribed, and their stored data will be deleted. As a result, you will lose access to your assistant and chat data.

Deleting the Joule Subscription

  1. Go to the cockpit of your subaccount.

  2. Select your Subaccount tile and click on Instances and Subscriptions in the left navigation pane.

  3. Locate the Joule application under Subscriptions.

  4. Click Delete in the actions menu on the right.

    The subscription will be deleted.

Once you have deleted the Joule subscription, you may not be able to resubscribe in the same subaccount. To resubscribe, create a subscription in a different subaccount where Joule has not been subscribed, or raise a ticket to CA-DAS-TO.
