Symptom

  • Attachments made to MICs in the Quality Inspection Results plugin are not sent to S4 or displayed in any reports in DM.
  • After completing the SFC, the attachments are not visible in the SFC report or MDO.
  • No Manufacturing Data Objects (MDOs) are available for MICs.

Environment

SAP Digital Manufacturing

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Use the Quality Inspection Results plugin to attach files to MICs.
  2. Complete the SFC process.
  3. Check the SFC report and MDO for the attached files.

Cause

The attachment functionality in the Quality Inspection plugins is not integrated with the ERP system; attachments are only relevant within the SAP Digital Manufacturing system.

Resolution

  1. Acknowledge the current design: attachments added to MICs via the Quality Inspection Results plugin are stored within SAP Digital Manufacturing and are not integrated with ERP/S4 or surfaced in SFC reports/MDO.
  2. Manage attachments within SAP Digital Manufacturing using the plugin’s upload/download capabilities (local system to remote file system and back).
