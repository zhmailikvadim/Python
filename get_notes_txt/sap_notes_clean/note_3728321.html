Symptom

After sending the award details from Ariba to S4, the following system error occurs in S4HANA SRT_MONI: An error occurred when deserializing in the simple transformation program.

Why is this error occurring?

Cause

The supplier is not selected for the service line when awarding a copy of the best bid scenario, leaving the field empty and causing the error.

Resolution

Ensure that the supplier is selected for the service line, as the system does not automatically trigger supplier selection upon creating a copy. You need to select the scenario and edit it to complete this step.
