Symptom

Operators are not working when they are used without a value. If a privilege is assigned to a role, and you try to remove the privilege by using "To Identity Store" pass and operator {E} ({R} or {D}) without values, the privilege is still assigned to the role.

Other Terms

IDM, Identity Management, Operators, {D}, {E}, {R}, Assigned Privilege

Reason and Prerequisites

You are using:

  • SAP Identity Management 7.2 SP10.
  • SAP Identity Management 8.0 SP04.

Solution

Apply the attached patch provided in the Support Packages & Patches section of this SAP Note.

Make sure all components are updated to the corresponding SP level before applying the patch.
