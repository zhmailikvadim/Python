Symptom

During inbound processing of a QM-relevant material, you configure direct putaway so the handling unit (HU) is moved straight to the final bin. After posting a partial usage decision for part of the quantity, you observe a negative quantity in the final bin.

Example: An HU containing 10 pcs is in final bin BIN1. You post a usage decision (UD) as follows: 3 pcs are to be scrapped and repacked into a new HU; 7 pcs are accepted and remain in BIN1. After the UD is posted, BIN1 shows −3 pcs.

Other Terms

/SCWM/CHECK_QUANTITY, /SCWM/HU_GT_FILL_FROM_STOCK QA32 QA11 negat

INCON_STOCK_AQUA #WOP_INCON_STOCK_AQUA INCON_STOCK_QUAN #WOP_INCON_STOCK_QUAN; /SCWM/L3694; Error while importing HU stocks for HU &1

Reason and Prerequisites

BIN1 contains more than 100 handling units (HUs)

Solution

Implement the correction
