Symptom

When navigating from the serial number history in transaction IQ03 to display the material document in MIGO, the runtime error RAISE_EXCEPTION with exception condition "NOT_FOUND" is triggered.

Other Terms

MAA, MIGO_DIALOG, ZEILE, CURRENT_LINE, GOITEM, GODETAIL, GODYNPRO, TABIX, LINE, IE03, SAPLMIGO, LMIGOKL1, LINE_MAPPING, MB_CALL_MIGO_DIALOG

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason is a program error.

Prerequisites:

  • The business function LOG_MM_MAA_1 is active and the purchase order uses multiple account assignment (MAA).
  • The material is subject to serial number management.

Solution

Implement the attached corrections or import the relevant Support Package.
