Symptom

The AI toolkit uses a Outbound Sync Job to sync the data from commerce to AI toolkit, and some situations require a sync progress check.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

  • SAP Commerce Cloud
  • AI Toolkit

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Integrate with AI toolkit: Integrating SAP Commerce Cloud
  2. In Commerce Backoffice:
    1. Go to integration UI tool
    2. Under OutboundSync > Schedule > Cronjobs
      • Check if the AI toolkit jobs are created and running:
        • outboundDigitalAssistantClassAttributeSyncJob
        • outboundDigitalAssistantClassSyncJob
        • outboundDigitalAssistantProductReviewsSyncJob
        • outboundDigitalAssistantProductSyncJob 

Cause

There are a few reasons for monitoring the Sync, the most common is slowness in the Sync.

Resolution

  1. On Dynatrace go to services
  2. Check the "requests to public networks"
  3. Click on "View requests"
  4. AI toolkit requests will be on domain "cloud.sap"
  5. Each AI Toolkit Sync job will have their own endpoint, In the bellow example just Products Sync is running:
  6. The throughput should be around 500 requests per minute, if it is less, it could mean performance issues on commerce.
  7. In such cases check the distributed traces to identify the reason.
