Symptom

The value description of 'Payment Terms' field does not show during contract create in Maintain ACM Trading Contract Fiori Application.

Following are the replication steps:
1. Launch the Maintain ACM Trading Contract Fiori app.
2. Click the Create button.
3. Navigate to the Organization section via the top-right button.
4. Fill in the required organization data and click Add button.
5. Select the Side, Contract Category from the drop down and fill Counterparty.
6. Observe that once the Counterparty is entered, the Payment Terms field auto-populates the value, but the corresponding description is missing.

Other Terms

Agricultural Contract Management; ACM; Maintain ACM Trading Contracts Application; Payment Terms

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
