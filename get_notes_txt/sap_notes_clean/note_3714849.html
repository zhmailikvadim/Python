Symptom

Suppliers were unfollowed from the Supplier Risk Summary without any user manual action.

Cause

User was inactivated on the system

Resolution

When a user is disabled, all followed suppliers are removed.

If you decide to reactivate the user, you need to follow all the suppliers again to see them on the Supplier Risk dashboard.
