Symptom

This migration is required for partners who are currently subscribed to the premium-test service plan of "Cloud Test, Demo, and Development" for SAP Logistics Management (SKU 8020372).

Other Terms

Migration from 'premium-test' to 'premium-partner'

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason:

SAP Logistics Management has introduced a dedicated service plan called "premium-partner" specifically for the partner SKU 8020372. This update provides partners with a dedicated type of tenant. Functionally, it is identical to the existing offering, with the only difference being the plan name, which allows SAP to distinguish the two use cases internally.

Prerequisites:

You must have administrator access to the Global Account to which SAP Logistics Management is entitled.

Solution

Partners, who setup new tenants should use the service plan called "premium-partner" from the beginning.

Partners who have already subscribed to the service are requested to switch their current service plan to "premium-partner" to fully benefit from the latest entitlements and features. There is no downtime and data loss during migration.

1. Check entitlements

Your SAP Logistics Management tenant should have a new service plan called "premium-partner" that has been automatically assigned to your list of entitlements.

Access the Global Account from which you subscribed to SAP Logistics Management as a partner.:

  • Navigate to Entitlements → Service Assignments
  • Filter by 'SAP Logistics Management' in Entitlement Source.
  • Confirm that "premium-partner" is already assigned to the account.
  • If the plan is not available, open a support ticket using component 'SCM-LGM-OPS' and temporarily pause the migration.

Reference: check-entitlements.png

2. Assign premium-partner to subaccount

Access the subaccount from which you’ve subscribed to SAP Logistics Management as a partner:

  • Navigate to Entitlements -> Edit -> Add Service Plans
  • Search for 'SAP Logistics Management' 
  • Select 'premium-partner (Application)'
  • Add the Service Plan

Reference: assign-entitlement.png

3. Update service plan

Access the subaccount from which you’ve subscribed to SAP Logistics Management as a partner:

  • In the same Subaccount, go to Services → Instances and Subscriptions -> for the application "SAP Logistics Management”. 
  • Change the plan to "premium-partner". Reference: update-service-plan-1.png
  • Click "Update Subscription". Reference: update-service-plan-2.png
  • You will be redirected to check the details. Reference: migration-status-check.png

Expected Outcome: The subscription now displays the service plan "premium-partner".

4. Support

If experiencing technical issues during the migration, create a support ticket under component 'SCM-LGM-OPS'.
