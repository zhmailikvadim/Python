Symptom

A partner wants a demo system under TDD license and has doubts about this topic. 

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll
  • S/4HANA technology stack

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Attempt to procure a new Employee Central Payroll instance under Partner Edge licensing.
  2. Verify the stack availability for the newly provisioned instance.
  3. Observe that documentation does not provide clarity on a topic that partner needs.

Cause

The inquiry pertains to licensing and provisioning details, which are handled by the TDD team and not SAP Support.

Resolution

  1. Contact the TDD team for licensing, entitlement, and provisioning inquiries by email at tdd@sap.com.
  2. Ensure that the request includes existing procurement details for accurate processing.

See Also

  • Refer to SAP Note ##3597596: TDD-related licensing/entitlement/provisioning queries, issues, and requests for SAP BTP.
  • Refer to SAP Note ##3428090: Get assistance for SAP TDD subscription or usage-related topics - SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition.
