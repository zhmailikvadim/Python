Symptom

The customer desires to use Joule under the BTP Global Account for performing administrative tasks; however, he is not able to find the Joule icon (diamond). 

Environment

BTP Cockpit

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the BTP Cockpit.
  2. Look at the upper right section of the screen and try to find the Joule icon next to the User Menu.

Cause

Joule should be enabled manually under the Global Account. It is not enabled by default.

Resolution

  1. Click on Settings button;

  3. Joule is ready to use (it can be disabled at any time)
