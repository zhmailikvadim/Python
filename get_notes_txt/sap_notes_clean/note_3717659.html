Symptom

When I click a link in an SAP Ariba email notification, I am redirected to my company’s Single Sign-On (SSO) login page instead of the content in the link. As a third-party user, this prevents me from accessing the information.

Cause

This is expected behavior when SSO is enabled for the realm. The system is designed to route all authentication attempts, including those from email links, through the designated SSO provider for security.

Resolution

To access content from email links, you must be pre-authenticated in the system.

  1. Before clicking any email links, you must first sign in to your SAP Ariba account. For instructions on how to construct your specific third-party access URL, see How do third-party users access my company’s site once SSO is enabled?
  2. Once you are successfully signed in, open the email and click the link. You will now be directed to the correct page without being routed to the SSO login screen.
