Symptom

You notice that the Approver party is not automatically determined as a party for your cases, even though in the approval process flow, the Approver is determined, it is still not added as an Involved Party to the case.

Environment

SAP Sales and Service Cloud Version 2.

Cause

The Case entity currently does not support the automatic determination of the Approver party.

Resolution

For this party to be included to the case, it would be necessary to manually add the Approver to the case. This would ensure that the party gets determined, and other processes e.g. any template using for example the placeholder {{approver.name}} would be replaced with the actual approver party associated with the case.
