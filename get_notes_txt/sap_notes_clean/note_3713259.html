Symptom

This SAP Note delivers the below according to Singapore’s Infocomm Media Development Authority (IMDA) requirements:

  • Support individual Peppol Endpoint IDs (UEN) for B2G invoicing; use UEN from Tax Category "SG3" (Tax Number 3) as Endpoint ID in addition to AGD.
  • Enforce 5‑character Sub‑BU in buyer reference for government e‑Invoices during transactions. 
  • Apply mandatory AGD validation checks and remove the GST number validation, enabling support for Singapore Government agencies with UEN-only registration.

For the required implementation activities, see the following What's New documents on SAP Help Portal:

  • SAP S/4HANA and SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition: B2G Invoicing: Meet the New Requirement for Peppol Participant IDs
  • SAP ERP: B2G Invoicing: Meet the New Requirement for Peppol Participant IDs

Other Terms

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisite:

  • 3708482 - Electronic Invoice for Peppol Countries/Regions - Corrections
