Symptom

  • Custom reports are not being populated by the Update Analytics process.
  • The Update Analytics custom procedures table are empty.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
  • Incentive Management on Hyperscalers
  • Embedded Analytics

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Run the Update Analytics process for a determined period.
  2. Check the reports for the period.
  3. Observe that the reports are not refreshed and do not display data.

Cause

The Update Analytics custom procedures table was empty, which prevented the execution of the custom procedures required to populate the custom tables.

Resolution

  1. Verify the custom procedures and associated views in the configuration file provided.
  2. Follow the process outlined in KBA 3581877 to insert the required custom procedures into the CSA_CUSTOMPROCEDURES table.
  3. Once the query is executed, trigger the Update Analytics process again.
  4. Validate if the information is being populated as expected in the reports.
