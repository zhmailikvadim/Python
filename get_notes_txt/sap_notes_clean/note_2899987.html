Symptom

The language of a company will be changed in MDG-F application.

The assigned CompanyReplicationBulkRequest_In message will fail with an 'Application Error' containing message 'fbs_se 202' of type Warning.

Therefore the change will not be update in the linked systems.

Other Terms

CompanyReplicationBulkRequest; CL_FBS_CMP_RPLCTN_API; CHECK_UPDATE_ALLOWED_FIGL

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error implemented with note 2028650.

 A warning in the replication process of companies should not lead to an Application Error and failed SOA message.

Solution

Correction Instruction.
