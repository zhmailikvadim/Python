Symptom

The universal allocation posts its results with dedicated business transaction types. These business transaction types are different from those of previous allocations, like the cost center allocations (transaction code KSU5, for example). You want to make the resulting accounting document lines to be considered also in other applications, for example, in special ledgers.

Note that the resulting accounting document lines are in general automatically considered in G/L, FI, and CO, without any further measures.

Other Terms

BTTYPE ACTIVITY

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisite: You are using the universal allocation and want the resulting posting lines to be considered in other applications.

Solution

Include the business transaction types of the universal allocation in the configuration of the respective application:

ACAA - Univ. Allocation CC Act. Assm.
ACAD - Univ. Allocation CC Act. Dist.
ACPA - Univ. Allocation CC Plan Assm.
ACPD - Univ. Allocation CC Plan Dist.
APAA - Univ. Allocation PC Act. Assm.
APAD - Univ. Allocation PC Act. Dist.
APPA - Univ. Allocation PC Plan Assm.
APPD - Univ. Allocation PC Plan Dist.

As of OP1909, FPS01:
AAAT - Univ. Allocation Top Dw. Dist.

As of OP2020:
AMAA - Univ. Allocation PA Act. Assm.

As of OP2021:
AMAD - Univ. Allocation PA Act. Dist.
AMPA - Univ. Allocation PA Pla. Assm.
AMPD - Univ. Allocation PA Pla. Dist.

ACAC - Univ. Alloc. CC Act. ICO

to be confirmed:
ACPC - Univ. Allocation CC Plan ICO

Note that this list might be appended with additional business transaction types as further contexts are added to the universal allocation.
