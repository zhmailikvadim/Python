Symptom

You declare your transit declarations from SAP GTS to the German customs server ATLAS 10.1.

As described in ATLAS Info 0940/26, certain documents related to ODS (Ozone Depleting Substances), FGAS (Flourized Greenhouse Gases), and GGED (Common Health Receipt Documents) may no longer be reported at collective shipment or single shipment level, but only at item level.

The incompleteness check and the aggregation of item documents do not yet take these changes into account.
