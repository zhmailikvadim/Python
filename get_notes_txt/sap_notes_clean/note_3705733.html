Symptom

For faster analyzes the SAP Technical Support needs the direct URL of the compliance product structure to the access it directly in the Product Compliance Workbench.

Reproducing the Issue

-

Cause

Help for error analyzes

Resolution

  1. Open the Compliance Product Workbench via NWBC or Fiori tile, like "Search Compliance Information" by searching the product/material
  2. Open the Compliance Product Structure
  3. Navigate and open the tree to the related node
  4. Right click and open 'Technical help'
  5. Go to Section WD Application URL and copy the Start URL of Application, see below example image.
  6. Send the copied URL with the related specification within the case to SAP Technical Support

 Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.
