Symptom

If you are using MTM logistics versioned document and are at a release S4CORE 2025 and higher , and notice that the Timeseries key is not getting correctly populated in CMM_VLOGP_TSER table for a document . This might happen if the Period Determination Calendar ID is not correctly populated and in turn the Timeseries Key is not getting generated correctly for CMM_VLOGP-QUOT_TIMESERIES_KEY .

Other Terms

 CMM_VLOGP_TSER, Calender ID , Period Determination , CMM_VLOGP-QUOT_TIMESERIES_KEY

Reason and Prerequisites

 This is a program error .

Prerequisites:

You are at a release S4CORE 2025 or higher .

Solution

Implement the attached corrections .
