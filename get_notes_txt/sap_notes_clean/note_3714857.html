Symptom

  • Incentives in the forecast calculations window consistently show a value of 0 when conditional incentives are used.
  • The PMR UI displays reward incentives under the incentive field but does not display conditional incentives.

Environment

  • Offer

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the PMR What-if forecast functionality in the system.
  2. Add conditional incentives to an offer.
  3. Observe that the forecast calculations window shows incentive values as 0.
  4. Verify UDF modeling and forecasting to confirm that incentives are calculated correctly.
  5. Check the PMR UI to confirm that conditional incentives are not displayed under the incentive field.

Resolution

The PMR UI is designed to display reward incentives under the incentive field.
Conditional incentives are not displayed in the UI under the incentive field.
