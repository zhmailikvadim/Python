Symptom

The following error occurs during the BW/4 transfer:

RSO_RES_ADSO 183 "Remodeling pending on &1 and must be completed before activation"

Other Terms

aDSO, DataStore object (advanced), BW/4 BW4 transfer

Reason and Prerequisites

SAP Note 2742310 (or the corresponding support package) introduced a persistent lock for the remodeling of aDSOs. This lock is checked for each aDSO activation to prevent the aDSO from being activated again during a remodeling.

Since this persistent lock is also used for the remodeling, the system incorrectly assumes for an aDSO activation during a running transfer that a remodeling is active and therefore terminates the activation with the above error message.
