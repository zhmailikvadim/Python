Symptom

There are Update Activities changed by S4 Communication (SAP_S4_COM) in change log of Central Business Configuration.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

  • SAP Central Business Configuration
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

The Update Activities changed by S4 Communication (SAP_S4_COM) are related to the upgrade/update of the S/4 system. KBA 3097708 - Release upgrade for SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition, 2-System and 3-System Landscape 

You can find the date and time in Deployment History for the Upgrade type deployment.

Resolution

Update Activities changed by S4 Communication (SAP_S4_COM) are only related to the upgrade/update. They will not change your CBC workspace phase or change value in your CBC system.

If you want to check the user's operation history, you can completely ignore them.
