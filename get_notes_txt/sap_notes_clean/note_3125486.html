Symptom

Goods Receipt Fiori Apps (e.g.: the apps like "Post goods receipt for inbound delivery"  and "Post Subsequent Adjustment") don't support A&D subcontracting processes. But these APPs don't block A&D subcontracting processes.

But when you try to post goods receipt for A&D subcontracting by using the Fiori App "Post goods receipt for inbound delivery"  or the Fiori App "Post Subsequent Adjustment", you don't be informed that this use-case (concerning  A&D subcontracting ) is not supported.

Other Terms

Subcontracting, purchase order

Reason and Prerequisites

The problem is due to a program error.

Solution

Import the corresponding support package
