Symptom

  • Unable to schedule a job to expose S/4HANA content to BTP Work Zone properly with program /UI2/CDM3_EXP_SCOPE; manual exposure works normally.
  • No logs are generated when the job is run.

Environment

SAP Fiori Launchpad ABAP Services

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Attempt to schedule the program "/UI2/CDM3_EXP_SCOPE" in the background to expose S/4HANA content to BTP Work Zone.
  2. Verify changes in Work Zone after running the job.
  3. Observe that no content is exposed and no logs are generated.

Cause

By design, Transaction Code "/UI2/CDM3_EXP_SCOPE" does not expose any content.

Resolution

Schedule the job for the program "/UI2/CDM3_EXP_PUBLISH".

This is also explained in the SAP Help Portal page Schedule Content Exposure.

See Also

SAP Help Portal - Schedule Content Exposure
