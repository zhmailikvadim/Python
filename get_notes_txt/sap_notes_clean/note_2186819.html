Symptom

During SAPCL LINKEDIT and BIND, job skeleton variables like "$0" or "$DB2_LOAD_LIB" are incorrectly replaced or not replaced and the job execution failed.

After defining job variables in DBA Cockpit -> Job -> Job Profile, LINKEDIT and BIND fails again, even the variables have been defined and saved in Job Profile.

After switching to a different remote system, LINKEDIT and BIND fails.

Other Terms

DB2, CCMS, DB13, z/OS, OS/390, SAP Collector Settings, Alert Router, SAPCL, Job Profile, BIND, LINK-EDIT

Reason and Prerequisites

Job variables of the DBA Cockpit Job Profile have been accidentally cached in the job execution and therefore changes in Job Profile are not used until DBA Cockpit has been restarted.

Solution

This problem is corrected by the relevant Support Package. For an advance correction, implement the correction instructions.
