Symptom

  • Outstanding quantities are visible in SAP Ariba Planning Collaboration even when delivery dates are in the past.

  • Open quantities do not reduce unless Goods Receipt (GR) is posted.

  • Users expect outstanding quantities to be filtered or calculated based on a date range.

  • Whether Days of Supply (DOS) impacts outstanding quantities.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA / SAP ECC

  • SAP Ariba Planning Collaboration

  • SAP Cloud Integration Gateway (CIG)

Cause

  • Outstanding quantity in SAP is not date-dependent.

  • The system calculates open quantities strictly based on schedule line data and Goods Receipt postings.

  • SAP Ariba does not perform independent calculations; it consumes data from SAP.

  • Days of Supply (DOS) is a separate planning metric and is not linked to outstanding quantity logic.

Resolution

Outstanding quantity is calculated at the purchase order schedule line level as follows:

Outstanding Quantity = Schedule Line Quantity – Goods Receipt (GR) Quantity

  • Each schedule line is evaluated individually

  • Delivery date (past, present, or future) has no impact

  • Quantity remains open until fully received

Days of Supply (DOS) = Available Supply ÷ Average Daily Demand
