Symptom

  • This note contains information about the enhancements to come for the Variable Pay Solution as published in Taxation laws amendment act no 22, 2012 by SARS in South Africa
  • Important notes related to this legal change will be listed in the Related Notes section.
  • This information is based on legislation and regulations which could be subject to change at any time by the respective authorities and are therefore not binding. The note will be updated if necessary.

Other Terms

Variable Pay,  SITE, Tax Continuity

Reason and Prerequisites

  • The following areas have presented ongoing challenges within the payroll and have been reviewed:

    • Variable Pay (Phase 3)
    • Tax Continuity
    • SITE Calculation

Solution

  • The proposed enhancements include:

• Reverse Phase 3 of Variable Pay

  • Tax reporting for variable payments in “When Paid” period
  • Align Tax Reporting (EMP201) and Payments to SARS

• Tax Continuity

  • Reverse “nice to have” changes providing continuity of tax calculations across PAYE reference numbers
  • Reinstate mandatory independent calculation of tax for employees transferring across independent legal entities (Company Codes)
  • Revert to strict legal compliance

• SITE calculation

  • Reinstate WTAX SITE to provide full year recalculation of tax, and tax refund calculations

 

  • The delivery date of this new legal change is planned for CW 7 2015. For more information about the released solution, see the Reference to related Notes section. Note that this section will only be updated after the solution is released.
  • The planned delivery date can only be met if the legislative procedure, including the rules and regulations, is completed or updated in time.
  • To implement this legal change, implement the relevant correction instructions or apply the relevant Support Package when available.
