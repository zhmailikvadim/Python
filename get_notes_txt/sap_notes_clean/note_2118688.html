Symptom

If a group of business partners contains both partners that are liable to church tax and partners that are not required to pay church tax, the system may issue message /IBS/CY_DE 098 ("Church tax for partner &1 cannot be determined") during the tax calculation.

In the case of a prior period adjustment, the church tax determined in the original settlement is not refunded.

Other Terms

CYT, capital yield tax, AGST, flat withholding tax, KiStA, church tax, /IBS/CLCY_EN_DE, GET_TAB_PBA /IBS/CY_DE, 098, /IBS/CY_DE098

Reason and Prerequisites

There is a program error.

Solution

Import the Support Package relevant for your release. Use transaction SNOTE to implement the attached correction instructions in advance.

You do not have to perform any manual tasks if you implement the advance corrections.
