Symptom

You want to clear customer or vendor items using manual clearing transactions, e.g. with FB05. Before you select the open items, you manually enter a G/L item in the first item.
Afterwards, you 'Choose Open Items' and open items are displayed that actually are blocked by a payment proposal (Table REGUS/P).

The problem only occurs when the user enters a G/L item before the open items are selected.

Other Terms

REGUS_PRUEFEN, REGUS, REGUP, HBKID, HKTID, OP Selection

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error. You have implemented SAP Note 2580231.
