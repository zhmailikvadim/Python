Symptom

1. In HNOCRMB0 report, during editing an income form from ALV, the drop down list "Reduction Reason" has no values in webGUI.

Other Terms

HRNO, RMB, HNOCRMB0, reduction reason

Reason and Prerequisites

This SAP Note is relevant only for Norway.
HRSP SAPK-10201INS4HCM, HRSP SAPK-10111INS4HCM, HRSP SAPK-10024INS4HCM, SAPK-608D5INSAPHR, SAPK-604K7INSAPHR (or newer)

Solution

1. The drop down list "Reduction Reason" adjusted to work on webGUI as well.
