Symptom

You use NZDT SP >= 4.

During re-replication, dumps of type DYN_CALL_METH_PARAM_MISSING occurs for program CL_IUUC_S4_SDM_NZDT_GENERIC===CP occur on the receiver system.

Other Terms

downtime minimization tool for SAP S/4HANA, NZDT, S4CORE

Reason and Prerequisites

You use NZDT SP >= 4 and any of the following S4CORE components is installed in the receiver system:

  • S4CORE 108 FPS00 - FPS05
  • S4CORE 109 FPS00 - FPS01

Solution

Implement the automatic correction instructions on your receiver system. If the dumps occurred already, stop the re-replication and restart it after the not has been implemented.
