Symptom

After accessing Company Profile, my company country is not available in the list for Country/Region in Country Configuration tab.

Cause

The country does not appear in the Country Region configuration list because no specific configuration is required. Only countries that require additional or country-specific settings are displayed in Ariba Country Configuration.

Resolution

The company address and tax information can be added directly under Additional Entities tab.
