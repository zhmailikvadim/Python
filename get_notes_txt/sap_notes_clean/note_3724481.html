Symptom

  • There is a requirement to develop/modify form templates with Fragments designed for S/4 Output Management in SFP.
  • In the past, it was not possible to handle form templates with fragments in transaction SFP and it was required to develop/modify the templates in Adobe LiveCycle Designer standalone.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA FOUNDATION
  • SAP ERP
  • ABAP Platform
  • SAP NetWeaver
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP Solution Manager

Cause

In previous ABAP Platform versions, the system did not support to developing/modifying form templates designed for S/4 Output Management in SFP.

Resolution

As of ABAP Platform 2025 SP00, an extension to the SFP transaction which allows to develop/modify form templates with Fragments designed for S/4 Output Management is available. 

When editing a content template in ABAP Platform 2025 SP00 or higher, a pop-up to select a master template for preview will become available. Please note that the master will template in use will NOT be saved along the content template and is only used during the preview.

To be able to use this feature, upgrade to ABAP Platform 2025 SP00 or higher.

If an upgrade cannot be performed, use the workaround from KBA 3359317.
