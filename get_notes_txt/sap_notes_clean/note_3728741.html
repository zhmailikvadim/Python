Symptom

In the process "New Installation for Feed-In (SUP View)" (DE_B_MPES_NIN_SUP), a runtime error occurred in step 0900 during the rejection process. After step 0800 completed, the system didn't execute the next step 8950 as expected.

This issue is relevant for the market role: SUP

Other Terms

MPES, DE_B_MPES_NIN_SUP, New Installation for Feed-In, rejection process, precondition configuration, step 8950, feed-in

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because step 0850 had incorrect precondition configuration, which prevented the system from executing step 8950 after step 0800 in the rejection process for feed-in.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system executes step 8950 successfully after completing step 0800 in the rejection process.
