Symptom

Tring to un-schedule a SMS campaign but was not able to find a button to do so.

Environment

SAP Emarsys Customer Engagement

Cause

Resolution

  • The only way to unschedule an SMS campaign is to delete it. Once deleted, it will not be launched.
  • It is recommended to copy the SMS campaign before deletion to retain a reviewable copy and avoid starting from scratch.
