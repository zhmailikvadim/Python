Symptom

Pricing conditions are not updated during sales order creation, and it is resulting in the below error during Sales Order creation.

  • Error message: 'Pricing error: Mandatory condition PPR0 is missing'.

Environment

SAP S/4Hana Public Cloud

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to application Create Sales Order - VA01
  2. Enter the relevant Order Type - OR, Distribution Channel, Sales Org
  3. Enter the relevant Sold to Party, Sales Area, Distribution Channel, Product
  4. Select the item and navigate to the conditions tab to notice the error

Cause

This is due to the division mismatch at the header and item level during sales order creation.

Resolution

The division at the header level and item level must be consistent for the price list condition to be automatically determined/updated during sales order creation.
