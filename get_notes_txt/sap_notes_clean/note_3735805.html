Symptom

 During the integration of BOM from S/4HANA Public Cloud to SAP Digital Manufacturing, the Replication by Object Selection app is used for BOM replication.The backflush indicator is not being replicated from S/4HANA to SAP Digital Manufacturing during BOM integration.  

Environment

SAP Digital Manufacturing cloud

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Follow the guide Transferring a Master Routing 
  2. Enable backflush in routing operation in S4HC.
  3. In the Master BOM in DMC, the relevant BOM component does not have the backflush flag enabled. 

Cause

This is a current product limitation.

Resolution

The mapping of the backflush indicator from the inbound BOM payload is not supported in this integration scenario; 

Keywords

backflush, replication by object selection, s/4hana public cloud, digital manufacturing
