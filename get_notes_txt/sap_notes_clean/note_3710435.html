Symptom

The aim of this Knowledge Base Article (KBA) is to provide further details about the newly added Destination Fragment Administrator role in the Subaccount Administrator role collection.

Environment

  • SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP)
  • SAP Cloud Foundry (CF)
  • Release Independent

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access SAP Business Technology Platform (SAP BTP. URL: https://cockpit.btp.cloud.sap) Global Account.
  2. Select the Subaccount. 
  3. Navigate to Security -> Role Collections -> Subaccount Administrator and the role Destination Fragment Administrator is added to this role collection.

Resolution

The Destination Fragment Administrator is a specialized tool designed to manage destination fragments, offering administrators greater flexibility and control over technical connection configurations (also known as destinations). Unlike standard destinations, which provide a fixed set of properties, destination fragments introduce a dynamic layer that allows configurations to be adapted without altering the original destination itself. Through these fragments, administrators can enhance an existing destination by adding new properties, modifying values, or even overriding specific settings as needed. This approach not only streamlines configuration management but also enables more granular customization to support complex integration requirements. By leveraging destination fragments, organizations can address a variety of technical scenarios, from adjusting authentication parameters to fine-tuning service endpoints. This ultimately enables more robust, adaptable solutions that meet evolving business needs. 
The Subaccount Administrator is the main administrator role and hence the Destination Fragment Administrator role was added to it.
