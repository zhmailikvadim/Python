Symptom

You experience wrong stock delta and/or stock entries during stock posting in conjunction with quality stock posting.
This might happen especially with quality inspection usage decision or quality stock posting. Causing the unrestricted stock quantity to be posted as 0, instead of the expected quantity.

Other Terms

IBP Add-on, Stock, Zero Stock, Usage Decision, SDI, Smart Data Integration, Stock Delta, Stock_EXT, BTE

Solution

With this note implementation the quantity for unrestricted stock will be not posted as zero in conjunction with quality stock posting during a BTE (business transaction even)
