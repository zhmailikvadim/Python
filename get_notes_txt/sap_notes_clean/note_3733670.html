Symptom

You use transaction MR8M to reverse an MM document and the system issues error message M8 285 "Balance in currency & currency type 10".
The document is a foreign currency document and you use an exchange rate for taxes (T001-TXKRS = '1').

Other Terms

TXKRS, BTE 2051, M8 285, KURS_ERMITTELN, MR8M

Reason and Prerequisites

The exchange rate for the tax was incorrectly redetermined.

Solution

The corrections ensure that BTE 2051 is not called in the case of an MM reversal and, as a result, the exchange rate for the tax is not redetermined.
