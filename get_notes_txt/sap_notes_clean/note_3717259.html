Symptom

  • A column named Health is visible in SAP Fieldglass views related to Statements of Work.
  • Users are unclear about the purpose, calculation logic, and impact of this column on the SoW process or status.

Environment

Statement of Work - Edit

Resolution

  1. Health column represents the document's status concerning deadlines for approvals and submissions, indicating whether any deadlines are breached.
  2. The status Critical, Warning, Good are displayed in the column based on thresholds defined in the PMO Dashboard settings. Statements of Work in specific statuses such as Rejected, Pending Submission, Pending Approval, Withdrawn, Approval Paused, Pending Distribution, or Halted (not reaching PMO thresholds) will display as Pending.
  3. Please find more information on our help page :New Home Page Experience | SAP Help Portal
