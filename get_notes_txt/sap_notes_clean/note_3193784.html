Symptom

Component: Incident Management
Module: Tasks

You edit Incidents or Near Misses or Safety Observations in EHS Incident Management. You navigate to the tab Tasks. You click the link in column Task Title, however no action is being triggered.

Other Terms

﻿EHHSS_INC_REC_OIF_V3_TASK_ATS﻿

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason:
Program error.

Prerequisites:
For information about the validity of the corrections, see the correction instructions.

Solution

The "Support Packages" section lists the Support Packages that contain these corrections.

Alternatively, you can implement the attached correction instructions.

The link in the Task Title has been removed. To manage tasks in the task list, click button "Manage Tasks" and navigate to the corresponding task.
