Symptom

How to find my subscription bills in terms that my account is Enforced/ Suspended?

 

Cause

Account suspended due to overdue bill or unpaid invoice.  

Resolution

SAP Business Network suspends your account if you don't pay your outstanding fees.

To find your subscription invoices and bills, follow the steps:

  •  Click on your Initials in the upper right corner;
  • Choose Service subscription;
  • A page with the due bills, invoices and payment methods will appear;
