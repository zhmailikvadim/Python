Symptom

The aim of the Knowledge Base Article is to elaborate the steps that need to be performed to manage authorizations for the business users.

Environment

  • SAP BTP
  • Cloud Foundry Environment 

Resolution

Business users are authorized by assigning role collections to the shadow users with the following steps:

  1. Define role collections to control user authorization for the required apps and their data according to the business needs (see Build Role Collections).

  2. Assign role collections to users or user groups you have maintained in the identity provider (see Assign Role Collections to Users or User Groups).

Keywords

Business users , authorizations , role collections , identity provider
