Symptom

You are trying to find information concerning a declared disruption or degradation event that happened in SAP Analytics Cloud.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental." 

Environment

SAP for Me

SAP Analytics Cloud

Resolution

1. In SAP for Me, go to "Systems and Provisioning"

2. Change to the next tab "Systems"

3. In All Systems section, search for your SAP Analytics Cloud system

 

4. Click in the system and go to "Availability" 

 

5. Find the event based on the date

6. Click the event and see communicated details concerning the disruption or degradation
