Symptom

  • After creating a Multiple Transfer template with two transfers between different currencies
  • The summary view of the Multiple Transfer template does not show the amounts for the second transfer

Environment

  • SAP Online Retail Banking 8.3

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login to Retail Banking (CB)
  2. Create a new Multiple Transfer Template with two transfers:
    • For the first transfer, the from account should be in USD and the to account should be in CAD.  The transfer amount will be in USD.
    • For the second transfer, the from account should be in CAD and the to account should be in USD.  The transfer amount will be in CAD.
  3. Navigate Template Summary page
  4. Click the Info icon to view the summary of the template
  5. Note that the from and to amounts for the second transfer are no displayed

Cause

  • A Product Defect was found
  • When the base currencies of transfers included in a Multiple Transfer are different, the amounts for some transfers are not displayed

Resolution

SAP Product Development has included a fix for this issue in SAP Online Retail Banking 8.3 Support Package (SP) 01 Patch Level (PL) 04.

Keywords

Omnichannel, OCB, Multiple, Transfer, currency, missing, amounts
