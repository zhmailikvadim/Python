Symptom

The user who performed the provisioning of a Cloud System/Tenant is not always set as the Technical Administrator; sometimes this user becomes the Main IT Contact after provisioning.
It is necessary to understand why this happens.

Environment

SAP Business Technology Platform

Resolution

This process is defined at the contract level, so it may differ for each customer and product.
To get more information, contact the Account Executive and request details about user definitions after provisioning.

See KBA 2626344 - Who is the SAP Account Manager for my company?
