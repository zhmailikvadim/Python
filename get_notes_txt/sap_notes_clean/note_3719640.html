Symptom

Objects of type SIA6 (IAM Apps) can be transported, but they can also be generated automatically as local objects.

During transports, this may result in error message concerning "original objects" that can't be overwritten.

Reason and Prerequisites

The lifecycle of SIA6 objects was not clearly defined from the beginning. As a result, we have such clashes.

Solution

As of the version 09.02.26, R3trans does no longer care for the "originality" of SIA6 objects. So in case of collisions, the transported object will always replace a locally generated object, originals are no longer "protected".
