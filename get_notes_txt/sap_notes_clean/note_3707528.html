Symptom

When you run the report Read payroll result and update infotype 0354/0355/0818 (HTWLAM00), for employees who do not have the subtype 1 in the infotype New Pension (TW) (0818), the new OAI amount can be incorrect.

For example, employee A lacks a subtype 1 record in IT0818. This employee has a National Health Insurance (NHI) amount. In report HTWLAM00, the system displays the new NHI amount as 69,800 and the new OAI amount as 72,800. This calculation is incorrect. According to the calculation rule for OAI amounts in SAP Note 3166986, when an employee lacks a New Pension (NP) amount, the system should calculate the OAI amount based on the NHI amount in IT0355. The correct new OAI amount is 69,800.

Now after you apply this SAP Note, the new OAI amount will be 69,800 as expected.

Other Terms

HTWLAM00, /107, /109, IT0818, New Pension

Reason and Prerequisites

This is the correction of legal function.

Solution

Do either of the following:

  • Import the relevant Support Package into your system.
  • Apply the Correction Instructions in the note.
