Symptom

This KBA describes the donation types in IT0364-CHAMT "Donation" and IT0364-CHEDU "Donation(2)".

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll
  • SAP S/4 HANA

Resolution

Refer the following donation types when maintaining donations in IT0364:

  • IT0364-CHAMT "Donation"
    In this field, the tax exemption is the same amount of donations maintained, not exceeding 10% of the net income after all other deductions.
    1. Public Charity: Donations to temples, the Thai Red Cross, public hospitals, state or private schools, and registered public charities/foundations.
    2. School Lunch Program: For primary school lunch funds.
    3. Youth Crisis Project: Support for the "Roi Jai Chuay Yao Wa Chon Thai" project.
    4. Pho Thong Project: Support for the "Pho Thong Khong Chao Thai" project.
    5. Mangrove Reforestation: Support for the permanent mangrove reforestation project in honor of Queen Sirikit's 72nd birthday.
    6. Welfare & Sports Funds: Donations to the Fund for Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities, Social Welfare Fund, Child Protection Fund, or National Sports Development Fund.
    7. Natural Disaster Relief: Donations to help victims of floods, storms, fires, or other natural disasters in Thailand.
    8. Cultural Promotion Fund: General donations to the fund promoting national culture.
    9. Sports Authority of Thailand: Donations to the SAT, provincial sports committees, the Department of Physical Education, or authorized sports associations.
    10. Education for the Disabled: Donations to the Fund for Promotion and Development of Education for the Disabled.
    11. Educational Technology: Donations to the Educational Technology Development Fund under the Ministry of Education.
    12. Religious Landmarks: Donations for the construction/maintenance of the Great Holy Relics Pagoda (Naphamethinidon Naphaphonphumisiri) in Chiang Mai.
  • IT0364 - CHEDU "Donation(2)"
    In this field, the tax exemption is twice amount of the donations maintained, not exceeding 10% of the net income after expenses and primary allowances.
    1. General donations:
      1. Media and Cultural Funds: Donations to the Safe and Creative Media Development Fund, National Culture Promotion Fund, Contemporary Art Promotion Fund, National Archives Promotion Fund, or Archeology Fund.
      2. Justice Fund: Donations to the Justice Fund.
      3. Public Hospitals: Donations to government medical facilities/state hospitals.
    2. E-donations:
      1. Social Enterprise Fund: Donations to the Social Enterprise Promotion Fund.
      2. Forestry Projects: Donations to the Community Forest Support Partnership Project to reduce global warming.
      3. Development Funds: Donations to the National Metrology System Development Fund, Public Health System Development Fund, Science and Technology Development Fund, or Science Research and Innovation Fund.
      4. Equitable Education Fund (EEF): Donations specifically for educational equality.
      5. Knowledge Development: Donations to the Office of Knowledge Management and Development (OKMD).
      6. Thai Red Cross & 26 Specified Foundations: Includes the Thai Red Cross Society and foundations such as Chulabhorn, Chaipattana, Siriraj, Ramathibodi, and various state hospital foundations.
    3. Other donations:
      1. Educational Materials: Expenses for public or private schools to provide books or electronic media to promote reading.
      2. Facilities for the Disabled: Expenses to provide public facility access, welfare, or assistance to the disabled (100% of payment).
      3. Teacher Development: Donations to the Fund for Development of Teachers, Faculty, and Educational Personnel.
      4. Child Development Centers: Donations to local government organizations for establishing or supporting Child Development Centers.
      5. Juvenile Reform Support: Expenses for vocational training and rehabilitation activities for children and youth in observation and protection centers.

Note that there are time limit for these donations. Please refer Thailand Revenue Department PIT91 latest template for reference.
