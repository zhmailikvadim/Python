Symptom

To determine the person groups subject to withholding tax as part of the uniform wage notification procedure (ELM), it is necessary in the decision tree of the feature CHFID (WHT ELM) according to the employee subgroup (PERSK). In the standard system, the attribute PERSK is not available in the decision tree of feature CHFID.

Other Terms

WHT, withholding tax, ELM, CHFID, PERSK, employee subgroup, PMEC2, PE03, decision tree, feature, attribute

Reason and Prerequisites

The structure PMEC2 determines the structure for the decision operations of feature CHFID. In the standard system, this structure does not contain the field for the employee subgroup (PERSK).
