Symptom

The Down Payments from Hungary are not displayed in the eDocument Cockpit App. 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to the eDocument Cockpit App.
  2. Select the eDocuments from Hungary.

The Down Payments from Hungary are not in the eDocument Cockpit App.

Cause

Currently, the SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition does not support eDocuments for Down Payments in Hungary. If this functionality is needed for a business scenario, it is recommended to submit a request through the Influence Portal. This platform serves as the official channel for proposing ideas and improvement requests, allowing the development team to evaluate the feasibility of implementing the feature in the SAP Business system.

 SAP Continuous Influence Portal

  

Resolution

This is the currently standard system behavior.

Additionally, consider the Community Blog linked below as a potential workaround for the reported scenario:

SAP Community Blog - Customer down payment in S4HC – temporary workaround for Hungary

Note: This option isn't standard functionality, and it should be reviewed with by the respective SAP Consultant before implementation.
