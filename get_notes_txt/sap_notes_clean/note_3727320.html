Symptom

This article covers the most common issues related to SmartRecruiters provisioning requests.

Environment

SmartRecruiters for SAP SuccessFactors

Resolution

You have a valid SmartRecruiters license, however, the SmartRecruiters product is missing on SAP for Me → Provisioning → Systems & Provisioning → Systems to be Provisioned.

  • If the product is not listed and provisioning cannot be started, the customer must contact their SAP Account Manager or the Implementation Partner.
  • To identify your SAP Account Manager: 2626344 - Who is the SAP Account Executive for my company?
  • If the contract start date has not started yet, the product may not be available.
  • Creating a ticket to Product Support is not recommended if the issue is only related to the product missing on SAP for Me.

You are able to click Start Provisioning, but the provisioning does not finish or shows an error.

  • Create a case for component XX-SER-FORME.

If you are not able to click Start Provisioning, either because the button is greyed out or because an error appears.

  • Refer to 3084213 - Cannot access Provisioning or the Start Provisioning button is grayed out - SAP for Me.
