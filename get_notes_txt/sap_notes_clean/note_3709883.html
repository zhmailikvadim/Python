Symptom

You have applied note 3565053 to enable the functionality to hide the sales organization from value help. Even the configuration is done properly and function works in SAP GUI, the same is not applied in MDG UI.

Environment

  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 2020
  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 2021
  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 2022
  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 2023
  • Master Data Governance on S/4 HANA on-premise 2025

Resolution

Please apply the following notes on your system and retest:

  • 3578958 - MDG-C/S: Sales Areas & Purchasing Organizations - Options to customize the Validity / Visibility

  • 3684343 - MDG-C: F4-help for Sales Office / Sales Group does not consider the flag "Hide"

Keywords

Value help, Sales Office, Sales Area, Purchasing Organization, Hide, Validity, Visibility
