Symptom

You transfer a SD invoice with a negative total net value. The invoice has at least one down payment clearing line with a negative value. In the financial accounting, the posting keys and the debit/credit signs of the down payment clearing items are incorrect.

Other Terms

down payment clearing, VF01, VF02, VF04, negative down payment, negative net value, AC_DOWNPAYMENT_CHARGE, t_accdpc-wrbtr, t_accdpc-xnetwr, xneg, bschl, shkzg

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Please implement the attached correction instruction in your system.
