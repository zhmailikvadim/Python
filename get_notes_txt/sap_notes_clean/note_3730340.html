Symptom

  • December Holiday Pay (single and double) for period 12 (Y+1) is not processed when the employee moves from Full Time PL to Part Time PL
  • No December regularisation wage types (/3F*, /3G*, /3H*) are generated in payroll results.
  • No error message is displayed.

Environment

  • Product: SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • Belgium

Cause

Periods of full-time parental leave recorded with 0 hours/days in Infotype 0007 are not assimilated for holiday-right accrual; as a result, no holiday rights are accrued for those months and there is nothing to regularise in December

Resolution

When an employee is on a 100% parental leave, though his contract is still fully active, his/her leave itself is not assimilated. This means the employee is not accruing any holiday rights. Which means that when the employee moves to a 80% work schedule for ex, if we do the calculation, there are no accrued rights to pay out.

Its not that the system assumes that the payout has already happened. The system looks at what has been accrued as holiday pay right, assesses how much holiday right there should be if the employee were to have worked 80% all year, and then payout the difference. But in the case the employee has not accrued any holiday pay rights, as the parental leave is not assimilated for the purpose of holiday pay, SAP system doesn't calculate any pay out any holiday pay. The calculation would have been very different, if the employee only had 1 month of parental leave, before the move to 80%.
