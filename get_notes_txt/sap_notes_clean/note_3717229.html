Symptom

As reported with SAP Note: 3683742 (Local Network Access (LNA) restrictions in Chrome and Edge browsers).


Chrome or Edge shows the following error when opening links in Web Dynpro or WebGUI apps impacting launch of configuration in JSP UI.:
The connection is blocked because it was initiated by a public page to connect to devices or servers on your private network. Reload this page to allow the connection.

This specific error is due to a new feature introduced in Google Chrome 142 or Edge 143 and is reported under Symptoms 2 and 3

Other Terms

JSPUI, FBS_SOLCONF_SHRWEB, SSC(Solution Sales Configuration)

Solution

The solution as suggested for Symptoms 2 and 3, has been adopted accordingly in the iframes property used in JSP pages with allow="local-network" attribute.


For more details refer: LNA Adoption Guide, and possible solution. For us, we followed the solution under "How can I make local network requests from inside an iframe?"
