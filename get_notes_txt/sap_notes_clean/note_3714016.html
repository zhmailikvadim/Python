Symptom

When a supplier confirms a purchase order and adds shipping costs in the order confirmation, those shipping costs do not appear on the Purchase Order Summary page or while performing Review Order Confirmation in Ariba Buying

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Purchase Order is created in Ariba Buying
  2. Supplier confirms the Purchase Order and adds shipping costs.
  3. Buyer reviews the Order Confirmation in Ariba Buying.
  4. Shipping costs are not displayed in the Purchase Order Summary page within Ariba Buying.

Cause

Resolution

This is expected behavior. When a supplier adds shipping costs in the order confirmation, they are not displayed on the Purchase Order Summary page or during Review Order Confirmation. The shipping costs are visible only in the Purchase Order line item Fulfillment section.

If any changes are required on the same, an improvement request needs to be raised.
