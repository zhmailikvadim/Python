Symptom

System shows that "No matching items found" message on value help (F4) within the Partner tab in Maintain ACM Trading Contract fiori application.

Replication steps:
1. Launch the Maintain ACM Trading Contract Fiori application.
2. Click the Create button to initiate a new trading contract.
3. Enter all mandatory information in the header section.
4. Navigate to the Partner tab at the header level and click the Add button.
5. Select the Sales Employee partner function from the value help.
6. Use the value help by clicking the F4 on partner field.
7. Observe that the system displays a "No matching items found" message instead of the partner details.

Other Terms

Agricultural Contract Management; ACM; Maintain ACM Trading Contract application; Partner

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
