Symptom

You record more activity periods other than regular employment on infotype 0023 (SAP Note 3666533), before the functionality becomes active (depending on switch T7PL13[PY-3666533]).
When the 2nd of such periods is recorded, then an IT0041 entry is tried to be updated with an incorrect validity period, namely with an end date that is before the begin date. As a result, the updating of IT0041 entries stops.

Other Terms

IT0023, IT0414, IT0041, 3666533.

Reason and Prerequisites

Gap in the implementation.

Solution

A change in the seniority calculation has been implemented.

Apply the correction by importing the Support Package specified for your release, or by using the Note Assistant.
