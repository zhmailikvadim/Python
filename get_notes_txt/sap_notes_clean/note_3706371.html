Symptom

  • ASE errorlog reports a signal 11 at address sendtext followed by a stack trace.
  • The stack trace includes the functions:

sendtext
LeSendRow::_sendColsToClient(pss*, int, int)
LeSendRow::sendRowToClient(pss*, unsigned char, short)
LeEmitSndOp::_LeOpNext(ExeCtxt&)
LePlanNext
exec_lava
s_execute

  • The affected sql query contains a large IN list.

Environment

  • SAP ASE 16.0 SP03 PL12 or higher.
  • SAP ASE 16.0 SP04 PL02 or higher.

Cause

These symptoms are under investigation under CR 831537.
Title as "infected with 11 at sendtext with large in clause". 

Resolution

As CR 831537 is unresolved, there is currently no target Fix Pack or Service Pack scheduled for this issue.

A shared memory dump triggered by a signal 11 would help progress the investigation of CR 831537 .

Please refer to KBA 1986667 for steps on how to configure this.
