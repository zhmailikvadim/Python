Symptom

Risk Exposure is showing as Not Available on the New Supplier Profile Summary for my suppliers.

Cause

Custom Categories scoring is enabled on your site

Resolution

The New Profile Summary feature does not currently support Custom Categories scoring. Because of this, the Risk Exposure field shows as Not Available in the Supplier Profile. At this time, only Legacy Scoring is supported.

Please note that this issue might also occur with Legacy Scoring. A code correction is scheduled for the March release (March 13th, 7 PM PST) to fix this problem.
