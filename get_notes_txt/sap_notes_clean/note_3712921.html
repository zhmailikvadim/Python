Symptom

  • Information is needed how to extend the QM Fiori app 'Record Defects' (F2929).
  • It is not possible to extend the app 'Record Defects' (F2929) via UI5 adaptation.

  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Resolution

To extend the app 'Record Defects' (F2929), check the relevant documentations at:

'App Extensibility: Record/Process Defect'
https://help.sap.com/docs/SAP_S4HANA_ON-PREMISE/2bc3ee8d1c83404e8cf62418640004f2/d96a7e63595d4de8a561ead8b51dd0ae.html?version=LATEST&locale=en-US

'Add Customer Fields on UI'
https://help.sap.com/docs/SAP_S4HANA_ON-PREMISE/2bc3ee8d1c83404e8cf62418640004f2/253ec4c8d01f44a0b256615bf3d581c1.html?version=LATEST&locale=en-US

***

It is not yet supported to use SAPUI5 Extension Projects / SAPUI5 Adaptation Projects to extend this app.
