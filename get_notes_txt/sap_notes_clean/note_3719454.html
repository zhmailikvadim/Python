Symptom

When you copy project, a popup is raised for missing mandatory field values. You enter the missing field and update some other organization data as well. Then it duplicates the WBS element entries in the list and creates incorrect hierarchy. If you only update mandatory field and hit enter, it works fine.

Prereq to reproduce: You have enabled the multi-language short text for WBS and marked a field as mandatory.

project template, standard project, copy project, copy WBS element, CNLDST, short text, OPUJ, mandatory field

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error
