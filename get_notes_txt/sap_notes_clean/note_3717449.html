Symptom

Authorization checks should be used to secure reports against being called by unauthorized users.

Every CIP2SAP report must have a ZX-Include at the beginning of the report execution (e.g. START-OF-SELECTON) where authorization statements can be implemented.

Other Terms

CIP2SAP , SIEMENS,  missing authorization check, C2SFIBU, LE

Reason and Prerequisites

The reports do not have a ZX-Include.

Solution

Implement correction instruction.
