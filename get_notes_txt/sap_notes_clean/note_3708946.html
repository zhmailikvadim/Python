Symptom

The mapping table /SCWM/TRCLOCSTAG is used to determine the staging area dependent on different attributes like warehouse, product, recipient location or staging area and door determination group. Currently the product must be filled in the record if you are using the staging area and door determination group as a condition field. This means, records for all products with the staging area and door determination group must be added to the table /SCWM/TRCLOCSTAG. But you want to use the staging area and door determination group as a grouping field for different products.

Other Terms

/SCWM/TRCLOCSTAG, DRDETGR, Staging Area and Door Determination Group, /SCWM/CL_RCLOC

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing functionality.

Solution

Implement the correction instruction or the corresponding support package.
