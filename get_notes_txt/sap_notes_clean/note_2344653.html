Symptom

The area menu 'Contract Accounts Receivable and Payable' of the SAP components IS-U and/or IS-M has been enhanced or changed.

Other Terms

Area menus EVKK (IS-U, SAP Utilities) and ISM_CA (IS-M, SAP Media)

Reason and Prerequisites

Introduction of new or changed functions in the relevant area menu.

Solution

Enhance the area menu ‘Contract Accounts Receivable and Payable’ in your system.

  1. Call transaction SE43. 
  2. Select the area menu to be changed (the name of this area menus is EVKK for SAP Utilities, ISM_CA for SAP Media)
  3. Choose F6 (Change Area Menu)
  4. Add the new transaction at the required location by right-clicking and selecting a suitable function.
  5. Save your entries.
