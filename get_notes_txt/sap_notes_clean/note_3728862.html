Symptom

During the data sync between SuccessFactors and SmartRecruiters, if the field from SuccessFactors has the same ID from an existing field on SmartRecruiters, the mapping will fail. 

Environment

  • SmartRecruiters for SAP SuccessFactors
  • SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting

Resolution

The issue is currently being addressed by SAP Software Engineering team.

For Product Support, refer to Internal Memo.
