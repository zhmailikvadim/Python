Symptom

Existing values can be viewed under Maintain Basic Configurations > Distribution Channel, but there is no option to add or create new entries.

Environment

SAP Entitlement Management. 

Cause

User does not have the required EMS role/authorization. EMS uses role templates to control who can maintain configuration entries.

Resolution

  1. Review SAP Entitlement Management role templates and identify the template that grants maintenance rights for the configuration area you need: SAP help document
  2. Provide the identified role/template name to your SAP BTP/system administrator and request they assign the role to your user account.
  3. After the admin assigns the role, sign out and sign back in and verify you can add entries.

See Also

SAP help document
