Symptom

Request to link supplier companies under a supplier parent for reporting purposes.

Resolution

SAP Fieldglass does not support linking if supplier companies under a supplier parent. In SAP Fieldglass, only buyer companies can be configured as child companies. Even when a supplier parent is involved, the child entity must still be a buyer company, and this linkage is supported only in conjunction with an MSP instance. Currently, parent–child relationships between supplier companies are not supported in Fieldglass. While suppliers can transact and collaborate with buyers in the system, they cannot be linked to one another in a hierarchical structure.
Enhancement Request needs to be raised for such set up.
