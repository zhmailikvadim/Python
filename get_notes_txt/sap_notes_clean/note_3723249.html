Symptom

  • Inbound and outbound deliveries are created without Advanced Shipping and Receiving (ASR) relevance.
  • The ASR indicator and warehouse execution status are not set in the delivery.
  • ASR-related Freight Units and Freight Orders are created correctly in Transportation Management (TM).

Environment

SAP S/4HANA 1709 +

Cause

Advance Shipping and Receiving(ASR) relevance is not determined correctly for locations(i.e.: shipping points) due to missing assignment of business system group (BSG). The BSG assignment can be checked for locations in table /SAPAPO/LOCMAP and the necessary BSG is visible in transaction /SAPAPO/C2. 

The absence of the BSG assignment is likely related to manual location creation. Furthermore, in cases where the report /SAPAPO/CREATE_LOCATION was executed, the BSG field may be empty if it had not been maintained at the time of location creation.

Resolution

Use report /SAPAPO/UPDATE_LOC_BSG to fix and update the missing BSG assignment for the affected locations (i.e.: shipping points). After the assignment, the ASR relevance should be determined correctly. This report was introduced in SAP Note 3315995 - Location: Report BSG adaption.
