Symptom

When the system language is set to Swedish or any other language, the "Workflow Comment" label in the Approval History section is displayed in English instead of the system language.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to an employee profile.
  2. Navigate to Employment Information → Job Information.
  3. Open a history record.
  4. Scroll down to the Approval History section.
  5. Observe that the "Workflow Comment" label is displayed in English.

Resolution

  1. This is a known limitation in the current View Approval History page, there is no workaround at this time.
  2. we encourage you to submit an enhancement request to have this functionality considered within future development cycles – you can do this on our Customer Community Portal at http://community.successfactors.com/.
  3. For detailed steps on how to raise an enhancement request please follow KBA Article 2090228 on “How to submit an enhancement request”.
