Reason and Prerequisites

Customer has licensed one ore more of SAP margin optimization solutions by Vistex and is about to onboard the solution(s).

Solution

Welcome on behalf of SAP and Vistex organization, we would like to welcome you to our suite of SAP margin optimization solutions by Vistex.

To help you get started with your onboarding journey, this message provides an overview of the entire process.
Along the way, you will find links to documents with specific instructions and detailed information.

Cloud Access

If this is your first time licensing a solution deployed on SAP Business Technology Platform (SAP BTP),
you will be granted a BTP Global Account:

  1. SAP provisions entitlement for a BTP Global Account and BTP administrative User ID
  2. SAP sends a welcome email for BTP
  3. Follow those onboarding steps for the BTP Global Account

This is only done once and may have already been performed if you previously licensed other SAP BTP services or applications.

For information regarding SAP BTP see:
https://help.sap.com/docs/btp/sap-business-technology-platform/btp-basic-platform-concepts

Solution Deployment
You acquired solution is one or more of SAP margin optimization solutions by Vistex.
If this is your first SAP margin optimization solutions by Vistex, you will complete the following steps.

If you already have one or more SAP margin optimization solutions by Vistex, these steps are not necessary. In that case, simply an additional solution is added to your existing suite.

  1. Communicate to your SAP Account Representative any deployment preferences for:
    1. Hyperscaler (Vistex solutions can be deployed on AWS, GCP or Azure)
    2. EU Access (processing data restricted to the European Economic Area)
  2. Create a subaccount in your SAP BTP Global Account.  This is a general platform task described in more detail here.

  3. Once the sub-account is created, please share the subdomain details of sub-account with Vistex.

  4. Vistex will make a reservation before the subscription. Once the reservation is complete by Vistex, please proceed with subscription

  5. Subscribe to SAP margin optimization solutions by Vistex. 
    You can find step-by-step instructions within the Customer Admin Guide.
  6. When deployment of your cloud tenants is complete, you will be notified of your solution admin User ID (separate from your BTP administrative User ID provided earlier).
  7. Using your solution admin User ID, create additional users for others who need access.  This includes business, IT, and any partner or Vistex Solution Delivery staff who may be involved in your implementation project.

Solution Adoption

Now you are ready for solution implementation to begin.  You should engage Vistex Solution Delivery team or a Vistex ecosystem partner to plan your implementation.

  • Select appropriate company and/or industry template(s) to be installed to jumpstart your realization
  • Plan enhancements and extensions of templates to add more business process coverage and value
  • Assess business practices and propose improvements that increase efficiency and align with solution capabilities
  • Provide training for best and fastest adoption

SAP and Vistex are committed to helping you get the most out of your investment in our margin optimization solutions. 

If you encounter any issues with SAP margin optimization solutions by Vistex, report an incident on the SAP Support Portal.
To report an incident, go to the SAP Support Portal at Services & Support - SAP for Me. In the incident, specify the component XX-PART-VIS-GTM to indicate the incident is related to SAP margin optimization solutions by Vistex.
