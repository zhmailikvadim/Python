Symptom

Error GLT2201 "balancing field 'profit center' in line item *** not filled" is issued during internal order settlement in KO88, when the receiver is Asset (FXA).

Environment

  • SAP R/3
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Execute KO88.
  2. Provide the parameters and execute.
  3. The error GLT2201 is triggered.

Cause

  • The GL account associated with the asset class is defined as a balance sheet account and not as a cost element category 90 (record account assignment not flagged).  
  • In SAP ECC, profit center derivation often relies on document splitting or order master profit center for settlement postings, allowing KO88 without an AUC cost center if reconciliation accounts are category 90 cost elements.  
  • In SAP S/4HANA, stricter Universal Journal (ACDOCA) balancing for profit center in Asset Accounting postings is enforced, requiring derivation via cost center on AUC even if the profit center is maintained in the order.

Resolution

  1. Ensure the AUC asset class GL (reconciliation account) is defined as cost element category 90 (non-operating expenses/revenues). This enables profit center inheritance from the sender (order) line during settlement.
  2. Maintain derivation OKB9 for the AUC GL to default the profit center from the order, bypassing asset master dependency.
