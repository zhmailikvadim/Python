Symptom

Change in Scotland public holiday calendar 2026 due to Scottish Bank holiday on June 15, 2026

Other Terms

SCAL, Calendar, Public Holiday, Factory Calendar, United Kingdom, Great Britain, Bank Holiday, Scotland

Reason and Prerequisites

 Change in Scotland public holiday calendar 2026 due to Scottish Bank holiday on June 15, 2026

Solution

Correct the calendar definition in customizing: IMG -> Global Settings maintain calendar (transaction SCAL)

1. Goto->  Holidays ->  Create  -> Is a moveable holiday -> Enter configurations for 15.06.2026 -> Name it "World Cup Bank Holiday"
-> Save
Note -   Only fill for 2026!

2. Goto -> Public Holiday Calendar -> Select GB ->
Assign the newly created Public Holiday "World Cup Bank Holiday" to the calendar GB.

When saving, changes to factory calendar are generated automatically.

See SAP note 501670 for additional info on calendar creation/change.
