Symptom

In Career Site Builder, a Cookie Section such as below is enabled:

  • Functional Cookies Section
  • Performance Cookies Section
  • Advertising Cookies Section

When a user accesses the RMK Career Site and view cookie preferences, the Cookie Section is disabled or set to "No". Is it possible to set them to "Yes" or "Accepted" by default?

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Marketing

Reproducing the Issue

  1. In Career Site Builder enable a cookie section from Global Settings > Cookie Manager page
  2. Access the external RMK Career Site
  3. Click on "Manage Cookie Preferences" in the cookie consent banner
  4. Observe that the cookie section is default to "No"

Resolution

Understand that the cookie section slider (e.g. "Enable Functional Cookies Section") only controls whether to display the section in the Cookie Consent Manager dialog. Enabling the cookie section doesn't mean that it is by default set to "Yes" or "Accepted" on behalf of the users.

Cookies Section are set to "No" due to legal requirements, as users must provide explicit consent for enabling these cookies.

Only the "Required Cookies Section" is always enabled and cannot be switched off.
