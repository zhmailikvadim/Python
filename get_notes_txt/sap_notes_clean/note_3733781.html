Symptom

In UK starter Checklist compliance forms, there are questions for New Hire to complete.

Is it possible to hide questions on UK Starter Checklist compliance form?

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Resolution

It is not possible to hide questions on UK Starter Checklist compliance form.

This is a standard compliance form and it does not accept customizations.
