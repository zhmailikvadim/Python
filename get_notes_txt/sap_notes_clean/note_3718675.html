Symptom

When manually posting an FI document only in non leading ledger, the CO document is not generated. 

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Post an FI document manually (FB01)  using with Cost elements only on  the non leading ledger.
  2. Observe that the CO document is not generated.

Cause

  • CO Documents are generated for postings associated with the leading ledger (0L)only, unless a specific scenario is in place where FINS_COGM is active.
  • In standard functionality, FI postings to non- leading ledgers do not generate CO documents.

Resolution

    Consider defining a specific ledger group that includes both the leading and non leading ledgers.
