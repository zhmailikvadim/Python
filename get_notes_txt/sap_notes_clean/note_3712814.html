Symptom

A health insurance fund confirms the amount of the compensation benefit in the EEL procedure. According to the notification, no compensation benefit is paid. At a later point in time, the health insurance fund cancels this notification and now reports a compensation benefit. After processing by the report Process Confirmations of Compensation Benefits (RPCEEVD0_IN), the reversal receives the status To Be Checked with the warning message "Compensation benefit is not granted" (HRPAYDESVEE125). As a result, this reversal is selected again by the report RPCEEVD0_IN and may result in an incorrect status in the infotype Electronic Data Exchange (0700) for the subtype DBHE.

Other Terms

Social Insurance

Solution

The program error is corrected.

In the future, the reversal for this situation will be processed in the same way as before the introduction of the DSLW data record version 13 on 1/1/2026. Depending on whether the subapplication Transfer of Compensation Benefits Reported from IT 0700 Subtype DBHE (KGUE) is active, the reversal is assigned the status To Be Checked with warning message HRPAYDESVEE 123 (KGUE not active) or HRPAYDESVEE124 (KGUE active). In both cases, the reversal is not processed again by the report RPCEEVD0_IN.

If a valid record of the infotype 0700 for the subtype DBHE was deleted due to such a situation due to the reprocessing of a reversal for the compensation benefit amount, create the record again manually.

This SAP Note continues to enhance the log of the report RPCEEVD0_IN so that reversals of health insurance fund notifications are easier to recognize in the future.

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified support package. After you import the HR Support Package or implement the correction instructions, you do not have to perform any further follow-on activities. For Release 6.04, manual pre-implementation steps (execution of an UDO report) are required.
