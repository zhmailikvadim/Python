Symptom

  • Reference classifications of export control classes are not displayed in Reclassify Products – legal control”  Fiori App.
  • In the “Show Reference Classification”, the system shows “No Data”.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud
  • International Trade Classification

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the app Reclassify Products – Legal Control (F2391).
  2. Select a material classified under legal regulation A.
  3. Choose “Show Reference Classification”.
  4. No data is displayed even though there is a corresponding classification exiting for legal regulation B.

Cause

The country of origin is not maintained in customizing for legal regulation B.

Resolution

  1. Open configuration point 'Define Trade Compliance Legal Regulations'.
  2. Maintain the Country of Origin for the relevant legal regulation(s) (in this case legal regulation B).
  3. Save the changes.
