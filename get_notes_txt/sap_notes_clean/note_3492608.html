Symptom

On the selection screen of the transaction Update Product Retention Period in Material Document (/n/SAPSLL/RET_PERI_UPD), you cannot restrict the selection to the goods supplier.

As a result, the product retention period for a specific goods supplier cannot be updated in the material documents.

Other Terms

Preference, product retention period, /SAPSLL/RET_PERI_UPD, /SAPSLL/PREMDOC_RET_PERIOD_UPD

Solution

Implement the correction instructions and carry out the steps described in the manual correction instructions.
