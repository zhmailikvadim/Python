Symptom

Why do I receive the error "On Behalf Of has been deleted" while approving approvables?

Cause

The requester (On Behalf Of) is inactive in the realm.

Resolution

The requester must be active for the approvable (e.g., PR, Invoice, SDU, Invoice Reconciliation, etc.) to proceed with approval.
