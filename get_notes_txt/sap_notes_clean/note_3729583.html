Symptom

When a location with manual geocoordinates is accessed, its geocoordinates are overwritten automatically by geocoordinates on the most specific geocoding level. This happens if the location is accessed (e.g. a Freight Order is created with the location) and the validity of the geocoordinates on the most specific geocoding level is over and therefore recalculated automatically.

Other Terms

preserve manual flag, legal handler

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement attached correction instructions or apply appropriate Support Package.

This note should be implemented together with note 3719711 which corrects behavior of the /SAPAPO/MASS_GEOCODE_LOC report.
