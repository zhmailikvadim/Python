Symptom

The Credit limit card under Customer Insight in Account shows NaN %

Environment

SAP Cloud for Customer

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Navigation Menu
  2. Go to Customers
  3. Go to Accounts
  4. Open an Account
  5. Go to Customer Insights facet
  6. Under Key Metrics section, credit limit card shows NaN%

Cause

The credit segment data maintained in S/4 HANA does not match the data maintained in the iFlow "Request Credit Account Data from SAP Business Suite" in CPI

Resolution

  1. Verify the mapping in CPI and ensure that the correct credit segment data as per S/4 HANA is maintained.
  2. Kindly note that the current standard system behavior does not support more than one credit segment data.
