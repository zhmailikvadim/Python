Symptom

Online utilization posting is not triggered for purchase order with multiple account assignment. However, the periodic utilization works.

Other Terms

POAC, MIRO, MIGO, ME23N, ZEKKN, XMACC

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a program error. The constellation of values ZEKKN, XMACC and newly created items was not expected.

Solution

Apply the advanced correction manually or upgrade to corresponding support package.
