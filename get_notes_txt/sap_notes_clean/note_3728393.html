Symptom

  • Feasibility of initiating a workflow to send email notifications to approving authorities after successful payroll execution in PCC to display total net results.

Environment

  • Human Capital Management (HCM)
  • Payroll Control Center (PCC)
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Execute payroll in Payroll Control Center.
  2. Complete the payroll process successfully.
  3. Attempt to configure a workflow to send email notifications to CFO and CEO displaying the total net payroll results for approval.

Cause

 There is no standard solution available for sending email notifications for payroll results approval in Payroll Control Center.

Resolution

You can try to implement customized codes to achieve the desired functionality.

Keywords

payroll control center, email notification, workflow, payroll results approval, feasibility, configurations, payroll execution, Email
