Symptom

When the field Kind Pension Calculation (P0503-KINCA) on infotype Pensioner Definition (0503) is set to ‘2’ the pensioner is single.

When the field Kind Pension Calculation (P0503-KINCA) on infotype Pensioner Definition (0503) is set to ‘1’ the pensioner is head of family.
