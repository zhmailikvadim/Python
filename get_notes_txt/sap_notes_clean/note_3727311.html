Symptom

You would like to retrieve the logs of the Changes made in the AI Agents like prompts or conditions.

Environment

AI CX Toolkit

Resolution

Currently, the AI CX Toolkit doesn't have this functionality for the AI Agents.

If this feature/functionality is required urgently, please refer to KBA 3475641 - Functionality Currently not Available.

An alternative option may be to check the corresponding SAP Customer Influence Site to submit an idea:

  • SAP Sales Cloud
  • SAP Service Cloud
