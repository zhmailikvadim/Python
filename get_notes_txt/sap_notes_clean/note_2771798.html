Symptom

The S/4HANA readiness check identified that a specific Add-On is not compatible with the checked S/4HANA version.

Other Terms

S/4HANA Transformation; AddOn; add-on; S/4HANA; compatibility; S/4HANA System Conversion; S/4HANA Upgrade

Reason and Prerequisites

A Fiori UI Add-On is installed on the source system. Transformation to S/4HANA is planned

Solution

No upgrade path exists for the specific solution. S/4HANA has a dedicated UI delivery with UIs and content and does not use the previous UI Add-ons for Business Suite.

The uninstallation of the Add-On is optional for most of the addons and should only be performed if the apps are not used in combination with another Business Suite system. Before deleting the add-on, you need to make sure that the apps were not used in a Hub Scenario for multiple / different backend systems.

Information about uninstallation instruction of the individual add-ons are listed in notes below

  • 2408541 - Uninstallation of Fiori based Front End Software Components.
  • 2011192 - Uninstallation of ABAP add-ons
