Symptom

In Manage Force Elements the actions for Delete, Delete Plan and Delimit are enabled although they are not supported according to their planning status. In further an entry can be edited although its planning status does not allow any change.

The inconsistent behaviour occurs in the facets of:

  • Name
  • Description
  • Characteristics
  • Readiness

Other Terms

S/4HANA Defense & Security, Manage Force Elements, F4112, Name, Description, Characteristics, Readiness

Reason and Prerequisites

The system does not correctly control the availability of action buttons in the user interface.
As a result, buttons are shown as active even when the action cannot be performed.

Solution

 Implement the correction instruction.
