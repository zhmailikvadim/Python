Symptom

How to create a user for SAP Support in Agent Performance Management.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
  • Agent Performance Management

Resolution

  1. During a SAP case, SAP Support may request you to provide them with access to your Agent Performance Management environment. This allows further troubleshooting, logs access and system analysis.
  2. To create the user, follow the steps outlined in: Creating a New User.
  3. Note that your administrator environment team is responsible for managing all users present in your environment, including those from SAP.
