Symptom

The use of IBP forecast data for product allocation (PAL) check is not clear.

Environment

  • S4 Hana
  • Advanced ATP
  • Product Allocation (PAL)

Cause

There is no standard solution available to transfer the data from IBP to advanced ATP-PAL area.

Due to the high complexity of both areas (PAL and IBP) – there are a lot of different planning concepts, technical objects, key figures/aggregation level, characteristics and combinations possible. Thus it is very difficult to provide a standard integration/mapping and concept/functionality for this issue.

If required, please raise an influence request for the SAP roadmap -> note 11.
