Symptom

On a yearly basis the tax administration publishes the values that are used within the individual tax evaluation (≠ advance taxes). These monetary values are used in evaluation 2027 upon the different income types 2026 and for reason of different tax reductions / exemptions. Due to increase of cost of living most of these values are subject of indexation.

Most of the published values do not have impact upon the monthly advance taxes being computed in payroll upon professional incomes. And several of them are incorporated in changes of Key Formula and ANNEX III for advance taxes. Therefore this indexation is only reflected in a few other values that have a relevancy in monthly payroll calculations for taxes.

The official values after indexation have not yet been published. But based upon the coefficient for the indexation, several Social Secretaries have announced the indexed values in advance.

SAP Note 3707437 is currently based upon to these preliminary announcements. Once the official values after indexation have been published, this SAP Note will be reviewed.

  1. Advantage Home Work
    The T511P-BESA2 (Limit Not Taxable Work-Home) now is 500,00 EUR.
  2. Minimum advantage company car
    The minimum amount for the monetary value of the advantage for company car now is 1.690,00 EUR.  
  3. Non-recurring result bonus
    Within the calculation of taxes the maximum of non-recurring result bonus exempted from taxes is increased to 3.701,00 EUR as of 01.01.2026. 

Note 

Via SAP Note 3555850 these indexations were delivered last year. With that delivery the threshold Limit Not Taxable Work-Home (T511P-BESA2) was already increased from 490.00 EUR to 500.00 EUR. However, checking the source documents shows that this increase was erroneously announced already last year. Consequently, the value for 2025 should have been 490.00 EUR. As part of this SAP Note the value for 2025 will be corrected and will be set back from 500,00 EUR to 490,00 EUR.

This change should only affect employees benefitting from a tax exemption for home-work travel. In the SAP standard solution that is limited to Home-work transport compensation wage types entered in Infotypes 0014/0015 with Processing Class 62 value 7 and for company cars used exclusively for Home-work travel (Wage types /167 and /169). It may, however, also apply to customer specific calculations using this constant. We advise customers to recalculate all employees with company cars used exclusively for home-work travel and employees with other home-work travel compensation before the tax year is closed. The recalculation should be done from January 2025 after this SAP Note has been implemented to ensure the tax exemption is calculated with the correct limit.

Other Terms

BESA2, ST035, T5BFD, ST170, ST171, T511P
