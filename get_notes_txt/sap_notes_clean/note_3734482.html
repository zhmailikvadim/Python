Symptom

I have a custom mapping configured for a document type and when I integrate I see the following error in the SAP ERP:

<ChainedException>
   <Exception_Name>CX_ST_CONSTRAINT_ERROR</Exception_Name>
   <Exception_Text>Value is longer than the maximum permitted length X: "XXX": Main Program:| Program:| Line:  0|               Valid</Exception_Text>
</ChainedException>

Environment

Managed Gateway for Spend&Network

SAP ERP

Cause

The SAP ERP field's domain has a maximum length, however the related payload field is exceeding this maximum length due to a custom mapping.

Resolution

Use the Document Validator in Managed Gateway for Spend&Network to identify the field that is exceeding the length. You can do that by searching for the value indicated in the error.

After that verify if the field is affected by your custom mapping. If this is happening adapt the custom mapping to obey the length indicated in the error. If you need assistance with your custom mapping, contact your consulting partner.

Keywords

Cig, standard mapping, deserialization, serialization
