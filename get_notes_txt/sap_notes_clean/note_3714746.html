Symptom

Contrary to the recommendation from SAP Note 1965800, you want to take into account the fiscal year variants defined in Asset Accounting during the extraction of the DataSource 0FI_AA_11 (instead of the fiscal year variant assigned in the general ledger). To do this, you have set the parameter BWOM2_TIMEST-BWFIAAFYV accordingly.

The DataSource 0FI_AA_11 takes the parameter BWFIAAFYV into account when determining the posting period, but the fiscal year variant of the general ledger is always loaded in the extraction structure (field PERIV) regardless of the setting for BWFIAAFYV. A fiscal year variant defined at depreciation area level is not loaded.

Other Terms

0FI_AA_11, BWFIAAFYV, PERIV, T093B-PERIV

Reason and Prerequisites

Cause

This is due to an incomplete function.

Prerequisites

  • You want the FI-AA-specific fiscal year variants defined in depreciation areas to be taken into account during the extraction (BWFIAAFYV = L).
  • You use Asset Accounting (new) (business function FIN_AA_PARALLEL_VAL) or you have activated the business function FIN_AA_CI_1 (this is automatically the case in sFIN and SAP S/4HANA releases).

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the relevant support package.

The extraction of the DataSource 0FI_AA_11 then returns the fiscal year variants assigned at the level of depreciation areas in the field PERIV if the FI-AA-specific fiscal year variants are to be taken into account during the extraction in accordance with BWFIAAFYV.

However, note that it does not make sense to load a fiscal year variant defined in FI-AA instead of the fiscal year variant of the general ledger. This function is only available for compatibility with older, faulty releases. For more information, see SAP Note 1965800.
