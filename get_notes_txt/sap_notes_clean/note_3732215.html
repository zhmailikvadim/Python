Symptom

The job SAP_EHFND_PCEP for report R_EHFND_PCEP_EVT_QUEUE_WORKER is consistently failing due to a Unit-of-Measure conversion output overflow in CL_UOM_CONVERSION, with message BM(398) “Field overflow.”

Other Terms

SAP_EHFND_PCEP, R_EHFND_PCEP_EVT_QUEUE_WORKER, CALC_ALCMP, CALC_LCMP

Solution

This SAP note implements a correction that correctly calculates concentrations and prevents the Unit-of-Measure conversion overflow.
