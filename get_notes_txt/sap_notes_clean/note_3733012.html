Symptom

Parallel line functionality is not visible, cannot be activated, or relevant configuration options are missing in Starter or Sandbox tenants.

Environment

  • SAP Central Business Configuration
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

The behavior occurs because Starter and Sandbox tenants are not part of the supported landscape for parallel line functionality. 

Resolution

  • The parallel line feature is only available for a 3-system landscape (Development, Test, Production).
  • Starter and Sandbox tenants are not included or integrated into the parallel line. Therefore, parallel line activities should be carried out only within the Development, Test, and Production systems.
