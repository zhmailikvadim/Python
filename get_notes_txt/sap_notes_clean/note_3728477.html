Symptom

  • A business rule created to default the pay group in the New Hire Data step based on specific criteria is not functioning as expected.
  • The business rule "Comp Default Pay Group Test" was assigned onInit in Business Configuration in compInfo but does not produce the desired outcome.

Environment

SuccessFactors Onboarding

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a business rule named "Comp Default Pay Group Test" to default the pay group in the New Hire Data step based on specific criteria.
  2. Assign the rule onInit in Business Configuration in compInfo.
  3. Attempt to verify the defaulting of the pay group when opening the New Hire Data step.

Resolution

It appears that the rule currently set is incorrect. Specifically, the condition

  • Job information model.country.value is equal to has to be replaced with Job information model.company.country.value is equal to
