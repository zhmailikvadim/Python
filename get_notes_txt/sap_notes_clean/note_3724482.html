Symptom

  • I am a worker and I need IT-related equipment.
  • I am a supplier and I am need IT-related equipment for a new employee.
  • I am a worker account and I need a creation of my email account.
  • I am a worker and I need a local login account.

Cause

Employee needs IT equipment.

Resolution

Requests for IT equipment (laptops, computers, peripherals), email account creation, or local system access must be handled by the employee’s internal IT department.

See Also

KB0220080 - Worker should contact their manager to return equipment

Keywords

laptop, computer, IT equipment, peripherals
