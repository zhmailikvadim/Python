Symptom

Import status displayed as "Success" even though no records were successfully imported what does this indicate

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Prepare a file containing formatting errors, such as extra commas causing a column mismatch.
  2. Upload the file to the Data Import/Export functionality within Supplier Lifecycle & Performance SM Administration.
  3. Observe that the import job completes with a Success status.
  4. Navigate to the supplier profile and verify that no new supplier contacts are created or visible in the Contacts section of the profile.

Cause

Resolution

Import Status populating as Success means there is no successful imports done.

If Success  has a suffix , lets say Success 1, Success 2 , Success 3, indicates no of records were imported successfully.

This is the intended behavior of the system.
