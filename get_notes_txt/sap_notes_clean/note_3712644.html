Symptom

I'd like to report the information from SAP Ariba Supplier Lifecycle & Performance ERP Data tab but I could not find any fields available on Analytical Reports to report the Bank Account, Last Integration State information, for example.

Resolution

This is a limitation. The information from ERP Data section is not reportable in Analytical Reports.

You could extract the same through the Supplier Data API.
