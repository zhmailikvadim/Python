Symptom

B2B Scenarios tab not visible in CPI
or seeking guidance on how to Activate TPM capability

Environment

  • SAP Business Technology Platform
  • SAP Integration Suite

Reproducing the Issue

Go to CPI WebUI, the B2B Scenarios tab not visible in left panel

Cause

TPM capability not activated

Resolution

TPM is a capability of ISuite, to subscribe TPM:

1. Before activate the TPM capabilities, please assign "Integration_Provisioner" role collection to logon user
2. Open Integration Suite. and goes to Home page of Integration Suite and perform below steps:

    Click “Manage Capabilities”.
    Click “Add Capabilities”.
    Select “Manage Integrations with Trading Partners”.
    TPM activation reply on MQ service, so before activate the TPM, MQ should be activated first.
    Activate TPM capability
    You can access TPM capability from Integration Suite UI
