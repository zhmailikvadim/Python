Symptom

I would like to understand what is the impact and what are the actions I need to take if I change the P user email in Managed Gateway for Spend&Network (ISMG).

Resolution

There is no impact from changing the e-mail of the P User in ISMG. The password is not affected. Also, transactions are not impacted. 

You only need to ensure the P User's e-mail belongs to an active employee who is familiar with the ISMG integration scenario and can either act as a point of contact or can reach the appropriate points of contact as needed, if there is an issue with the ISMG integration.

The only action to be taken is to confirm the e-mail as explained in KBA KB1345969 - What actions do I need to take if I change the P user email in Managed Gateway for Spend&Network?
