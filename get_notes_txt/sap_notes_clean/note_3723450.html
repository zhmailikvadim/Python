Symptom

The National Tax Service (NTS) has announced two tax legal changes effective in 2026 that will impact the simple tax table and non-taxable overtime allowance.

Here are the details:

  • As of March 1, 2026, the amount of tax credit for children between the ages of 8 and 20 is calculated as follows:
    • Tax credit for one child: KRW 20,830
    • Tax credit for two children: KRW 45,830
    • Tax credit for more than two children: KRW 45,830 + KRW 33,330 x (Number of Children - 2)
  • As of January 1, 2026, the criteria for non-taxable overtime income are revised. An employee is eligible for non-taxable overtime if the following conditions are met:
    • Monthly Income does not exceed KRW 2,600,000.
    • Previous Year Income does not exceed KRW 37,000,000.

Other Terms

STT, T7KRSS

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a legal change.

Solution

You can choose one of the following solutions:

  • Apply relevant HR support package.
  • Apply relevant correction instructions with manual activities.
