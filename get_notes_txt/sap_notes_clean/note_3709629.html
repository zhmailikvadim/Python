Symptom

You reprocess postponed Revenue Accounting Items (RAIs). An error is raised during reprocessing and the exception CX_FARR_ASSERT is raised. The RAIs are processed, the entry in FARR_D_MAPPING is created. But no POBs and no RAR contracts are created in RAR engine tables (in the tables FARR_D_CONTRACT or FARR_D_POB).

Other Terms

FARR, Revenue accounting, CX_FARR_ASSERT, dump, UNCAUGHT_EXCEPTION FARR_REPROCESS_POSTPONED_RAI, SDOI, CAOI

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

The exception CX_FARR_ASSERT was not handled properly. The raising of the exception could cause the discrepancies between data on RAR engine side and RAR Inbound processing.

Only optimized inbound processing is affected.

Solution

Implement the SAP Note or the corresponding Support package.
