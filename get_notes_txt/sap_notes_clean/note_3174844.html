Symptom

How do I create a Draft Invoice in SAP Business Network?

How do I save a draft of my invoice to work on later?

Resolution

  1. Sign in to your Business Network Supplier Account 
    • Click Orders > Purchase Orders (For suppliers without SAP Business Network for Supply Chain)
    • Click Orders > Orders and Releases (For suppliers with SAP Business Network for Supply Chain)
  2. Search and Click [Purchase order] 
  3. Click Create Invoice > Save
    • The message Invoice [invoice number] is saved. The saved invoice will be kept until dd/mm/yy is displayed.
  4. Click Exit.
  5. Click Save

See Also

  • You can submit the draft invoice or edit it again within the next 50 days.
  • If you edit it again, the draft is saved for 50 days from its latest edit date, rather than from the original edit date.
  • You can edit a draft invoice more than once.
  • If the invoice is not submitted or edited within 50 days from the last edit date, the draft invoice is automatically deleted.
  • The draft invoice is only accessible within the user account that created it. You cannot view or modify draft invoice created by other users. 

You may refer Creating a Draft Invoice for more information

If you choose to upgrade to an Enterprise account, you gain the benefit of seeing the full list of invoice drafts in your Invoices > Drafts tab.
