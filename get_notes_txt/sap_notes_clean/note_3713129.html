Symptom

In SOA service ExportDeclarationSUITERequest_Out, in the Sales Order reference, there is no distinction between a Sales Order (BusinessTransactionDocumentTypeCode 114) and a Sales Scheduling Agreement (BusinessTransactionDocumentTypeCode 734).

Other Terms

ExportDeclarationSUITERequest_Out, SalesOrderReference, 114, 734, BusinessTransactionDocumentTypeCode

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or apply the corresponding support package.

To enable your GTS system, implement in addition note 3712968.

When implementing the correction instructions, you might need in addition the related XI CONTENT patches:

  • XICNTS4CORE3713129 for SWCV XI CONTENT S4CORE 109
  • XICNTS4CORE3713129 for SWCV XI CONTENT S4CORE 108
