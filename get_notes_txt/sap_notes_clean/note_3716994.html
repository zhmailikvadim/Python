Symptom

  • Reverse after Putaway Warehouse Monitor function does not work in a system copy.
  • Reverse after Putaway Warehouse Monitor function does not work in a development system environment.
  • Message raised: Can't reverse goods receipt of stocks for party entitled to dispose &1. /SCWM/GM410.

Environment

SAP Extended Warehouse Management with SAP S/4HANA version 2025 and up.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to /SCWM/MON
  2. Stock and Bin > Physical Stock
  3. Select stock, then use Monitor Method: "Reverse after Putaway"

Cause

  • Business system names are not maintained properly after system/client copy.
  • Business system names are not maintained properly.
  • The message is raised because there is a difference in BSKEY maintained in /SCMB/TOWNBS and /SCMB/TBUSSYS tables and system is unable to fetch the Control Parameters for ERP Version Control structure.
    • Examples:
      • /SCMB/TOWNBS = S4D_001
      • /SCMB/TBUSSYS = S4DCLNT001

Resolution

Adjust Business System names in Customizing:

  • /SCMB/TOWNBS: SAP Customizing Implementation Guide > SCM Extended Warehouse Management > Extended Warehouse Management > Interfaces > ERP Integration > General Settings > Define Own Business System
  • /SCMB/TBUSSYS: SAP Customizing Implementation Guide > SCM Extended Warehouse Management > Extended Warehouse Management > Interfaces > ERP Integration > General Settings > Define Business System

See Also

  • General information:
    • System is designed to have the 1 entry that is maintained in /SCMB/TOWNBS also maintained in /SCMB/TBUSSYS. In other words: The 1 entry that is maintained in /SCMB/TOWNBS must be also maintained in /SCMB/TBUSSYS.
      • In case the system is configured with S4D_001 in /SCMB/TOWNBS, the same S4D_001 entry should exists in /SCMB/TBUSSYS.
      • Or if S4DCLNT001 is maintained in TBUSSYS, use the same format in TOWNBS.
