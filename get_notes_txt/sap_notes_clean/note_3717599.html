Symptom

Purchase Orders created with BAPI_PO_CREATE1 or changed with BAPI_PO_CHANGE may present pricing inconsistencies. The following issues may occur:

  • Incorrect Net Price at item level
  • Unexpected condition added to PO item
  • Expected condition not added
  • Incorrect condition value
  • Duplicate condition lines
  • Pricing differs from a PO created in ME21N
  • Pricing changes after redetermination in ME22N
  • PO creation fails with error message: E 06 218 - Net price must be greater than 0

Environment

  • Materials Management (MM)
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a Purchase Order using BAPI_PO_CREATE1.
  2. Display the document in ME23N.
  3. Observe incorrect pricing or condition behavior.

The issue does not occur when the Purchase Order is created manually using ME21N.

Cause

Incorrect or incomplete pricing-related parameters are passed to the BAPI. Purchasing BAPIs contain pricing control fields that are not available in Enjoy transactions (ME21N/ME22N).

If these fields are not properly controlled, the system may:

  • Redetermine pricing automatically
  • Copy conditions from the last PO
  • Apply manual and automatic conditions simultaneously
  • Delete or duplicate conditions

Resolution

Recheck and review the following BAPI fields when creating or changing Purchase Orders:

1. NO_PRICE_FROM_PO

Controls whether pricing conditions are copied from the last Purchase Order referenced in the Purchasing Info Record (if applicable). Use this flag if you want more control over pricing via BAPI input.

  • ' ' (blank): Price and conditions may be copied from Last Document, if it exists.
  • 'X': No conditions are copied. Pricing determination according to the pricing procedure is still executed.

 

2. POITEM-PO_PRICE

This field controls how Net price (POITEM-NET_PRICE) is processed. 

  • ' ' (blank): Conditions may be determined automatically, ignoring the value from NET_PRICE.
  • '1' – As Gross Price: NET_PRICE is adopted as gross price into the base price condition (e.g. PB00 or PBXX). Other condition types remain unchanged, and no conditions are copied from Last Document.
  • '2' – As Net Price: NET_PRICE is adopted as net price into the base price condition (e.g. PB00 or PBXX). All other condition types are removed during pricing processing.

 

3. POITEM-CALCTYPE

Defines the pricing type at item level. This behaves similarly to the Update button on the Conditions tab in ME21N, controlling how pricing is redetermined.

 

4. POCOND-CHANGE_ID

Defines if a condition line is to be Inserted, Updated or Deleted.

During PO creation, automatic pricing determination is executed before manually passed conditions are processed. Duplicate conditions typically occur when an Inserted condition is also determined automatically or copied from Last PO.

When creating a PO item, these actions apply on top of the automatically derived conditions and may update or delete them. When changing it, these apply to already existing conditions.

 

5. POCONDHEADER-CHANGE_ID

Same logic as POCOND-CHANGE_ID, but for header conditions.

 

Important: Ensure that corresponding fields in POITEMX, POCONDX and POCONDHEADERX are filled correctly. Missing or inconsistent flags may result in pricing being redetermined or condition changes not being applied.

 

You can find detailed instructions on how to use the BAPI pricing fields in these notes:

  • SAP Note 580225 Purchasing BAPIs: Conditions and pricing
  • SAP Note 571860 BAPI_PO_CREATE1: Price is not transferred

Specifically for error 06 218 - "Net price must be greater than 0"

1) Verify the pricing fields as described above.

  • Avoid copying pricing from the last Purchase Order if it may override the intended price determination. 
  • POITEM-PO_PRICE must be consistent with the pricing and conditions passed to the BAPI. If necessary, test alternative values.
  • Ensure that conditions passed in POCOND do not remove or overwrite the base price condition. CHANGE_ID values "Insert" or "Update" may produce different results.
  • Ensure that the pricing type selected in POITEM-CALCTYPE does not trigger full redetermination that may remove manually passed conditions.

2) Implement SAP Note 3489762 if applicable to your system release.
