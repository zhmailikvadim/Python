Symptom

Component 'FPM_CHART_UIBB' is deprecated resulting in no display of Pie charts in 'Issue and Changes' and Risk Management application.

Other Terms

FPM_CHART_UIBB, FPM_VIZFRAME_UIBB, Pie Chart, Issue and Change Request, Risk Management.

Reason and Prerequisites

Component FPM_CHART_UIBB is deprecated.

Solution

Implement the SAP Note. Also, Implement the below Prerequisite SAP Notes:

  • 3711228 - VizFrame UIBB: Graphic is getting black if property "plotArea.colorPalette" is being set
  • 3711670 - VizFrame UIBB: Single and Exclusive Selection Mode do not work as expected
