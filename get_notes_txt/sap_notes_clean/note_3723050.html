Symptom

This SAP Note contains enhancements for invoice printing for XML Version 5.0 that resulted from the first practical applications.

SAP Note 3691318 added that the XML elements xtra_service:token and value are filled with ICD10  and  Capitulum for outpatient flat rates and TARDOC services.
The output of this data is currently missing from the invoice form.

The guidelines for the invoice are created by the Forum Data Exchange.

Other Terms

MEDIDATA 4.0, XML standard 5.0, INXML, electronic invoice, EOXML, electronic invoice for external order, invoice printing

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisites:

  • Country Version Switzerland (CH) 
  • Use of XML standard 5.0 (for INXML or EOXML or invoice printing)  
  • SAP Notes 3583411 and 3678928 or the relevant patch level and follow-on notes for this topic
  • SAP Note 3691318

Reason:

Adjustments to the new Version 5.0 for invoice printing

Solution

Note the manual activities.

The information regarding ICD10 and Capitulum are printed at item level after the manual activities have been implemented.
