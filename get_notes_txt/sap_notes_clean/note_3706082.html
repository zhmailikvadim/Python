Symptom

The Financial Data Quality (FDQ) App currently available via SAP for Me will be sunset by December 31, 2025.

Customers using the FDQ App for monitoring the data quality of their ECC systems in the FI-GL, AA, ML modules should prepare for the transition.

Other Terms

FDQ app, finance data quality

Reason and Prerequisites

SAP is extending its strategy around data quality to align with the SAP Business Data Cloud prerequisites, as well as with the clean core data principles and the RISE with SAP Methodology.

Solution

The data previously accessible via the FDQ App can also be found in the FDQ chapter of the SAP Readiness Check, as well as in the FDQ chapter of the EarlyWatch Alert.

New checks and KPIs on the financial data quality in SAP S/4HANA systems are planned to be available in the RISE with SAP Methodology Dashboard (accessible via SAP Cloud ALM).
Timelines to be published once available.
