Symptom

  • The Fiori WebGUI/Web Dynpro app is failing with an “unexpectedly closed the connection” error.
  • The Fiori WebGUI/Web Dynpro app is failing with an “<HOST NAME>unexpectedly closed the connection” error.

Environment

  • SAP NetWeaver 7.5 and higher
  • SAP S/4HANA 1709 and higher 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login to the Fiori Launchpad and search for any WEBGUI/WEB DYNPRO app
  2. Click on the app

Cause

The Port number is missing in the HTTPS connection. 

Resolution

  1. Log in to the SAP GUI and execute transaction SM59.
  2. Expand HTTP Connections to ABAP System and double-click on the HTTPS.
  3. Click Edit, then enter port number <HTTPS PORT NUMBER> in the Port field.
  4. Click Save.
