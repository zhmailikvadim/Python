Symptom

Error message "Duplicate resource" is raised when attempting to display line-item exposure data in Fiori App "Display Credit Exposure" (F4826).

Environment

  • SAP Credit Management (FSCM)
  • Fiori-UI for Credit Management
  • SAP S/4HANA 2020 and higher
  • SAP Fiori

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Display Credit Exposure Fiori app.
  2. Attempt to display line-item exposure data for a business partner.
  3. Observe the error message "Duplicate resource".

Cause

Resolution

Implement SAP Note 3321180 - FIORI Display Credit Exposure: Dump when navigating to items (double entries) .

Keywords

SAP Credit Management, FSCM Credit Management, Fiori App, duplicate resource, display credit exposure, line item, business partner, 3321180
