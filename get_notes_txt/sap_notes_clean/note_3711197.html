Symptom

At 'Admin' under 'Messages' > 'Email' > 'Email Messages' Email notifications fail to send and display the error “Failed to send email due to email configuration validation error”. 

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

SAP Field Service Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in Admin 
  2. Navigate on Company Level to 'Messages'
  3. Enter 'Emails' and then 'Email Messages'
  4. At 'Subject' filter for 'failed'
    Actual behavior
  5. Emails fail to send and show the following error: Failed to send email due to email configuration validation error
    Expected behavior:
  6. Email notifications should be sent successfully without error message

Cause

Email settings were turned off at the company, so all emails failed, and no automatic alert exists to flagged the issue.

Resolution

A Business Rule is needed to provide proactive monitoring of email delivery with the following capabilities:

  • Real-time monitoring of outbound email processing
  • Immediate notification on any email delivery failure or when email settings are disabled
  • Automatic alerting of the account owner
  • Optional configuration of additional recipients

This ensures administrators are notified instantly if email functionality is disabled or if a send attempt fails for any reason.
