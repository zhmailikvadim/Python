Symptom

  • When adding a Sales Team Member to an Opportunity, the role Sales Employee is determined, despite being made inactive in the Opportunity Party Schema
  • An error message is also displayed: "Invalid participant role or invalid participant role type."

Environment

SAP Sales Cloud Version 2

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open any Opportunity.
  2. Click Search and Add and choose any employee
  3. Role 46 - Sales Employee is automatically determined, and an error is thrown.

Cause

During the "search and add" process, the role is defaulted to the standard role "46 - Sales Employee," which has been disabled in the Opportunity Party Schema.

Resolution

Use the "Add" option instead of "Search & Add" to manually select the correct role and team member.

Alternatively, switch the role after using "Search & Add" and ignore the error message.

Keywords
