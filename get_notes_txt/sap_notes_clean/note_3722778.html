Symptom

  • The lifeCycleStatus of an employee remains ACTIVE.
  • This occurs even after the Valid To date has expired.
  • The Status field cannot be modified using standard methods.  

Environment

  • SAP Sales Cloud and SAP Service Cloud Version 2
  • SAP Sales Cloud  Version 2
  • SAP Service Cloud Version 2

Reproducing the Issue

1. Click on the profile icon.  
2. Navigate to Settings and select All Settings.  
3. Open the Employee section.  
4. Observe that employees with a "Valid To" date in the past still display a status of "Active."

Cause

For Employees, the lifeCycleStatus attribute serves a different purpose compared to other business partner objects (such as Accounts or Contacts).

As per the system design behavior:

  • All employees are marked as ACTIVE by default.
  • The lifeCycleStatus changes to DELETED only when the employee’s DPP (Data Protection & Privacy) is removed.
  • The primary control for employee usability is the Valid To date—not the Status field.
  • The system does not derive or update the Status based on the validity period.

Resolution

  1. This behavior is working as designed.
  2. Upon expiration of the Valid To date, the employee becomes invalid for business usage or logon.  
  3. The lifeCycleStatus remains ACTIVE.  
  4. There is no standard functionality to automatically update the lifeCycleStatus based on the validity period.
  5. This behavior is consistent with Version 1
