Symptom

During billing creation for Poland, VBRK-GLO_LOG_REF1_HD is set to specific format depending on different business indicators set in  the Poland tab in sales order and later in billing document, if activated for Poland.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA private cloud
  • SAP ERP

Reproducing the Issue

  1. In a sales order for a Poland-registered company, activate the Poland tab in the header; if no set, this updates VBAK-GLO_LOG_REF1_HD with zeros only.
  2. Create the billing document from this sales order.
  3. Observe that VBRK-GLO_LOG_REF1_HD in the billing document contains zeros.

Note: the business process indicators should be set only if this is relevant for business process according to legal requirements.

Cause

For Poland-registered companies, activating the Poland tab sets VBAK-GLO_LOG_REF1_HD to zeros; during billing, VBRK-GLO_LOG_REF1_HD is updated with zeros

Resolution

The system behavior for Poland-registered companies is as follows:

  • For sales orders where JPKVAT is applicable, the "Poland" tab is activated. 

For Poland the additional Poland tab should be activated to cover additional Business Indicators required by law for e-invoicing, SAF-T and some other processes depending on business scenario.

The system then populates the header field VBAK-GLO_LOG_REF1_HD with zeros respective values.

  • For sales orders/billing documents where the "Poland" tab is not activated, the VBAK-GLO_LOG_REF1_HD field remains blank.

The same is explained in the MPP in Sales and Billing | SAP Help Portal.
