Symptom

The "Last Visited Date" field in the Account Visiting Information tab is not updated despite the visit being marked as completed.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Cloud Version 2

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to Accounts work center.
  2. Open an Account.
  3. Navigate to Activity History and check the Visit; confirm it is completed.
  4. Go to Visiting Information.
  5. Observe that the "Last Visited Date" field in the Visit Plan is not populated.

Cause

The Sales Area maintained in the Visit does not exactly match the Sales Area maintained in the Visit Plan.

Resolution

    In order to solve this, ensure both Sales Areas are aligned. Once the alignment is complete, the "Last Visited Date" field will be populated correctly.
