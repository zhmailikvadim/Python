Symptom

In the processes "Start of Supply for Electricity (DSO View)" (DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SOS_REG) and "Start of Basic or Backup Supply for Electricity (DSO View)" (DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SBS), the system doesn't include a one-hour wait step before continuing the process.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Wait step 0605 added in process Trigger of Master Data Changes (DE_B_GPKE_TRIG_MDC_ASSGN_DSO) which is embeded process of DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SOS_REG & 
DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SBS .

Other Terms

GPKE, DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SOS_REG, DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SBS, DE_B_GPKE_TRIG_MDC_ASSGN_DSO,Trigger of Master Data Changes, wait deadline, start of supply, basic supply, backup supply, electricity

Reason and Prerequisites

 The error occurred because the processes were missing a one-hour wait deadline step.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system includes a one-hour wait deadline step in the processes. Step 3000-605 is added to process DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SOS_REG, and step 2000-605 is added to process DE_B_GPKE_DSO_SBS. Import the BC set /US4G/3_0_SUP_SP07_NOTE_3706468 to apply these changes. You can verify the fix by retesting the scenario and confirming that the one-hour wait step runs successfully in both processes.
