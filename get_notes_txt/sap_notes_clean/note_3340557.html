Symptom

The substitute item that was posted by means of a clearing reset to a payment via payment lot in the grid operator's contract account in the supplier system can no longer be cleared with other items in this contract account using transaction EACCMAIN .

Other Terms

FP07, EK05, payment request, payment lot, deregulation, incoming invoice, clearing reset, EACCMAIN

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason:

The substitute item was created due to a clearing reset and does not have the clearing restriction 'G' (Budget Billing as Down Payment on Vendor Side (Deregulation))

Prerequisite:

The payment via payment lot was incorrectly posted to the contract account of the grid operator in the supplier system and has a statistical budget billing request or payment request cleared (internal main transaction 4550).

Solution

For an advance correction, you can implement the attached correction instructions. Otherwise, import the next support package.
