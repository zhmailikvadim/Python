Symptom

  • When viewing the Characteristics tab for a child SOW, some line items are highlighted yellow
  • Why are some characteristics highlighted versus others that are not highlighted

Resolution

When viewing a Child SOW's characteristics page, if any line item is different than that of the Master SOW the item will be highlighted

The highlighting let's users know that the SOW characteristic is modified as compared to the Master SOW

An example would be a Fee Definition with a modified rate on a Child SOW that is different than the fee definition as defined on the corresponding Master SOW
