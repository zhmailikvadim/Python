Symptom

In /PRA/RP_WORKPLACE transaction code, for GLO2 Royalty Report, under Report Details tab, you are trying to figure it out how "Formation Production Volume" and "3B Plant Shrink" volume are being calculated.

Environment

SAP ERP 6.0

Resolution

The formula calculation used to determine Formation Production Volume and 3B Plant Shrink volumes are:

Formation Production Volume = VT 91 − non-formation volumes + Volume Type 36.

3B Plant Shrink = Formation Production Volume − all other disposition volumes − non-formation production volume.

Keywords

/PRA/RP_WORKPLACE, GLO2 Royalty Report, "Formation Production Volume", "3B Plant Shrink".
