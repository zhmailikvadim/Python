Symptom

This SAP Note is relevant to Brazil.

ATU and STU status is not getting updated correctly after invoice posting for subsequent settlement.

Steps:

1.Create a provisional settlement.
2.Release , approve and do the invoice posting.
3.Create final settlement and do the invoice posting.
4.Create reversal settlement for the final settlement.
5.Recreate the final settlement and do the invoice posting.

Other Terms

ACM; Agricultural Contract Management; Brazil Localization; Subsequent Settlement; ATU & STU Status Update

Reason and Prerequisites

ATU & STU status update logic is not correct.

Solution

Import the support package or implement the correction instructions.
