Symptom

When you open any service contract either the cost or revenue or service contract are displayed incorrect.

Other Terms

CRM-S4-ANA-BI , Service contract

Reason and Prerequisites

In the table IFIGLACCTLITST , the entries are not filled properly .

Solution

The service contracts derive the data from the table IFIGLACCTLITST.

In this table for any corresponding entry these criterias should be met
1. The orderID is not initial
2. Account assignment Type should be either 'EO' or 'OR'
3. AccountAssignment and Ledger are not initial
4. GLAccountHirearchy has to be filled .
5. SemanticTag should be 'REC_MARGIN' to display profit margins or It should be 'RECO_COS' for costs or it should be RECO_REV for revenue .

If these critera are met , the service contract will automatically display the entries for the respective fields.
