Symptom

Some invoices that are expected to appear during the consolidation process are missing from the list of invoices available for consolidation

Cause

Invoices associated with a closed Site do not appear in the consolidation list and cannot be included in the consolidation process. 

Resolution

There are two ways to resolve this issue:
  1. Update the Work Order: Revise the Work Order to associate it with a different active Site. Then reject and remove the related invoice so that existing document can be resubmitted with the active Site
  2. Reopen the Site: Use the Site Upload connector to reopen the closed Site, making the associated invoices eligible for consolidation.
