Symptom

In this SAP Note, the form from SAP Note 2844838 ﻿is adjusted. The form text in line 15 of the employment tax statement (ETStmt) for 2020 changes from:

Reduced hours compensation, allowance for maternity pay, compensation for loss of earnings (German Infection Protection Act), supplementary amount, and semiretirement allowance

to

(seasonal) reduced hours compensation, allowance for maternity pay, compensation for loss of earnings (German Infection Protection Act), supplementary amount, and semiretirement allowance.

Other Terms

 ETStmt form

Reason and Prerequisites

 Error in form 2020.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified Support Package.
