Symptom

 The Custom Reportable Field Mapping feature display slow to unresponsive performance when mapping fields with high-value of available field. 

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the Manage Reportable Custom Field.
  2. Select the target template.
  3. Choose which field tab for mapping of field. 
  4. Click on the drop down menu per category where high volume of data is present and available 
  5. Notice that after each selection, the page free and sometime unresponsive.

Resolution

The issue has been reported to our Engineering team and is under investigation.
