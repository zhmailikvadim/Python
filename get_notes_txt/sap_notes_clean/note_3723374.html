Symptom

What statuses of receipt are included in the scheduled task ProcessAutoReceipts?

Resolution

Receipts with statuses of Submitted and Composing will be picked up by this task.

The ProcessAutoReceipts task runs nightly to transition any pending receipts for auto-receiving from Submitted or Composing to Approved status after the lead time has passed.

See Also

The scheduled task ProcessAutoReceipts runs at 12 AM PDT everyday. This run cannot be changed as it is a global task.
