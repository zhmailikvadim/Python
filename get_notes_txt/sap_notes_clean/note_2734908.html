Symptom

With SAP Note 2412892 the selection of payment items and payment orders were improved. One improvement of this correction was related to resolving the external order reference to PE-internal business key in method MAP_ORDER_KEY from class /PE1/CL_IPM_P_XML_ORDER_SRV. This method checks if the clearing area from the retrieved business key is the same as the clearing area of the process. This check avoids the data selection from other clearing areas which leads to wrong results. This check is working correctly but there are two callers of this method which do not provide the clearing area of the process.

Other Terms

search of original item; search of original payment order

Solution

Implement the provided SAP Note or the corresponding Support Package.
