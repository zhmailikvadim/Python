Symptom

The borrower change process only allows a date in the past for the borrower change via FIN.

However, the date is transferred at the interface and is not checked using the process. The behavior of the system is inconsistent.

Reason and Prerequisites

The check for whether the date of the borrower change is in the past is called only in the dialog, not in the interface.

Solution

The system behavior has been standardized.
A further check for the date has been added to also check the data during data receipt in the interface.

At the same time, you can use dependency injection to enhance or deactivate the checks in the customer room without modifications.
