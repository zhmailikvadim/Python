Symptom

The database tables in Material Ledger are growing up to an extent where they have a negative impact on your daily processes in terms of performance or usage of database ressources.

Other Terms

Archiving; Partitioning; FCML4H_DOC; CO_ML_DAT; CO_ML_SPL; CO_ML_BEL.

Reason and Prerequisites

You need more information on how partitioning or archiving can help you to handle high data volume on Material Ledger database tables.

Solution

In general, archiving helps you to control the amount of data which is kept in your productive system. It is strongly recommended to do comprehensive archiving on a regular basis. OSS note 744085 gives you the necessary recommendations and explanations for archiving Material Ledger data.

In a HANA DB system, partitioning of large tables is a must as the size of not partitioned tables is limited to 2 billion records due to technical reasons. In Non-HANA DB systems, partitioning is optional. With the respective partitioning strategy, the access to your data that is spread over the partitions can get faster. Hence, partitioning helps you to handle the (large) number of (required) database entries in a database table.

OSS note 2044468 explains the various partitioning strategies. It is highly recommended to well decide upon the partitioning strategy as repartitioning comes along with high effort and should therefore be avoided. Please check OSS note 3139140 for detailed information on partitioning MLDOC and MLDOCCCS.
