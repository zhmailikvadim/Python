Symptom

After receiving a REQOTE message, the system checks only whether an agreement exists but does not validate the contract account or recipient type. Then, in the process "Request for Quotation (MOS View)" (DE_C_MOSB_REQT_MOS), step 0110 does not trigger a rejection when the service provider agreement (/US4G/CTR) lacks a contract account or when the recipient type is customer.

This issue is relevant for the market roles: MOS, SUP.

Other Terms

REQOTE, service provider agreement, contract account, recipient type, /US4G/CL_UBD_MOSB_CTR, QUERY_MOS, QUERY_SUP, MOSB

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error. The system checked only for the existence of an agreement after receiving a REQOTE message but did not perform additional validation for the contract account or recipient type.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system determines that no valid agreement exists between MOS and supplier when the service provider agreement (/US4G/CTR) has no contract account or when the recipient type is customer. The system then triggers a rejection in the "Request for Quotation (MOS View)" process on the market communication side.
You can verify the fix by checking that the system triggers a rejection on the market communication side when the service provider agreement (/US4G/CTR) has no contract account or when the recipient type is customer.
