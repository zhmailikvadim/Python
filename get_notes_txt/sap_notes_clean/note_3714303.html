Symptom

  • Missing extracts in payout run observed under the "Extracts" tab.
  • Extract files do not appear even after finalizing the payout in the test environment.
  • Extract files were previously accessible under the "Artifacts" section of the Summary screen in prior releases.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
  • Agent Performance Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Run a payout in the test environment.
  2. Open the payout and navigate to the "Extracts" tab.
  3. Observe that no extracts are displayed under the tab.

Cause

  • The Payout had no extracts generated, resulting in no results displayed under the "Extracts" tab.
  • The task responsible for generating extracts was skipped due to the payout being executed in a test system.

Resolution

The absence of extracts is an expected behavior due to the skipped task in the test system.
