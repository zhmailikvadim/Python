Symptom

  • Unable to consolidate Business Partner Master records into a golden Business Partner master record using the "Manage Migration Business Partner Multiple Addresses V2" app.
  • The progress is showing as 0% 

Environment

S/4 HANA Public Cloud Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open "Manage Migration Business Partner Multiple Addresses V2" app & Create.
  2. Enter description & Start.
  3. Records found with progress 0%.

Cause

Missing activation of the feature MDC_BP_CLOUD_READY.

Resolution

  1. Open the Manage Your Solution APP
  2. Open the SSCUI 106573 - Activate Features & configure
  3. Active the feature MDC_BP_CLOUD_READY

Keywords

business partner, golden bp master, non-golden bp master, manage migration business partner multiple addresses v2, consolidation issue, MDC_BP_CLOUD_READY, SAP S/4HANA Cloud
