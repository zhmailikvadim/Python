Symptom

You have configured your EWM system to communicate all serial numbers corresponding to the delivery (Confirmation Type of SNs) - can be found in SPRO "Set Control Parameters for ERP Version Control". When you process an outbound delivery the invoice before goods issue message is sent without any serial number information.

Other Terms

Confirmation Type of Serial Numbers, IBGI, "Communicate All SNs Corresponding to Delivery", /SCWM/TCTRLPROCS-SERIAL_NUMBERS

Reason and Prerequisites

This issue is caused by program error.

Solution

Implement the relevant support pack or the attached corrections.
