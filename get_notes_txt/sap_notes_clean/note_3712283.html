Symptom

Recruiting Email Triggers Page
On the Recruiting Email Triggers page, the table header text is displayed in black against a dark blue background. This results in insufficient contrast and poor readability of the table header.

Manage Recruiting Groups Page
A similar issue is observed on the Manage Recruiting Groups page. When editing a Recruiting Group and attempting to add a user, the table header text is also rendered in black on a dark background, making it difficult to read.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Cause

It is a known error. 

Resolution

  • This issue will be corrected in the 1H2026 Release.
  • Release dates can be found on  SAP SuccessFactors Product Release & Road Map Information
