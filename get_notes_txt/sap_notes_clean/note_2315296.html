Symptom

Other Terms

GRC AC 10.0 , GRC AC 10.1, Valid from , Valid To , Provision , Blank

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing functionality for the web service GRAC_USER_ACCESS_WS

Solution

Implement the attached correction instruction
