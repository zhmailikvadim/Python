Symptom

  • After migrating reports publication starts to fail with:
    ERROR[PublishingService:RunInstancePool-XXX] BusinessObjects_publicationAdminErrorLog-Instance_XXXXXXX - [Publication ID # XXXXXXX] - An SDKException was caught. Is your CMS running and configured properly? (FBE60003)

  • Right click on any publication > go to Formats > you get error java.lang.UnsupportedOperationException

       

  • Right click on publications > to go prompts settings >you get the error "Internal error occurred: while trying to invoke the method
    com.businessobjects.clientaction.shared.promptsSummary.SharedPromptsBean.toString() of a null object loaded from a local variable at slot 30. Contact your administrator."

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform 4.x
  • All Operating Systems

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Migrate reports from another environment.
  2. Check publication starts to fail with An SDKException was caught. Is your CMS running and configured properly? (FBE60003)"
  3. Right click on publications > Go to prompts settings >get the error "Internal error occurred: while trying to invoke the method
    com.businessobjects.clientaction.shared.promptsSummary.SharedPromptsBean.toString() of a null object loaded from a local variable at slot 30. Contact your administrator."
  4. Right click on any publication > Go to Formats > get error java.lang.UnsupportedOperationException"

Cause

  • Due to corruption of the migrated reports from lower environments

Resolution

  • Re-migrate the reports again from the source system to target system again.

Keywords

(FBE60003), java.lang.UnsupportedOperationException, publication, fail.
