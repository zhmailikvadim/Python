Symptom

Error message: "The link does not exist" when attempting to open short links (e.g. https://w.ems.to/123ABC)in SMS messages.

Environment

SAP Emarsys Customer Engagement

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Send SMS messages containing shortened URLs.
  2. Wait for a few days then attempt to open the links within the SMS messages.
  3. Observe the error message: "The link does not exist".

Cause

  • Shortened URLs in SMS messages have a time limit for validity.
  • For test SMS messages, shortened URLs are valid for 1 day.
  • For real SMS campaigns, shortened URLs are valid for 30 days.

Resolution

Please launch real SMS campaigns then retest the system behavior.
