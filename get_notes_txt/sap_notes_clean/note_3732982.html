Symptom

Logo URL for an image that is hosted in SAP's SFTP is too long and it doesn’t fit in full in Manage Recruiting Sites logo configuration, which is making the image not to work.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Resolution

Engineering is aware of the issue, and a code fix for this will be deployed on b2611 release.

The release dates are scheduled to be deployed on October 12th, 2026 for Preview, and on November 13th, 2026 for Production.

Keywords

host, image, url, recruiting, site, admin, sftp, logo.
