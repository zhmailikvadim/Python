Symptom

Badan Penyelenggara Jaminan Sosial (BPJS) Ketenagakerjaan issued an announcement letter to change pension benefits and stipulated that the maximum wage is the base for calculating the Jaminan Pensiun (JP) contribution in 2026 and change the maximum contribution amount to IDR 11,086,300.00.

This change is effective as of March 2026. The contributions of scheme "/JP" in V_T7ID13 is delimited with the new limit amount IDR 11,086,300.00 from March 1, 2026.

Other Terms

V_T7ID3, /JP, BPJS pension

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a Legal Change

Solution

To get this legal change, apply the HR support package specified in this note.

Alternatively, you can follow the attached manual instruction to change the table entry by yourself.

For more information about the development delivered with this SAP Note, see the entry for this SAP Note in the What's New topic on SAP Help Portal.
