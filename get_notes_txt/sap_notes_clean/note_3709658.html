Symptom

Restriction is required for Business Partners based on BP Grouping.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

Restricting Business Partner based on BP Grouping is a standard system limitation.

Resolution

  • Currently, restricting Business Partners based on BP Grouping is not supported in the system.
  • The feature request is available in the Customer Influence portal. Upvoting it can support its consideration for future enhancements -  Improvement Request Details - Customer Influence.

Keywords

bp supplier restriction, bp grouping, sap s/4hana cloud, public edition, standard system limitation, bp role type, supplier account group, feature request, customer influence portal, master data
