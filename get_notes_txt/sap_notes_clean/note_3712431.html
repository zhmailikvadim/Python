Symptom

You post a retirement with retirement category 1 or 4 for which the gain or loss is recognized as liability. After the SAP Note 3669069, the retirement posts the revaluation of ordinary depreciation successfully.

However, the respective value is not visible in AW01N Transactions table, and you are not allowed to assign the revaluation posted with transaction type 770 to any Asset History Sheet item.

Other Terms

Special retirement, revaluation of ordinary depreciation, transaction type 770

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or upgrade to the corresponding support pack.
