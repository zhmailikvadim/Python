Symptom

  • Changes were made in the Recruit-To-Hire Data Mapping tool configuration, and we want to know who did the change.
  • Recruit-To-Hire Data Mapping tool configuration disappeared, request to provide change audit logs.
  • Change Audit Report for Recruit-To-Hire Data Mapping tool

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Onboarding

Cause

Product Limitation:

  • Change Audit Logs for Recruit-To-Hire Data Mapping tool is currently not supported.

Resolution

  • Upon confirmation from the Product Team, we have verified that there is no change audit available for Recruit to hire data mapping page.
  • Additionally, the team has confirmed that backend logs do not currently capture changes made within the tool.
  • We understand that this limitation may not meet your expectations, and we sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may cause.
  • To address this, we recommend submitting an enhancement request. This will allow the team to evaluate the feasibility of including this functionality in future releases.
