Symptom

When processing an inbound delivery for a product managed with a Batch-Specific Unit of Measure (BSUOM), a runtime error (ABAP dump) might occur. This leads to a stuck inbound queue (type qRFC) in the SAP Extended Warehouse Management (EWM) system.

Other Terms

Catch-Weight, CW, BSUOM, embedded, decentral

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

 Implement correction instruction.
