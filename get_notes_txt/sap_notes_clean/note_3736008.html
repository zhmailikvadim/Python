Symptom

When approving PR in my inbox app, the following errors happen:
"Target was not found
failed to load
'sap/ui/s2p/mm/lib/reuse/text/component/Component.js' from
/sap/bc/ui5_ui5/sap/mm_reuse_texts1/..."
(The details can be vary depending on the inactive service)

Environment

  • Materials Management (MM)
  • SAP FIORI
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a PR subject to Flexible workflow or Classic workflow.
  2. Try to approve it in My Inbox app.

Cause

Necessary services are not active.

Resolution

Activate the service referring to the error message.

For example, "from /sap/bc/ui5_ui5/sap/mm_reuse_texts1/...", activate /sap/bc/ui5_ui5/sap/mm_reuse_texts1.
