Symptom

In SAP Transportation Management (SAP TM),  Carrier Invoices for orders containing charge lines with a resolution base cannot be processed via B2B in TM releases earlier than S/4Hana 2020.

Environment

  • SAP Transportation Management 9.3+
  • SAP Transportation Management embedded in S/4HANA 1709, 1809, 1909

Cause

SAP TM supports only a single occurrence of a condition type mapped to a TM charge type for carrier invoice creation via the B2B interface.

Resolution

Ensure that the Order does not contain any resolution base charge lines.

See Also

SAP Note 3475010 - Dump occurs during creation of Carrier Invoice via B2B.
