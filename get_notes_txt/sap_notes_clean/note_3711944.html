Symptom

What are the URLs for SIPM (Supplier Information and Performance Management) self-registration in various data centers? 

Resolution

Below are the self registration URLs (Uniform Resource Locators) based on the data center where your site is hosted. Replace [realmname] in the URLs below with your site's unique name, which appears in the web browser when you are logged into SAP Ariba.

US Data Center:

http://[realmname].supplier.ariba.com/register

EU Data Center:

http://[realmname].supplier-eu.ariba.com/register

See Also

For more information, please refer to: What are the URLs for SM self-registration in various data centers?
