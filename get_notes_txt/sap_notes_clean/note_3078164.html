Symptom

The execution of the domain API BankAccountContractLockRequest terminates with a mapping error for the GDT/SDT BankContractLockRequestUserStatusCode.

Other Terms

BACLR

Reason and Prerequisites

SAP Note 3073088 is implemented in your system.

Solution

Import the relevant Support Package for your release. You can also use transaction SNOTE to implement the correction instructions relevant for your release in advance. You do not have to perform any manual tasks.
