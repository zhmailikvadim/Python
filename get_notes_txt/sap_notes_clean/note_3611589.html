Symptom

You want to search for business partners using the identification number (search help: /PM0/ABB_BP_SEARCH_IDENT_ES).

To do this, you must select an identification category (search help: /PM0/ABB_BP_SEARCH_IDENTTYP_ES).

You can select only identification categories that can be assigned to a business partner from the "Person" business partner category.
