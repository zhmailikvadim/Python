Symptom

Document is displayed under the incorrect report period in the SST report despite belonging to another fiscal year.

Environment

  • SAP S/4 HANA
  • SAP S/4 HANA Finance

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Run DRC MY SST Report 
  2. Locate document.
  3. Verify the fiscal year of the document.
  4. Check it in the SST report and observe that the document appears under another period.

Cause

The document appears in MY SST report is based on the tax reporting date.

Resolution

  1. Confirm the tax reporting date of the document.
  2. Adjust the reporting configuration to align the document's fiscal year with the correct report period, if applicable.
  3. Validate the changes in the SST report to ensure the document is displayed under the correct period.

Keywords

incorrect report period, fiscal year mismatch, tax reporting date, SST report, document display issue, report configuration, tax reporting adjustment, fiscal year alignment, reporting error, document period error
