Symptom

You can delete items from the item list of a renegotiation, after it is initiated. The deletion takes place in the SAP Business Technology Platform system for renegotiation, the SAP Ariba Contract Price Renegotiation Add-on. You can view the updated item list in the Manage Renegotiations app in SAP S/4HANA capability for product sourcing.

Other Terms

S/4 HANA Product Sourcing, Manage Renegotiation, Contract Price Renegotiation, Remove Items in Renegotiation

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisite: S4CORE 109 - 0001(SAP S/4HANA 2025-FPS1)

Solution

Please implement this note to use this feature.
