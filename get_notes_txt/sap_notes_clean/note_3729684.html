Symptom

Error "Page not found" appears when a user opens any Solution Process Flow link from Transformation Planning > Process Flows.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Resolution

Normally, you get this page asking your agreement to join the public Signavio instance SAP Signavio Process Explorer. See below.

If you don't the above page when you click on the links in Solution Process Flow from Transformation Planning in SAP Signavio Process Insights, please go to this URL to do the agreement instead. It's also listed in Transformation Planning with Process Flows.
