Symptom

This new functionality allows to parallelize the documents update when activating or deactivating an account with FINS_SWITCH_TO_OPEN_ITEM, FINS_SWITCH_XLGCLR and FINS_SWITCH_XUJCLR. It allows to activate accounts with a large amount of already posted items faster and without the risk of memory shortage/dump.

Other Terms

Activation of opem item management, open item management by ledger group and extended open item management. FINS_ACTIVATE_OIM, FINS_ACTIVATE_XLGCLR, FINS_ACTIVATE_XUJCLR

Reason and Prerequisites

New functionality

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or upgrade to the latest support package.
