Symptom

Pricing conditions maintained in a Service Order item are not copied to the corresponding Confirmation Item when the confirmation is created via the FSM Cloud Connector. As a result, the pricing element assigned to the item is missing in the confirmation item.

Other Terms

FSM, E4C, Connector, Price Element, Price Condition, Service Item, Service Confirmation Item

Reason and Prerequisites

Due to a missing pricing condition transfer in the Connector logic, pricing elements assigned to Service Order items are not propagated to the corresponding Confirmation Items during confirmation creation. The problem is located in method DOC_FLOW (class /PACG/ECM_CL_A_I_LO_CONF).

Solution

Please, implement this correction instruction.
