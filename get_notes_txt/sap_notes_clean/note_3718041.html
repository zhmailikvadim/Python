Symptom

When creating or modifying a channel with inbound bindings, SM59 destinations are needed, as they define the channel's connection endpoints.

During creation of a channel or a topic consumer, no authority check for S_RFC_ADM (Activity=01) is performed for the SM59 destinations. The same applies to deletion.

Other Terms

SM59, Authorizations, S_RFC_ADM, Channel Creation, Topic Consumer

Reason and Prerequisites

The current admin user role template (SAP_IWXBE_RT_XBE_ADM) provided by us ensures the creation and modification of a channel, as well as all related tasks (e.g SM59 destination handling).
To increase the usability, the user has the ability to generate an SM59 destination without manual steps, for example, when creating a channel via service key.
Due to security concerns, we enabled the S_RFC_ADM (Activity=01 and Activity=06) checks during the creation / deletion of channels or the corresponding consumer destinations.
This allows for a more distinct separation between the process of creating a channel and managing the associated SM59 destinations.

Solution

Please apply the correction instructions and follow the manual steps.
