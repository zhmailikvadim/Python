Symptom

What are the differences between Contract Requests in Buyer and Sourcing?

How do we search for Contract Requests on the Buyer side?

Resolution

There are two different documents named Contract Request in SAP Ariba: one in Sourcing and one in Buyer. Contract Requests in Buyer is the document that when processed, generates a Contract to be used in Requisitions. They have some characteristics that distinguish them:

  • The ID usually starts with Prefix "CR". This can be altered.
  • In parent-child environments, they exist only in the child realms.
  • In a Contract Workspace, they are represented by the document named Contract Terms.

A direct way to identify them is by how it appears in search. To search, a user assigned to the Contract Manager group can follow these steps:

  1. Click on the magnifying glass icon at the top right of the screen > More...
  2. In the Contracts section, click on Contract Request
  3. Apply any search filters as needed and click Search

For comparison, Contract Requests in Sourcing appear under the SAP Ariba Contracts section, and they are named Contract Requests (Procurement) or Contract Requests (Sales).
