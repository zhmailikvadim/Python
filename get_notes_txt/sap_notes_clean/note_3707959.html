Symptom

Choosing view 'By Document' In F1662A Operational Supplier Evaluation still display the data by supplier instead of Purchase Order.

Environment

  • Materials Management (MM)
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Fiori 

Cause

Incorrect usage.

Resolution

Add column 'Purchasing Doc. Item' and 'Purchasing Document' in the data view settings.

Keywords

F1662A, Operational Supplier Evaluation, document, PO, Purchase Order, By Document, view, supplier
