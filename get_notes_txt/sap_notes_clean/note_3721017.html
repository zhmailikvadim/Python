Symptom

A list of compatible pre-recorded video interview platforms that enable direct integration with recruiting

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Search for pre-recorded video interview platforms that can be integrated with SuccessFactors Recruiting.
  2. Attempt to locate a predefined list of platforms or partners from SAP documentation or websites.

Cause

Currently, there is no standardized or recommended list of pre-recorded video interview platforms maintained by SAP for direct embedding or integration with SuccessFactors Recruiting.

Resolution

At this time, we do not provide a standardized or recommended list of pre-recorded video interview platforms for direct embedding or integration from our end. Integration capabilities and supported platforms may vary depending on your system configuration, existing architecture, and implementation approach.

It is recommend reaching out to your implementation partner to explore the available options. They will be best positioned to assess whether a specific video interview platform can be integrated with your recruiting system, and to advise on the technical feasibility, required configurations, potential custom development, or use of APIs and third-party connectors.
