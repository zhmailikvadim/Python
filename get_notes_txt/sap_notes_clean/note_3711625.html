Symptom

How to to configure additional recipients (CC / group mail IDs) for the sales document workflow in approval notification emails? These additional recipients do not participate in the workflow approval but can receive the emails.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

Resolution

  • Only the approver can receive the workflow approval email.
  • To have someone other than the approver receive the approval emails, submit this requirement as a feature request through the Customer Influence Portal under the session SAP S/4HANA Cloud for Sales.
