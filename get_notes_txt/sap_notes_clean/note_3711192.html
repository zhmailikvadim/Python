Symptom

When a carrier invoice is created with an attachment through InvoiceRequest_In, an exception (CX_ODATA_CV_BASE_EXCEPT) occurs due to the attachment.

Other Terms

ATCMT, Default, DEFLT_ATS, blank, alternate name, /BOBF/FRW, 032, Processing stopped.

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Please implement attached correction instructions or upgrade to relevant SP.
