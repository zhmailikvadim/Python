Symptom

Guidance is required to provision SAP Ariba Intake Management using SAP for Me.

Resolution

  1. Log in to SAP for Me with your S-User account.

  2. Go to Portfolio & Products → My Product Packages, and select SAP Ariba Suite.

  3. Open the Provisioning and Integration dashboard and select the Applications tab.

  4. Choose SAP Ariba Intake Management and select Start Provisioning – New Solution.

  5. Select the tenant type (Development, Test, or Production) and maintain the required basic details.

  6. Review the information and choose Finish to start provisioning.
