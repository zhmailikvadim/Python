Symptom

  • User unable to move or paste the crystal reports in Personal Folders.
  • Paste not Allowed

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects BI Platform 4.x 
  • All supported OS

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Administrators and users with Administrator rights are able to copy and paste into personal folders.
  2. Users without Administrator rights are able to copy but encounter the error “Paste Failed: Not Allowed” when attempting to paste into personal folders.

Cause

The issue was caused by the absence of the right "Download files associated with the object" in folder-level security settings for Crystal Reports in the Test and Production environments.

Resolution

  • Adding the right "Download files associated with the object" at the folder level:

    Under User Security → Advanced Rights → Content → Crystal Reports

Keywords

copy paste crystal reports, paste not allowed, BI 4.x, paste not allowed for crystal reports.
