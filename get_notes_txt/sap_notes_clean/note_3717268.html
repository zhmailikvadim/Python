Symptom

When the approvers come to the FC-Approval Hub application to approve any items from their list, The documents that were attached during the creation process were not shown while doing the action of approving and there was no check whether the uploaded documents were supporting the adjustment or not.

Other Terms

FCFPSL , FCA , Adjustment hub , My Worklist , Manual Posting , Business Transaction

Reason and Prerequisites

The system previously was not used to show the attachments which were uploaded during the process of adjustment creation either at individual level or group level and the approver could directly approve the adjustment without even opening the attachment.

There was no functionality or configuration support to:

- See the documents at approval process which were uploaded during the creation or Post creation.

- Make the attachments available at Individual Level & Group Level

- Make the attachments to be mandatorily opened by the approver before approving the item(s)

- Dynamically control field behavior

Solution

New enhancement delivered to make the attachments and notes available at both Individual & Group level and an extra check that the approval must open at least one attachment form the list in order to approve the item(s) If the required configurations are maintained.

Approver can see the Noted and attachments from the approval popup and should now open the attachment mandatorily.

This feature has been enabled for:

Worklist Approve Button

Bulk Details Page Approve Button (Partial Approval)
