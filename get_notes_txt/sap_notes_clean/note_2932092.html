Symptom

You are using method RESET_BUFFERED_DATA of class CL_MPE_ORDER_UPDATE_EXT to insert rotuings into shop floor orders. Even in case the order the order was already (partially) released the consistency between the inspection charateristics in the routing and inspection settings in the material master are checked. This check is not required and can have a negative impact on the performance.

Other Terms

 Inspection Type, MKAL

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Apply correction instruction.
