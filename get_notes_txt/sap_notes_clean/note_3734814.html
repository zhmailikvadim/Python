Symptom

For troubleshooting, it is necessary to check the metadata of an OData V4 service binding from ABAP Development tools.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

  • SAP BTP ABAP Environment
  • SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud
  • SAP S/4HANA 1909 and above

Resolution

In order to check the metadata, follow these steps:

1. Open the Service Binding in ADT

2. Click on the Service URL link

3. The URL will open in the browser. Replace the text behind the last / to $metadata. In this case, ?sap-client=100 to $metadata
