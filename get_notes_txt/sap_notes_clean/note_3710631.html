Symptom

After implementing referenced note 2907475 (HR -> BP: Tax number longer than 20 digits) if tax number is maintained in a different infotype than infotype 0002 and you trigger the employee to business partner synchronization with the report /SHCM/RH_SYNC_BUPA_EMPL_SINGLE or /SHCM/RH_SYNC_BUPA_FROM_EMPL, then the tax number will be stored in field 'TAXNUM' in table 'DFKKBPTAXNUM' if the tax number has a lengt lower equal to 20. But as you can see in referenced notes 2014715 (Tax Number field extension in Master Data to occupy large Tax Number) and 3643430 (Correction Program to Move Tax Number Data from TAXNUM to TAXNUMXL for Tax Categories ≥ 5) the tax numbers for tax categories 0 to 4 have to be stored in the field TAXNUM, whereas other tax categories (e.g. tax category 5 and above) have to be stored in the field TAXNUMXL.

Other Terms

 TAXNUMXL, TAXNUM, /SHCM/RH_SYNC_BUPA_EMPL_SINGLE; /SHCM/RH_SYNC_BUPA_FROM_EMPL;

Reason and Prerequisites

Programm error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified support package.
