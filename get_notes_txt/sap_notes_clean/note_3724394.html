Symptom

  • Managers are unable to view Pay Components (Hourly Salary) in the history view of Compensation Information, although individual pay components are visible in the View All section after changing the As of Date.
  • Desired behavior: Pay components should also be visible in the history view, consistent with the View All section.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central - Compensation Information

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Proxy as the Manager
  2. Search for any profile of the manager's direct report
  3. Navigate to Compensation Information > View All > History button
  4. Observe that in the History page, it shows (no data).
  5. Then compare the page outside the History view, pay components are visible  

Cause

Identified as defect

Resolution

This issue has been fixed in the upcoming 1H 2026 release (JIRA# ECT-268576). 

As a workaround:

- Grant at least one field permission from the Compensation Information section to ensure the Pay Component is visible in the history view of PP3.
