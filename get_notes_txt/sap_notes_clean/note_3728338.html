Symptom

You have a Business Completed Billable Maintenance Order and a connected Service Order. You business completed, then archived the Service Order, but haven't deleted the Service Order yet. You try to reverse the Business Completion on the Billable Maintenance Order.  The error message says 'Reverse the service order status to cancel business completion' (EAM_SRV074) although the reversal on the Service Order is not possible anymore.

Other Terms

Plant Maintenance, Billable Maintenance Order, BAPI, Manage Maintenance Orders, Cancel Business Completion

Reason and Prerequisites

Issue is caused by a program error.

Solution

Implement the attached correction or install the corresponding support package.
