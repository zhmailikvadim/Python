Symptom

  • The B1SiteUser account in SAP Business One gets locked or disabled repeatedly after being unlocked multiple times.

Environment

SAP Business One

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Attempt to log in using the B1SiteUser account.
  2. Unlock the B1SiteUser account.
  3. Observe that the B1SiteUser account gets locked or disabled again after some time.

Resolution

  1. Log in to the Authentication Server using the structure: https://server address:40020/auth.
  2. Perform a login with the B1SiteUser account.
  3. Change the user to 'sapb1' on the left panel.
  4. Check the 'Events' section to identify the login failure.
  5. Verify the failure by reviewing the IP address and client information.

See Also

  • Refer to: SAP Support Portal
  • Refer to: SAP Note 3518137 - B1SiteUser gets locked or disabled continuously when using Remote Support Platform for Business One
