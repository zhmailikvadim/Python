As the above mentioned traces shows that there are missing logs because of the persistence data mismatch as seen in the above logs.

GUID: 85c8be3d-606d-20250608-104707-041000000f  different from

PersGUID: 2372f03d-7c0e-20250921-103018-0090000010 

Resolution

Possible Reasons Why Full Replica Was Triggered:

  1. The secondary was not fully in sync with the primary, as the GUID has a difference of 3.5 months (June 8, 2025 vs. September 21, 2025).
  2. The full replica was previously interrupted, causing a sync data difference.
  3. The secondary site was restored from an old backup.
