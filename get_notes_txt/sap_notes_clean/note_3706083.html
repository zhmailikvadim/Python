Symptom

As part of the declaration exemption of items for an export declaration to the US customs system, items are excluded from the declaration because they are below the value limit of USD 2500 (code 37A). This is incorrect. The symptom occurs if item aggregation is active and the relevant item is incomplete, for example, due to a missing commodity code. For an incomplete item, no declaration exemption 37A can be assigned because this could be summarized with other items after the missing data has been completed and could therefore exceed the value limit of USD 2500.
