Symptom

Discrepancy in delivery status for email campaigns launched over 28 days ago.

Environment

SAP Emarsys Customer Engagement

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Use the API endpoint documented at: Get the URL of a Launched Email Campaign Content to retrieve campaign URLs.
  2. Attempt to retrieve URLs for campaigns launched more than four weeks ago.

Cause

• The API endpoint relies on a live mapping between Contact ID and Launch ID.  
• To maintain platform performance, SAP Emarsys archives this data after approximately 30 days.  
• This prevents the API from verifying the "received" relationship required to generate personalized URLs beyond this period.

Resolution

  1. Store a version of the email HTML in the user's system to ensure access to campaign URLs beyond the 30-day archive period.
  2. Ensure that campaigns are not deleted from SAP Emarsys to avoid URL breakage.
