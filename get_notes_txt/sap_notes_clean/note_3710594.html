Symptom

If unused or orphan documents are deleted with report SOLMAN_UNUSED_72_DOCUMENTS, also started with transaction SMUD_TOOL, then the archived documents header information could be deleted too.
You can reassign marked as deleted and archived documents to SOLDOC with report SMUD_RESTORE_ORPHAN_DOCUMENT. But they are inconsistent.

Other Terms

Archiving Documents, RSMUD_ARCH_DOCU_DOWNLOAD, RSMUD_ARCHIVE_DOCU, SMUD_RESTORE_ORPHAN_DOCUMENT

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error. The where used of the orphan documents must be extended.
Also it should forbidden to assign with report SMUD_RESTORE_ORPHAN_DOCUMENT marked as deleted and archived documents

Solution

Implement the correction instructions with the transaction SNOTE.
After that, the documents, which are archived, are not deleted with report SOLMAN_UNUSED_72_DOCUMENTS.
Also marked as deleted and archived documents could not be reassigned to SOLDOC with report marked as deleted and archived documents.
