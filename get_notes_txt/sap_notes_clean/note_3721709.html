Symptom

  • Sending Germany Electronic invoices via email fails in DRC for SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition.
  • Issue occurs when sending invoices to public institutes (where e-invoicing via email is mandatory).
  • Business Partner has an email address maintained.
    However, the “Customer” button is greyed out in the Business Partner master data.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition
  • SAP Document and Reporting Edition, Cloud Edition
 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Configure the SSCUI for assigning Party ID Types to Business Partners and document type for sending via email.
  2. Ensure the Business Partner has an email address.
  3. Attempt to send electronic invoices via email to public institutes.
  4. Observe that the "Customer" button is greyed out, and the electronic invoice fails to send.

Cause

The issue is caused by missing or incomplete communication configuration in the Business Partner master data—specifically under the Address communication settings.

Resolution

Maintain the correct communication details in the Business Partner:

  1. Navigate to Maintain Business Partner.
  2. Go to:
    Address → Communication → Other Communication
  3. Perform the following:
    • Select Communication Method = E-mail
    • Enter the Email Address
    • In the Notes field, enter: EDOC_DE

This ensures the system correctly identifies the email channel for eDocument processing.
