Symptom

Standard delivered PaPM roles do not have authorization default for service /NXI/P1_PM_EDITOR in the auth object S_START

Other Terms

LOD-PER-OP, NXI 3.0, S_START, /NXI/P1_PM_EDITOR, Authorization, PFCG Role

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

The authorizations for the roles (PFCG) must be adjusted manually since they cannot be part of an automatic instruction or report.

The manual steps help you to adjust the NXI roles such as /NXI/P1_MODELING_USER and /NXI/P1_MODELING_USER_ALL.

Please follow the steps as per the Manual instruction.
