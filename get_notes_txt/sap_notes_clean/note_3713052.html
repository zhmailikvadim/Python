Symptom

Mass edit in Purchase Requisition is not working when the line items has both Material and Service items

Resolution

The mass edit functionality is designed to work only when a requisition contains either material items or service items exclusively. When both types of items are present in the same requisition, the items cannot be mass edited.

See Also

Refer to Purchasing Guide for Procurement Professionals for more details.
