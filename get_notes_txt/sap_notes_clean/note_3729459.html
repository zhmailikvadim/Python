Symptom

The signature field “FIRMA DEL SOSTITUTO DI IMPOSTA” is not displayed in the output of the Italy Withholding Tax Declaration (CU) statutory report. The issue occurs even after setting up all the necessary configurations in the following activities:

SSCUID: 101071 "Setting Up Your Statutory Reporting”
SSCUID: 106752 “Maintain Details of Tax Code Representative” (First Name and Last Name are maintained)

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 2602

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the app "Run Statutory Reports".
  2. Enter the report name "IT_WHT_DCL_CU", reporting entity "IT_RPG_ENT", and reporting year "2025".
  3. Navigate to the activity named "Italy CU Withholding Tax Report".
  4. Click on "New Run".
  5. Enter all required details.
  6. Generate the report and check the output.
  7. Observe that the signature is missing in the field "FIRMA DEL SOSTITUTO DI IMPOSTA".

Resolution

A solution to populate the “FIRMA DEL SOSTITUTO DI IMPOSTA” field in the form is currently under preparation and will be delivered as part of an upcoming hotfix release.

The fix is planned to be available by April 20th.

Workaround:
Until the fix is available, the signature can be added manually to the form.
