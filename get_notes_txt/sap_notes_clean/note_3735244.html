Symptom

Why am I not able to configure more than 20 Tax values in SAP Business Network?

Why do I see following error when trying to configure more than 20 tax values in SAP Business Network?

!Cannot create more than 20 items

Cause

System design limitation restricts the maximum number of configurable tax values to 20.

Resolution

As per the current system design, a maximum of 20 tax values can be configured in the SAP Business Network account.

This limit cannot be increased through configuration or standard settings.

If a higher limit is required, please submit an enhancement request through the appropriate channel for product team review and consideration in future releases

See Also

The transaction rule Require suppliers to choose from your list of valid tax values. if enabled, Buyer can create their own list of valid tax values which applies to their business.  
Up to 20 tax values can be configured from which their suppliers must choose to add tax to the invoice.

To log any enhancement or improvement request you may refer How do I create an improvement or enhancement request for the SAP Business Network?

To configure tax values in your account, please refer How do I configure tax categories for my suppliers to choose from when invoicing?
