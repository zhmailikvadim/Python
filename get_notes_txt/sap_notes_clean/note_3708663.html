Symptom

Message "Credit limit exceeded" appears when creating sales order even though the credit limit used is not 100%, and the sales order credit value does not exceed the available credit limit. This KBA explains one of the possible causes.

Environment

  • Sales and Distribution (SD)
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Check the credit limit in FD33 for the customer.
  2. Observe the credit limit used is not 100%.
  3. Create a sales order in VA01 with credit value less than the available credit limit.
  4. Observe the credit limit exceeded message being triggered.

Cause

  • In transaction OVA8, Credit limit seasonal factor is configured with a percentage and 'Minus' flag activated.
  • This configuration reduces the effective credit limit used for credit checks corresponding to the percentage specified, which causes the credit limit used during credit check to be lower than the one displayed in FD32/FD33 (for example: if seasonal factor is 1% with 'Minus' flag activated, then the effective credit limit used during credit check is only 99% of the credit limit in FD33).
  • If the sales order credit value is higher than the credit limit with seasonal factor, then the credit limit exceeded message will be triggered.

Resolution

Remove the seasonal factor value and the Minus flag in transaction OVA8 for the affected credit control area.
