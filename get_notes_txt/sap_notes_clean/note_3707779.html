Symptom

With the introduction of the BASS business day (/IBS/TMO_CMU_DT-CLOSINGDATE) in BASS Credit, the requirement has also arisen to automatically fill this date in the fields of the financing or loan.

Reason and Prerequisites

 Provide the option to map the date from /IBS/TMO_CMU_DT-CLOSINGDATE to a field that meets the customer's requirements in accordance with Customizing.

Solution

The enhancement of the domain enabled a new Customizing setting for controlling the transfer of the CLOSINGDATE date to the corresponding tables. In addition, the existing function that is responsible for mapping this date has been enhanced with the necessary code to support the new function.
