Symptom

The customer requires the ability to update regulatory information in WLI (Work List Items) that have been inactive for more than 30 days.

Environment

SAP Batch Release Hub

Cause

The system deactivates business rules for WLI after 30 days by standard design to limit background workload; the threshold is not available as a functional configuration and cannot be controlled per material type.

Resolution

  1. Acknowledge that the 30‑day automatic inactivation of business rules for WLI is standard behavior and is not adjustable via functional configuration, per‑material type control and bulk activation are not supported in standard.
  2. If extended or configurable thresholds (for example, per material type) or bulk reactivation are required, submit a product enhancement request via the Customer Influence Program so product management can evaluate the requirement.
  3. Until an enhancement is delivered, use manual reactivation of affected WLI/business rules as needed, no standard automated workaround is available.

See Also
