Symptom

It is possible to release a designated hedging relationship with deals that are not settled yet. Unsettled deals can create issues during the life span of the hedging relationship for example during a reversal.

Other Terms

SAP Treasury and Risk Management, TPM100, Hedge Accounting for Positions

Reason and Prerequisites

Customer is using SAP Treasury and Risk Management with a hedge accounting scenario.

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction (CI) or the relevant Support Package.
