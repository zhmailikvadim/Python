Symptom

In retroactive accounting to December, payroll terminates with the error message Negative church tax due to annual employment tax declaration (HRPAYDEST641).

The termination occurs if the church tax was reimbursed by the annual employment tax declaration (ETNotif.) in the original payroll run. There are no regular church taxes, but only other church taxes in December.

Other Terms

 DST, KIKO, CL_HRPAYDE_ST_KIJA, HRPAYDEST, 641

Reason and Prerequisites

You have implemented SAP Notes 3703814 - Church tax: Correction of negative church tax for annual employment tax declaration and 3702909 - Church tax: Negative church tax due to annual employment tax declaration (LStJA).

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified support package. Repeat payroll for personnel numbers with a termination.
