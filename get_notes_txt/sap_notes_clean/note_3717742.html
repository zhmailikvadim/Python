Symptom

We are accessing the prepackage Sourcing Usage Reports and some users are not considered as Procurement Contract Workspace Ability for Analytical Reports.

  • Why is it happening even the user being assigned to a Sourcing group?
  • Which project team roles are counted as a licensed user?

Resolution

The user should belong to any of the mentioned sourcing groups: Contract Agent, Contract Manager, Contract Administrator, Commodity Manager, Customer Administrator for being considered as a Procurement Contract Workspace Ability in the report. There is no condition to just consider the Enterprise or just the Third-Party ones, the condition is just based on the mentioned groups.
