Symptom

In the section headed "Type of Fuel or power used" there are 2 options
for Type of Fuel: Diesel cars (all Euro standards) and All other cars.
The selected Key letter A or D does not appear in the box provided (the
box is on the line below).

Other Terms

P46 Car, HR_GB_P46_2015

Reason and Prerequisites

Form correction

Solution

To get the new form is your system, apply the manual corrections located in the Assigned Correction Instructions section of this Note. Alternatively, apply the HR Support Package(s) indicated for your release under Reference to Support Packages.
