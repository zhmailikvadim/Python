Symptom

  • The calling hours for Sold-To Party Business Partner (BP) were changed
  • Identify how the change occurred or who made the changes

    Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental. 

Environment

SAP Solution Manager 7.2

Resolution

  1. Execute SM_CRM transaction code
  2. Select SOLMANPRO business role
  3. Select Master Data Work Center
  4. Search > Accounts
  5. Account ID = BP Number
  6. Change History Assignment Block

Keywords

Sold to party, Support Team, Processor, Reporter, Business Partner change log
