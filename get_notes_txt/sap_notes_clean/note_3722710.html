Symptom

Error 1069: The service did not start due to a logon failure.

The windows service account was a member of the local Administrators group. The real problem is the precedence Windows gives to user permissions.  

Environment

SAP Enterprise Continuous Testing by Tricentis

Resolution

Check the following knowledge article from Tricentis:

Keywords

TOSCA, maximum, age, group, policy, precedence
