Symptom

  • Options to enable a custom SAML Identity Provider are missing in SAP Analytics Cloud (SAC)
  • This issue may also happen with SAP Business Data Cloud (BDC) and SAP Datasphere (DSP) due to same reason

Environment

  • SAP Analytics Cloud, Enterprise Edition
  • SAP Business Data Cloud
  • SAP Datasphere

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log on to SAC Tenant.
  2. From the side navigation, go to System  > Administration > Security.
  3. All of options required to enable a custom SAML Identity Provider are missing:
    • The whole Authentication Method area
    • Step 1 - Step 4 under SAML Single Sign-On (SSO) Configuration area

Cause

  • We are enabling the new IDP Admin tool in a controlled release as per SAP Blog: Easier Integration of SAP CIS with BDC (SAC, DSP, BDCC) with a new Identity Provider Administration, which will allow customer to create or modify a bundled SAP Cloud Identity Services Tenant to work with SAP Analytics Cloud. 
  • Your tenant may have been bundled by following steps described in Create or Modify a Bundled SAP Cloud Identity Services Tenant.
  • Alternatively, your tenant may be greenfield, on which you can only use the bundling feature while self-service SAML to Enable a Custom SAML Identity Provider (Legacy Custom IdP) will be disabled.

Resolution

Please use Identity Provider Administration tool to manage their IdP configuration, including:

  • Modify Your Authentication Setup (changing attributes or toggling dynamic user creation)
  • Disable bundled IAS tenant to default IDP or custom IDP (Self Service SAML) (brownfield only)
