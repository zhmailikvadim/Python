Symptom

Business documents like Purchase Requisitions(PR) are being routed to an incorrect or unintended backend system or environment from Managed Gateway For Spend & Network.

Environment

SAP Ariba Buying and Invoicing
Managed Gateway for Spend & Network

Cause

Due to presence of stale connection, the documents are being routed to the incorrect system id from Managed Gateway For Spend & Network, as the system cannot identify the correct system where the document needs to be routed.

 

Resolution

Navigate to Managed Gateway For Spend & Network Portal:
1. Go to My Configuration>Connections
2. Delete the stale or duplicate connection
3. Try resending a fresh document and validate if the documents are now being routed to the correct system ID from Managed Gateway For Spend & Network Portal.
