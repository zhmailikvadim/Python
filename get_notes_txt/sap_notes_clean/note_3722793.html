Symptom

This SAP Note enables the Withholding Tax Statement (Japan) (RPCEADJ1) report to output a new text in the Tekiyo column. This text in Tekiyo column indicates that the common life insurance deduction in Year-End Adjustment (YEA) is calculated using the special deduction table for the employees with dependents under age 23.

Other Terms

TEXT2, /YS5, NLTOT

Reason and Prerequisites

This revision is a part of a legal change. You have generated YEA results after you implement the revisions in SAP Note 3714966.

Solution

You can apply the relevant HR Support Package. Alternatively, you can implement the manual changes together with the correction instructions in this SAP Note.

You may find the details of the text in Tekiyo column from the manual activities in this SAP Note.
