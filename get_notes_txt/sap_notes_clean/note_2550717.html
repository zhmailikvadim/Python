Symptom

Before an Upgrade or DataSource transport the operational delta queue shall be emptied, in order to exclude the potential risk of data loading issues due to changed structures.

The units of the last delta request (push delta) are not deleted from the operational delta queue, although the retention time is reached. The starting of further delta loads with 0 delta records does not help, only loading a request with new delta records will lead to deletion of the former old units.

Other Terms

ODQMON

Reason and Prerequisites

program error

Solution

Import the Support Package relevant for your SAP_BASIS release: Alternatively, you can also implement the advanced correction provided with this SAP Note.
