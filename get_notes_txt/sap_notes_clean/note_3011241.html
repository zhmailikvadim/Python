Symptom

  • Message: "bgRFC Inbound Destination &QD_EQ_LOCAL& does not exist and cannot be edited" when accessing app F1873 in Fiori
  • Error: Failed to create "bgRFC Inbound Destination &QD_EQ_LOCAL& does not exist and cannot be edited" when train a Predictive model in Fiori

Environment

  • S/4HANA 1809 onPrem
  • S/4HANA 1909 onPrem
  • F1873 (Predictive Scenario)
  • Predictive Analytics Integrator (PAi)

Reproducing the Issue

  1.  Logon to S/4 Fiori Launch Pad.
  2. Click to open app Predictive Models.
  3. Open any of the listed Predictive Scenarios.
  4. Choose the Model and click Train.
  5. Observe Failed to create "bgRFC Inbound Destination &QD_EQ_LOCAL& does not exist and cannot be edited" error message displayed.

Cause

bgRFC Inbound Destination is not defined.

Resolution

Define Inbound Destination with name QD_EQ_LOCAL and associate prefix EQQD.

Installation Guide for SAP S/4HANA 1909 - 7.10.2 Setting up the bgRFC Queue

And, need to check if all setting in "7.10 Follow-Up Activities for Predictive Analytics integrator" has been implemented.
