Symptom

During update of the table /ACCGO/T_CANC_CC with settlement indicator, the table was not getting updated when the report for auto generation of non-standard settlement was getting executed. As a result, multiple settlements were getting created for one cancellation when the report was executed multiple times.

Other Terms

Agricultural Contract Management; ACM; Trading Contract; Cancellation; Settlement Indicator

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
