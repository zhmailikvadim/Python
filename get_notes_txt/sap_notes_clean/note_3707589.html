Symptom

Error message: "No SAP Ariba Sourcing system with the required solution found" is encountered during Supplier Management provisioning from SAP for ME.

Environment

Supplier Profile Summary (NG 360)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to SAP for ME portal.
  2. Go to Systems & Provisioning.
  3. Start a new provisioning request for SAP Ariba Supplier Management.

Cause

The SAP Ariba realm is not enabled with the entitlement SAP Ariba Supplier Lifecycle and Performance Management.

Resolution

In order to overcome the error in subject,

  1. Confirm whether SAP Ariba Supplier Lifecycle and Performance Management entitlement is included in the contract. It is most likely included as SAP Ariba Supplier Management appears under provisioning.
  2. If the entitlement is included, contact the SAP Ariba deployment lead or the Customer Success Manager (CSM) to have the SAP Ariba Supplier Lifecycle and Performance (SLP) enabled in the realm.

Please note that enabling or disabling an product entitlement controlling feature is not within the scope of SAP support.
