Symptom

During the supplier onboarding process, when the external supplier request form is submitted, the notification sent to the supplier does not include the company logo, despite the logo being maintained in the system.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Fill in the external supplier request form and submit it.
  2. Verify the notification sent to the supplier upon submission.
  3. Observe that the notification does not include the company logo.
  4. Reject the external supplier request form as a buyer.
  5. Verify the notification sent to the supplier upon rejection.
  6. Observe that the notification does not include the company logo.

Cause

The company logo is not designed and supported for self-registration email notifications in SAP Ariba Supplier Lifecycle and Performance.

Resolution

Please be noted that the company logo is not supported for self-registration email notifications as per the current product design. Logo can be noticed in other Supplier Management projects, but not having in any self registration email notification is an expected system behavior.
