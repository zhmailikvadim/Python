Symptom

The care charge (nursing portion of the DRG) is not listed in the print structure RNFDRG if the underlying DRG results in a day-related charge with charge type 15.

Other Terms

Nursing DRG, nursing portion, care charge, RNFDRG

Reason and Prerequisites

This problem is caused by a missing function.

The print structure RNFDRG is not filled for day-related charges with charge type 15.

Solution

Refer to the correction instructions for your release.

After you implement the correction instructions, charge type 15 is also used to supply the print structure RNFDRG with the information about the care charge for day-related charges.
