Symptom

All the spaces in your SAP Datasphere instance have been locked with a message "All spaces are locked because the tenant disk usage has reached a critical threshold. You must either increase your tenant disk storage or delete unneeded data" in SAP Datasphere Space Management.

Reason and Prerequisites

When the tenant disk usage reaches a critical threshold, all spaces are automatically locked to protect your tenant from storage-related outages.

Your SAP Datasphere tenant is on or above version 2026.05

Solution

In such a situation, the following actions are possible:

  • Increase tenant disk storage (Recommended Solution)

A user with an administrator role can increase the tenant disk storage (see Configure the Size of your SAP Datasphere Tenant and SAP Note for Scaling Down your SAP Datasphere tenant)

  • Delete one or more spaces that are no longer needed

A user with a space administrator role can use the Unlock button on the Space Management page or on the space page to unlock the space so that the space can be deleted. (see Unlock a Locked Space and Delete Your Space)

  • Delete space data

Users assigned to a space can delete data. (see Unlock a Locked Space)

If one or more spaces are unlocked without any reduction in disk usage or increasing the tenant disk storage, the spaces will be locked again in 5 minutes. Once the required actions are taken, all the locked spaces must be manually unlocked to restore normal operations.
