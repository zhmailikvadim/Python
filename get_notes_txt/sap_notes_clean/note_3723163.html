Symptom

When you search for materials in F5726 Maintain Time-Dependent Stock Levels, only materials maintained in MRP areas of type storage location or subcontracting are shown. The materials are not displayed for the plant MRP areas.

Other Terms

F5726, Maintain Time-Dependent Stock Levels, MRP safety stock method, material requirements planning, Static Safety Stock, Time-Dependent Safety Stock, Time-Dependent Days of Supply, no results, materials missing, no materials

Reason and Prerequisites

It is caused by an incorrect selection condition in one of the underlying CDS views.

Solution

Implement this SAP Note to correct the condition so that materials in plant MRP areas show up again.
