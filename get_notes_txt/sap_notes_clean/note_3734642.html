Symptom

During the creation of RBM notifications (according to the latest status), these are compared with existing notifications and the old notifications are reversed.

Other Terms

CL_HRPAYDE_RBM_NOTIF_MANAGER->MATCH_NOTIFS

Reason and Prerequisites

The prerequisite is the correction from SAP Note 3712442 - RBM: MZ01 notifications for inflow years after the year of death of the pension recipient. This is to reverse existing notifications if they were created for a benefit year > the year of death.

However, if the year of death is unknown, the value for this is '0000' and then all existing comparison notifications are reversed.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified support package.

As a result, existing comparison notifications are only reversed if a year of death > '0000' is found and the benefit year of the comparison notification is > the year of death.

After you import the HR Support Package or implement the correction instructions, you do not have to perform any further follow-on activities.
