Symptom

The Service Organization field remains empty in the Service Confirmation search results (or any other search) , although it has been updated in the document. 

Detail of document

Environment

SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  • Execute a search for Service documents (Service Order, Service Contract, Service Quotation, Service Confirmation, and Service Request).

  • Some organizational fields are displayed as blank even though they have been updated in the documents.

Cause

Wrong fields : depending on your organizational settings system uses different fields. In fact, usually there are two fields called Service Organiztion

Resolution

- First, verify the organizational settings and confirm whether the Enterprise Organization or the Legacy Organization model is being used. 

SAP Customizing Implementation Guide -> Service -> Basic Functions -> Enterprise Organizational Management -> Enable Enterprise Organizational Model

-Click Configure Area and select the highlighted area. 

- Check the available fields: two fields named Service Organization are listed. Select Show Technical Details to identify the correct one. 

The field SERVICE_ORG is used for Legacy organizational data, while Enterprise Service Organization is used for Enterprise organizational data. 

-Select the appropriate option based on the setting identified in the first step.
