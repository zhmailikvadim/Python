Symptom

CDS view based analytical Fiori app like app i.e. F0959 does not consider parameter entered in field "Key date"  and does not filter results as expected. 

Environment

  • Controlling (CO)
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP Fiori

Reproducing the Issue

1. Launch Fiori App, for example F0959 Profit Centers – Actuals.
2. Select a specific Key Date. 
3. Observe that the system displays result that does not consider Key Date.

Cause

The Key Date is not for filtering transactional data within the query. The Key Date is used only for checking the attributes of time dependent Master Data. 

Resolution

When entering Key Date and Fiscal Year of Ledger, the key date is irrelevant. The report will display cumulative balance of fiscal year.
