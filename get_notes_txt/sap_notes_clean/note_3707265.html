Symptom

During mass processing of INVOIC generation, the gv_leitweg_equiv flag is not cleared, causing incorrect behavior:

  • First invoice: Processed as an X-Invoice.
  • Second invoice: Incorrectly treated as a non-X-Invoice with zero tax, generating faulty TAX segments (header and item) in the IDOC despite no tax being applicable.

A previous X-Invoice in the message processing flow triggers incorrect tax segment generation for subsequent invoices.

Other Terms

CIP2SAP, SIDOC, IDOC, X-Rechnung, INVOIC OUT, TTP2667, TTP2478

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the correction instruction
