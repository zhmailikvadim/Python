Symptom

  • The system allows uploading a bank statement for a bank account with status 15 "Closed" using FF_5, even though the account is closed.
  • The system prevents uploading a bank statement for a bank account with status 11 "Closed at Bank" using FF_5, displaying the error message FB736: "Bank account management has no entry with bank key and account number."
  • The discrepancy in system behavior is considered illogical, as status 15 and 11 both represents  bank account closure.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • Finance > Treasury Management > Cash and Liquidity Management > Bank Account Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Select a bank account with status = 15 "Closed" in the FCLM_BAM_AMD table.
  2. Upload an MT940 bank statement for this account using FF_5. The system posts the statement.
  3. Select another bank account with status = 11 "Closed at Bank" in the FCLM_BAM_AMD table.
  4. Attempt to upload an MT940 bank statement for this account using FF_5. The system does not allow the statement to be posted and displays an error FB736.

Cause

The discrepancy arises due to differences in how the system handles validity conditions for accounts based on their status. For accounts with status 15 "Closed", entries exist in table T012K, allowing the upload of bank statements. For accounts with status 11 "Closed at Bank", no entries exist in table T012K, preventing the upload. 

Resolution

For the account in status 2,9,10,11 the valid_to in the house bank connectivity has to be more than today's date but for the closed account only valid_from is considered in DDLS FCLM_BAM_DISTINCT_HBA . bank accounts with status 02, 09, 10, or 11 (not in final closed status), the valid to date of the house bank account is used to hide invalid T012K entries, since the Product manage/owner considers this validity to be intentionally maintained by the customer for business purposes. This is how the T012K is designed.
