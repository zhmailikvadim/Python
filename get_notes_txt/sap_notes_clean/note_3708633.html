Symptom

When processing quantity differences entries, and these selected entries involve both goods being received and issued with cancellations, the adjusted stock levels don't match the total quantity from those entries.

Other Terms

US, FTZ, /SAPSLL/CUSWLO_18, /SAPSLL/CUSWLO_FTZ_INT_CREATE

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Apply correction instruction of the note.
