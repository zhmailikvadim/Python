Symptom

  • After upgrading the BI, logging into BI Launchpad and CMC results in a blank page.
  • The issue persists even after redeployment.

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform 4.x
  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform 2025

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Perform the BI upgrade according to the best practice guide.
  2. Attempt to log into BI Launchpad or CMC.
  3. Enter credentials and click "Logon".
  4. A blank page appears.

Cause

The BOE folder under the Warfiles folder was corrupted during the upgrade due to unexpected behavior.

Resolution

  1. Stop Tomcat.
  2. Copy the BOE folder located under <INSTALL DIRECTORY>\SAP BusinessObjects\tomcat\webapps\BOE from a functioning environment or internal system of the same version.
  3. Replace the corrupted BOE folder in the non-working environment with the copied folder from the working environment under <INSTALL DIRECTORY>\SAP BusinessObjects\tomcat\webapps\BOE.
  4. Clear the Tomcat cache as per KBA 1879431.

IMPORTANT: Copy the BOE folder located in <INSTALL DIRECTORY>\SAP BusinessObjects\SAP BusinessObjects Enterprise XI 4.0\warfiles\webapps\BOE from a working environment to the non-working system to prevent issues during future patches or deployments.
