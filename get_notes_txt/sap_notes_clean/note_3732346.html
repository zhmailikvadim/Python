Symptom

Field Education Level is part of Romanian infotypes 3523 and 3524 used for REGES reporting. The value 'Medium studies with license' (Medii cu licenta) was removed from the official list of available values and is no longer accepted by REGES.

Other Terms

HRRO; Romania; REGES; PRO_EDUCL

Solution

Value 'Medium studies with license' has been marked as obsolete in field Education Level of Romanian infotypes 3523 and 3524. Users have to manually update the master data of affected employees.

Implementation
