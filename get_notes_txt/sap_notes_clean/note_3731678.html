Symptom

 Customer is having EWMCP add-on and upgrading to S/4 HANA 2025

Other Terms

EWMCP , SAP S/4HANA EWM FOR CP 2210 , EWM for Consumer Product

Reason and Prerequisites

Customer is having EWMCP in S/4 HANA 2023 or below versions

Solution

1. EWM for Consumer Product is sunset and not sold anymore to customers


2. EWMCP add-on is not compatible with S/4 HANA 2025


3. Customer has to un-install before upgrading to S/4 HANA 2025 - reference un-installation notes provided below
    2899706 - Uninstallation of EWMCPS4 Add-On
    3127872 - Uninstalling EWMCP


4. Please refer Product Availability Matrix for End of Mainteance Date  - link
