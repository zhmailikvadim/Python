Symptom

You are confirming Putaway WTs into a storage type with check on maximum quantity. If the WT is confirmed with exception CHBD, it could happen that the putaway is rejected with an error, saying that the maximum quantity is exceeded (/SCWM/PUT_BIN_DET071 - "Storage type &1: No putaway - max. storage type qty exceeded (current:&2)"), even though the quantity confirmed should still be possible to put away.

The error only occurs when the putaway is confirmed in /SCWM/RFUI

Other Terms

Enqueue /SCWM/S_SMAQ_MAXBIN_LOCK_S /SCWM/ESMAQY storage type maximum quantity MAX_QTY chksmaq
