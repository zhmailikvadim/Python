Symptom

This note provides a list of country specific notes released for SAP S/4HANA and SAP GTS (Global Trade Services). The notes listed here are required for Intrastat compliance in 2026. The notes applicable to your system version, should be implemented in all systems. It is recommended that, after implementation and before transporting to Production, Intrastat testing is performed.

Note: All notes provided with FAQ Note "3583011 - INTRASTAT: Legal requirements for compliance in 2025" must be implemented prior to implementing the notes provided in current note. It is expected that, by 2026, all legal requirements from 2025 are already implemented and tested in all systems. Without all the notes implemented, Intrastat cannot work correctly if legal requirements are missing. So the notes listed in FAQ Note 3583011 should be checked first.

Note: Higher versions of S/4CORE may only need a few notes from the list below (because corrections are already included in service pack), while lower versions (and especially lower service packs) may need to implement most notes from the list below. Only the notes applicable to the system version should be implemented. For example: SPCORE 108 may require very few notes from the list below, while SPCORE 105 may require more notes from the list below. It is recommended to upgrade to higher versions and/or service pack to avoid maintenance effort.

Note: The list below might be updated in 2026 with additional country legal requirements till the end of the year. Any changes to the list will be recorded under a different version of the current note. The notes in the list are maintained in ascending order to make the maintenance easier.

Other Terms

Intrastat, compliance, 2026, S/4HANA, Private Cloud Edition, GTS, Partner VAT Number, Sale to Private Consumer, Business  Transaction Type

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a legal change for 2026.

Prerequisite notes: all notes listed under FAQ Note "3583011 - INTRASTAT: Legal requirements for compliance in 2025".

Solution

Implement or check the notes below in order, from lowest to highest note number.

Refer to the table below for country-specific and country-not-specific notes.

Note: Country non-specific notes are legal corrections for Intrastat reporting that are applicable to all the countries.
