Symptom

  • How can I get the full list of catalogs approved during a specific time frame?
  • Is it possible to check the list of catalogs that were denied during this year?

Resolution

  1. If you have an FPC setup, login to your parent realm
  2. Only the users who are assigned with "Catalog Approver" group have access to this information
  3. If you are an admin, make sure to act as a user who is assigned to this group if you don't have it assigned to your user profile
  4. Click on "Search" icon on the top right of your screen -> Choose "More"
  5. Locate and choose the option "Supplier Data Update"
  6. Filter the results according to your need and extract the results using the "Table Options Menu" icon that is located below the "Save Search" button
