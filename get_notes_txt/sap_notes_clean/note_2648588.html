Symptom

You start the Update Secondary (US) transaction to reprocess old postings made on entangled Financial Positions (FPs) such as hedged products or FPs involved in a securities account transfer.
On one of those linked FPs, an Asset and Liabilities (A/L) transfer occurred. Therefore you expect it to be regenerated with this new US run but unfortunately this is not the case, and consequently, your FP A/L status is now incorrect.

Other Terms

/BA1/B1_US_SL - Update Secondary Business Transactions

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisite: you have installed the note 2007083

Reason: in case one of the entangled FPs has not been set for reprocessing with the transaction /BA1/B1_FP_TOUCH_SL, its calc_from date is set to the 31.12.9999 date by the US run. This infinite date setting prevents the invalidation of necessary FP characteristic versions, especially the version with the new A/L status.

Solution

Implement the attached correction or corresponding support package.
