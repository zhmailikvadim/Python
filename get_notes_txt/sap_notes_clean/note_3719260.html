Symptom

When executing DRFOUT and trying to replicate Business Partner, it fails with the error message {"invalid_request","error_description":"Wrong company ID header or company name query parameter value"}

Environment

  • SAP Field Service Management
  • S4 On-Premise via CPI Connector

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Enter Replication Model into DRFOUT
  2. Fill details of Business Partner along with Filter
  3. Press the button "Execute"
  • Expected: Business Partner successfully replicated 
  • Actual: Error "{"invalid_request","error_description":"Wrong company ID header or company name query parameter value"}'"

Cause

Client configured on FSM does not save access to respective company

Resolution

Provide access to company in Client & update the configuration in order to ensure both S4 & FSM systems are in sync.
