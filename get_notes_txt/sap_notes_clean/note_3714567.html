Symptom

  • After updating to BOBJ 4.3 SP04, all the hyperlinks in WebI report display as content, and they cannot be clicked

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 4.3 SP04 onwards 
  • Web Intelligence

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login to BI Launchpad
  2. Open a Web Intelligence document
  3. Notice all the hyperlinks show as content

Cause

Due to the growing number of security threats, the use of hyperlinks is prevented in Web Intelligence by default to avoid links that potentially open malicious locations or trigger unwanted actions.

Resolution

All the URLs used in Web Intelligence must be explicitly authorized in the CMC by following below steps:
  1. In CMC, navigate to Applications.
  2. Right click on "Web Intelligence" application and click on "Properties".
  3. In the "Authorized URLs" page, click on "Add a new URL" button.
  4. Add the used URL and click on OK.
  5. Then log off from the session, then logon again.
