Symptom

In the process "Setup/Replacement of Devices (MOS View)" (DE_B_IM_DEV_CHANGE), step 0390 stops and throws an exception due to incorrect data update.
This issue is relevant for the market roles SUP, DSO, MOS, and PRS.

Other Terms

PoD ID, MaLo, MEMA Relation, PoD relation, Device Installation, MCM_DE, DE_B_IM_DEV_CHANGE

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because MaLos that were marked for deletion were not excluded from billing-related installation.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.
After implementing this SAP Note, the system excludes MaLos marked for deletion from billing-related installation.
You need to retrigger the process from the cloud again. You can verify the fix by checking that device installation and PoD relations are established correctly.
