Symptom

In business partner master data of a payment consolidated customer, the dunning level on the Accounting tab is not updated according to the dunning level displayed in the dunning wizard.

Reproducing the issue

Prerequisites:

  • Business partner 'C001' is payment consolidated to business partner 'C002'.
  • An overdue A/R invoice exists for customer 'C001'.

 

Scenario:

Execute a dunning run in the database.

 

Actual Behavior:

In business partner master data for customer 'C002', on the Accounting tab, the dunning level is not updated according to the dunning level displayed in the recommendation report (Step 5 of the dunning wizard).

 

Expected Behavior:

The dunning level of customer 'C002' in the business partner master data is updated according to the dunning level displayed in the recommendation report (Step 5 of the dunning wizard).

 

Cause

Application error.

Solution

SAP intends to provide a patch or patches in order to solve the described problem.

The section Reference to Related Notes below will list the specific Patches once they become available.

The corresponding Info file of the patches on SAP Service Marketplace will also show the SAP Note number.

Be aware that these references can only be set at Patch release date.

SAP will deliver Patches only for selected Releases at its own discretion, based on the business impact and the complexity of the implementation.

Other terms
