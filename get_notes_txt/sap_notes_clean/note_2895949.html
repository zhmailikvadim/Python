Symptom

On creating new retention or residence policies for ILM Objects BBP_AUC, BBP_AVL, BBP_BID,  BBP_CF, BBP_CTR, BBP_INV, BBP_PCO, BBP_PO, BBP_QUOT you want to use the CREATION_DATE as time reference, but only LAST_CHANGE_DATE is available.

Also Standard conditions like BS_COUNTRY_OF_BUKRS, BS_COUNTRY_OF_VKORG and BS_COUNTRY_OF_WERKS are available while creating new policy, but do not work.

Other Terms

ILM policy, time reference, retention rule, residence rule, ILM object, IRM constant, Retention Management, CREATION_DATE; LAST_CHANGE_DATE

Reason and Prerequisites

As of now the support for  Standard conditions like BS_COUNTRY_OF_BUKRS, BS_COUNTRY_OF_VKORG and BS_COUNTRY_OF_WERKS is not provided, but due to errornous configuration, the option were seen while creating the policy.

For CREATION_DATE, the configuration was incomplete, thus it was not working. It is now corrected with the note.

Solution

Implement the attached manual corrections Instructions or upgrade to relevant support package.
