Symptom

I am the approver of an expense sheet and need to take action to approve it.

Resolution

Follow the below steps to approve Expenses sheets:

Through Work Items Menu:

  1. Sign into your SAP Fieldglass account
  2. Click on the Work Items Menu
  3. Under Expense Sheet > Click Approve
  4. Select the Expense Sheet ID > Click Approve

Through Menu Navigation:

  1. Sign into your SAP Fieldglass account
  2. Click View> Expense sheet 
    • If you are using the vertical menu navigation click Worker> Expense sheet
  3. Open the Expense Sheet ID > Click Approve

Through Worker Profile:

  1. Sign into your SAP Fieldglass account
  2. Click View> Worker 
    • If you are using the vertical menu navigation click Worker> Worker 
  3. Open the Worker ID > Click Time & Expense tab
  4. Scroll down to the Expense Sheets section 
  5. Click Expense Sheet ID > Click Approve

Through Email:

  1. Approver will receive an email notification to approve expense sheet
  2. Click Approve button on the email > click Send
