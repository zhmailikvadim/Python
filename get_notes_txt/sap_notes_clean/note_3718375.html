Symptom

Why am I getting the following error when trying to create a purchase order based/PO-Flip invoice?

Error: Customer does not accept Electronic Invoice based on the Buyer Vat ID value.

Cause

Your customer has enabled country based invoice rules and under the tab Prevent suppliers from sending invoices to the following countries some of the countries are selected. 

Resolution

Please contact your customer to edit this list so you can invoice.

See Also

Buyers can select to block countries based on Ship To, Buyer VAT ID, Sold To, and Bill To. 

Keywords

electronic invoice issue, buyer tax ID, invoice settings, invoicing error
