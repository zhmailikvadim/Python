Symptom

You are using Active Ingredient Management  with Material Ledger.

Reason and Prerequisites

The Active Ingredient Management and the Material Ledger is active.

Solution

The Material Ledger and Active Ingredient Management are not fully integrated. If you use Active Ingredient Management and Material Ledger, a seamless value flow can only be expected in company code currency in legal valuation.

For additional Material Ledger currencies, the value flow differs.

An example of non-seamless value flow is the inability to make sure that dynamically created standard prices are converted using the appropriate historical exchange rate.
