Symptom

The system does not allow the desired Planning and Execution Group assignment in the Freight Unit and Freight Order applications.
The system enforces that the Planning and Execution Group is the child of the Planning and Execution Organization (maintained in the PPOME transaction).
If the restrictive assignment is not fulfilled, the following error occurs in the Freight Document applications on when changing / saving the 2 field contents:

Group <group> is not assigned to organization <organization>

Other Terms

 /SCMTMS/COMMON 081

Reason and Prerequisites

Restricted functionality.

Solution

Implement attached correction instructions or apply appropriate Support Package.
