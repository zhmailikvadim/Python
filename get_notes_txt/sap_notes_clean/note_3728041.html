Symptom

  • The total amount of adjustment value displayed in the Adjustment section of the SOW Invoice is incorrect
  • Why is the invoice adjustment total amount (Subtotal) not including the Total Discount?

Cause

The Total Discount value, representing the overall SOW discount for line items, is not factored into the Subtotal. The Subtotal strictly represents the invoice adjustment amount alone.

Resolution

The Total Discount adjustment exists on the invoice for record-keeping purposes only, storing the discount value at the invoice level. It is not utilized in tax/discount calculations or adjustment reconciliations, which is why it is excluded from the Subtotal amount
