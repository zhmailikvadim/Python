Symptom

You are implementing AskHR, but when looking to configure BTP, you are not able to find AskHR subscription on SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP).

You are going to:

  1. SAP BTP Cockpit > Services > Application Development and Automation;
  2. Try to enable either SAP SuccessFactors EC Service Center (Preview) or SAP SuccessFactors EC Service Center (Production) like the screenshot below:

But you are unable to find them in your BTP.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite
  • Ask HR - Employee Central Service Center (ECSC)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in to SAP BTP Cockpit.
  2. Navigate to Services > Application Development and Automation;
  3. Try to enable either SAP SuccessFactors EC Service Center (Preview) or SAP SuccessFactors EC Service Center (Production);

Cause

  1. AskHR functionality is not supported on BTP Cloud Foundry environment. It requires a BTP Sub-account in NEO environment with the appropriate entitlements to enable the service.
  2. You have a Neo Sub-account, but the entitlement is not assigned to it. 

Resolution

For Cause #1: If you have only Cloud Foundry sub-accounts, follow steps below:

  1. Create the Subaccount in NEO environment
    • NEO environment are regions provided by SAP.
    • These regions have only 1 digit, example: eu1, eu2, eu3...
      • In comparison, CF regions have 2 digits: eu10, eu11, eu20...
  2. Assign the entitlement for "SAP SuccessFactors EC Service Center (Preview)" OR "SAP SuccessFactors EC Service Center (Production)" service to this NEO environment Subaccount
    • If you are unable to locate the entitlement, you must reach the SAP Account Owner: 0002626344 - Who is the SAP Account Manager/Executive for my company?
      If this is for a TDD account, then tdd@sap.com needs to be reached instead of the SAP Account Owner
       
  3. Then you can proceed with the steps described at 'Subscribing to the HR Services Application on SAP Business Technology Platform'

For Cause #2: If you already have a Neo Sub-Account, please follow also the steps above, starting from Step 2.
