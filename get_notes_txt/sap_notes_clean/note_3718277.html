Symptom

The status of the electronic document remains “Acknowledged by Rec. Acc. Point” in the eDocument Cockpit or Manage Electronic Documents app, without any further update such as Accepted or Rejected by Receiver.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition
  • SAP Document and Reporting Compliance, Cloud Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to the eDocument cockpit application.
  2. Observe that the status of the eDocument is "Acknowledged by Rec. Acc. Point". 

Cause

This is standard and expected system behavior for the status to remain as “Acknowledged by Rec. Acc. Point”. 

Resolution

  • The receiver access point must enable Message Level Response (MLR) to send validation feedback. If MLR is not enabled or not supported by the receiver, the final status will remain “Acknowledged by Rec. Acc. Point” and will not change to Accepted or Rejected by Receiver Access Point or show a validation error.
  • For more details, refer to the blog: Message Types: Transport Acknowledgement, MLR, BLR.
