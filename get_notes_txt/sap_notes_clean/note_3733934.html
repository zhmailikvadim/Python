Symptom

 In the process "Sending of 55643 from Responsible MOS to DSO" (DE_B_MDC_55643_MOS2DSO_SEND), the system cannot determine the business partner (BP) number for the MOSB partner.

This issue is relevant for the market roles: MOS, DSO.

Other Terms

MDC, DE_B_MDC_55643_MOS2DSO_SEND, business partner number, MOSB partner, /US4G/CL_UBD_DATA_ADDRESS, GET_BP_FROM_MOSB_CONTRA_W_FUT, 55643

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because the condition was incorrect when reading the internal table.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system determines the BP number correctly. You need to retrigger the process. You can verify the fix by checking that the BP number is determined successfully.
