Symptom

  • After opening MKV Cloud, selecting any app tile results in an error popup:
    • “Fehler – Die App konnte leider nicht geöffnet werden. Details anzeigen”
    • Details sometimes show: “Das Navigationsziel ‘…-manage?app-id=…’ konnte nicht aufgelöst werden.”
  • The issue is intermittent; when it occurs, all apps are affected.
  • Switching browsers or clearing cache does not help.

Environment

MCM Cloud

Reproducing the Issue

  • Steps:
    1. Log in to the MKV Cloud Launchpad (any of the URLs above).
    2. Click any app tile (e.g., Messkonzeptmodelle verwalten, Messkonzeptinstanzen anzeigen).
    3. Expected: The app opens.
    4. Actual: Error popup “Die App konnte leider nicht geöffnet werden.” Details may include “Das Navigationsziel … konnte nicht aufgelöst werden.”
  • Note: The behavior is intermittent; when it occurs, all apps in the session fail to open.

Cause

  • Known defect in the current MCM Cloud build that intermittently prevents FLP navigation target resolution, causing apps not to open and showing “Die App konnte leider nicht geöffnet werden.”
  • Root cause has been identified by development; fix is in delivery.

Resolution

  • A hotfix is being delivered and is expected to be available by Monday, March 23, 2026.
  • No customer-side workaround at this time; browser change and cache clearing do not help.
  • Recommendations:
    • If the issue occurs, try again later (intermittent behavior).
    • After the hotfix is deployed, please re-test by logging in fresh and opening several apps. If the issue persists, open a new ticket with exact timestamps and app names.

Keywords
