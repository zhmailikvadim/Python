Symptom

Is it possible to mass update field in Contract Terms such as Bill To or other data?

Environment

  • SAP Ariba Sourcing Contracts
  • SAP Ariba Buying & Invoicing

Resolution

This is not support. 

Contract terms documents—including tables, structured terms, clauses, and fields like Bill To embedded within them—are excluded from mass‑update capabilities.

The solution is to update fields manually.
