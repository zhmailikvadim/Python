Symptom

SAP Payroll has made provision for the following payroll frequencies:

1. Weekly

2. Monthly

3. Bi-Weekly (except ETI calculation)

Template Payroll areas delivered by SAP:

Payroll Area Period Parameter Name
W0 16

Monthly - ZA
W1 17

Weekly - ZA
W2 18

Bi-weekly - ZA

 

Other Terms

Weekly, bi-weekly, fortnightly, monthly, payroll frequency, South Africa localization,

Reason and Prerequisites

Announcement

Solution

Legal functionality delivered by SAP South Africa Localization addresses weekly, bi-weekly and monthly payroll frequencies.
