Symptom

When using the standard function MDI Business Scenarios: Workforce Replication from Employee Central (EC) to SAP Master Data Integration (MDI), applying a Legal Entity Filter for a Company results in the error: "No adequate employment found because of skipped assignment" for some employees. 

You wish to replicate only employees for specific Company/Legal Entity. 

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite
  • S4 Hana
  • Master Data Integration (Business Scenarios)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Set up integration between SuccessFactors Employee Central and S/4 HANA using SAP Master Data Integration (MDI).
  2. Use the standard function Business Scenario - Workforce Replication from EC to SAP MDI.
  3. Run the replication with a Legal Entity Filter for a Company.
  4. Observe the error message: "No adequate employment found because of skipped assignment"

Cause

  • The engineering team confirmed that the Legal Entity filter is not officially supported by SAP Master Data Integration (MDI).
  • The error message "No adequate employment found because of skipped assignment" is caused by skipped assignments, such as missing job information, employees terminated before the cutoff date, or employees with multiple employments in different legal entities.

Resolution

In order to solve this issue, please use the recommended approach, which is to send all data to SAP Master Data Integration (MDI) and apply filters on the consumer side.

Hence, configure the MDO/BDO distribution pull model for S/4 HANA to read data in S/4 and include only the necessary legal entities required for your business needs.

Later on, you can adjust or remove the filters on the consumer side when additional countries or legal entities are included/needed in S/4 HANA in the future.
