Symptom

Is not possible to open a form, error:

Looks like something went wrong
Please try again or contact your administrator for help.

Error just happens when have the latest experience of Performance Management enabled.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Performance Management

Reproducing the Issue

When you open the form error below is showed:

Looks like something went wrong
Please try again or contact your administrator for help.

Cause

Missing translations for En_US for rating scale.

Resolution

Is required to add English US translation for rating scale, after this is required to relaunch the form to translation take effect.

Keywords

Latest experience, form open, issue to open form, error page, contact support
