Symptom

You declare your exports from SAP GTS to the Belgian customs system AES.

In the export declaration, packages can be reported together as supplementary items and non-supplementary items for one item until the end of the transition phase in AES (see SAP Note 3569364). After the end of the transition phase, this is no longer permitted, which means that for such a package constellation, the packages must be split in the item so that the supplementary items are reported in a separate item.

Currently, the system only takes into account that the gross weight is to be reported with 0 KG for items that contain at least one supplementary item. When you do this, an error may occur that causes the gross weight to not be adjusted correctly.

This leads to an error in the incompleteness check with reference to the rule B2101.
