Symptom

How can I configure the Can participants submit tie bids rule so that the first supplier who submits a tie bid appears as rank 1?

Resolution

In the Bidding Rules section of the template, set the Can participants submit tie bids rule to Break tie bids by submit time.

With this configuration, the supplier who submits the tie bid first will be suggested as Rank 1 in the scenario created by the system.

See Also

For more information about this rule, you can check Can participants submit tie bids.
