Symptom

 The Internal learning requests waiting for my approval, Joule incorrectly responds "No requests need your approval right now. Let’s try something else"

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Learning

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open Joule.
  2. Goto My Learning -> Pending Approval
  3. Enter the prompt: "Show the internal learning requests waiting for my approval".
  4. Observe the system response: "No requests need your approval right now. Let’s try something else".

Cause

Approvals feature for BizX Homepage cards are not enabled.

Resolution

Enable the Approvals feature for the BizX Homepage cards and the issue should be resolved
