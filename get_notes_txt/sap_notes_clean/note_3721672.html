Symptom

All documents are displayed in the Linked Documents tab even after selecting the option Show Documents Related to the BP for business partner (BP) activities.

Reproducing the issue

Prerequisites:

Working with SAP Business One or SAP Business One for SAP HANA, Version 10.0 FP 2602.

Scenario:

  1. Open Business Partners → Activity.
  2. Create a new activity for a BP.
  3. Go to the Linked Documents tab.
  4. Select a document type.
  5. Select the option Show Documents Related to the BP.
  6. Tab out of the Document Number field.

Expected behavior:

Only documents linked to the current BP are listed.

Actual behavior:

Documents linked to other BPs are also listed.
