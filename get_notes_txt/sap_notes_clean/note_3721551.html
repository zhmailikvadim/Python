Symptom

The technical user SAP_SYSTEM is making updates to Opportunities, which including deleting important IDs and changing sales data. 

Environment

  • SAP cloud for Customer

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the C4C system.
  2. Go to the Sales Work center.
  3. Open the Opportunities View.
  4. Open the Opportunity "XXXX".
  5. Check under "Changes" tab and observe that there are several changes made by technical SAP_SYSTEM.

Cause

The scoping question "Do you want to automatically re-determine opportunity parties based on changes to the account team of the associated account" is checked in your business configuration. 

Resolution

Uncheck the scoping question Do you want to automatically re-determine opportunity parties based on changes to the account team of the associated account in your Business Configuration.

Keywords
