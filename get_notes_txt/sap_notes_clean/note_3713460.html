Symptom

  • Promoting job containing Web Intelligence Reports based on SAP BW universe generates lcmbiar file with significant size as compared with previous releases
  • For instance, significant size differences between SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 4.3 Support Package 04 Patch 7 and SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 2025 Patch 7 can be observed

Other Terms

webi, webintelligence, BEX, query, BICS, UNX, promotion, management, promote, CMC, Central, Console

Reason and Prerequisites

Environment:

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 2025 Patch 7 
  • Central management Console
  • Web Intelligence

Steps To Reproduce:

  1. In SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 4.3 Support Package 04 Patch 7, create a Web Intelligence document based on SAP BW universe
  2. Purge and save the document
  3. Log on to Central Management Console and use Promotion Management to promote LCMBIAR file containing this document  
  4. Import the lcmbiar in SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 2025 Patch 7 Central Management Console
  5. Log on to BI Launchpad and open the imported Web Intelligence document
  6. Refresh, purge and save the document
  7. Log on to Central Management Console and use Promotion Management to promote LCMBIAR file containing this saved document
    1. >> Notice that the size of this LCMBIAR file is much greater than the size of LCMBIAR file generated in step 3.

Cause:

  • This issue is caused by a defect in the product

Solution

This issue is fixed in the patches listed in the "Support Packages & Patches" section below.
Due to different internal processes, the "Support Packages & Patches" section of the SAP Note will be populated as below:

  • for fixes delivered in "Support Packages": immediately (even if the "Support Package" is planned to be released several months later)
  • for fixes delivered in "Patches": ONLY once the "Patch" is released AND available on SMP (not before)

For Business Intelligence Platform maintenance schedule and strategy see the Knowledge Base Article 2144559 in References section.
