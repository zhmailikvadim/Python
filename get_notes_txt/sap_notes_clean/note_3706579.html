Symptom

When you generate a monthly DSN using the P26V01 version for an employee having a complementary or additional payroll, the system displays the following DSN-Val error 51.002/CCH-13:
"Si la rubrique « Type - S21.G00.51.011 » est renseignée avec la valeur « 002 - Salaire brut servant aux calculs des droits de l'Assurance chômage », et, si la rubrique « Montant - S21.G00.51.013 » est différente de « 0.00 », et, si la Date de fin du contrat est renseignée dans un bloc « Fin du contrat - S21.G00.62 » avec une valeur renseignée différente de « 099 – annulation » au niveau de la rubrique « Motif de la rupture du contrat – S21.G00.62.002 », la date de fin de période doit être inférieure ou égale au surlendemain de la date de fin de contrat.
Ce contrôle ne s'applique qu'aux dates de fin de période incluses dans le mois principal déclaré, et, il ne s'applique pas lorsqu'une annulation de fin de contrat est déclarée."

Other Terms

RPLDSNF0

Reason and Prerequisites

Your payroll is not compliant with regulations because you have entered or transferred a gross salary for calculating unemployment insurance rights during the main declared month (MPD) for an employee without an active contract.

However, Decree 2016-1567 of November 21, 2016, which came into effect on January 1, 2018, requires that amounts paid after the termination of the employee's contract be associated with the employee's last work period. Contributions are calculated according to the basis, rate, and ceiling rules applicable during the employee's last work period.

Furthermore, unemployment insurance regulations require that remunerations be declared in the original/employment period.

Solution

After having installed this SAP Note, the system displays an error message in the DSN protocol to indicate non-compliance in your payroll.

The system transfers the declaration of gross salary used to calculate unemployment insurance rights for the main declared month to the employee's final work period. It modifies in the DSN the dates of block 51 - Remuneration of type 002 (Gross salary used for calculating unemployment insurance rights) from the current period (MPD) to the employee's final work period.

This modification of the dates in block 51 allows for passing Dsn-Val checks on block 51 type 002 without being blocked, thereby enabling you to submit your DSN. However, it does not correct your payroll or your declaration, as block 78 - Taxable base remains declared in MPD with erroneous rules of basis, rates, and ceiling.

Caution:
Modification by the system constitutes an exceptional means allowing you to submit your DSN within the deadlines in case of blockage or specific situations, such as the absence of historical payroll data due to a change in payroll software.
Bring your payroll setup into compliance with regulations as soon as possible. To do this, allocate the amounts paid after the termination of the employee's contract to their last work period and do not transfer them to the current period.

Attention:
If you are not in the specific situation of lacking historical payroll data mentioned above, you cannot, under any circumstances, choose to continue not applying the regulations.
SAP's responsibility in the event of non-compliance of your payroll and DSN cannot be engaged because SAP has delivered the necessary developments and instructions for the implementation of Decree 2016-1567 in SAP Note 2554008.
