Symptom

You have a query related to a configuration for the certificates maintained in the BTP in order to properly submit the eDocuments in SAP S/4HANA Public Cloud environment.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Cause

The certificate configuration is not a service delivered by SAP and it is recommended to follow the consulting SAP Note 2706322 - What is Support – What is Consulting: Cloud Solutions. These processes are not requirements from SAP, they are requirements from Country Tax Authorities. SAP Support does not provide support for Certificate Consulting topics.

Resolution

For questions related to Certificate Implementation or Configuration, please reach out to your SAP Consultant to ensure the setup aligns with your specific business needs.
