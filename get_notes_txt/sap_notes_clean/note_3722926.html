Symptom

Starting from release S/4H 2025 it is possible to split consumption quantities during goods issue postings from EWM production supply areas. But this new feature was not available for batch managed materials.

Other Terms

Synchronous Goods Issue, Partial Quantity, MIGO, CO11N, CORK, MFBF

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing Feature

Solution

After applying the correction instruction a split of the consumption quantity will also be done for batch managed materials. The quantity split applies for batches that were already predefined for the component. If during the posting of the confirmation a batch determination is done, then the system will already check for available quantities at the production supply area.
