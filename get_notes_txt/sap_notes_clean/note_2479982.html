Symptom

  • You create a follow up quotation from an opportunity and the price determination is not copied over
  • In a follow up transaction the Price List, Price Group and Customer Group is blank / empty

Environment

  • SAP Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP CRM
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP CRM, version for SAP HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create an Opportunity
  2. Create a follow up Quotation from this Opportunity
  3. Open the assignment block 'Price Details' in the Quotation
  4. Price List, Price Group and Customer Group is empty

Cause

Price_list, Cust_group and Price_group are empty in the opportunity and so cannot be copied over to the follow up quotation.

Resolution

There are 2 steps to complete to resolve this issue:

Step 1:

  1. Adjust the copy control settings from the opportunity's item category to the quotation's item category.
  2. Change the Copy Condition to "Do not copy" or "Only copy manual conditions".

Step 2:

  1. Implement BADI CRM_COPY_BADI method PRICING to prevent the copy.
  2. Remove price_list, cust_group and price_group from input_field_names.
