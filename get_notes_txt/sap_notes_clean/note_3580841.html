Symptom

Several calculated members are calculated in an MDX statement. No data is displayed for some lines even though corresponding data exists in the system.

Other Terms

Missing data calculated member MDX hierarchy cells

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

This problem occurs in very specific circumstances. Among other things, for the MDX hierarchy for which the calculated members are calculated, only these calculated members are displayed in the result and no members of this MDX hierarchy that exist directly in the InfoProvider.
