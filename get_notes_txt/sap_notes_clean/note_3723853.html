Symptom

  • When trying to create a Brazilian Sales Order, using transaction VA01, it is appearing the following warning message:
     VH807 - Error during transfer of tax condition ICM0
  • The condition mentioned in the message appear in the pricing, under the conditions tab, highlighted in red and without a value.
  • The warning message may appear for other tax conditions

“Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.“

Environment

  • Localization Brazil
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA


Cause

Flagging condition types in the SD tax code, under the MM tax code, triggers the error: "VH807 - Error during transfer of tax condition ICM0"

Resolution

Follow these steps to adjust the customization:

1. Open transaction J1BTAX.
2. Navigate to Tax Calculation -> Tax Codes MM.
3. Enter the pricing name in the dialog.
4. Select the SD tax code.
5. Go to the menu path: Goto -> Maintain Conditions.
6. Uncheck the Active flag for all conditions.
7. Save the changes.

This adjustment will help ensure proper functionality and prevent errors related to condition transfer.
