Symptom

SAP Note 3707437 contained a recommendation, how to deal with a retroactive change of the threshold Limit Not Taxable Work-Home (T511P-BESA2).

Based on new information, the conclusion is that this recommendation is not correct. There is no legislative basis for the recalculation of the payroll and it can lead to unwanted recalculations or inconsistencies in payroll. (i.e. changes in assignment, no recalculation for terminated employees). For the withholding tax calculation in payroll the value of 500.00 EUR need to be used, whereas for the tax declaration the posted amount of 490.00 EUR must be taken into account.

The new recommendation is to keep the constant T511P-BESA2 at 500.00 EUR for 2025. If an adjustment of the tax declaration is required, the recommendation is to use infotype 3207 to declare the amount above the 490.00 EUR.

Other Terms

BESA2, T511P
