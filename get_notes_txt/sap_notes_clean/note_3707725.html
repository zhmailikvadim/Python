Symptom

You are using app F6678 with Safety Precaution after implementing Note #3620222. You get short dump when you save a Safety Certificate with Safety Precaution added.

Other Terms

EAM, WCM, F6678, Safety Certificate, PTW, Permit to work, Safety Precaution Item, Safety Precaution Group category, VSFTYPRCAUTNGRP, RAISE_SHORTDUMP

Reason and Prerequisites

This is due to the key field 'group category' missing from CDS view I_SftyCertSafetyPrecaution.

Solution

The missing projection was added.
Please implement the corrections found in this SAP Note or upgrade to equivalent Support Pack.
