Symptom

In VF04, a document can be selected/processed for billing. However, the background job for billing cannot be created for this document in VF06.

Environment

  • Sales And Distribution (SD)
  • Logistics Execution (LE)
  • SAP R/3
  • SAP R/3 Enterprise 4.7
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP Enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to VF04, fill SD document number, billing date as blank
  2. Click on the "Display Billing List" button -> the document is showing for billing
  3. Go to VF06, fill SD document number, billing date as blank
  4. Click on the "Execute" button -> the background job for billing is not created

Cause

In VF06, when billing date is not entered on the selection screen, the "billing date to" is automatically set as today's date.

If the billing date of document is later than today's date (a future date), the document won't be selected/processed and the background job won't be created.

Resolution

Fill "billing date to" in VF06.

See Also

Note 2206926 - VF04/VF06 accepts billing date from > billing date to
