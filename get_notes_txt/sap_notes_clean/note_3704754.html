Symptom

If a Workflow Administrator forwards the Purchase Order (PO) task to another user, the system does not populate the APPROVEDBYUSERFULLNAME field in the CDS view C_PurOrdWorkflowApprovalEmail.

Environment

  • Materials Management (MM)
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Fiori

Reproducing the Issue

1. SWIA forward final approval step to any user.
2. in email, field APPROVEDBYUSERFULLNAME is empty.

Cause

missing configuration.

Resolution

Su01->documentation-> description->username.

Keywords

SU01, APPROVEDBYUSERFULLNAME, C_PurOrdWorkflowApprovalEmail, forward, SWIA
