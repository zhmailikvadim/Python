Symptom

During the import of a PAIN.001.001.02 file in Advanced Payment Management, the tags CdtrRef and RfrdDocNb are not mapped to the corresponding fields.

Other Terms

/PF1/CL_IPM_PAIN_001_001_002, MAP_RMTINF

Reason and Prerequisites

 The issue was caused by an incorrect mapping of the CdtrRef and RfrdDocNb tags.

Solution

The issue has been resolved by correcting the mapping of the CdtrRef and RfrdDocNb elements during PAIN.001.001.02 file import in Advanced Payment Management.
