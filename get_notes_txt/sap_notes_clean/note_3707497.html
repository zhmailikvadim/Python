Symptom

When a goods movement is triggered for clinical trial related distribution documents (such as Sales Order, Stock Transfer Order, Outbound Delivery, Inbound Delivery), the system also triggers queued processing to update the PCN status, which is not applicable to these documents. Sometimes the blocks subsequent, regular queued RFC processing due to the use of the same queue name.

Other Terms

ISLS, Manufacturing, Intelligent Clinical Supply Management, Manage PCNs, QRFC, queued RFC

Reason and Prerequisites

You use SAP Intelligent Clinical Supply Management or this SAP Note is a prerequisite of another SAP Note.

Solution

Implement the attached correction.
