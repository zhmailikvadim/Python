Symptom

The table DDNTF doesn't exist any more at Database level after the system is upgraded to S/4HANA 2023.

Environment

S/4HANA >=2023

Reproducing the Issue

Upgrade a system to S/4HANA 2023

Cause

The information in DDNTF is merged into DDNTT.

Resolution

It's not an issue.

The information in DDNTF is merged into DDNTT since S/4HANA 2023. 

Meanwhile, the table DDNTF is removed from the system.
