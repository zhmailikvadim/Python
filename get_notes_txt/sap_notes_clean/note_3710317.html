Symptom

 The Blanket Agreement’s Cumulative Quantity is higher than the Planned Quantity.

Environment

SAP Business One 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a Blanket Agreement. (AP or AR)
  2. Go to Purchasing - A/P - Purchase Blanket Agreement. (BA)
  3. Add an item with Planned Quantity of 10. Save the BA.
  4. Create a GRPO linked to the Blanket Agreement with quantity 5.
  5. Verify cumulative quantity on the Blanket Agreement=5
  6. Create a A/P Invoice linked to the Blanket Agreement with quantity 10.
  7. Verify cumulative quantity on the Blanket Agreement=15
  8. Create a Purchase Order linked to the Blanket Agreement with quantity 10.
  9. Copy the Purchase Order to a GRPO, before adding, change the quantity to 20.
  10. Verify cumulative quantity on the Blanket Agreement=35

    Cause

    Correct Behavior

    Resolution

    Blanket Agreements add up quantities from all linked documents. If you receive more than planned, the Cumulative Quantity can exceed the Planned Quantity. The Blanket Agreement does not stop additional receipts once the planned quantity in the Blanket Agreement is reached.
