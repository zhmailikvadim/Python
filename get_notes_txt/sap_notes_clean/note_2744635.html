Symptom

In transaction /SCWM/PACK, user collapses all HUs in order to hide their stock contents.
Upon saving, the tree control (left portion of the screen) automatically expands and stocks are shown again.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems.
Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management (SCM-EWM);
  • SAP Extended Warehouse Management Work Center (SCM-EWM-WC);
  • SAP S/4HANA;
  • SAP NetWeaver.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to /SCWM/PACK;
  2. Open a Workcenter;
  3. Collapse all nodes;
  4. Click on the Save button;
  5. Observe that the nodes will be expanded automatically.

Cause

Customizing

Resolution

The tree control is customized on path:

Open SPRO
SCM Extended Warehouse Management
Extended Warehouse Management
Master Data
Work Center
Specify Work Center Layout

Once in the customizing screen, double click on work center line and navigate to 'Tree' Section.
There is a field named 'Expand Tree Control' (TREE_EXP), that controls how many levels are expanded when you open the work center:

Certainly, here user is only defining the work center layout structure.
Thus, this layout must be assigned to the desired work center under customizing:

Open SPRO
SCM Extended Warehouse Management
Extended Warehouse Management
Master Data
Work Center
Define Work Center
