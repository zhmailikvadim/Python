Symptom

  1. A new role has been created with access to the Fiori app 'Schedule Job for consolidation Tasks' - F5572.
  2. Failed to restrict the role such as make this role can only run the parameter 'SAP_FIN_CS_REALIGNMENT_FUNCTION'.

Environment

  • SAP Fiori for SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance for Group Reporting

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create a new role and provide access to the Fiori app 'Schedule Job for consolidation Tasks' - F5572.
  2. Attempt to restrict the role.

Resolution

For the App F5572, currently there is no way to restrict authorization to a specific job template. This is system standard behavior.

Keywords

schedule job for consolidation tasks, f5572, restrict role, authorization, job template restriction
