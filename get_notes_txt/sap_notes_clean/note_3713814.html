Symptom

You are using the Supply Assignment(ARun) functionality to reserve the stock against the requirement documents.

You perform transfer posting process for the ARun relevant materials to move stock from one storage location to another. Also you have the same stock assigned to requirement documents.

For transfer Posting involving storage location change your customization is maintained as NO ACTION. Hence, in such cases the assigned quantities should be unassigned.

You notice in certain cases, the unassignment process does not completely de-allocate the stock. This leads to overassignment of the stock and can lead to inconsistencies in the ARun process.

Other Terms

Supply Assignment(ARun), Transfer Posting, Unassignment Process, 321, 322

Reason and Prerequisites

Prerequisites:

For transfer Posting involving storage location change your customization is maintained as NO ACTION

Reasons:

This is due to the program error.

Solution

Please implement the SAP Note to get the solution. You can also upgrade your system to the relevant support package.
