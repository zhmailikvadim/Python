Symptom

You activate an integration model for classes and characteristics through a transaction CFM2 (or call the transaction CFM9) to send your classes and characteristics from your ECC or S4 system to your SCM or PPDS system but you face an error message CLMM 809 "Class &1 of class type 230 could not be created" in an inbound queue of a target SCM or PPDS system.

Other Terms

CLMM809, /SAPAPO/CHECK_INCON_TCL_CL_CHR

Reason and Prerequisites

Class data inconsistency in your SCM or PPDS system.

Solution

Implement attached correction instructions or install a relevant Support Pack in your system.
