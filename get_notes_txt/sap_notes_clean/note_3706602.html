Symptom

In an SAP liveCache-based Product Availability Check for Advanced ATP (aATP), inconsistent results can occur when quality inspection stock is included in the scope of check. This happens because incorrect quantities are written to SAP liveCache during postings of quality inspection stock.

Other Terms

INSMK, CL_ATP_PAC_LC_AGGR_INSPLOT, aATP, SAP liveCache

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error.

Solution

Implement the correction instructions.
