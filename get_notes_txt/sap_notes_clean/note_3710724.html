Symptom

When changing Business Partner master data in a Portugal system with Portugal Digital Signature active for digitally signed invoices, the system blocks the change by raising error SIPT 663 or triggers a short dump.

Environment

  • SAP ERP

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Business Partner transaction.
  2. Attempt to change the VAT registration number for a customer (for example, customer number XXXXX).
  3. The system blocks the change and raises error message SIPT663, SIPT612, or SIPT613, indicating that the VAT registration number cannot be changed.

Cause

Error SIPT 663 occurs because the Portugal Digital Signature framework applies strict VAT registration number validation to Business Partner master data, even in scenarios where the Business Partner is not relevant for Portuguese tax reporting.

Resolution

Error SIPT 663 is triggered by the Portugal Digital Signature framework, which enforces strict validation of VAT registration numbers for Business Partners that are relevant for Portuguese tax reporting.

To resolve this issue, implement the correction instructions provided in the SAP Note below. In addition, review and adjust your system configuration to ensure that Portugal Digital Signature validations are applied only to Portuguese company codes and sites.

This configuration ensures that VAT validations are executed only where legally required, preventing the error from occurring in non-Portugal scenarios.

3497275 - Digital Signature Portugal: Maintain Business Partner Master Data – Error Message SIPT 663 Raised in SIPT_BTE_00001340
