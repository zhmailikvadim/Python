Symptom

The procurement proposal dates are different between single-item planning and total planning in MRP Live.

Other Terms

MD01N, MRP Live, receipts, dates, quantity, PERAZ, LGKND MTO, lot-size, short term planning, available dates, Make to Order, Lot Size: MtO, Lot size in short term, no.of.periods.

Reason and Prerequisites

Pre-requisite: Periodic lot size maintained in short term horizon with no.of.periods greater than '1' and Lot size in Make to Order production is maintained as '2'- Lot size as in short term period.

Reason: The lot-size period calculation is not determined by the planning date. Instead, it is based on the earliest requirement date, which leads to inconsistent dates whenever the material is planned in MRP Live.

Solution

The SAP Note evaluates the MTO segment and determines the lot-size periods based on the planning date, consistent with the standard scenario.
