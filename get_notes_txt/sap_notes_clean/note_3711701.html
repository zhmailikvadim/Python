Symptom

You are using the Advanced Scheduling Board application in Production Planning and Detailed Scheduling and you may notice the following symptoms:

1. Shape selection not retained after adding/removing charts (2025 FPS1).

2. Search results do not automatically expand in the Product Chart when the Resource Chart is also open (till 2023 FPS0).

3. Incorrect Source of supply error message is shown in other languages (2025 FPS1).

4. After resetting the simulation session, opening Network Charts results in an error indicating that the simulation session is inactive (2025 FPS1).
