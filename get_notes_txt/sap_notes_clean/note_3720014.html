Symptom

In HANA Studio (SAP HANA Development perspective) -> Repositories, when attempting to open a HANA system, the following error is displayed: 

You are not authorized to execute this action; you do not have the required privileges.


 "Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

SAP HANA, platform edition 2.0

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open SAP HANA Studio (SAP HANA Development perspective)
  2. Navigate to the "Repositories" tab.
  3. Attempt to open a HANA system.

Cause

The user lacks the required privileges.

Resolution

Grant the "MODELING" role to the user.

After granting the role, the error should no longer appear and it displays as below.

Keywords

HANA Studio, repository, repositories, You are not authorized to execute this action, you do not have the required privileges
