Symptom

  • While migrating from ECC to S/4HANA, the standard structure RIWO1 has the append structure IST_REFOBJECT_IO missing in S/4HANA.
  • Fields in the append structure IST_REFOBJECT_IO are used in a custom Adobe form, resulting in a dump in S/4HANA due to the absence of the append structure in S/4HANA.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA, release IS-UT 801 and higher

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Migrate from ECC to S/4HANA.
  2. Check the standard structure RIWO1 in S/4HANA.
  3. Observe that the append structure IST_REFOBJECT_IO is missing in S/4HANA.
  4. Use a custom Adobe form that relies on fields from IST_REFOBJECT_IO.
  5. Encounter a dump due to the missing append structure in S/4HANA.

Cause

  • The append structure IST_REFOBJECT_IO exists in ECC systems but does not exist in S/4HANA systems because it was deprecated.
  • All IST_*related structures in package EET_CORE_MOD were deprecated during the migration to S/4HANA. 

Resolution

  1. Check if fields COUNTRY_CODE, AREA_CODE, and TELNUM are available in another related object that can be used. 
  2. Adapt the custom Adobe form to utilize alternative fields.
