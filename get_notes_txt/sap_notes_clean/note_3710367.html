Symptom

Attempting to remove a single participant user from the section of a survey results in the removal of the entire participants list. The issue occurs when editing the survey in the supplier performance management project.

Environment

SPM Projects

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the Supplier Performance Management project.
  2. Edit the Survey.
  3. Go to the "Content" tab and edit a section.
  4. Attempt to remove one participant from the list.

Cause

The participants were set as a Response Team in the survey. When participants are grouped as a Response Team, removing one participant results in the removal of the entire team due to the bundling functionality.

Resolution

  1. Open and edit the survey document.
  2. Navigate to the Participants tab.
  3. Select all users listed in the participant list.
  4. Click on Set/Clear > Response Team.
  5. Ensure the Response Team column displays No for all users.
  6. Navigate to the Content tab.
  7. Edit the Section.
  8. Select the user to be removed and click Remove.
  9. The selected user will be removed from the participant list without affecting other participants.
