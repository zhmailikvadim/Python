Symptom

After you have posted valuation asset cash flows with a due date in a closed fiscal year (backdated), you experience unexpected asset depreciation planned values in AW01n.

Other Terms

IFRS leasing, AW01N, RoU

Reason and Prerequisites

You use RE-FX Asset Accounting integration.

The historical processing of the contract valuation does not conform to consulting note 2662137 Point 28. Can open contract valuations be posted or reversed in a (partially) closed fiscal year?.

Once the month-end closing or year-end closing has been performed, subsequent valuations should always be carried out with a start of consideration in opened period and not retroactively.

Solution

Reprocess existing valuations as per note 2662137 Point 14. Recalculation of existing valuations starting from the backdated valuation step. Make sure you use a new time slot in the opened fiscal year.

In case the backdated asset posting lies in a closed fiscal year that cannot be reopened (usually for accounting reasons):

  • Terminate the contract and retire the asset as per 2662137 Point 1. How do I enter an early termination in the system?.
  • Recreate the contract with a new asset.

Please also consult note 2662137 Point 14. When do I have to use a new time slot for a valuation change….and when do I not have to?.

Please note a proper contract valuation processing is under customer responsibility. You must take organizational measure in order to ensure the issue does not occur again in the future.
