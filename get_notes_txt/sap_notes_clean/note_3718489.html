Symptom

After migrating Fiori content from groups to spaces & pages.

Is there a way to mass delete all the groups?

Environment

SAP S/4HANA

Resolution

There is no option to mass delete groups.

Only option is to delete them in the Fiori Designer

  1. Choose a group from the group list in the left panel.
  2. Drag and drop the selected group in the trash zone.

    A Confirmation dialog box appears.

  3. Choose OK.
