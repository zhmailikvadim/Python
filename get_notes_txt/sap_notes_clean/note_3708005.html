Symptom

The following error messages are raised after recheck a customs document or transfer a customs document:

Unable to determine the relevant number to depreciate.

Quantities were not written off completely.

Other Terms

Message No. /SAPSLL/CON_LDET139, Message No. /SAPSLL/CON_LDET110, STCTS, CCNGN

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Apply correction instructions in this note or upgrade to relevant SP.
