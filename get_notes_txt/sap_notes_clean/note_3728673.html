Symptom

There is no option to determine which splitted non-cash flow item belongs to which coinsurance partner ( field COINSS_ID ).

Other Terms

Basis line of business

Coinsurance

Distribution plan

Coinsurer

Non-cash flow

Reason and Prerequisites

You use a distribution plan.

Missing functionality.

Solution

This Note provides:

A new method /PM0/CL_AB_INT_AC->MODIFY_COINSDOC_NC. This method will get all relevant data and references, so that you can get all needed information.
The information can then be placed in customer fields of the relevant non-cash flow and you can determine which splitted non-cash flow item belongs to which coinsurance partner.

Adjustments in method SPLIT_NC_DOCS_FOR_COINSURANCE.

New method SET_DOCNRNC4COINS, which serves as complete method for not splitted and splitted nc documents.

New method SET_PSSTATE4COINS_NC, which serves as complete method for orginal nc documents.



The following examples are intended to explain the situation in more detail:

  • There is option to add information from the distribution plan to non-cash flow coinsurance documents.
    • You use a distribution plan with two participants - a direct participation and an indirect participation - but the same coinsurer in both cases.
    • You are now able to use the coinsurance key in the non-cash flow coinsurance document to determine which participant formed the basis for the non-cash flow coinsurance document.
      As a result, you can determine whether the non-cash flow coinsurance document belongs to a direct participation or an indirect participation, for example.
