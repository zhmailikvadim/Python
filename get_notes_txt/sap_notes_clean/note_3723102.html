Symptom

Unable to delete a Custom Business Object (CBO) in  SAP S/4HANA system. The CBO appears to be locked or has references preventing deletion.

Environment

S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the Custom Business Object maintenance screen.

  2. Attempt to delete CBO.

  3. System displays error "Deletion failed"

Cause

The CBO can't be deleted due to a known error with the deletion of the change document object belonging to the CBO. 

Resolution

The problem is solved with release 2602.

When your system has been upgraded to release 2602 you will be able to delete the CBO together with its change document object.

Keywords

CBO object, cannot be deleted, Customer business object , CBO, change document
