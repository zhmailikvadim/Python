Symptom

Requirement to monitor the status of scheduled import/export jobs collectively across various models in SAC

Environment

SAP Analytics Cloud 2026.2.6

Resolution

  1. Login to SAC 
  2. Navigate to the Connections --> Schedule Status. 
  3. Monitor the status of scheduled import/export jobs collectively across various models.
  4. If errors or warnings occur, select the job to view detailed information in the Data Timeline panel.
