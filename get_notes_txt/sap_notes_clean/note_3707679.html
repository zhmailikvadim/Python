Symptom

The "Team Calibration Summary" appears in the To-do list for all managers, that are participants of a calibration session.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Calibration

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Proxy as a calibration session participant, who is a calibrated employee's manager.
  2. Navigate to the To-do items.
  3. Observe the "Team Calibration Summary" appearing in the To-do list.

Cause

As part of the Intelligent Services framework, when the calibration session is activated, managers who are participants in the sessions receive a to-do item on the Team Calibration Summary tile. To receive the to-do item for calibration session activation, the data source of the session must be Performance Management.

Resolution

This is expected system behaviour per the guide Implementing and Managing Intelligent Services - Calibration:

You can enable and disable To-do notifications for calibration session activation following this guide: Enabling Notification on Team Calibration Summary Tile | SAP Help Portal.
