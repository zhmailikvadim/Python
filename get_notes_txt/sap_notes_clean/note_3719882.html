Symptom

After setting Quantity Scale in Condition Contract, condition is not triggered in Settlement Document item although the sum of all Settlement Document Item Quantity reached the Quantity Scale.

Environment

Condition Contract Management in SAP S/4HANA

Cause

Total quantity of business volume is not transferred to Settlement Document on header level during settlement run.

Resolution

Transaction WLFA -> Condition Contract Management -> Condition Contract Settlement -> Define and Configure Profiles for Business Volume Determination -> Double clicks on the relevant Profile -> Transfer Bus Volume Total Quantity, change it to Y Yes.

Keywords

Condition Contract, Quantity, Quantity Scale, Settlement document, condition, business volume
