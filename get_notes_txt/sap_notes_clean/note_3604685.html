Symptom

  • A request was made to expand the decimal places in the RESRTMSBRP_ALV structure field RENTPERCENT in Contract (RECN) to 4 digits.
  • The current configuration does not allow for the representation of share percentages with 2 digits and 4 decimals, such as 22.3458% or 22.234%.

Environment

  • Flexible Real Estate Management (RE-FX)
  • Contract and Lease Management
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Finance
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the RESRTMSBRP_ALV structure field RENTPERCENT in Contract (RECN).
  2. Attempt to expand the decimal places to 4 digits.
  3. Observe that the current configuration does not allow for the representation of share percentages with 4 decimals, such as 22.3458% or 22.234%.

Cause

The change in decimal places would impact further database tables, specifically VISRCALCDETAIL and VISRTMSBRPI, which is not allowed. Additionally, the data element RESRRENTPERCENT is utilized in various structures and BAPI interfaces, making the change more complex.

Resolution

  1. Implement BADI BADI_RESR_CALC_RENT, Method CALCULATE. This method allows modification of the result according to specific needs with a defined percentage rate.
  2. Enhance table VISRTMSBRPI over an APPEND for a certain field with the desired percentage rate. This field should then be available in parameter IT_SALESGRADING in the interface to BAdI-Method CALCULATE.
