Symptom

After a deal amend replacing the material the VLOGP Sales Quantity is not converted to base unit and remains in sales unit.

The same issue happens at VA43 Document Flow where the sales quantity was not converted do base unit.

Amending the deal a second time replacing the material will show the sales quantity correctly converted to base unit.

Sales unit is not defined for the material at material master.

Other Terms

VA43; VLOGP; sales quantity; sales unit; base unit; Deal Capture; DMBE

Reason and Prerequisites

Software error

Solution

Apply this SAP Note
