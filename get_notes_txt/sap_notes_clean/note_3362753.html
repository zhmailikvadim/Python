Symptom

Charges with an amount equal to zero are not displayed correctly. On the charges tab page of the building savings contract display (transaction /IBS/BSC_SHOW), values are missing for the date of the first and last execution for such charges.

Reason and Prerequisites

The charges are not displayed correctly because the displayed data is a mixture of charge and condition data. Since no conditions are created for charges with zero, their data is missing in the display. This is correct.

Solution

The display logic is adjusted so that all zero charges are hidden.
