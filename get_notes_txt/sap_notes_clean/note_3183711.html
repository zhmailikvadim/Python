Symptom

I'm a supplier based in Mexico and I send my customers CFDI invoices and payment receipts. How can I pull a list of invoices that do not have associated payment receipts yet?

Resolution

Suppliers in Mexico can generate reports in their SAP Business Network accounts to get visibility into payment receipts and their status, and also into invoices that don't have payment receipts associated yet.

In order to generate this invoice report in SAP Business Network, follow the steps below:

  1. Sign in to your SAP Business Network account.
  2. Click Reports.
  3. Click Create.
  4. Enter the required information to create a new report.
  5. Choose Invoice.
  6. Click Next.
  7. Select Manual Report.

The Criteria section will now be displayed.

  1. Select the Invoice Status.
  2. Check the box Invoices without Payment Receipts.
  3. Click Submit

Your report will be put into the reports queue, and processed shortly. You can view and download it when its status is Processed.

See Also

You can also generate payment receipts reports, including buyer details, payment dates, payment amounts, and statuses. Refer to How do I know if my CFDI payment receipts have been approved by my customers? Can I pull a report? for step by step instructions on how to do that.

See also NP-25590: Supplier reports for payment receipts in Mexico in What's new in SAP Ariba 2011 (Q4 2020) for more information on this feature.
