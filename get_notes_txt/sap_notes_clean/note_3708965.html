Symptom

In the 2505 GA release, a What's New guide was published stating that the use of the new domains would become mandatory on January 31, 2026 for for SAP Integration Suite, Managed Gateway for Spend Management and SAP Business Network solution. But this deadline doesn't appear to be valid and remains visible in previous communications creating confusion. Could you please clarify?

Resolution

The previously communicated mandatory deadline of January 31, 2026, has been revoked. Customer notification (EVB8339922) regarding this change was issued on October 17, 2025. 

While the mandatory requirement has been removed, SAP strongly recommends that customers transition to the new domains as soon as possible to avoid potential future disruptions. At this time, no service interruptions are expected for customers still using legacy domains. Updated guidance will be proactively communicated once the new enforcement timeline is finalized.
