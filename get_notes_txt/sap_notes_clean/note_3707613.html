Symptom

As per KBA 2087436 : Changing the SuccessFactors Company ID, once you try to access LMS after the company ID change, the links should get updated/corrected on their own, but that is not happening in the sealed tenants. 

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Learning

Cause

This is a Known Issue.

Resolution

The Learning engineering team has addressed the issue by correcting the backend code.

  • The fix for this issue is scheduled for deployment in the 1H 2026 release. 
  • For release timelines, please review SAP SuccessFactors Product Release & Road Map Information.

Workaround: Please raise a ticket with the Support team. Our engineering team will execute scripts directly in the database to resolve the issue.
