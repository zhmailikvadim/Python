Symptom

You have made the supplier field LFA1-PROFS ("profession") mandatory/hidden/display-only in the supplier settings, e.g. at (transaction sm30) V_T077K > KRED > General Data > Control > Person subject to withholding tax.

You do not expect the business partner field BUT000-JOBGR to become mandatory/hidden/display-only because of this but it does.

Other Terms

Field group, field status, mandatory fields, required fields

Reason and Prerequisites

Note 2902871 removed the mapping in the code of CVI_FM_BP_VENDOR->MAP_BP_GENERAL_DATA called from CVI_MAPPER, therefore the field status mapping should have been removed, too.

Solution

Follow the manual steps.
