Symptom

  • Users with a role that has Update permission on Plans cannot change the Business Unit on a plan.

  • The pencil icon next to the Business Unit field in a plan is not available.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
  • Incentive Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in to Incentive Management with a role that has Update permission on Plans but no specific Business Unit permissions.
  2. Open an existing plan.
  3. Attempt to edit the Business Unit field: the pencil icon is not available and the Business Unit cannot be changed.

Cause

The role lacks the required Business Unit permissions under the Administration group (Workspace, Update, and Assign), which prevents editing the Business Unit on plans.

Resolution

  1. Open the Admin application in Incentive Management and navigate to role management.
  2. Select the affected role.
  3. Under Administration > Business Units, grant the following permissions: Workspace, Update, and Assign.
  4. Save the role changes.
  5. Verify that the user can open a plan and edit the Business Unit field (the pencil icon should be available).
  6. Ensure the role also retains the necessary Plan permissions (for example, Update on Plans) for other plan edits as needed.
