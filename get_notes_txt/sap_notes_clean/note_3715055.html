Symptom

Price inconsistency between the ACM Pricing view and the WB2_D_PRASP table.

Replication steps:
1. Launch transaction WB21 to create a new Trading Contract.
2. Maintain header details ensuring the Document Currency differs from the Market Currency.
3. In the Commodity tab, maintain a past delivery period and create the item with Price Fixation. Save the contract.
4. Access transaction OB08 and adjust the Foreign Exchange (FX) rate for the relevant currency pair.
5. Open the contract in Change Mode (WB22). Modify the delivery period in the Commodity tab at the item level.
6. Navigate to the Conditions tab and click the Change button. The Provisional FX value is adjusted here.
7. Click the Execute button and Save the contract.
8. Compare the condition values in the ACM Pricing View against the entries in table WB2_D_PRASP.
9. The amounts in the ACM Pricing View and table WB2_D_PRASP are not synchronized.

Other Terms

Agricultural Contract Management; ACM; Pricing; WB2_D_PRASP

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Import the relevant support pack or apply the correction instruction(s).
