Symptom

The list of delivery note items contains packaging materials and auxiliary packaging materials with the same product name.

After a product item is packed into a packaging material, there is still enough remaining quantity of the product and of the packaging material to pack another package.

However, when you choose Copy Single in the packaging hierarchy, the auxiliary packaging material is taken instead of the remaining packaging material because it has the same name. The action results in an error.

Other Terms

ICR, inbound cargo registration, packing dialog, packages, packaging hierarchy

Reason and Prerequisites

You use SAP S/4HANA for manufacturing logistics or this SAP Note is a prerequisite of another SAP Note.

Solution

Implement the attached correction.
