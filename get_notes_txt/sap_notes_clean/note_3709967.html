Symptom

In the My Request application, dates towards the end of December (such as December 31st) show as one year greater than expected when formatted, i.e. Dec 31 2025 becomes Dec 31 2026.

Other Terms

My Request, Date format, yyyy, Format pattern, Date picker wrong year, MYREQUESTS1, Student Lifecycle Management

Reason and Prerequisites

 Programming error

Solution

 Implement the correction instructions
