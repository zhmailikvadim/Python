Symptom

In Process Receivables transaction code, Customer Contact Person is not filled in. 

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental." 

Environment

  • Collections Management
  • SAP ERP 
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA        All versions 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access transaction code UDM_SPECIALIST/UDM_SUPERVISOR, select the BP and navigate to Process Receivables.
  2. Try to select a Customer Contact Person in the Customer Contact Person tab during letter/correspondence creation.



  3. Select it in case it is available and exit the session or allow a time lapse.
  4. Reopen the transaction and observe that the selected Customer Contact Person does not persist and must be reselected.

Cause

  • In the business partner, the relationship category 'Has Contact Person' is not created.
  • When a contact person is created, the contact person key must exist.
  • The synchronization of the table KNVK is inactive, preventing automatic updates.

Resolution

  1. Ensure the relationship category 'Has Contact Person' is created for the business partner.



  2. Add a contact person in the relationships section of the business partner (BP).
  3. Confirm that synchronization is active to update the KNVK table automatically.
  4. If synchronization is inactive, manually enter the contact data into the KNVK table to ensure the information is read during process receivables for contact person.
