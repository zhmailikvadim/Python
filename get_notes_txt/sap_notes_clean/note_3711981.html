Symptom

Setting business complete or business reopened for service orders leads to errors.

Other Terms

A_ServiceOrder_2, I_ServiceDocument, API_SERVICEORDER, V4 ODATA API for Service Orders, Service Management Prerequisite for Joule capability trans_service_order_capability

Reason and Prerequisites

The fields SrvcOrdIsBusinessCompleted and SrvcOrdIsBusinessReopened did not have the proper annotation for boolean values. Also, the description was inadequate.

If you use Joule capabilities for service orders, this correction is a prerequisite so that you can complete business and reopen business for service orders.

For other applications it is also useful.

Solution

Please apply the attached correction instruction, or upgrade to FPS2.
