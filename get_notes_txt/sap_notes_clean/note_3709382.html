Symptom

You are running internal cash concentration for your IHB accounts where subsidiaries are zero-balancing into the head office accounts. When the head office receives its bank statement, it is not clear which transaction is linked to which counterparty.

Other Terms

cash pooling, APM, CAMT053, IHB EOD

Reason and Prerequisites

If the bank statement of the head office has the subsidiary counterparties mentioned, it has a better tracking on where the cash flow is originating from. So we are populating counterparty IHB account number to the camt.053 tag BkToCstmrStmt/Stmt/Ntry/NtryDtls/TxDtls/RmtInf/Ustrd.

Solution

Please apply this SAP Note or the assigned Support Package.
