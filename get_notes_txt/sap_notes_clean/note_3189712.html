Symptom

How can I manage users' Roles in SAP Business Network?

Environment

SAP Business Network

Resolution

As a prerequisite, only the SAP Business Network account administrator or users with the User Administration permission, can manage these settings. If you're entitled to proceed, follow the steps below: 

To create a Role:

  1. Click [User Initial] > Settings > Manage Users.
  2. Under the Manage Roles tab, click the [plus sign] icon.
  3. Enter Name and Description for the role
  4. Select the permissions by ticking the checkboxes under Permissions 
  5. Click Save.

To edit a Role:

  1. Click [User Initial] > Settings > Manage Users
  2. Under the Manage Roles tab, click the Role name
  3. Tick the Show me all the available permissions checkbox for a complete view and make the necessary changes
  4. Click Save.

SAP Business Network applies your changes to all users assigned to the role. Changes take effect for each user at their next sign in.
