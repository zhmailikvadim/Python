Symptom

  • Pay Target section remains visible in the Compensation Information category of the Employee Profile, even after all permissions for Pay Target have been removed.
  • When clicking on 'View All' in the Compensation Information card, the Pay Target section is visible at the bottom of the page.
  • Historical records for Pay Target are accessible but do not appear in the 'History' section.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central
    • Compensation Information
    • Pay Component Recurring

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Ensure Employee Data > Pay Target permissions are revoked for the login user over target employee.
  2. Navigate to the Employee Profile.
  3. Select the Compensation category.
  4. Click on 'View All' in the Compensation Information card.
  5. Observe that the Pay Target section is still visible at the bottom of the page.

Cause

Known defect with code change provided for the next release.

Resolution

The fix patch (ID: ECT-264633) has been planned to be deployed in 1H 2026 Release (May 16th).
