Symptom

Based on the Act on minimum salary in RS, Art. 6, the new amount of minimum salary for full-time work in year 2026 is calculated and published on the government web page

  • minimum salary amount = 1.481,88 EUR

New minimum salary amount applies for work from 01.01.2026 onwards.

Other Terms

MINRS, minimum salary

Reason and Prerequisites

  • Official Gazette UL RS 6/2026 Act nr. 175.

Solution

Payroll constant MINRS in table T511K was changed to value 1.481,88 in a record valid from 01.01.2026.
