Symptom

When selecting a task to rate a candidate in Interview Central, only the first 10 candidates are displayed. The Items per page field is not editable, preventing users from increasing the number of visible candidates.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management 

Cause

This scenario was identified as a defect. It's currently being analyzed by our engineering team.
