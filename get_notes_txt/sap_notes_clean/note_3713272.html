Symptom

"Done" SFC after completion cannot be pulled back if NC code logged and disposition triggered.

Environment

  • SAP Digital Manufacturing 2602

Reproducing the Issue

Disposition Routing does not bring "Done" SFC back to "In Queue".

Cause

Generally SAP Digital Manufacturing does not support NC Disposition of "Done" SFCs.

Resolution

The system works as designed.
An enhancement request can be opened to influence it at https://influence.sap.com/sap/ino/#/campaign/3537 .
