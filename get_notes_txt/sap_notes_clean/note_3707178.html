Symptom

Detailed information cannot be displayed in the Cost Center Budget Report application.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

After navigating to the Cost Center Budget Report app, no data is visible.

Cause

After the detailed line item postings associated with the relevant cost center were reviewed, it was identified that the data includes information tied to the Sales Organization.

The detailed information was prevented from being displayed in the Cost Center Budget Report by specific limitations applied to the cost center settings.

Resolution

The restriction on the Sales Organization should be removed through Maintain Business Roles; then the data in the Cost Center Budget Report can be seen.

Keywords

Cost Center Reports, restriction, Maintain Business Roles, Sales Organization
