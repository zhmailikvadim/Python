Symptom

I am attempting to create or edit a Pick List of type 'Custom Field Segmented Pick List' or 'Integration Segmented Pick List. And when providing value for 'Segment Header', system restricts input to 40 characters.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Sign in to your SAP Fieldglass account;
  2. Click on Admin;
  3. Click on Pick List;
  4. Click New;
    • Type: Custom Field Segmented Pick List or Integration Segmented Pick List

Enter your desired value in the Segment Header field. Please note that no more than 40 characters will be permitted.

Cause

The system does not permit more than 40 characters to be provided.

Resolution

There is no option to increase the character limit. Please rewrite your sentences to stay under 40 characters.

Keywords

pick list, segment, header, limit, threshold, forty, characters, 40
