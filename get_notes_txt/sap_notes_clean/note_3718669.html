Symptom

When navigating to employee profile and immediately click on "all actions" and select "change job information"; the "view org chart" icon will be missing next to the position field.

Environment

  • SAP Successfactors Employee Central
  • Position management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login in and go to any employee profile
  2. Click on "all actions"
  3. Select to change job information
  4. Fill in effective date, event reason etc
  5. Note that next to the position field the "view org chart" icon is missing

Cause

This issue has been deemed a UI defect by our engineering team 

Resolution

Engineering are working on a fix for this issue. Currently this is targeted to be fixed in the next release (1H 2026)

Workaround: Instead of clicking" all actions" and change job information; go to the job data section first and open the job information portlet. In here choose to edit or insert new record and the "view org chart" icon will just show fine here.
