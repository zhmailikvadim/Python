Symptom

In payment items generated by iBS-BASS (table BKKIT) for charge postings, the field "GUID from Business Transaction" (/IBS/BTRA_GUID) of the append "iBS-BASS: BSK-Spec. Data" (/IBS/SACC_BKKIT_ENHA) is filled with the GUID of the relevant building savings contract and therefore has the same content as the "Building Savings Contract GUID" field (/IBS/BBS_GUID).

Reason and Prerequisites

In the task "Execute Building Savings Contract (Planned Record) Postings" (BSC_TK_CHNG_BOOKING_V2), the GUID to which the business transaction references is transferred to the field BTRA_GUID instead of its own GUID.

Solution

The implementation /IBS/CLBSC_TK_CHNG_BOOKING_V2 is corrected.

This affects the following processes:

  • "Cancellation Process: Preparation of High-Interest Rate Cancellation" (BSC_TMT_PR_TERMIN_HIR_PREP)
  • Cancellation Process: Flag for High Interest Rate Notice (BSC_TMT_PR_TERMIN_HIR_RES)
  • "Increase of building savings amount after expiry of rescission period" (BSC_PR_CHNG_WR_INCR)
  • "Residential riester-forced increase" (BSC_PR_CHNG_WR_INCR_OBLIGATION)
  • "Assignment: Undo Assignment Acceptance" (BSC_PR_CHNG_ALC_WITHDRAW)
  • "Assignment: Change Assignment Options" (BSC_PR_CHNG_ALCOPTIONS)
  • "Assignment: Enter Assignment Acceptance" (BSC_PR_CHNG_ALC_ACCEPT)
