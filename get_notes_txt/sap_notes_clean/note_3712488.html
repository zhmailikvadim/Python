Symptom

This SAP Note gives a summary of legal changes, corrections and enhancements that are available with the country-specific legal change packages below for HCM Portugal (components PA-PA-PT, PY-PT, and PY-PT-PS):

  • S4HCM ECC 102:SAPK-10201INS4HCMCPT
  • S4HCM ECC 101: SAPK-10127INS4HCMCPT
  • S4HCM ECC 100: SAPK-10040INS4HCMCPT
  • SAP_HR EnhP6.08: SAPK-608F1INSAPHRCPT
  • SAP_HR EnhP6.04: SAPK-604M3INSAPHRCPT

    Other Terms

    SAP_HRCPT; Support Package; CLC; HR; legal change;

    Reason and Prerequisites

    Prerequisite is the installation of the Synchronization HR Support Package (or the last available CLC Package) that is relevant for your release and listed below:

    ReleaseSupport Package
    S4HCM 102 SAPK-10205INS4HCMCPT
    S4HCM 101 SAPK-10131INS4HCMCPT
    S4HCM 100 SAPK-10044INS4HCMCPT
    SAP_HR 6.08 SAPK-600F5INSAPHRCPT
    SAP_HR 6.04 SAPK-604M7INSAPHRCPT
