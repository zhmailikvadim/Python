Symptom

You have created an inbound delivery with a product that uses  A-Serial Numbers. If you pack the product into multiple subHUs of a topHU, it is possible that the Serial Numbers are not displayed in the Goods Receipt Warehouse Task.

Other Terms

/scwm/ordim_hs /scwm/ordim_h /scwm/ordim_cs

Reason and Prerequisites

The issue doesn't occur if the stock is packed directly in the topHU (without subHUs).

Solution

Implement the Correction Instruction.
