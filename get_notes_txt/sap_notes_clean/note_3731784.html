Symptom

You process a key date valuation for a position that has subpositions because it’s part of a hedging relationship. The MANIPULATE_VALUATION_FLOWS method in BAdI TPM_TRL_MANIPULATE isn't processed when executing TPM1.

Other Terms

TPM100, TPM_TRL_HREL_EVALUATE

Reason and Prerequisites

You are using hedge accounting in SAP Treasury and Risk Management.

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction (CI) or the relevant Support Package.
