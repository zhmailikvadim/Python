Symptom

Catalogs with items expiring in 30 days report don't always show information.

Environment

SAP Ariba Catalogs

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the Catalog Content Manager section in Administration.
  2. Click on Catalog Content Reports.
  3. View the Catalogs with items expiring in 30 days report.

Resolution

The behavior of the Catalogs with items expiring in 30 days report follows these rules:

  • A maximum of six customer catalogs are displayed as individual slices.
  • If there are more than six customer catalogs, the top five catalogs containing the highest number of expiring items are shown individually, while the remaining catalogs are grouped together and displayed as an “Other” slice.
  • The report only displays information when there are catalogs with items expiring within the next 30 days.

This behavior is expected and aligns with the current system design.
