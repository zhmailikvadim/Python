Symptom

After configuring the SAP Signavio Integration, returning to the Integrations page does not display the integration card.

Environment

SAP LeanIX - SAP Signavio Integration

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to Administration > Discovery and Integrations > Integrations.
  2. Use "Add Integration" button and click "Configure" on the SAP Signavio tile.
  3. Configure the credentials.
  4. Keep the "active" check-box unchecked. 
  5. Click "Save".
  6. Navigate back to the Integrations page. 

Cause

Known issue.

Resolution

If the integration is not visible on the Integration Page, follow the below steps:-

  1. Navigate to Administration > Discovery and Integrations > Integrations.
  2. Click on "Add Integration" button on the Integrations page.
  3. Click the Configure button on the SAP Signavio tile.
