Symptom

  • The process document’s link to the business partner cannot be navigated in the standard system, preventing users from switching to the linked business partner application.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Utilities Master Data Updates Option for Germany

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open a process document in the business system.
  2. Locate the link to the associated Business Partner within the document.
  3. Click the link to navigate to the Business Partner application.
  4. Observe that no navigation occurs, as this action is not supported in the standard system.

Cause

The user does not have authorization to access the Business Partner App F3163.

Resolution

  1. Refer to the Manage Business Partner Master Data (F3163) documentation available on the SAP Help Portal.
  2. Follow the instructions provided in the documentation to verify and adjust user authorizations for accessing the Business Partner App F3163.
  3. Ensure the user has the necessary permissions to navigate to the Business Partner application via the process document link.
