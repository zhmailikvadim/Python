Symptom

When you find that there are two HANA Cockpit installations within a single instance, but only one is needed, you may want to remove the redundant one. This KBA guides you on how to delete HANA Cockpit in a specific space.

Environment

  • SAP HANA Cockpit 2
  • SAP HANA, platform edition 2.0

Cause

Having more than one HANA Cockpit deployed within the same instance is not the recommended by SAP and may cause confusion or conflict when managing HANA resources.
To streamline administration and avoid potential resource contention, it is recommended to keep only a single HANA Cockpit per instance. 

Resolution

We recommend retaining only one Cockpit per instance. Follow the steps below to remove HANA Cockpit from a specified space (e.g. “SAP”):

  1. Log in to the HANA Cockpit host as the <sid>adm user.
  2. Switch to the space where you want to perform the deletion. Important: Ensure you are targeting the correct space (e.g., "SAP") before proceeding. 
    xs t -s SAP
  3. Uninstall HANA Cockpit (com.sap.hana.cockpit) in the specified space: 
    xs uninstall XSAC_COCKPIT
  4. Uninstall HRTT (com.sap.xsa.hrtt) in the specified space: 
    xs uninstall XSAC_HRTT
  5. Uninstall Portal Service (XSAC_PORTAL_SERV) in the specified space: 
    xs uninstall XSAC_PORTAL_SERV
  6. Uninstall XSA Cockpit (com.sap.core.account) in the specified space: 
    xs uninstall XSAC_XSA_COCKPIT
  7. Confirm the uninstallation. There should now only be "alm-product-installer" remaining in the space: 
    xs mtas
