Symptom

There is an upcoming mandatory legal change related to France Electronic Invoicing solution. This KBA provides guidance on where to find the relevant information regarding this legal change and SAP solution.

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition
  • SAP Document and Reporting Compliance, Cloud Edition

Cause

  • Article 26 - Law No. 2022-1157 of 16 August 2022 amending the 2022 Finance Law (1) - Legifrance
  • 28/07/2023 – Generalization of electronic invoicing: Postponement of the planned entry into force to 2024 – Press – Ministry of Finance

Resolution

1. Where can we find information on the SAP solution related to France Mandatory B2B electronic invoicing 2026 legal change?
A: Refer to the following resources:

  • Regulatory Change Manager: Subscribe to RCM entry Mandatory B2B electronic invoicing 2026
  • WorkZone channel: Stay up to date with the news related to the solution delivered by SAP. Follow KBA 3608110 - How to join the localization/regional collaboration WorkZone Groups and Community? - SAP for Me and join WorkZone channel France - SAP Document and Reporting Compliance
  • Webinar: Get ready for France’s B2B e-invoicing and e-reporting with SAP Document and Reporting Compliance (5216780) (Feb 17, 2026)
  • Community Blog: News from e-invoicing and e-reporting in France

2. What is the go-live date for this legal mandate?
A: For official timelines and important dates related to this legal change, please refer to the RCM topic Mandatory B2B electronic invoicing 2026.

3. What are the SAP Notes to implement this solution?
A: Go through following overview notes:

  • 3413565 - Electronic Document Processing for France via Approved Platform: Implementation Overview - SAP for Me
  • 3713986 - Electronic Reporting for France via Approved Platform: Implementation Overview - SAP for Me

4. What are the pre-requisites for this solution Or, How do I keep my system ready before implementing the actual solution?
A: Review the Prerequisites section in the above-mentioned SAP Notes to ensure system is ready prior to implementation.

5. Where can we find product documentation related to this legal change?
A: Product documentation is available here:

  • SAP S/4HANA and SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition: Electronic Invoicing and Reporting via Approved Platform
  • SAP ERP: Electronic Invoicing and Reporting via Approved Platform

6. Which component should be used to report issues related to France PDP solution?
A:  Depending on the nature of the issue, use one of the following component while reporting an issue to SAP:

ComponentDescriptionPurpose
CA-GTF-CSC-EDO-FRFrance Electronic InvoicingElectronic Document Processing/eInvoicing
CA-GTF-CSC-EDO-FR-REFrance Electronic ReportingElectronic Reporting/eReporting

7. What are the available Regions for provisioning SAP Document and Reporting Compliance, Cloud Edition?
A: Check KBA 3661316 - What are the available Regions for provisioning SAP Document and Reporting Compliance, Cloud Edition? - SAP for Me.

8. Is SAP a Plateforme agréée (PA)?
A: Yes, check SAP Blog SAP is now Plateforme agréée (PA) for e-invoicing and e-reporting. You may also refer official list from here Je consulte la liste des plateformes agréées | impots.gouv.fr.
