Symptom

• In the "Manage Bank Accounts" app, the House Bank Account ID field appears blank for bank accounts on the main screen.  
• When accessing the details of the bank accounts, the House Bank Account ID field is visible and not blank.  

Environment

• S/4 HANA On Premise  
• Private Cloud  
• Bank Account Management  

Reproducing the Issue

1. Open the "Manage Bank Accounts" app.  
2. View the bank accounts.  
3. Observe that the House Bank and Account ID fields appear blank on the main screen.  
4. Navigate to the details of the bank accounts and observe that these fields are not blank.  

Cause

The House Bank Account ID field appears blank because the house bank account is not within a valid period.  

Resolution

1. On the "Manage Bank Accounts" main screen, only "House Bank Accounts" that fall within their valid period will be displayed.  
2. This behavior is part of the system's standard design to ensure that only relevant accounts are visible for operational purposes.
