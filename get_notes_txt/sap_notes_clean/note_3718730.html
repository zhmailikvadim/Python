Symptom

When I attempt to push or repush invoices, work orders, or SOWs to SAP Ariba, the items do not appear in the downstream system. Upon investigation, the Fieldglass connector logs may show that there is "no data to be pushed" or return errors from the Ariba side such as:

  • No PO matching for update/cancel
  • No relationship with Supplier

Cause

The integration logic between Fieldglass and Ariba requires specific mandatory fields to be populated to establish a successful connection. Common causes for push failures include:

  • Missing PO Numbers: Invoices cannot be pushed if the related Work Order or SOW does not have a Purchase Order (PO) number associated with it.
  • Revision Mismatch: If a PO number was added on a later revision (e.g., Revision 2), invoices tied to the initial revision (Revision 0 or 1) may still fail because the PO number was not present when those specific records were created.
  • Zero-Amount Invoices: By system design, invoices with a 0.00 amount are not included in the integration flow.
  • Supplier Relationship: The supplier must have a valid established relationship and ANId in Ariba to receive pushed documents.

Resolution

You must ensure all mandatory data points are present on the Fieldglass objects before the integration can successfully transmit them.

  1. Verify PO Numbers: Open the Work Order or SOW related to the failing invoice. Ensure the Purchase Order field is populated.
  2. Address Revision Gaps: If the PO number is missing from early revisions, you may need to Reject and Resubmit the invoice for approval once the PO number is present on the current active Work Order revision.
    • Note: In some cases, a manual backend update by SAP may be required to sync PO numbers to legacy revisions.
  3. Check Supplier ANId: Verify with your IT or Ariba team that the supplier name and ANId match the relationship established in the Ariba Network.
  4. Repush Item: Once the data is corrected, use the Repush feature in the Data Monitor to re-trigger the integration.
