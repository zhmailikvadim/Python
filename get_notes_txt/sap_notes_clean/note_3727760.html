Symptom

  • Inquiry regarding the possibility of migrating from a non-CDB database to a multitenant architecture in database 19c.

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 4.3
  • All operating system

Resolution

  •  Oracle Database 19c with multitenant architecture is officially supported for SAP environments.
  •  Oracle Real Application Cluster (RAC) and Oracle Exadata is also supported.

Refer to page 11 of the product availability matrix for further details on the compatibility of BO4.3:

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence Platform   4.3PAM   

NOTE :

    If further assistance is required regarding Oracle database-specific procedures, contact Oracle support or consult with a database administrator (DBA).
