Symptom

The thresholds to calculate the amounts for SII (Sickness Incapacity and Invalidity) contributions ( ZIV (Ziekte- en invaliditeitsverzekering) / AMI (Assurance maladie-invalidité) ) for pensioners has been indexed as from 01.03.2026.

The payroll parameters Exemption family at charge (BEBPG) and Exemption no family at charge (BEBPN) of table Payroll Parameters (T511P) must be delimited.

Start date End date BEBPG BEBPN
01.03.2026 31.12.9999 2.463,25 € 2.078,46 €

Other Terms

BEBPN, BEBPG, RPCMSVB0, V_T511P, T511P, SII, ZIV, AMI
