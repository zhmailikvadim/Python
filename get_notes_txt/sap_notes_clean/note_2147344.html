Symptom

If the period of overseas posting is lasting from pervious tax year to this tax year, the commencement date in IR8A form for this tax year display as "N/A".

After you apply the adjustment, the next day after the end of overseas posting ("RE" date in Infotype 0041) will be set as the commencement date in IR8A form for this tax year.

For example, the period of overseas posting is from 2013/10/01 to 2014/04/30, for YOA 2015, the commencement date will display as 2014/05/01 in the IR8A form.

Other Terms

IR8A, Cross Year, Commencement Date, Overseas posting

Reason and Prerequisites

This is an enhancement.

Solution

Apply the HR Support Package to get this solution. Alternatively, you may apply the Correction Instructions attached with this SAP Note if the changes are required immediately.
