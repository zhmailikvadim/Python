Symptom

In the "Manage In-House Bank Payment Items" application, under the "Future Dated" section, when you try to open the object page of the list items, the page header reads Payment Item "Undefined" and the rest of the information does not load.

Other Terms

F6339, IHB, In-House Bank, Manage In House Bank Payment Items, Manage In-House Bank Payment Items

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a coding issue. Logic to fetch elements Clearing Area and PaymentItemTechnicalIdentifier from the CDS C_IHBPaymentMonitorTP have been added to address the issue. These elements were missing earlier.

Solution

Implementing this SAP Note to fixes the issue.
