Symptom

The Goods and Services Tax (GST) system in India has introduced a new valuation method for specific tobacco and tobacco-related products, as mandated by Notification Nos. 19/2025-Central Tax and 20/2025-Central Tax.

Other Terms

Digital Compliance Service, DCS, RSP, Retail Sale Price, Tobacco

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason

RSP-based valuation of notified tobacco goods under GST.

Solution

This SAP note delivers the necessary data dictionary objects to facilitate Retail Sale Price(RSP) based valuation for tobacco and tobacco-related products.

A new classification field is added to the view EDOINTXCODEMAPV to indicate whether a tax code is RSP-relevant.
