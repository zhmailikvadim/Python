Symptom

  • Purchase requisitions created in PP/DS are visible in /SAPAPO/RRP3 but do not appear in MD04.
  • No inconsistencies shown in /SAPAPO/CCR.
  • No stuck queues observed in SMQ1/SMQ2.
  • No entries stuck in /SAPAPO/CPP.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental."

Environment

  • SAP ERP/ECC connected with SCM APO System
  • S4HANA (releases independent)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Manual create one purchase requisition in t-code /SAPAPO/RRP3.
  2. In S/4(ECC), open MD04 for the same product/location and observe that the corresponding purchase requisition is missing.
  3. Check t-codes SMQ1, SMQ2, /SAPAPO/CCR, /SAPAPO/CPP but did not find entry stuck.

Cause

In /SAPAPO/RRPCUST1, the transfer setting for External Procurement is not set to 'Always Create Transfer Events', which prevents the integration of purchase requisitions transfer from PPDS to S/4.

Resolution

Open transaction /SAPAPO/RRPCUST1, change the transfer setting to blank or 'Always Create Transfer Event', save the customizing.
