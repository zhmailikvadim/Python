Symptom

  • The write access of business role is configured as "No Access", so the price category of it is "Self Service". 
  • In the Display IAM Apps, the price category for this business role is displayed incorrectly as "Advanced". 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the Display IAM App.
  2. Navigate to the "Used in Business Roles" tab.
  3. Observe that the price category for the business role configured as "display only" with "Self Service" price category is incorrectly displayed as "Advanced".

Resolution

It's a known issue and will be fixed in hotfix collection 07(Feb 28th&29th). 

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition, 3-System Landscape – Upgrade & Maintenance Schedule

This issue is "only" a display issue in the "Display IAM App" app.
The correct Price Category was always shown in the "Maintain Business Roles" app.
