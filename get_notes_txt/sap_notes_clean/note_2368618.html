Symptom

Missing CPE functions when certain business functions or switches are activated. Switch BC sets based on customizing tables and with delivery class C are not automatically activated.

Other Terms

CPE, Switch BC Sets

Reason and Prerequisites

Some release-specific functions are provided by switch BC sets, that you have to activate manually.

Solution

Kindly make sure that corresponding Switch is active before you try to activate the BC Sets mentioned below.
