Symptom

How the Total Cycle Time is calculated on the Approvals / Audit Trail tab of a work item

Resolution

Total Cycle Time is calculating elapsed time between Submit action and the current Approved level

Example:

  • Submitted -12/10/2025 05:05 PM
  • Current Approve level: Approved: 12/14/2025 09:17 PM
  • Pending approvals: 2 levels
  • Total Cycle Time: 4 Days, 4:11 Hours
  • /today's date: 2/10/2026/
