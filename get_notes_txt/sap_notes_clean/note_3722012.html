Symptom

You execute a split of a production order. For doing the transfer of costs a batch managed by-product is added to the parent order and correspondingly the child order gets a component with same material and batch. The batch of the by-product should be valuated accordingly to the planned costs of the parent order. However, due to a programming error the valuation is not calculated correctly or even missing.

Other Terms

 Batch Valuation, AFSPREL, CK 465, CK465, No price could be determined for material/batch &1 plant &2

Reason and Prerequisites

 Program Error

Solution

Apply program corrections.
