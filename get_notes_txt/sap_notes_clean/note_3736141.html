Symptom

When emails with embedded screenshots are converted into tickets, the screenshots do not appear in the ticket interactions. Even clicking ‘View Original Content’ does not show the images.

Environment

SAP Cloud for Customer core applications

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Send or receive an inbound email that includes pasted screenshots.
  2. Allow the email-to-ticket conversion to create a service ticket.
  3. Open the ticket and navigate to Interactions.
  4. Open the inbound email entry and observe that the screenshots are not displayed.
  5. Clicking on “view original content” also does not show the images.

Cause

The inbound email embeds the screenshot with the content type “text/html” instead of an image MIME type (for example, image/png or image/jpeg). The system interprets it as an HTML document rather than an image, so the screenshot is not rendered in the ticket interaction.

Resolution

Ensure the sending mail client or automation embeds screenshots with proper image MIME types (for example, image/png or image/jpeg).

Resend email with correct image MIME types and confirm that screenshots render in the ticket interaction after conversion.
