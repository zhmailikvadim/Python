Symptom

Value errors appear due to incorrect data type of field  req_qty of RFA SD.

Other Terms

FS-PER Rel 3.0 SP11

Reason and Prerequisites

Correction regarding RFA SD

Solution

The data type of field req_qty is corrected to QUAN of Length 13 and Decimals 3.

Implement the automatic correction instructions using SNOTE and perform the manual post-implementation steps.
