Symptom

  • A Statement of Work (SOW) remains in Response Pending Approval status.
  • The user is not listed as the next approver.
  • The approval workflow shows a user such as Web Services, Integration, or a technical user.
  • The SOW appears to be waiting for approval but no business user is assigned.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Sign in to SAP Fieldglass.
  2. Locate the relevant Statement of Work (SOW).
  3. Review the SOW Status.
  4. Navigate to the Audit Trail tab.
  5. Review the approval workflow and note if the approver is listed as an integration or technical user (for example: Web Services or Integration user).

Cause

This behavior is expected when the buyer organization uses integrations to process approvals instead of manual approval through the user interface.

In these cases, the approval step may be assigned to a technical integration user (for example, Web Services or a connector user). This indicates the SOW approval is processed through an external system or integration rather than directly within SAP Fieldglass.

Resolution

Administrator users can review the approval workflow details in the Audit Trail or Integration Audit Trail to confirm whether the approval is handled through integration. If you are not an Administrator or cannot view these details, contact your Program Management Office (PMO) or system administrator for clarification on the approval process. If the approval appears delayed, the buyer organization may need to review the status of their integration process.

Note: SAP Fieldglass Technical Support cannot approve Statements of Work or modify customer approval workflows.
