Symptom

  • The Sort Order field in Pay Component Group is missing in the system, which is required for modification in the Compensation History Widget.
  • The issue stems from the `hris-field id="sortOrder"` being configured with `visibility="none"`

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental. 

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Core
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central - Foundation Objects (Organisation, Pay and Job Structures)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to Manage Organisation > Pay and Job Structures > Pay Component Group: Annual Salary (XXX.
  • Result: The Sort Order field is missing.

  1. Verify the Corporate Data Model:
  • Go to Admin Center > Import/Export Corporate Data Model > Export.
  • Result: The `hris-field id="sortOrder"` is configured with `visibility="none"`.

Cause

  • The `hris-field id="sortOrder"` is configured with `visibility="none"` in the Corporate Data Model.

Resolution

  1. Update the Corporate Data Model to change the `hris-field id="sortOrder"` visibility to `visibility="both"`.
  2. Refer to Knowledge Article 3066117 - How to export Data Models.
  • Navigate to Admin Center > Import/Export Corporate Data Model > Export.
  • Locate the `hris-field id="sortOrder"` configuration.
  • Modify the visibility attribute from `visibility="none"` to `visibility="both"`.
  1. Once updated, the Sort Order field should appear correctly in the Pay Component Group.
