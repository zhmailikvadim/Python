Symptom

Entity SchedAgmtReleaseListItems of Service PP_MRP_COCKPIT has no filter for MRP Area. It is causing that all Schedule Line Agreements with Release Strategy are listed, which are available for the plant.
It can lead to wrong selection in case of storage location or subcontracting MRP Area, for example in app F0251 'Manage Material Coverage', screen 'Delivery Schedule'.

Other Terms

PP_MRP_COCKPIT, SchedAgmtReleaseListItems, SLA, Schedule Line Agreement, MRP Area, Berid, LGORT, EMLIF, Missing Filter, Manage Material Coverage, Odata, Sloc, Subcon

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing field/filter for MRPArea in Entity SchedAgmtReleaseListItems.

Solution

Please implement this SAP Note.
Afterwards the Entity SchedAgmtReleaseListItems will contain field/filter MRPArea.
Please clear the Gateway Cache after implementation.
