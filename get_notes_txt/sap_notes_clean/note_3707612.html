Symptom

  • An inconsistency seems to occur during the "MDC: Receipt of 55649 from Responsible MOS to SUP" process.
  • The qualifier ZF3 is present in the additional data of step 1000 in the cloud process (DE_C_MDC_55649_MOS2SUP_RECV):

    /MP4G/FILE_DATA[1]-MESSAGE_DATA[1]-UTILMD_TRANS[1]-UTILMD_TRANS_ATTR[1]-POD_DATA[1]

  • The qualifier is missing in the additional data of step 1300 in the business system process (DE_B_MDC_55649_MOS2SUP_RECV).
  • Due to this absence, the value cannot be processed in the business system.

Environment

  • SAP Market Communication for Utilities
  • SAP S/4HANA Utilities Master Data Updates Option for Germany

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the "Monitor Process Documents" Fiori app.
  2. Search with the given CAR (Common Access Reference) or PDoc (Process Document) number.
  3. Verify if qualifier ZF3 is present in the additional data of step 1000 in the cloud process.
  4. Verify the absence of the qualifier in step 1300 in the business system.

Cause

Searching in the wrong place.

Resolution

  • Verify the availability of the control channel by reviewing the additional data (of step 1300 on the business system) located at:

    /STEP/UBD/POD_DATA_PERIOD[1]/DATA[POD_ID = NeLo PoD]/NELO_DATA/CTRL_CHANNEL_NELO.

  • Within this section see the property: CTRL_CHANNEL_NELO: ControlChannelAvailable.
  • This confirms that the control channel is available.
