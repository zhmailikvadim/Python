Symptom

  • The report HBRTRSF0 generates pre-marked intervals in the ACJEF and AFDT files even if the employee has flexible interval time and a real record in the electronic time register (REP).

Environment

  • HR renewal 

  • SAP S/4HANA 

  • SAP ERP  

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll 

 

Cause

The report HBRTRSF0 generates pre-marked intervals automatically when the employee has the field RINOB (Reg. interv. obrig.) from IT0398 (subtype of IT0016) uncheked. 

Resolution

When the flag is checked, the system will not generate pre-marked intervals and will only generate intervals according to the records in the electronic time register (REP).
