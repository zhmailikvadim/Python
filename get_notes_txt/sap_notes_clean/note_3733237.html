Symptom

  • From a custom ABAP program, prepare data to update contact note KNVK-PARAU.
  • Call CL_MD_BP_MAINTAIN=>MAINTAIN to perform the update.
  • Observe that no error is raised, but the KNVK-PARAU fields are not updated in the database.

Environment

  • Sales and Distribution (SD)
  • Logistics Execution (LE)
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
  • SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
  • SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. From a custom ABAP program, prepare data to update contact note KNVK-PARAU.
  2. Call CL_MD_BP_MAINTAIN=>MAINTAIN to perform the update.
  3. Observe that no error is raised, but the KNVK-PARAU field is not updated in the database.

Resolution

To update KNVK-PARAU data, in I_DATA of CL_MD_BP_MAINTAIN, the information must be passed into LT_DATA-PARTNER_RELATION-CENTRAL_DATA-DATA-COMMENTS .
