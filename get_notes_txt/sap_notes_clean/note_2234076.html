Symptom

Copy and paste in the "Journal Entries" grid in Journals does not work.

 

[Reproducible Steps]

1. Log in to the Web client.

2. Go to the "CONSOLIDATION" menu and click "Journals".

3. Open a Journal ID.

4. Try to copy and paste cells or rows of the "Journal Entries" grid into Microsoft Excel.

5. Try to copy and paste cells of the "Journal Entries" grid into the "Journal Entries" grid.

6. Try to copy and paste cells from Microsoft Excel into the "Journal Entries" grid.

Other Terms

SAP Business Planning and Consolidation 10.1 for Microsoft platform, SP04, Journal, Copy, Paste, Copy&Paste

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

For SAP Business Planning and Consolidation 10.1, version for Microsoft, upgrade to (Component CPMBPC 810) support package SP04.
