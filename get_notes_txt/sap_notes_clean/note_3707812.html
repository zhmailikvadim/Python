Symptom

You have moved an HU to a staging bin with a product warehouse task. This has updated the delivery item with a PWP 'Putaway Planned' document flow link. If you create another product warehouse task to a different staging bin, the system updates the delivery item with the PWP DocFlow again, leading to a blocked delivery item with error '/SCDL/BO_VALIDATION009 - The quantity of role "Put Away Planned" is greater than the delivery quantity'.

Other Terms

PWP PWC TOR #WOP_INCON_DOCFLOW_PWP staging area group work center work-center in staging area door gr area TU

Solution

Implement the Correction Instruction.
