Symptom

You are using the transaction /US4G/UOP_TRIG_PROC to order TAF7 (RUC7) for a feed-in market location (MaLo). However, the sent ORDERS 17121 message contains the products for the consumption metering location (MeLo).

Other Terms

Rate-use-case 7, Tarifanwendungsfall 7

Reason and Prerequisites

The method /US4G/IF_BADI_MP_DSO~DETERMINE_MEASUREMENT_PRODUCT in the delivered BAdI implementation /US4G/CL_BADI_MP_DSO for BAdI /US4G/BADI_MP_DSO incorrectly determines the energy direction for the MeLo.

Solution

Implement the SAP Note.

It will change the logic of method /US4G/IF_BADI_MP_DSO~DETERMINE_MEASUREMENT_PRODUCT in /US4G/CL_BADI_MP_DSO so that the energy direction of the MaLo is used to determine the BDEW products for MaLo and MeLo.
