Symptom

Authorization issue in F1702 (Schedule Purchasing Jobs - Advanced app)

Environment

  • SAP Fiori 
  • SAP S/4 Hana

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Fiori app F1702 (Schedule Purchasing Jobs).
  2. Attempt to schedule or view available purchasing jobs: not all jobs are visible.
  3. Sign in with a privileged user with more authorizations and verify that more jobs are visible.

Cause

Missing athorizations

Resolution

  1. Reproduce the behavior in F1702 and immediately run transaction SU53 to capture any authorization errors.
  2. Open transaction SU56.
  3. Choose the menu path Authorization Values -> Different User/Authorization Objects.
  4. Enter the affected user and specify the authorization object/field as S_PROGRAM (field S_PROGNAM).
  5. Review the results to identify missing authorizations related to the programs behind the F1702 job templates (for example, scheduling agreement release).
  6. Assign the missing authorizations to the user's roles in line with your security policy and retest in F1702.
  7. If the issue persists after adjusting authorizations, repeat SU53/SU56 to confirm no further authorization failures occur.
