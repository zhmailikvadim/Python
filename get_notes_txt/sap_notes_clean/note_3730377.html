Symptom

Company code can still be updated or added in MDG even if the FI Vendor role is not included in the BP roles.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Master Data Governance

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Remove the FI Vendor role for the Business Partner in transaction BP.
  2. In MDG for Business Partner, open the same Business Partner and navigate to company code data.
  3. Add or update company code information and observe that changes are allowed despite the missing FI Vendor role in BP.

Resolution

SAP MDG does not support the “role” concept in the same way as transaction BP in the ERP system. The role-based behavior known from transaction BP does not exist in the standard BP/Customer/Supplier APIs used in MDG.

Therefore, if there is a requirement to enforce specific behavior depending on a BP role (for example, mandatory fields, visibility restrictions, or role-dependent validations), this is not available out-of-the-box in MDG.
