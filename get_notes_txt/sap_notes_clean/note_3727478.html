Symptom

You cannot edit the plant field in service confirmations for service part items in non-reservation scenarios.

Other Terms

Service Confirmation, Service Part, BUS2000142, non-reservation scenario.

Reason and Prerequisites

The system does not allow you to edit the plant field in service confirmations for service parts in non-reservation scenarios.

Solution

After implementing this SAP Note, the system allows you to edit the plant field for service parts in non-reservation scenarios. You can now change the plant during confirmation.
