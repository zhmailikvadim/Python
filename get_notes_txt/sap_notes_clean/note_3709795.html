Symptom

Uncertainty regarding the correct procedure for generating the Income Declaration (Informe de Rendimentos) following the replacement of DIRF by eSocial.

Environment

  • HR renewal 

  • SAP S/4HANA 

  • SAP ERP  

  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central Payroll 

Resolution

As of now, no new regulation regarding the Income Declaration (Informe de Rendimentos) has been announced.

Therefore, the generation and delivery of the Income Declaration to employees remain the employer’s responsibility.

  • Generation of the Income Declaration:

    • Use SAP report HBRCCED0, which generates the Income Declaration based on payroll results.

    • The Income Declaration is generated following the same approach used in the previous year, and customers are enabled to issue the statement accordingly.

  • Existing Functionality with eSocial Validation:

    • Data can be compared with eSocial totalizers (report RPU_PAYBR_EFD_EE_SUMM_EVENTS) to identify possible discrepancies.

  • The Income Declaration is a legal report that follows specific regulations requiring the exclusion of negative values. The SAP system is designed to automatically discard such values to ensure compliance with these legal requirements.  The negative values are discarded in class CL_HR_BR_CCED, method DELETE_NEGATIVE_VALUES. For more information, please refer to KBA 2751972 - Income Declaration (HBRCCED0) / DIRF - Difference between program results and detailed log.
