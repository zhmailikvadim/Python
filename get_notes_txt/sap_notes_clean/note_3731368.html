Symptom

  • There is a requirement to display only customer open items with debit balance excluding the credit memos and down payments.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental." 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the app Create Correspondence - SAP08 Open Item List.
  2. Run it for a customer who has open items and at least one down payment.
  3. Generate the customer statement and observe the down payment appears as a credit.

Cause

Resolution

  1. Open the app Create Correspondence - SAP08 Open Item List.
  2. Select the relevant correspondence type for customer statements.
  3. Expand Advanced Parameters.
  4. Use the Posting Key filter to exclude credit postings relevant for credit memos from the Correspondence Open List.
  5. Use the Special G/L Indicator filter to exclude down payment from the Correspondence Open List.


  6. Run the correspondence again and verify the statement now shows only open items (no down payments or credit memos).
  7. Optional: Save these settings as a Correspondence type variant to reuse the prefilled defaults and Advanced Parameters in future runs.
