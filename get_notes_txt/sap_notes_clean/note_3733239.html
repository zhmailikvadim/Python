Symptom

This KBA describes how to add, hide, or reorder quick actions and other cards on the SuccessFactors home page. Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors HCM Suite
  • SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central - Time Sheet
  • SAP SuccessFactors Time Tracking

Resolution

On the SuccessFactors homepage, multiple cards are available to provide users with quick access to the Time Sheet. By default, certain cards are displayed automatically.

To hide unused Time Sheet cards or to add custom cards for business purposes, admin center tool - Manage Home Page can be used.

  • To enable, disable, or add quick actions: Manage Home Page → General → Manage Quick Actions
  • To customize other cards: Manage Home Page → Cards
