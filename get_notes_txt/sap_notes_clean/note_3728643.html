Symptom

  • How do I determine the estimated spend for an SOW Worker?

Resolution

The estimated spend is calculated as below:

Fieldglass will use average of the Rate amounts, Number of positions and Period Worked in Days/Hours/Months to calculate an estimated spend for each role. Sum of estimated spend for each role will be the total SOW Worker budget. Below is an example of how this calculation will be done using number of positions, period and rate amount:

Example:

  • Number of position - 2
  • Period worked - 100 hours
  • Rates:
    • ST/HR - 100 - Committed spend check
    • OT/HR - 1.50 factor of ST/HR - Committed spend check
    • DT/HR - 2.00 factor of ST/HR - Committed spend check

  • Estimated spend - 2 positions * 100 hours * avg. of the three checked rates

    Total SOW Worker estimated spend: Sum of the estimated spend for all roles, if more than one roles defined
