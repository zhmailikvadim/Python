Symptom

When adding a Video Component with Layout configured as "Left Justified With Text" or "Right Justified With Text", the text and video overlaps

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Marketing

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the career site page that contains a Video component with text.
  2. Scroll to the section where the Video component is displayed.
  3. Observe that the text and video render without space and appear to touch or overlap

Cause

When Video component has text and "Enable Spacing" turned on. The setting adjusts the spacing around the entire component (outer margins) rather than adding internal spacing between the text and the video, resulting for the 2 elements to overlap.

Resolution

  1. In Career Site Builder, go to Pages
  2. Click Home > Locale > Brand > Home
  3. Edit the Video component.
  4. Turn off the "Enable Spacing" setting for the component to restore default spacing behavior.
  5. Preview to verify that there is now visible space between the text and the video.
  6. Save and Publish
