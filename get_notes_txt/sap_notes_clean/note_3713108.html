Symptom

You run the UniEmens (RPCUEMI0) report and the following symptoms occur:

  1. The <TipoCoperturaGiorn> element of the first three days of the continuation of an illness absence is filled with 0.
  2. The <TipoCoperturaGiorn> element of all the days of illness absences with 7 days of duration is filled with 0.
  3. The <CodiceEvento> MAL is generated in the <Settimana> section even when there are no indemnity amounts paid for the illness event.
  4. The amount of the daily lost salary for the <DifferenzaAccredito> section does not consider the percentage configured in the Moduli: definizione gruppo sorgente voce retributiva (V_T5F99FW) and Moduli: ampliamento cliente gruppo sorgente voci retributive (V_T5F99FX) table views.
  5. The <DiffAccredito> element's amount is not equal to the sum of all <DiffAccreditoEvento> elements.

Other Terms

Transaction: 

&UNIEMENS (for more information, see SAP Note 2327917)

Illness, Ricaduta, Indennizzo

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason

  1. The system considers the first three days of the continuation of an illness absence as if they were the carenza period of another, independent illness.
  2. The system considers only illnesses longer than 7 days for the generation of <TipoCoperturaGiorn> with values other than 0.
  3. The program does not consider the verification for indemnity amounts paid for the generation of <CodiceEvento> MAL in <Settimana>.
  4. The calculation of the daily lost salary does not consider the percentage from the Moduli: percentuale per un campo (PERCT) field configured in the V_T5F99FW and V_T5F99FX table views.
  5. The <DiffAccredito> element's amount is rounded during the processing of <DiffAccreditoEvento> elements, instead of after the calculation of all amounts from those elements. This causes differences between the amount of lost salary calculated for the <DiffAccredito> element and the sum of all <DiffAccreditoEvento> elements.

These issues are side effect caused by the solution delivered by SAP Note 3695910.

Prerequisite

The following Support Package (SP) level is the minimum prerequisite:

ReleaseSupport Package
S4HCM 102 HR SP 00
S4HCM 101 HR SP 05
S4HCM 100 HR SP 18
SAP_HR 6.08 HR SP C9
SAP_HR 6.04 (EhP6 and EhP7) HR SP K1

Solution

  1. The system considers only the first three days of the first illness absence as the carenza period of an illness with continuation.
  2. The system considers illnesses with duration of 7 days or more for the generation of <TipoCoperturaGiorn> with values other than 0.
  3. The program considers the verification for indemnity amounts paid for the generation of <CodiceEvento> MAL in <Settimana>.
  4. The calculation of the daily lost salary considers the percentage from the PERCT field configured in the V_T5F99FW and V_T5F99FX table views.
  5. The <DiffAccredito> element's amount is rounded after the compilation of all <DiffAccreditoEvento> elements.
