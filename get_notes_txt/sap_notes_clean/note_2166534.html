Symptom

Preferred language is english, but not with the standard setting of 12 hours(am. pm.).

Environment

SAP Manufacturing Execution

Reproducing the Issue

Language setting is english at installation.

Cause

English language at installation means american english with am and pm time format, not with 24 hours.

Resolution

There are two kinds of solution:

  1. Setting browser's language to "English(UK) [en-GB]".
  2. Setting user's language data in Netweaver at Configuration/Identity Management for "en_GB - English(United Kingdom)".
