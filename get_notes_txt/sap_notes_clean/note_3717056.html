Symptom

Error when attempting to create a space in SAP Datasphere : <space_name> already exist.

Environment

SAP Datasphere

Cause

The space <space_name> exists in the Recycle Bin.

Resolution

In order to resolve the issue follow the steps below:

  1. Under Space Management go to Recycle Bin.
  2. Select the space <space_name>.
  3. Either restore it or deleted it and create a brand new one.

Keywords

space, recycle, create, restore, delete
