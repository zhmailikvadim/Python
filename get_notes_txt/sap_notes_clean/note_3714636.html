Symptom

You are replicating materials into SAP Cloud for Customer (C4C) but get the following error:

Quantity type DEN not valid; it is not summable

Environment

SAP Cloud for Customer

Cause

A unit conversion is not allowed. Here is an example:

<QuantityConversion actionCode="04">
    <Quantity unitCode="A93">900</Quantity>
    <CorrespondingQuantity unitCode="KGM">1</CorrespondingQuantity>
</QuantityConversion>

Here, A93 and KGM are both ISO standard codes. A93 is a unit of density (gram per cubic metre, or g/m³) whereas KGM is a unit of mass (kilogram, or kg).

The conversion above, from density to mass, is not permitted, hence the error.

Resolution

Density quantities are considered to be non-summable. Avoid sending density to mass conversions.

See Also

3247514 - Error Message: "Quantity Type ABC Not Valid; It Is Not Summable"
