Symptom

I am attempting to add an invoice to a Statement of Work but the system triggers the error message:
Cannot have duplicate SOW Invoice entries.

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Statement of Work.

  2. Click on Actions > Add & Complete SOW Invoice.

  3. Fill in all the required fields for the invoice.

  4. The system triggers the error message: Cannot have duplicate SOW Invoice entries.

Cause

The invoice name you are trying to add already exists under this Statement of Work Characteristics tab.

Resolution

  1. Open the Statement of Work.

  2. Click on Actions > Add & Complete SOW Invoice.

  3. Enter a unique invoice Name and fill in all required fields.

  4. Click Submit.

  5. The system will now allow the invoice to be created successfully.
