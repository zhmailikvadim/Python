Symptom

When creating or changing a financial transaction, warning message FTR_GUI220 "Partner &1 cannot be used, as per contract &2" is displayed by clicking Check or Save button.

Environment

• Treasury and Risk Management (TRM)
• SAP ERP
• SAP ERP Central Component
• SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP,
• SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
• SAP Financial Supply Chain Management
• SAP S/4HANA
• SAP S/4HANA Finance
• SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create or edit a treasury deal with a contract date that is not within the “Valid from/Valid to” range of the intended counterparty’s role TR0151 (Counterparty).
  2. Assign the business partner as counterparty to the deal.
  3. Observe that warning FTR_GUI220 is displayed stating the role is not valid for the contract date.

Cause

The business partner’s counterparty role (TR0151) validity period does not cover the deal’s contract date.

Resolution

  1. Open transaction BP and navigate to the business partner intended to be used as counterparty.
  2. Select the role “TR0151 Counterparty”.
  3. Choose “Role Detail” to display the role’s Valid from and Valid to dates.
  4. Verify whether the role validity covers the deal’s contract date.
  5. Adjust the role validity dates so they include the deal’s contract date, or select a counterparty whose role is valid for that date.
  6. Save the changes and retry assigning the counterparty in the deal.
