Symptom

A Nota Fiscal was issued and subsequently reversed.
However, the reversal was not properly registered with SEFAZ, keeping the invoice active with the agency.
The invoice was only canceled latter after the issuance.
SAP system reversed the Nota Fiscal and the material for the customer, but SEFAZ did not recognize the cancellation, and the invoice remains active.
With that scenario the user wants to keep the invoice active, since cancellation with the Sefaz will no longer be possible

For this the user wants to reverse the the reversal made (canceling the “reversal of the reversal”) for both the invoice and the material sent to the customer.

Environment

SAP Document and Reporting Compliance, Outbound Nota Fiscal

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Issue a Nota Fiscal in SAP.
  2. Perform a reversal of the Nota Fiscal a couple of hours after issuance.
  3. Observe that SEFAZ does not recognize the cancellation, leaving the invoice active.

Cause

Sefaz communication issue. 

Resolution

In SAP system there is no such process to cancel the cancellation, however there are some hints below to be followed.

  1. Create a credit memo request referencing the original sales document/invoice for traceability using transaction VA01.
  • Use a document type tailored for fiscal return scenarios.
  • Ensure pricing condition setup offsets the original Nota Fiscal amounts.
  1. Generate the billing document (credit memo or custom billing type) using transaction VF01.
  • Ensure this billing type does not trigger goods movement.
  • Set correct CFOP/CSOSN per tax rules for fiscal return without goods movement.
  • Reference the original Nota Fiscal access key in the DRC eDocument additional data/fields.
  1. Submit the new eDocument for authorization via the DRC Monitor (eDocument Brazil – Nota Fiscal outbound).
  • Once SEFAZ authorizes the counter Nota Fiscal, validate the legal pair: Original Nota Fiscal (Autorizado) + Counter Nota Fiscal (Autorizado).
  1. Ensure proper accounting and accounts receivable adjustments:
  • Verify that the new credit memo nets to zero or neutralizes the original invoice per the chart of accounts design.
  • Reset clearing and re-clear appropriately if any clearing artifacts exist from earlier attempts.
  1. Perform housekeeping in DRC Monitor:
  • Do not retry the old cancellation.
  • Ensure the final status for the original Nota Fiscal is "Autorizado," and the newly created counter Nota Fiscal is also "Autorizado."

Alternative Resolution:

  • If the state still allows cancellation (110111) for this document and business approves:
  1. Re-attempt Nota Fiscal cancellation with a compliant justification.
  2. If cancellation is accepted, the counter Nota Fiscal flow is not required.

Important Notes:

  • State-specific rules may apply regarding the correct "Finalidade" and CFOP for "no goods shipped" and "cancellation window closed." Consult a tax advisor for confirmation.
  • CC-e cannot change the fiscal status or neutralize taxes.
  • Inutilização is not applicable after authorization; error 241 is expected since the number is already used.
