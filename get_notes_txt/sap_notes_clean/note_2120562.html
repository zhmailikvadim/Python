Reason and Prerequisites

This symptom is caused by a program error.

You use SAP EC 3.0 with a Support Package or patch level lower than the level specified in the Support Packages & Patches section.

Solution

Download the complete solution for your Support Package level using the appropriate link in the Support Packages & Patches section and implement it in your system.

Note: Patches for ﻿Env.Compl. Centr. Services 3.0﻿ require a server restart to become fully active.
