Symptom

During the display of vendors or customers for the business partner transaction (BP), no navigation takes place if the business partner role is hidden. The system issues error message R1 206.

Other Terms

Role, role group, redirection, BP, vendor, customer, LIFNR, KUNNR, R1206, QA02, QA03, QI02, QI03

Reason and Prerequisites

The problem occurs if the roles have been intentionally hidden to meet certain business requirements and role groups are used for the business partners.

Solution

Implement this SAP Note to navigate to the assigned business partner role grouping instead of to the hidden business partner role.
