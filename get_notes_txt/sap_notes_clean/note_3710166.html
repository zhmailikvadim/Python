Symptom

  • Clarification is requested on transferring Minimum and Maximum Supply Flexible constraints from IBP Time Series Supply Planning to ePPDS in S4.
  • The IBP help documentation does not specify the Source Key Figures required for this transfer.
  • Inquiry on whether these constraints can be transferred from IBP  to S/4 HANA using CI-DS

Environment

  • SAP Integrated Business Planning for Supply Chain (SCM-IBP)
  • Time-Series-Based Integration
  • SAP Cloud Integration for data services (CI-DS)
  • SAP ERP, supply chain integration add-on for SAP Integrated Business Planning (SCM-IBP-INT-ECC)
  • SAP S/4HANA, supply chain integration add-on for SAP Integrated Business Planning (SCM-IBP-INT-ECC)
  • ePPDS in S/4HANA

Resolution

  1. Production Receipts Min/Max keyfigures can not be integrated using the standard IBP JOB templates at the moment.
  2. As standard templates do not cover specific key figures and consider submitting an enhancement request via SAP Influence Portal by following note 2479068 
  3. However implement a custom development solution can transfer these key Figures from IBP to ePPDS in S/4 HANA
