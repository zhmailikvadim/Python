Symptom

It is unclear when the Telecom Expense Management (TEM) data from an iOS device is collected by Afaria.

Environment

  • SAP Afaria 7 Service Pack 4
  • SAP Afaria 7 Service Pack 5

Resolution

Not only is the TEM data collected every time the device user manually taps "connect" on the Afaria client, but it is also collected on a randomized daily schedule.  In addition, it can also be collected during the apply policies and device refresh schedules.
