Symptom

An InfoPackage run, whether started manually or from within a process chain, remains in overall status "yellow" even though the associated background job has finished successfully. The InfoPackage monitor shows no errors, and data processing appears to have completed, but the status does not progress to "green".

Environment

SAP NetWeaver 7.x

Cause

  • In source system, an error is reported in extraction job (BIREQU_ ): Error in QRFC playback FM: TRFC_QOUT_PLAYBACK
  • In some cases, TRFC_QOUT_PLAYBACK may misinterpret a sequence of bytes in the payload as an RFCID header, which triggers an RFC_PROTOCOL_ERROR.
  • This is a transient condition in the playback/check phase only; the actual application data is subsequently processed and received correctly.

Resolution

Implement the following correction notes: 

  • 3288784 - RSA7 shows 'Error in QRFC playback FM'
  • 3318367 - RSA7 shows 'Error in QRFC playback FM' (2)
