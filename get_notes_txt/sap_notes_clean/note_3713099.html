Symptom

Additional Informations for waste disposal order items have a length of 255 characters (field ORDERTXT of table EWA_ORDER_OBJECT). After transferring this text to Planning&Dispatching (P&D), this addtional information was cut to 80 characters.

Other Terms

/PLCE/PDADDITIONAL_TEXT, PLCE, PLCP

Solution

Implement the correction instructions.

The underlying domain /PLCE/PDADDITIONAL_TEXT in P&D was extended to 255 characters to hold the complete ORDERTXT. Dependent data definition views were adjusted to handle the larger contents.
