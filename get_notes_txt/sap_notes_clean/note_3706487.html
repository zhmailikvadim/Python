Symptom

  • Spaces created in the SAP Fiori Launchpad are not visible in the Spaces area of the home page.
  • How to add spaces in home page. 

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition 2602

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log into the SAP Fiori Launchpad system.
  2. Create a new space.
  3. Create a new page.
  4. Assign the space to the role.
  5. Assign the role to the user.
  6. Observe that the space is not displayed in the Spaces area of the home page. 

Cause

Spaces are not added in home page. 

Resolution

1. Click All Spaces. 

2. Pin the space. 

Keywords

spaces not visible, pages session, SAP Fiori Launchpad, add, space, pin, remove
