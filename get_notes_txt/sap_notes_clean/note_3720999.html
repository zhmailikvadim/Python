Symptom

  • Adding a Fiori app to a catalog and running PRGN_COMPARE_ROLE_MENU causes unexpected deletion of authorization objects when merging the role.
  • Syncing a catalog in PFCG results in unrelated authorizations being deleted.
  • Switching to the ONLY VARIANTS in the applications tab deletes defaults for the other applications.

Environment

SAP Netweaver

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Add an app to the catalog associated with a role.
  2. Execute the PRGN_COMPARE_ROLE_MENU report.
  3. Select all line items and adapt changes.
  4. Click on "Role Change" and choose "Change Authorization Data" or "Expert Mode".
  5. Generate the role and observe the deletion of authorization objects, including tcodes and services.

Resolution

Implement the SAP Note 3633537 to correct the behavior.

Additional explanation on the behavior using SU25: 

SAP Basis 816 introduces enhanced functionality for the merge process in SU25 Step 2C.
SU25 Step 2C includes a new functionality called "Update of applications and default variants".
This functionality ensures that the necessary data for the role's menu is added, preventing the deletion of authorization objects during the merge process.
This step can be used as a workaround for the behavior until the correction 3633537    is implemented.R
Refer to SAP Note 3633537 for further details on the corrected behavior in PRGN_COMPARE_ROLE_MENU.
