Symptom

  • Freight order notes containing multiple lines are not displayed fully.
  • Clicking on the content field causes the text to disappear.

Environment

  • SAP S/4HANA 2020+ 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Maintain a text with multiple lines in the freight order tab "Notes".
  2. Reload the freight order.
  3. Attempt to read the newly created text by clicking in the content field of the text.
  4. Observe that the text are not displayed fully.

Cause

This is a known issue in the Unified Rendering framework of the SAP_UI component. 

Resolution

According to note 3714143, the correction will be delivered in their next cumulative patch planned to be released in March. You can find more information on the update process of UR in note 2500800.
