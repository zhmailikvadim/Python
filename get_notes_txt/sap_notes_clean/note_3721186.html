Symptom

When you send material master data with plants that do not exist yet on the database, the E1MARC1 segment is included in the IDoc, whereas the E1MARCM segment is not included in the IDoc. This leads to an inconsistent IDoc with syntax error.

Other Terms

MASTERIDOC_CREATE_MATMAS, MASTERIDOC_CREATE_MATMASMASS, MASTERIDOC_CREATE_MATMAS_MASS, MATMAS, E1MARC1, BD10, BD21

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions.

After implementation, neither E1MARC1 nor E1MARCM are part of the IDoc, if the corresponding plant does not yet exist on the database.
