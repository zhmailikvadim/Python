Symptom

Interview results card displays the following error message "the interviewer has not yet provided a rating for this applicant"

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the new applicant workbench.
  2. Select a candidate where competencies have not been rated.
  3. Observe the interview results card displaying the error message: "the interviewer has not yet provided a rating for this applicant".

Cause

Competencies have not been rated.

Resolution

This has been confirmed as expected behavior.

You must create at least one competency and provide a rating for it to resolve the error message displayed in the interview results card.
