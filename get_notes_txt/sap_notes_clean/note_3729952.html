Symptom

You are integrating process orders from PP to PP/DS. You observe that the duration of phases in PP is not correct although you followed the recommendations from SAP Consulting Note 783512. You have maintained your shifts of the PP/DS-relevant workcenters identically in PP and PP/DS. Furthermore, you have set the "Scheduling with breaks" indicator in the scheduling parameters for the order type in transaction OPUZ.

Other Terms

process orders, shifts, workcenters, scheduling with breaks, OPUZ, SAP Consulting Note 783512

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

Implement the correction attached to this SAP Note or the relevant Support Package.
