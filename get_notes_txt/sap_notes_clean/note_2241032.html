Symptom

When you process multiple invoice revenue accounting items (RAIs) in one period, if one invoice RAI has error, other invoice RAIs that belong to other contracts also fail in processing.

Here is an example:

  1. Create contract 1 with a performance obligation 1 (POB 1) and contract 2 with POB 2. Both POBs have the quantity of 3 units.
  2. Post goods issues for 1 unit of POB 1 and POB 2 respectively.
  3. In POB Detail UI, do some changes to make validation result of POB 1 error.
  4. Issue the invoice for POB 1 and POB 2 and change the invoice price for both POBs.

After step 4, invoice RAIs fail in processing.

This is incorrect. The invoice RAI for POB 1 fails due to error validation result. But invoice RAI for POB 2 should not have failed in the RAI processing.

Other Terms

FARR, Revenue Accounting, Invoice

Reason and Prerequisites

This is because mo_msg_handler in the class CL_FARR_INVOICE_MGMT is not initialized when the system calls the method IF_FARR_CONTRACT_INVOICE~PROCESS_INVOICE of class CL_FARR_CONTRACT_MGMT. The error messages of the former contracts in mo_msg_handler affect the latter contract. The latter contract therefore is marked as a failed contract.

Solution

Apply this note.
