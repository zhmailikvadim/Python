Symptom

While creating a Business Partner (BP), the system throws an error message “Fill the Street” even though the Street field has already been entered in the BP address screen. 

Environment

SAP ERP

SAP S/4 HANA

Reproducing the Issue

Steps to reproduce the error:

  1. Create a BP under FLVN00 role (Vendor role)
  2. Enter all mandatory fields
  3. Maintain Street in Address section
  4. Save BP
  5. System throws error “An entry is required in field "Street”

Cause

Field groups 0098 and 0099 are currently maintained as Required Entry in the BDT configuration under the Business Partner – Vendor (FLVN00) role.  

Resolution

Change these fields to Not Specified instead of Required Entry. The following path can be used to change them:

SPRO → SAP Reference IMG → Cross-Application Components → SAP Business Partner → Business Partner → Basis Settings → Field Groupings → Configure Field Attributes per BP Role → FLVN00 → BP Search

Keywords

street, fill the street, mandatory, required, optional, BP, FLVN00, BP Search, field groupings, field group 0088, field group 0089, BDT configuration.
