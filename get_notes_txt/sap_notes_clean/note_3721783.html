Symptom

If a currency is inactivated effective a certain date, users cannot select it in past-dated records, even if it was active at that time.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central - Compensation Information

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Inactivate the currency object effective from January 1, 2026.
  2. Attempt to make changes to past-dated records (e.g., records dated before Dec 1, 2025) in the Compensation Information portlet.
  3. Observe that the currency is no longer available for selection in past-dated records.

Resolution

  • Engineering team has confirmed this is known issue and it will be fixed in next release 1H2026.
  • You may contact your Customer Success Manager or call our hotline number to know status of this enhancement request ECT-266489.
