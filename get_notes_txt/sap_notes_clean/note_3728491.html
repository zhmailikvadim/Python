Symptom

In case a delivery group contains an ABC item and a non ABC item and both of them have the same product plant combination and the ABC item has a higher priority, then it can happen that the ATP check confirms more quantity than is physically available.

Other Terms

aATP; ABC; Delivery Group; Overconfirmation

Reason and Prerequisites

The reason is that the ABC group frees quantity after its first correlation execution that can be used by the non ABC item and afterwards executes another correlation execution afterwards, using the buffered quantity already freed. With this correction, ABC is executing a re-check of the basic methods, including PAC, during its correlation execution in order to prevent such overconfirmations.

Solution

Apply the correction instructions for this note or upgrade to a SAP S/4HANA support package that contains this correction.
