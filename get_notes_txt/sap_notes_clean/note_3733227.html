Symptom

  • After submission of eDocuments for Poland (KSeF), the status shows "Rejected by DRCC".
  • Retry or resubmit options are not available.

Environment

  • SAP ERP
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP Document and Reporting Compliance service

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Submit eDocument in the EDOC_COCKPIT for Poland DRC.
  2. Error "Rejected by DRCC" received by KSeF.

Cause

Missing SAP note.

Resolution

Fully implement and activate SAP Note 3722249 - Electronic Document Processing for Poland - KSeF: Incorrect 'Rejected by DRCC' status and Outdated XML display in the affected environment. Don't forget to check for pre requisites and fully implement those aswell.

See Also

KBA 3346323 - eDocument Poland B2B(KSeF) Troubleshooting Guide
