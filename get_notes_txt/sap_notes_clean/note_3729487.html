Symptom

Component: Incident Management
Module: Review Incident Notifications in Business Workplace

You review notification tasks in SAP Business Workplace (Transaction SBWP). When you double-select the notifications of "Review and complete Incident" or "Review and complete Incident Investigation" or "Perform Investigation Step" or "Assign HR absence to Incident", the system opens a new browser window with an outdated SAP NetWeaver Business Client (Transaction NWBC) URL instead of SAP Fiori Launchpad URL.

Other Terms

CL_EHHSS_INC_WF_ADAPTER, CL_EHHSS_HR_ABS_WF_ADAPTER, CL_EHHSS_INC_INRES_WF_ADAPTER, CL_EHHSS_INC_WF_ADAPTER

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason:
Program error.

Prerequisites:
For information about the validity of the corrections, see the correction instructions.

Solution

After implementing this SAP Note, the system opens a new browser window with the correct SAP Fiori Launchpad URL when you double-select incident notifications in SAP Business Workplace.

The "Support Packages" section lists the Support Packages that contain these corrections.

Alternatively, you can implement the attached correction instructions.
