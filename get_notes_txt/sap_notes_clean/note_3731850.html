Symptom

What is the difference between SAP Account Manager and SAP Account Executive? 

Environment

SAP for Me

Resolution

The SAP Account Executive is a person which is responsible to handle issues related to contracts and other non-technical topics as informed on KBA 2626344 - Who is the SAP Account Executive for my company? 
Every company has a SAP Account Executive associated to them, they can be contacted by following the procedure described on the KBA above. 

The SAP Account Manager is a service provided by SAP where users can aggregate their S and P-users, it the way how users update and interact with their SAP Universal ID. On the SAP Account Manager it is possible to edit information of the account and explore all areas of SAP to acquire more information of SAP products.
