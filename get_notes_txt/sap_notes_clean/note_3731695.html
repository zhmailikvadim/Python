Symptom

After changing the priority of a maintenance order, the Final Due Date (FDD) is automatically recalculated based on the new priority instead of remaining unchanged.

Other Terms

Final Due Date (FDD), Maintenance Order, SAP GUI Transactions (IW31, IW32), Web Dynpro applications (Create Maintenance Order, Change Maintenance Order), Manage Maintenance Order (F5241).

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error.

Solution

To resolve this issue, apply the correction from this note.
