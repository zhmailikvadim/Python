Symptom

After a contract has been rolled over, there are superfluous/incorrect planned records in the database in the period of the new condition header.

The planned records belong to conditions from the old condition header. There are no new conditions in the rollover contract for condition type.

In the system, the indicator C_FLG_ADJ_PREVIOUS_KOPOS is set in the method SET_GLOBAL_FLAGS (BAdI FVD_LOAN_MASTERDATA) (see SAP Note 2327030).

Other Terms

FNP0

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error
