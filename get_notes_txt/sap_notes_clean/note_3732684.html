Symptom

You run the Individual Income Tax Report by Month (HCNCMTX0) report. When the Tax Entry Date entered in Infotype 0531 differs from the Hire Date, both the Tax Entry Date and the Hire Date can be displayed in the Standard Monthly Tax Data section of ALV output view.

However, in the aforementioned case, the system only displays the Tax Entry Date in all four Individual Income Tax Basic Info sections of ALV output view.

This SAP Note enhances the report to display the Hire Date instead of the Tax Entry Date in the Individual Income Tax Basic Info sections of ALV output view.

Other Terms

PCMTXCN2, PCMTXCN3

Reason and Prerequisites

This is an enhancement.

Solution

Do either of the following:

  • Apply the Support Package;
  • Implement the corresponding correction instructions.
