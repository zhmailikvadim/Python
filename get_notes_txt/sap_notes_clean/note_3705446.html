Symptom

When checking replicated hours a different value is expected to be replicated from SAP Employee Central (EC) to SAP Employee Central Payroll (ECP) IT 2010.

Environment

  • Time data replication from EC to ECP
  • Time data replication from EC to S/4Hana private cloud
  • Time data replication from EC to ERP On Premise

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Access the EC system and open the employee's time sheet week.
  2. Approved time sheet
  3. Replicate the time sheet data to ECP IT 2010.
  4. Observe that the replicated values show different hours than expected. 

Cause

The value replicated is the time valuation result and customer is checking a different value. 

Resolution

This KBA explains one of the ways to verify the time valuation results that will replicate from EC to payroll system that may be useful to understand the hours replicates. The steps to follow are:

1. Access the EC system.
2. Navigate to the employee's profile and open the time sheet.
3. Click on "Trace" and note the external ID that appears for the employee's time sheet.
4. Proceed to "Manage Data" and "Employee Time Sheet," then search for the external ID.
5. Open the time sheet and scroll down to examine the time valuation results which are payroll type and check their quantities.
6. Ensure that the time valuation results and the quantities replicated to the payroll system coincide.
