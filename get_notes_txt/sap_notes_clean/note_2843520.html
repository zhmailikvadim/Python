Symptom

  • SuccessFactors mobile app has a Demo link at the top right corner.
  • If end user clicks the Demo link they are redirect to a demo environment instead of a live production environment

Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Mobile

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Download the SuccessFactors mobile app
  2. Open the app
  3. Click "Demo" on top right of the screen as screenshot below:

  1. It will open a demo environment

Resolution

The Demo feature is available exclusively on iOS devices and cannot be disabled or hidden.

Its purpose is to allow users to preview the app experience without requiring a login account.
