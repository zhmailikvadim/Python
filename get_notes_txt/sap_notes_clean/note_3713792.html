Symptom

Why can't I see the Reports tab in my SAP Business Network account?

Cause

There are two possible reasons for this:

  1. You don’t have any customer relationships yet. If no customers have established a trading relationship with your account, features like Reports will remain inaccessible.
  2. You're a sub-user without the right permissions. Your company’s account has user roles, and you are assigned to a sub-user role with limited permissions. 

Resolution

For reason 1, please reach out to your customers and ask them to establish a trading relationship with your account.

For reason 2, please contact your administrator to get the related permissions to access the Reports tab.
