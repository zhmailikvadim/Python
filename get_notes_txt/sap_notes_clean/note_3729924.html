Symptom

Other Terms

FY 088 FY088 FCC1 RFCCSSTT Settlement FBRC CREDIT_CARD_POST_REFUSAL

Reason and Prerequisites

The payment document you try to reverse contains a line item for exchange rate differences.

Solution

 Implement the attached correction.
