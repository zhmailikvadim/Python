Symptom

When creating a Purchase Requisition (PR) and copying an item line, the valuation price is not automatically updated after changing the material in the copied item.

Environment

•Materials Management (MM)
•SAP ERP Central Component
•SAP ERP
•SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP
•SAP enhancement package for SAP ERP, version for SAP HANA
•SAP S/4HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create PR in ME51N, add item line 10 that has material ID, the valuation price will be determined from material master data. Or we can maintain valuation price manually in Valuation tab.
  2. Copy item 10 as PR item 20.
  3. Change material ID in  PR item 20, the valuation price will not get updated nor be cleared.

Cause

This is system standard behavior.

The valuation price does not trigger re-fetching of the valuation price after a material change.

Resolution

After copying line item, the valuation price has to be rechecked and can be changed by manually, to ensure the correct price is accessed.

See Also

KBA 3235760 - Purchasing group is not redetermined with 'Copy' function

Keywords

PR, ME51N, valuation price, copy, material, change, Material Master
