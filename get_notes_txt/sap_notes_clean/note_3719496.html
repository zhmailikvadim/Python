Symptom

If you open the view settings in the tables contained in the application Schedule S "Manage Parts and Sections" or "Manage NAIC Company Code & ID", some incorrect fields are available for selection under Columns, Filter, Sort, and Group.

Affected tables in the application Schedule S "Manage Parts and Sections":

  • Search Result Part/Section
  • Rows
  • Columns
  • KPI columns:
    • Assignment of Entry Codes
    • Assignment of Calculation Bases
    • Assignment of Hierarchy Key Figures
  • Accounting unit attributes (in rows and columns)
  • Calculation:
    • Calculation Definition



Affected tables in the "Manage NAIC Company Code & ID" application:

  • Business Partner Search Result
  • Authorization Levels

Other Terms

Schedule S

NAIC

Reason and Prerequisites

You use the applications for Schedule S data extraction.

Solution

If you open the view settings in the tables contained in the Schedule S application "Manage Parts and Sections" or "Manage NAIC Company Code & ID", the correct fields are available for selection under Columns, Filter, Sort, and Group.
