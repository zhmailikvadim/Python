Symptom

I want to change the Rate Category in a Job Posting Template. 

Resolution

  1. Sign into your SAP Fieldglass account

  2. Click Admin Configuration (Gear Icon) > Job Posting Template

  3. Search for the Template ID and select it from the results > Edit

  4. Click Continue until you reach the Accounting tab

  5. Under the Rates section, click + Add or Remove Rates

  6. Select the desired Rate Categories and click Add Selected > Update

  7. Click Continue > Update
