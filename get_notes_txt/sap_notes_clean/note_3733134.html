Symptom

In the My Change Requests Monitoring app, users experience display issues, where column headers may not correctly reflect the underlying data, columns appear too narrow and cannot be expanded, and the horizontal scroll bar is missing, preventing access to columns on the right side of the screen. 

Environment

  • SAP Master Data Governance
  • SAP S/4 HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in to SAP Fiori Launchpad.
  2. Open the "My Change Requests Monitoring" app.
  3. Click on the Settings button.
  4. Add all available columns.
  5. Observe:
    • Columns cannot be resized.
    • No horizontal scroll bar is available.
    • Right-side columns are not accessible.

Cause

Incorrect configuration in "Displayed Columns" settings.

Resolution

Adjust the number of displayed columns to enable horizontal scrolling:

  1. Navigate to: Settings → Display → Displayed Columns: Number Of
  2. Reduce the value to a lower number (e.g., 34 or less).
  3. Once reduced, the horizontal scroll bar will appear, allowing access to additional columns.
