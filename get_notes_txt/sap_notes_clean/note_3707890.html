Symptom

After performing a contract extension from SOM side, the terms for recurring billing plan items are set incorrectly, resulting in incomplete term coverage.

Other Terms

FKKBIX_BIP, TERM_TO, TERM_FROM, Recurring BIP Item.

Reason and Prerequisites

Incorrect handling of the TERM_FROM date. This applies to the recurring items only.

Solution

Implement this SAP Note or the corresponding Support Package
