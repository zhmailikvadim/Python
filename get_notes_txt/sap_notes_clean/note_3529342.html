Symptom

A selection is defined for (e)SPP services on /SAPAPO/PE_SELECTION, for example, for product group AND replenishment indicator. 
System returns products with the correct product group but replenishment indicators that are not on the selection.

Environment

  • SAP Supply Chain Management (SCM)
  • SAP Supply Chain Management Service Parts Planning (SCM-APO-SPP)
  • S4 HANA
  • SAP Supply Chain Management extended Service Parts Planning (eSPP)

Reproducing the Issue

For example, a selection is defined for product groups X, Y and Z AND replenishment indicators SL, SN, ST. Then, for example, EOQ/SFT or other SPP services such as DRP run in the system, and when the log is checked, it is noticed that location-products were processed which are assigned to group X, but have replenishment indicator NS, which was not on the selection.

Cause

This is the system standard behavior.

Resolution

Most of (e)SPP main services work based on the BOD. This may be due to the logic said service implements (like in the case of the DRP service), or due to the fact that some consistency constraints have to be kept along the BOD (like e.g. in the cases of the Stocking and Destocking services). Such a service will always operate on all locations (from the corresponding BOD) if only the product itself (or one of the locationproducts) was selected by the selection and package-building phases of the PSM execution. 

Due to this extension of the worklist locationproducts may show up as being processed even if they were excluded from the selection. This is by design, and is not due to an error in the program.
