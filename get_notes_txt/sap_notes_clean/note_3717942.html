Symptom

  • Background execution finishes successfully even when the step program generates errors or warning messages related to the application program.
  • The application step program creates a spool with errors written inside the spool.

Environment

ABAP Platform

Cause

The background job framework marks a job as "Finished" when the job step program completes.  The framework does not check the content of the spool messages created by the step programs for specific errors or warnings.

Resolution

  1. From the BTC side, the job is completed with the status Finished if the job step program finishes.  This is working as designed.
  2. BTC does not check the content of spools or other application related errors and warnings for particular messages.
  3. If required, check with the application program owner / component to determine any specific handling or monitoring of errors generated by their program.
