Symptom

I would like to change the status of an item to Rejected in the SAP Fieldglass system, but I am not sure who the appropriate person is to contact for this.

Resolution

Please contact the Program Management Office team (PMO), since they are the system administrators and can help you further on that matter. 

SAP Fieldglass support does not have the authority to make changes in the production environment, which is why SAP Fieldglass support agents are unable to proceed with these actions.
