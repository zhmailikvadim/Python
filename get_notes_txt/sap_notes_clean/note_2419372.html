Symptom

When processing invoice rai items, invoice amount in FARR_D_INVOICE and inv_amt_delta in FARR_D_DEFITEM don't match each other, and these inconsistent data are generated without any check.

Other Terms

invoice amount, inv_amt_delta, revenue accounting

Reason and Prerequisites

No consistency check between invoice amount and inv_amt_delta are executed before saving data to DB.

Solution

Apply this note.
