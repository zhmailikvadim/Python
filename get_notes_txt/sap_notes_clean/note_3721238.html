Symptom

You use the clinical work station to manage your processes. If you select a patient in the departures view and try to open the patient list, you notice that the patient profile displays an error message. Although it determines the correct department, it determines the incorrect POE (nursing organizational unit).

Other Terms

CWS, POE, UIBB, Patient Profile, Departure View, Departures

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

The patient profile now determines the correct POE if you select a patient in the departures view and open the patient list.

Import the specified Support Packages to solve the problem. You can implement the corrections in advance.
