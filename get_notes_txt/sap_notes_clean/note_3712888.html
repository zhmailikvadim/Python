Symptom

  • Time approvals in My Inbox do not display project-related information for certain entries.
  • Approvals appear in My Inbox with blank fields for Project, Work Package, or Work Item.
  • This behavior occurs even though other time entries have project-related details.

Environment

SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to My Inbox.
  2. Observe approvals that do not contain any project-related information in the approval details.

Cause

  • The system handles time entry approvals based on accounting object types, not exclusively by project.
  • Time entries recorded against non-project cost objects such as Cost Center, Internal Order, or generic task types do not have associated Project, WBS, or Work Package information, resulting in blank fields for these columns.
  • My Inbox supports approval tasks for all types of time entries, including non-project-related ones, which is standard behavior in SAP S/4HANA Cloud Public Edition.

Resolution

To further confirm this behavior, please check the below:

  1. Open the specific time entry in My Inbox.
  2. Access the workflow log for the entry.
  3. Verify the accounting object referenced by the time entry to confirm its type.
