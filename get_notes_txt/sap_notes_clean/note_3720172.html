Symptom

User tries to access SAP Ariba Invoicing application and gets the following error message

  • Cannot find a space assigned to you. Please contact your system administrator.

Cause

Administrator role is missing from the user profile.

Resolution

The visibility of application in the launchpad depends on the assigned roles.

In this case the user has to assign the Administrator roles in BTP.

See Also

Assigning Administrator Roles/Groups to Yourself or to the Administrator | SAP Help Portal
