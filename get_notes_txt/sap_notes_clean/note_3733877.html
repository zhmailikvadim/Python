Symptom

The formation type of "Integration with SAP Business Data Cloud" has been created and is in Ready status, but it still does not appear in the BDC Cockpit System Landscape. 

Environment

SAP Business Data Cloud

Resolution

Each formation with "Integration with SAP Business Data Cloud" must include one SAP Business Data Cloud cockpit tenant.

See official documentation: https://help.sap.com/docs/business-data-cloud/administering-sap-business-data-cloud/creating-sap-business-data-cloud-formations#sap-business-data-cloud-core-components
