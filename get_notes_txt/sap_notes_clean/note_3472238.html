Symptom

After inbound delivery creation the item split information is missing on the freight unit product items which refer to the same inbound delivery item and are packaged into mutliple package items. The issue occurs in case the packages are not distributed to the delivery upon creation. This can lead to errors during repacking of those product items in freight documents because of failed validations. The product items should be merged in case they are packed underneath the same parent item, but the merge of the items cannot be executed successfully because of the missing split information.

Reason and Prerequisites

 Program error

Solution

Implement the attached Correction Instruction or the relevant Support Package.
