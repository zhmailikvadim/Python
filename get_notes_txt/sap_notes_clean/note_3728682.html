Symptom

In the S/4HANA System with a Central Purchasing scenario the search for material groups does not work in the Fiori app 'Process Purchase Requisitions Centrally' (F3290).

Searching for material group XYZ gives back no results, despite the presence of purchase requisitions with that material group.

Environment

Central HUB 2XT scenario

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Start Fiori app 'Process Purchase Requisitions Centrally' (F3290).
  2. Search for material group XYZ.
  3. Observe that no search results are returned, even though purchase requisitions with the material group exist.

Cause

The material group search uses the 3TE operation with the service MM_PUR_GF_BULK_EXTRACT_SRV, which was not activated in the system.

Resolution

1. Activate the OData service MM_PUR_GF_BULK_EXTRACT_SRV in the System.

  • Navigate to transaction OLME.
  • Go to Central Procurement > Settings in Hub System > Configure Control Plane for the APIs of Central Procurement.

2. Schedule the job in the Fiori app 'Schedule Import of Purchasing Documents'.

  • Run a full import initially.
  • Schedule a delta load subsequently.

3. Run the job in the Fiori app 'Import Texts for Purchasing Documents from Connected Systems'.
