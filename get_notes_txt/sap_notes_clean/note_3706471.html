Symptom

When an onboardee is rehired on a previous employment, the onboardee data is replicated from SuccessFactors to ERP through BIB. For employees with a daily or weekly payroll frequency, payroll may already be executed even though the onboardee has not actually joined the organization or their joining is delayed. In such cases, the onboardee record is not automatically filtered out during replication, unlike in first-time hire scenarios where the isOnboardingCompleted flag prevents premature replication. This results in unintended payroll processing for onboardees who have not yet started work.

Other Terms

Incorrect replication of onboarding hires
Onboarding
Employee replication
Rehire on Old employement

Reason and Prerequisites

1. Ensure Onboarding 2.0 is enabled and configured in provisioning and SuccessFactors.
2. Maintain OnboardingInfo in CompoundEmployeeObjectTypes (Manage Data) to enable data extraction via the Compound Employee API 
3. MetaData for EC Entity WS_ONB_INF should be updated before triggering replication.

Solution

Implement this Note.
