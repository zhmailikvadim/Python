Symptom

Significant performance degradation occurs during mass material maintenance (e.g., via transaction MM17 or MASSD, or during large data migrations).

The bottleneck is caused by follow-up material checks within BADI MPE_MATERIAL_CHECK.

These checks are executed for all materials, even in systems where Production Engineering and Operations (PEO) is not active or relevant.

Other Terms

follow-up material, Material master validations, MARA, material save BADI

Reason and Prerequisites

Program complexity. Unnecessary implementation for non-PEO systems.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions.
