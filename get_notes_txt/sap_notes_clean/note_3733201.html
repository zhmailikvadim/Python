Symptom

In the process "Device Takeover (Old MOS View)" (DE_B_WIM_DEV_TKO_MOSO), the device takeover item list is not filled in step 0300 when you use the transactions "Decide on Quotation Automatically" (/US4G/DTKO_SQ_DC) and "Determine Price Automatically" (/US4G/DTKO_SQ_PR) to automatically determine whether to rent or sell the technical equipment.

This issue is relevant for the market role: MOS.

Other Terms

Device Takeover, /US4G/DTKO_SQ_DC, /US4G/DTKO_SQ_PR, DCL list, external device number, internal device number, /US4G/IF_BADI_WIM_PROCESS, /US4G/CL_DEF_BADI_WIM_PROCESS, /US4G/V_DTKOSQDC, /US4G/V_DTKOSQPR

Reason and Prerequisites

 The error occurred because the system did not delete the leading zero when mapping between the external device number and the internal device number.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.

After implementing this SAP Note, the system fills the price for the item list, and the BPEM case in step 0300 displays the maintained price from transaction /US4G/DTKO_SQ_PR. You can verify the fix by checking that the price is filled when you use the automatic determination for rent or sale of the device.
