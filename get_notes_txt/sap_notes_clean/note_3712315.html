Symptom

End date appears different depending on whether I enter the duration as days or months (e.g., 546 days vs 18 months result in different end dates in a Job Posting)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Set Start Date = April 1, 2024
    • Enter Duration = 546 days -> End Date = September 28, 2025
    • Enter Duration = 18 months -> End Date = September 30, 2025
  2. Note the difference in end dates

Resolution

This is expected behaviour. There are two distinct calculation methods:

  • Days: End Date = Start Date + (Days - 1). Inclusive of the start date, exact day count, accounts for month lengths and leap years.
  • Months: End Date = (Start Date + N calendar months) - 1 day. Adds whole calendar months, does not convert months to days.

Days are fixed units, months vary in length, so results may differ.

  • Use days when you need an exact number of days.
  • Use months when you want full calendar-month coverage.
