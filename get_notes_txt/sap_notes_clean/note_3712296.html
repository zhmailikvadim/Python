Symptom

Slow performance of Vehicle Contracts in ACM when:

Mass pricing of vehicle contracts is done from Maintain ACM Contracts App and Maintain ACM Pricing App

AND

Mass Settlements is done for vehicle contracts with overfills or underfills for each vehicle price lot.

Other Terms

Agricultural Contracts Management(ACM), Commodity Pricing Engine(CPE), Advanced CPE, Vehicle Contracts, Performance,  Note 3628704, Note 3639703, Note 3631220

Reason and Prerequisites

Slow performance of Vehicle Contracts in ACM when pricing is not done using Advanced Commodity Pricing Engine(CPE). Subsequent processes (like mass applications , mass settlements) on vehicle contracts is also impacted.

Solution

ACM is integrated with the Advanced CPE architecture S/4HANA 2025 FPS0 onwards, enabling significantly enhanced system throughput and optimized performance, particularly for high-volume and mass processing operations.

S/4HANA 2025 FPS0 onwards, SAP recommends to use ACM with Advanced CPE for better performance for processing vehicle contracts throughout ACM processes.
