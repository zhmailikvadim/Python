Symptom

Newly distributed documents do not open properly and is showing blank in the user's Messages workspace.

Environment

  • SAP Sales Performance Management
  • Incentive Management on Hyperscalers

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the Documents workspace.
  2. Upload a PDF document and assign it to a specific Business Unit.
  3. Generate a distribution utilizing the uploaded PDF document.
  4. Distribute the document for payees to accept.
  5. Proxy as one of the payees.
  6. Navigate to the Messages workspace.
  7. Open the distributed document.
  8. Observe that the PDF tool does not open the document properly and remains blank.

Cause

This is a Known Issue.

Resolution

Product Engineering is investigating a solution.
