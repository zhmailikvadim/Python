Symptom

When using the SAP Fiori app Maintain Bill of Material (F1813), you encounter the following behavior:

You attempt to edit a Bill of Material (BOM) that is subject to a history requirement (configured in T-code OS27 or OS25).

Upon clicking the Edit button, the system does not prompt for a Change Number.

The expected warning or error message "Enter Change Number - BOM has history requirement" (Message no. 29045) is not displayed, even if the "Hist req" flag is active in Customizing.
