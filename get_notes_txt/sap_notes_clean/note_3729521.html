Symptom

Within the Advance Tax Calculation for early Retirees a check on the maximum unemployment allowance is executed. The Maximum unemployment amount (SWT/RCC) is stored in payroll parameter T511P-BUNMA.

The Maximum unemployment amount (SWT/RCC) has been increased as of 01.03.2026 from 1.726,40 EUR to 1.760,98 EUR.
