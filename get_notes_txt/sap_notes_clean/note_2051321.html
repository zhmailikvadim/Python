Symptom

When at the log in page, and appending the url with ?cid= to force to connect to a cluster, if your URL is constructed incorrectly, it is not validated until AFTER you enter the user ID and password.  So you will get a valid login page, but you will not be able to login.

 

Reproducing the Issue

For example:

http://SAPSourcing:55000/sourcing/fsenterprise/portal/login/?cid=internal.SAP

The above looks correct, but there is an extra "/" after login.  This will take you to a valid log in page, but will not work when you attempt to log in.

Resolution

http://SAPSourcing:55000/sourcing/fsenterprise/portal/login?cid=internal.SAP

Make sure that the URL is constructed correctly.  The above shows a properly constructed URL.
