Symptom

MDG EAM 2023, 2024

When changing BOM description of Bill of Materials using Manage Mass processing application, Price attributes(PREIS & PEINH) is getting wiped off once the Change Request is created.

This issue is present across all the Bill of Material i.e., Material BOM, Equipment BOM, Functional Location BOM

Other Terms

MDG EAM, PREIS, PEINH, MBEW-VERPR, MBEW-PEINH, Price Unit, Bill of Material

Reason and Prerequisites

Program Error

Solution

Implement the correction instruction
