Symptom

Customer is requesting for RCA (Root Cause Analysis) for an issue which occurred only for some duration and then was working as expected.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Recruiting Management 

Reproducing the Issue

RCA for the issue reported by customer 

Cause

Please note that a formal RCA is provided only for specific cases that meet the defined guidelines. This policy applies exclusively to major, infrastructure-level incidents or outages that are officially published in the Cloud Availability Center (CAC) at the time of occurrence.

For other types of issues—such as general application defects, configuration-related concerns, or individual feature-specific issues—an official RCA document is not provided under the current policy.

Resolution

Formal RCA is provided only for specific cases that meet the defined guidelines.
