Symptom

The broker reports with the status 'S' (partially reversed) cannot be archived.

Other Terms

VVKK_BRCOL , SAPRVAM0

Reason and Prerequisites

Reference to SAP note 2125299 and 1836126

Solution

Implement the corrections relevant for your release, or import the relevant Support Package. The report does not check whether the balance on the clearing account of the broker report with the status 'S' is correct, you must check this yourself before archiving.  If only a few documents for the broker report have been reversed, you must reverse the remaining documents, reverse all reversal documents, or make manual adjustment postings.
