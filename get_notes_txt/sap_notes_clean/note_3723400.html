Symptom

  • The system displays the message "Comments not provided" under the Rating section in the "Other's Rating Tab" of a performance management form

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Performance Management
SAP SuccessFactors 360 Reviews

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Enable "Other's Rating Tab Permission" in the meta section of the form template.
  2. Configure "None" permission for the Item Comments field in all steps of the section.
  3. Enable the Latest PM form experience.
  4. Observe the "Comments not provided" message under the Rating section in the "Other's Rating Tab"

Cause

This is a Known Issue.

Resolution

The fix for this issue is planned to be deployed on 1H 2026 release (b2605). It is also required to add the following configuration in the form:

<others-ratingtab-item-permission type="none">
<role-name>*</role-name>
<tab-item refid="item-comment"/>
<route-step stepid="*"/>
</others-ratingtab-item-permission>

For release timelines, please review SAP SuccessFactors Product Release & Road Map Information.

Workaround

No workaround is available.
