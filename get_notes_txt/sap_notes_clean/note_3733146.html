Symptom

• Unable to hide or remove the "Opportunity Type" field in Page Layout during Adaptation Mode.  
• The field does not appear in the adaptation field list for hiding or removal.  

Environment

  • SAP Sales Cloud and SAP Service Cloud Version 2 
  • SAP Sales Cloud Version 2 

Reproducing the Issue

1. Navigate to the "Guided Selling" menu.  
2. Open any opportunity in detailed view.  
3. Click on "Start Adaptation Mode" from the profile icon on the top right.  
4. Expand the header section of the Opportunity.  
5. Observe that the "Opportunity Type" field is not displayed in the adaptation field list for hiding or removal.  

Cause

The "Opportunity Type" field is labeled as "Document Type" in Adaptation Mode.  

Resolution

1. Search for the field labeled "Document Type" in the adaptation list view.  
2. Hide or remove the field as required.  

Keywords
