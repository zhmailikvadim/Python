Symptom

 You create purchase orders from the "create multiple orders for materials" screen in application F1700, "Create Optimal Orders For Shipment", and get delivery dates that do not reflect the date shown in the creation confirmation dialog before.

Other Terms

F1700, PP_TRUCKOPTIMS1, Object.controller.js

Reason and Prerequisites

The date format of the model is wrongly interpreted by the parser

Solution

We adjusted the date pattern in the controller for the "create multiple orders"-view.
