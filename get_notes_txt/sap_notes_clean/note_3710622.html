Symptom

Application "Application Jobs" allows to schedule a job of Balance validation template with non-existing values of Consolidation group, Consolidation unit, Task, Consolidation COA, Version. Even if you check a job with those parameters you get OK message: "You can go ahead and schedule the job." After you scheduled the job, status becomes "Failed" and you cannot prewiev details or download  "Result".

Reason and Prerequisites

Missing checks.

Solution

This SAP Note adds value existance checks for Consolidation group, Consolidation unit, Task, Consolidation COA, Version.

Implement the attached Correction Instruction (CI) or the relevant Support Package.
