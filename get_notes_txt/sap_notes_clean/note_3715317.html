Symptom

  • Users require clarification on the functionality of the 'Confirm' and 'Not Relevant' buttons seen in SAP HotNews application and in the related SAP Notes

Environment

SAP for Me -> SAP HotNews

Resolution

  1. General Purpose of the Buttons:
    • Confirm: This button is used to indicate that a specific SAP HotNews Note has been reviewed and is acknowledged as relevant for your system. By confirming, you are essentially marking it as important for your records.
    • Not Relevant: This button is used when you determine that a particular SAP HotNews Note does not apply to your system or situation. By marking it as not relevant, you are indicating that it does not require any action or attention.

  2. How to Use the Buttons:
    • In the HotNews List View:
      1. Navigate to the SAP HotNews application.
      2. In the list view, select the checkbox next to the article(s) you wish to act upon.
      3. Click on the Confirm button in the bottom right corner to acknowledge the selected notes.
      4. Alternatively, click on the Not Relevant button to mark the selected notes as not applicable.
    • From Within the SAP Note:
      1. Click on the specific SAP Note you want to review.
      2. In the top right corner of the note, you will find the Confirm and Not Relevant buttons.
      3. Click Confirm if the note is relevant, or Not Relevant if it does not apply to your situation.

Note: The SAP HotNews (or Security Notes) application is S-user based not Customer Number based. That means if one person flags a note as Not Relevant it does not change it for other S-users.
