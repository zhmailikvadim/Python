Symptom

What are the roles and permissions required for navigation through supplier profile in joule ?

Environment

Joule integration with SLP

Cause

Resolution

To navigate to supplier profiles from Joule, including Open in App navigation, users in your SAP Ariba site must be members of the Joule Supplier Profile User group.
