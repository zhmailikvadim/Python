Symptom

Using Schedule General Ledger Jobs to schedule Post Credit-Risk-Based Impairment, the job picks up future clearing documents for valuation. For example, although invoice is cleared in next period, impairment is fully released in current period.

Other Terms

 Advanced valuation, post credit impairment

Reason and Prerequisites

Whether the invoice is cleared should depend on key date. Even though invoice is cleared but if the clearing happens after key date, the invoice should still be treated as open item.
