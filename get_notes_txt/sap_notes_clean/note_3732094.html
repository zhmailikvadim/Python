Symptom

Tcode : /PRA/TEMP_PP_OWN

When executing Maintain Owner Info transaction, the Tax ID is not getting displayed for respective owners.

Other Terms

Related Keywords:

• General keywords: Add-on, IS-Oil, Industry Solution, Oil and Gas Upstream, Roadmap, PRA, Ownership
• Problem-specific key words: IS-OIL-PRA-REV-OW, PRA Vendor, Tax ID, /PRA/TEMP_PP_OWN, BADI: /PRA/BP_TAX_ID, /PRA/CL_BP_TAX_ID, LFA1, STCD3, STCD4, STCD5

Reason and Prerequisites

The respective Tax ID which is getting picked from Table LFA1 is not maintained in STCD1 and STCD2 rather in fields STCD3 or STCD4 or STCD5.

Solution

 Apply the correction instructions attached with the SAP Note.
