Symptom

You use production planning integration of SAP Integrated Business Planning (SAP IBP) order-based planning with Production Planing and Detailed Scheduling (PP/DS) in SAP S/4HANA.

You execute the heuristic Hand Over External Planned Orders (SAP_SYP_001) for a product in an storage location MRP area. Instead of handing over external planned orders based on the DPS handover time, all external planned orders are handed over.

Other Terms

synchronized production planning, order-based planning, SAP Integrated Business Planning, SAP IBP,  subcontracting

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Solution

Implement the attached corrections or import the relevant support package.
