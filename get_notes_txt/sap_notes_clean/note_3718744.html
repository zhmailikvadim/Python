Symptom

After unassigning an Activity while the company setting SAP.FSM.WFM.UnassignReassignWithoutCopy is disabled, the Activity and its Service Call do not reappear in the planning list, even though the Activity still exists in the database.

The ServiceAssignment is deleted as expected, but the Activity cannot be found in the Service Call list, Planning list, or Activity app search, even when the filter indicates that the Activity exists.

Environment

SAP Field Service Management

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Log in to the Shell.
  2. Go to the Planning and Dispatching Board.
  3. Assign and release an activity to a technician.
  4. Unassign the activity from the technician.

Actual Result:
The activity does not return to the Service Calls list and is no longer visible.

Expected Result:
The activity should return to the Service Calls list and remain visible after being unassigned from the technician.

Cause

Disabled SAP.FSM.WFM.UnassignReassignWithoutCopy company setting.

Resolution

  1. Log in to the Admin Portal.
  2. Navigate to the affected Company.
  3. Open Company Settings.
  4. Search for the setting SAP.FSM.WFM.UnassignReassignWithoutCopy.
  5. Enable this company setting (set to true).
  6. Go to the Planning and Dispatching Board.
  7. Assign and then unassign an activity.
  8. Verify whether the activity is visible in the Planning Board and on the Activities page as expected.
