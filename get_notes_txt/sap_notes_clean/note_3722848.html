Symptom

The inability to withdraw a draft work order from the supplier side despite meeting the defined SOW rules.

Environment

Statement of Work

Cause

Resolution

  1. Enable the company configuration Suppliers can withdraw SOW Workers. This configuration allows suppliers with the User Role Permission (URP) to manage and respond to SOW to withdraw SOW Workers submitted during an SOW Response, SOWR Response, or on the fly, as long as the submitted SOW Workers are in Draft, Pending Approval, or Approval Paused status.
  2. This config is available to Configuration Manager to edit.
  3. Confirm that the withdrawal functionality is available from the supplier side after enabling the configuration.
