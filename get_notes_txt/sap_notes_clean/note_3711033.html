Symptom

In the process "Sending of Deregistration Due to End of Supply for Electricity (DSO View)" (DE_B_GPKE_DEREG_DSO), step 2000, the system does not trigger MDC 55126 and 55218 for the future supplier during deregistration. As a result, no MDCs are sent to the future supplier when deregistration is processed.

This issue is relevant for the market role: DSO.

Other Terms

GPKE, DE_B_GPKE_DEREG_DSO, deregistration, MDC, MDC 55126, MDC 55218, future supplier, process step 2000, DSO, end of supply

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because the system was missing MDC triggers for the future supplier in the deregistration process.

Solution

Implement the corrections of this SAP Note or import the relevant support package.
After implementing this SAP Note, the system triggers MDC 55126 and 55218 for the future supplier in the deregistration process. You need to retrigger the process. You can verify the fix by checking that MDCs are triggered for the future supplier successfully.
