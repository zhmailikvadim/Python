Symptom

In My Timesheet (S/4HANA/Version 4) application,

  • In Quick Entry, non-working days are not respected while entering time records.
  • In Quick Entry, a UI glitch is observed when selecting worklist entries.

Other Terms

F3074A, My Timesheet V4, Quick Entry, Worklist Selection.

Reason and Prerequisites

  • Due to a mapping issue in the coding, the system is considering non-working days during time recording, even though the data entry profile is configured to allow only working days.
  • Selection inconsistencies are occurring due to redundant manual handling of worklist selection.

Solution

Please implement the automatic correction instruction attached to this SAP Note or alternatively you can implement the corresponding support pack.
