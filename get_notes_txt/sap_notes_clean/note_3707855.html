Symptom

After completing a 360 form, the competency rating is not visible in the "sysOverallCompetency" trend portlet.

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors 360 Reviews
SAP SuccessFactors Employee Profile

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Complete the 360 form.
  2. Navigate to the People Profile.
  3. Attempt to view the "Competencies" portlet.
  4. "sysOverallCompetency" rating is not displayed.

Cause

  • The overall competency rating is derived from the Objective Competency Summary section in a PM form.
  • The 360 form does not support the Objective Competency Summary section type, which is required to generate the overall competency rating.

Resolution

This is expected behavior.
