Symptom

You use the SAP Fiori app "Transfer Business Partner Items" (F4398) to transfer a document of a business partner to another business partner. In addition to the new business partner, you enter the corresponding contract account and the contract. After you confirm using the "Transfer" button, the input field for the contract is highlighted in red. As soon as you then click on this field, the system issues the relevant error message "Contract &1 is not assigned to this business partner or contract account".

Other Terms

cus.o2c.bpitem.transfer.s1, FP-EVENT-0200

Reason and Prerequisites

Reason:
Event 0200 is defined in the system. This is run when the document is transferred to another business partner to check the assignment of the business partner, contract account, and contract fields. However, the SAP Fiori app does not call event 0200 dynamically so that the function modules defined in the system for event 0200 are called. Instead, it explicitly calls only the provider-contract-specific function module for event 0200 (FKK_VT_EVENT_0200).

Prerequisites:
You use the SAP Fiori app "Transfer Business Partner Items" (F4398).

Solution

After you implement this SAP Note, event 0200 is called dynamically so that the function modules relevant for you are called for event 0200.
