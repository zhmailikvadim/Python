Symptom

SAP Deutschland offers various consulting services for SAP Patient Management (IS-H). For customer planning when replacing IS-H with other systems based on SAP S/4HANA, information about the further use of the previous services for the country version Germany is required as early as possible.

Other Terms

Healthcare Hospital LV DE Services Consulting

Reason and Prerequisites

Longer Term Information

Solution

Several manufacturers have launched “IS-H-like” solutions as an add-on to SAP S/4HANA, private cloud. For SAP Patient Management (IS-H) within ECC, SAP Deutschland offers some healthcare services, some of which are widely used. In this SAP Note, we would like to inform you about our plans for the possible continuation of these services on the new systems.

The following service is already available for S/4HANA + side-by-side and can be used without one of the new “IS-H-like” solutions:

  • Service Billing Document

If necessary, the conversion/adjustment of the following services takes place via SAP Consulting. Please contact us if necessary.

  • Copayment Monitor
  • Day Patient Absences
  • Referral Matching
  • Interfaces ID-Berlin (DIACOS, Grouper, …)
  • Solventum Interface (KODIP, QS-MED)

Completion dates cannot currently be specified.

The following services are not continued by SAP Consulting and must be taken into account in the new solution approaches:

  • Additional Procedure §302
  • Billing Check
  • Individual Contracts

If you have any questions, please contact Ms. Rentschler (berenice.rentschler@sap.com).
