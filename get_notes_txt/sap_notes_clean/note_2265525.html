Symptom

You have been using SAP Single Sign-On 2.0 and you have Identity Provider (IdP) extension policy scripts. Now you have upgraded to SAP Single Sign-On 3.0 and you want to migrate your policy scripts to the new central storage for policy scripts (the Policy Scripts Administration Console) and to be able to create and recall all versions of a policy script.

Other Terms

Access Policies, RBA, SSO, Identity Provider extensions, SAML2

Reason and Prerequisites

You have upgraded to SAP Single Sign-On 3.0.

Solution

1. Depending on your SAP NetWeaver version, apply the corresponding patches provided in the Support Packages & Patches section of this SAP Note.
2. In SAP NetWeaver, search for the Identity Provider extension policy scripts.
3. Follow the instructions in the UI to migrate your Identity Provider extension policy scripts from the SAML2 configuration to the to the central storage.
