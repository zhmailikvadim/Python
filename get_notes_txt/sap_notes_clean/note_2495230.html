Symptom

  • You execute transaction TPM_INITIALIZE for OTC deals with a certain key date.
  • "Unlike the initialization for securities and loans, the key date in this case does not act as a block;
    meaning that you can continue to process business operations dated before the key date and can make
    changes to the position (such as rollovers) retrospectively." (help.sap.com)
  • Nevertheless, for some reason, you want to exclude certain transactions from this process.

Environment

  • Treasury and Risk Management (TRM)

Reproducing the Issue

  • Execute transaction TPM_INITIALIZE for OTC-deals
  • All deals are selected for initialization, although you provide a key date

Resolution

  • In case you'd like to exclude certain deals, please use report RFTR_MIGRATION_EXCLUDE_OTC.
  • Be certain to make use of the Test-Run-Flag to check the outcome before executing the report in real run.
  • After execution, these deals will not be considered for initialization.
  • Please be aware that this will also affect transaction TPM_MIGRATION.
  • The excluded deals will not be considered for migration.
  • Also, they will not be shown in TPM12 or TPM13, they won't be selected in LDB-Reporting and they will not be editable in FTR_EDIT (display-only!).

See Also

SAP note 731175 - TPM_MIGRATION: Exclude OTC transactions from migration/init.
