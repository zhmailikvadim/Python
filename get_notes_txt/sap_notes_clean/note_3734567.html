Symptom

In the process "Start of Supply" (1012), step 0060, a registration for a supplier change in the gas division was rejected incorrectly when the deadline was 7 business days before the start of supply. The system should reject the registration only if the deadline is less than 10 business days before the start of supply.

Other Terms

IDEXDE, Start of Supply, 1012, Gas division, deadline, CHECK_SOS_DATE_UPDATE_G, DSO

Reason and Prerequisites

Program errors.

Solution

Please implement the automatic corrections or import higher relevant support packages.
This SAP Note is relevant for the following role: DSO.
