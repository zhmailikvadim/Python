Symptom

Two operations of the same planned order have equal duration and are linked by Start-Start and End-End relationships, but the CTP heuristic does not schedule them in parallel. After running CTP, the two operations' start and end dates/time are different. 

Environment

  • SAP Supply Chain Management
  • Production Planning and Detailed Scheduling

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Create or use a order whose two operations have equal duration and are connected with Start-Start and End-End relationships.
  2. Run an ATP check using CTP for a sales order that plans these operations.
  3. Observe in the product view, order details that the two operations are scheduled at different times by CTP, even though their relationships are Start-Start and End-End.

Cause

The "Constraint TP for liveCache" between the operations in the PDS is not set to enforce parallelism; thus CTP is allowed to place the operations non-parallel.

Resolution

If it is required for both activities to be scheduled in parallel, the “Constraint TP for liveCache” setting in PDS should use the constraint type of Minimum Interval = Maximum Interval = 0(Equal Constraint) or Min./Max. Interval (Combination Min. and Max. Constraints).
