Symptom

  • The "Manage Business Partners - SPL Screening" app does not display blocked business partners correctly after upgrading to GTS E4H 2025.
  • The SPL screening status is missing, preventing the display of blocked Business Partners.
  • The SPL screening status is not shown in the details of the Business Partner.

Environment

  • Global Trade Services (GTS)
  • SAP Global Trade Services 

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the app "Business Partner" and navigate to the "SAP GTS" tab.
  2. Verify that the "Block" exists for business partner.
  3. Open the "Manage Business Partners - SPL Screening" app in Fiori launchpad.
  4. Use the blocked BP number to filter business partners. Confirm that no entry is displayed unless the SPL screening status is missing.
  5. Check the details of the business partner and confirm that the SPL screening status is not shown.

Cause

With SAP GTS, edition for SAP HANA 2025, a new SPL status field STSPL has been introduced in table /SAPSLL/ADRCON. The new SPL status field is not updated after the system upgrade, which prevents the display of blocked business partners.

Resolution

  • For case that classic audit trail is used in SAP GTS 11.0, periodic screening (B1M) should be executed to fill the optimized audit trail and SPL status field in table /SAPSLL/ADRCON in SAP GTS, edition for SAP HANA system. Report /SAPSLL/SPL_UPDATE_STSPL needn’t to be executed in this case.
  • For other case, execute report /SAPSLL/SPL_UPDATE_STSPL after the system conversion to fill this field according to the system decision and user decision in optimized audit trail.
