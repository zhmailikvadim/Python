Symptom

The UOM conversion is not happening for pricing subtotals in settlement management documents.

Environment

  • Settlement Management
  • Agency Business
  • Condition Contract Management
  • Condition Contract Settlement
  • SAP ERP Central Component
  • SAP S/4 HANA

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Start transaction WZR1.
  2. Select settlement process type.
  3. Enter organizational data.
  4. Maintain Bill-to Party.
  5. Enter document date and reference.
  6. Open the line item, go to Pricing.
  7. Enter condition rate for condition type.
  8. Navigate back and save the settlement document.

Cause

System works as designed.

Resolution

The unit of measure used for pricing subtotals depends on the pricing procedure side:

  • For sales-side pricing procedures, subtotals use the material’s base UOM and no conversion to the sales UOM is performed in subtotals.
  • For MM-side pricing procedures, subtotals consider the settlement item’s UOM.

Possible solution:

  1. Review which pricing procedure is assigned and determine whether it is sales-side or MM-side.
  2. If subtotals must reflect the settlement item’s UOM or a converted value, switch to or adapt an MM-side pricing procedure as per project design.
  3. If a procedural change is not desired, implement an enhancement to influence the pricing communication structures:
    1. Use BAdI WLF_DOCUMENT_EXT (IF_WLF_DOCUMENT_EXT-CHANGE_PRICING_ITEM).
    2. Alternatively, use BAdI IF_EX_WLF_ADDITIONAL_DATA2 (method CHANGE_PRICING_ITEM).
  4. Ensure prerequisites for quantity conversion in CCS are met when relevant (for example, material as split criterion or assigned in the business volume determination profile).
