Symptom

I'm not able to find some questions on Analytical Reports fields for my Supplier Registration questionnaires. How can I report them?

Resolution

The SLP Supplier Registration questionnaires are placed under the SM Project Questionnaire Response fact for Analytical Reports.

The question information you are looking for it's under the Section and Question fields depending on the level you have created the questions on your SLP questionnaire.

See Also

When working with SM Project Questionnaire Response fact (SMQR) it's important to add the fields to locale the question inside the SLP Vendor Registration questionnaire:

  • SM Vendor ID
  • Survey 
  • Survey ID
  • Section (L1)
  • Section (L2)
  • Question


It'll help to identify the info in the UI side, the questions are not fields, but a value inside the Section or Question fields. You could filter the Survey field with the survey name you are looking for (for example: Supplier Registration Questionnaire, or other specific questionnaire you may have).

If you need further support to create the reports based on your business needs, please contact your CEE/CSP to engage the Best Practices Support team to provide a coach about report creation since this is out of our support scope.
