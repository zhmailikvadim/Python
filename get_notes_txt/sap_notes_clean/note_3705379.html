Symptom

When you manually enter input GAS meter readings with reading reason 06 (move-in) using transaction EL28, the system sets an incorrect quantity determination status in the reading documents within the EABL.This issue is relevant for the market role: SUP.

Other Terms

Move-in, GAS, meter reading, EL28, ISU_QD_GAS, reading reason 06, quantity determination status, EABL

Reason and Prerequisites

The error occurred because there was no specific logic to process meter readings with reading reason 06 (move-in) for quantity determination status.
