Symptom

The error message M7036 "No goods receipt possible for purchasing document" occurs when try to perform the GR posting for inbound delivery within advanced intercompany stock transfer process.

Reproducing the Issue

VL23N try to posting the goods receipt for inbound delivery (step GRID)

Cause

For advanced intercompany stock transfer process, the GRID step is the last step for the goods movement posting.

Resolution

Needs to complete the GRRV (Post Goods Receipt to Receiving SiT) first by VCM in order to be able to trigger the GRID (Post Stock Transfer into Physical Stock)

Keywords

VL32N, GR posting
