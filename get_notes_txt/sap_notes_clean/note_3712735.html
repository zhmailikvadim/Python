Symptom

This SAP Note provides you with the following enhancements on the new form E for CP8-Pin. 2025, published by the Inland Revenue Board (IRB) of Malaysia:

  • The year reference of 2024 is changed to 2025.
  • Section B regarding the tax agent is added with its value as blank.

Other Terms

CP8-Pin. 2025, Form E, RPCTEAL0_01, EA or EC Annual Tax Report, HR_MY_FORME_2025,  e-E, V_T799BSFT

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a legal change.

You run the EA or EC Annual Tax Report (RPCTEAL0_01) report, enter 2025 in the Year of EA Tax Assessment field, and select the Print Borang E form checkbox.

Note: The generated Form E in PDF format is for reference only and it cannot be uploaded in myTax e-Filing system directly.

The due date to submit Form E 2025 is March 31, 2026.

Form E is considered complete only if you submit C.P.8D on or before March 31, 2026. Employers who have submitted information via e-Data Praisi or e-CP8D before February 25, 2026 are no longer required to complete and submit C.P.8D via Form E.

Solution

Apply the HR Support Package or implement instructions in this SAP Note and then carry out the manual activities attached.

For more information about the development delivered with this SAP Note, see the entry for this SAP Note in the What's New topic on SAP Help Portal.
