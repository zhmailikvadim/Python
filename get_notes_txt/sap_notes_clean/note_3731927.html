Symptom

When the service order has service quotation as a predecessor, the service contract assigned to the order is not considered in the pricing. When the 'Complete Reprice' button is pressed in service order item, the price agreements are not taken over from the service contract.

Reason and Prerequisites

The service order has service quotation as a predecessor.

The service contract is saved in the transaction history of the service order.

Solution

Please upgrade to OP2025 FPS2
