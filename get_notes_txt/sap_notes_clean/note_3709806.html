Symptom

Refresh Structure button is grayed out on Data Foundation from an universe in Information Design Tool (IDT)

  • The scenario happen to all kinds of users, including administrator

Environment

  • SAP BusinessObjects Business Intelligence platform 4.3 SP3 and above
  • Information Design Tool (IDT)

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open IDT; 
  2. Open a universe that has a lot of tables;
  3. Open the Data Foundation;
  4. Try to use the "Refresh Structure" option (through IDT menu or right clicking in the table > selecting the "Refresh Structure" button);
  5. The option is grayed out

 

Cause

The tables are not in ‘View' option. Tables are closed / summarized. 

Resolution

To open the tables: 

  1. Open IDT;
  2. Open the Data Foundation from an universe;
  3. You will see two columns, the table list in the left and in the right you will see a rectangle the says “Master” with info about tables and joins;
  4. Click in this rectangle and it will pop up a screen asking you to confirm the ‘View Activation’, once you confirm it will open the tables;
  5. After the tables are opened, the refresh structure option will be available.

How to use the Refresh Structure option, please check KBA 3710878 - How to use the Refresh Structure option in Information Design Tool (IDT)
