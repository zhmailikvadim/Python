Symptom

Contribution percentages (Employer Contribution and Your Contribution) are displayed correctly in English but appear as 0 in other languages during employee enrollment in the Benefits section.

"Image/data in this KBA is from SAP internal systems, sample data, or demo systems. Any resemblance to real data is purely coincidental." 

Environment

SAP SuccessFactors Employee Central

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Navigate to the Benefits Section;
  2. Select the pension plan under and click the enroll button;
  3. Review the plan details (Employer Contribution and Your Contribution) in English; percentages are displayed correctly;
  4. Switch the system language to another language;
  5. Access the enrollment summary or registered benefits;
  6. Observe that contribution percentages are displayed as 0.

Cause

The system debug key BENEFITS_EOI_INS_COST is incorrectly resolving the parameter {0} in non-English languages, reading it as (0) instead of the actual calculated percentage value.

Resolution

Known issue.

The fix will be deployed with the 1H2026 release.

For the Product Release schedule, refer to Customer Community Product Release and Road Map Information page.
