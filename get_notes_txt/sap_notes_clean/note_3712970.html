Symptom

You are in the item tab of the warehouse inspection.
In certain situations, it can happen the lines in the the table control on top lose internal information (information which is not displayed in the table control) which are than missing when the method CHEK_INSP_DATA of the BAdI MSR_TRC is called.
This can happen when you scroll to the last line of the table control and try to select the line after the last line.

Other Terms

Quartz dark theme, msr_s_insp_ui_itm_tc, IF_MSR_TRC_INSP~CHECK_DATA

Reason and Prerequisites

initialization issue

Solution

Implement the correction instructions.
