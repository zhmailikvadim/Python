Symptom

For Blanked Purchase Orders, the Effective date and Expiration date are configured for a specific date, but the portal shows a day more or less. 

Cause

This happens because the portal considers the timezone configured on the computer being used. If the Effective date and Expiration date are set for a specific day and time, depending on the timezone the supplier is in, the system will automatically convert the date accordingly.

Resolution

If the supplier needs to see the exact date configured in the cXML, it's necessary to set the information taking into account the timezone in which the supplier is located.

See Also

The date formatting follows the ISO 8601.
