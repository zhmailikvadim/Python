Symptom

You cannot mark an item as "not discountable". The item marked as non-discountable should not receive a discount. 

Solution

As of SAP Business One 10.0 FP 2502, a new checkbox Do Not Apply Discount is added to the General tab of Items Master Data.

In the SAP Business One, Web client, this new checkbox Do Not Apply Discount is added to the Pricing tab of Items Master Data.

If you select the Do Not Apply Discount checkbox, the discounts applied for the item lines in the Discount % field in all marketing documents will be set to zero, while for the unit price, there is no change. This checkbox is relevant for lines discount applied by the following sources:

  • Blanket agreement monetary method (for blanket agreement items method, the discount % field is not relevant)
  • Special price for BP
  • Period and volume discount
  • Discount groups

Please note that this checkbox is not relevant for the discount given in the Discount field of the marketing document header. This checkbox is relevant for non based document lines only, while not relevant for the target document (copied from a base document) lines.

This definition does not change the hierarchy of the pricing assignment.
