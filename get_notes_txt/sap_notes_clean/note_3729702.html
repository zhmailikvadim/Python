Symptom

When using a special procurement key in the product master for stock transfer between two plants, the mandatory transportation lane is not created automatically.

The issue occurs specifically for retail articles in embedded PPDS systems.

Environment

  • Embedded Production Planning and Detailed Scheduling (PPDS)
  • SAP S/4HANA Retail
  • SCM APO

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Activate the Advanced Planning checkbox for the material master record.
  2. Execute the report /SAPAPO/CREATE_LOCATION to activate the locations for stock transfer in embedded PPDS.
  3. Assign a special procurement key in the product master for stock transfer between two plants.
  4. Verify the transportation lane creation in transaction /SAPAPO/SCC_TL1 is missing.

Cause

The functionality for automatic transportation lane creation based on special procurement type is not implemented for retail articles in embedded PPDS systems.

Resolution

The automatic creation of transportation lanes based on special procurement type is not possible for retail articles.
