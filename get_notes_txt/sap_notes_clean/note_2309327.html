Symptom

When you print a document, you do not receive any data of the cash flow documents.

Other Terms

Basis line of business

Correspondence Cache

Update policies or contracts

Print correspondence

/PM0/ABY_FPP_FP_PA

/PM0/ABY_FPP_CORR

Reason and Prerequisites

Program error

Prerequisites

- For the processing of the affected document, you use the correspondence cache.

- The affected document originates from a product of the customer-specific or partner-specific line of business.

Cause of the problem

During the determination of the data of the cash flow documents, the system does not execute the line of business-specific program parts.

Solution

Implement the attached correction instructions or import the specified Support Package.
