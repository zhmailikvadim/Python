During scheduling a shipment error O9 396: Internal error reading Table G_OIGSI_TAB appears when the sequence of actions is as below.

1) Schedule a shipment with vehicle which has single compartment and 2 deliveries each having 2 materials and save the shipment(not confirming it).

2) After saving change the shipment (O4F2) go to the Document quantities tab and change the quantity of the second delivery second material.

3) After this delete the second delivery and save the shipment.

4) Try to open the shipment in change or display mode and error 'O9 396: Internal error reading Table G_OIGSI_TAB' will be thrown.

Other Terms

O4F2, SORT, OIGSVMQ, auto allocation,  s_compartment_unloading_one

Reason and Prerequisites

During delivery deletion since the vehicle has single compartment the compartment unloading routine is called and both the delivery items should be unallocated. In this routine the order of the records in OIGSVMQ table gets changed after the first delivery item is unallocated. Due to this the second position item in the delivery doesnt get unallocated and results in O9 396 error.

Solution

Sort is introduced to avoid this error.
