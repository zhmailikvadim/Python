Symptom

  • The field Mode of Transport (MOT_UI_ST) is not populated in the Transportation Cockpit under the Freight Unit Stages area.
  • The related description is displayed correctly, but the field remains empty.
  • A 500 Internal Server Error occurs when accessing the Transportation Cockpit.
  • Error message: "Internal application error of BO /SCMTMS/PROF_LAYOUT_SET".

Environment

  • SAP Transportation Management
  • SAP S/4HANA 2023
  • SAP S/4HANA 2025

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open the Transportation Cockpit.
  2. In the Profile Selection, choose the relevant planning profile.
  3. Under the FU Stages area, click the dropdown next to Standard, select Manage, and then choose Transportation Mode.

Cause

This is due to Program Error.

Resolution

If you want to see the mode of transportation, under the Freight Unit Stages in the Transportation Cockpit. In order to get the expected behavior, implement the below SAP Note:
3692463 - TC: Transportation Mode is not populated in quadrant 'Freight Unit Stages'
