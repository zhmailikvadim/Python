Symptom

• When a user is terminated, scheduled reports and integration jobs in Integration Center may become inactivated.

• Jobs may fail due to an invalid user or job owner. 

Environment

  • SAP SuccessFactors HXM Suite
    • Integration Center

Reproducing the Issue

1. Terminate a user who is the owner of scheduled reports or integration jobs in Integration Center.

2. Observe that the scheduled reports and integration jobs become inactivated. 

Cause

• Jobs fail due to an invalid user or job owner.

• SAP does not recommend assigning individual users as responsible for initiating jobs. 

Resolution

1. Create a dedicated system user, such as SFADMIN, to act as the job owner instead of assigning individual users.

2. Change the job owner to the newly created system user to eliminate dependency on a specific user.

3. Set up a new schedule with the newly assigned system user to ensure jobs continue running as scheduled after the original user's termination. 

Keywords
