Symptom

In a scenario with zero confirmation at check horizon and a check horizon end date before the requested date, Supply Protection quantity assignments are not correctly deleted in a recheck.

Other Terms

aATP; PAC; SUP; Zero Confirmation at Check Horizon end date

Reason and Prerequisites

The reason is that the check call for PAC is skipped in that case and no update call is triggered.

With this correction this is changed by triggering a check of the product availability and an update call to Supply Protection also in this case.

Solution

Apply the correction instructions for this note or upgrade to a SAP S/4HANA support package that contains this correction.
