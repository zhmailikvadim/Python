Symptom

You are performing a Migration of Equipment Resources, but you are getting the following error in the Simulation step:

"Resource XXXXX relevant for plng/schedulng; operating time details missing"

(XXXXX refers to the ID of the Resource you are migrating).

Environment

SAP Business ByDesign

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Go to the Business Configuration work center and access the view Overview;
  2. Search for the activity Migration of Equipment Resources and open it;
  3. Click on the option Migrate equipment resources using the migration tool;
  4. Perform the upload of your Migration Template and click on Execute Migration Step by Step;
  5. On step 3 you will see the above mentioned error.

Cause

If the flag Relevant for Capacity Planning is marked, then all tabs related to Operating Times have to be maintained for this resource.

Resolution

Maintain the information for this resource in the following tabs:

Std Operating Times

Recurrence - Std Ops Times

Operating Hrs - Std Ops Time

Time Dependent Operating Times

Recur-Time Dependent Ops Times

Ops Hrs-Time Dependent Op Time
