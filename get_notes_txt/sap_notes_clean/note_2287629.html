Symptom

  • Testing the pcRatingWS on a replicated FS-PRO's Admin Console fails with a java.net.MalformedURLException.
  • Exception: "java.net.MalformedURLException: while trying to invokethe method java.lang.String.length() of an object loaded from Local variable 'spec'"

Environment

  • SAP Product Lifecycle Management for Insurance
  • SAP Product and Quotation Management for Insurance 2.0

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Login to the FS-PRO Admin Console.
  2. Go to Product > Manage Product Web Services.
  3. Click on the "Runtime" tab.
  4. Select the relevant product.
  5. In the Operations dropdown, select pcRatingWS web service, click "Test" and then "Execute".
  6. Looking in the default_trace.trc file shows the full error text:

Cause

  • Dependent products jars needed to main product to run calculations in the pcRatingWS were not present in the replicated FS-PRO instance.

Resolution

  • When replicating an FS-PRO environment ensure that the full product data set is copied over as well.
