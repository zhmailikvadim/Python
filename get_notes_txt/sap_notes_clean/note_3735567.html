Symptom

An employee has no PCB2 form on current year but has PCB2 form on previous year.

When the employee views the PCB2 form on the Employee Self-Service (ESS) page, the system displays the PCB2 form from the previous year successfully. However, an error message displays in the bottom left corner:

No data has been found for the specified selection

After you've installed the SAP Note, above error message does not occur.

Here is an example:

An employee joined the company on 05.12.2024 and views the PCB2 form on Employee Self-Service (ESS) page on some day of year 2026.

Year 2024 Year 2025 Year 2026 Action of employee Before SAP Note implemented After SAP Note implemented Changed
1 1 1 Open the page Form of Year 2026 displayed Form of Year 2026 displayed  
1 1 0 Open the page Form of Year 2025 displayed with error message Form of Year 2025 displayed without any message Yes
1 0 0 Open the page The Year field changed as 2025 without Form, with error message The Year field changed as 2025 without Form, with error message  
1 0 1 Select 2025 from the dropdown list of Year field Form of Year 2024 displayed with error message The Year field is 2025 without Form, with error message Yes
0 1 1 Select 2024 from the dropdown list of Year field The Year field changed as 2023 without Form, with error message The Year field is 2024 without Form, with error message Yes

* 1 means PCB2 form was generated, 0 means PCB2 form isn't generated at that tax year.

Other Terms

No data has been found for the specified selection, 001(XSS_MY), HRXSS_MY_PCB2_GET_PDF, PCB2 on ESS, CL_HRESS_REP_MY_PCB2, SAP&EA

Reason and Prerequisites

This is a program error.

Solution

You can apply HR Support Package or implement the correction instructions in this SAP Note.
