Symptom

In the Fiori app Find Maintenance Order Confirmation (F2174), the results table at the bottom includes the Date and Time fields "Actual Start" and "Actual End". Either field may appear blank, even though the confirmation has data stored.

Other Terms

Find Maintenance Order Confirmation, F2174, C_ObjPgMaintOrderConfirmation, 24:00:00, dats_tims_to_tstmp

Reason and Prerequisites

The Date (day) and Time are stored separately for the mentioned fields. CDS view C_ObjPgMaintOrderConfirmation merges date and time in to one single field. Time field sometimes stores 24:00:00 due to historical reasons, but the conversion function dats_tims_to_tstmp handles 24:00:00 as invalid. Hence the merged field shall be calculated in a more complex way for each field.
