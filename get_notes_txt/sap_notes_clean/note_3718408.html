Symptom

  • Error message 'Enter valid partner country - /ECRS/BL_TD 69' occurs in transaction /ECRS/RP_EDIT.  
  • The error also appears in the app 'Manage Intrastat declaration'.

Environment

  • SAP Global Trade Services, Edition for HANA
  • SAP Global Trade Services 11.0
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud Private Edition
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • Intrastat Reporting

Reproducing the Issue

  1. Open transaction /ECRS/RP_EDIT.  
  2. Enter POI (provider of information).  
  3. Select a specific Intrastat declaration and navigate into details.  
  4. Select an item.

Cause

Incomplete customizing for partner countries. 

Resolution

Maintain the following customizing for partner countries: 

  • Navigate to  > Governance, Risk and Compliance > International Trade > Intrastat > Basic Data > Partner Country
    1. Define Partner Countries:
      • Define the standard codes for partner countries.  
      • Ensure the national authorities publish these standard codes.  
      • Note that this customizing activity is relevant for all member states of the European Union.

    2. Assign Partner Country to Country of Declaration:
      • In this customizing setting partner countries must be assigned to every individual country of declaration. 

    3. Define Exceptions for Conversion of Partner Countries:
      • In this Customizing activity, you define exceptions for the conversion of partner countries. 
      • By default, the system converts the SAP internal code of partner countries to the respective ISO code. If exceptions to this standard logic are required, define these exceptions.
      • Example: 
        In Germany, Monaco (MC) must be converted to France (FR).
        In Ireland, Northern Ireland (part of GB) must be converted to a separate partner country (XI) and must not be reported as United Kingdom (GB).
